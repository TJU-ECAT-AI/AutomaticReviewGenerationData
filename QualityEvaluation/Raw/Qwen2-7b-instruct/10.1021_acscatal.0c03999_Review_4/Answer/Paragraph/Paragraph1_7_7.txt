        Detailed analysis established from the study of reference [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4], [10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019], [10.1021/cm902015f], and others, revealing the intricate relationship between the structural characteristics, surface properties (especially acidity and basicity), and Lewis acid sites of various gallium oxide polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-Ga2O3) and their catalytic performance in carbon dioxide-olefin dehydration (CO2-ODHP) reactions. This review emphasizes the pivotal role these properties play in influencing both the kinetic behavior and product selectivity during both the initial and steady-state stages of the reaction.

        β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs, characterized by their tetrahedral Ga cations and coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites, outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 polymorphs in terms of conversion of propane to olefins and selectivity for propene. The surface acidity density, decreasing in the order of β > γ > δ > α, correlates with the observed selectivity and activity trends. The initial activity is highly dependent on the surface acid density, with β-Ga2O3 polymorphs demonstrating the highest activity due to their specific Lewis acid sites that efficiently promote oxidative dehydrogenation. 

        The surface basic sites, however, are insufficient for effective CO2 adsorption and activation, leading to lower CO2 conversion compared to propane. The surface base site density correlates with the initial activity, suggesting a promotional effect in the early stages of the reaction. The weak reducibility of Ga2O3 and the presence of Ga+ and Ga3+ species indicate that the reduction of Ga cations occurs primarily at the onset of the reaction, enhancing dehydrogenation efficiency but only during the initial phase.

        The dehydrogenation process is believed to proceed via heterolytic dissociation on the Ga oxide surface, without involving a redox cycle. The reaction dynamics are further modulated by the presence of different support materials, such as zeolites with medium pore sizes and weak acidity, which can alter the selectivity and stability of the catalysts. For instance, HZSM-48-supported catalysts exhibit higher propene selectivity compared to HZSM-5-supported ones, attributed to their weaker acid strength, which reduces the risk of unwanted side reactions.

        The activity of Ga2O3 catalysts can be tuned by adjusting the textural properties through methods like nanocasting with mesoporous carbon as a hard template or the urea-based hydrothermal method using polyethylene glycol as a soft template agent. These strategies aim to optimize the surface area, pore size distribution, and acidity/basicity balance, thereby enhancing the catalytic performance.

        In summary, the structure-property-performance relationships in Ga2O3 polymorphs for CO2-ODHP catalysis highlight the importance of fine-tuning the surface characteristics to achieve optimal catalytic activity and selectivity. Future research directions could focus on developing new synthesis methods, exploring the synergistic effects of different metal oxides, and optimizing the reaction conditions to improve the efficiency and sustainability of CO2 utilization in olefin production.

        从参考文献[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]、[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019]、[10.1021/cm902015f]和其他相关研究中深入分析，揭示了不同镓氧化物晶相（α-、β-、γ-、δ-Ga2O3）的结构特征、表面性质（特别是酸性和碱性）、以及Lewis酸中心在二氧化碳-烯烃脱水（CO2-ODHP）反应中的催化性能之间的复杂关系。本文重点探讨了这些性质如何影响反应的动力学行为和产物选择性，特别是在初始和稳态阶段。

        β-和γ-Ga2O3晶相因其四面体Ga离子和协调不饱和Ga3+位点而表现出较高的活性，在丙烷转化为烯烃以及丙烯选择性方面的性能优于α-和δ-Ga2O3。表面酸密度按照β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，与观察到的活性趋势和选择性相关。初始活性高度依赖于表面酸密度，β-Ga2O3晶相由于其特定的Lewis酸中心能够高效促进氧化脱氢作用，表现出最高的活性。

        然而，表面碱性位点对于有效的CO2吸附和活化不足，导致CO2转化率低于丙烷。表面碱基位点密度与初始活性之间存在关联，表明在反应初期具有促进效果。Ga2O3的弱可还原性和Ga+和Ga3+的存在表明，Ga原子的还原主要发生在反应开始时，这在初始阶段提高了脱氢效率。

        预计CO2脱水过程通过Ga氧化物表面的异裂解进行，而不涉及氧化还原循环。反应动力学受到不同支撑材料的影响，例如具有中等孔径和弱酸性的沸石，它们可以改变催化剂的选择性和稳定性。例如，HZSM-48支持的催化剂表现出比HZSM-5更高的丙烯选择性，归因于其较弱的酸强度，这降低了发生意外副反应的风险。

        通过纳米铸造法使用多孔碳作为硬模板或尿素基水热法使用聚乙二醇作为软模板剂调整文本性质的方法可以优化表面面积、孔径分布和酸碱平衡，从而增强催化性能。

        总之，Ga2O3晶相在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系强调了精细调节表面特性以实现最佳催化活性和选择性的重要性。未来研究方向可能集中在：
        
        1. **开发新型Ga2O3晶相**，具有定制的结构特征，以增强CO2-ODHP反应的催化活性和选择性。
        2. **探索复合催化剂系统**，结合Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物或功能性材料，利用协同效应并扩大催化应用的范围。
        3. **提高催化剂稳定性和寿命**，通过设计坚固的支撑材料和集成稳定剂来实现。
        4. **深入研究反应机制**，旨在优化反应条件和催化剂设计，以阐明CO2-ODHP反应的详细途径。
        5. **考虑环境和经济效益**在可持续CO2利用技术的发展中，专注于减少能源消耗和废物生成。
        6. **将CO2捕获与利用集成**到工业过程中，目标是建立闭环系统，有效捕获CO2排放并将它们转化为有价值的产品。
        7. **探索替代反应途径**，超越传统的CO2-ODHP路径，可能引领发现更高效和选择性的CO2转换催化过程。

        这些途径代表了推进CO2催化转换领域的有前景的方向，有助于实现更大规模地管理大气CO2水平的目标，并为可持续管理这一关键资源作出贡献。
