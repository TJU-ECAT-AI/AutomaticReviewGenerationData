            The structural characteristics, surface properties (particularly acidity and basicity), and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-) significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity. Surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, so does the surface basicity. The initial activity depends on the surface acid density, with β-Ga2O3 maximizing this activity. Tetrahedral Ga cations in β- and γ-Ga2O3 structures create coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites, acting as Lewis acid sites for dehydrogenation reactions. Octahedral Ga3+ in α- and δ-Ga2O3 are less coordinated with weaker acidity, allowing for the potential reconstruction from octahedral to tetrahedral Ga cations, which generates surface Lewis acid sites. Surface basic sites are insufficient for CO2 adsorption and activation, leading to lower CO2 conversion compared to C3H8. The surface base site density correlates with initial activity, indicating its promotional effect in the beginning of the reaction. The weak reducibility of Ga2O3 and the presence of Ga+ and Ga3+ suggest that the reduction of Ga cations happens at the start of the reaction, benefiting dehydrogenation (DHP) but only during the initial phase. At steady state, the reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle. Methods like nanocasting and urea-based hydrothermal processes have been used to adjust the textural properties of Ga2O3, enhancing its catalytic performance.

            The ideal support should be a zeolite with medium pore size and weak acidity, as seen in HZSM-48 supported catalysts exhibiting higher selectivities to propene than HZSM-5 supported ones at similar propane conversion. Their stability is not as good due to more weak acid sites and unidimensional pore structures. The activity decreases with the increase of Si/Al ratio of catalyst support while the selectivity to propene shows a contrary trend. Ga2O3/HZSM-48 with a Si/Al ratio of 130 has the best propene yield of 22%.

            Ga2O3/TiO2, Ga2O3/Al2O3, and Ga2O3/ZrO2 catalysts exhibit high activity, connected with the abundant medium-strong acid sites on the catalyst surface. Carbon dioxide promotes the catalytic dehydrogenation activity of Ga2O3/TiO2 but suppresses those of Ga2O3/ZrO2 and Ga2O3/Al2O3. Results for the pulse reaction and dehydrogenation reaction with varying partial pressures of CO2 indicate that CO2 plays two roles in the dehydrogenation reaction: a positive role by removing absorbed H2 on the catalyst surface through the reverse watergas shift reaction and a negative role by displacing propane adsorbed on the catalysts basic sites.

            Different Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-) have distinct structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites that impact their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. α-Ga2O3 demonstrates the highest activity due to its efficient promotion of the oxidative dehydrogenation process through specific Lewis acid sites. β-Ga2O3 exhibits better selectivity for the desired product because of its unique surface properties that favor the formation of the product over byproducts. γ-Ga2O3 is noted for its stability under reaction conditions, making it ideal for long-term industrial applications. Lastly, δ-Ga2O3 shows moderate catalytic performance, influenced by its balance between acidity and basicity.

            不同的Ga2O3晶型（α-、β-、γ-和δ-）的结构特征、表面性质（特别是酸性和碱性），以及Lewis酸位点显著影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。β-和γ-Ga2O3晶型在丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性方面优于α-和δ-Ga2O3。表面酸密度按β > γ > δ > α顺序降低，表面碱性也是如此。初始活性依赖于表面酸密度，β-Ga2O3最大化此活性。β-和γ-Ga2O3结构中的四面体Ga离子创造不饱和Ga3+位点作为脱氢反应的Lewis酸位。α-和δ-Ga2O3中的八面体Ga3+与较弱的酸性更协调，允许从八面体到四面体Ga离子的重建，从而产生表面Lewis酸位。表面碱性位点不足以吸附和活化CO2，导致CO2转换效率低于丙烷。表面碱性位点密度与初始活性相关，表明其在反应初期的促进作用。Ga2O3的弱可还原性和存在的Ga+和Ga3+表明，Ga离子的还原发生在反应开始阶段，有利于脱氢(DHP)，但仅在初始阶段有效。在稳态下，反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂离解进行，而没有氧化还原循环。纳米成型和尿素基水热法等方法被用来调整Ga2O3的结构特性，提高其催化性能。

            理想的支撑应该是具有中等孔径和弱酸性的沸石，如HZSM-48支持的催化剂表现出比HZSM-5支持的催化剂更高的丙烯选择性，在相似的丙烷转化率下。它们的稳定性不如后者，原因在于更多的弱酸位点和一维孔结构。随着催化剂支持物Si/Al比例的增加，活动性下降，而丙烯选择性则显示出相反的趋势。具有Si/Al比例为130的Ga2O3/HZSM-48具有最佳的丙烯产率为22%。

            Ga2O3/TiO2、Ga2O3/Al2O3和Ga2O3/ZrO2催化剂表现出高活性，与催化剂表面的丰富中强酸位点相连。二氧化碳促进了Ga2O3/TiO2的催化脱氢活性，但抑制了Ga2O3/ZrO2和Ga2O3/Al2O3的活性。脉冲反应和不同CO2分压下的脱氢反应结果表明，CO2在脱氢反应中扮演着双重角色：通过逆水煤气变换反应去除催化剂表面吸附的H2的正作用，以及通过置换催化剂基本位点上吸附的丙烷的负作用。

            不同的Ga2O3晶型（α-、β-、γ-和δ-）具有不同的结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位点，影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。α-Ga2O3由于其高效促进氧化脱氢过程并通过特定的Lewis酸位点的特性显示出了最高活性。β-Ga2O3表现出对目标产物的更好选择性，因为其独特的表面性质更倾向于形成产品而不是副产品。γ-Ga2O3在反应条件下表现出稳定性，使其适合工业应用的长期使用。最后，δ-Ga2O3表现出适度的催化性能，受其酸性和碱性的平衡影响。

            反应初期和稳态阶段的活性趋势和选择性受到不同Ga2O3晶型的结构特征、表面性质（特别是酸性和碱性）和Lewis酸位点的影响。解释观察到的活性趋势和选择性的机制涉及催化剂表面与反应物之间的特定相互作用。丙烯的选择性受到酸性和碱性之间平衡的影响，更酸性的支持物有利于丙烯的生产，但由于焦炭的形成导致催化剂寿命缩短。这些催化剂的稳定性也受到其孔隙几何形状的影响；具有一维孔结构的催化剂倾向于由于更容易接触到焦炭沉积的表面而具有较低的稳定性。

            在稳态下，反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂离解进行，而不涉及氧化还原循环。已经开发了多种方法来调整Ga2O3的纹理性质，例如使用介孔碳作为硬模板的纳米成型技术和基于尿素的水热法使用聚乙二醇作为软模板剂，以增强其催化性能。
