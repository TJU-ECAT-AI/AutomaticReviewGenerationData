        The in-depth analysis of Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-) reveals their pivotal role in catalytic performance for CO2-ODHP reactions, primarily influenced by their structural characteristics, surface properties (including acidity and basicity), and Lewis acid sites. β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs stand out for their superior performance in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity. Surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, paralleling the surface basicity. The initial activity of β-Ga2O3 is maximized due to its efficient promotion of the oxidative dehydrogenation process through specific Lewis acid sites. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, which create coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites, functioning as Lewis acid sites for dehydrogenation reactions. Octahedral Ga3+ in α- and δ-Ga2O3, though more coordinated with weaker acidity, allows for the potential reconstruction from octahedral to tetrahedral Ga cations, generating additional surface Lewis acid sites. Surface basic sites, while insufficient for CO2 adsorption and activation, contribute to lower CO2 conversion compared to C3H8. The surface base site density correlates with initial activity, highlighting its promotional effect in the reaction's initial stages. The weak reducibility of Ga2O3 and the presence of Ga+ and Ga3+ suggest that the reduction of Ga cations occurs primarily at the reaction's onset, benefiting dehydrogenation (DHP) but only during the initial phase. At steady state, the reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle. Various methods, such as nanocasting and urea-based hydrothermal processes, have been employed to adjust the textural properties of Ga2O3, optimizing its catalytic performance.

        This investigation underscores the importance of structurally tailored Ga2O3 polymorphs in achieving optimized catalytic activity and selectivity for CO2-ODHP reactions. The ability to fine-tune these polymorphs through textural adjustments holds promise for enhancing their performance in industrial applications, particularly in the realm of sustainable energy production and waste management.

        Looking ahead, future research should focus on exploring the synergistic effects of combining different Ga2O3 polymorphs with optimized supports and modifying their surface chemistry to further improve catalytic efficiency and longevity. Potential areas for innovation include the development of novel synthesis routes that allow for precise control over the polymorph composition and the integration of Ga2O3 with other catalytically active materials to create hybrid catalysts with enhanced activity and selectivity. Additionally, understanding the dynamic behavior of these catalysts under reaction conditions, including the mechanisms governing their activity decline and regeneration, could lead to the creation of more durable and efficient catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions.

        In summary, the detailed exploration of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis provides a robust foundation for advancing the field of green chemistry and sustainable technology. By leveraging the insights gained from this research, scientists and engineers can develop more effective and environmentally friendly solutions for converting CO2 into valuable chemicals and fuels, contributing to the global effort in addressing climate change and promoting sustainable energy practices.

        对不同 Ga2O3 多晶型（α-、β-、γ- 和 δ-）的深入分析揭示了它们在 CO2-ODHP 反应中的催化性能的关键作用，主要受到其结构特征、表面性质（包括酸性和碱性）、以及Lewis 酸位点的影响。β- 和 γ-Ga2O3 多晶型在 C3H8 转化率和 C3H6 选择性方面表现出卓越性能。表面酸密度按 β > γ > δ > α 的顺序递减，与表面碱性一致。β-Ga2O3 的初始活性得到最大化，这是由于其通过特定的 Lewis 酸位点高效促进氧化脱氢过程。β- 和 γ-Ga2O3 中的四面体 Ga 离子创造了不饱和 Ga3+ 位点，作为脱氢反应中的 Lewis 酸位点。α- 和 δ-Ga2O3 中的八面体 Ga3+，虽然与较弱的酸性更协调，但允许从八面体向四面体 Ga 离子的潜在重构，产生额外的表面 Lewis 酸位点。表面碱性位点虽然不足以吸附和活化 CO2，导致 CO2 转化率低于 C3H8。表面碱性位点密度与初始活性相关，表明它在反应初期的促进效果。Ga2O3 的弱还原性以及存在 Ga+ 和 Ga3+ 表明，在反应开始时 Ga 离子的还原作用主要发生，有利于脱氢（DHP），仅在初始阶段有效。稳定状态下的反应通过 Ga 氧化物上的异裂进行，而无需循环还原。已经开发出诸如纳米铸造和基于尿素的水热法等方法来调整 Ga2O3 的纹理性质，优化其催化性能。

        这一研究强调了结构上定制的 Ga2O3 多晶型在实现 CO2-ODHP 反应优化催化活性和选择性方面的关键作用。通过精确控制这些多晶型的方法来调整纹理，有望增强它们在工业应用中的性能，特别是对于可持续能源生产和废物管理而言。

        朝前看，未来的研究应专注于探索不同 Ga2O3 多晶型与优化支撑材料的协同效应，并修改其表面化学，进一步提高催化效率和持久性。创新方向可能包括开发新型合成途径，以精确控制多晶型组成，并将 Ga2O3 与其他具有催化活性的材料集成，形成具有增强活性和选择性的复合催化剂。此外，深入了解这些催化剂在反应条件下的动态行为，包括活动下降和再生机制的机制，可以导致创建更耐用且高效的 CO2-ODHP 反应催化剂。

        总之，对 Ga2O3 多晶型在 CO2-ODHP 催化剂领域的深入探索为推进绿色化学和可持续技术提供了坚实的基础。通过利用从这项研究中获得的见解，科学家和工程师可以发展更有效的和环境友好的解决方案，用于将 CO2 转换成有价值的化学品和燃料，从而促进全球应对气候变化的努力和推动可持续能源实践。
