        The structural characteristics, surface properties (particularly acidity and basicity), and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-) significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity. Surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, so does the surface basicity. The initial activity depends on the surface acid density, with β-Ga2O3 maximizing this activity. Tetrahedral Ga cations in β- and γ-Ga2O3 structures create coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites, acting as Lewis acid sites for dehydrogenation reactions. Octahedral Ga3+ in α- and δ-Ga2O3 are less coordinated with weaker acidity, allowing for the potential reconstruction from octahedral to tetrahedral Ga cations, which generates surface Lewis acid sites. Surface basic sites are insufficient for CO2 adsorption and activation, leading to lower CO2 conversion compared to C3H8. The surface base site density correlates with initial activity, indicating its promotional effect in the beginning of the reaction. The weak reducibility of Ga2O3 and the presence of Ga+ and Ga3+ suggest that the reduction of Ga cations happens at the start of the reaction, benefiting dehydrogenation (DHP) but only during the initial phase. At steady state, the reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle. Methods like nanocasting and urea-based hydrothermal processes have been used to adjust the textural properties of Ga2O3, enhancing its catalytic performance.

        For the DHP reaction, various methods have been developed to tune the textural properties of Ga2O3, such as the nanocasting technique using mesoporous carbon as a hard template (131) and the urea-based hydrothermal method using polyethylene glycol as a soft template agent. These adjustments enable the optimization of Ga2O3’s surface properties, leading to improved catalytic performance.

        An integrative understanding and prospective outlook suggests that future research might focus on exploring the synergistic effects of combining different Ga2O3 polymorphs or incorporating them with other catalysts to achieve enhanced catalytic activity and selectivity. The development of new synthesis techniques could also lead to the creation of novel Ga2O3-based catalysts with tailored properties for optimized CO2-ODHP reactions. Moreover, investigating the dynamic behavior of Ga2O3 catalysts under various reaction conditions and their long-term stability could provide insights into improving the efficiency and sustainability of the process.

        在二氧化碳氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）反应中，不同镓氧化三价体（α-, β-, γ-, δ-）的结构特征、表面性质（特别是酸性和碱性），以及路易斯酸位点显著影响其催化性能。β- 和 γ-Ga2O3 多晶相在丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性方面表现出色。表面酸密度按 β > γ > δ > α 的顺序降低，表面碱性也遵循相同规律。初始活性取决于表面酸密度，β-Ga2O3 最大化了这一活性。β- 和 γ-Ga2O3 结构中的四面体镓离子产生了未配位的 Ga3+ 离子位点，作为脱氢反应的路易斯酸位点。α- 和 δ-Ga2O3 中的八面体 Ga3+ 由于较弱的酸性而与较少的配位，允许潜在地从八面体到四面体的 Ga 离子重构，从而在表面上产生路易斯酸位点。表面碱性位点不足以吸附和激活 CO2，导致 CO2 转化率低于丙烷。表面基团密度与初始活性相关，表明它在反应初期具有促进作用。镓的弱可还原性和存在 Ga+ 和 Ga3+ 表明镓离子的还原发生在反应开始阶段，对脱氢（DHP）有益，但仅在反应初期。

        对 DHP 反应而言，已开发了多种方法来调整 Ga2O3 的结构特性，如使用介孔碳作为硬模板的纳米成型技术（131）和基于尿素的水热法使用聚乙二醇作为软模板剂的方法。这些调整使 Ga2O3 的表面性质得到优化，提高了其催化性能。

        集成理解与前瞻展望指出，未来研究可能专注于探索结合不同 Ga2O3 多晶相或将其与其他催化剂结合以实现增强的催化活性和选择性的策略。新型合成技术的发展也可能导致新型 Ga2O3 基催化剂的创造，具有为优化 CO2-ODHP 反应定制的性质。此外，研究 Ga2O3 催化剂在各种反应条件下的动态行为及其长期稳定性，可以提供提高过程效率和可持续性的见解。
