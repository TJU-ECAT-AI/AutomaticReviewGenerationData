[I] "Sulphur/ Halogen Promoted Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Hydrocarbons"
</quotes>
<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Sulphur and halogens, particularly bromine and chlorine, can be used as promoters for the oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons in place of iodine or iodine/halogen mixtures. High product yields are achievable with the use of suitable catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 硫和卤素，特别是溴和氯，可以作为氧化脱氢反应中氢化物的促进剂来替代碘或碘/卤素混合物。在使用合适的催化剂的情况下，可以获得高产品产率。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

4. The replacement of iodine or bromine with sulfur in a halogen-promoted system could have significant economic benefits, especially if there are high losses of promoters due to the formation of undesirable by-products. Even small quantities of sulfur can lead to considerable reductions in promoter costs.
