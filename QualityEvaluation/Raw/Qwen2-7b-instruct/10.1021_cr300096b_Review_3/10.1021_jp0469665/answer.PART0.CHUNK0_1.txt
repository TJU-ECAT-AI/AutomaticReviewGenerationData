<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Our group has synthesized [Au](Pd) core/shell structures by selectively reducing PdCl4- onto Au55 seeds using H2.
2. Representative HRTEM micrographs and particle-size distributions for various bimetallic core/shell structures have been provided.
3. EDS experiments have confirmed that the core/shell structures are indeed bimetallic, with compositions reflecting the Pd:Au ratio used during synthesis.
4. The hydrogenation of allyl alcohol in water is significantly improved when using these Pd-rich core/shell bimetallic DENs compared to pure Pd DENs.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 我们的团队最近通过将H2选择性地还原至G6-Q116(Au55)种子上的PdCl42-，合成了[Au](Pd)核壳结构。
2. 提供了G6-Q116(Au55)、G6-Q116[Au55](Pd95)和G6-Q116[Au55](Pd455)等不同双金属核壳结构的代表性HRTEM显微图和颗粒尺寸分布。
3. EDS实验确认了所有[Au](Pd)核壳纳米粒子都是双金属的，其组成反映了合成时使用的Pd：Au比率。
4. 相对于仅含Pd的单金属DENs，使用这些富含Pd的核壳双金属DENs进行异丙醇氢化反应在水中表现出显著增强的效果。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
