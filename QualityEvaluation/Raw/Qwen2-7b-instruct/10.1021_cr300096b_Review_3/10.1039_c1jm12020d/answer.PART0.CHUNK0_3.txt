<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The most commonly utilized synthesis methods for supported bimetallic catalysts are impregnation, co-precipitation, coprecipitation, and sol-gel processes. These methods offer advantages in terms of control over the composition and size of the catalyst particles, making them widely applied in the production of bimetallic catalysts due to their simplicity and efficiency. Impregnation involves soaking the support material in a solution containing metal precursors. Co-precipitation combines the precipitation of metal salts to form the desired catalyst. Coprecipitation involves the simultaneous precipitation of two or more metals from a single solution. Sol-gel processes utilize a precursor solution that undergoes a gelation step to form the catalyst. Each method has its own advantages and limitations, which affect the properties of the resulting catalyst.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
常用的制备支持型双金属催化剂的方法包括浸渍、共沉淀、共混沉淀和溶胶凝胶法。这些方法在控制催化剂颗粒的组成和大小方面具有优势，因此被广泛应用于双金属催化剂的生产，因其简便性和高效性而受到青睐。浸渍法是将支撑材料浸泡在含金属前体的溶液中。共沉淀是将金属盐的沉淀结合起来形成所需的催化剂。共混沉淀是在单一溶液中同时沉淀两种或多种金属。溶胶凝胶法利用一种前体溶液，在经过凝胶化步骤后形成催化剂。每种方法都有其自身的优点和限制，这影响了最终催化剂的性质。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
