<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Among various hydrogen storage materials, ammonia borane (NH3BH3, AB) has attracted significant interest due to its high hydrogen content of 19.6 wt%.

2. Effective hydrogen release is crucial for the utilization of hydrogen energy.

3. The catalysts are synthesized using a polyol process with oleic acid as a surfactant.

4. The prepared catalysts demonstrate high activity for ammonia borane hydrolysis.

5. The method employed for the synthesis of the catalysts differs from the preparation of PtxNi1x alloys in a previous study.

6. Within the Ni1x@Ptx series, Ni0.33@Pt0.67/C NPs exhibit the highest catalytic performance, with a hydrogen release rate surpassing 5000 mL min1 g1 at 25°C, showcasing high efficiency and a low activation energy.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在各种氢储存材料中，氨硼烷（NH3BH3，AB）因其高达19.6 wt%的氢含量而受到广泛关注。

2. 有效的氢释放对于氢能源的应用至关重要。

3. 催化剂通过聚醇合成过程制备，使用油酸作为表面活性剂。

4. 制备的催化剂表现出对氨硼烷水解的高活性。

5. 所使用的合成方法与之前关于PtxNi1x合金合成的研究不同。

6. 在Ni1x@Ptx系列中，Ni0.33@Pt0.67/C NPs展现出最高的催化性能，其在25°C下的氢释放速率超过5000 mL min1 g1，表现出高效率和低活化能。
