<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The most commonly utilized synthesis methods for supported bimetallic catalysts include chemical reduction, hydrolysis, vacuum deposition, and colloidal methods. Among these, the use of microemulsion provides a convenient control of size and composition. This method allows for the accurate determination of the composition of the mixed metal nanoparticles, leading to improved control over the catalyst's properties.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
