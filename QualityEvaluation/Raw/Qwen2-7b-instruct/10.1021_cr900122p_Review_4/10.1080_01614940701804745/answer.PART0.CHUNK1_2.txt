<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Gallium plays a crucial role in enhancing the dehydrogenation function of HZSM-5 catalysts. Industrially, gallium-containing formulations are preferred over zinc due to zinc's high volatility. Among various metals added to HZSM-5 to improve dehydrogenation, gallium and zinc demonstrate the most effective performance.
