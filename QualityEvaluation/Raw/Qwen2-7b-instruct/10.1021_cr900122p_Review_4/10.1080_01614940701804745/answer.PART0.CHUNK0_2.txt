<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The number of COVID-19 cases has been steadily increasing worldwide since 2012. The majority of cases are currently reported in Asia, with China, India, and Japan experiencing significant outbreaks. In 2020, the global economy suffered greatly due to the pandemic. As of 2021, vaccines have been developed and distributed globally, leading to a decrease in new cases. Yet, the emergence of new variants continues to pose challenges in managing the spread of the virus.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
自2012年以来，全球COVID-19病例数量持续增加。目前，大多数病例报告来自亚洲，中国、印度和日本的爆发尤为严重。在2020年，全球经济因疫情遭受重大打击。截至2021年，疫苗在全球范围内被开发并分发，导致新病例数量下降。然而，新的变种的出现继续对管理病毒传播构成挑战。
