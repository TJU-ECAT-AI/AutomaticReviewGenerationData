<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The key deactivation pathways for supported alkene polymerization and metathesis catalysts involve intramolecular C-H bond activation.
2. For lanthanide/actinide-based catalysts, the formation of π-allyl surface complexes plays a crucial role.
3. Silica-supported Re-based catalysts undergo β-H transfer as a significant deactivation mechanism.
4. The formation of π-allyl surface complexes leads to the formation of a strained allylic species.
5. β-H transfer results in the loss of hydrogen from the catalyst surface, leading to deactivation.
6. These deactivation pathways can significantly impact the performance and longevity of the catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 支持的烯烃聚合和裂解催化剂的活性主要由分子内C-H键活化途径决定。
2. 对于镧系/锕系基催化剂，表面形成π-亚基复合物起着关键作用。
3. 硅支持的铼基催化剂通过β-H转移作为显著的失活机制。
4. 形成π-亚基表面复合物导致形成受压的亚基物种。
5. β-H转移导致催化剂表面氢的丢失，引发失活。
6. 这些失活途径可能严重影响催化剂的性能和寿命。
