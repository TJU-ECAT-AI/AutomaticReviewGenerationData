<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This review paper discusses the use of acid and bifunctional zeolite catalysts in the transformation of C2C4 alkanes into more valuable products. It covers various transformations such as n-butane isomerization, C2C4 alkane aromatization, isobutanebutene alkylation, and benzene alkylation with ethane or propane.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
