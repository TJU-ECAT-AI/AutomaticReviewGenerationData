[2] "Reduced Ga is present in highly dispersed form without Ga nearest neighbors, probably as a monomeric hydride species coordinated to basic oxygens within zeolite channels."

The primary challenges in elucidating Ga speciation in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts under propane dehydrogenation (PDH) conditions include the dynamic nature of Ga species, which undergo reduction and oxidation under reaction conditions. In situ or operando characterization techniques are crucial because they allow for real-time monitoring of the catalyst's behavior during PDH, providing insights into the chemical form of Ga species and their role in catalysis. These methods overcome the limitations of traditional pre- and post-reaction characterization, which may not accurately reflect the catalyst's active state under operational conditions.
