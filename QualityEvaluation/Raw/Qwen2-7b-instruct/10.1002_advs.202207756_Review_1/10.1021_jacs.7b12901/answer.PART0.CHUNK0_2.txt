<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The primary challenge in elucidating Ga speciation in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts under propane dehydrogenation (PDH) conditions is the complexity of the Ga species present within the catalyst structure. In situ or operando characterization techniques have contributed to overcoming these challenges by providing real-time insights into the dynamic behavior of Ga species during PDH reactions. These techniques allow researchers to monitor changes in the catalyst's structure and activity, advancing our understanding of the structure-activity relationships in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
