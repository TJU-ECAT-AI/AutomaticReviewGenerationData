<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The role of CO2 in the soft oxidative dehydrogenation of propane over ceria-based catalysts was investigated. Reducibility and support materials of iron oxide-based catalysts have a significant impact on their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. These factors provide insights into the proposed reaction mechanism and offer guidance for optimizing catalytic systems for this process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
