<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Ordered mesoporous Ce1-xZrxO2 solid solutions were synthesized with crystalline walls. The reducibility of Fe oxide-based catalysts was studied under different conditions. Support materials had a significant impact on the performance of these catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions. Insights into the proposed reaction mechanism were obtained through detailed analysis of the catalytic systems. Optimization strategies for enhancing the catalytic systems in the CO2-ODHP process were discussed based on the experimental findings.
