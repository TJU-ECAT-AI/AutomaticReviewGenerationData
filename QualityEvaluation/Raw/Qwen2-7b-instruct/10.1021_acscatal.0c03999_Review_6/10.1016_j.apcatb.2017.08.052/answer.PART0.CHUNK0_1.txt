The reducibility of iron oxides and the type of support materials significantly influence the performance of catalysts used in CO2-ODHP reactions. These factors play a crucial role in the formation of active species on the catalyst surface, which facilitates the conversion of propane to propylene. Optimizing these aspects can lead to more efficient and selective catalytic systems for this process.
