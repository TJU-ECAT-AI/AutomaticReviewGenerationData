<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. These factors determine the catalyst's activity and selectivity, which are crucial for the efficient conversion of propane to propylene. Insights into the proposed reaction mechanism indicate that Fe oxide-based catalysts enable the oxidative dehydrogenation process by effectively utilizing CO2. For optimizing catalytic systems, one must carefully choose the reducibility and support materials to ensure high efficiency and selectivity.
