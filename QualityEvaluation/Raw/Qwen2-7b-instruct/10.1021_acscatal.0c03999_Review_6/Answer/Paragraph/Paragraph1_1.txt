        The performance, reducibility, and reaction mechanism of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions are significantly influenced by the type of support materials used. Reducibility plays a crucial role in determining the reactivity of the catalysts towards CO2 and propane, impacting the oxidation process. Support materials, such as carbon and metal oxides, stabilize the catalyst structure and enhance its activity, offering valuable insights into the proposed reaction mechanism and guiding the optimization of catalytic systems for this process.

        Insights from these studies suggest that optimizing the reducibility and carefully selecting appropriate support materials could lead to more efficient catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions. The presence of CO2 promotes the dehydrogenation of propane, enhancing the yield of propylene and suppressing catalyst deactivation, especially on silica-supported catalysts. Pulse reaction studies further indicate that CO2 accelerates the dehydrogenation rate over Fe oxide catalysts, suggesting its beneficial role in the reaction mechanism.

        The use of ceria as a support material enhances the stability and selectivity of the catalysts, indicating that both the reducibility and the type of support material are crucial for optimizing catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP processes. The DFT-calculated Gibbs free energy profile suggests that propane dehydrogenation in the presence of CO2 can occur through both direct oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by reverse water gas shift (RWGS) reaction. The activation of the secondary CAH bond of propane is identified as the rate-determining step in both pathways.

        The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts significantly impact their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Understanding these factors offers insights into the reaction mechanism and guides the optimization of catalytic systems for this process.

        Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能、可还原性和反应机制受所使用的支持材料类型的影响。可还原性在决定催化剂对CO2和丙烷的氧化过程的活性方面起着关键作用。碳和金属氧化物等支持材料稳定催化剂结构并增强其活性，为理解拟议的反应机制提供了有价值的信息，并指导了此过程催化系统的优化。

        这些研究的见解表明，通过优化可还原性和精心选择合适的支持材料可以提高CO2-ODHP反应中催化剂的效率。CO2促进了丙烷的脱氢，提高了丙烯的产率并抑制了催化剂失活，特别是在硅酸盐支持催化剂上。脉冲反应研究表明，CO2加速了Fe氧化物催化剂的脱氢速率，表明其在反应机制中的有益作用。

        使用铈作为支持材料增强了催化剂的稳定性和选择性，表明可还原性和支持材料类型对于优化CO2-ODHP过程的催化系统都是至关重要的。DFT计算的吉布斯自由能图表明，在CO2存在的条件下，丙烷脱氢可以通过直接氧化脱氢（ODH）和非氧化脱氢后由逆水煤气转换（RWGS）反应进行。丙烷中次级CAH键的激活被识别为两个途径中的决定性步骤。

        Fe氧化物基催化剂的可还原性和支持材料显著影响CO2-ODHP反应的性能。理解这些因素为反应机制提供洞察，并指导催化系统的优化。
