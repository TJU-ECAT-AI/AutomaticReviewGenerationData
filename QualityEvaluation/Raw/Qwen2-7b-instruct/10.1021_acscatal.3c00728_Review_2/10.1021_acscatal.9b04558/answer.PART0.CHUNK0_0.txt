<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction are developed through recent research efforts. These sites offer higher active site density compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, leading to improved catalytic performance due to increased surface area for reactant adsorption and product desorption.
2. The unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites attract significant research interest because they enable more efficient use of catalysts and potentially reduce energy consumption during the ODHP process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 对于丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）反应，硼基催化剂中2-D分散活性位点的发展是近期研究努力的重点。这些2-D分散活性位点比线性分散边缘位点提供了更高的活性位点密度。这导致了增强的催化性能，因为增加了反应物吸附和产物脱附的表面积。
2. 2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征之所以引起大量研究兴趣，是因为它们能够更有效地利用催化剂，并可能降低ODHP过程的能量消耗。
