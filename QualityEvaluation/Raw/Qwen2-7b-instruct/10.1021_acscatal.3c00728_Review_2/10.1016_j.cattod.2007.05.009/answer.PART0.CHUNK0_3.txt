<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Lemonidou and co-workers' findings indicate that the ability of vanadium to activate the CH bond in propane diminishes with increasing density of vanadium oxide on titania and zirconia supports. 
2. The study reveals that the degree of reduction of vanadium during steady-state oxidative dehydrogenation is less compared to stoichiometric reduction of vanadium pentoxide to vanadium tetravalent or trivalent states. 
3. Mixed structures like VOx-CrOx and VOx-MoOx, when incorporated into alumina supports, result in a higher rate and selectivity of propane oxidative dehydrogenation compared to pure alumina supports with similar vanadium oxide density. 
4. Additives like phosphorus (P), potassium (K), calcium (Ca), nickel (Ni), chromium (Cr), niobium (Nb), magnesium (Mg), gold (Au), zinc (Zn), and molybdenum (Mo), when added in proportion to vanadium, modify the acid-basic and redox properties of supported vanadium oxide catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. Lemonidou等人的研究结果表明，在二氧化钛和氧化锆支持的钒氧化物上，随着钒氧化物密度的增加，钒激活丙烷中的CH键的能力减弱。
2. 研究发现，在稳定态氧化脱氢过程中，钒的还原程度远低于五价钒氧化为四价或三价的完全还原。
3. 当将VOx-CrOx和VOx-MoOx等二元分散结构引入纯氧化铝支持材料中时，可以得到比相同表面密度下纯氧化铝上存在的VOx区域更高的丙烷氧化脱氢速率和选择性。  
4. 添加剂如磷（P）、钾（K）、钙（Ca）、镍（Ni）、铬（Cr）、铌（Nb）、镁（Mg）、金（Au）、锌（Zn）和钼（Mo），以与钒的比例添加，可以改变支持的钒氧化物催化剂的酸碱性和还原性。
