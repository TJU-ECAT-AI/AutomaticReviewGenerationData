This research focuses on developing 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction. These 2-D dispersed active sites provide higher active site density than linearly dispersed edge sites, leading to improved catalytic performance.
