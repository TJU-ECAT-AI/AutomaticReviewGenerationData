<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction are developed by creating a high density of active sites on a two-dimensional material. These sites offer higher active site density, enhanced surface area, and improved accessibility to reactants compared to linearly dispersed edge sites.
2. The unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites have attracted significant research interest because they have the potential to significantly boost catalytic performance in ODHP reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在硼基催化剂中，通过在二维材料上创建高密度活性位点来开发氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）反应。这些二维位点与线性分散边缘位点相比，提供了更高的活性位点密度、增强的表面积和对反应物的改善可达性。
2. 由于它们有潜力显著提高ODHP反应的催化性能，因此2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量的研究兴趣。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
