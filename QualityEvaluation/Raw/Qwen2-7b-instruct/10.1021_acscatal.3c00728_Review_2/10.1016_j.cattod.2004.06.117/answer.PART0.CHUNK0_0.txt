<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts are developed through creating a two-dimensional structure with even distribution of active sites on the surface.
2. Compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, these 2-D sites provide a higher density of active sites, which results in improved catalytic performance.
3. The distinctive structural characteristics of 2-D dispersed sites draw substantial research attention because of their potential to enhance reactivity and selectivity in chemical reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
