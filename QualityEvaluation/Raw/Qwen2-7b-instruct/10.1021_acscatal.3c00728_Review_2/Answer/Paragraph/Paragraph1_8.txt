Synthesized comprehension stemming from references [10.1016/S0167-2991(98)80509-0] and [10.1016/0926-860X(95)00273-1]. The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction involves creating a two-dimensional structure with active sites spread throughout the material, offering several advantages over linearly dispersed edge sites. Firstly, they significantly increase the active site density, leading to enhanced catalytic performance due to more direct contact with reactants. Secondly, the unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites attract significant research interest because they enable better control over the reaction pathways, potentially leading to improved selectivity and efficiency in the ODHP process. The antioxidant ability of materials like silicon boride ensures the stability of the reaction, contributing to its effectiveness in the ODHP process.

        The recent discovery that antioxidant and chemically inert hexagonal boron nitride (h-BN) is extremely active for the ODH process of ethane and propane opens up a new research direction in selective cleavage of CH bond of alkanes by the metal-free catalyst. It consequently expands the fundamental understanding of the industrially important ODH process. Our spectroscopic investigation suggested that the BOH could be the active sites which selectively broke the CH bond but simultaneously shut off the pathway of propylene overoxidation towards CO2. In the following up study, Su et al. proposed that and the BO(H) formed at the edges of h-BN were the active sites for ethane ODH reaction. Recently [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014], we showed that boron nitride can also oxidize methane to valuable products. Moreover, Xie and coworkers demonstrated that carbon-doped BN catalyst showed good activity in ODH of ethylbenzene to styrene. Anyhow, people in different groups identified with the idea that the boron species could be truly indispensable element for high ODH activity. Silicon boride (SiB3-6), a metal-free material enriching in boron sites, exhibits good oxidation resistance property at high temperature and could be as a potential catalyst for ODH of light alkanes. Study on the metal-free silicon boride may make a better understanding on the catalytic origin of the boron species, and further initiate the exploration of other new boron-based catalysts. Based on spectra and dynamics analysis, the BOH groups generated from the BO species with the assistance of steam might be catalytic activity sites. This work experimentally supports that the catalytic origin of the boron species from the metal-free catalyst initiate the oxidative dehydrogenation of light alkanes.

        The integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on optimizing the dispersion of active sites, enhancing the selectivity in the ODHP reaction, and exploring the role of boron in catalyst design. The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts represents a significant step forward in the field of chemical catalysis, offering promising avenues for the production of valuable chemical intermediates with reduced environmental impact.

        基于\[10.1016/j.jmmm.2003.12.918\]参考文献的深度分析。从\[10.1016/S0167-2991(98)80509-0\]和\[10.1016/0926-860X(95)00273-1\]的参考文献中获得的综合理解。

        在氧化脱氢化丙烷（ODHP）反应中，2-D分散活性位点在硼基催化剂中的发展涉及在材料中形成二维结构，其中活性位点分布在整个材料中，这为线性分散边缘位点提供了一些优势。首先，它们显著增加了活性位点密度，从而提高了催化剂性能，由于与反应物的直接接触更多。其次，2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量研究兴趣，因为它们能够更好地控制反应路径，可能促进ODHP过程的选择性和效率提高。抗氧化能力如硅硼化物确保了反应的稳定性，对ODHP过程的有效性做出了贡献。

        最近发现抗氧化和化学惰性的六方硼氮化物（h-BN）对于乙烯和丙烷的ODH过程具有极高的活性，这开辟了在无金属催化剂下选择性裂解烷烃中CH键的新研究方向。它扩展了工业上重要ODH过程的基础理解。我们的光谱学调查表明，BOH可能是选择性切断CH键但同时关闭丙烯过氧化向CO2途径的活性位点。在后续研究中，苏等人提出，和h-BN边缘形成的BO(H)是乙烷ODH反应的活性位点。最近\[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]，我们展示了硼氮可以氧化甲烷以产生有价值的产品。此外，谢及其同事证明了碳掺杂BN催化剂在乙苯到苯乙烯的ODH过程中表现出良好的活性。无论如何，不同小组都认同硼元素是高ODH活性不可或缺的元素。硅硼化物（SiB3-6），一种富含硼位点的无金属材料，显示出在高温下的良好氧化耐久性，并可作为轻烷烃的潜在ODH催化剂。对无金属硅硼化物的研究可能有助于更深入地理解硼的催化起源，并进一步启动对其他新的硼基催化剂的探索。基于光谱和动力学分析，从BO物种在蒸汽的帮助下生成的BOH组分可能是催化活性位点。这项工作实验证明了金属自由催化剂中硼物种的催化起源启动了轻烷烃的氧化脱氢化过程。

        集成理解与未来展望：考虑到文件中讨论的进步和发现，存在探索新兴领域和创新方法的机会。未来的研究努力可能集中在优化活性位点的分散、增强ODHP反应的选择性以及探索硼在催化剂设计中的作用。2-D分散活性位点在硼基催化剂中的发展代表了化学催化领域的重要进展，为有价值的化学中间体的生产提供了有前景的途径，同时减少了环境影响。
