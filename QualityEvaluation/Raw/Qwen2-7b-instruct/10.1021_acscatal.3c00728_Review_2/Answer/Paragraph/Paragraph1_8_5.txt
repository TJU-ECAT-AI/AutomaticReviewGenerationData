        The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction involves creating a two-dimensional structure where the active sites are distributed throughout the material. These sites offer several advantages over linearly dispersed edge sites. Firstly, they significantly increase the active site density, leading to enhanced catalytic performance due to more direct contact with reactants. Secondly, the unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites attract significant research interest because they enable better control over the reaction pathways, potentially leading to improved selectivity and efficiency in the ODHP process.

        Compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, 2-D dispersed sites offer a higher density of active sites, potentially enhancing the catalytic performance. The unique structural features of these 2-D dispersed sites attract significant research interest due to their potential for achieving high selectivity in the ODHP reaction, particularly in the production of propylene, with minimal formation of CO2. The antioxidant ability of the silicon boride catalyst ensures the stability of the reaction, contributing to its effectiveness in the ODHP process.

        The recent discovery that antioxidant and chemically inert hexagonal boron nitride (h-BN) is extremely active for the ODH process of ethane and propane opens up a new research direction in selective cleavage of CH bond of alkanes by the metal-free catalyst. It consequently expands the fundamental understanding of the industrially important ODH process. Our spectroscopic investigation suggested that the BOH could be the active sites which selectively broke the CH bond but simultaneously shut off the pathway of propylene overoxidation towards CO2. In the following up study, Su et al. proposed that and the BO(H) formed at the edges of h-BN were the active sites for ethane ODH reaction.

        The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction involves the formation of boron oxide (BO) species on the surface of the catalyst. These BO species can generate borohydride (BOH) groups with the assistance of steam, which are believed to be the active sites responsible for the cleavage of the carbon-hydrogen (CH) bond in propane. The antioxidant ability of the silicon boride catalyst ensures the stability of the reaction, contributing to its effectiveness in the ODHP process.

        The unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites have attracted significant research interest due to their potential for enhancing efficiency and selectivity in the ODHP process, which is crucial for the production of valuable chemical intermediates. The study on metal-free silicon boride may make a better understanding on the catalytic origin of the boron species, and further initiate the exploration of other new boron-based catalysts.

        Based on spectra and dynamics analysis, the BOH groups generated from the BO species with the assistance of steam might be catalytic activity sites. This work experimentally supports that the catalytic origin of the boron species from the metal-free catalyst initiate the oxidative dehydrogenation of light alkanes.

        在氧化脱氢反应（ODHP）中，硼基催化剂的二维分散活性位点的发展涉及在材料中创建二维结构，其中活性位点分布在整个材料中。这些位点相对于线性分散边缘位点提供了几个优势。首先，它们显著增加了活性位点密度，从而提高了催化性能，主要是通过与反应物的直接接触增加。其次，2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量研究兴趣，因为它们能够更好地控制反应途径，可能提高选择性和效率ODHP过程。

        相比于线性分散边缘位点，2-D分散位点提供了更高的活性位点密度，有可能增强催化性能。2-D分散位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量研究兴趣，因其潜力在ODHP反应中实现高选择性，特别是生产丙烯，同时最小化二氧化碳的形成。硅硼化合物的抗氧化能力确保了反应的稳定性，对ODHP过程的有效性做出了贡献。

        最近发现抗氧化剂和化学惰性的六方硼氮化物（h-BN）对于乙烷和丙烷的ODH过程极为活跃，开辟了新的研究方向，在无金属催化剂中选择性裂解烷烃中的CH键。它因此扩展了工业上重要ODHP过程的基本理解。我们的光谱学调查表明，BOH可能是选择性地断裂CH键但同时关闭丙烯过氧化向CO2路径的活性位点。在后续研究中，苏等人提出了和h-BN边缘形成的BO(H)是乙烷ODH反应的活性位点。

        在氧化脱氢反应（ODHP）中，硼基催化剂表面的硼氧化物（BO）物种的形成涉及到催化作用。这些BO物种可以通过蒸汽辅助产生硼氢化物（BOH）组分，被认为是负责丙烷中碳-氢（CH）键断裂的活性位点。硅硼化合物的抗氧化能力确保了反应的稳定性，对ODHP过程的有效性做出了贡献。

        2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量研究兴趣，因为它们有可能增强效率和选择性ODHP过程，这是生产有价值化学中间体的关键。对无金属硅硼化合物的研究可能使我们更好地理解硼物种的催化起源，并进一步推动其他新硼基催化剂的探索。

        根据光谱和动力学分析，BOH组分由BO物种在蒸汽辅助下生成，可能成为催化活性位点。这项工作实验证明，金属自由催化剂中的硼物种的催化起源启动了轻烷烃的氧化脱氢过程。
