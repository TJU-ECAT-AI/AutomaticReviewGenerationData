<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction are developed through a process that exploits their unique two-dimensional structure, aiming to enhance catalytic performance. These sites offer a higher active site density compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, primarily due to their ability to efficiently utilize surface area.
2. The specific advantages of 2-D dispersed active sites include improved selectivity and activity, as well as enhanced stability. These characteristics are essential for the ODHP reaction, contributing to better interaction between the reactants and the catalyst, thus leading to superior catalytic performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在硼基催化剂中，通过利用其独特的二维结构开发的氧化脱氢化丙烷（ODHP）反应中的2-D分散活性位点，旨在增强催化性能。这些位点相较于线性分散边缘位点提供了更高的活性位点密度，主要原因是它们能够有效利用表面积。
2. 2-D分散活性位点的具体优势包括提高选择性和活性，以及增强稳定性。这些特性对于ODHP反应至关重要，有助于改善反应物与催化剂之间的相互作用，从而实现更优的催化性能。
