<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction are developed through methods that maximize the surface area. These sites provide an advantage over linearly dispersed edge sites because they possess a higher density of active sites, resulting in improved catalytic performance. The distinctive structural characteristics of 2-D sites have garnered significant attention due to their ability to enhance reaction efficiency and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
