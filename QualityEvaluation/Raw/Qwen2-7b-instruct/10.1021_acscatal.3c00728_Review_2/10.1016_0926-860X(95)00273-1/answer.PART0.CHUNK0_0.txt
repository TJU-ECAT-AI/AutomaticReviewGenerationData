<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction are developed through creating a two-dimensional structure that enables a higher density of active sites than traditional linearly dispersed edge sites.
2. These 2-D dispersed sites provide a notable advantage in terms of active site density, leading to improved catalytic performance due to an increased number of reactive surfaces.
3. The distinctive structural characteristics of 2-D dispersed active sites have garnered considerable research interest due to their potential to enhance efficiency and selectivity in the ODHP process, which is vital for the production of valuable chemical intermediates.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在氧化脱氢化丙烷（ODHP）反应中，通过构建二维结构来开发硼基催化剂中的2-D分散活性位点，这种结构允许活性位点密度高于传统的线性边缘位点分布。
2. 这些2-D分散位点在活性位点密度方面提供了显著优势，由于增加了反应表面的数量，导致催化性能得到增强。
3. 2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量研究兴趣，因为它们有望提高ODHP过程的效率和选择性，这对于生产有价值的化学中间体至关重要。
