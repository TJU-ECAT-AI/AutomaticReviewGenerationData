<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction are developed by creating a high density of active sites on the catalyst's surface.
2. These 2-D dispersed active sites offer a higher active site density compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, which results in improved catalytic performance.
3. The unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites have garnered significant research interest because of their potential to enhance the efficiency and selectivity of the ODHP reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在硼基催化剂中，通过在催化剂表面形成高密度的活性位点来开发氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）反应的二维分散活性位点。
2. 这些二维分散的活性位点与线性分散的边缘位点相比，具有更高的活性位点密度，从而提高了催化性能。
3. 由于其提高ODHP反应效率和选择性的潜力，这些二维分散的活性位点的独特结构特征吸引了大量的研究兴趣。
