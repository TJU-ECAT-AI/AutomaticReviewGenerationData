<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) are created by depositing boron oxide onto silica support materials.
2. These 2-D dispersed active sites provide a higher density of active sites compared to linearly dispersed edge sites.
3. The increased active site density leads to improved catalytic performance due to more efficient interactions between reactants and the catalyst surface.
4. The unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites attract significant research interest because they have the potential to enhance the selectivity and efficiency of the ODHP reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在氧化脱氢化丙烷（ODHP）反应中，通过将硼氧化物沉积在硅酸盐支撑材料上，开发出二维分散活性位点。
2. 这些二维分散的活性位点提供了与线性分散边缘位点相比更高的活性位点密度。
3. 增加的活性位点密度导致催化性能提升，因为可以更有效地在催化剂表面和反应物之间发生相互作用。
4. 这些独特的结构特征吸引了大量的研究兴趣，因为它们有可能提高ODHP反应的选择性和效率。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
