Bulk metal oxides, for instance, offer a larger surface area for reactions, potentially enhancing the conversion efficiency. Supported catalysts, on the other hand, can offer better stability and selectivity, as the metal particles are dispersed on a support, which can affect the reaction pathway and product distribution.

The formation of M-alkyl intermediates, where M represents the metal, is another essential step, as it can lead to the formation of reactive intermediates that drive the reaction towards desired products.
