<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH) is a vital process in the chemical industry for converting hydrocarbons into valuable chemicals. Various catalysts, such as bulk metal oxides and supported systems, have been investigated for their potential in ODH reactions. Achieving high selectivity and conversion rates remains a significant challenge. Proposed reaction mechanisms for ODH involve C-H bond activation, the formation of M-alkyl intermediates, and 2H-abstraction. Site isolation plays a crucial role in addressing selectivity issues by selectively activating specific C-H bonds while inhibiting unwanted side reactions. Understanding the interplay between catalyst type, reaction conditions, and selectivity is key to optimizing ODH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

氧化脱氢化烷烃（ODH）是化学工业中一个关键过程，能够将碳氢化合物转化为有价值的化学品。各种催化剂，包括体金属氧化物和支持系统，已经被探索用于ODH反应的可能性。实现高选择性和转化率仍然是开发高效ODH过程的重大障碍。ODH的提议反应机制包括C-H键活化、M-烷基中间体的形成和2H-抽象。隔离位点在解决选择性问题中起着关键作用，它允许对特定的C-H键进行选择性激活，同时抑制不希望的副反应。理解催化剂类型、反应条件与选择性之间的相互作用对于优化ODH过程至关重要。
