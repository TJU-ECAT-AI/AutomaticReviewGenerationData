<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH), the interplay between various catalysts such as bulk metal oxides and supported systems is significant. Challenges in achieving high selectivity and conversion are discussed, alongside the proposed reaction mechanisms including C-H bond activation, M-alkyl intermediates, and 2H-abstraction. These factors collectively influence process efficiency. Site isolation is identified as a key factor in addressing selectivity issues.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
