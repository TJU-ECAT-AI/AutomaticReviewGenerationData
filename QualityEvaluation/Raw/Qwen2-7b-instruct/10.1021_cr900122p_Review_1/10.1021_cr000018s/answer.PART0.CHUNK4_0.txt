<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH) is a chemical transformation that converts hydrocarbons into olefins and aromatics, offering potential applications in the production of valuable chemicals. Various catalysts, both in bulk form and supported systems, are utilized in these reactions. Achieving high selectivity and conversion rates is a challenge due to the complexity of alkane structures and the presence of competing reactions. Mechanisms typically involve the activation of C-H bonds, the formation of metal-alkyl intermediates, and the abstraction of a hydrogen atom. Process efficiency is dependent on factors such as catalyst activity, selectivity, and stability. Site isolation techniques can improve selectivity by confining the reaction to specific sites on the catalyst surface, thus enhancing the overall efficiency of the ODH process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

氧化脱氢烷烃（ODH）是一种将烃转化为烯烃和芳香族化合物的化学转变，其在生产有价值化学品方面具有潜在应用。不同类型的催化剂，包括整体金属氧化物和支持系统，被用于这些反应中。实现高选择性和转化率是一个挑战，这主要是由于烷烃结构的复杂性以及存在竞争反应。通常涉及C-H键的活化、形成金属-烷基中间体和随后的氢原子抽象等机制。过程效率依赖于催化剂活性、选择性和稳定性的因素。通过隔离反应位点技术可以提高选择性，将其限制在催化剂表面的特定位置上，从而增强ODH过程的整体效率。
