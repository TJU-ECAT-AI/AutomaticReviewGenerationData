        Challenges in oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH) primarily revolve around achieving high selectivity and conversion, often hindered by difficulties in C-H bond cleavage and competition between various reaction pathways. Catalysts, particularly bulk metal oxides and supported systems, play a pivotal role in this process. Key insights from the literature suggest that the surface density of vanadium oxide (VOx) affects propane combustion rates less, leading to a decrease in propene production rate relative to propane dehydrogenation with increased surface density. This highlights the importance of site isolation in addressing selectivity issues.

        Mechanisms for ODH typically involve C-H bond activation, formation of metal-alkyl intermediates, and 2H abstraction. High selectivity and conversion in ODH require careful optimization of the catalyst and reaction parameters, including catalyst loading, support material, and reaction conditions. Proposed catalysts like supported vanadium oxide, phosphorus-modified silica-supported vanadia, and molybdenum-based catalysts demonstrate the potential for high propene selectivities and production rates, showcasing the effectiveness of these strategies in achieving high conversion and selectivity.

        在氧化脱氢（ODH）烷烃的过程中，主要挑战在于实现高选择性和转化率，这通常受到难以切断C-H键和各种反应途径竞争的困扰。催化剂，尤其是批量金属氧化物和支持系统，在这个过程中起着关键作用。文献中的关键见解表明，范德华氧化物（VOx）表面密度对丙烷燃烧速率的影响较小，导致了随着表面密度增加，丙烷脱氢化生成丙烯的速率相对于丙烷脱氢化减少。这强调了在选择性问题上通过隔离位点优化反应路径的重要性。

        ODH机制通常涉及C-H键活化、金属-烷基中间体的形成以及2H抽取。为了实现高选择性和转化率，优化催化剂和反应参数（包括催化剂负载、支持材料和反应条件）至关重要。支持的钒氧化物、磷改性的硅酸盐支持的钒亚铁、钼基催化剂等提出的催化剂显示出高丙烯选择性和生产率的能力，这表明这些策略在实现高转化率和选择性方面的有效性。
