By ensuring that active sites are isolated, the risk of unwanted side reactions is minimized, leading to enhanced conversion and selectivity.

4. **Process Efficiency**: The efficiency of the ODH process is influenced by the interplay between catalyst structure, reaction mechanisms, and operational parameters.
