<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH) transforms alkanes into alkenes and hydrogen. 

2. Various catalysts, both bulk metal oxides and supported systems, have been investigated for this conversion.

3. Achieving high selectivity and conversion rates is difficult due to the complexity of the reactions and the formation of byproducts.

4. Proposed mechanisms for ODH reactions include activation of C-H bonds, creation of metal-alkyl intermediates, and 2H-abstraction.

5. Site isolation strategies mitigate selectivity challenges by minimizing the occurrence of unintended reactions.

6. The effectiveness of ODH processes hinges on the design of catalysts, operational parameters, and the understanding of reaction kinetics.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 包括块状金属氧化物和负载系统在内的多种催化剂被探索用于ODH过程。
