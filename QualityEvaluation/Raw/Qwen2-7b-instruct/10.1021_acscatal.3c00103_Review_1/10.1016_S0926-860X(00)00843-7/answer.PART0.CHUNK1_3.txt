<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The initial metal dispersion or percentage exposed affects the sintering process. The second-order sintering rate constant, derived from the general power law expression (GPLE), is expressed with units of h1. The sintering activation energy for the GPLE is described by the equation d(D/D0)/dt=ks[D/D0Deq/D0]m, where m=2.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
