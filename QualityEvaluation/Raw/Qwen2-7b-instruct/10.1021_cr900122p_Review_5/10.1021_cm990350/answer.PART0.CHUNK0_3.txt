<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The synthesis of compound 5 involves the reaction of OVCl3 with 3 equivalents of (tBuO)3SiOH in the presence of pyridine, yielding a white opaque solid in 85% yield after crystallization from dichloromethane.
2. Thermolysis of 5 was initially monitored through slow heating in a sealed capillary under nitrogen using a melting point apparatus.
3. After four days of heating at approximately 180°C, thermolysis resulted in the formation of a dark-green opaque gel.
4. The IR spectrum of the V/Si/O xerogel shows similarities to that of vanadiasubstituted silicalite with MFI (ZSM-5) topology.
5. The dispersion of vanadia in silica was determined through 29Si MAS NMR spectroscopy on the calcined V/Si/O xerogel.
6. The surface area of the V/Si/O xerogel remains constant up to 500°C during thermal treatment, with a surface area of 311 m2/g after calcination at 500°C.
7. The propane ODH catalysis results for the V/Si/O xerogel catalyst show propene selectivities of 86.6%, 82.1%, and 73.2% at 400°C, 450°C, and 500°C, respectively, with corresponding activities of 2.1 mmol g−1 h−1, 5.6 mmol g−1 h−1, and 19 mmol g−1 h−1.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 化合物5的合成涉及将OVCl3与3等当量的(tBuO)3SiOH在吡啶的存在下反应，然后通过二氯甲烷结晶，得到一种白色不透明固体，产率为85%，结晶后为85%。
2. 5的热解首先通过在氮气下使用熔点装置对密封管进行缓慢加热来监测。
3. 在大约180°C加热四天后，热解产生了深绿色的不透明凝胶。
4. V/Si/O凝胶的红外光谱显示出与含范德萨利硅酸盐的MFI（ZSM-5）拓扑结构相似性。
5. 通过29Si MAS核磁共振光谱分析了煅烧后的V/Si/O凝胶中范德萨的分散状态。
6. V/Si/O凝胶的表面积在500°C的热处理过程中保持不变，煅烧后在500°C的表面积为311 m2/g。
7. V/Si/O凝胶催化剂的丙烷氧化脱氢催化结果显示，在400°C、450°C和500°C时的丙烯选择性分别为86.6%、82.1%和73.2%，相应的活性分别为2.1 mmol g−1 h−1、5.6 mmol g−1 h−1和19 mmol g−1 h−1。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
