<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The comprehensive mechanism of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides involves both two-site and one-site schemes. Linear and cyclic alkane transformations occur through the formation of alkenes and metallacyclobutanes as key intermediates. These factors contribute to the high reaction selectivity and excellent catalyst performance in various alkane conversions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
