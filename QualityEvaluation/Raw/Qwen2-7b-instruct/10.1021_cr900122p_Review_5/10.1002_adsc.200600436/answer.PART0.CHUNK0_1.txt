<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This research investigates the mechanism of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides, considering both two-site and one-site schemes. It delves into the processes involved in transforming linear and cyclic alkanes, highlighting the significance of alkenes and metallacyclobutanes as key intermediates. These factors play a pivotal role in determining the reaction selectivity and overall performance of the catalyst in various alkane conversions.
