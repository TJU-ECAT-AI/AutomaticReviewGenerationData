<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The comprehensive mechanism of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides involves intricate processes such as the formation of alkenes and metallacyclobutanes as key intermediates. These intermediates play pivotal roles in linear and cyclic alkane transformations, respectively. The selectivity and performance of the catalysts in various alkane conversions are significantly influenced by their ability to effectively manage and control the formation and fate of these intermediates. The stability and reactivity of the catalyst surface are crucial factors contributing to the efficiency of alkane metathesis. Catalysts facilitate alkane activation, thereby enabling the production of aromatic hydrocarbons with high yields.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
烷基化催化由硅负载钽氢化物进行的全面机制涉及复杂的过程，如形成烯烃和金属环丁烷作为关键中间体。这些中间体在线性烷烃转换中起着关键作用，在环烷烃转换中则以形成金属环丁烷为关键步骤。在各种烷基化转化中的选择性和催化剂性能受到有效管理并控制这些中间体形成和命运的能力的影响。催化剂表面的稳定性和反应性是烷基化效率的关键因素。催化剂促进烷基化激活，从而实现高产芳香烃。
