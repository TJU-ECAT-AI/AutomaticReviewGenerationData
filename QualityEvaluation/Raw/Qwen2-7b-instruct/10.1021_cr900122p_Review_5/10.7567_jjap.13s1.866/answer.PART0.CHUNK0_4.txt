The Japanese Journal of Applied Physics, published by the Japan Society of Applied Physics (JSAP), is a leading academic journal in Japan that covers a wide range of scientific and technological fields. It explores cutting-edge and interdisciplinary topics, organizes annual conferences, publishes scientific journals, sponsors educational events, and compiles information on advanced technologies for the public.
