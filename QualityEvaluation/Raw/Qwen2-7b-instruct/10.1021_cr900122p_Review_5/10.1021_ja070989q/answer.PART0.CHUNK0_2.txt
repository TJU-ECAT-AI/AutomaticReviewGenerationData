<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The comprehensive mechanism of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides involves two-site and one-site schemes, focusing on the detailed analysis of linear and cyclic alkane transformations. Key intermediates, including alkenes and metallacyclobutanes, are essential in these processes. These elements significantly influence the reaction selectivity and the performance of the catalyst in various alkane conversions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
