<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The uniform catalytic site in Sn-β-zeolite was determined through X-ray absorption fine structure analysis. The research concentrated on alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides, examining both two-site and one-site schemes. Linear and cyclic alkane transformations were explored, with alkenes and metallacyclobutanes identified as crucial intermediates. Their formation and function were pivotal in achieving high reaction selectivity and optimizing catalyst performance in various alkane conversions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
