<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The comprehensive mechanism of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides is analyzed. This includes the examination of both two-site and one-site schemes. Processes involved in linear and cyclic alkane transformations are discussed, along with the formation and role of key intermediates like alkenes and metallacyclobutanes. These elements contribute to reaction selectivity and the performance of the catalyst in various alkane conversions.
