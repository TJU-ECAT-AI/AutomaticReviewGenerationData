To answer the question, the physicochemical properties of catalysts, including their chemical qualities and morphological characteristics, significantly influence their performance in dehydrogenation reactions. The low surface barrier facilitates easier access of reactants to the active sites, leading to improved dehydrogenation reaction rates.
