<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The physicochemical properties of catalysts, particularly the chemical qualities such as acid strength and stability, and morphological characteristics, play crucial roles in influencing their performance in dehydrogenation reactions. In this study, the synthesis of mesoporous zeolites under sodium-free conditions led to the spontaneous generation of silanol nests, which promoted the formation of intermetallic Pt3Y alloy nanoparticles. These nanoparticles exhibited high catalytic durability with high propane conversion and propylene selectivity in the propane dehydrogenation reaction. The presence of silanol nests on the zeolite surface was confirmed by IR and NMR spectroscopies. The results suggest that the silanol nests generated by direct synthesis can effectively promote the intermetallic compound formation, leading to improved catalytic performance. This finding is expected to stimulate further research on the development of metal alloy catalysts for various industrial applications, potentially simplifying the dehydrogenation process through the use of fixed-bed systems.

催化剂的物理化学性质，特别是诸如酸强度和稳定性等化学特性以及形态特征，在决定其在脱氢反应中的性能方面起着关键作用。在本研究中，通过无钠条件下的合成方法制备了具有硅醇巢的介孔沸石，这些硅醇巢有效地促进了Pt3Y合金纳米颗粒的形成。这些纳米颗粒在丙烷脱氢反应中显示出极高的催化耐久性、高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性。通过红外光谱和核磁共振光谱证实了沸石上硅醇巢的存在。结果表明，直接合成过程中生成的硅醇巢能够有效促进互金属化合物的形成，从而提高催化剂性能。这一发现有望激发对其他金属合金催化剂的研究，可能通过固定床系统简化脱氢过程。
