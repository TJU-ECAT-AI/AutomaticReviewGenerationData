<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts, specifically the 1-D edge active sites, significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. Understanding the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of these sites aids in designing more efficient catalysts for commercial applications. These sites exhibit distinct characteristics compared to 2-D and 3-D sites, contributing to enhanced efficiency and selectivity for industrial-scale processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
