The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in boron-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. By comprehending the distinct characteristics and reaction processes of these sites, particularly one-dimensional (1-D) edge active sites, researchers can design more effective catalysts suitable for industrial use.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
