<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The performance of B-based catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions is influenced by the spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites. This study focuses on a high surface area boron nitride catalyst and explores how the arrangement and size of active sites impact the reaction efficiency.
2. The investigation reveals that the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of active sites, especially those with 1-D edges, are essential factors in enhancing the performance of these catalysts for commercial applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
