<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The high surface area boron nitride catalyst demonstrates exceptional selectivity for olefins during the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) process, especially at high conversion rates.
2. The spatial arrangement and dimensionality of active sites within boron nitride-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in ODHP reactions.
3. The study emphasizes the importance of comprehending the distinctive characteristics and reaction pathways of these sites, particularly those with one-dimensional edge configurations, in the development of more efficient catalysts suitable for industrial use.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
