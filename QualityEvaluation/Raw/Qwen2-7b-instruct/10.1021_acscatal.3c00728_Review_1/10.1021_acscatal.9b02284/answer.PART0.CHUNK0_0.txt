<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. Among these, 1-D edge active sites are particularly important due to their unique properties and reaction mechanisms. Understanding these aspects helps in designing more efficient catalysts for commercial applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
