<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. Understanding the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of these sites, especially 1-D edge active sites, is essential for designing more efficient catalysts for commercial use. These insights contribute to enhancing catalytic activity and selectivity, which are critical for industrial applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
