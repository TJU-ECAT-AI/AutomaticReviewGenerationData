<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts impact their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane reactions. This effect is due to the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of these sites, especially 1-D edge active sites. This knowledge aids in designing more efficient catalysts suitable for industrial use.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
