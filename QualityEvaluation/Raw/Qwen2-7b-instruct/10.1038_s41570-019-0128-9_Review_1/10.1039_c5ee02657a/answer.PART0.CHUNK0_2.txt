<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. In typical FT reactions using syngas (CO + H2), iron is the preferred metal for higher temperatures, whereas cobalt is favored at lower temperatures. 
2. When comparing the conversion of CO and CO2 through FT, CO shows significantly higher conversion rates (up to 87%) compared to CO2 (up to 45%), indicating current FT catalysts are not optimized for CO2-FT processes.
3. Iron supported on gamma-alumina (γ-Al2O3) facilitates the formation of hydrocarbons with at least two carbon atoms (C2+), while nickel catalysts predominantly produce methane.
4. Introducing manganese into a Fe/γ-Al2O3 catalyst enhances the synthesis of long-chain olefins and reduces methane formation.
5. A Fe/Al2O3 catalyst operating at 3:1 H2:CO2 ratio, 290°C, and 1.4% conversion produces hydrocarbons with 34.9% of the total being C2-C5+.
6. A Fe-Zr-Cu-K/TiO2 catalyst at 3:1 H2:CO2 ratio, 300°C, and 1% conversion yields 30% C5-C15 hydrocarbons.
7. A Fe-La-Cu-K/TiO2 catalyst at 3:1 H2:CO2 ratio, 300°C, and 1% conversion produces 40% C5-C15 hydrocarbons.
8. A Fe-Ru-Zn-K/TiO2 catalyst at 3:1 H2:CO2 ratio, 300°C, and 1% conversion produces 37% C5-C15 hydrocarbons.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

3. 铁支持在γ-Al2O3上促进形成至少两个碳原子（C2+）的烃类化合物，而镍催化剂主要产生甲烷。
4. 在Fe/γ-Al2O3催化剂中加入锰可以增强长链烯烃的合成并减少甲烷的生成。
5. 在3:1 H2:CO2比例、290°C和1.4%转化率下运行的Fe/Al2O3催化剂产生总C2-C5+为34.9%的烃类化合物。

7. 在3:1 H2:CO2比例、300°C和1%转化率下运行的Fe-La-Cu-K/TiO2催化剂产生40%的C5-C15烃类化合物。
8. 在3:1 H2:CO2比例、300°C和1%转化率下运行的Fe-Ru-Zn-K/TiO2催化剂产生37%的C5-C15烃类化合物。
