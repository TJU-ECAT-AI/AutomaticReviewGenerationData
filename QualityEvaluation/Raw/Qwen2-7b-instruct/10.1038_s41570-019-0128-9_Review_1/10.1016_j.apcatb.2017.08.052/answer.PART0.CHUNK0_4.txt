<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, especially Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrated superior performance in CO2-ODH reactions compared to vanadium oxide catalysts in terms of activity, selectivity, and reaction mechanisms. Support materials, composition, and structural factors played critical roles in determining their effectiveness. Challenges exist, such as enhancing stability and activity, which require further optimization. Potential improvements include refining the catalyst composition and adjusting the support materials.
