<EnglishVersionAnswer>
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrate superior activity and selectivity in CO2-ODH reactions compared to vanadium oxide catalysts. Support materials, composition, and structural factors significantly influence the catalyst performance. Challenges for improvement include maintaining stability, reducing costs, and minimizing environmental impact. Further research is essential to enhance catalyst efficiency and sustainability.
