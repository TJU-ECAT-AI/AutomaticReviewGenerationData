<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study investigates the use of bimetallic CrV impregnated catalysts in the oxidative dehydrogenation of n-butane using CO2 as the oxidant.
2. The high activity of 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 catalyst is attributed to its reducibility and the balance between acidity and basicity for producing butenes.
3. In terms of hydrogen consumption, 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 consumes the most, followed by 1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5, 1.2Cr2.8 V/MesoZ-5, and 2.7Cr1.2 V/M-22.
4. When using 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41, the predominant product is 1,3-butadiene, with smaller quantities of trans 2-butene, 1-butene, cis 2-butene, and isobutene.
5. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22 catalyst exhibits the highest CO2 conversion of 2.35 to 2.2% at 550°C.
6. The CO2 activation efficiency of 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 improves at higher temperatures.
7. The apparent activation energies for dehydrogenation across the four catalysts range from 67 to 72 kJ/mol.
8. The variation in the apparent activation energies for cracking (80 to 157 kJ/mol) suggests differing cracking mechanisms.
9. The ratio of conversion to coke weight percentage for the four catalysts, under the same reaction conditions, ranks as follows: 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/mesoZ-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 研究考察了含双金属CrV浸渍的介孔和微孔催化剂在使用CO2作为氧化剂的n-丁烷氧化脱氢反应中的应用。
2. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41催化剂的高活性归因于其可还原性和酸碱功能平衡，用于生成丁烯。
3. 在四种样品中，1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41的氢消耗量最多，其次是1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5、1.2Cr2.8 V/MesoZ-5和2.7Cr1.2 V/M-22。
4. 使用1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41时，主要产物是1,3-丁二烯，其次有少量顺式2-丁烯、1-丁烯、反式2-丁烯和异丁烯。
5. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22催化剂的CO2转化率在550°C范围内为2.35至2.2%。
6. 随着温度升高，1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41的CO2激活效率有所提高。
7. 四种催化剂的脱氢反应的表观活化能范围为67至72 kJ/mol。
8. 破裂机制的不同反映了裂解作用的表观活化能在80至157 kJ/mol之间的变化。
9. 在相同的反应条件下，四类催化剂的转化率与焦炭重量百分比的比例如下排列：1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/mesoZ-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22。
