<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study examines the oxidative dehydrogenation of n-butane using CO2 as an oxidant with bimetallic CrV impregnated mesoporous and microporous catalysts.
2. The high activity of the 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 catalyst relies on its reducibility and acid-base functionality for butene production.
3. The 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 catalyst primarily produces 1,3-butadiene, with smaller quantities of trans 2-butene, 1-butene, cis 2-butene, and isobutene.
4. The 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22 catalyst exhibits the highest CO2 conversion in the range of 2.35-2.2% at 550°C.
5. The apparent activation energies for dehydrogenation across the four catalysts are similar, from 67 to 72 kJ/mol.
6. The ratio of conversion to coke weight percentage across the four catalysts at the same reaction conditions is ranked as 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/mesoZ-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 研究考察了使用CO2作为氧化剂，CrV双金属浸渍的介孔和微孔催化剂在异丁烷氧化脱氢反应中的活性。
2. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41催化剂的高活性依赖于其可还原性和酸碱功能，用于生成丁烯。
3. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41催化剂主要产生1,3-丁二烯，同时少量产生顺式2-丁烯、1-丁烯、反式2-丁烯和异丁烯。
4. 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22催化剂在550°C时的CO2转化率范围为2.35-2.2%。
5. 四种催化剂中，脱氢的显热活化能相似，从67至72 kJ/mol。
6. 在相同反应条件下，四种催化剂的转化率与焦炭重量比百分比排名为：1.2Cr2.8 V/M-41 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/Z-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/mesoZ-5 > 1.2Cr2.8 V/M-22。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
