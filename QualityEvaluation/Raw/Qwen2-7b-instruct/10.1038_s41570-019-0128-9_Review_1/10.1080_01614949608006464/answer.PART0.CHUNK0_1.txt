<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Ceria (CeO2) and CeO2-containing materials are promising for various catalytic applications because of their unique properties. These materials have been extensively studied for their catalytic properties in oxidation, hydrogenation, and decomposition reactions. Ceria plays a crucial role in the dynamic behavior of three-way catalysts (TWC) for the efficient conversion of multiple pollutants in automotive exhaust gases.

2. In CO2-ODH reactions, FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts demonstrate potential in terms of activity, selectivity, and reaction mechanisms compared to vanadium oxide catalysts. The performance of these catalysts is influenced by several factors, including the support materials, composition, and structural characteristics.

3. Challenges and opportunities for improvement in FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts involve enhancing stability, improving activity under practical conditions, and optimizing the interaction between the metal component and ceria.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 氧化钇稳定的氧化铈（CeO2）是各种催化应用中极具前景的材料，因其独特的性质。这些材料在氧化、氢化和分解反应中的催化性能得到了广泛的研究。铈在三元催化转化器（TWC）的动态行为中起着关键作用，对于有效转换汽车排气中的多种污染物至关重要。

2. 在CO2-ODH反应中，FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在活性、选择性和反应机制方面显示出与氧化钒催化剂相比的潜力。这些催化剂的性能受到支持材料、组成和结构特性等多方面因素的影响。

3. FexNiy/CeO2催化剂面临的挑战和改进机会包括提高稳定性、在实际条件下提高活性以及优化金属成分与铈之间的相互作用。
