<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study investigated the impact of hydration and oxygen vacancies on the adsorption and activation of CO2 on β-Ga2O3(100). The catalyst system Fe3Ni1/CeO2 demonstrated higher activity in CO2-ODH reactions than V2O5.
2. The performance of catalysts, including Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts, was affected by the type of support materials, composition, and structure. 
3. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts showed superior selectivity in CO2-ODH products compared to vanadium oxide catalysts.
4. Oxygen vacancies played a crucial role in improving CO2 adsorption and activation processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
