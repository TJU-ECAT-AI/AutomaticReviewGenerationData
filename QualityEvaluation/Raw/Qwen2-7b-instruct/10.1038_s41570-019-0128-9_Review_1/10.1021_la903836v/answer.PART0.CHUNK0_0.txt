<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines the influence of hydration and oxygen vacancy on CO2 adsorption and activation on β-Ga2O3(100). It reveals that the catalyst system exhibits remarkable activity for CO2 activation. Among various catalysts, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 was found to have superior performance compared to V2O5-based catalysts. The research underscores that support materials, composition, and structural factors significantly affect catalyst performance. Notably, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 showed higher activity and selectivity in CO2-ODH reactions. Structural defects, specifically oxygen vacancies, were identified to enhance catalytic properties. The study suggests potential avenues for improving catalyst performance through optimization of composition and structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

本研究探讨了水分化和氧空位对β-Ga2O3(100)上CO2吸附与活化的效应。结果显示催化剂体系在CO2激活方面表现出显著的活性。在各种催化剂中，Fe3Ni1/CeO2被发现具有优于V2O5基催化剂的性能。研究强调，支撑材料、组成和结构因素对催化剂性能产生重大影响。值得注意的是，Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH反应中显示出更高的活性和选择性。结构缺陷，特别是氧空位，被认定能增强催化性能。研究建议通过优化组成和结构来改善催化剂性能的潜在途径。
