<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study examines how hydration and oxygen vacancy impact the adsorption and activation of CO2 on the β-Ga2O3(100) surface. Hydration increases CO2 adsorption capacity, while oxygen vacancies enhance CO2 activation. 
2. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts outperform vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions in terms of activity and selectivity. Support materials, composition, and structure play crucial roles in determining catalyst performance.
3. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts have the potential for improvement by optimizing the aforementioned factors.
