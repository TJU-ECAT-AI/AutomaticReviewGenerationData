<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. This study reveals that the activity of modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts varies in ethane dehydrogenation with carbon dioxide.
2. Adding iron, cobalt, and manganese oxides notably enhanced ethylene selectivity, whereas nickel primarily promoted reforming and cracking reactions.
3. At 650°C, the catalytic activity of modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composites ranked Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr based on ethylene yield.
4. The modifier's type influences the catalyst's texture, structure, chromium species distribution, oxygen species, and acid/base properties, collectively affecting the catalyst's activity in ethane dehydrogenation with carbon dioxide.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
