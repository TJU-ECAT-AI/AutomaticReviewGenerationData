<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study highlights that the introduction of various modifiers, including Fe, Co, Mn, and Ni oxides, into Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts leads to diverse catalytic activities in the oxidative dehydrogenation of ethane using carbon dioxide as an oxidant. The nature of the modifier impacts the catalyst's textural and structural properties, the distribution of chromium species and oxygen species, and the acid/base properties of the catalyst surface. These factors collectively determine the catalytic efficiency of the modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts in ethane dehydrogenation reactions.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>
