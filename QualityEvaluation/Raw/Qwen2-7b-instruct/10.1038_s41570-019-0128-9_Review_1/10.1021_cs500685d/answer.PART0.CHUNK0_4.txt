<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts outperform vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions in terms of activity and selectivity. Support materials, composition, and structure significantly influence catalyst performance. These catalysts exhibit better stability and lower coke formation, addressing major challenges in CO2-ODH reactions. Enhancements in support material design, catalyst composition optimization, and structure control could boost the performance of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
