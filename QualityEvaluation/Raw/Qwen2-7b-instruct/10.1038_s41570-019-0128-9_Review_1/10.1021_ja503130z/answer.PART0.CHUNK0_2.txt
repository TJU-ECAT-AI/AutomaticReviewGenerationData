<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines how the size affects the catalytic activity of vanadium oxide species used in CO2-ODH reactions. It assesses various catalysts, including FexNiy/CeO2, specifically focusing on Fe3Ni1/CeO2, against vanadium oxide catalysts. The research highlights the importance of support materials, composition, and structure in influencing the performance of these catalysts. It also addresses the challenges and opportunities for enhancing their efficiency.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
