1. The study examines the catalytic activity of vanadium oxide species in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, focusing on its size dependence. It also investigates FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions, finding that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits superior activity compared to other catalysts.
2. The performance of the catalysts is affected by various factors including support materials, composition, and structural aspects.
