        In the context of CO2 exchange processes, the role of platinum (Pt) is temperature-dependent. At lower temperatures (200-400°C), Pt accelerates the exchange rate, favoring the exchange of the second oxygen atom of C18O2. The exchange of the second oxygen atom of C18O2 can occur with C18O16O in the gas phase or with C18O16O-like surface species, yielding C16O2. Understanding these mechanisms is crucial for developing highly selective FTS processes.

        Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中展现出更高的活性和选择性，凸显了支撑材料和组成对实现最佳性能的重要性。结构缺陷，特别是氧空位，增强了催化性质，表明通过改进组成和结构来提升催化剂性能的潜力。在规模化生产和成本效益方面存在的挑战需要进一步优化合成方法和催化剂组成。
