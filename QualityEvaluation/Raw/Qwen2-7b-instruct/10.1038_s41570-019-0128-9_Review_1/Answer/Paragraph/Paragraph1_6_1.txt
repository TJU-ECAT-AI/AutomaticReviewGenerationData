        Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation

        Detailed analysis established from the study of reference [10.1021/la903836v], synthesized comprehension stemming from references [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052] and [10.1002/anie.201603556]. The examination reveals that FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrate superior performance in CO2-ODH reactions compared to vanadium oxide catalysts. This superiority is attributed to enhanced activity, selectivity, and optimized structure and composition, which significantly influence the catalyst's performance. Structural defects, notably oxygen vacancies, are shown to enhance catalytic properties, and potential for improvement lies in refining the catalyst composition and structure.

        The study also highlights that the selectivity for propylene formation is notably enhanced with FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, offering a competitive advantage over vanadium oxide catalysts in terms of product distribution. However, challenges in scaling up production and cost-effectiveness remain significant obstacles. Future research might focus on optimizing the catalyst composition and synthesis methods to address these challenges.

        The role of support materials, composition, and structural factors in determining the performance of these catalysts is crucial. Optimizing these parameters can lead to more efficient CO2 utilization and enhanced product yields in CO2-ODH reactions. This comparative analysis underscores the importance of considering these factors in the design and development of high-performance catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions.

        The study also delves into the impact of hydration and oxygen vacancy on the adsorption and activation of CO2 on β-Ga2O3(100), revealing remarkable activity towards CO2 activation. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts were found to surpass V2O5-based catalysts in performance, demonstrating higher activity and selectivity in CO2-ODH reactions. The presence of structural defects, particularly oxygen vacancies, improves catalytic properties. The study suggests potential for enhancing catalyst performance through refining the composition and structure.

        The investigation into the effects of modifying iron catalysts with zinc and sodium, focusing on how these modifications impact the selectivity and activity in CO2-ODH reactions, provides insights into the role of electronic structure modifications in controlling product distribution. Zinc acts as a structural promoter, changing the size of iron species, while sodium ions alter the electronic structure of the catalyst, facilitating CO activation and promoting the desorption of products. The introduction of sodium ions can significantly increase the conversion of CO and the selectivity for alkenes, especially those with five or more carbon atoms.

The presence of Pt allows for the exchange of the second oxygen atom of C18O2 with C18O16O in the gas phase or with C18O16O-like surface species, leading to the formation of C16O2.

        FexNiy/CeO2与vanadium氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性及其对反应机制和产品形成的影响

        根据参考文献[10.1021/la903836v]的深入分析，结合了参考文献[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]和[10.1002/anie.201603556]的综合理解。研究显示，FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，尤其是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在CO2-ODH反应中的性能优于vanadium氧化物催化剂。这一优越性体现在增强的活性、选择性和优化的结构与组成上，这些因素显著影响催化剂的性能。结构缺陷，特别是氧空位的存在，被证明能提升催化性能，改善催化剂性能的潜力在于优化其组成和结构。

        研究还强调了Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂在丙烯形成上的选择性提高，与vanadium氧化物催化剂相比，展现出更优的产品分布效率。然而，扩大生产规模和成本效益方面仍存在挑战。未来研究可能集中于优化催化剂组成和合成方法以解决这些问题。

        支持材料、组成和结构因素在确定这些催化剂性能方面起着关键作用。优化这些参数可以提高CO2利用效率和CO2-ODH反应中的产物产率。这种比较分析凸显了在设计和开发高效CO2-ODH反应催化剂时考虑这些因素的重要性。

        对于β-Ga2O3(100)上CO2的吸附和激活影响的考察揭示了显著的活性。Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂被发现超越V2O5基催化剂，在性能上表现出更高的活性和选择性在CO2-ODH反应中。结构缺陷，特别是氧空位的出现，提高了催化性能。研究建议通过改进组成和结构来提升催化剂性能。

        对铁催化剂进行锌和钠改性的研究，专注于这些修改如何影响CO2-ODH反应的选择性和活性，提供了关于电子结构修改在控制产物分布中的作用的见解。锌作为结构促进剂，改变了铁物种的大小，而钠离子则改变了催化剂的电子结构，促进了CO激活并促进了产物的脱附。引入钠离子可以显著增加CO的转化率和五碳以上烷烃的选择性。

        对于CO2和CeO2之间的氧交换过程的详细研究，关注Pt的作用过程，提供了交换机制的深入理解。研究揭示了Pt的参与取决于温度，特别是在200至400°C之间加速了氧交换速率，主要有利于交换C18O2分子的第二个氧原子。在Pt的存在下，C18O2的第二氧原子可以通过在气相或C18O16O类似表面物种与C18O16O发生交换，最终形成C16O2。

        鉴于文件中讨论的进展和发现，未来的展望提出了探索新兴领域和创新方法的机会。未来研究的努力可能集中在细化催化剂组成、改进合成方法以及解决可扩展性和经济性问题上，以提高FexNiy/CeO2和vanadium氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能。

        未来研究可能深入探讨多种改性（锌、钠和铂掺杂）对催化剂性能的协同效应，以及探索新型支持材料，进一步增强CO2激活和产物选择性。基于结构-性质关系预测催化剂性能的高级计算方法的应用也可能提供有价值的见解，用于理性催化剂设计。此外，开发具有可扩展性和经济可行性的生产方法对于这些催化剂的广泛应用至关重要。
