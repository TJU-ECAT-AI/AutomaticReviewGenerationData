<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The understanding of coke deposition in Pt-based catalysts has evolved significantly, recognizing its adverse impact on catalytic activity and selectivity. Challenges associated with coke deposition include its detrimental effects on catalyst performance. Strategies to address these challenges include prevention methods aimed at inhibiting coke formation through surface property modifications or reaction condition alterations. Novel catalyst designs enhance intrinsic stability and resistance against coke formation. Regeneration techniques involve post-catalyst treatments to remove deposited coke and restore catalyst performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
