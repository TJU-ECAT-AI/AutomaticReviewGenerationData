<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Our understanding of coke deposition in Pt-based catalysts has evolved significantly over time.
2. Coke deposition challenges arise due to its capacity to deplete active sites on the catalyst surface.
3. Strategies such as prevention methods, innovative catalyst designs, and effective regeneration techniques have been developed to tackle these challenges.
4. Prevention methods typically involve adjusting the catalyst composition to improve stability against coke formation.
5. New catalyst designs concentrate on creating structures that reduce coke deposition.
6. Regeneration techniques aim to eliminate accumulated coke, restoring catalyst activity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
