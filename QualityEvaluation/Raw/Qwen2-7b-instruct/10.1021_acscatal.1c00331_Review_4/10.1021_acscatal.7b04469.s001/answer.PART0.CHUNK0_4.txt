<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Our understanding of coke deposition in Pt-based catalysts has evolved to recognize it as a complex process involving the accumulation of carbonaceous deposits on the catalyst surface. Challenges include the detrimental impact on catalyst activity and selectivity. Various strategies such as prevention methods, novel catalyst designs, and regeneration techniques address these issues. The effectiveness of these strategies depends on the specific operating conditions.
2. Remaining obstacles in this field include the development of more durable catalyst materials and the refinement of regeneration processes to efficiently remove coke without compromising the catalyst's performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
