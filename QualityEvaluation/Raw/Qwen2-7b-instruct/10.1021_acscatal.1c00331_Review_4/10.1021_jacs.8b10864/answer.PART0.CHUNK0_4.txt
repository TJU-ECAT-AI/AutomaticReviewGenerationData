How has our understanding of coke deposition in Pt-based catalysts evolved?

Our understanding of coke deposition in Pt-based catalysts has evolved significantly, recognizing it as a critical challenge affecting catalyst performance. Strategies such as prevention methods, novel catalyst designs, and regeneration techniques have been developed to mitigate this issue. Prevention involves modifying catalyst surface chemistry, while novel designs incorporate additives or support materials. Regeneration focuses on restoring catalyst activity. However, challenges persist in effectively preventing coke formation, maintaining high stability, and improving regeneration efficiency, necessitating further research for more robust catalyst development.

我们的Pt基催化剂焦炭沉积的理解如何发展？

我们对Pt基催化剂中焦炭沉积的理解已经显著发展，认识到这是一个影响催化剂性能的关键挑战。已经开发出预防方法、新型催化剂设计和再生技术等策略来缓解这一问题。预防涉及修改催化剂表面化学，而新型设计则采用添加剂或支持材料。再生则集中在通过去除沉积的焦炭来恢复催化剂活性。然而，在有效防止焦炭形成、保持高稳定性以及提高再生效率方面仍存在挑战，需要进一步的研究来开发更稳健、抗焦炭沉积能力更强且再生能力更好的催化剂。
