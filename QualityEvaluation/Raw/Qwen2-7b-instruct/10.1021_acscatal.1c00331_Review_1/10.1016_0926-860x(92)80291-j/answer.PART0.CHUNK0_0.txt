<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Coke deposits in catalytic systems, particularly in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions, exhibit varying compositions and locations depending on different reaction conditions and catalyst types. Analytical techniques including gas chromatography, mass spectrometry, and X-ray diffraction have been utilized to understand the complex structure and diverse components of coke in these systems.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
