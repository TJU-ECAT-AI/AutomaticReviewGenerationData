Key areas of research focus have included understanding the catalyst structure, identifying the active sites responsible for the reactions, selecting appropriate support materials, and incorporating promoters to enhance performance. 2. The dehydrogenation activity of chromium oxide-based catalysts is closely tied to the amount of surface chromium present in the material.
