<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Since their discovery in 1933, chromium oxide-based catalysts have evolved significantly, finding application in various industrial processes including polymerization and selective alcohol oxidation. Recent interest has grown due to their potential in isobutene synthesis for MTBE and competing with high-capital steam-cracking methods for producing pure light olefins for specialized applications.

2. Key industrial applications of these catalysts include dehydrogenation processes, where they are crucial for the conversion of paraffins into olefins. They are also pivotal in polymerization and selective oxidation of alcohols. Research focuses on their structure, active sites, support materials, promoters, and reaction mechanisms to enhance their efficiency and selectivity.

3. The chemical and physical characteristics of the catalysts, such as their surface area and the nature of the chromium species, play significant roles in their catalytic activity. Chromium oxide loadings, the type of support material, and the presence of dopants like alkali metals affect the catalyst's performance.

4. The characterization techniques used to study these catalysts include chemical analysis, UVVisNIR diffuse reflectance spectroscopy, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy, X-ray diffraction, and surface area measurements. These methods help understand the distribution and state of chromium species, which is crucial for their catalytic function.

5. The research aims to provide a comprehensive picture of the catalyst's behavior in isobutane dehydrogenation. The experimental error in determining the chromium species' composition is estimated to be around 10% of the average value, indicating a certain level of precision in the measurements.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 这些催化剂的关键工业应用包括脱氢过程，它们在将烷烃转化为烯烃方面至关重要。它们在聚合和选择性醇氧化中也起着关键作用。研究重点在于催化剂的结构、活性位点、支持材料、促进剂和反应机制，以提高其效率和选择性。

3. 催化剂的化学和物理特性，如表面面积和铬物种的状态，对其催化活性具有重要意义。铬氧化物负载量、支撑材料类型以及碱金属等掺杂剂的存在会影响催化剂的性能。

4. 用于研究这些催化剂的技术包括化学分析、紫外可见近红外漫反射光谱、X射线光电子能谱、X射线衍射和表面面积测量。这些方法有助于理解铬物种分布及其状态，这对了解催化剂的功能至关重要。

5. 研究旨在提供催化剂在异丁烷脱氢作用下行为的全面图像。确定铬物种组成的实验误差估计约为平均值的10%，表明测量具有一定的精确度。
