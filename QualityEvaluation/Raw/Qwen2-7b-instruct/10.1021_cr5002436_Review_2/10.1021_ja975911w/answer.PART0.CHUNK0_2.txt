<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Merck Index CD-ROM provides a powerful search engine with three search routines of increasing complexity and power. It allows users to search for chemical structures and copy them to the clipboard. These clipboard images are compatible with both Chemintosh (Softshell) and ChemDraw (CambridgeSoft Corp.) software, enabling full manipulation of the structures within these applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
