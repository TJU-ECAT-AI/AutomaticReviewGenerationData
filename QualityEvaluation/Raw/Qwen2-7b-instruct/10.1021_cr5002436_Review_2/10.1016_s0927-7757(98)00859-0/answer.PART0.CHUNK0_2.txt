<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Chromium oxide-based catalysts have been extensively studied for their environmental and industrial importance, particularly in the oxidation of CO using O2. They are used in a variety of practical applications.

2. Precious metals like Pt, Pd, Rh, and Au are highly efficient catalysts for the CO oxidation reaction, especially at ambient temperatures.

3. Multi-component catalyst systems, such as manganese-silver composite oxides, copper-chromium, CuO-ZnO/Al2O3, CuO-CeO2, and manganese-silver composite oxides, exhibit superior catalytic performance compared to single-component systems.

4. The surface properties of chromium oxides influence the adsorption behavior of CO, CO2, and O2, as evidenced by studies employing infrared techniques.

5. Gamma radiation treatment affects the surface and catalytic properties of Cr2O3/Al2O3 catalysts.

6. The current research focused on understanding how the amount of chromium oxide and the precalcination temperature impact the physical, surface, and catalytic properties of the Cr2O3/Al2O3 system.

7. By analyzing the specific surface areas of adsorbents preheated at different temperatures, researchers were able to determine the activation energy of sintering using the relation SBET=A exp DEs RT.

8. For AlCr-I, AlCr-II, AlCr-III, AlCr-IV, and AlCr-V adsorbents, the computed activation energies of sintering were found to be 21, 7.6, 7.6, 15.3, and 33 kJ/mol, respectively.

9. The increase in catalytic activity due to increased chromium oxide content could also be attributed to a potential rise in the specific surface area of the treated catalysts.

10. Heating at around 400°C or exposing the catalysts to gamma radiation leads to the release of strongly bound OH groups from the catalyst surface.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 铬氧化物基催化剂因其环境和工业重要性，在各种实际应用中被广泛研究，特别是在氧气氧化二氧化碳的过程中。

2. 在室温附近，铂、钯、铑和金等贵金属是高效的CO氧化催化剂。

3. 多组分催化剂系统（如锰银复合氧化物、铜铬、CuO-ZnO/Al2O3、CuO-CeO2和锰银复合氧化物）在催化性能上优于单一成分系统，表现出优越的催化性能。

4. 铬氧化物表面性质影响CO、CO2和O2的吸附行为，这通过红外技术的研究得到了证实。

5. 研究人员发现伽马辐射处理对Cr2O3/Al2O3催化剂的表面和催化性质有影响。

6. 当前的研究重点是探索铬氧化物含量和预煅烧温度如何影响Cr2O3/Al2O3系统的物理、表面和催化性质。

7. 通过分析不同温度预热吸附剂的特定表面积，研究人员使用关系式SBET=A exp DEs RT确定了烧结过程的激活能。

8. 对于AlCr-I、AlCr-II、AlCr-III、AlCr-IV和AlCr-V吸附剂，计算得到的烧结过程激活能量分别为21、7.6、7.6、15.3和33 kJ/mol。

9. 铬氧化物含量增加导致催化活性提高的原因之一可能是处理后的催化剂表面积增加。

10. 加热至约400°C或对催化剂进行伽马辐射处理可导致催化剂表面强烈结合的OH基团释放。
