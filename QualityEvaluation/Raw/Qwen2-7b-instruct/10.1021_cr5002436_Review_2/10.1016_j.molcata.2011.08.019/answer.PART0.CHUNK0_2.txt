1. Chromium oxide-based catalysts have evolved significantly since their discovery in 1933, with advancements focusing on understanding the structure, active sites, support materials, promoters, and reaction mechanisms. These catalysts play a crucial role in industrial applications such as the production of polymers and chemical intermediates, notably polypropylene, acrylonitrile, OXO alcohols, isopropyl alcohol, acrylic acid, and propylene oxide.

2. The key industrial applications of chromium oxide catalysts include the production of polymers and chemical intermediates like polypropylene, acrylonitrile, OXO alcohols, isopropyl alcohol, acrylic acid, and propylene oxide. Research focuses on optimizing catalyst structure, enhancing dispersion of active sites, utilizing appropriate support materials, incorporating promoters, and elucidating reaction mechanisms. 

3. Chromium oxide catalysts show contrasting effects when exposed to CO2. In Cr2O3/Al2O3 catalysts, CO2 negatively impacts catalytic properties, whereas it exhibits a positive effect on Cr2O3/SiO2 catalysts. In the presence of CO2, Cr3+ and Cr2+ sites can engage in alternative oxidative pathways for propene formation and participate in the consumption of hydrogen through the reverse water-gas shift reaction.

1. 自1933年发现以来，铬氧化物催化剂经历了显著的发展，研究重点集中在理解催化剂结构、活性位点、支撑材料、促进剂以及反应机制。这些催化剂在生产聚合物和化学中间体（如聚丙烯、丙烯腈、OXO醇、异丙醇、丙烯酸和丙二醇）等工业应用中发挥着关键作用。

2. 铬氧化物催化剂的主要工业应用包括生产聚合物和化学中间体，如聚丙烯、丙烯腈、OXO醇、异丙醇、丙烯酸和丙二醇。研究聚焦于优化催化剂结构、提高活性位点分散度、使用合适的支撑材料、引入促进剂以及阐明反应机制。

3. 在铬氧化物催化剂暴露于二氧化碳时，其表现出不同的效果。对于Cr2O3/Al2O3催化剂，二氧化碳对催化性能产生负面影响；而对于Cr2O3/SiO2催化剂，则显示出正向促进效果。在二氧化碳存在下，Cr3+和Cr2+位点可以参与替代氧化途径的丙烯形成，并参与通过逆水煤气转换反应消耗氢气。
