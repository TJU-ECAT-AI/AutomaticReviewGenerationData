<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The evolution of chromium oxide-based catalysts since their discovery in 1933 includes significant advancements in their industrial applications and research focus areas. Key applications encompass:

1. **Polymerization**: Chromium catalysts, particularly on silica (Cr/SiOz), are pivotal in the Phillips process for producing high-density polyethylene. Their use has been extended to the synthesis of linear low-density polyethylene and the copolymerization of ethylene and butene-1.

2. **Propylene Polymerization**: Chromium on silica-alumina (Cr/Si02*A1203) is utilized for the polymerization of propylene.

3. **Alkane Dehydrogenation**: Chromium on alumina (Cr/A1203) catalysts exhibit activity in alkane dehydrogenation, dehydrocyclization, and catalytic reforming.

4. **Analytical Techniques**: Various methodologies, including infrared spectroscopy (IR), diffuse reflectance spectroscopy (DRS), X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), Raman spectroscopy, and electron spin resonance (ESR), have been employed to study the catalysts.

5. **Redox Behavior**: The study of chromium's redox behavior on aluminum oxide (Al203), silica (SO2), and silica-alumina (Si02.A1203) is a critical area of investigation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
自1933年发现以来，铬氧化物基催化剂的发展涉及其在工业应用和研究重点领域的重大进步。关键应用包括：

1. **聚合**：铬催化剂，尤其是硅（Cr/SiOz），对乙烯在较低压力下的聚合至关重要。它们的应用已扩展到生产高密度聚乙烯、线性低密度聚乙烯以及乙烯和丁烯-1的共聚。

2. **丙烯聚合**：硅酸铝（Cr/Si02*A1203）上的铬用于丙烯的聚合。

3. **烷烃脱氢**：铝氧（Cr/A1203）上的铬催化剂在烷烃脱氢、脱氢环化和催化重整中表现出活性。

4. **分析技术**：红外光谱（IR）、漫反射光谱（DRS）、X射线光电子光谱（XPS）、拉曼光谱和电子自旋共振（ESR）等方法已被用于研究催化剂。

5. **还原行为**：在铝氧化物（Al203）、二氧化硅（SO2）和硅酸铝（Si02.A1203）上铬的还原行为是研究的重点领域。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
