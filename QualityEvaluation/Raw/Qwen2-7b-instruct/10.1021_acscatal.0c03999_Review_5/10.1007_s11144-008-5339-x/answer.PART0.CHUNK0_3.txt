How do these modifications contribute to optimizing catalytic performance, and what are the key considerations in selecting and implementing these strategies for different types of zeolites used as supports?"

<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The properties that make zeolites promising support materials for CO2-ODHP catalysts include their high surface area, pore structure, and ability to modulate acidity through various techniques like dealumination and weakening. Dealumination modifies the zeolite's acidity by removing aluminum atoms, which can increase the number of acidic sites and improve the catalyst's activity and selectivity. Weakening methods adjust the acidity by altering the framework structure of the zeolite, potentially enhancing its catalytic performance for specific reactions. Key considerations in selecting and implementing these strategies include understanding the type of zeolite, the desired reaction conditions, and the specific requirements for catalyst performance. Different zeolites have unique structures and properties, necessitating tailored approaches to optimize their use as support materials for CO2-ODHP catalysts.

沸石作为CO2-ODHP催化剂支撑材料的优点包括其高表面积、孔结构和通过各种技术（如去铝化和弱化）调节酸性的能力。去铝化通过移除铝原子来修改沸石的酸性，这可以增加酸性位点的数量，从而提高催化剂的活性和选择性。弱化方法通过改变沸石的框架结构调整酸性，可能增强其特定反应的催化性能。选择和实施这些策略的关键考虑因素包括理解沸石类型、所需反应条件以及催化剂性能的具体要求。不同的沸石具有独特的结构和性质，需要采取定制的方法来优化其用作CO2-ODHP催化剂支撑材料的用途。
