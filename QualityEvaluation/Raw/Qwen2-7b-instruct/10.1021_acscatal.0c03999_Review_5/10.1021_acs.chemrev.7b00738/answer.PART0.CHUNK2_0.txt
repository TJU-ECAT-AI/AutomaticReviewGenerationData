<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the properties of zeolites that make them promising support materials for CO2-ODHP catalysts, it's evident that their high surface area, porosity, and tunable acidity are key factors. Dealumination and weakening methods are two strategies aimed at optimizing catalytic performance by adjusting acidity. Dealumination involves reducing the amount of aluminum in zeolites, which could decrease the number of acidic sites. Weakening methods, on the other hand, involve altering the structure of zeolites to reduce acidity. Selecting and implementing these strategies depends on the type of zeolite used. For instance, LTA zeolites with Si/Al ratios higher than 10 might benefit from dealumination to enhance CO2 adsorption. Conversely, MFI zeolites may require weakening methods to prevent over-acidity that could hinder catalytic efficiency.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

分析为什么沸石是CO2-ODHP催化剂的理想支持材料，可以看出其高比表面积、孔隙率和可调酸性是关键因素。消除铝和削弱方法是两种旨在通过调整酸性优化催化性能的策略。消除铝涉及减少沸石中的铝含量，这可能会减少酸性位点的数量。另一方面，削弱方法涉及改变沸石的结构以减少酸性。选择和实施这些策略取决于使用的沸石类型。例如，Si/Al比大于10的LTA沸石可能从消除中获益，以增强CO2吸附。相反，MFI沸石可能需要削弱方法来防止过度的酸性，这会阻碍催化效率。
