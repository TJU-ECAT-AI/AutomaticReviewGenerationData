<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Zeolites are promising due to their unique pore structures and adjustable acidity, which are advantageous for CO2-ODHP catalysts.
2. Acidity modifications are essential for improving catalytic efficiency.
3. Dealumination reduces aluminum content, increasing acidity and catalytic performance.
4. Weakening methods adjust acidity through chemical treatments or synthesis adjustments.
5. Choosing and applying these strategies requires considerations of zeolite type, desired acidity level, and reaction conditions.
6. The efficacy of modifications is influenced by the degree of change, catalyst stability, and product selectivity.
