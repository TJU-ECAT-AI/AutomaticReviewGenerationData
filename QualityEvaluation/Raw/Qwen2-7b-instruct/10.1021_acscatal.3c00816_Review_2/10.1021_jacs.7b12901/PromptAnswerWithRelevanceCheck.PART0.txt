2. MFI and BEA topological zeolites dominate in PDH applications because of their specific pore structures and surface functionalities, which facilitate efficient adsorption and desorption of reactants and products. Their effectiveness is attributed to the balance between pore size, shape, and accessibility, as well as the presence of active sites on the zeolite surface.
3.
