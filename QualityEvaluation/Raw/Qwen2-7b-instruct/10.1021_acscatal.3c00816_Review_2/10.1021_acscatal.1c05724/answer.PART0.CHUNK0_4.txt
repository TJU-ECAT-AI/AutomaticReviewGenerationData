<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This article discusses the use of Ga2O22+ stabilized by paired framework Al atoms in MFI zeolites as a highly reactive site in nonoxidative propane dehydrogenation (PDH). The authors, Yong Yuan, Raul F. Lobo, and Bingjun Xu, explore the effectiveness of MFI and BEA topological zeolites in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
