<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The primary synthesis methods for metal-zeolite catalysts used in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions involve sequential impregnation and calcination processes. MFI and BEA topological zeolites dominate in this application due to their unique structural characteristics and properties. Their high surface area, uniform pore size, and well-defined pore structure facilitate the adsorption and diffusion of reactants, enhancing the efficiency of the PDH reaction. These zeolites allow for optimal control of the reaction environment, promoting the desired chemical transformations. Future developments in PDH catalyst design aim to incorporate advanced materials and functional groups to further improve selectivity, activity, and stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
金属-沸石催化剂在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的主要合成方法涉及顺序浸渍和煅烧过程。MFI和BEA拓扑结构的沸石在该应用中占据主导地位，由于它们独特的结构特性和属性。它们的高比表面积、均匀孔径和明确的孔结构有助于吸附和扩散反应物，增强PDH反应的效率。这些沸石允许对反应环境进行最佳控制，促进所需化学转化。未来PDH催化剂设计的发展目标是整合先进的材料和功能团，进一步提高选择性、活性和稳定性。
