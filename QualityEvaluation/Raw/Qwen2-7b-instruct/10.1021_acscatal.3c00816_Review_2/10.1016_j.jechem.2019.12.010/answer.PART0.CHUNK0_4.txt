<quotes>
CrOx supported on high-silica HZSM-5 with a SiO2/Al2O3 ratio of 260 (Cr/Z-5(260) is synthesized by a simple wet impregnation method, which exhibits high catalytic activity, good selectivity and excellent stability for PDH.
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The primary synthesis method for metal-zeolite catalysts used in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions involves impregnation, specifically wet impregnation, as exemplified by the preparation of Cr/Z-5(260) catalyst. This method allows for the dispersion of metal species, such as chromium oxide (CrOx), on the zeolite support. The high-silica HZSM-5 zeolite with a specific SiO2/Al2O3 ratio of 260 is chosen for its ability to provide a suitable environment for the metal species, ensuring high catalytic activity, selectivity, and stability in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
金属-沸石催化剂在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的主要合成方法是浸渍法，具体为湿浸渍法。以Cr/Z-5(260)催化剂为例，该方法允许金属物种，如铬氧化物（CrOx），在沸石载体上的分散。具有特定SiO2/Al2O3比例（260）的高硅含量HZSM-5沸石被选择，因为它能为金属物种提供一个合适的环境，确保PDH反应中具有高活性、选择性和稳定性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
In particular, the Brnsted acid sites in HZSM-5 zeolite could increase the propane adsorption and enhance the CH bond activation.
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Brnsted acid sites present in the HZSM-5 zeolite play a significant role in enhancing propane adsorption and activating the CH bonds, contributing to the high catalytic performance of the metal-zeolite catalysts in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
特别是，HZSM-5沸石中的Brnsted酸性位点能够增加丙烷吸附并增强CH键的活化，对金属-沸石催化剂在PDH反应中的高催化性能起到了关键作用。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
The Cr species supported on HZSM-5 with low SiO2/Al2O3 ratio (i.e., SiO2/Al2O3 = 28, 43 and 80) possess low propylene selectivity during the PDH, due to the high amount of acidic sites of HZSM-5 support resulting in deep dehydrogenation and cracking of propane.
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Metal species supported on HZSM-5 zeolites with a low SiO2/Al2O3 ratio, such as SiO2/Al2O3 = 28, 43, and 80, exhibit low propylene selectivity in PDH reactions. This is attributed to the high concentration of acidic sites in the HZSM-5 support, which leads to excessive dehydrogenation and cracking of propane, negatively impacting the selectivity towards propylene.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
在较低的SiO2/Al2O3比例下支持在HZSM-5沸石上的金属物种（例如SiO2/Al2O3=28、43和80），在PDH反应中显示出低丙烯选择性。这是由于HZSM-5支撑材料中高浓度的酸性位点导致大量丙烷的深度脱氢和裂解，从而负向影响了向丙烯的选择性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
