Fundamentals of Industrial Catalytic Processes (Second Edition) offers a balanced academic and industrial perspective on industrial catalysis. It serves as a comprehensive course text for academics and provides detailed information on industrially relevant processes, making it useful for practicing chemists and engineers. The book utilizes fundamental aspects of reaction engineering and heterogeneous catalysis for industrial design and optimization. This structure enables experienced practitioners to efficiently find relevant information, akin to a traditional handbook format.
