The Soviet Russian State is a comprehensive textbook written for undergraduate courses on Soviet politics. It offers balanced coverage of various aspects of the system, providing depth in its analysis. The book explores the historical background of the contemporary Soviet system, including its multinational character, the tension between technological introduction and political control, and the evolution from a revolutionary state to an authoritarian one. It also critiques recent attempts to redefine questions in Soviet politics, focusing on power distribution rather than the complexities of organizational relationships within the system. Despite some criticisms regarding the emphasis on power and the inclusion of pro-American comparisons, the book successfully serves as an accessible and informative resource for students seeking to understand Soviet politics.

《苏联俄罗斯国家》是一本为政治科学本科生设计的全面教科书。它对苏联政治的各种方面提供了均衡的覆盖，并在分析中提供了深度。这本书探讨了当代苏联制度的历史背景，包括其多民族特征、技术和控制之间的紧张关系以及从革命国家到权威国家的演变。它还批评了最近重新定义苏联政治问题的尝试，更侧重于权力分配而不是系统内组织关系的复杂性。尽管有人批评这本书过于强调权力和频繁进行美式比较，但它成功地为学生提供了一种易于理解且信息丰富的资源，以了解苏联政治。
