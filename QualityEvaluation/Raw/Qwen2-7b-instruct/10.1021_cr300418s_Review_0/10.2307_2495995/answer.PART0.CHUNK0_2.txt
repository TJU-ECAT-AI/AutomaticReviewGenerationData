The Soviet Russian State is a comprehensive textbook designed for undergraduate courses on Soviet politics, offering balanced coverage and depth across various aspects of the system. It contrasts favorably with other recent publications in the field. The book delves into the historical tension between integrating Western technology and maintaining political control within the empire. Despite minor flaws, Professor Wesson has successfully fulfilled his objective, producing a textbook that is accessible, well-organized, and rich in illustrative detail, catering to the needs of students.
