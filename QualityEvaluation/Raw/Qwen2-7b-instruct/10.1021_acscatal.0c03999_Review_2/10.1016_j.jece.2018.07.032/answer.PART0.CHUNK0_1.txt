<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Promoters tackle the limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness in chromium-based catalytic systems through enhancing CO2 activation, boosting the oxidizing capability, and ensuring selective and stable catalytic performance. Compared to alternative strategies such as oxygen co-feeding, promoters offer more efficient CO2 utilization, lower energy consumption, and better control over the reaction outcomes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
