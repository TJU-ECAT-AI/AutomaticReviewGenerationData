<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Promoters tackle the challenges posed by CO2's low reactivity and inert nature within chromium-based catalysts, offering distinct benefits compared to methods such as oxygen co-feeding. These benefits encompass the enhancement of CO2 activation, improvement in the catalyst's oxidizing capacity, and the attainment of both selectivity and stability in the catalytic process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
