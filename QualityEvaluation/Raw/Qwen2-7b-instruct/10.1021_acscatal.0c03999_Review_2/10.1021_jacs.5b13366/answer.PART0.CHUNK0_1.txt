<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Promoters tackle the challenges posed by CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness in chromium-based catalysts by boosting CO2 activation. They offer several key benefits over alternatives such as oxygen co-feeding, including enhanced activation of CO2, increased oxidizing capability, and the achievement of selective and stable catalytic activity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
