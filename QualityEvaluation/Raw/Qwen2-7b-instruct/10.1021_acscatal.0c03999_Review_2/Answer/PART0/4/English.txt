Promoters have been utilized to address the challenges posed by CO2's low oxidation capability and inertness in chromium-based catalytic systems. Compared to alternative methods such as oxygen co-feeding, they provide distinct benefits in enhancing CO2 activation, improving the catalyst's oxidizing capability, and achieving selective and stable catalytic performance.
