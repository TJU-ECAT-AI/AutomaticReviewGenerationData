<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Promoters are utilized to tackle the challenges posed by CO2's low oxidation capability and inert nature within chromium-based catalyst systems. Compared to methods such as oxygen co-supplying, they provide distinct benefits including boosting CO2 activation, enhancing the catalyst's oxidizing capacity, and ensuring both selectivity and stability in the catalytic process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
