<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) over vanadia-based catalysts has been extensively studied, with the addition of secondary surface metal oxides enhancing activity and selectivity towards propylene.
2. Carbon dioxide (CO2) has been utilized as an oxidant in the ODHP process, resulting in higher propylene yields compared to other oxidants like oxygen or air.
3. Nitrous oxide (N2O) has been investigated as an oxidant in the ODHP process, offering potential advantages in propylene selectivity.
4. Critical challenges associated with catalyst deactivation and stability are faced in the ODHP process, particularly when employing N2O as an oxidant.
5. Strategies to address these limitations may include developing more stable catalyst supports, optimizing the catalyst composition, or refining the reaction conditions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 在范亚基催化剂上进行丙烷氧化脱氢反应（ODHP）已被广泛研究，添加表面金属氧化物如铈氧化物、锆氧化物和钛氧化物可提高活性和丙烯选择性。
2. 在ODHP过程中使用二氧化碳（CO2）作为氧化剂，可以得到比氧气或空气更高的丙烯产率。
3. 硝酸氧化物（N2O）作为ODHP过程中的氧化剂被探索，显示出在丙烯选择性方面可能的好处。
4. 在ODHP过程中，特别是当使用N2O作为氧化剂时，催化剂失活和稳定性问题是一大挑战。
5. 应对这些限制的策略可能包括开发更稳定的催化剂支撑材料、优化催化剂组成或改进反应条件。
