<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Nitrous oxide (N2O) is utilized in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) because of its affordability and environmental advantages.
2. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are commonly applied in ODHP processes.
3. A significant challenge in ODHP is the degradation and instability of catalysts.
4. Potential solutions to overcome these issues involve tweaking the catalyst formulation, enhancing synthesis methodologies, and fine-tuning reaction parameters.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
