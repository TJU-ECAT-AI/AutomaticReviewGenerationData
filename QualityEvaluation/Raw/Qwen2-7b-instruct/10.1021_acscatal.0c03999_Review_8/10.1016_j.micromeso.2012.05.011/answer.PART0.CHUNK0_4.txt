<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. Nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) offers high reactivity and selectivity towards propylene production, making it advantageous compared to other oxidants.

2. Supported chromium oxides, iron-based catalysts, and transition metal catalysts have been employed in the N2O-ODHP process. These catalysts enable the necessary redox reactions for propylene formation.

3. Challenges in the N2O-ODHP process include catalyst deactivation and stability issues, primarily coke formation leading to decreased activity and stability over time. Other concerns are catalyst poisoning and the need for high operating temperatures, which can cause degradation.

4. Strategies to tackle these limitations involve creating more stable and active catalysts through the incorporation of noble metals or the use of supported catalysts with enhanced thermal stability. Potential research directions include optimizing reaction conditions, such as pressure and temperature, to minimize coke formation and improve catalyst longevity.

5. Advanced characterization techniques to monitor catalyst performance and deactivation mechanisms in real-time could offer insights into improving catalyst design and operation.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1.Nitrous oxide（N2O）作为丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）过程中的氧化剂，以其高反应性和丙烯选择性而著称，这使其在与其他氧化剂相比时具有优势。

2. 在N2O-ODHP过程中使用了支持型铬氧化物、铁基催化剂和过渡金属催化剂。这些催化剂能够促进形成丙烯所需的还原反应。

3. N2O-ODHP过程面临的挑战包括催化剂失活和稳定性问题，主要是由于焦炭形成导致的活动性和稳定性下降。其他关注点是催化剂中毒和需要高温操作，这可能导致催化剂降解。

4. 应对这些限制的策略包括开发更稳定和活性更高的催化剂，例如通过引入贵金属或使用具有增强热稳定性的支持催化剂。研究方向可能集中在优化反应条件，如压力和温度，以减少焦炭形成并延长催化剂寿命。

5. 开发先进的表征技术实时监控催化剂性能和失活机制，可以提供改善催化剂设计和操作的见解。
