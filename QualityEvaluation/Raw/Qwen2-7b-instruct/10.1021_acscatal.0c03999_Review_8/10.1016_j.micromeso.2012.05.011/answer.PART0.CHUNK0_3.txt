<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The use of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) offers several advantages compared to other oxidants. 

2. Advantages in terms of propylene selectivity: N2O has a lower oxidative capacity than conventional oxidants such as air or oxygen, leading to improved selectivity towards propylene.

3. Various catalysts employed in this process include metal oxides, metal sulfides, and mixed metal oxides.

4. Critical challenges associated with catalyst deactivation and stability: Catalysts often experience deactivation due to coke formation, sintering, and poisoning by trace contaminants.

5. Strategies or research directions to address these limitations: Developing more robust catalyst materials, optimizing reaction conditions, and exploring alternative oxidants with better compatibility.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1.Nitrous oxide（N2O）作为丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）过程中的氧化剂，与传统的氧化剂（如空气或氧气）相比，具有几个优势。

2.在丙烯选择性方面的优势：N2O的氧化能力较低，导致丙烯选择性提高。

3.在这一过程中使用的催化剂包括金属氧化物、金属硫化物和混合金属氧化物。

4.催化剂失活和稳定性的问题：催化剂经常由于焦炭形成、烧结和痕量杂质中毒而失效。

5.解决这些限制的策略或研究方向：开发更耐用的催化剂材料，优化反应条件，并探索与兼容性更好的替代氧化剂。
