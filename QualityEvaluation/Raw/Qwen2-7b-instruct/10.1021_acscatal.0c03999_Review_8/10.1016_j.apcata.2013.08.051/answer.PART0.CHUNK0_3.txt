<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the use of nitrous oxide (N2O) in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP), it is noted that N2O provides advantages in propylene selectivity over other oxidants. The study utilized Fe-ZSM-5 catalyst prepared through grafting for this process. Challenges like catalyst deactivation and stability were encountered. Potential solutions include refining reaction conditions, adjusting catalyst formulation, and enhancing regeneration techniques.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
