<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Nitrous oxide (N2O) was utilized in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP). Compared to other oxidants, N2O offers an advantage due to its lower oxygen consumption.
2. Iron-ZSM-5 catalysts were used in the ODHP process with N2O.
3. Challenges encountered include problems with catalyst deactivation and maintaining stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
