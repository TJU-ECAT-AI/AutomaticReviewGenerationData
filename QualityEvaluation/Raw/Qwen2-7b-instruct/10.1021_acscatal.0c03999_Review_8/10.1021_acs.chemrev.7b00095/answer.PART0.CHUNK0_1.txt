<EnglishVersionAnswer>
One strategy to address the limitations of using nitrous oxide (N2O) in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) is to incorporate metal adsorbents as a final polishing step. This approach can help limit the energy requirement for adsorption and regeneration processes, making N2O-ODHP more feasible for practical implementation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

</ChineseVersionAnswer>
