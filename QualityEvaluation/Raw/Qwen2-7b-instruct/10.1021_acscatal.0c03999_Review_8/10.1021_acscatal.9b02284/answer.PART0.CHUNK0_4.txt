<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. This study focuses on the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) utilizing nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant. Compared to other oxidants like air and oxygen, N2O offers distinct advantages in the ODHP process.
2. In the context of the ODHP process, supported boron oxide catalysts have been utilized effectively.
3. A significant challenge faced by catalysts in this process involves deactivation and maintaining stability under operational conditions.
