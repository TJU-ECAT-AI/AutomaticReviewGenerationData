<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Merck Index, 12th Edition, CD-ROM provides a searchable electronic version that includes a powerful search engine with three routines of increasing complexity and power. Chemical structures found through the search methods can be copied to the clipboard and utilized in other applications, compatible with Chemintosh (Softshell) and ChemDraw (CambridgeSoft Corp.) software, allowing for manipulation of the structures.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
