<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The document discusses the Euro debt crisis, the implications of Eurobonds, and the risks associated with central bank interventions in government bond markets. It introduces an alternative solution called fiscal interest rate equalization (FIRE) which aims to stabilize bond markets by transferring some of the interest rate savings from countries with a relatively high creditworthiness to those facing crises.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>
