<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study explored the connections between teachers' work engagement, their burnout levels, and their confidence in professional training. It was found that teacher confidence in training policies should be assessed considering their engagement and burnout. The analysis demonstrated that teachers' burnout acted as a mediator in the connection between their work engagement and confidence in training.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
