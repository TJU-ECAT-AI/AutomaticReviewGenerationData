<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The synthesis and characterization of silica-supported boron oxide catalysts for the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane are discussed. These catalysts have been synthesized using various methods such as sol-gel processes and impregnation techniques, which lead to the presence of 3-D dispersed active sites in the porous frameworks. This feature significantly enhances the catalytic performance of the materials. Different methods for incorporating boron active components were compared based on their effectiveness for different catalytic applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
