<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Silica-supported boron oxide catalysts were synthesized using a sol-gel method.
2. The catalysts were characterized using X-ray diffraction (XRD), N<sub>2</sub> adsorption-desorption, and temperature-programmed desorption (TPD) techniques.
3. The B-based materials had 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks.
4. These features enhanced catalytic performance due to increased surface area and controlled pore size.
5. Boron oxide was incorporated into the silica matrix via co-precipitation.
6. The effectiveness of the catalysts varied depending on the specific application.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 使用溶胶-凝胶法合成了二氧化硅支持的硼氧化物催化剂。
2. 催化剂通过X射线衍射(XRD)、氮气吸附-脱附和程序升温脱附（TPD）技术进行了表征。
3. 这些基于B的材料在多孔结构中具有分散的三维活性位点。
4. 这些特征提高了催化性能，由于增加了表面积并控制了孔径大小。
5. 硼氧化物通过共沉淀法被引入到二氧化硅基质中。
6. 催化剂的有效性根据具体应用而异。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
