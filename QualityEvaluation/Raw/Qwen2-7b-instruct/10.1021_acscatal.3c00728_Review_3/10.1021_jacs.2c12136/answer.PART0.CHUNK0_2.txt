<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks enhance catalytic performance. These features allow for better interaction with reactants, leading to improved efficiency in reactions. The study demonstrates the effectiveness of such materials in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane under high-temperature and water-rich conditions. Various methods for incorporating B active components into these structures have been assessed, showing their adaptability for different catalytic applications.
