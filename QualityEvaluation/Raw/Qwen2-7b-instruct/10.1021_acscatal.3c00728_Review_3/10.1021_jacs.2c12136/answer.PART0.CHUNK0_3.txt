<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks reveals that these features enhance catalytic performance by increasing the surface area and accessibility of active sites. Various methods such as chemical vapor deposition (CVD), sol-gel, and sonochemical synthesis are employed to incorporate B active components into these structures. The effectiveness of these methods varies depending on the specific catalytic application, including factors like temperature, pressure, and the nature of the reactants.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

分析B基材料在多维分散活性位点的孔框架结构的特性显示，这些特征通过增加表面积和提高活性位点的可接近性来增强催化性能。采用多种方法如化学气相沉积（CVD）、溶胶-凝胶法和声化学合成等来将B活性成分融入这些结构中。这些方法的有效性因具体催化应用而异，包括温度、压力以及反应物的性质等因素。
