The structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks enhance catalytic performance through increased surface area and better accessibility of reactants. The incorporation of B active components into these structures is accomplished via impregnation and deposition techniques, which vary in effectiveness based on the specific catalytic application.
