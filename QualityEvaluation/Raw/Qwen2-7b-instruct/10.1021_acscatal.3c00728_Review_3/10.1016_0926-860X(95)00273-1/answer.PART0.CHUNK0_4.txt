<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks are promising candidates for various catalytic applications because of their unique structural characteristics.
2. Incorporating boron (B) active components into these structures typically involves chemical vapor deposition (CVD), impregnation, and sol-gel methods.
3. These methods vary in their efficiency and control over the distribution and size of the active sites.
4. The presence of 3-D dispersed active sites improves catalytic performance by increasing the surface area and providing more accessible reactive sites for reactants.
5. The effectiveness of these methods for different catalytic applications depends on factors such as the specific type of catalyst, reaction conditions, and desired product yield.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 含有3D分散活性位点的孔隙框架结构的B基材料是各种催化应用的有前景候选者，因为它们具有独特的结构特性。
2. 将硼（B）活性成分引入这些结构通常涉及化学气相沉积（CVD）、浸渍和溶胶-凝胶方法。
3. 这些方法在效率和对活性位点分布和大小的控制上存在差异。
4. 3D分散的活性位点的存在通过增加表面积并提供更多的可访问反应位点来提高催化性能，以供反应物使用。
5. 这些方法在不同催化应用中的有效性取决于催化剂的特定类型、反应条件和预期产物收率等因素。
