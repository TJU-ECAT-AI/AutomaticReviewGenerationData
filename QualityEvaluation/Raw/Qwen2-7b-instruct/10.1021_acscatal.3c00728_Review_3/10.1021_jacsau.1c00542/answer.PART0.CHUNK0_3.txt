<EnglishVersionAnswer>
B-based materials featuring three-dimensional dispersed active sites within porous frameworks have been developed. These materials improve catalytic performance due to their unique structure. The study discusses various methodologies for integrating boron (B) active components into these structures, evaluating their effectiveness across different catalytic applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
