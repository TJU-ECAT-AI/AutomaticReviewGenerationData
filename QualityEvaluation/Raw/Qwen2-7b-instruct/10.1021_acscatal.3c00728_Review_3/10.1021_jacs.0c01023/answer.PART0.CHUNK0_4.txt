<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The spherical superstructure of boron nitride nanosheets derived from boron-containing metalorganic frameworks exhibits unique 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks. This feature enhances catalytic performance due to the incorporation of boron active components into these structures through various effective integration methods. The structural characteristics of boron-based materials significantly influence their catalytic applications.
