<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The coordination environment significantly influences the activity and stability of atomically dispersed Pt catalysts. Incorporating boron (B) active components into porous frameworks featuring 3-D dispersed active sites boosts catalytic performance. Different methods for integrating B active components into these structures have been assessed for various catalytic uses.
