<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks significantly enhance their catalytic performance due to the increased surface area and efficient diffusion of reactants. The incorporation of B active components into these structures can be achieved through various methods, including deposition, impregnation, and co-precipitation. These methods exhibit varying degrees of effectiveness depending on the specific catalytic application, with deposition often providing superior results for applications requiring high stability and selectivity. However, the selection of the most effective method for a particular application depends on factors such as the desired level of activity, stability, and cost-effectiveness.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

B基于材料在多维分散活性位点和孔性框架中的结构显著提高了其催化性能，由于增加了表面积和反应物的有效扩散。将B活性成分引入这些结构可以通过沉积、浸渍和共沉淀等多种方法实现。这些方法在不同特定催化应用中的有效性存在差异，其中沉积通常为需要高稳定性和选择性的应用提供了更优的结果。然而，对于特定应用选择最有效的方法取决于所需活性、稳定性和成本效益等因素。
