        The synthesis and analysis of boron-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks have been extensively studied for their enhanced catalytic performance, particularly in oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) processes like propane dehydrogenation. These materials, characterized by unique structural features such as increased surface area and improved mass transfer properties, significantly boost catalytic efficiency. Incorporation methods, including chemical vapor deposition (CVD) and impregnation, vary in their effectiveness for different catalytic applications, as highlighted in references \[10.1021/acscatal.9b02284\], \[10.1021/acs.oprd.8b00301\], and \[10.1021/acscatal.2c04889\].

        The catalytic advantages of these materials are rooted in their ability to anchor boron species in open coordination within the matrix, facilitated by hydrogen bonding between surface SiOH groups and BOH in defective boron species, which prevent boron leaching. Aggregated BOx species with BOH groups hydrogen bonding to SiOH are identified as the active center for low-temperature ODH reactions, as explained in reference \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\].

        The effectiveness of these materials in catalysis is further underscored by the study's finding that homogeneous boron distribution enhances catalytic properties, making borosilicate zeolites promising catalysts for ODH reactions, as noted in reference \[10.1021/jacs.2c12136\].

        The study also delves into the synthesis of antiexfoliating h-BNIn2O3 catalysts, combining the high-temperature stability of h-BN and the catalytic activity of In2O3. This method, detailed in reference \[10.1021/jacs.2c12136\], demonstrates the versatility of boron-based materials in catalytic applications.

        The coordination environment plays a crucial role in determining the activity and stability of atomically dispersed Pt catalysts, as discussed in reference \[10.1021/acscatal.9b04558\]. For instance, vapor-phase deposition techniques can effectively integrate boron into the framework while maintaining the desired 3-D dispersion, as opposed to chemical vapor infiltration, which offers better control over boron distribution but may result in less uniform dispersion.

        The influence of reactor parameters on the boron nitride-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation of propane is another area of focus, with studies revealing that the presence of BOH groups on h-BN and BNNT surfaces significantly enhances catalytic performance, serving as active sites in the ODH reaction, as outlined in reference \[10.1039/D0CS01174F\].

        The BS-1 catalyst, synthesized through a solvent-free crystallization strategy, exhibits an Si/B ratio of 62 post-calcination, and its hollow anatase TiO2 nanospheres were prepared using a modified sol-gel process, offering enhanced catalytic performance due to the large surface area provided by the porous framework and the dispersion of active sites, as described in reference \[10.1021/jp0377782\].

        The team's successful synthesis of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks, as reported by Xiao Jiang et al. in reference \[10.1021/jacsau.1c00542\], showcases the potential of these materials in catalysis, offering a promising avenue for enhanced catalytic efficiency and selectivity in various chemical transformations.

        In summary, boron-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks are pivotal in enhancing catalytic performance, particularly in ODH processes, through optimized structural characteristics and effective incorporation methods tailored to specific catalytic applications.

        在硼基材料中，具有三维分散活性位点的多孔框架结构在催化性能上表现出显著的优势，特别是在丙烷脱氢等氧化脱氢（ODH）反应中。这些材料的独特结构特征，如增加的比表面积和优化的质量传递性能，极大地提升了催化效率。不同的集成方法，包括化学气相沉积（CVD）和浸渍，对于不同催化应用的有效性各不相同，这在引用\[10.1021/acscatal.9b02284\]、\[10.1021/acs.oprd.8b00301\]和\[10.1021/acscatal.2c04889\]中得到了阐述。

        这些材料的催化优势在于它们能够将硼物种锚定在多孔框架内的开放配合物中，通过表面SiOH组与缺陷硼物种中的BOH之间的氢键作用，防止硼的流失。聚合的BOx物种中，带有BOH的组分通过氢键与SiOH相互作用，成为低温ODH反应中的活性中心，这一特点在引用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]中得到了详细说明。

        另一项研究探讨了高表面面积的硼化氮催化剂在丙烷脱氢过程中的作用，指出其展现出极高的选择性，特别是对于烯烃的形成，这在引用\[10.1039/D0CS01174F\]中得到了支持。

        在铂催化剂的活性和稳定性的影响因素中，研究人员发现硼基材料的结构特征对提高催化活性至关重要，例如，通过蒸汽沉积技术可以有效将硼集成到框架中，保持所需的三维分散，而化学气相渗透可能提供更好的硼分布控制，但可能导致分布不均匀，这一点在引用\[10.1021/acscatal.9b04558\]中被提及。

        对于丙烷脱氢反应中反应器参数的影响，研究表明，硼化氮表面的BOH组分显著提高了催化性能，作为ODH反应中的活性位点，这一特性在引用\[10.1039/D0CS01174F\]中得到了深入探讨。

        通过溶剂自由结晶策略合成的BS-1催化剂，其Si/B比为62，通过修改的水热法过程制备了具有空心的锐钛矿TiO2纳米球体，提供了大表面积以及活性位点的分散，从而优化了催化性能，这在引用\[10.1021/jp0377782\]中得到了详细描述。

        小乔等人的研究团队成功合成了具有三维分散活性位点的多孔框架结构的硼基材料，在引用\[10.1021/jacsau.1c00542\]中，他们展示了这些材料在催化领域的潜力，特别是在各种化学转化过程中的高效性和选择性提升。

        总之，具有三维分散活性位点的硼基材料在催化性能上发挥着关键作用，尤其是在ODH过程中，通过优化结构特性和针对特定催化应用的集成方法实现了显著的性能提升。
