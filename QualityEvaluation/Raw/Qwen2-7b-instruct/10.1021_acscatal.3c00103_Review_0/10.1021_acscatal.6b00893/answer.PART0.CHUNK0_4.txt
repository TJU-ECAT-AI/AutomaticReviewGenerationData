<EnglishVersionAnswer>
To address the increasing industrial demand for propylene and overcome limitations in traditional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques are being developed with innovative catalyst design strategies. These strategies aim to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
