<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address the challenges of increasing industrial demand for propylene and limitations of traditional production methods by offering an alternative process for propylene production. This method facilitates the manufacturing of essential petrochemical products such as polymers and solvents. Innovative catalyst design strategies aim to overcome deactivation issues, increase stability, and enhance recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
