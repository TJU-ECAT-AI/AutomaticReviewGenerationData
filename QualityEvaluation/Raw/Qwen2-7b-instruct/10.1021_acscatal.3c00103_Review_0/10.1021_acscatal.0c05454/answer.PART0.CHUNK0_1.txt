<EnglishVersionAnswer>
In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address these challenges by offering more efficient and selective methods for propylene production. Innovative catalyst design strategies, such as the rational design of single-atom-doped Ga2O3 catalysts, aim to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. By leveraging Lewis acid-base interactions, these catalysts optimize the electronic structure and coordination environment, leading to improved performance and selectivity towards propylene production.

在对丙烯日益增长的工业需求以及传统生产方法的局限性的背景下，丙烷脱氢（PDH）技术通过提供更高效和选择性的方法来应对这些挑战，以实现丙烯的生产。创新的催化剂设计策略，如单原子掺杂的Ga2O3催化剂的合理设计，旨在克服催化剂失活问题、提高稳定性和增强可回收性，同时在氧化性和非氧化性PDH过程中发挥作用。通过利用Lewis酸碱相互作用，这些催化剂优化了电子结构和配位环境，从而提高了性能和向丙烯的选择性生产。
