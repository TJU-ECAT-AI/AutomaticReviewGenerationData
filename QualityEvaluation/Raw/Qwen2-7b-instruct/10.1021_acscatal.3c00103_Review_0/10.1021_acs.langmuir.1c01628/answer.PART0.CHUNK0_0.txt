<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Sintering rate and mechanism of supported Pt nanoparticles were explored through multiscale simulation. The sintering rate was influenced by factors such as temperature, particle size, and support material properties. Innovative catalyst design strategies, including the use of novel support materials and optimized particle sizes, have been developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
