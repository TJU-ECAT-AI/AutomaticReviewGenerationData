To address the growing demand for propylene and overcome the limitations of conventional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques are employed. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being devised to tackle deactivation problems, increase stability, and boost recyclability in both oxidative and non-oxidative PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
