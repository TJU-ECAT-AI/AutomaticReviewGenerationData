<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques tackle the challenges of increased industrial demand for propylene and limitations of traditional production methods by converting propane into propylene more efficiently. Innovative catalyst design strategies aim to overcome deactivation problems, improve stability, and increase recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
