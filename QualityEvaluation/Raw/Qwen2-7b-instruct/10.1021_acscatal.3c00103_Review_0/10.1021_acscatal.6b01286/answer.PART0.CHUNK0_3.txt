<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH techniques address the growing need for propylene and the constraints of conventional production methods by employing advanced catalyst design strategies. These strategies aim to mitigate deactivation, increase stability, and optimize recyclability in both oxidative and non-oxidative PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
