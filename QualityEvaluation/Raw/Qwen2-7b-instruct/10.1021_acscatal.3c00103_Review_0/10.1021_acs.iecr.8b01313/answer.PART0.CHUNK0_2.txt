<EnglishVersionAnswer>
To address the growing demand for propylene and overcome limitations in traditional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques utilize innovative catalyst design strategies. These strategies aim to tackle issues such as deactivation, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes.
