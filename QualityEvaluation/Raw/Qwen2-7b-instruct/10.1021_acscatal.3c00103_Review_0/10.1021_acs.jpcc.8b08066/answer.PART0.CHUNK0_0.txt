<EnglishVersionAnswer>
In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route to produce propylene from propane. Traditional PDH processes often suffer from high energy consumption and environmental concerns, while also facing difficulties in catalyst deactivation and stability. Recent studies have focused on developing more efficient and environmentally friendly PDH catalysts, particularly metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) derived catalysts, which offer potential solutions to the aforementioned issues. These MOF-derived iron catalysts show promise in nonoxidative PDH processes due to their enhanced activity, selectivity, and stability compared to conventional catalysts. The development of these catalysts aims to overcome deactivation issues, improve stability, and increase recyclability, thereby addressing the challenges posed by traditional PDH processes and contributing to the sustainable production of propylene.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

在工业对丙烯日益增长的需求以及传统生产方法的局限性背景下，丙烷脱氢（PDH）技术通过提供从丙烷制备丙烯的替代途径来应对这些挑战。传统的PDH过程往往伴随着高能耗和环境问题，同时面临着催化剂失活和稳定性的问题。近年来的研究集中在开发更高效、更环保的PDH催化剂，特别是基于金属有机框架（MOF）的催化剂，这为解决上述问题提供了潜在解决方案。这些基于MOF的铁基催化剂在非氧化性PDH过程中表现出前景，因为它们与传统催化剂相比具有增强的活性、选择性和稳定性。开发这些催化剂旨在克服失活问题、提高稳定性并增加可回收性，从而解决传统PDH过程面临的挑战，并促进丙烯的可持续生产。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
