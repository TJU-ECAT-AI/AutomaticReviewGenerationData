<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to tackle deactivation issues, boost stability, and enhance recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes.

2. The future directions and challenges for PDH over heterogeneous catalysts are outlined for further advancements.

3. The volume of related research studies has escalated from 2291 to a growing number based on the Web of Science database over the past decade, spanning from 2010 to 2020.

4. The Oleflex and Catofin processes have seen widespread industrial implementation among the various PDH technologies.

5. There is a high demand for alternative metal oxide catalysts with lower or no chromium content.

6. The propylene formation rates for the most Pt-based catalysts are around 5 mol gpt1 h1 at a deactivation rate of 0.05 h1.

7. CrOx-based catalysts demonstrate high reactivity and selectivity, but exhibit low stability, with deactivation rates reaching 1 mol gCr1 h1 or 1 mol gcat1 h1 at a deactivation rate of 0.5 h1.

8. VOx-based catalysts offer superior reactivity and reduced deactivation rates compared to CrOx-based catalysts, with formation rates of about 0.3 mol gV1 h1 at a deactivation rate of 0.2 h1.

9. GaOx-based catalysts have been intensively studied for PDH reactions. Both bulk and supported GaOx show activity and enhanced stability in PDH, with formation rates of 0.1 mol gGa1 h1 or 0.1 mol gcat1 h1 at a low deactivation rate of 0.03 h1.

10. ZrO2-based catalysts display superior dehydrogenation activity, though careful management of over-reduction is essential to prevent detrimental effects.

11. The need for improvements in activity and propylene selectivity, along with robust stability during dehydrogenation and regeneration phases, is highlighted as key areas for future catalyst development.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1. 正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，以解决氧化性和非氧化性PDH过程中出现的失活问题、增强稳定性并提高可回收性。

2. 对于PDH在异质催化剂上的未来方向和挑战进行了概述，以促进进一步的发展。

3. 基于Web of Science数据库的数据显示，自2010年至2020年间，相关研究论文数量从2291篇逐渐增长。

4. Oleflex和Catofin过程已经在各种PDH技术中广泛实施。

5. 对于PDH而言，希望开发出含铬量较低或无铬的金属氧化物催化剂。

6. 最佳铂基催化剂在失活率为0.05 h1时，丙烯形成率约为5 mol gpt1 h1。

7. CrOx基催化剂表现出高活性和选择性，但稳定性低，失活速率可达1 mol gCr1 h1或1 mol gcat1 h1，在失活率为0.5 h1时。

8. 与CrOx基催化剂相比，VOx基催化剂在保持高活性的同时降低了失活速率，形成速率为约0.3 mol gV1 h1在失活率为0.2 h1。

9. 对于PDH反应，GaOx基催化剂被深入研究。无论是块体还是支持型GaOx，在PDH中均展现出活性和增强稳定性，形成速率为0.1 mol gGa1 h1或0.1 mol gcat1 h1在低失活率为0.03 h1。

10. ZrO2基催化剂在脱氢作用上表现优异，但应谨慎管理过度还原，以避免负面影响。

11. 提高活性和丙烯选择性，以及在脱氢和再生阶段的稳定性的改进是未来催化剂开发的关键领域。
