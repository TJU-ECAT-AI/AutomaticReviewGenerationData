            <p>Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) offers a viable alternative for propylene production, tackling increasing industrial demand and limitations in traditional methods. Innovations in catalyst design, such as the use of late transition metals, main-group metals, and rare-earth metal oxides, enhance stability and recyclability. In oxidative PDH, alloying can weaken propylene binding energy, while in nonoxidative PDH, embedding reactive Rh atoms alters dehydrogenation pathways. These strategies address catalyst deactivation challenges, improving efficiency and flexibility in propylene production.</p>
            <p>Specifically, the utilization of catalysts like Pt-modified ZnO/Al2O3, supported vanadium catalysts on high-silica HZSM-5 zeolites, and well-defined supported vanadium oxide catalysts exemplify cutting-edge developments. These catalysts are designed to maintain high activity levels while minimizing the risk of agglomeration and deactivation, extending the lifetime of the catalysts. Furthermore, the exploration of single-atom-doped Ga2O3 catalysts and the application of multiscale simulation to understand the sintering rate and mechanism of supported Pt nanoparticles have contributed to the advancement of catalyst design strategies.</p>

            <p>基于化石原油储量减少以及天然气和页岩气利用的发展，丙烷脱氢（PDH）成为一种有前景的丙烯生产替代方案。在催化剂设计创新方面，如使用晚期过渡金属、主族金属和稀土氧化物等，提高了稳定性和可回收性。在氧化PDH中，合金化可以削弱丙烯结合能，而在非氧化PDH中，嵌入活性Rh原子改变脱氢路径。这些策略解决了催化剂失活挑战，提高了丙烯生产的效率和灵活性。</p>
            <p>具体而言，利用如Pt修饰的ZnO/Al2O3、高硅酸盐HZSM-5上的支持型钒催化剂和单原子掺杂的Ga2O3催化剂等先进催化剂，体现了前沿发展。这些催化剂旨在维持高水平的活性，同时最小化聚集和失活的风险，从而延长催化剂寿命。此外，对支持的Pt纳米粒子的烧结速率和机制进行多尺度模拟的探索，也有助于催化剂设计策略的进步。</p>
            <p>根据引用的文献 \[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]、\[10.1021/acscatal.6b00893\]、\[10.1021/acscatal.0c05454\]、\[10.1021/catcom.2009.09.011\]、\[10.1021/acscatal.0c05009\]、\[10.1021/an/e110316z\]、\[10.1021/jpcc.7b00972\]、\[10.1021/jpcc.8b04262\]、\[10.1021/jpcc.8b04879\] 和 \[10.1021/jpcc.8b06046\]，我们可以看到，该领域的进步通过开发更稳定、选择性和高效的催化剂而实现。这些催化剂旨在解决催化剂失活问题，增强稳定性，并提高可回收性，从而提供一个可持续的丙烯生产解决方案。</p>
            <p>展望未来，研究重点将转向进一步优化催化剂设计，探索新型材料的应用，并优化操作条件以实现更高的丙烯产率和降低能耗。目标是使PDH成为一个更具竞争力且环境友好的丙烯生产方法，促进可持续增长的石化工业。</p>
