By utilizing the abundance of propane, especially in regions with significant shale gas production, PDH processes become more commercially attractive.\n\nTraditional catalysts, such as those based on platinum (Pt) and chromium oxide (CrOx), are restricted by their high cost and toxicity. In contrast, supported vanadium catalysts are considered a viable alternative due to their high activity, proper propylene selectivity, and excellent regeneration performance.
