<EnglishVersionAnswer>
To tackle the rising industrial demand for propylene and the constraints of conventional production methods, scientists are innovating in catalyst design for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) processes. These innovations target overcoming deactivation challenges, boosting stability, and enhancing recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
