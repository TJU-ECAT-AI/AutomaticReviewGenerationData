Experimental techniques such as HREELS and AES have been utilized to measure the oxygen-induced surface segregation kinetics and activation barriers of subsurface Ni and Co atoms in Pt-3d-Pt(111) bimetallic systems. These measurements allow for a comparison with DFT predictions, enabling a quantitative assessment of their agreement.
