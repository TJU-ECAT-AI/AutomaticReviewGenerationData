<quotes>
[1] "The Journal of Physical Chemistry CVol 113/Issue 42Article Cite Share Jump to Expand ARTICLESeptember 25, 2009 Probing Selectivity over PtSn Catalysts in Reactions of n-C6 Hydrocarbons: Adsorption and Reactivity of n-Hexane, 1-Hexene, and 1,5-Hexadiene on Pt(111) and Sn/Pt(111) Surface Alloys"
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This research investigates how the addition of tin (Sn) to platinum (Pt) catalysts affects the adsorption and reactivity of linear C6 hydrocarbons. The focus is on n-hexane, 1-hexene, and 1,5-hexadiene on Pt(111) and Sn/Pt(111) surface alloys. The authors use temperature-programmed desorption (TPD) and Auger electron spectroscopy (AES) to analyze the adsorption, desorption, and reactive chemistry of these molecules.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这项研究探讨了锡（Sn）添加到铂（Pt）催化剂上对线性C6烃反应的影响。重点是n-己烷、1-己烯和1,5-己二烯在Pt(111)和Sn/Pt(111)表面合金上的吸附和反应性。作者使用温度程序脱附（TPD）和Auger电子能谱（AES）来分析这些分子的吸附、脱附和反应性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
