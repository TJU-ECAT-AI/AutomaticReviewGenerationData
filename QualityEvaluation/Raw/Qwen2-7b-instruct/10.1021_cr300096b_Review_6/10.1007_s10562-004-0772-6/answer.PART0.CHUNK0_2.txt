In addition, we place a lower limit of E dehyd* 97 kJ mol on the barrier to ethyl hydrogenation on this alloy.

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The activation energy for ethyl dehydrogenation on the Sn/Pt(111) surface alloy is 97 kJ/mol, which is twice the value reported for Pt(111). Additionally, a lower limit of 97 kJ/mol is set for the barrier to ethyl hydrogenation on this alloy.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
