<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Experimental techniques such as HREELS and AES have been used to verify and quantify the oxygen-induced surface segregation of subsurface 3d atoms, particularly Ni and Co, in Pt-3d-Pt(111) bimetallic systems. The literature indicates that alloying does not significantly affect the selectivity towards the formation of unsaturated alcohols compared to the presence of an additional terminal methyl group.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
实验技术，如HREELS和AES，被用来验证并量化氧气诱导的表面亚表面3d原子（特别是Ni和Co）在Pt-3d-Pt(111)双金属系统中的表面分布。文献表明，合金化对形成不饱和醇的选择性影响不大，与额外的终端甲基团的存在相比。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
