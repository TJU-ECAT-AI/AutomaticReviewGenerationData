<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines the impact of crystal planes on the activity of oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) and the stability of subsurface 3d metal atoms within Pt3dPt(111) and Pt3dPt(100) bimetallic systems under oxygen exposure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
本研究探讨了晶面对于氧还原反应（ORR）活性以及氧暴露下Pt3dPt(111)和Pt3dPt(100)双金属体系中亚表面3d金属原子稳定性的效应。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Experimental techniques like HREELS (High Resolution Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy) and AES (Auger Electron Spectroscopy) were used to validate and quantify the oxygen-induced surface segregation of subsurface 3d atoms, particularly nickel (Ni) and cobalt (Co), in Pt-3d-Pt(111) bimetallic systems. The measured segregation kinetics and activation barriers were compared with DFT (Density Functional Theory) predictions for these systems.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
使用HREELS（高分辨率电子能量损失光谱）和AES（俄歇电子能谱）等实验技术验证并量化了氧诱导的表面亚表面3d原子，特别是镍（Ni）和钴（Co），在Pt-3d-Pt(111)双金属体系中的表面分离。测量的分离动力学和激活势垒与这些系统的DFT（密度泛函理论）预测进行了比较。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
