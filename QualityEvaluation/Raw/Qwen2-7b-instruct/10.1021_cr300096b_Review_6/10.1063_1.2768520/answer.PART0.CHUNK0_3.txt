<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations were conducted using the Vienna Ab initio Simulation Package (VASP).
2. The experimental methods employed in this research consist of Temperature Programmed Desorption (TPD) and High Resolution Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy (HREELS).
3. TPD experiments were executed with a helium gas flow at room temperature, followed by heating to temperatures between 200°C and 700°C.
4. HREELS measurements were taken within a kinetic energy spectrum from 0.5 eV to 5 eV.
5. The TPD data revealed greater reaction yields for the Pt-Co-Pt(111) surface than for the pure Pt-Pt(111) surface.
6. Contrarily, the HREELS outcomes displayed distinct surface intermediates for the Pt-Co-Pt(111) and Co-Pt-Pt(111) surfaces.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 密度泛函理论（DFT）计算使用维也纳原位模拟包（VASP）进行。
2. 本研究采用的实验方法包括温度程序脱附（TPD）和高分辨率电子能量损失光谱（HREELS）。
3. TPD 实验在氦气流中于室温下进行，随后加热至200°C至700°C之间。
4. 在0.5 eV到5 eV的动能范围内收集了HREELS谱图。
5. 对于Pt-Co-Pt(111)系统，TPD结果表明更高的反应产率比纯Pt-Pt(111)表面。
6. 相反，HREELS结果显示了Pt-Co-Pt(111)和Co-Pt-Pt(111)表面的不同表面中间体。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
