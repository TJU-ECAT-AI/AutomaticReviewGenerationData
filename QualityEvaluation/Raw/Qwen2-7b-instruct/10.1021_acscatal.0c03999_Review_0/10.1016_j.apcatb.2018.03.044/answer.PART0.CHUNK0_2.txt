<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The primary challenge in current propylene production methods is the high energy consumption required for the steam cracking process. The oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses this issue by offering a more energy-efficient alternative. However, ODHP presents its own set of challenges, particularly related to the selection of oxidant and the specific challenges associated with using oxygen.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
