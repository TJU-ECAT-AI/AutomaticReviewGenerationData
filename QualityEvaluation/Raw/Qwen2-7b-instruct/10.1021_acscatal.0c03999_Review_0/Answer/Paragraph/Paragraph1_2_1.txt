        Challenges in propylene production include high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene, as identified in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. The oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) is presented as an attractive alternative, offering reduced energy consumption and CO2 emissions, as highlighted in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. However, the selection of an appropriate oxidant remains a significant challenge, particularly in relation to the use of pure oxygen which can lead to unwanted byproducts such as CO and CO2, as noted in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. Carbon dioxide (CO2) can be utilized as an oxidant, potentially enhancing propylene selectivity, as suggested in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. The efficiency of the ODHP process is influenced by factors such as temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties, as observed in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3].

        In contrast to conventional methods, ODHP utilizes a more economical feedstock, such as shale gas, and produces pure olefins without the need for costly FCC or steam cracking processes, as documented in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. Despite these advantages, challenges remain, especially concerning the selection of an appropriate oxidant. The use of oxygen as an oxidant is advantageous due to its abundance and low cost, yet non-oxygen-based systems may offer better performance in terms of avoiding coke deposit formation and maintaining catalyst activity and selectivity, as analyzed in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are studied for their potential in improving the performance of ODHP processes, as emphasized in [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052], [10.1002/cctc.201200913], [10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044], [10.1021/cr5002436], and [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3].

        The TUD-1 material, due to its unique three-dimensional mesoporous network structure, offers improved catalytic activity by providing high substrate accessibility and facilitating the formation of a titanium-enriched phase on its surface, as reported in [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. Microwave synthesis techniques enhance the incorporation of hetero-atoms, increase hydrophobicity, and improve thermal stability, as detailed in [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3]. These studies collectively suggest that ODHP has the potential to revolutionize propylene production by addressing key challenges, although further advancements in oxidant selection and catalyst optimization are needed to fully realize its benefits.

The potential for using CO2 as a soft oxidant, for instance, opens new avenues for reducing energy consumption and CO2 emissions, warranting further investigation.

        In summary, ODHP presents a promising avenue for propylene production, offering a more sustainable and economically viable pathway compared to traditional methods.         从参考文献 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 的深入研究中建立了详细的分析。通过参考文献 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 合成了综合理解。

        当前丙烯生产面临的主要挑战包括高能耗、高资本成本和丙烯选择性低，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中所述。氧化丙烷脱氢（ODHP）被看作是一个有吸引力的选择，它能降低能耗并减少二氧化碳排放，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中强调。然而，在适当氧化剂的选择上仍存在重大挑战，尤其是使用纯氧可能导致形成不希望的副产品，如CO和CO2，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中指出。二氧化碳（CO2）可以作为氧化剂使用，可能有助于提高丙烯选择性，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中提到。ODHP过程的效率受温度、压力和催化剂性质等因素的影响，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中观察到。

        相比传统方法，ODHP利用更经济的原料，如页岩气，并且无需昂贵的FCC或蒸汽裂化过程就能生成纯烯烃，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中所述。尽管这些优势，挑战仍然存在，尤其是在适当氧化剂的选择上。使用氧气作为氧化剂具有成本低廉和丰富性优势，但非氧气基系统可能在避免焦炭沉积形成、维持催化剂活性和选择性方面表现更好，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中分析。支持的K2O/WO3/Al2O3催化剂被研究用于改善ODHP过程的性能，如在 [10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052]、[10.1002/cctc.201200913]、[10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044]、[10.1021/cr5002436] 和 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中强调。

        TUD-1材料，由于其独特的三维介孔网络结构，提供了更高的底物可接近性和促进了表面钛富集相的形成，从而提高了催化活性，如在 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中报告。微波合成技术增强了杂原子的整合，增加了疏水性，提高了热稳定性，如在 [10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3] 中详细说明。这些研究表明，ODHP有可能彻底改变丙烯生产方式，提供比传统方法更可持续和经济高效的途径。然而，进一步开发高级催化剂和氧化剂系统对于实现ODHP技术的全部潜力至关重要，这将为石油化学行业开辟更加清洁和高效的发展道路。

        总结起来，ODHP为丙烯生产提供了一个有前景的选择，它提供了与传统方法相比更具可持续性和经济效益的途径。在未来的研究中，优化催化剂以提高选择性和效率、开发更高效的氧化剂以及调整工艺参数以实现更高的丙烯产率并减少环境影响将是关键。例如，使用CO2作为软氧化剂的可能性为减少能耗和二氧化碳排放开辟了新的途径，值得进一步研究。
