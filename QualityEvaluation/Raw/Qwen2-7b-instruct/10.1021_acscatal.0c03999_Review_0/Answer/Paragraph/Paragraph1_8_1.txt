        Challenges in propylene production methods include high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient and cost-effective method for propylene production, contrasting with conventional methods that suffer from high energy consumption and environmental impacts. However, selecting an appropriate oxidant remains a significant challenge in ODHP, with pure oxygen leading to the formation of unwanted byproducts like CO and CO2, and carbon dioxide (CO2) as an oxidant offering potential advantages in enhancing propylene selectivity. The efficiency of the ODHP process is influenced by various factors including temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties.

        The use of oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP is advantageous due to its abundance and low cost, making it a promising choice for reducing energy consumption and CO2 emissions. However, non-oxygen-based systems may offer better performance in terms of avoiding coke deposit formation and maintaining catalyst activity and selectivity. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are studied for their potential in improving the performance of ODHP processes.

        The challenges in current propylene production methods are high energy consumption, low selectivity towards specific olefins, diminishing petroleum reserves, and rising oil prices. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) tackles these issues by utilizing a more economical feedstock, shale gas, and generating pure olefins without the need for expensive FCC or steam cracking processes. Ethane, a relatively inexpensive natural gas liquid, has led to a shift from traditional naphtha-based steam cracking to ethane cracking in the US, with ethane crackers being established and naphtha crackers either dismantled or repurposed.

        The use of carbon dioxide as a soft oxidant in ODHP can mitigate the issues related to process flammability and poor product selectivity due to over-oxidation. This provides a safer and more readily available alternative to oxygen. Chromium-loaded catalysts, with their activity determined by the nature of chromium species on the support surface, are particularly effective in ODHP processes.         Propylene is one of the most important feedstocks in the chemical and petrochemical industries, serving as a versatile starting material for the synthesis of numerous commodity chemicals. Oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) provides a favorable alternative to conventional methods due to its low energy requirement, no catalyst regeneration obligation, and the elimination of thermodynamic limitations. However, the presence of O2 in the ODH process may lead to process flammability and the production of carbon oxides due to over-oxidation, resulting in poor process selectivity. This problem can be solved by employing a much safer, milder, and readily available carbon dioxide as a soft oxidant. The issue of CO2 emission has become one of the main subjects in environmental chemistry, and interestingly, CO2 as a soft oxidant has also been reported to shift the equilibrium to the product side, promoting dehydrogenation through the reaction coupling between a simple dehydrogenation of propane and the reverse water-gas shift reaction. However, designing a better catalytic system with high product yield and good propylene selectivity remains a challenging task due to the tendency of the formed alkenes towards further oxidation.

        现有丙烯生产方法面临的挑战包括高能耗、高资本成本和丙烯选择性低。氧化脱氢化丙烷（ODHP）通过提供一种更节能、成本效益更高的丙烯生产方法来解决这些问题，与传统方法相比，后者存在高能耗和环境影响的问题。然而，在ODHP中选择合适的氧化剂仍然是一个重大挑战，纯氧可能导致形成CO和CO2等不需要的副产品，而二氧化碳（CO2）作为氧化剂在提高丙烯选择性方面可能具有优势。ODHP过程的效率受到温度、压力和催化剂性质等因素的影响。

        在ODHP中使用氧气作为氧化剂因其丰富性和低成本而具有优势，使其成为减少能耗和CO2排放的有前景的选择。然而，非氧气基系统可能在避免焦炭沉积形成、保持催化剂活性和选择性方面表现更好。研究了支持型K2O/WO3/Al2O3催化剂的潜力，以提高ODHP过程的性能。

        当前丙烯生产方法面临的主要挑战包括高能耗、特定烯烃选择性低、石油储备减少和油价上涨。ODHP通过利用更经济的原料，即页岩气，并且无需昂贵的FCC或蒸汽裂解过程就能产生纯净的烯烃，解决了这些问题。乙烷，一种相对便宜的天然气液体，导致了美国从基于石脑油的蒸汽裂解转向乙烷裂解，使得乙烷裂解器得到建立，而石脑油裂解器要么被拆卸，要么被重新配置。

        在ODHP中使用二氧化碳作为软氧化剂可以缓解与过氧化导致的工艺可燃性和产品选择性差相关的问题。这提供了比氧气更安全、更容易获取的替代方案。铬负载催化剂，其活性由支持表面上的铬物种的性质决定，特别适用于ODHP过程。TUD-1材料由于其独特的三维介孔网络结构，提供了改善的催化活性，通过提供高底物可接近性并促进钛富集相在其表面上的形成，提高了催化活性。微波合成技术增强了异质原子的掺杂、增加了疏水性，并提高了热稳定性。

        丙烯是化学和石油化工行业最重要的原料之一，是合成大量商品化学品（如异丙醇、丙烯腈、丙烯酸、丙烯氧化物、丁醛和cumene）以及非常复杂的分子（如聚丙烯、塑料和油漆）的通用起始材料。氧化脱氢化（ODH）为传统方法提供了有利的替代方案，因为它具有低能量需求、无需催化剂再生义务以及消除热力学限制的优点。然而，在ODH过程中，氧气的存在可能导致过程可燃性和因过氧化而导致的产品选择性差。这可以通过采用更安全、温和、易于获取的二氧化碳作为软氧化剂来解决。二氧化碳排放问题已成为当今环境化学的重要议题，有趣的是，二氧化碳作为软氧化剂也被报道能够使平衡向产物侧移动，并通过将简单的丙烷脱氢与逆向水煤气变换反应耦合来促进脱氢。然而，设计具有高产品产率和良好丙烯选择性的更好的催化系统仍然是一个具有挑战性的任务，因为形成的烯烃倾向于进一步氧化。
