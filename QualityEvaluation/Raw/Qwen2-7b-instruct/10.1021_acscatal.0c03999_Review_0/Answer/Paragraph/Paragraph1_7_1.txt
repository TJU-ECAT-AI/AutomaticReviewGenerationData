        Challenges in propylene production are primarily associated with high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene. These issues contrast with the more energy-efficient and cost-effective methods offered by oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP), which can significantly reduce energy consumption and CO2 emissions compared to conventional production methods.

        ODHP presents its own set of advantages and limitations, particularly in the context of oxidant selection. Oxygen is an abundant and inexpensive oxidant, making it an attractive choice. However, its use can lead to the formation of unwanted byproducts such as CO and CO2. Carbon dioxide (CO2) can be used as an oxidant, offering certain advantages. Its use can potentially enhance the selectivity towards propylene, but the efficiency of the process is affected by factors such as temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties.

        In terms of oxidant selection, non-oxygen-based systems may offer better performance in avoiding coke deposit formation and maintaining catalyst activity and selectivity. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are studied for their potential in improving the performance of ODHP processes. The TUD-1 material, with its unique three-dimensional mesoporous network structure, offers improved catalytic activity by providing high substrate accessibility and facilitating the formation of a titanium-enriched phase on its surface. Microwave synthesis techniques enhance the incorporation of hetero-atoms, increase hydrophobicity, and improve thermal stability.

Using pure oxygen as an oxidant can lead to the formation of unwanted byproducts, while carbon dioxide (CO2) can be used with potential advantages in enhancing propylene selectivity. The efficiency of the ODHP process is influenced by various factors including temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties.

        当前丙烯生产的主要挑战包括高能耗、高资本成本和丙烯选择性低。这些问题与氧化脱氢法（ODHP）提供的更节能、成本效益更高的生产方法形成鲜明对比，ODHP能够显著降低能源消耗和二氧化碳排放量，相比传统方法。

        ODHP具有自己的优势和局限性，特别是在氧化剂选择方面。氧气是一种丰富且低成本的氧化剂，使其成为有吸引力的选择。然而，其使用可能导致有害副产品的形成，如CO和CO2。二氧化碳（CO2）可以作为氧化剂使用，提供一定的优势。其使用可能增强丙烯的选择性，但过程效率受到温度、压力和催化剂性质等因素的影响。

        在氧化剂选择方面，非氧气基系统可能在避免焦炭沉积形成和保持催化剂活性和选择性方面提供更好的性能。研究支持K2O/WO3/Al2O3催化剂的潜力以提高ODHP过程的性能。TUD-1材料的独特三维介孔网络结构提供了改善的催化活性，通过提供高的底物可接近性和促进钛富集相在其表面上的形成来提高活性。微波合成技术增强了异质原子的掺杂、增加了疏水性并提高了热稳定性。

        目前丙烯生产方法中的主要挑战，如高能耗、高资本成本和低丙烯选择性，由ODHP解决。然而，合适的氧化剂的选择仍然是一个重大挑战。使用纯氧作为氧化剂可能导致有害副产品的形成，而使用二氧化碳（CO2）在增强丙烯选择性方面可能具有优势。ODHP过程的效率受温度、压力和催化剂性质等多因素影响。

        将来研究可能关注探索现有挑战的新兴领域和创新方法。潜在路径包括开发更高效的催化剂，可在较低温度下运行同时保持高丙烯选择性；调查使用可再生能源进行丙烯生产的可能性，进一步减少环境影响；通过新颖的材料和催化剂设计增强丙烯产率和选择性；优化过程参数如温度、压力和反应物比例以最大化丙烯产率；探索将ODHP与其他化学过程集成以创建更加循环和可持续的生产系统。
