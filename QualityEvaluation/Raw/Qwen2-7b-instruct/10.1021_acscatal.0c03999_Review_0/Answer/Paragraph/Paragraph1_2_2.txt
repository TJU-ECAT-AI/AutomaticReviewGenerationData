        The challenges in current propylene production methods are primarily high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient and cost-effective method for propylene production. This alternative contrasts with the high energy demands and environmental impact of conventional methods such as steam cracking and fluidic catalytic cracking. The use of oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP is advantageous due to its abundance and low cost, but non-oxygen-based systems may offer better performance in terms of avoiding coke deposit formation and maintaining catalyst activity and selectivity. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are explored to enhance the performance of ODHP processes.

        Carbon dioxide (CO2), as a soft oxidant, has shown potential in enhancing propylene selectivity by shifting the equilibrium to the product side and promoting dehydrogenation through the reaction coupling between simple dehydrogenation of propane and the reverse water-gas shift reaction. However, designing a better catalytic system with high product yield and good propylene selectivity remains a challenge, given the tendency of formed alkenes towards further oxidation. The use of bromine in mixtures with low iodine content (up to 20%) has been shown to achieve up to 80% single-pass yield of C3H6+C3H7X at moderate reaction temperatures, demonstrating significant improvements over previous yields.

        The oxidative dehydrogenation of propane offers a potential solution to reduce energy consumption and CO2 emissions in propylene production, making it an attractive alternative. Despite this, the selection of an appropriate oxidant, particularly pure oxygen, can lead to the formation of unwanted byproducts such as CO and CO2. Carbon dioxide can be used with potential advantages in enhancing propylene selectivity, although the efficiency of the process is affected by factors such as temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties.

        The challenges in propylene production are further exacerbated by diminishing petroleum reserves, rising oil prices, and the need for more sustainable feedstocks. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, utilizing a more economical feedstock like shale gas, promises to produce pure olefins without the need for costly FCC or steam cracking processes. Ethane, a relatively inexpensive natural gas liquid, has facilitated a shift from naphtha-based steam cracking to ethane cracking in the United States, leading to the establishment of ethane crackers and the dismantling or repurposing of naphtha crackers.

        The TUD-1 material, with its unique three-dimensional mesoporous network structure, offers improved catalytic activity for ODHP processes by providing high substrate accessibility and facilitating the formation of a titanium-enriched phase on its surface. Microwave synthesis techniques enhance the incorporation of hetero-atoms, increase hydrophobicity, and improve thermal stability, contributing to the overall performance of the catalytic system.

        In summary, oxidative dehydrogenation of propane presents a promising avenue for propylene production, offering solutions to the primary challenges faced by current methods. However, the development of a more efficient, selective, and robust catalytic system remains critical for realizing the full potential of ODHP as a viable and sustainable alternative in the chemical and petrochemical industries.

        当前丙烯生产方法的主要挑战包括高能耗、高昂的资本成本以及丙烯的选择性低。氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）通过提供一种更节能、成本效益更高的丙烯生产方法来解决这些问题，与传统方法如蒸汽裂化和流化催化裂化过程相比，这种替代方法具有明显的优越性。使用氧气作为ODHP的氧化剂因其丰富性和低成本而具有优势，但非氧气基系统可能在避免焦炭沉积形成、保持催化剂活性和选择性方面表现更好。研究了支持K2O/WO3/Al2O3催化剂以提高ODHP过程的性能。

        二氧化碳（CO2），作为一种软氧化剂，显示出通过将简单丙烷脱氢反应与逆向水煤气变换反应耦合，促进丙烷脱氢并使平衡向产物侧移动的可能性，从而增强丙烯选择性的潜力。然而，设计一个能够实现高产品产率和良好丙烯选择性的更好催化剂体系仍然是一个挑战，因为形成的烯烃倾向于进一步氧化。通过将溴与碘混合（碘含量不超过20%），在适中的反应温度下实现了高达80%的C3H6+C3H7X单次转化率，这代表了之前产量的重大改进。

        丙烯氧化脱氢提供了减少丙烯生产过程中能耗和二氧化碳排放量的潜在解决方案，使其成为有吸引力的替代方法。尽管如此，选择合适的氧化剂，尤其是纯氧，可能导致CO和CO2等副产品的生成。二氧化碳可以利用其安全、易得且成本低廉的优势，在提高丙烯选择性方面带来优势，尽管过程效率受到温度、压力和催化剂性质等因素的影响。

        随着石油储量的减少、油价上涨以及对可持续原料的需求增加，丙烯生产面临的挑战更为严峻。利用经济型原料如页岩气进行氧化脱氢丙烷，承诺生产纯烯烃而不必依赖昂贵的FCC或蒸汽裂化过程。乙烷，作为相对便宜的天然气液体，已经促进了美国从基于石脑油的蒸汽裂化转向乙烷裂化，导致乙烷裂化装置的建立以及石脑油裂化装置的拆卸或再利用。

        TUD-1材料，由于其独特的三维微孔网络结构，为ODHP过程提供了改善的催化活性，通过提供高底物可接近性并促进表面钛富集相的形成在其表面上，提高了催化剂的活性。微波合成技术增强了异质原子的掺杂、增加了疏水性，并提高了热稳定性，从而对整个催化系统的性能做出了贡献。

        总之，丙烷氧化脱氢提供了丙烯生产的一个有前景的途径，解决了当前方法面临的主要挑战。然而，为了充分利用ODHP作为可持续和可行的替代方案的潜力，开发更高效、选择性和稳定的催化剂体系至关重要。
