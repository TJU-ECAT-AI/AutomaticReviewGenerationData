        The challenges in current propylene production methods, as highlighted in studies \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\], include high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene. These issues contrast with the more energy-efficient and cost-effective oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) method, which, according to \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\], has the potential to reduce energy consumption and CO2 emissions. However, the selection of an appropriate oxidant remains a significant challenge in ODHP, as detailed in \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

        Using pure oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP can lead to the formation of unwanted byproducts such as CO and CO2, as reported in \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]. Carbon dioxide (CO2), however, as an oxidant, may offer certain advantages, particularly in enhancing the selectivity towards propylene, as noted in \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

        The efficiency of the ODHP process is influenced by factors such as temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties, as explained in \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

        The use of bromine in combination with iodine, with low iodine content, has been shown to achieve up to 80% of C3H6+C3H7X single-pass yield at moderate reaction temperatures, as demonstrated in \[10.1002/cctc.201200913\].

        To address the challenges of process flammability and poor product selectivity due to over-oxidation in ODHP, the use of carbon dioxide as a soft oxidant is suggested as a safer and more readily available alternative, as indicated in \[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

        The potential for developing a low-temperature, high-yield propylene technology through the use of optimized catalysts and process conditions, as discussed in \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\], offers promising avenues for future research and development.

    <Chinese>
        当前丙烯生产方法面临的主要挑战包括高能耗、高资本成本和低丙烯选择性，如文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]所述。相比之下，氧化脱氢法（ODHP）为丙烯生产提供了一种更节能、更经济的方法，具有降低能耗和减少二氧化碳排放的潜力，正如文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]所指出的。

        在ODHP中使用纯氧作为氧化剂可能导致形成CO和CO2等副产品，文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]表明。然而，二氧化碳（CO2）作为一种氧化剂，可能在提高丙烯选择性方面提供某些优势，正如文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]所述。

        ODHP过程的效率受到温度、压力和催化剂性质等因素的影响，如文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]所述。

        文献\[10.1002/cctc.201200913\]显示，通过使用低碘含量的溴与碘的混合物，在适中的反应温度下可实现高达80%的C3H6+C3H7X单次转化率。

        为了应对过氧化导致的工艺易燃性和产品选择性差的问题，文献\[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]建议将二氧化碳作为安全且易于获取的软氧化剂，以解决这些问题。

        通过优化催化剂和工艺条件开发低温高产丙烯技术的潜力，如文献\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2017.08.052, 10.1002/cctc.201200913, 10.1016/j.apcatb.2018.03.044, 10.1021/cr5002436, 10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]所述，为未来的研发提供了有希望的方向。
