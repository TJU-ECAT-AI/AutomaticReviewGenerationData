        The challenges in current propylene production methods include high energy consumption, high capital costs, and low selectivity to propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient and cost-effective method for propylene production. However, choosing an appropriate oxidant, particularly pure oxygen, can lead to the formation of unwanted byproducts such as CO and CO2, while carbon dioxide (CO2) can be used with potential advantages in enhancing propylene selectivity. The efficiency of the ODHP process is influenced by various factors including temperature, pressure, and catalyst properties. Non-oxygen-based systems may offer better performance in terms of avoiding coke deposit formation and maintaining catalyst activity and selectivity. Supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts are studied for their potential in improving the performance of ODHP processes. Carbon dioxide as a soft oxidant shifts the equilibrium to the product side, promoting the dehydrogenation through the reaction coupling between simple dehydrogenation of propane and the reverse water-gas shift reaction. Despite these advantages, designing a better catalytic system with high product yield and good propylene selectivity remains a challenge due to the tendency of formed alkenes towards further oxidation. The use of bromine and iodine mixtures with low iodine content shows promise in achieving up to 80% single-pass yield of C3H6+C3H7X at moderate reaction temperatures, offering a significant improvement over previous yields.

        当前丙烯生产方法面临的挑战包括高能耗、高昂资本成本和丙烯选择性低。氧化脱氢法（ODHP）通过提供更节能、成本效益更高的丙烯生产方法来解决这些问题。然而，选择合适的氧化剂，尤其是纯氧，可能导致CO和CO2等副产品的形成，而使用二氧化碳（CO2）可能在提高丙烯选择性方面具有优势。ODHP过程的效率受到温度、压力和催化剂性质等多种因素的影响。非氧基系统可能在避免焦炭沉积、保持催化剂活性和选择性方面表现出更好的性能。研究了支持型K2O/WO3/Al2O3催化剂用于改善ODHP过程性能的潜力。二氧化碳作为软氧化剂，可以通过简单丙烷脱氢反应与水煤气逆向反应耦合，推动平衡向产品侧移动，从而促进脱氢。尽管存在这些优势，设计能够实现高产物产率和良好丙烯选择性的更好催化剂仍是一个挑战，因为形成的烯烃倾向于进一步氧化。将溴和碘以低碘含量混合物使用的实验显示，在适中的反应温度下，可以实现高达80%的C3H6+C3H7X单次通过产率，这代表了之前产率的重大改进。
