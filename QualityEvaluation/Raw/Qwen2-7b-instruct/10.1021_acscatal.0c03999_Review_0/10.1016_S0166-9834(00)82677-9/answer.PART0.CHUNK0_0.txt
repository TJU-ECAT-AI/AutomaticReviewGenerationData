However, ODHP presents its own set of challenges, such as the need for an effective oxidant and the complexity of the reaction pathways, especially concerning the selection of the oxidant and managing the side reactions that can occur during the process.

2. In the context of oxidant selection for ODHP, the choice of oxidant plays a critical role in determining the efficiency and selectivity of the process. Managing the oxygen concentration and ensuring optimal conditions to maximize propene conversion while minimizing side products is crucial for the success of the ODHP process.
