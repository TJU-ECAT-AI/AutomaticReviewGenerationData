[H2S promoted oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons can be efficiently carried out if a molten salt medium, e.g. LiCI/KCl, is used to dissipate the heat from the highly exothermic reaction.]

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The primary challenge in current propylene production methods is the high energy consumption due to the endothermic nature of the process. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by converting propane to propylene exothermically, which reduces the need for external energy input. This method is more energy-efficient compared to traditional methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
[Optimization of reaction conditions by varying temperature, space velocity and quantity of promoter results in a 53% olefin yield (5% butenes, 48% butadiene) at 80% butane conversion.]
</quotes>
<EnglishVersionAnswer>
ODHP presents advantages over conventional methods in terms of energy efficiency. By optimizing the reaction conditions through changes in temperature, space velocity, and promoter quantity, high yields of olefins can be achieved. For instance, at 80% butane conversion, a 53% olefin yield with 5% butenes and 48% butadiene can be obtained. However, the use of oxygen as an oxidant poses specific challenges, including the potential for increased burning and cracking of the feedstock.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
<ChineseVersionAnswer>
ODHP在能源效率方面优于传统方法。通过调整温度、空间速度和促进剂量优化反应条件，可以获得高的烯烃产率。例如，在80%丁烷转化率下，可以得到53%的烯烃产率，其中5%为丁烯，48%为丁二烯。然而，使用氧气作为氧化剂存在特定挑战，包括可能增加原料的燃烧和裂解。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
[Specialized catalysts have been developed for the dehydrogenation of butene to butadiene, ethane to ethylene, and ethylbenzene to styrene. These catalysts serve to promote the oxidative reaction and/or minimize undesirable side reactions such as cracking or uncontrolled burning of the hydrocarbon.]
</quotes>
<EnglishVersionAnswer>
In the context of oxidant selection for ODHP, the use of oxygen introduces risks of increased burning and cracking of the feedstock. To mitigate these issues, specialized catalysts have been developed. These catalysts facilitate the oxidative reaction while reducing unwanted side reactions like cracking or uncontrolled burning of the hydrocarbon. For instance, catalysts are specifically designed for butene-to-butadiene, ethane-to-ethylene, and ethylbenzene-to-styrene conversions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
<ChineseVersionAnswer>
在ODHP的氧化剂选择背景下，使用氧气增加了原料燃烧和裂解的风险。为了减轻这些问题，开发了专门的催化剂。这些催化剂促进氧化反应同时减少不希望的副反应，如碳氢化合物的裂解或失控燃烧。例如，专门设计的催化剂用于丁烯转化为丁二烯、乙烷转化为乙烯以及乙基苯转化为苯乙烯的转化。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
[In the case of ethane dehydrogenation, more severe conditions - 1300F (705C) - are required due to the absence of such activated hydrogen atoms. This high temperature is allowable only because of the high stability of the small ethane molecule.]
</quotes>
<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Ethane dehydrogenation requires more severe conditions, reaching up to 1300F (705C), because it lacks activated hydrogen atoms that are present in butene or the benzene nucleus in ethylbenzene. This high temperature is feasible only due to the high stability of the small ethane molecule. The absence of these activated hydrogen atoms necessitates higher temperatures to achieve dehydrogenation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
<ChineseVersionAnswer>
乙烷脱氢化需要更严重的条件，达到1300F（705C），因为缺少类似于但烯或乙基苯中苯核中的活性氢原子。只有由于小乙烷分子的高度稳定性，这种高温才可行。缺乏这些活性氢原子需要更高的温度来实现脱氢化。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
