1. The catalyst under discussion is a tungsten-based heterogeneous alkane metathesis catalyst, supported on a silica-alumina matrix. It shares characteristics with both silica- and alumina-supported systems.
2. In H/D exchange reactions, the mechanism includes σ-bond metathesis, hydrogenolysis, and chain walking. 
3. When comparing silica-supported zirconium hydrides with alumina-supported thorium hydrides, the catalytic properties differ notably in alkane metathesis reactions, showing variations in selectivity and reaction rates based on the support material.
