<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Silica-supported zirconium hydrides are prepared by loading zirconium hydride onto silica support material. 
2. Alumina-supported thorium hydrides are made by impregnating thorium hydride onto alumina.
3. Silica-supported zirconium hydrides enable H/D exchange in C-H bond activation through catalytic action.
4. Alumina-supported thorium hydrides also exhibit similar catalytic performance for H/D exchange.
5. Both catalysts demonstrate high reactivity in alkane metathesis, with selective production of certain alkane derivatives.
6. The mechanism involves σ-bond metathesis, hydrogenolysis, and chain walking in the activation process.
7. Silica-supported zirconium hydrides typically exhibit faster reaction rates than alumina-supported counterparts.
8. Selectivity of the catalysts fluctuates based on the specific alkane compound and reaction conditions.
9. The catalytic efficacy of both materials relies on the accurate preparation method and composition.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 将锆化氢负载到硅胶载体上制备了硅胶支持的锆化氢。
2. 通过将钍化氢浸渍到氧化铝中，制备了氧化铝支持的钍化氢。
3. 硅胶支持的锆化氢通过催化作用促进C-H键的H/D交换。
4. 氧化铝支持的钍化氢也展现出类似的催化性能用于H/D交换。
5. 两种催化剂在烷烃裂合反应中均表现出高活性，且能选择性地生成某些烷烃衍生物。
6. 反应机制包括σ键裂合、氢解和链行走。
7. 硅胶支持的锆化氢通常比氧化铝支持的催化剂具有更快的反应速率。
8. 催化剂的选择性取决于特定的烷烃化合物和反应条件。
9. 这两种材料的催化效果依赖于精确的制备方法和组成。
