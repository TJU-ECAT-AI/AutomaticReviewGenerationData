These materials are utilized in H/D exchange and alkane metathesis processes. The study delves into the mechanisms underlying these reactions, including the rates at which they occur and the selectivity of the transformations. The roles of σ-bond metathesis, hydrogenolysis, and chain walking are also discussed in relation to the activity of these catalysts.
