The article "Ethane to Aromatic Hydrocarbons: Past, Present, Future" discusses the conversion of ethane into aromatic hydrocarbons, covering historical developments, current research, and future prospects. It delves into thermodynamic and kinetic considerations, catalyst selection, and studies on the aromatization mechanism of ethane. Additionally, it explores catalyst stability and deactivation, and provides an outlook on potential advancements in the field.
