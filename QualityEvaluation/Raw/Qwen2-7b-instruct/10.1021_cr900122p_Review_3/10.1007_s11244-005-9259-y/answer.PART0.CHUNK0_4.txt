Preparation: Silica-supported zirconium hydrides are typically prepared by coating the surface of partially dehydroxylated silica with organometallic compounds containing zirconium, followed by heating under hydrogen conditions. Alumina-supported thorium hydrides might be synthesized similarly, using thorium-based organometallic compounds on alumina surfaces.
   
   Structure: Zirconium hydrides often exist as monohydrides (ZrH) and dihydrides (ZrH2) on silica supports, whereas thorium hydrides could potentially form trivalent hydrides (ThH3) on alumina. 

   Catalytic properties: In C-H bond activation, silica-supported zirconium hydrides can facilitate processes like olefin polymerization and hydrogenolysis of alkanes. Alumina-supported thorium hydrides may enable similar reactions, with the potential for enhanced stability or selectivity due to the different support materials.

   Mechanisms: Reaction mechanisms involve σ-bond metathesis, hydrogenolysis, and chain walking. For both systems, these processes can lead to the formation of olefin polymers, the cleavage of alkanes, or the isotope exchange in methane.

   Rates: Rates of reactions can vary significantly based on factors such as the presence of hydrogen, substrate type, and the specific hydride species involved.

   Selectivity: Low regioselectivity is characteristic of reactions involving highly coordinated zirconium and thorium species. This aligns with experimental observations showing mixtures of products in hydrogenolysis of alkanes and polyethylene formation.

   Roles: In both systems, the role of σ-bond metathesis is crucial for alkane metathesis, while hydrogenolysis facilitates the cleavage of C-H bonds. Chain walking is a key aspect of olefin polymerization on both zirconium and thorium hydrides.

1. 准备：二氧化硅支持的锆化物通常通过在部分脱水的二氧化硅表面上涂覆含锆的有机金属化合物，然后在氢条件下加热来制备。铝酸盐支持的钍化物可能以类似方式合成，使用钍基有机金属化合物在铝酸盐表面上。

   结构：锆化物在二氧化硅支持上通常以单氢化物（ZrH）和二氢化物（ZrH2）形式存在，而钍化物在铝酸盐支持上可能形成三价氢化物（ThH3）。 

   催化性质：在C-H键激活中，二氧化硅支持的锆化物可以促进如乙烯聚合和烷烃氢解等过程。铝酸盐支持的钍化物也可能使类似反应成为可能，由于不同的支持材料，这些系统可能表现出增强的稳定性和选择性。

   机制：反应机制涉及σ键迁移、氢解离和链行走。对于两个系统，这些过程可能导致烯烃聚合物的形成、烷烃的断裂或甲烷的同位素交换。

   速率：反应速率会根据氢的存在、底物类型等因素显著变化。

   选择性：高度协调的锆和钍物种表现出的低区域选择性与实验观察到的烷烃氢解和聚乙烯形成的混合产物一致。

   角色：在两个系统中，σ键迁移对于烷烃的迁移至关重要，而氢解离则促进了C-H键的断裂。链行走是锆和钍氢化物上乙烯聚合的关键方面。
