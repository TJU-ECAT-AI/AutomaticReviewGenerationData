The structure of these catalysts often involves a network of silica particles, which provides a large surface area for the immobilization of zirconium hydrides. These catalysts show high efficiency in C-H bond activation reactions, particularly in H/D exchange and alkane metathesis. They often demonstrate rapid reaction rates due to the high dispersion of the catalyst, allowing for efficient contact with reactants.
