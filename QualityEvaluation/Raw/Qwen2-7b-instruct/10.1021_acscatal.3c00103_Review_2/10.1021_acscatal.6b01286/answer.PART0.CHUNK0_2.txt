<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve understanding and controlling the structural evolution of metal species. This study specifically focuses on alumina-supported iron/phosphorus catalysts, aiming to optimize catalyst design and fabrication through the precise control of iron species. By investigating various synthesis conditions, researchers have successfully achieved high activity and propylene selectivity, addressing challenges in catalyst performance optimization for industrial applications.
