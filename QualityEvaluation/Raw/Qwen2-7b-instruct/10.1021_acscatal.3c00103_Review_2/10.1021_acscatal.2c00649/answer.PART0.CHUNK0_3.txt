<EnglishVersionAnswer>
These recent advancements focus on enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes through strategic modifications. Specifically, the elimination of the non-selective Fe(0) phase in a PtFe bimetallic catalyst promotes propane dehydrogenation while simultaneously incorporating CO2. This approach addresses challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications by increasing selectivity and efficiency.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
