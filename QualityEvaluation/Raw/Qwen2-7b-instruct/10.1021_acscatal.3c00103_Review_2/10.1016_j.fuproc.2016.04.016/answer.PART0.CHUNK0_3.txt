<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Recent advancements in enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes have primarily focused on improving the design, fabrication, and performance optimization of industrial catalysts. Key strategies include:

1. Utilizing non-oxidative, autothermal, and oxidative dehydrogenation approaches to overcome the challenges in catalyst design. 

2. Enhancing the activity of platinum-based catalysts by incorporating redox active adatoms within zeolite frameworks to facilitate the Lewis base-assisted deprotonation mechanism for CH activation.

3. Optimizing the phase(s) of vanadium oxide (VOx) where the oxygen ligands are bonded to the catalyst support via non-covalent forces to achieve high alkene selectivity in oxidative dehydrogenation processes.

4. Investigating electrophilic and nucleophilic CH activation mechanisms on catalysts such as NiOx (NiOLiCl/SZ), Li-doped MgO, and halide-containing catalysts (MgDyLiCl, CeO2/2CeF3).

5. Drawing insights from homogeneous catalyst systems to inform the development of more efficient heterogeneous alkane dehydrogenation catalysts.

These advancements aim to address the challenges in catalyst stability, activity, and selectivity, ultimately leading to more effective and sustainable dehydrogenation processes for industrial applications.

最近，增强直接脱氢过程中支持金属催化剂的催化稳定性的主要策略集中在改进催化剂设计、制造和性能优化上。关键策略包括：

1. 使用非氧化性、自热性和氧化性脱氢方法来克服催化剂设计的挑战。

2. 通过在沸石框架中引入具有还原活性的原子来增强铂基催化剂的活性，以促进Lewis碱辅助去质子化机制进行CH激活。

3. 优化范德华力结合的VOx相位，其中VOx的氧配体与催化剂支撑物相结合，以实现氧化脱氢过程中的高烯烃选择性。

4. 研究NiOx（NiOLiCl/SZ）、锂掺杂的MgO和卤素含有的催化剂（MgDyLiCl、CeO2/2CeF3）上的电负性和亲核CH激活机制。

5. 从均相催化剂系统中汲取经验，为发展更高效的异相烷烃脱氢催化剂提供新的视角。

这些进步旨在解决催化剂稳定性、活性和选择性方面的挑战，最终导致更有效和可持续的脱氢过程，适用于工业应用。
