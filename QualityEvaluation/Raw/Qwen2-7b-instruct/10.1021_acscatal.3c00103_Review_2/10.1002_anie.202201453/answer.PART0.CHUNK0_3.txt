<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. To address the growing demand-supply gap in the propylene market, researchers have developed alternative metal oxide catalysts with lower costs and toxicity, such as VOx, GaOx, ZnO, ZrO2, etc.
2. Despite advancements, metal oxide-based catalysts typically exhibit lower propylene dehydrogenation reactivity compared to platinum-based catalysts.
3. Noble metals can play crucial roles in regulating metal oxide catalysts, enhancing their performance in various catalytic processes.
4. In the study, the focus was on investigating the synergistic effect between platinum and gallium oxide for propylene dehydrogenation with hydrogen co-feeding.
5. On a 3Ga2O3 catalyst, 5nm gallium oxide nanoparticles were observed, indicating the size and structure of the catalyst material.
6. The presence of platinum led to a deeper reduction of gallium oxide, with the fraction of Ga3+ decreasing and the fraction of partially reduced Ga (Gaδ+) increasing.
7. Compared to the base catalyst, the introduction of platinum resulted in an increase in the number of smaller gallium oxide particles and/or positively charged gallium ions (GaO+).
8. The in situ DRIFTS technique was employed to study the evolution of surface species during the propylene dehydrogenation process, revealing the importance of hydrogen in stabilizing gallium hydride species (Gaδ+H).
9. The study also included comparisons with other catalysts with varying platinum loadings to further elucidate the synergistic effect.
10. During the reaction, hydrogen atoms tended to bond to gallium sites on the surface of the defective gallium oxide, rather than oxygen sites, highlighting the unique chemistry involved in the process.

1. 面对丙烯市场日益增长的需求与供应缺口，研究人员开发了成本更低、毒性更小的金属氧化物催化剂，如VOx、GaOx、ZnO、ZrO2等。
2. 尽管如此，金属氧化物基催化剂在丙烯脱氢反应中的活性通常低于铂基催化剂。
3. 贵重金属在调节金属氧化物催化剂性能方面发挥着关键作用，能够增强它们在不同催化过程中的表现。
4. 在研究中，关注了铂与镓氧化物协同作用对丙烯脱氢的影响，特别是在氢气共饲喂条件下的应用。
5. 对于3Ga2O3催化剂，观察到了5nm的镓氧化物纳米颗粒，显示了催化剂材料的尺寸和结构特征。
6. 铂的加入导致镓氧化物的深度还原，镓三价（Ga3+）的比例下降，部分还原镓（Gaδ+）的比例增加。
7. 相比基质催化剂，铂的引入导致较小的镓氧化物粒子和/或正电荷镓离子（GaO+）数量的增加。
8. 利用原位DRIFTS技术研究了表面物种在脱氢反应过程中的演变，揭示了氢在稳定镓氢物种（Gaδ+H）中的重要性。
9. 研究还包括了其他负载量不同的催化剂的比较，以进一步阐明协同效应。
10. 反应过程中，氢原子倾向于与缺陷镓氧化物表面的镓位点结合，而不是氧位点，突显了该过程中独特的化学特性。
