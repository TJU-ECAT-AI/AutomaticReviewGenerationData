<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include the use of MOF-derived iron catalysts. These catalysts offer improved stability and selectivity compared to traditional metal catalysts. The strategies involve modifying the structure and composition of the MOF material to optimize its catalytic properties, such as tailoring the pore size, surface area, and chemical composition of the MOF framework. The fabrication process often involves careful control over the synthesis conditions to ensure uniformity and high purity of the catalyst. Performance optimization is achieved through systematic screening of different catalyst formulations and operating parameters. These advancements aim to address the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

最近，领域内的发展集中在开发基于MOF的铁催化剂用于非氧化性丙烷脱氢。这些催化剂在稳定性和选择性方面优于传统金属催化剂。关键策略包括调整MOF材料的结构和组成以优化其催化性能，例如调整MOF框架的孔径大小、表面积和化学成分。制备过程通常涉及精确控制合成条件，确保催化剂的均匀性和高纯度。性能优化通过系统筛选不同的催化剂配方和操作参数实现。这些进步旨在解决工业应用中催化剂设计、制备和性能优化的挑战。
