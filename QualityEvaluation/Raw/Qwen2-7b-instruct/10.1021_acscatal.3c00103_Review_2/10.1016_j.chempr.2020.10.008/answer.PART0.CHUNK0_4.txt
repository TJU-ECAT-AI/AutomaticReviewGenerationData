The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include modifications of support materials to improve interaction with the metal active phase, development of advanced synthesis techniques for metal nanoparticles to ensure uniform dispersion and size control, surface modification techniques to alter the electronic properties of the catalyst surface, integration of co-catalysts or promoters to enhance selectivity and activity, and optimization of operating conditions such as temperature, pressure, and reaction medium composition.
