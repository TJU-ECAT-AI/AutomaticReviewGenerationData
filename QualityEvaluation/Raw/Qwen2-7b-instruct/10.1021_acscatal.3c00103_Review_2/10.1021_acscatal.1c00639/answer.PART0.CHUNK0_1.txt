<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve modifying the composition and structure of the catalysts through new synthesis methods and the use of advanced materials science techniques. One notable approach is the incorporation of alloying elements, such as tin (Sn), to optimize the catalyst properties. This research specifically focuses on the role of Sn segregation in Pt-Sn catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, aiming to improve the catalyst's stability and efficiency by understanding the mechanisms of Sn segregation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
