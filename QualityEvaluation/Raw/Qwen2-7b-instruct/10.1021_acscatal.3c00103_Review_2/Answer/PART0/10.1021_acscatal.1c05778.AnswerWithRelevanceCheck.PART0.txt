          Recent advancements in enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve the development of advanced materials for non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation, focusing on the synthesis of highly active, selective, and durable catalysts. The selection of the support material plays a crucial role in designing efficient and long-lasting catalysts for direct dehydrogenation processes.
