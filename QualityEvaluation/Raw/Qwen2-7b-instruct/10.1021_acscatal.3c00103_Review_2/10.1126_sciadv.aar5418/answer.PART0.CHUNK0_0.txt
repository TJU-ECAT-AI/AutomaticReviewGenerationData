<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include the use of metal-organic frameworks (MOFs) as supports, incorporating metal nanoparticles onto mesoporous silica supports, and utilizing noble metals like platinum and palladium. These advancements address the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization through computational methods to predict and optimize catalyst structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
