[6] "A slow deactivation in this catalyst is ascribed to the formation of coke, and the catalytic performance can be fully restored in the consecutive dehydrogenation-regeneration cycles via a simple calcination treatment."
[7] "Pt-Sn clusters anchored on the Al3+penta sites of mesoporous Al2O3 show high sinter-resistance and regenerability in the propane dehydrogenation reaction."
