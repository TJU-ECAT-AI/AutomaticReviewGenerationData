<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include:

1. Utilizing advanced synthesis techniques like solvothermal and hydrothermal methods to create highly dispersed metal nanoparticles.
2. Incorporating promoters or co-catalysts to improve activity and selectivity.
3. Designing catalyst supports with tailored surface properties, such as high porosity and large surface area.
4. Employing computational methods for predicting and guiding the design process to optimize performance.

These advancements address challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization by balancing stability, activity, and selectivity, while aiming to minimize cost and environmental impact.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

最近开发的关键策略和方法，以增强支持金属催化剂在直接脱氢过程中的催化稳定性包括：

1. 使用先进的合成技术，如溶剂热和水热方法，来创建高度分散的金属纳米颗粒。
2. 引入促进剂或共催化剂以提高活性和选择性。
3. 设计具有特定表面性质的催化剂载体，如高孔隙率和大表面积。
4. 利用计算方法预测并指导设计过程，以优化性能。

这些进步通过平衡稳定性和活性，同时尽量降低成本和环境影响，来解决催化剂设计、制造和性能优化的挑战。
