<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include the use of novel surface modifications and support materials to improve thermal and chemical stability. This involves incorporating metal nanoparticles onto stable supports like carbon or silica and utilizing alloying of metals to create more stable catalyst surfaces. These advancements aim to tackle the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications.
