<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Key strategies and approaches to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include:
1. Incorporating metal-support interactions to improve stability and activity.
2. Using highly dispersed metals on the support surface to prevent agglomeration and maintain high reactivity.
3. Synthesizing supports with tailored surface functionalities to promote better adsorption and desorption of reactants, thereby enhancing catalytic performance.
4. Innovations in catalyst fabrication techniques, such as sol-gel methods and impregnation processes, have also played a crucial role in developing more stable and active catalysts.
5. These advancements are essential for addressing the challenges in achieving sustainable and industrially viable dehydrogenation processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

增强支持型金属催化剂在直接脱氢过程中的催化稳定性的关键策略和方法包括：
1. 在催化剂设计中引入金属-载体相互作用，以提高稳定性和活性。
2. 使用高度分散的金属在载体表面，防止聚集并保持高反应性。
3. 合成具有定制表面功能的载体，以促进更好的吸附和脱附反应物，从而增强催化性能。
4. 催化剂制备技术的创新，如溶胶-凝胶法和浸渍工艺，也对开发更稳定、活性更高的催化剂起到了关键作用。
5. 这些进展对于实现可持续和工业可行的脱氢过程至关重要。
