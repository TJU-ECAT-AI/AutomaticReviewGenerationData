<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include the development of new support materials, the introduction of metal additives or modifiers, and the use of nanotechnology to fabricate catalysts with enhanced surface area and controlled particle size. These advancements address the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications by improving stability, selectivity, and efficiency.
