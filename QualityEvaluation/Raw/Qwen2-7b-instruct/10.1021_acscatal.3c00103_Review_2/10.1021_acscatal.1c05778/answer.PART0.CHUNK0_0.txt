<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve the incorporation of Silicalite-1 as a support material. This approach addresses the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications by leading to enhanced thermal and chemical stability, which in turn improves the catalytic activity and durability of the ZnOx/Silicalite-1 catalysts. The selection of the support material plays a crucial role in the efficiency and longevity of the catalysts used in direct dehydrogenation processes.
