<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve controlling the reaction-induced loss of active sites in materials such as ZnOx/Silicalite-1. These advancements aim to address challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications by focusing on durable nonoxidative propane dehydrogenation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
