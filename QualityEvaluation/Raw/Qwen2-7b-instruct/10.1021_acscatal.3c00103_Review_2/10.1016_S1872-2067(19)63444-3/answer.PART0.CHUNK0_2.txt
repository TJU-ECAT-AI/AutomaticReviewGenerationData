<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key strategies and approaches that have been recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes involve the use of noble metals, the preparation of nanosized particles, and optimizing reaction conditions. These advancements address challenges such as corrosion resistance, poisoning by impurities, and maintaining high activity over multiple cycles.
