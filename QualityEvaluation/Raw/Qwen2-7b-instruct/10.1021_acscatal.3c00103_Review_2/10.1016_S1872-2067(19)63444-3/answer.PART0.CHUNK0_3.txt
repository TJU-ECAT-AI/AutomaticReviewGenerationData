The key strategies and approaches recently developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include modifying the support material, optimizing the metal loading, and employing advanced surface modification techniques. These advancements address the challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization for industrial applications by contributing to increased stability, selectivity, and activity of the catalysts.
