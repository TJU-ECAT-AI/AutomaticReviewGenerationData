<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The lattice strain distributions in individual dealloyed PtFe catalyst nanoparticles are studied through CO and H2 chemisorption techniques.
2. These methods are crucial for characterizing bimetallic catalysts by identifying active metal sites and metal dispersion.
3. CO chemisorption focuses on surface chemistry, whereas H2 chemisorption explores electronic properties.
4. Data interpretation should consider factors like temperature, pressure, and adsorbent type.
5. Normalization of reaction rates necessitates adjustments for nanoparticle size, shape, and coexisting metal species.
