The book offers detailed discussions on experimental setup and data analysis questions in several contributions, addressing practically all measurement problems encountered in experiments. The theoretical chapters are well-written, systematically progressing from simple EXAFS models to multi-body effects, similar being true for the XANES chapter. Perhaps, the argument by D. C. Koningsberger regarding the setup of experiments in one's own laboratory will resonate particularly strongly.
