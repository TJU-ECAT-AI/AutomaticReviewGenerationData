<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study presents a novel experimental setup that integrates two operando spectroscopic techniques, UV-VIS and Raman, alongside online mass spectrometry for real-time analysis of a chromium oxide on alumina catalyst during propane dehydrogenation. The purpose is to showcase the benefits of using multiple techniques simultaneously in catalytic testing, proving their ability to provide complementary and consistent information.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
