<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The recent economic factors, especially the substantial increase in shale gas production, have profoundly affected the development and adoption of dehydrogenation technologies in olefin production. Key advancements in catalysis, membrane technology, and reactor design have significantly enhanced the efficiency, sustainability, and industrial viability of these processes. However, current limitations include high energy consumption, low selectivity towards desired products, and difficulties in maintaining catalyst stability and longevity. Areas requiring improvement encompass the development of more efficient catalysts, optimization of process conditions, enhancement of membrane permeability and selectivity, and refinement of reactor design to improve heat and mass transfer.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
最近的经济因素，特别是页岩气产量的大幅增长，对烯烃生产中的脱氢技术的发展和采用产生了深远影响。催化、膜技术和反应器设计的关键进展显著提高了这些过程的效率、可持续性和工业可行性。然而，当前的限制包括高能耗、对所需产品的低选择性以及催化剂稳定性和寿命的挑战。需要改进的领域包括开发更高效的催化剂、优化工艺条件、增强膜的渗透性和选择性，以及改进反应器设计以提高热和质量传递。
