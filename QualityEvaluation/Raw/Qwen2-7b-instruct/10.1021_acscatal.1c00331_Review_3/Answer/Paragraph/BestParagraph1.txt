Hierarchical pore structures, however, can enhance catalyst performance by offering better accommodation for reactants and products, potentially improving the diffusion pathways and reducing coke accumulation. Uniformity in pore size contributes to more consistent catalytic activity across the support surface, ensuring a more stable and predictable reaction environment.
