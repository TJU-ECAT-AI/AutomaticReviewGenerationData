Its market dynamics are closely tied to the global economy's cyclical fluctuations, particularly in the paper, automotive, plastics, and construction sectors.

2. Ground calcium carbonate (GCC) is the largest and fastest-growing segment in the global market for calcium carbonate.

3. Asia Pacific represents the largest and fastest-growing regional market for calcium carbonate, primarily due to the rapid expansion of its plastics and paper industries. China, in particular, is anticipated to be a significant contributor to growth in this region.
