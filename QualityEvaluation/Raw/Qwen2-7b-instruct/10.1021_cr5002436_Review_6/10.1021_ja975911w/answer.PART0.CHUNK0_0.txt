<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Merck Index, 12th Edition, is an encyclopedia of chemicals, drugs, and biologicals that comes in a searchable electronic version on a Macintosh CD-ROM. It features a powerful search engine with three levels of complexity, allowing users to search through various fields including names, text, entries, properties, and more. Chemical structures found through searches can be copied and used in other applications such as Chemintosh or ChemDraw. The Merck Index has been referenced in 10 different publications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
