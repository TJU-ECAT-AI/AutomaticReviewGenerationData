The final products were further processed through intramolecular cyclization in boiling acetic anhydride, yielding (3E)-3-{[2-(3-R-adamantan-1-yl)-2-oxoethylidene]hydrazinylidene}-5-aryl(het)-aryl)furan-2(3H)-ones.
