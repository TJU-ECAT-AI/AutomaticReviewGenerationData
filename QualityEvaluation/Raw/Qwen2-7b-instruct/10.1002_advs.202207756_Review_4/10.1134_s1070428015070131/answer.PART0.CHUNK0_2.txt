The synthesis of 3-{[2-(3-R-adamantan-1-yl)- 2-oxoethylidene]hydrazinylidene}-5-aryl(hetaryl)fur- an-2(3H)-ones involves cyclization of 2-hydrazinyl derivatives of 4-aryl(hetaryl)-4-oxobut-2-enoic acids containing a pharmacophoric adamantyl fragment. This process utilizes a general procedure where a suspension of 0.01 mol of acid 1a1e and 0.01 mol of triphenylphosphazene 2a or 2b in 3040 mL of anhydrous toluene is stirred at 20-25°C until complete dissolution of the reactants. The synthesized compounds exhibit specific melting points and yields, with an example being (3E)-3-{[(2Z)-2-(Adamantan-1-yl)-2-oxoethyli- dene]hydrazinylidene}-5-phenylfuran-2(3H)-one, which has a yield of 3.37 g (93%) and melts at 149-150°C.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
