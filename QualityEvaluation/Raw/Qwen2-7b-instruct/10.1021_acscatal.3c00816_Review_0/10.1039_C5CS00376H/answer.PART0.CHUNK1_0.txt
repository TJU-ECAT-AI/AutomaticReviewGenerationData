[2] "PDH technology specifically addresses the challenges by offering a more efficient use of propane as a feedstock, which is abundant and widely available."
[3] "It also improves process efficiency by converting propane directly into propylene without the need for intermediate products, reducing energy consumption and increasing yield."
[4] "In terms of product selectivity, PDH technology enhances the purity and quality of propylene, making it more suitable for downstream applications such as plastics production."
