In light of the growing global demand for propylene, the key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production are its ability to address the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. PDH technology specifically addresses these challenges by utilizing propane as a primary feedstock, which is abundant and widely available. It enhances process efficiency through a streamlined direct conversion process, eliminating the need for intermediate steps and reducing energy consumption. Additionally, it ensures high product selectivity, producing propylene with minimal byproducts, thus improving the economic viability and environmental impact of propylene production.
