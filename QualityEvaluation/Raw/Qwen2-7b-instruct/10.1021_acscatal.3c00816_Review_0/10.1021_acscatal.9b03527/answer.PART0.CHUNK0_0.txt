<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Answer: The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production are its ability to address several challenges faced by conventional production methods. These advantages include feedstock flexibility, high product selectivity, and efficient energy utilization. PDH specifically addresses the challenges of conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability by utilizing abundant propane as the primary feedstock. In terms of process efficiency, PDH demonstrates higher yield and lower energy consumption compared to traditional methods. Regarding product selectivity, PDH shows superior performance in producing propylene with minimal byproducts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
