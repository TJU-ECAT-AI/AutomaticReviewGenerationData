<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production include addressing the challenges of conventional methods. These challenges involve feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. Conventional production methods face difficulties due to the high demand and limited sources of suitable feedstocks. PDH technology improves process efficiency by effectively utilizing propane as a feedstock and enhancing product selectivity, leading to a higher yield of propylene with fewer byproducts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
