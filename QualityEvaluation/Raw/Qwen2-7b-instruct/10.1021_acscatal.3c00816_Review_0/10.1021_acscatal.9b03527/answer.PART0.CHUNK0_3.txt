<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) technology stands out due to its ability to directly convert propane into propylene without intermediate steps, making it more energy-efficient and environmentally friendly.
2. It specifically tackles the challenges of conventional production methods by utilizing a readily available and cost-effective feedstock, propane, ensuring sustainability.
3. PDH technology enhances process efficiency through higher conversion rates and propylene yields, reducing costs and minimizing waste.
4. In terms of product selectivity, PDH offers superior control over reaction conditions, allowing for the production of high-purity propylene essential for diverse chemical applications.
