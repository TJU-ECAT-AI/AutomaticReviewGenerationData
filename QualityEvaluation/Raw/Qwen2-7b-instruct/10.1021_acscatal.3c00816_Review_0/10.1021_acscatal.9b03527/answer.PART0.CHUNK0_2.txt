<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology stands out as a promising method for propylene production due to its ability to overcome several challenges faced by conventional production techniques. These challenges primarily revolve around feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. By utilizing propane as a feedstock, PDH technology addresses the scarcity issue associated with traditional oil-based feedstocks. This not only enhances feedstock flexibility but also leads to higher process efficiency and improved product selectivity. Additionally, PDH technology contributes to a more sustainable production process by reducing the environmental impact compared to conventional methods.
