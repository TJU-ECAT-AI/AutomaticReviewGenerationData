Moreover, the presence of Cl has a crucial role in the aggregation of platinum particles and carbon deposition, impacting the catalytic performance of Pt-Al2O3 catalysts with various support phases for propane dehydrogenation. The results indicate that the θ-Al2O3 phase with a high Cl content demonstrates the highest activity and stability.
