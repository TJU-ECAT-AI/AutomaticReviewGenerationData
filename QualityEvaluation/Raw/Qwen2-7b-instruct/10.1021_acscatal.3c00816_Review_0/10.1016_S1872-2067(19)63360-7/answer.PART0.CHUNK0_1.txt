<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production are its higher feedstock flexibility, improved process efficiency, and enhanced product selectivity. It specifically addresses the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability by utilizing propane, an abundant and widely available resource. Additionally, PDH technology offers lower energy consumption and reduced operational costs, improving process efficiency. Product selectivity is also significantly improved, leading to higher yields of propylene with less byproduct formation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
