In light of the growing global demand for propylene, propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology is the most promising method for propylene production because it addresses the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. It specifically addresses these challenges by utilizing the abundant availability of propane as a feedstock, optimizing the production process for high efficiency, and enhancing product selectivity, particularly in the production of propylene. This technology is efficient, environmentally friendly, and cost-effective, making it a preferred choice for propylene production in the petrochemical industry.
