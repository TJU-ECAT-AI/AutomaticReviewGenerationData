<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology is the most promising method for propylene production due to its enhanced process efficiency, improved product selectivity, and greater flexibility in feedstock usage. It allows for a more direct conversion of propane to propylene, reducing energy consumption and potentially lowering operational costs. The availability of abundant propane reserves ensures a stable supply, mitigating risks associated with fluctuating prices and supply chain disruptions. Additionally, PDH processes exhibit higher product selectivity, leading to a higher yield of propylene with less by-product formation, improving overall economic viability and environmental performance.
