<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology stands out as the most promising method for propylene production due to its enhanced feedstock flexibility, improved process efficiency, and superior product selectivity. It addresses the challenges faced by conventional methods by leveraging abundant propane resources, ensuring greater accessibility and resilience against geopolitical disruptions. This technology also boosts process efficiency through the high yield of propylene, reducing energy consumption and waste. Moreover, it optimizes product selectivity, delivering a higher purity of propylene essential for applications like plastics manufacturing.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
