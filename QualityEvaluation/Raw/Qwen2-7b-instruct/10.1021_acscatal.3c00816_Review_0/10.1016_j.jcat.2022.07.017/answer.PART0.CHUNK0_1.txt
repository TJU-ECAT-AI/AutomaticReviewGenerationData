<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Vanadium-based catalysts are highly active and selective for propylene production, offering good regeneration ability compared to platinum and chromium-based catalysts. 

2. The redox cycle involving V5+/V4+ or V5+/V3+ is considered significant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane over vanadium-based catalysts.

3. Additives like phosphorus (P) and magnesium (Mg) can modify the surface acidity and interaction between VOx species and support, thereby impacting catalytic performance.

4. The structure of VOx species can be adjusted by changing the type of support, affecting its texture properties, acidity, and vanadium-support interaction, which tunes the polymerization degree of VOx.

5. Rodemerck et al. compared the intrinsic activity, on-stream stability, and product selectivity of vanadium oxide on silica, aluminum oxide, and silica-aluminum oxide.

6. The current study aimed to comprehensively explore the nature of VOx species and the catalytic performance of vanadium-based hexagonal mesoporous silica (HMS) catalysts in propylene production.

7. The polymerization degree, redox properties, and acidic characteristics of VOx species were analyzed using X-ray diffraction (XRD), UV-visible spectroscopy, hydrogen temperature-programmed reduction (H2-TPR), ammonia temperature-programmed desorption (NH3-TPD), etc., to understand their impact on catalytic performance.

8. A catalyst with 8% vanadium on HMS demonstrated a propylene space-time yield of 0.73 kg·h1·kgcat1, maintaining high initial activity even after three regeneration cycles.

9. Hydrogen formation was identified as the rate-limiting step in the PDH reaction over vanadium-based HMS catalysts.

10. In-situ Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FTIR) results indicated that VOx-C3H5 species plays a crucial role as an intermediate in both propane dehydrogenation and propylene hydrogenation processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 钒基催化剂在丙烯生产中表现出高度的活性和选择性，并且具有良好的再生能力，与铂和铬基催化剂相比。

2. 在钒基催化剂上进行的丙烷氧化脱氢反应中，V5+/V4+或V5+/V3+的氧化还原循环被认为是非常重要的。

3. 添加剂如磷(P)和镁(Mg)可以调整表面酸性和VOx物种与支持物之间的相互作用，进而影响催化性能。

4. 通过改变支撑材料的类型，可以调节VOx物种的结构，影响其纹理特性、酸性和钒-支撑相互作用，从而调控VOx的聚合度。

5. 鲁德梅克等比较了二氧化硅、氧化铝以及二氧化硅-氧化铝复合物上不同负载的钒氧化物对丙烯生成的内在活性、在线稳定性及产品选择性的评估。

6. 当前研究旨在系统地探索VOx物种的性质及其在丙烯生成反应中vanadium基六方微孔硅（HMS）催化剂的催化性能。

7. 分析了VOx物种的聚合度、氧化还原特性和酸性特性，使用X射线衍射（XRD）、紫外可见光谱、氢气温度程序化还原（H2-TPR）、氨气温度程序化脱附（NH3-TPD）等方法，以理解其对催化性能的影响。

8. 载有8%钒的HMS催化剂展示了0.73 kg·h1·kgcat1的丙烯时空产率，并且在三次再生周期后仍能保持高初始活性。

9. 氢的形成被确定为PDH反应中的限速步骤。

10. 原位傅里叶变换红外光谱（FTIR）结果表明，VOx-C3H5物种在丙烷脱氢和丙烯氢化过程中都作为关键中间体发挥作用。
