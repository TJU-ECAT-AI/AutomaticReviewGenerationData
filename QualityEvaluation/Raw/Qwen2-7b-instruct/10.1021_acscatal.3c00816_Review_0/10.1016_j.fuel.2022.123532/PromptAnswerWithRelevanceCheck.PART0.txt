1. **Feedstock Availability**: PDH utilizes abundant propane resources as a feedstock, reducing dependency on scarce or expensive sources such as naphtha or natural gas. This makes PDH more sustainable and economical compared to traditional methods.

2. **Process Efficiency**: PDH processes are designed to maximize propylene yield while minimizing energy consumption and waste generation. This leads to higher efficiency and lower operational costs, making the technology more attractive for industrial scale production.

3. **Product Selectivity**: By focusing on direct dehydrogenation of propane, PDH offers high selectivity for propylene production, reducing the formation of byproducts and increasing the purity and value of the final propylene product.
