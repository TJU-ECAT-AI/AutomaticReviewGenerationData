<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology stands out as the most promising method for propylene production because it effectively tackles the challenges posed by traditional methods. These challenges involve ensuring the availability of suitable feedstocks, optimizing the efficiency of the production process, and achieving high selectivity in product output. By utilizing propane as a direct feedstock, PDH technology ensures an abundant and widely accessible source. It also improves process efficiency, resulting in higher propylene yields. Additionally, PDH technology enhances product selectivity, minimizing the formation of byproducts and maximizing the production of pure propylene.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
