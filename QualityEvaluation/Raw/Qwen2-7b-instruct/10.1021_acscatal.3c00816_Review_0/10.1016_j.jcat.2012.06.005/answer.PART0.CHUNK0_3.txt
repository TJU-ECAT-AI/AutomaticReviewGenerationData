The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology are its ability to efficiently produce propylene from propane feedstock, address challenges in feedstock availability and process efficiency, and enhance product selectivity compared to conventional methods. PDH technology specifically addresses these challenges by utilizing a more direct conversion pathway, optimizing feedstock utilization, improving energy efficiency, and increasing propylene yield through selective catalytic processes.
