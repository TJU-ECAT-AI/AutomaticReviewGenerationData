<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology offers several key advantages for propylene production. Firstly, it directly converts propane into propylene without requiring intermediate products, which addresses the issue of limited feedstock availability encountered by traditional methods. Secondly, PDH technology improves process efficiency compared to conventional production techniques. Lastly, it enhances product selectivity, ensuring higher yields of propylene. These improvements collectively make PDH technology the most promising method for propylene production, offering a more sustainable and efficient solution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
