<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology stands out for its ability to provide higher feedstock flexibility, improved process efficiency, and enhanced product selectivity compared to traditional methods. It tackles the challenges faced by conventional production techniques by offering broader access to feedstocks, optimizing energy utilization, and reducing byproduct formation. Consequently, PDH ensures a sustainable and dependable supply of propylene, which is essential for sectors like petrochemicals and plastics manufacturing, thus effectively addressing the escalating global demand for propylene.
