<EnglishVersionAnswer>
PDH technology is considered the most promising method for propylene production due to its advantages over conventional methods. These include enhanced feedstock flexibility, improved process efficiency, and higher product selectivity. PDH technology directly converts propane into propylene, offering a more consistent supply of raw materials. It also ensures lower energy consumption and reduced waste generation, making it environmentally friendly. Additionally, PDH achieves a higher yield of propylene, minimizing the production of byproducts and enhancing economic viability.
