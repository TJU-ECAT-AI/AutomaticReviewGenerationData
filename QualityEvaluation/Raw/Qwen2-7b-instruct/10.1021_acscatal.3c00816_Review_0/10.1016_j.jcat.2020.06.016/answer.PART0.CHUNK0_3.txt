<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propylene, essential for various chemical products, benefits significantly from propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology. This method stands out due to its advantages in feedstock flexibility, process efficiency, and product selectivity. PDH tackles feedstock availability issues by leveraging the abundance of propane, a widely distributed resource. It also boasts higher process efficiency, reducing energy consumption and costs. Additionally, PDH exhibits superior product selectivity, maximizing propylene yield while minimizing byproduct formation.
