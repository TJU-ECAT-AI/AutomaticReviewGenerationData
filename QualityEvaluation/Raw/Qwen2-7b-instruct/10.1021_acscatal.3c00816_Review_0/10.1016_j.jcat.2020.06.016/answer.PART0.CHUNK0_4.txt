<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production include enhanced feedstock flexibility, improved process efficiency, and increased product selectivity. PDH technology specifically addresses the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability by utilizing propane as a versatile feedstock source. Process efficiency is significantly improved due to the direct conversion of propane to propylene, reducing energy consumption and operational costs. PDH also boasts superior product selectivity, allowing for a higher yield of propylene compared to traditional methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术在丙烯生产方面具有几个关键优势，包括增强的原料灵活性、提高的工艺效率和增加的产品选择性。PDH技术通过利用丙烷作为多用途原料来源，具体解决了传统生产方法面临的原料可用性挑战。由于直接将丙烷转化为丙烯，工艺效率显著提高，从而减少了能源消耗和运营成本。PDH还具有更高的产品选择性，允许获得比传统方法更高的丙烯产量。
