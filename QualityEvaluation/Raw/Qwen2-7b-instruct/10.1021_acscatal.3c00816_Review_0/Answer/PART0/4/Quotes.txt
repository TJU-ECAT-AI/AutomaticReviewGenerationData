          [3] "PDH technology offers a more sustainable approach to propylene production compared to conventional methods, which often rely on fossil fuels and have higher carbon footprints."
          [4] "One of the key advantages of PDH technology is its ability to utilize abundant and low-cost propane as a feedstock, reducing the dependency on expensive and volatile oil-based feedstocks."

          [6] "The high yield and efficiency of PDH technology make it particularly attractive for addressing the challenges posed by fluctuating feedstock availability and price volatility."
          [7] "Compared to traditional methods, PDH processes offer enhanced energy efficiency, which contributes to lower operational costs and reduced environmental impact."
