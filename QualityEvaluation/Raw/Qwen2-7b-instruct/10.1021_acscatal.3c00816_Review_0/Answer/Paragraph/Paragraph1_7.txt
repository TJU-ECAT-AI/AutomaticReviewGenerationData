The key advantage of PDH lies in its efficient use of abundant propane feedstock, high propylene selectivity, and improved catalytic performance through optimized Co species synthesis and stabilization. The future of PDH is promising, with advancements in catalysts, such as those supported by materials like Al2O3, ZrO2, and SiO2, and the development of PDH over Cr/MCM-41 catalysts, highlighting the technology's potential for commercial applications.
