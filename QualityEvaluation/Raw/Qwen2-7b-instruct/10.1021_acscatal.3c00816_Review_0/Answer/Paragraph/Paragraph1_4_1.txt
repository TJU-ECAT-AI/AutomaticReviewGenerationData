        Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology emerges as the most promising method for propylene production, addressing challenges like feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. Its ability to effectively tackle these concerns makes PDH stand out in response to the growing global demand for propylene. Quotes emphasize PDH's potential to solve conventional method challenges, confirming its status as a viable solution.

        The key advantages of PDH technology include:
        1. Direct conversion of propane into propylene without intermediates, bypassing the need for additional processing steps.
        2. Utilization of abundant propane resources, ensuring a steady and inexpensive feedstock supply.
        3. Streamlined process optimized for propylene production, reducing energy consumption and waste generation.
        4. Improved product selectivity, leading to higher yields of propylene with minimal byproducts.

        The technology specifically addresses conventional production methods by:
        - Enhancing feedstock flexibility and availability.
        - Improving process efficiency through optimized catalytic reactions.
        - Maximizing product selectivity, ensuring a more consistent and higher-quality propylene output.

        The PDH process utilizes a variety of catalysts, including zeolite-supported metal catalysts, hierarchical Ga-MFI catalysts, and modified Ti-ZSM-5 zeolites, each designed to enhance activity, stability, and selectivity in the conversion of propane to propylene. These catalysts, along with advanced synthesis methods for controlling catalyst structure and composition, contribute to PDH's efficiency and sustainability.

        在响应全球日益增长的丙烯需求背景下，丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术脱颖而出，作为最具前景的丙烯生产方法，其关键优势在于解决传统生产方法面临的挑战，包括原料供应、过程效率和产品选择性。PDH技术能够有效克服这些挑战，确保丙烯生产的可持续性和经济性。

        PDH技术的关键优势包括：
        1. 直接将丙烷转化为丙烯，无需中间产物，简化了生产流程。
        2. 利用丰富且成本低廉的丙烷资源，确保稳定的原料供应。
        3. 优化催化反应过程，提高能效，降低能耗和废物产生。
        4. 提高产品选择性，实现更高比例的丙烯产出，减少副产品的形成。

        PDH技术具体解决了传统生产方法的问题，包括：
        - 增强原料的灵活性和可获取性。
        - 提升过程效率，通过优化催化反应实现更高的丙烯产出率。
        - 最大化产品选择性，确保更稳定和高质量的丙烯输出。

        该技术利用各种催化剂，如负载金属的沸石催化剂、层状镓-MFI催化剂以及改性Ti-ZSM-5沸石，每种催化剂都旨在增强丙烷转化为丙烯的活性、稳定性和选择性。通过控制催化剂结构和组成的合成方法，PDH技术提高了其效率和环境友好性。
