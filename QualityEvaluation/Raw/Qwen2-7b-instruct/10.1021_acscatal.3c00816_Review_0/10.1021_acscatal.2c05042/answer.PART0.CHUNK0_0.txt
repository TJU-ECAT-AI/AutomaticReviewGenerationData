<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology is the most promising method for propylene production due to its ability to directly convert propane into propylene, thereby optimizing feedstock utilization, enhancing process efficiency, and improving product selectivity. This method reduces the need for intermediate chemical reactions, decreases energy consumption, and offers a more versatile approach to propylene production compared to conventional methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
