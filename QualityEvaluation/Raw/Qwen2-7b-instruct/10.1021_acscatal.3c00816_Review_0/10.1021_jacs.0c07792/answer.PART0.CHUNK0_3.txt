[1] "In light of the growing global demand for propylene, what are the key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production?"
[2] "and how does it specifically address the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity?"

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology include its ability to efficiently convert propane into propylene, addressing challenges in feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity compared to conventional methods. PDH technology offers a more direct route for propylene production, potentially reducing energy consumption and increasing yield efficiency.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
