<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is an emerging industrial method for propylene production. It addresses the challenges of conventional production methods by offering a more sustainable solution due to its use of alternative non-noble metal catalysts, particularly focusing on zinc, which is abundant and non-toxic. Zeolites are utilized as efficient support materials that stabilize and distribute zinc species within the catalyst.

2. The scarcity of platinum and the environmental toxicity of chromium are major drawbacks of traditional PDH catalysts. These issues have driven the search for alternative catalysts, such as those based on non-noble metals like zinc, to improve sustainability and efficiency.

3. Zeolites play a crucial role in the stabilization and distribution of zinc species in PDH catalysts. Their ordered porous structure facilitates the effective trapping and stabilization of zinc cations, enhancing the overall catalytic performance.

4. Partial reduction of zinc species is observed in the PDH reaction, with partially reduced zinc cations identified as the active sites responsible for propylene production. This finding highlights the importance of zinc's reduced form in the catalytic process.

5. Achieving a propylene space-time yield (STY) of 24 and a selectivity of 97% at 853 K demonstrates the high potential of PDH catalysts based on zinc-containing zeolites for industrial applications. This efficiency and selectivity are indicative of a promising method for propylene production.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.PDH是一种新兴的丙烯生产途径，使用Pt或Cr基催化剂。它通过提供更可持续的解决方案来应对传统生产方法面临的挑战，特别是通过利用非贵金属如锌作为活性成分，锌丰富且无毒。沸石被用作有效稳定和分布锌物种的支撑材料。

2.铂的稀缺性和铬的环境毒性是当前商业PDH催化剂的主要缺点。这些问题推动了对替代催化剂（尤其是基于非贵金属如锌的催化剂）的探索，以提高可持续性和效率。

3.沸石在PDH催化剂中的作用至关重要，它们有序的孔结构能够有效地捕获和稳定锌离子，从而增强整体催化性能。

4.PDH反应中观察到锌物种的部分还原，部分还原的锌离子被确认为活性位点，负责丙烯的生成。这一发现强调了还原态锌在催化过程中的重要性。

5.在853 K下达到24的空间时间产率（STY）和97%的选择性表明，基于含锌沸石的PDH催化剂具有工业应用的巨大潜力。这种效率和选择性表明这是一种有前景的丙烯生产方法。
