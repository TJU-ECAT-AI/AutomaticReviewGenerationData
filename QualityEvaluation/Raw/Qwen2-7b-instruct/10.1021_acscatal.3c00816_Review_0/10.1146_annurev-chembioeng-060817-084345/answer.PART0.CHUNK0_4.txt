<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology in addressing the challenges faced by conventional production methods for propylene include:

1. **Feedstock Availability**: PDH technology utilizes propane as its primary feedstock, which is abundant and readily available. This contrasts with conventional methods that often rely on ethane, which requires additional processing steps such as steam cracking or reforming, leading to higher costs and complexity.

2. **Process Efficiency**: PDH offers a more streamlined and efficient process compared to traditional methods like steam cracking or naphtha cracking. It directly converts propane to propylene without the need for intermediate products, reducing energy consumption and lowering the risk of byproduct formation.

3. **Product Selectivity**: PDH technology has demonstrated the ability to produce propylene with high selectivity. By carefully controlling the reaction conditions, the process can optimize the yield of propylene while minimizing the formation of unwanted byproducts.

These advantages make PDH technology particularly promising for propylene production, especially in the context of the increasing demand for propylene and the search for more sustainable and cost-effective production methods. PDH complements other production routes, offering a viable alternative or enhancement to existing industrial practices.

丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术在应对丙烯生产传统方法面临的挑战方面的关键优势包括：

1. **原料供应**：PDH 技术以丙烷为主要原料，该原料丰富且易于获取。相比之下，传统方法通常依赖于乙烷，需要额外的处理步骤，如蒸汽裂解或重整，导致成本和复杂性增加。

2. **工艺效率**：与传统的蒸汽裂解或石脑油裂解等方法相比，PDH 提供了一种更为高效、简洁的过程。它直接将丙烷转化为丙烯，无需中间产物，从而减少了能源消耗并降低了副产品形成的风险。

3. **产品选择性**：PDH 技术能够实现高选择性地生产丙烯。通过精确控制反应条件，过程可以优化丙烯的产率，同时最大限度地减少副产品的生成。

这些优势使得 PDH 技术特别有前景，特别是在对丙烯需求日益增长和寻找更可持续、成本效益更高的生产方法的背景下。PDH 技术补充了现有的工业实践，提供了一种可行的替代方案或增强途径。
