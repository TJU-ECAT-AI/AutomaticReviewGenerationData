What are the key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production?

</questions>

<quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production are its ability to address the challenges faced by conventional production methods. These challenges include ensuring feedstock availability, enhancing process efficiency, and improving product selectivity. PDH technology enables propylene production from propane, a readily available feedstock derived from natural gas. This method offers higher product selectivity than traditional processes and improves process efficiency by reducing energy consumption and increasing yield.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
