<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology in addressing the challenges faced by conventional production methods are:

1. PDH utilizes propane as a feedstock, which is more abundant and less costly than the hydrocarbon feedstocks used in conventional methods, reducing dependency on fossil fuels and enhancing sustainability.

2. PDH offers superior process efficiency, requiring fewer steps and less energy input compared to competing technologies, leading to higher yield and purity of the final product.

3. It provides high product selectivity by directly producing propylene, minimizing byproducts and waste streams, resulting in a cleaner and more efficient production process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
