<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production include its utilization of readily available and affordable natural gas as a feedstock, higher process efficiency that leads to increased yield and reduced waste, and selective production of propylene that avoids the formation of unwanted byproducts, thereby improving product purity and value. These features specifically address the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity.
