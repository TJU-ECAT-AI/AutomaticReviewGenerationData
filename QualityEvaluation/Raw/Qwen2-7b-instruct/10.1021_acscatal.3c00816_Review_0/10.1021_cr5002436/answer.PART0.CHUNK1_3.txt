<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology offers several key advantages over conventional production methods for propylene. It directly converts propane to propylene, addressing challenges related to feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. PDH technology utilizes abundant propane as feedstock, simplifying the process flow and reducing energy consumption compared to traditional methods. This direct conversion enhances process efficiency and enables better control over product quality, yielding high-purity propylene with minimal byproducts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
