<EnglishVersionAnswer>

PDH technology stands out as the most promising method for propylene production due to its advantages in feedstock flexibility, energy efficiency, product selectivity, and addressing the issue of feedstock availability. PDH allows for the utilization of a broad range of hydrocarbons including propane, naphtha, and butane, offering better flexibility compared to traditional methods. It also boasts higher energy efficiency, resulting in reduced operational costs and lower environmental impact. Additionally, PDH enhances product selectivity for propylene, increasing the yield and purity of the final product. This method effectively tackles the challenge of feedstock availability by leveraging abundant propane resources, which are more easily accessible than other feedstocks.
