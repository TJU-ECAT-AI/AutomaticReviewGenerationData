<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production are as follows:
1. It enables direct large-scale production of propylene, which addresses the growing global demand for propylene.
2. It facilitates fundamental analysis of C-H activation, providing insights into chemical reactions and potentially leading to further advancements in chemical synthesis.
3. It tackles the challenges associated with achieving high activity, selectivity, and on-stream stability in cost-effective and environmentally friendly catalysts, particularly avoiding the use of non-noble metals.

The specific manner in which PDH technology addresses the challenges faced by conventional production methods includes:
1. The utilization of supported ultrasmall ZnO nanoparticles encapsulated within a nitrogen-doped carbon (NC) layer. This innovative approach ensures that the ZnO nanoparticles maintain their size and stability at high temperatures, preventing sintering and volatilization.
2. The NC layer acts as a protective barrier, significantly reducing the loss of ZnO nanoparticles during high-temperature operations, thereby enhancing the overall stability of the catalyst.
3. As a result of these design features, the catalysts exhibit exceptional activity and stability, enabling the production of propene with high selectivity—approaching 90%—even at 44.4% propane conversion rates at 600°C after approximately 6 hours of operation.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
PDH技术用于丙烯生产的主要优势如下：
1. 它能够实现大规模直接生产丙烯，以应对全球不断增长的丙烯需求。
2. 它为开展C-H键活化的基本分析提供了平台，有助于深入理解化学反应并可能推动合成化学的进一步发展。
3. 它解决了实现高活性、选择性和在线稳定性的问题，特别是在低成本且环保的催化剂中，避免使用贵金属。

PDH技术在解决传统生产方法面临的挑战方面的具体做法包括：
1. 使用封装在氮掺杂碳（NC）层中的支持超小型ZnO纳米颗粒。这种创新方法确保了在高温下纳米颗粒的尺寸和稳定性，防止了聚集和挥发，从而提高了催化剂的整体稳定性。
2. NC层作为保护屏障，显著减少了高温操作中ZnO纳米颗粒的损失，从而增强了催化剂的稳定性。
3. 由于这些设计特征，催化剂表现出出色的活性和稳定性，在600°C下实现了接近90%的丙烯选择性，即使在44.4%丙烷转化率下，经过大约6小时的操作后仍能保持稳定。
