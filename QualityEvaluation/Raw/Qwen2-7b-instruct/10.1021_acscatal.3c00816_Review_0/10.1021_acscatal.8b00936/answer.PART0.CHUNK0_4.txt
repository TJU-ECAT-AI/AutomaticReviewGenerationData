<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology is considered the most promising method for propylene production due to its ability to address several challenges faced by conventional production methods. These challenges include issues related to feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. PDH technology specifically addresses these challenges by offering better feedstock utilization, enhancing process efficiency, and improving product selectivity compared to traditional methods.
