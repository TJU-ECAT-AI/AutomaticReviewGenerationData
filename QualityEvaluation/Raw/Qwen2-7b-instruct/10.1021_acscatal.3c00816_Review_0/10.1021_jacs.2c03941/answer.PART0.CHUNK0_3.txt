<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production are its ability to directly convert propane to propylene without requiring an intermediate oxygenated compound, which addresses the challenge of feedstock availability. PDH technology specifically tackles the limitations of conventional production methods by improving process efficiency and product selectivity. This method provides higher selectivity towards propylene, resulting in increased yield and reduced energy consumption compared to traditional methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
