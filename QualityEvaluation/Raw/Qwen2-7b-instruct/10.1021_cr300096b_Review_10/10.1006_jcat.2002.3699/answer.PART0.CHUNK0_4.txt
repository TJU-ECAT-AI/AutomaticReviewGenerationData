<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The selective dehydrogenation reactions are fundamental in chemical production, fuel synthesis, and hydrogen storage. Reactor studies and UHV studies enhance our comprehension of these processes, particularly focusing on C-H and N-H bond dehydrogenation over supported catalysts. Key research methods involve temperature-programmed reduction, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy, and transmission electron microscopy. These studies aid in optimizing catalytic processes by elucidating the structure-reactivity relationships of catalysts. Understanding the behavior of C-H and N-H bonds under selective dehydrogenation is essential for designing more efficient catalysts for industrial applications. The collaboration between experimental techniques and theoretical modeling is vital for advancing catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

选择性脱氢反应在化学生产、燃料合成和氢存储中起着重要作用。反应器研究和超高真空（UHV）研究有助于我们理解这些过程，特别是关注在支持催化剂上的C-H和N-H键脱氢。关键的研究方法包括程序升温还原、X射线光电子能谱和透射电子显微镜。这些研究通过揭示催化剂的结构活性关系来优化催化过程。理解C-H和N-H键在选择性脱氢下的行为对于设计更高效的工业应用催化剂至关重要。实验技术和理论建模之间的协同作用对于推进催化研究领域至关重要。
