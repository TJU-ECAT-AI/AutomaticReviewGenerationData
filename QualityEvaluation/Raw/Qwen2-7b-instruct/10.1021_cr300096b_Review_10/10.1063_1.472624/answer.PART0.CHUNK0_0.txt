<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study focuses on the coverage, lateral order, and vibrations of atomic nitrogen adsorbed on the Ru(0001) surface. It utilizes a blend of theoretical calculations and experimental methods to explore the structural and dynamic characteristics of the adsorbate. The research aims to enhance comprehension of adsorption phenomena, which might impact catalytic processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
