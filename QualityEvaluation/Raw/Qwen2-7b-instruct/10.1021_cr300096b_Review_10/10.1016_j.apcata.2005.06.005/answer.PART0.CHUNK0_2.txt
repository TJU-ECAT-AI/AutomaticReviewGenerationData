<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Selective dehydrogenation reactions are pivotal in various industrial applications, including chemical production, fuel synthesis, and hydrogen storage. Reactor studies and UHV studies provide insights into these processes, particularly focusing on C-H and N-H bond dehydrogenation over supported catalysts. Key research methods encompass temperature-programmed reduction (TPR), adsorption microcalorimetry, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy, and differential heats of adsorption. Characterization techniques utilized include XPS, AES, and microcalorimetry. Reaction conditions typically involve specific temperatures, pressures, and gas flows. These studies contribute to the optimization of catalytic processes by elucidating structure-reactivity relationships. The formation of bimetallic PtZn particles is associated with enhanced catalytic properties. In Pt/C, carbon deposition is implicated in deactivation mechanisms.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

选择性脱氢反应在化学生产、燃料合成和氢存储等领域至关重要。反应器研究和UHV研究为我们理解这些过程提供了重要见解，特别是针对在支持催化剂上进行的C-H和N-H键脱氢。关键的研究方法包括温度程序化还原（TPR）、吸附微热流法、X射线光电子能谱（XPS）和吸附气体的热响应。用于表征的技术包括XPS、AES和微热流法。通常涉及特定温度、压力和气流的反应条件。这些研究通过揭示结构-活性关系来优化催化过程。形成双金属PtZn颗粒与增强催化性能相关联。对于Pt/C，碳沉积被认为是导致失活的机制之一。
