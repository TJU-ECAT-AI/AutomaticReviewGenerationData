The ongoing debate revolves around which of these steps is considered rate-limiting, with various studies proposing different steps as the rate-determining step based on their findings. The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism remains a topic of scientific interest and controversy due to its fundamental importance in understanding catalytic processes and its practical applications in industries like petrochemistry.
