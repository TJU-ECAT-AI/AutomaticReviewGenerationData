Its rate-limiting step is debated, with some arguing it's either the initial adsorption or final desorption. This mechanism is of scientific interest due to its insights into catalytic reactions, aiding in process design and optimization. The mechanism's accuracy, as evidenced by experimental data and quantum chemistry calculations, supports its validity. The first CH cleavage is identified as a rate-determining step, and the model developed based on this mechanism effectively predicts dehydrogenation under various conditions. The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism's enduring significance in scientific literature is underscored by its relevance to understanding and optimizing catalytic processes, especially in the context of propane dehydrogenation. It continues to attract attention due to the ongoing debate on the rate-limiting step and the challenges in accurately modeling its dynamics. Despite nearly a century of study, the mechanism's ability to provide a comprehensive framework for understanding dehydrogenation processes makes it a central topic in scientific research.

        催化脱氢反应的霍里乌蒂-波拉尼机制包含四个主要步骤：吸附、激活、反应和脱附。其速率限制步骤的争论仍在继续，有人认为是最初的吸附或最终的脱附。这一机制在科学界具有重要意义，因为它提供了对催化反应的见解，并有助于过程设计和优化。通过实验数据和量子化学计算验证了该机制的准确性，进一步支持了其有效性。识别出第一个CH断裂作为决定性步骤，并基于此机制开发的模型能够有效地预测不同条件下的脱氢作用。霍里乌蒂-波拉尼机制在科学文献中的持久重要性体现在它对理解并优化催化过程的关键作用，特别是在丙烷脱氢的背景下。尽管近一个世纪的研究，该机制提供的全面框架用于理解脱氢过程的能力使其成为科学研究的核心话题。
