1. **Adsorption**: The molecule binds to a surface.
2. **Activation**: The molecule undergoes a transformation, often involving the breaking of bonds.
3. **Conversion**: The activated molecule is transformed into a different chemical entity.
4. **Desorption**: The final product is released from the surface.

Regarding the debate about which step is rate-limiting, it is crucial to understand that the specific step that determines the overall reaction rate can vary depending on the conditions and the molecules involved.
