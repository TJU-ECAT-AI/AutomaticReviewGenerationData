<quotes>
The process of catalytic dehydrogenation of alkanes into their corresponding alkenes is of increasing importance because of the growing demand for alkenes. Propene is an important raw material for the production of polypropene, polyacrylonitrile, acrolein, and acrylic acid. Dehydrogenation of propane is an endothermic reaction that requires relatively high temperature to obtain a high yield of propene. The high reaction temperature favours thermal cracking reactions to coke and light alkanes, leading to a decrease in product yield and an increase in catalyst deactivation.
</quotes>

Answer: The dehydrogenation of alkanes to alkenes, including the process of converting propane to propene, is crucial due to the increasing demand for alkenes. Propene is vital for producing materials like polypropene, polyacrylonitrile, acrolein, and acrylic acid. This reaction is endothermic, meaning it absorbs heat, necessitating a high temperature to achieve a high yield of propene. The elevated temperature tends to favor cracking reactions that produce coke and lighter alkanes, which reduces the product yield and accelerates catalyst deactivation.

<quotes>
Dehydrogenation of propane over different polymorphs of gallium oxide in the presence or absence of CO2 was studied. β-Ga2O3 exhibits the highest activity among the polymorphs, and it is even more active than chromium oxide catalyst in the presence of CO2. H2-TPR and XPS studies show that gallium oxide is hardly reduced below 600 C. The dehydrogenation reaction is suggested to proceed through a heterolytic dissociation reaction pathway, and it is enhanced by CO2 because of the existence of the reverse water gas shift reaction and the Boudouard reaction.
</quotes>

Answer: 在不同形态的镓氧化物存在或不存在二氧化碳的情况下，研究了丙烷的脱氢反应。在各种形态中，β-Ga2O3表现出最高的活性，并且在存在二氧化碳时比铬氧化物催化剂更活跃。H2-TPR和XPS研究表明，镓氧化物在600°C以下几乎不被还原。脱氢反应建议通过异裂解反应途径进行，二氧化碳的存在通过逆水煤气变换反应和布杜尔反应增强了反应活性。

<quotes>
The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for catalytic dehydrogenation reactions describes the four main steps: 1) Adsorption of alkane onto the catalyst surface, 2) Activation of the alkane, 3) Formation of a transition state involving a reactive intermediate, and 4) Desorption of the alkene product. The ongoing debate regarding which step is considered rate-limiting continues due to the complexity of the mechanisms involved and the dynamic nature of the reactions.
</quotes>

Answer: 催化脱氢反应的Horiuti-Polanyi机制描述了四个主要步骤：1）烷烃吸附到催化剂表面，2）烷烃活化，3）形成涉及反应中间体的过渡态，4）烯烃产物脱附。关于哪一步被认为是决定速率的关键的持续争议源于所涉及机制的复杂性和反应的动态性质。
