<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The investigation comprehensively characterizes the structure and local chemical environment of Pd species in the 1 at. % Pd/CeO2 catalyst.
2. High-resolution XRD demonstrates the absence of Pd metal or PdO diffraction lines, indicating the formation of a solid solution in 1 at. % Pd/CeO2.
3. Rietveld refinement verifies the presence of oxide ion vacancies in the 1 at. % Pd/CeO2 catalyst.
4. XPS reveals that Pd exists in a highly ionic Pd2+ state within the 1 at. % Pd/CeO2 catalyst.
5. XANES spectroscopy indicates the presence of a Pd2+-like species in the catalysts.
6. EXAFS analysis confirms that Pd2+ ions replace Ce4+ sites, accompanied by oxide ion vacancies around the Pd2+ ions.
7. The Pd2+ ion is stabilized in the Ce4+ site, resulting in a robust PdCeO2 interaction within the Ce1-xPdxO2-δ solid solution, characterized by Pd2+O2-Ce4+ linkages.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 此处对1 at. % Pd/CeO2催化剂中钯物种的结构和局部化学环境进行了全面表征。
2. 高分辨率XRD显示，1 at. % Pd/CeO2中没有钯金属或PdO衍射线，表明形成了一种固溶体。

6. EXAFS分析证明Pd2+离子取代了Ce4+位点，并且周围存在氧离子空位。
7. Pd2+离子在Ce4+位点上稳定下来，导致了Ce1-xPdxO2-δ固溶体中牢固的PdCeO2相互作用，特征为Pd2+O2-Ce4+连接。
