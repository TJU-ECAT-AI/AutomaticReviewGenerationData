<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The Rh ions dispersed over CeO2 are more active for CO oxidation compared to Rh metal and Rh2O3.

2. The H2/Rh ratio indicates a significant contribution from ceria reduction at lower temperatures for the 0.5, 1, and 2% Rh/CeO2 catalysts.

3. Rietveld refinement results confirm the absence of rhodium metal or rhodium oxide phases in the catalysts.

4. TEM studies show the absence of Rh metal particles in 0.5 and 1 atom % Rh/CeO2 catalysts.

5. XPS and XANES analyses reveal that Rh is present in the ionic state in the catalysts.

6. EXAFS analysis demonstrates that Rh ions substitute for Ce4+ ions in the surface layers of CeO2 crystallites with oxide ion vacancies around them.

7. A Rh3+OCe4+ correlation at 3.16 Å is identified in Rh/CeO2 catalysts through EXAFS analysis.

8. The metalceria interaction in the form of a Ce1-xRhxO2-δ solid solution in the surface layers of CeO2 crystallites is responsible for the enhanced catalytic activity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 铑离子在氧化铈上的分散比铑金属和铑氧化物更有利于一氧化碳氧化。

2. H2/Rh比率表明，对于0.5、1和2%铑/氧化铈催化剂，低温下铈氧化的贡献相当大。

3. 精细化刘维尔方法的结果确认了催化剂中不存在铑金属或铑氧化物相的存在。

4. 透射电子显微镜研究表明，0.5和1原子百分比的铑/氧化铈催化剂中没有铑金属颗粒。

5. XPS和XANES分析显示，铑以离子状态存在于催化剂中。

6. EXAFS分析表明，铑离子在氧化铈晶粒表面层取代了铈4+离子，并且周围有氧离子空位。

7. EXAFS分析在铑/氧化铈催化剂中发现了3.16 Å的Rh3+OCe4+相关性。

8. 形成在氧化铈晶粒表面层的Ce1-xRhxO2-δ固溶体形式的金属铈相互作用是提高催化活性的原因。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
