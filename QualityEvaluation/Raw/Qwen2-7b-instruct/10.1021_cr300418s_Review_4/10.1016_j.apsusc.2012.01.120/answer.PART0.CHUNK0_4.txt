<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. A modified solgel method was used to prepare a series of NiZrO catalysts with different Zr contents using Ni(NO3)2·6H2O and Zr(NO3)4·5H2O as precursors.
2. Specific surface areas of the catalysts were determined using the Brunauer-Emmett-Teller (BET) method with a Quantachrome Autosorb-1 surface area analyzer at liquid nitrogen temperature (−196°C).
3. X-ray diffraction patterns were obtained using a Philips PW3040/60 powder diffractometer with Cu kα radiation (λ = 0.15406 nm) at 40 kV and 40 mA within the 2θ range of 20° to 90°.
4. High-resolution transmission electron microscopy was performed using a JEM-2010F electron microscope at an acceleration voltage of 200 kV.
5. O2 temperature-programmed desorption experiments were carried out on a mass spectrometer.
6. The acidity of the samples was measured through temperature-programmed desorption of ammonia.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 使用溶胶-凝胶改性方法，采用Ni(NO3)2·6H2O和Zr(NO3)4·5H2O作为前体制备了一系列不同Zr含量的NiZrO催化剂。
2. 催化剂的比表面积使用Quantachrome Autosorb-1表面分析仪通过氮吸附分析在液氮温度（-196°C）下采用BET方法测定。
3. 使用Philips PW3040/60粉末衍射仪获取X射线衍射图谱，使用Cu kα辐射（λ = 0.15406 nm），电压40 kV，电流40 mA，在2θ范围为20°到90°内进行。
4. 高分辨透射电子显微镜使用JEM-2010F电子显微镜，加速电压为200 kV进行操作。
5. 在质谱仪上进行了氧气温度程序脱附实验。
6. 通过氨温度程序脱附测量样品的酸性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
