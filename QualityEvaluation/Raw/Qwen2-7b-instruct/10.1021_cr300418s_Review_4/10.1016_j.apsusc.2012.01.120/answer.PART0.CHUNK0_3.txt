<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. A modified solgel method was utilized to prepare a series of NiZrO catalysts with varying Zr contents using Ni(NO3)2·6H2O and Zr(NO3)4·5H2O as precursors.
2. X-ray diffraction patterns were acquired using a Philips PW3040/60 diffractometer at 40 kV and 40 mA, with Cu k radiation (λ = 0.15406 nm) and step scanning in the 2θ range from 20° to 90°.
3. High-resolution transmission electron microscopy was conducted using a JEM-2010F electron microscope at an acceleration voltage of 200 kV.
4. X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy measurements were executed with a VG ESCALAB/Auger spectrometer to determine surface oxidation states through chemical shift analysis.
5. The interaction between Ni and Zr caused difficulty in catalyst reduction.
6. It was concluded that dispersing Zr into NiO could enhance catalyst performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 使用Ni(NO3)2·6H2O和Zr(NO3)4·5H2O作为前体，通过修改溶胶-凝胶法制备了一系列具有不同Zr含量的NiZrO催化剂。
2. 使用Philips PW3040/60衍射仪在40 kV和40 mA下获取X射线衍射图案，使用Cu k辐射（λ = 0.15406 nm）和步进扫描，在2θ范围从20°到90°之间进行扫描。
3. 高分辨率透射电子显微镜使用加速电压为200 kV的JEM-2010F电子显微镜进行操作。
4. 使用VG ESCALAB/Auger光谱仪通过化学位移分析执行X射线光电子能谱测量以确定表面氧化状态。
5. Ni和Zr之间的相互作用导致催化剂难以还原。
6. 结论是将Zr分散到NiO中可以提高催化剂性能。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
