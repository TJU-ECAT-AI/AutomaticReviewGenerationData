<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study aims to investigate the oxidation state and chemical shift in transition metal oxides through Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy (EELS).

2. The research presents the Excitation Loss Near Edge Spectroscopy (ELNES) for various transition metal oxides and metallic Fe.

3. The findings are crucial for achieving atomic resolution information on oxidation states in contemporary experiments.

4. The ELNES fitting method has been applied to measure the oxidation states of elements such as V, Ti, Mn, Fe, and Ce.

5. This technique requires the selection of appropriate reference spectra and is adaptable for non-integer valence compounds.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 该研究旨在通过电子能量损失谱(EELS)来探究过渡金属氧化物中的氧化状态和化学位移。

2. 研究展示了不同氧化状态的过渡金属氧化物和金属Fe的激发损失近边谱(ELNES)。

4. ELNES拟合方法已被用于测量V、Ti、Mn、Fe、Ce等元素的氧化状态。

5. 这种技术需要选择合适的参考光谱，并且可以适应非整数价化合物。
