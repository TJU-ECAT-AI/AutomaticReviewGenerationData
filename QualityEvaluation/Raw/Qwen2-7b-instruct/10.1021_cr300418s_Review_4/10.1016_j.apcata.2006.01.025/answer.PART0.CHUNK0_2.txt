<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Four ceria-zirconia binary oxides were prepared: Ce0.90Zr0.10, Ce0.75Zr0.25, Ce0.50Zr0.50, and Ce0.25Zr0.75.

2. XPS results, normalized to subtract out the signal from carbon, are shown in Fig. 1AC and Table 2.

3. XANES was used to analyze the results, and the findings are depicted in Fig. 4A and B.

4. Important observations regarding formate stability are discussed.

5. Table 3 presents the outcomes of activity tests in the fixed bed reactor, including thermodynamically predicted equilibrium conversions as a function of temperature.

6. Based on 0.15-life (85% formate decomposition) results, the first order rate constant was estimated under a first order decay assumption.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
