Key challenges in theoretical modeling involve accurately predicting the complex interactions between the catalyst components and reactants. Balancing efficiency, selectivity, and safety necessitates careful control of operating parameters and the design of robust catalyst systems that can withstand harsh reaction environments without degradation.
