CO2 utilization as an oxidant in the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene over MnO2-ZrO2 catalysts
</literature>
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study demonstrates the use of MnO2-ZrO2 mixed oxide catalysts for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene (EB) to styrene (SM) with CO2 as an oxidant. The 10% MnO2-ZrO2 mixed oxide catalyst showed the highest conversion of 73% with a selectivity of 98% at 650°C. The MnO2-ZrO2 binary oxides were characterized as amorphous solids with enhanced catalytic activity compared to individual MnO2 and ZrO2 oxides, which were crystalline. The gradual enhancement of activities was demonstrated with increasing CO2 to EB ratios, indicating CO2's role as a soft oxidant in improving both activity and stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
