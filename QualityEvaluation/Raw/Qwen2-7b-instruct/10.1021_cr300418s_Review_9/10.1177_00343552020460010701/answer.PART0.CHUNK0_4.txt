The book "Enabling Lives: Biographies of Six Prominent Americans with Disabilities" discusses various topics relevant to rehabilitation counseling, including the Americans with Disabilities Act, personal life details, and recurring themes of spirituality and conscientious living. The text is versatile and can be used as a supplementary or primary text for courses focused on historical and psychosocial topics, problem-based learning activities, class discussions, or research projects. Personal life details, such as romantic pursuits and failures, are occasionally included, though the reviewer notes that some may consider this information extraneous.
