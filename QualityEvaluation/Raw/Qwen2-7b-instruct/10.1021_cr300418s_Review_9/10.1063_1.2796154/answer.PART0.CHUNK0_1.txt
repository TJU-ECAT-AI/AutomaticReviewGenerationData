<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on cationic and anionic vacancies on the NiO(100) surface through DFT + U and hybrid functional density functional theory calculations. It examines the perfect NiO surface, oxygen vacancy, and nickel vacancy. The key challenges in modeling, optimizing processes, and balancing efficiency, selectivity, and safety in catalytic systems based on nickel oxide are highlighted.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
