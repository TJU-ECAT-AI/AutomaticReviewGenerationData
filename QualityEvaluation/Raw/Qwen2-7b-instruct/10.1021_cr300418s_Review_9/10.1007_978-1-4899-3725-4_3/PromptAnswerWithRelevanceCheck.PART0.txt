Process optimization faces hurdles in achieving a balance between maximizing efficiency, ensuring high selectivity, and maintaining safety measures during the reaction. 2. The development of new catalysts starts from identifying the underlying process needs. This could be driven by the requirement for more efficient, selective, or durable catalysts to improve the performance of industrial processes.

3. Catalyst development involves addressing challenges related to the electronic structure and catalytic performance of catalysts, particularly in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.
