The book Fundamentals of Industrial Catalytic Processes (Second Edition) serves as both an instructional and reference text for chemical science and engineering audiences. It offers a balanced academic and industrial perspective on industrial catalysis, addressing the need for a comprehensive course text while also providing detailed information on industrially relevant processes that will be of interest to practicing chemists and engineers. The text is grounded in the use of fundamental aspects of reaction engineering and heterogeneous catalysis for industrial design and optimization of catalytic processes.
