The book Fundamentals of Industrial Catalytic Processes serves as both an instructional and reference text for chemical science and engineering audiences. It balances academic and industrial perspectives on industrial catalysis, addressing the need for a comprehensive course text while also providing detailed information relevant to industrial catalytic processes. The text is designed to be accessible to engineers and chemists, grounded in fundamental aspects of reaction engineering and heterogeneous catalysis for industrial design and optimization.
