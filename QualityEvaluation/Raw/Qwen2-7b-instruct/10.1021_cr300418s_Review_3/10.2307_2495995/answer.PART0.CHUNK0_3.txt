The Soviet Russian State is described as a comprehensive textbook aimed at undergraduate courses focusing on Soviet politics. Robert G. Wesson's approach is characterized as historical-descriptive, highlighting parallels between the Soviet and tsarist regimes, as well as with earlier imperial orders. Despite some minor oversimplifications or questionable factual assertions, Wesson has successfully created a textbook that meets the needs of many students seeking a readable, well-integrated resource with practical details.
