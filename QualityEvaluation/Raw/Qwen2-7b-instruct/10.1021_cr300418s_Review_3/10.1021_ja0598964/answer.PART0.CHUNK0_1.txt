<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Chapters 8 through 11 delve into the redox and acid-base characteristics of diverse catalyst systems, encompassing zeolites.
2. Chapter 12 focuses solely on synthetic methodologies, particularly combinatorial synthesis and high-throughput evaluation techniques.
3. Chapters 13 to 15 investigate alkane reactions on metal oxides, with specific attention to propane selective oxidation, methane oxidation, and oxidative dehydrogenation of lower alkanes.
4. Chapters 17 and 18 address total combustion of volatile organic compounds and hydrogenation reactions, respectively.
5. The utilization of photocatalysis for water splitting is explored in Chapters 19 and 20, utilizing TiO2-based systems and RuO2-promoted p-block (d10) metal oxides.
6. Chapter 21 discusses selective catalytic reduction for NOx abatement employing ammonia or hydrocarbons for selective reduction on metal oxides, while Chapter 22 examines semiconducting SnO2 gas sensors.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 第8至第11章深入探讨了各种催化剂体系的氧化还原和酸碱性质，涵盖了沸石。
2. 第12章专注于合成方法，特别是组合合成和高通量评估技术。
3. 第13至第15章研究了金属氧化物上的烷烃反应，特别关注丙烷选择性氧化、甲烷氧化以及较低烷烃的氧化脱氢。
4. 第17章和第18章分别处理了挥发性有机化合物的完全燃烧和氢化反应。
5. 章节19和20利用TiO2基系统和RuO2促进的p族(d10)金属氧化物，探索了光催化水分解。
6. 第21章讨论了选择性催化还原用于NOx消除，使用氨或碳氢化合物在金属氧化物上进行选择性还原。第22章则研究了半导体SnO2气体传感器。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
