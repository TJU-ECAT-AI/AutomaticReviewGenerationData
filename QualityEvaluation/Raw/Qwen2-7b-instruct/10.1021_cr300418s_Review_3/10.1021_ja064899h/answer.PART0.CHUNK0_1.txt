<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1.Ultrasonic spray pyrolysis was used to synthesize porous carbon powders from a glucose and urea precursor mixture.
2.Dopants, including Nb, were incorporated into the synthesis to modify the catalyst properties.
3.Varying preparation techniques influenced the structural characteristics of the catalysts.
4.The Nb-doped ceria catalyst demonstrated improved n-butane combustion efficiency.
5.The activation energy for the combustion process was reduced with the use of the Nb-doped ceria catalyst.
