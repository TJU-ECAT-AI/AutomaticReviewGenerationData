<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The coprecipitation method for synthesizing doped ceria catalysts involves the creation of a homogeneous mixture of the components, followed by their precipitation. This method was used to synthesize Nb-doped ceria catalysts. Different preparation techniques influenced the structural properties and catalytic performance of the resulting catalysts. Specifically, the Nb-doping had an effect on the n-butane combustion rates and activation energies, showcasing the importance of preparation methods in determining the effectiveness of these catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
