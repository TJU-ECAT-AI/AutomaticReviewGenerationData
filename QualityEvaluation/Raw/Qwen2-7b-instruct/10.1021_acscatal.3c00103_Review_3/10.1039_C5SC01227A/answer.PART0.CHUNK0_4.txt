The hydroxyl anchoring method enables the targeted inclusion of metal compounds within the crystal structure of solid supports, optimizing their interactions with the support material. This technique enhances catalyst stability, boosting activity and selectivity in heterogeneous catalysis. Precise metal placement through hydroxyl anchoring promotes efficient solid-gas reactions by strengthening the metal-support interactions.
