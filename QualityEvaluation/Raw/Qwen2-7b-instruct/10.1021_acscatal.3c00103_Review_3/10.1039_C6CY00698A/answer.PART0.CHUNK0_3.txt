<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The hydroxyl anchoring method enhances the controlled incorporation of active metal compounds in crystal materials by leveraging the polar characteristics of hydroxyl groups to strengthen metal-support interactions. This enhancement leads to improved catalytic performance, characterized by increased activity, selectivity, and stability in solid-gas reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
