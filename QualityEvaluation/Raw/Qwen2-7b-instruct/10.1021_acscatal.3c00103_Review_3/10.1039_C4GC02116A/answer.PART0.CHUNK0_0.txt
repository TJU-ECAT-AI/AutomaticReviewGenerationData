<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The hydroxyl anchoring method facilitates the controlled incorporation of active metal compounds in crystal materials by allowing precise placement of metals at specific sites within the crystal lattice. This enhances metal-support interactions, leading to improved activity, selectivity, and stability of heterogeneous catalysts in solid-gas reactions. The method ensures that metals are integrated efficiently, optimizing their catalytic properties.

2. Mesoporous metal ion-containing Beta zeolites, specifically Meso-Zr-Beta, Meso-Sn-Beta, and Meso-Ti-Beta, were investigated for their catalytic performance in the ring-opening reaction of styrene oxide with aniline, producing β-amino alcohols selectively.

3. Tetrahedrally coordinated Zr(iv) species in the Beta framework are identified as optimized active components for the aminolysis of epoxides, offering moderate Lewis acidity, surpassing Sn and Zr-silicate analogues.

4. The introduction of mesopores via alkaline treatment significantly improves catalyst activity by enhancing reactant access to active sites and promoting product diffusion, facilitating efficient mass transfer.

5. By varying the epoxides and amines, a diverse range of β-amino alcohols can be synthesized through the aminolysis of epoxides, showcasing the versatility of the reaction conditions.

6. Meso-Zr-Beta, prepared using post-synthesis procedures, is proven to be a durable catalyst in the ring-opening aminolysis of epoxides, demonstrating excellent recyclability.

1. 羟基锚定方法通过允许精确放置金属在晶体晶格的特定位置，促进了活性金属化合物在晶体材料中的可控整合。这增强了金属-支持相互作用，提高了固体-气体反应中异质催化剂的活性、选择性和稳定性。该方法确保金属高效集成，优化其催化性能。

2. 通过研究具有插入过渡金属离子的介孔Beta沸石，特别是Meso-Zr-Beta、Meso-Sn-Beta和Meso-Ti-Beta，发现在环氧化物与安息香酸的开环反应中，它们能选择性地生产2-苯基-2-(苯基氨基)乙醇。

3. Beta框架中的四面体配合态Zr(iv)物种被证明是开环反应中环氧化物与胺的最优活性组分，提供适中的Lewis酸性，超越Sn和Zr硅酸盐类似物。

4. 通过碱处理引入的介孔能够显著提高催化剂活性，通过增强反应物分子对活性位点的接近和促进产物在孔外的扩散，促进有效质量传输。

5. 通过改变环氧化物和胺的种类，可以合成一系列β-氨基酸醇，展示了反应条件的灵活性。

6. 使用后合成程序制备的Meso-Zr-Beta被证明是一种耐用的催化剂，在环氧化物的开环氨基化反应中，显示出良好的可回收性。
