<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The hydroxyl anchoring method enables the controlled addition of active metal compounds into crystal materials by employing functionalized organic molecules. This technique strengthens the interaction between metals and support materials, resulting in enhanced activity, selectivity, and stability of heterogeneous catalysts in solid-gas reactions due to optimized metal-support interactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
