<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The addition of a secondary component shows significant promise in boosting catalytic efficiency and reducing unintended coke formation during PDH processes.
2. High propane conversion rates and excellent stability can be concurrently achieved at reasonable reaction temperatures, making Pt-Zn/Si-Beta zeolite a durable and cost-effective catalyst suitable for industrial PDH processes.
3. The preservation of the microporous BEA structure of the Beta zeolite was confirmed through argon adsorption-desorption analysis.
4. The chemical surroundings of Zn elements on Si-Beta zeolite were assessed using UVvis analysis.
5. The morphology of the zeolite support and the dispersion of Pt and Zn elements on Si-Beta support were studied through electron microscopy.
6. The transfer of electrons from Zn species to Pt species results in Zn becoming electron-deficient and Lewis acidic.
7. The introduction of Pt into 2Zn/Si-Beta resulted in an ammonia desorption peak centered at 443 K on 0.1Pt-2Zn/Si-Beta.
8. A stable propylene selectivity of 98% and a high propylene yield of 63% were typically achieved on 0.1Pt-2Zn/Si-Beta catalyst at 823 K.
9. The loss of Zn species and the consequent aggregation of Pt species are identified as the reasons for the irreversible deactivation of Pt-Zn/Si-Beta catalyst during high-temperature PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 添加第二个成分在PDH过程中提高催化性能并抑制不希望的焦炭形成具有巨大潜力。
2. 高丙烷转化率和良好的稳定性可以在合理反应温度下同时获得，使得Pt-Zn/Si-Beta沸石成为适用于工业PDH过程的耐用且低成本催化剂。
3. 通过氩吸附脱附分析确认了Beta沸石的微孔BEA结构得到了保存。
4. 使用UVvis分析评估了Zn元素在Si-Beta沸石上的化学环境。
5. 通过电子显微镜研究了沸石支持物的形态以及Pt和Zn元素在Si-Beta支持物上的分散情况。
6. Zn元素向Pt元素的电子转移导致Zn变得电子缺乏且具有Lewis酸性。
7. 将Pt引入到2Zn/Si-Beta中，在0.1Pt-2Zn/Si-Beta上观察到了中心位于443K的氨脱附峰。
8. 在823K时，通常可以在0.1Pt-2Zn/Si-Beta催化剂上实现稳定的98%丙烯选择性和63%的丙烯产率。
9. 丙烷转化期间，Zn物种的损失和随后的Pt物种聚集被识别为Pt-Zn/Si-Beta催化剂不可逆失活的原因。
