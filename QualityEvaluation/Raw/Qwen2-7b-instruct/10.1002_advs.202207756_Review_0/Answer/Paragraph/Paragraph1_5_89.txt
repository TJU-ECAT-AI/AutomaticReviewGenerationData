            <p>Driven by the escalating demand for propylene, the petrochemical industry has embraced new production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH), which has significantly influenced the development of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. The understanding of these catalysts has evolved over time, particularly in relation to the ongoing debate around the active Ga species. Recent research findings have contributed to our current knowledge of these catalysts, shedding light on the mechanisms and functions involved in PDH processes. This review encapsulates the pivotal role of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in propylene production, reflecting the dynamic interplay between demand, innovation, and scientific inquiry.</p>
            <p>Key advancements in PDH technology have been driven by the quest for more efficient and environmentally friendly methods, with Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts emerging as a central focus. The active Ga species debate has been a focal point, with recent studies providing insights into their role in catalytic activity and selectivity. This evolution underscores the catalysts' importance in propylene production, highlighting the scientific discourse and progress in understanding these materials.</p>
            <p>The landscape of propylene production has thus been transformed by the development of PDH, with Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts playing a crucial role. The ongoing scientific debate and research findings have contributed to a deeper understanding of these catalysts, paving the way for future advancements in propylene production technology.</p>
            <p>Looking ahead, there is an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies, leveraging the advancements and findings discussed in this review. Future research endeavors might focus on optimizing PDH catalysts, enhancing their activity and selectivity, and addressing the remaining challenges in propylene production.</p>
            <p>Integration of the evolving landscape of propylene production reveals a path towards more sustainable and efficient methods, with PDH catalysis serving as a cornerstone. By synthesizing the insights and perspectives presented here, we can anticipate future directions and broader horizons in the field of propylene production, grounded in the current state of knowledge and research.</p>

            <p>随着丙烯需求的快速增长，石油化工行业已采用新的生产方法，特别是丙烷脱氢（PDH），这在很大程度上影响了Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的发展。对这些催化剂的理解已经随着时间的推移而演变，特别是在关于活性Ga物种的持续辩论中。最近的研究成果为我们当前对这些催化剂的理解做出了贡献，揭示了PDH过程中的机制和功能。本综述概括了Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在丙烯生产中的核心作用，反映了需求、创新与科学探索之间的动态互动。</p>
            <p>PDH技术的关键进展是推动更高效、环境友好的方法发展的努力，Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂成为关注焦点。关于活性Ga物种的辩论是焦点之一，近期研究提供了它们在催化活性和选择性方面的作用的见解。这一演变凸显了这些催化剂在丙烯生产中的重要性，突显了科学辩论和理解这些材料的进步。</p>
            <p>由此，丙烯生产景观通过PDH的发展发生了转变，Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂扮演着关键角色。正在进行的科学辩论和研究成果为深入了解这些催化剂做出了贡献，为未来的丙烯生产技术发展铺平了道路。</p>
            <p>展望未来，存在探索新兴领域和创新方法的机会，利用这里讨论的进展和发现。未来的研究重点可能包括优化PDH催化剂，提高其活性和选择性，并解决丙烯生产过程中的剩余挑战。</p>
            <p>整合丙烯生产的演进图景揭示了一条通往更加可持续和高效方法的道路，其中PDH催化作用作为基石。通过综合这里的见解和观点，我们可以预见到未来方向和更广阔的视野，这些视野基于当前的知识和研究。</p>
