    <English>
            <p>Driven by the escalating demand for propylene, new production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH), have emerged, focusing on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. The evolving landscape of propylene production, from traditional methods to the PDH process, has been significantly influenced by the development of more efficient and selective catalysts. The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved, addressing the ongoing debate on the nature of active Ga species and incorporating recent research findings that have contributed to our current knowledge of these catalysts. Key studies highlight the importance of specific Ga configurations within the zeolite framework for optimal PDH performance, resolving debates on Ga species' identity and reactivity. The integration of these catalysts in PDH processes has not only enhanced propylene production efficiency but also opened avenues for the development of more sustainable and cost-effective methods. This evolution underscores the pivotal role of scientific inquiry in refining and advancing the petrochemical industry's production capabilities.</p>

    <Chinese>
            <p>随着丙烯需求的持续增长，新的生产方法，尤其是丙烷脱氢（PDH），已经出现，专注于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂。丙烯生产景观的演变，从传统方法到PDH过程，受到了更高效、选择性更强催化剂发展的显著影响。Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的作用已得到深入研究，解决了关于活性Ga物种性质的持续辩论，并纳入了近期研究成果，这些成果深化了我们对这些催化剂当前知识的理解。关键研究强调了特定Ga配置在沸石框架内的重要性，以实现最佳PDH性能，解决了Ga物种身份和反应性的争议。这些催化剂在PDH过程中的集成不仅提高了丙烯生产的效率，还为开发更加可持续和经济高效的方法开辟了道路。这一演变凸显了科学研究在精炼和推进石油化工行业生产能力方面的重要作用。</p>
