The development and understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts have been pivotal in this transformation, particularly in elucidating the role of active Ga species and their impact on propylene yield and selectivity. Future research should focus on refining catalyst design to optimize activity and stability, exploring the integration of advanced materials science, and leveraging insights from computational chemistry to predict and enhance catalyst performance. The integration of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts with emerging technologies such as artificial intelligence for process control could further revolutionize PDH processes, making them more sustainable and economically viable for the petrochemical industry.
