The review delves into the advancements in PDH technology, particularly the role of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in enhancing propylene production efficiency. It highlights the pivotal role of gallium (Ga) species within these catalysts, addressing the ongoing scientific debate on their active nature and function. The review integrates findings from a diverse range of studies, emphasizing the critical role of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in the PDH process and the importance of understanding the active Ga species for improving catalyst performance.
