Chem. Int. Ed.</cite> [10.1039/c6sc05178b]. This demand has driven advancements in catalyst technology, with gallium (Ga)-based catalysts, notably those supported on silica, receiving considerable attention as alternative catalysts. Recent research, exemplified by the work on silica-supported gallium species prepared through electrostatic adsorption methods and surface organometallic chemistry (SOMC), has demonstrated the potential of gallium-based catalysts for optimizing PDH technology and enhancing propylene production efficiency [10.1039/c6sc05178b].

            <p>
                The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved significantly, with the ongoing debate about the active Ga species and recent research findings contributing to our current knowledge of these catalysts [10.1039/c6sc05178b]. This includes the synthesis of gallium species on silica surfaces through electrostatic adsorption and SOMC, yielding isolated gallium sites that display unprecedented catalytic performance in propane dehydrogenation [10.1039/c6sc05178b].
                
            </p>
            
            <p>
                This review provides a comprehensive overview of the evolving landscape of propylene production, emphasizing the transition from traditional methods to the emergence of PDH, particularly the development and ongoing scientific debate surrounding Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. It discusses the role of gallium species in PDH, the synthesis methods used, and the insights gained from recent research. This knowledge is crucial for optimizing PDH technology and enhancing propylene production efficiency.

        <p>
            随着丙烯需求的增长，新的生产方法，特别是丙烷脱氢（PDH），已经显著影响了丙烯生产的开发，这在<cite>应用化学国际版</cite>的论文中得到了强调([10.1039/c6sc05178b])。这种需求驱动了催化剂技术的进步，镓（Ga）基催化剂，尤其是那些支持在硅酸盐上的催化剂，作为替代催化剂受到了极大的关注。最近的研究，以通过静电吸附方法和表面有机金属化学（SOMC）制备的硅酸盐支持的镓物种为例，展示了镓基催化剂优化PDH技术并提高丙烯生产效率的潜力([10.1039/c6sc05178b])。

            <p>
                对于PDH而言，Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的理解已经显著发展，关于活性Ga物种的持续争论以及最近的研究成果为当前这些催化剂的知识做出了贡献([10.1039/c6sc05178b])。这包括通过静电吸附和SOMC在硅酸盐表面上合成的镓物种，产生孤立的镓位点，这些位点在丙烷脱氢过程中表现出前所未有的催化性能([10.1039/c6sc05178b])。
                
            </p>
            
            <p>
                这篇综述提供了丙烯生产演进景观的全面概述，特别强调了从传统方法到PDH的转变，以及围绕Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的发展和正在进行的科学辩论。它讨论了镓物种在PDH中的作用、合成方法和近期研究获得的见解。这些知识对于优化PDH技术并提高丙烯生产效率至关重要。

            </p>
        </p>
        <p>
            <b>未来展望：</b>
