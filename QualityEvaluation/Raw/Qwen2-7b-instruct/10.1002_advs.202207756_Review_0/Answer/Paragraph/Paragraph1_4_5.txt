            <p>Driven by the escalating demand for propylene, new production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH), have emerged, focusing on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. Recent studies highlight the pivotal role of gallium (Ga) species in enhancing PDH efficiency, especially in terms of selectivity and stability. The evolving landscape of propylene production underscores the significance of PDH and the ongoing scientific discourse around Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. This review synthesizes insights from the literature, emphasizing the development and understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in PDH, including the nature of active Ga species and the impact of recent research findings.</p>
            <p>The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved over time, including debates about active Ga species and recent research findings contributing to our current knowledge of these catalysts. The review delves into the structure and function of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, their role in PDH processes, and the ongoing scientific debate about the active Ga species.</p>
            <p>Recent studies have illuminated the Ga species involved in the PDH process, offering insights into their structure and function. The review discusses the synthesis and characterization of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, emphasizing the pivotal role of gallium in PDH catalysis. It also addresses the ongoing debate about the nature of active Ga species, exploring theories and evidence supporting different forms of gallium contributing to the catalytic activity.</p>
            <p>Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts with enhanced stability and selectivity, leveraging insights from recent studies. The exploration of novel Ga species and their interactions with the catalyst matrix could lead to the discovery of more efficient catalysts for PDH. Additionally, the integration of computational methods and advanced materials science could provide a deeper understanding of the catalytic mechanisms and guide the design of next-generation PDH catalysts.</p>

            <p>随着丙烯需求的增长，新的生产方法，尤其是丙烷脱氢（PDH），已经出现，并专注于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂。近期的研究强调了镓（Ga）物种在提高PDH效率中的关键作用，特别是在选择性和稳定性方面。丙烯生产格局的演变凸显了PDH及其围绕Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的持续科学辩论的重要性。本文综述了文献中关于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的发展和理解，包括活性Ga物种的性质以及最近研究发现对当前知识的贡献。</p>
            <p>丙烯需求的增长已显著推动了新生产方法的发展，特别是PDH。对于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的理解经历了时间的演变，包括活跃Ga物种的争论和最新研究结果对我们当前知识的贡献。本文深入探讨了Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的结构和功能，它们在PDH过程中的角色，以及围绕活性Ga物种的持续科学辩论。</p>
            <p>最近的研究揭示了参与PDH过程的Ga物种，提供了有关其结构和功能的见解。本文讨论了Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的合成和表征，强调了镓在PDH催化作用中的关键作用。它还处理了关于活性Ga物种性质的持续辩论，探讨了支持不同形式镓的理论和证据，这些镓对催化活性有贡献。</p>
            <p>综合理解和前景展望：考虑到文件中讨论的进展和发现，存在探索新兴领域和创新方法的机会。未来研究可能的重点是开发具有增强稳定性和选择性的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂，利用近期研究的洞察。对新型Ga物种及其与催化剂基质的相互作用的探索可能导致更高效的PDH催化剂的发现。此外，计算方法和先进材料科学的整合可以提供更深的理解催化机制，并指导下一代PDH催化剂的设计。</p>
