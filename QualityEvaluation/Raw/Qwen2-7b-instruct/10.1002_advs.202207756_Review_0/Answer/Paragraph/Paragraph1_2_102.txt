The evolving landscape of propylene production, from traditional methods to the emergence of PDH, underscores the pivotal role of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in this transition. The understanding of these catalysts, particularly regarding the active Ga species, continues to evolve, with each new finding contributing to a more nuanced view of their function and potential applications.
