        <p>Driven by the escalating demand for propylene, new production methods like propane dehydrogenation (PDH) have emerged, focusing on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts. Recent studies have significantly advanced our understanding of these catalysts, particularly regarding the active Ga species, contributing to the current knowledge base. The evolving landscape of PDH has seen significant shifts in catalyst technology, with Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts playing a pivotal role in propylene production efficiency and selectivity.</p>
        <p>The ongoing debate about active Ga species has been a focal point in the field, with recent research findings offering new insights into the nature of these catalysts. This dynamic area of research has contributed to a better understanding of PDH mechanisms, highlighting the importance of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in modern propylene production. The advancements in PDH technology reflect the industry's response to rising propylene demand, emphasizing the need for more efficient and environmentally friendly production methods.</p>
        <p>Key developments include the exploration of non-noble-metal intermetallic compound catalysts, the optimization of supports like Al2O3 for adsorption and activation, and the use of hydrothermal methods for synthesizing sheet-shaped CoAl2O3HT catalysts. These innovations aim to enhance PDH catalytic performance, showcasing potential for improved selectivity and stability. Efforts to develop cost-effective and environmentally friendly catalysts, exemplified by the development of Ga-, Zn-, or Sn-based candidates, remain a critical catalytic challenge in the field.</p>
        <p>Looking ahead, the future challenges and directions of PDH catalysts are provided to guide the development of their broader industrial application. As the industry continues to adapt to the growing demand for propylene, the focus on PDH technology and the role of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts will likely evolve, driving further advancements in propylene production methods.</p>

        <p>关于活性Ga物种的持续争论是该领域的焦点，最近的研究结果提供了对这些催化剂性质的新见解。这一活跃研究领域为PDH机制提供了更好的理解，强调了Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在现代丙烯生产中的重要性。PDH技术的进步反映了行业应对丙烯需求增长的响应，强调了更高效、更环保生产方法的必要性。</p>
        <p>关键进展包括非贵金属互金属化合物催化剂的探索、用于吸附和激活的Al2O3支持的优化以及通过水热法合成片状CoAl2O3HT催化剂的方法。这些创新旨在提高PDH催化性能，展示改善选择性和稳定性的潜力。发展成本效益高且环境友好的催化剂的努力，以Ga-、Zn-或Sn基候选催化剂为例，在该领域仍然是一个重要的挑战。</p>
        <p>展望未来，PDH催化剂的未来挑战和方向被提供以指导其工业应用的发展。随着行业继续适应不断增长的丙烯需求，PDH技术和Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的作用将继续演变，驱动丙烯生产方法的进一步发展。</p>
