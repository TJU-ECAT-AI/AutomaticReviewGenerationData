            The evolving landscape of propylene production: From traditional methods to the emergence of propane dehydrogenation (PDH), with a focus on the development and ongoing scientific debate surrounding Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH. The demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly PDH, which has become a cornerstone in the petrochemical industry for efficient propylene generation. The utilization of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts has been pivotal in this context, offering a more sustainable and cost-effective alternative to traditional catalysts. The understanding of these catalysts has evolved over time, particularly in relation to the role and nature of gallium (Ga) species within the catalyst structure. An ongoing debate surrounds the active Ga species, with recent research findings contributing to a better understanding of these catalysts' mechanisms and optimizing their performance in PDH processes. The active Ga species play a critical role in enhancing the selectivity and efficiency of PDH, making them a focal point of research and development efforts.

            烯烃生产方式的演变：从传统方法到丙烷脱氢（PDH）的出现，重点是Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的发展及其在PDH中的持续科学辩论。对丙烯日益增长的需求显著推动了新型生产方法的发展，尤其是PDH，已成为石油化学工业中高效丙烯生成的关键技术。利用Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的作用至关重要，提供了比传统催化剂更可持续和成本效益更高的替代方案。这些催化剂的理解随着时间的推移而演变，特别是在Ga物种在催化剂结构中的作用和性质方面的关系。关于活性Ga物种的辩论一直是研究的重点，近期的研究结果为这些催化剂的机制和优化PDH过程的性能提供了更好的理解。Ga物种在PDH过程中发挥着关键作用，提高选择性和效率，使其成为研究和开发工作的重要焦点。
