<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The increasing demand for propylene has spurred the development of advanced production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH), which is favored for its efficiency and selectivity. Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts have become a focus in PDH research, offering enhanced performance compared to conventional catalysts. The discussion on the active Ga species in these catalysts is pivotal to understanding their operational effectiveness. Recent studies have shed light on the characteristics of active sites and the underlying mechanisms of PDH catalysis.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
