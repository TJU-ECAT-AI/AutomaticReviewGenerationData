<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has led to the development of new production methods, especially propane dehydrogenation (PDH). A crucial catalyst in PDH is the Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalyst, which is subject to an ongoing debate about its active Ga species. Recent studies have provided insights into the functioning of these catalysts.
