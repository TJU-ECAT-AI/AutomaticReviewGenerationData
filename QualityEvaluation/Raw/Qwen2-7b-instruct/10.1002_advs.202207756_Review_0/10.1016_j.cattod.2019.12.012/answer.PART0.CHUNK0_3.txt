1. The increasing demand for propylene has led to the advancement of new production techniques, notably focusing on propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This process is crucial for propylene production, which is essential for various industries, including plastics manufacturing.
2. The comprehension of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts utilized in PDH processes has seen substantial progress. These catalysts play a pivotal role in the conversion of propane to propylene, and advancements in their understanding have enhanced the efficiency and selectivity of the PDH reactions.
3. There has been an ongoing discussion regarding the active forms of gallium (Ga) species within these catalysts.
