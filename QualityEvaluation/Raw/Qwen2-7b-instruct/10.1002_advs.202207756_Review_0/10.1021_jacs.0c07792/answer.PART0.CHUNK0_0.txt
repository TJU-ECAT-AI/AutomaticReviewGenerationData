The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Recent studies on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH have concentrated on elucidating the nature of active Ga species. These investigations have provided valuable insights into the functioning of these catalysts.
