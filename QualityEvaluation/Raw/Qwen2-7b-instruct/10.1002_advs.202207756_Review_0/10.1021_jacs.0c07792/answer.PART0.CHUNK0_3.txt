The growing demand for propylene has driven advancements in new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). A major focus has been the development of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH, which exhibit high activity and selectivity. The debate around the active Ga species has been central to this field, and recent research has elucidated aspects of these catalysts, enhancing our current knowledge.
