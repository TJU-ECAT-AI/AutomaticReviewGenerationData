<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has led to the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH), which has become a key process due to its high efficiency and selectivity. Research on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has focused on understanding the correlation between Ga speciation and propane dehydrogenation activity. There has been an ongoing debate about the active Ga species in these catalysts, and recent studies have contributed to a deeper understanding of the role of Ga species in the PDH process, emphasizing the importance of specific Ga configurations for optimal activity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
