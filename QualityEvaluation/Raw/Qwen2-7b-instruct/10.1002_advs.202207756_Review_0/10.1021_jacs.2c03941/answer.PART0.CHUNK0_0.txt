<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). PDH has become a focus due to its potential for producing propylene from propane in a cleaner manner. Recent studies on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH have advanced the field by improving catalyst selectivity and activity. There is a debate about the active Ga species involved in PDH, with recent research providing insights into the specific roles of Ga species, suggesting that certain forms are more effective in promoting the PDH reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
