<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The increasing demand for propylene has driven the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Researchers have focused on Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, exploring their evolution in terms of active Ga species and the mechanism behind their performance. Recent studies have provided new insights into these catalysts, contributing to our current understanding. The debate about the active Ga species remains, with ongoing research aiming to clarify their role in the non-oxidative PDH process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
