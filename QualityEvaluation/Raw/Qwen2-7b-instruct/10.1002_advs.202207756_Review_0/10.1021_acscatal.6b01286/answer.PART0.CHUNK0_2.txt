<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The increasing demand for propylene has driven advancements in production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Research on alumina-supported iron/phosphorus catalysts for PDH emphasizes the role of iron species' structural changes in achieving high activity and propylene selectivity. The study of gallium (Ga)-H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has seen discussions around the nature of active Ga species, with recent discoveries enhancing our comprehension of these catalysts' functionality.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
