<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has driven the development of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) methods to enhance propylene production. The study of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has seen advancements, resolving the debate around the active Ga species. Recent findings have shed light on the role of Ga species and their interactions with the zeolite framework, contributing to the current understanding of these catalysts.
