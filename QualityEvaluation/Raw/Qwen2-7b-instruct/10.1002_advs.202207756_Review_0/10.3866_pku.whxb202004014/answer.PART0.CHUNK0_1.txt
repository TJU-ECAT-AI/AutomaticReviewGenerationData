<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The increasing demand for propylene has fueled the development of new production techniques, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This process has been refined with the advent of more effective catalysts, such as Ga/H-ZSM-5. There is an ongoing discussion about the active Ga species involved in these catalysts, and recent research has shed light on the structure and reactivity of Ga sites within ZSM-5, enhancing our comprehension of their catalytic functions in PDH.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
