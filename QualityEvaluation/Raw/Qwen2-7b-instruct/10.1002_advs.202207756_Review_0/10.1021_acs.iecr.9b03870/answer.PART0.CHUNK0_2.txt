<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The increasing demand for propylene has driven advancements in production techniques, notably in propane dehydrogenation (PDH), leading to a rise in studies focused on improving PDH processes' efficiency and selectivity. Among the catalysts used in PDH reactions, Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts have received significant interest due to their capacity for high activity and selectivity. However, the identification of the active Ga species involved in the catalytic process is a topic of ongoing debate within the scientific community. Recent research has provided insights into the nature of these active Ga species and the factors impacting their performance.
