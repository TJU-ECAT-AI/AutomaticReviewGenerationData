The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, especially propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This process is vital for the production of propylene, a key component in the petrochemical industry. The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts used in PDH has advanced notably, encompassing discussions on the active Ga species. Recent studies have contributed to our current knowledge by revealing new details about the active sites and their function in PDH.
