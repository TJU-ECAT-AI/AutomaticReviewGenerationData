The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, with a particular focus on propane dehydrogenation (PDH). Recent studies have advanced our understanding of silica-supported GaPt nanoparticles used as PDH catalysts. The discussion around the active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts remains an ongoing area of research, highlighting continuous efforts to elucidate their function in PDH processes.
