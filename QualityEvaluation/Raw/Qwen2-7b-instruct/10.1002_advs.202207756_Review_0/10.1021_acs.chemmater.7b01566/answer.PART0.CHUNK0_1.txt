<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has led to the development of new production methods, notably propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This increase in demand has prompted researchers to explore and refine Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH. The field is marked by an ongoing debate about the active Ga species involved and recent studies have provided significant insights that enhance our understanding of these catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
