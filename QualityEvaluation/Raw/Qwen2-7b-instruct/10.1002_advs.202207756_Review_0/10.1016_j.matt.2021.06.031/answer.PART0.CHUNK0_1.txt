<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). PDH has become a promising method for direct propylene production, and recent advancements aim to enhance its performance with improved propane conversion, propylene selectivity, and durability. The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has also evolved notably. There is an ongoing debate regarding the active Ga species involved in the catalytic process. Recent research findings have provided valuable insights into the functioning of these catalysts, contributing to our current knowledge in this field.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
