Synthesized comprehension stemming from references \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032\] and \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]. The impact of various synthesis methods, such as wet ion-exchange (WIE), chemical vapor deposition/sublimation, and ex-framework method, on the catalytic performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites in the N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction has been thoroughly explored. It is evident that the WIE method, particularly when employing iron sulfate and Mohrs salt, offers high catalytic activity and ease of catalyst preparation. The pH of the iron salt and zeolite suspension during WIE plays a pivotal role in achieving high activity in N2O decomposition. The topology of the zeolite significantly affects both the activity and stability of the catalyst under realistic conditions. Over-exchange, aiming for an Fe/Al ratio of 1, which can be accomplished through chemical vapor deposition (CVD), is not a critical factor for attaining high activity in N2O decomposition. Moreover, WIE yields a catalyst where less than 50% of the exchange sites are occupied by Fe.

            Various synthesis methods, including steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange, influence the catalytic performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites in the ODHP reaction differently. Fe-ZSM-5, prepared via solid-state exchange using ferrocene as the iron precursor, demonstrates superior performance for ODHP with N2O. After severe calcination, Fe-ZSM-5 exhibits a maximal initial propylene yield of 30% at a high turnover frequency of 0.037 s−1. Active sites identified as FeOAl species enable the formation of stable mono-oxygen species through chemisorption of nitrous oxide, which then react with gaseous propane to produce propylene with high selectivity. Organic species, mainly alkylbenzenes, accumulate during the reaction. Remarkably, Fe-ZSM-5 maintains a stable propane conversion rate of about 13 mmol gcat−1 h−1 and a propylene production rate of about 6 mmol gcat−1 h−1 for over 40 hours with a N2O/C3H8 ratio of 1:2.

            Steam-activated Fe-zeolites, especially FeZSM-5, are highly active and selective catalysts for the ODHP reaction, achieving propylene yields of up to 25% at 723 K and space velocities of 400,000 ml/h/g-cat. The presence of extraframework iron species, generated during steam treatment, is indispensable for producing active catalysts. FeZSM-5 exhibits remarkable resistance against deactivation, contrasting with FeBEA which undergoes rapid and complete deactivation. The initial propene yield from FeZSM-5 and FeBEA is notably higher than from commercial H-ZSM-5 and H-BEA, which possess 30-40 times lower iron content. However, their residual activities after 400 minutes of operation are similar. Mesopores are present in ex-FeZSM-5 due to dealumination during steam treatment, which are absent in ex-Fe-silicalite. Coke formation physically blocks the active reaction sites within the zeolitic pore system, leading to a decreased C3H6 yield. The nature and location of the active extraframework iron species play a crucial role in the deactivation behavior. Additionally, ex-FeZSM-5 shows significantly higher activity in direct N2O decomposition compared to ex-Fe-silicalite.

            The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are profoundly influenced by the synthesis methods employed. These insights provide a foundation for optimizing catalyst preparation and enhancing the efficiency of propane dehydrogenation processes.

            基于\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2004.02.013\]参考文献的深度分析。从\[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032\]和\[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]的参考文献中获得的综合理解。在N2O辅助下的丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）反应中，不同合成方法如湿离子交换（WIE）、化学气相沉积/升华和ex框架法对铁基ZSM-5沸石的催化性能的影响进行了详细探讨。可以看出，WIE方法，特别是使用铁硫酸盐和摩尔斯盐的方法，提供了高催化活性和催化剂制备的便利性。在WIE过程中，铁盐和沸石悬浮液的pH值对在N2O分解中达到高活性起着关键作用。沸石的拓扑结构在现实条件下同时影响催化剂的活性和稳定性。过量交换，旨在实现Fe/Al比例为1的目标，可以通过化学气相沉积（CVD）实现，但并非在N2O分解中获得高活性的关键因素。此外，WIE产生了一个催化剂，其中交换位点不到50%被Fe占据。

            各种合成方法，包括蒸汽处理、固态离子交换和液体交换，在铁基ZSM-5沸石的ODHP反应中的催化性能有所不同。通过固态交换使用二茂铁作为铁前体制备的Fe-ZSM-5，在N2O辅助下的ODHP反应中表现出优越的性能。经过严重煅烧后，Fe-ZSM-5的最大初始丙烯产率为30%，在0.037 s−1的高周转频率下可获得。识别出的FeOAl物种通过硝酸氧化物的化学吸附形成稳定的单氧物种，然后与气体丙烷反应以高选择性生成丙烯。反应期间积累的有机物主要是芳烃衍生物。值得注意的是，Fe-ZSM-5保持了稳定的丙烷转化速率约为13 mmol gcat−1 h−1和丙烯生产速率约为6 mmol gcat−1 h−1超过40小时，N2O/C3H8比例为1:2。

            蒸汽活化的Fe-沸石，尤其是FeZSM-5，是ODHP反应中高度活性和选择性的催化剂，能够在723 K和400,000 ml/h/g-cat的空间速度下达到25%的丙烯产率。产生的额外框架铁物种在蒸汽处理期间对于产生活性催化剂至关重要。FeZSM-5展现出对抗失活的显著抵抗力，而FeBEA则经历了快速且完全的失活。FeZSM-5和FeBEA的初始丙烯产量明显高于商业化的H-ZSM-5和H-BEA，它们的铁含量分别为30至40倍低。然而，它们在400分钟流化操作后的残留活性相似。在蒸汽处理期间，ex-FeZSM-5由于去铝化而含有介孔，这在ex-Fe-silicalite中不存在。焦炭的形成物理地阻塞了在沸石孔系统内部的活性反应位点，导致C3H6产率降低。活性额外框架铁物种的位置和性质在失活行为中扮演着至关重要的角色。此外，ex-FeZSM-5在直接N2O分解中的活动性显著高于ex-Fe-silicalite。

            铁基ZSM-5沸石作为N2O辅助下的丙烷脱氢（ODHP）反应催化剂的有效性和性能受到所采用的合成方法的深远影响。这些见解为优化催化剂制备并增强丙烷脱氢过程的效率提供了基础。
