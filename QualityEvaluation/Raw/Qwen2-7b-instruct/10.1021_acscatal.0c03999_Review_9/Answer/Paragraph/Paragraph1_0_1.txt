The influence of various synthesis methods, including wet ion-exchange (WIE), steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange, on the catalytic performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites in the N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction has been comprehensively investigated. These studies highlight that WIE, particularly using iron sulfate and Mohrs salt, is favored for its high catalytic activity and ease of catalyst manufacture. The pH of the iron salt and zeolite suspension during WIE plays a crucial role in achieving high activity in N2O decomposition. The topology of the zeolite is also a significant factor affecting both the activity and stability of the catalyst under realistic practical conditions. Over-exchange, aiming for an Fe/Al ratio of 1, which can be achieved with the chemical vapor deposition (CVD) method, is not essential for obtaining high activity in N2O decomposition. Additionally, WIE yields a catalyst where less than 50% of the exchange sites are occupied by Fe.

        Fe-ZSM-5, prepared via solid-state exchange using ferrocene as the iron precursor, exhibits superior performance for ODHP with N2O. After severe calcination, Fe-ZSM-5 shows a maximal initial propylene yield of 30% at a high turnover frequency of 0.037 s−1. Active sites identified as FeOAl species enable the formation of stable mono-oxygen species through chemisorption of nitrous oxide, which then react with gaseous propane to produce propylene with high selectivity. Organic species, mainly alkylbenzenes, accumulate during the reaction. Remarkably, Fe-ZSM-5 maintains a stable propane conversion rate of about 13 mmol gcat−1 h−1 and a propylene production rate of about 6 mmol gcat−1 h−1 for over 40 hours with a N2O/C3H8 ratio of 1:2.

        Steam-activated Fe-zeolites, particularly FeZSM-5, are highly active and selective catalysts for the ODHP reaction, achieving propylene yields of up to 25% at 723 K and space velocities of 400,000 ml/h/g-cat. The presence of extraframework iron species, generated during steam treatment, is essential for producing active catalysts. FeZSM-5 shows remarkable resistance against deactivation, whereas FeBEA experiences rapid and complete deactivation. The initial propene yield from FeZSM-5 and FeBEA is notably higher than from commercial H-ZSM-5 and H-BEA, which have 30-40 times lower iron content. However, their residual activities after 400 minutes of operation are similar. Mesopores are present in ex-FeZSM-5 due to dealumination during steam treatment, which are absent in ex-Fe-silicalite. Coke formation physically blocks the active reaction sites within the zeolitic pore system, leading to a decreased C3H6 yield. The nature and location of the active extraframework iron species play a crucial role in the deactivation behavior. Additionally, ex-FeZSM-5 shows significantly higher activity in direct N2O decomposition compared to ex-Fe-silicalite.

        The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are influenced by the synthesis methods used. The key performance metrics, including C3H8 conversion, N2O conversion, C3H6 selectivity, and C3H6 yield, demonstrate their effectiveness under different reaction conditions. These findings provide a robust foundation for further exploration into the optimization of catalysts and the enhancement of reaction efficiency in this field.

        基于\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2004.02.013\]参考文献的深度分析。从\[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032\]和\[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]的参考文献中获得的综合理解。各种合成方法，包括湿离子交换（WIE）、蒸汽处理、固体离子交换和液体交换，对铁基ZSM-5沸石在N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）反应中的催化性能产生了显著影响。这些研究强调了WIE，尤其是使用硫酸铁和莫尔斯盐的方法，因其高催化活性和催化剂制造的便利性而受到青睐。在WIE过程中，铁盐和沸石悬浮液的pH值对在N2O分解中实现高活性起着关键作用。沸石的拓扑结构也是影响催化剂在实际应用条件下的活性和稳定性的主要因素。过度交换，旨在达到Fe/Al比例为1，可以通过化学气相沉积（CVD）方法实现，对于在N2O分解中获得高活性并非必要条件。此外，WIE产生的催化剂中，小于50%的交换位点被Fe占据。

        Fe-ZSM-5，通过固体交换法使用二茂铁作为铁前驱体制备，表现出与N2O辅助的ODHP反应中丙烷的优越性能。经过剧烈煅烧后，Fe-ZSM-5在0.037 s−1的高周转频率下显示出最大初始丙烯产率为30%。识别出的FeOAl物种通过硝酸氧化物的化学吸附形成稳定的单氧物种，然后与气体丙烷反应生成具有高选择性的丙烯。反应过程中积累的主要有机物种为烷基苯。值得注意的是，Fe-ZSM-5保持稳定的丙烷转化率为约13 mmol gcat−1 h−1，丙烯生产速率为约6 mmol gcat−1 h−1，在N2O/C3H8比为1:2的情况下持续超过40小时。

        蒸汽活化的Fe-沸石，特别是FeZSM-5，是ODHP反应中高度活性和选择性的催化剂，能够在723 K的温度下和每克催化剂400,000毫升/小时的空速下，实现高达25%的丙烯产率。在蒸汽处理期间产生的额外框架铁物种是产生活性催化剂的必要条件。FeZSM-5表现出对抗失活的显著抵抗力，而FeBEA则经历快速且完全的失活。FeZSM-5和FeBEA的初始丙烯产率明显高于商业化的H-ZSM-5和H-BEA，后者铁含量低30-40倍。然而，它们在连续操作400分钟后残留的活性相当。在蒸汽处理期间，ex-FeZSM-5中存在由于去铝化而形成的介孔结构，而在ex-Fe-silicalite中不存在。焦炭的形成物理阻塞了多孔系统内的活性反应位点，导致C3H6产率降低。活性额外框架铁物种的性质和位置在失活行为中扮演着关键角色。此外，ex-FeZSM-5在直接N2O分解中的活性显著高于ex-Fe-silicalite。

        铁基ZSM-5沸石在N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）反应中的有效性和性能受其合成方法的影响。关键性能指标，如C3H8转化率、N2O转化率、C3H6选择性和C3H6产率，证明了在不同反应条件下它们的有效性。这些发现为进一步优化催化剂和提高反应效率提供了坚实的基础。通过进一步探索改进合成条件以最大化催化剂的活性和稳定性、开发更高效的催化剂支撑以及探索结合多种催化剂以实现更高转化率和选择性的协同效应，可以推动这一领域的研究进展。此外，通过改变反应温度、压力和气体流速等实验条件来研究新型反应条件，有望发现更有效的操作参数，从而提高ODHP反应的效率。进一步地，发展实时监控技术来跟踪催化剂的活性和失活机制的变化，将有助于深入了解反应动力学，并为设计更稳健的催化剂系统提供洞见。结合先进的材料科学、计算化学和过程工程方法，有可能解锁铁基ZSM-5沸石在N2O辅助氧化脱氢过程中性能提升的新可能性，为可持续和高效的丙烷转换技术开辟道路。
