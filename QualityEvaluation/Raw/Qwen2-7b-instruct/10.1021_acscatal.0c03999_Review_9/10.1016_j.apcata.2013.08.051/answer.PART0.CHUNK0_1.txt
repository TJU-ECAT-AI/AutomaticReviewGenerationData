<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The synthesis methods of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites, including steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange, influence their catalytic performance in the N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction. The key performance metrics that demonstrate their effectiveness under different reaction conditions are C3H8 conversion, N2O conversion, C3H6 selectivity, and C3H6 yield.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
