[1] "Ethane and Propane Oxydehydrogenation over Alkaline Metal Modified Fe-ZSM-5 Zeolites"
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study investigates the influence of various synthesis methods on the catalytic performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites in the N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction. The methods include steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange. Key performance metrics include C3H8 conversion, N2O conversion, C3H6 selectivity, and C3H6 yield under different reaction conditions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
