<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The process of selective oxygenate reforming contributes to sustainable energy and chemical production by utilizing bio-ethanol as a renewable energy carrier for hydrogen production. Key advantages over traditional methods include:

1. **Product Yield**: Bimetallic catalysts, alloy catalysts, and double-bed reactors enhance hydrogen production, potentially increasing yield efficiency.
2. **Environmental Impact**: By using bio-ethanol derived from biomass fermentation, the process reduces reliance on fossil fuels, thereby decreasing greenhouse gas emissions and promoting sustainability.
3. **Practical Implementation**: Autothermal reforming reduces the need for external heat input, making the process more cost-effective and efficient, especially considering the integration of the ethanol feedstock for providing low-grade thermal energy. This also enhances the practicality of the technology for widespread adoption.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

选择性氧化物重整过程通过将生物乙醇作为可再生能源载体用于氢气生产，对可持续能源和化学生产做出了贡献。与传统方法相比，其关键优势包括：

1. **产品产量**：双金属催化剂、合金催化剂和双床反应器的发展可以增强氢气生产，可能提高产量效率。
2. **环境影响**：通过利用生物质发酵产生的生物乙醇作为原料，该过程减少了对化石燃料的依赖，从而降低了温室气体排放，促进了可持续性。
3. **实施可行性**：自热重整减少了对外部热输入的需求，使过程更经济、更高效，特别是考虑到使用乙醇原料提供低级热能，这也有利于技术的广泛应用。
