Often, the interactions between the two metals are complex and largely unknown, and consequently there are excellent opportunities for preparing bimetallic catalysts with new properties."

It offers several advantages over traditional methods, including higher product yields, lower environmental impact, and improved efficiency. [3] "The key advantages of selective oxygenate reforming compared to traditional methods are:

- Lower environmental impact because the process uses fewer energy inputs and produces less waste.
