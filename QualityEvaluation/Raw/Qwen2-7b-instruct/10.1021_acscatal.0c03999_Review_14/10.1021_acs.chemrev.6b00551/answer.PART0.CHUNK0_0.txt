<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants, the literature emphasizes the use of catalyst systems such as transition metal/metal oxides and main group element metal oxides. These systems are targeted for enhancing selectivity and conversion rates in the process. Persistent challenges in ODHP revolve around the corrosiveness of the reaction and its inherent properties, which pose difficulties in achieving high efficiency and longevity. Future research directions are anticipated to focus on improving the activation of C-H bonds, enhancing the regenerability of catalysts, and optimizing the reaction systems to achieve more efficient and sustainable ODHP processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
