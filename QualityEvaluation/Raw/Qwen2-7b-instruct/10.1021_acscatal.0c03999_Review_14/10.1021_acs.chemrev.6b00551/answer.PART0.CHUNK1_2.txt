<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants, various catalyst systems have been investigated, including transition metals and main group elements. Transition metals like iron, cobalt, and ruthenium have shown potential in enhancing ODHP efficiency, while main group elements such as tin, antimony, and tellurium have also been explored. Challenges persist due to the corrosiveness of the reaction environment and the exothermic nature of the reaction. Future research aims to improve C-H bond activation, catalyst regenerability, and optimize reaction systems to overcome these obstacles.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
