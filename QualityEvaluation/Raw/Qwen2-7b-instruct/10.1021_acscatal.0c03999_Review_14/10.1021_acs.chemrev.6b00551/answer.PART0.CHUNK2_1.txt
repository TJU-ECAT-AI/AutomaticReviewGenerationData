1. Recent advancements in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) have focused on developing new catalyst systems. Transition metal/metal oxides have demonstrated promising outcomes, while main group element metal oxides have also been explored. Challenges in this field encompass the corrosiveness of the reaction environment and the inherent properties that impact selectivity and conversion. Future research priorities include enhancing C-H bond activation, improving catalyst recyclability, and refining reaction protocols to optimize ODHP processes.
