[1] "New active and selective catalysts for propane to propene dehydrogenation in assistance of CO2 (CO2-PDH) were prepared by chromium incorporation in siliceous SiBeta."
[2] "Comparing of catalytic activity of Cr-containing Beta zeolite catalysts with the identical Cr content (2 wt % of Cr) supported on SiBeta and AlBeta revealed that the dealumination has great impact on catalytic properties."
[3] "High dispersion and presence of redox chromium species are important factors for achieving high catalytic performance in CO2-PDH."

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The document highlights recent advancements in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using chromium oxide-based catalysts. The dealumination process significantly impacts the catalytic properties, enabling high dispersion and presence of redox chromium species essential for achieving high catalytic performance in CO2-PDH. This method yields excellent catalysts for propane to propene dehydrogenation, particularly when supported on siliceous SiBeta zeolite.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
