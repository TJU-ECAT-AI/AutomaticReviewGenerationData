            Recent studies have illuminated the utilization of soft oxidants, such as oxygen and carbon dioxide, in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) over catalysts like chromium (Cr)-MCM-41, nickel (Ni)/silica, metal sulfides, and alkali metal chlorides. These catalysts have been pivotal in dehydrogenating propane to propene, with chromium's reduction and subsequent reoxidation by carbon dioxide playing a critical role in the reaction mechanism. The large difference in nickel particle size significantly impacts the kinetic parameters, CO and CH4 formation pathways, and the reaction selectivity. Metal sulfides, particularly when used with sulfur vapor as an oxidant, have shown promise in avoiding overoxidation to COx, offering a novel approach to propylene production. Supported alkali metal chloride melts have emerged as a new class of catalysts, minimizing metal ion surface concentration and providing a dynamic surface that minimizes unsaturation of ions, enhancing olefin selectivity.

    Persistent challenges in this field include corrosion and intrinsic reaction properties, which necessitate further research on improving C-H bond activation, catalyst regenerability, and optimizing reaction systems to enhance ODHP processes. Strategies for addressing these challenges might involve refining the open structure of catalysts to improve product transport, developing mesopores through desilication to mitigate deactivation, and exploring more open pore architectures.

    Key areas for future research and development include enhancing selectivity and conversion rates, addressing reaction properties, and improving catalyst stability and efficiency. This includes strategies for improving C-H bond activation, enhancing catalyst regenerability, and renovating reaction systems to optimize ODHP processes.

            最近的研究揭示了使用软氧化剂，如氧气和二氧化碳，在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）过程中的应用，特别是在铬（Cr）-MCM-41、镍（Ni）/二氧化硅、金属硫化物和碱金属氯化物催化剂上。这些催化剂在使用氮氧化物作为氧化剂时，成功地将丙烷转化为丙烯，其中铬的还原和随后由二氧化碳的再氧化在反应机制中起着关键作用。镍颗粒尺寸的巨大差异显著影响了动力学参数、CO和CH4的形成途径以及反应选择性。

    这个领域持续面临的挑战包括腐蚀和固有的反应特性，这需要进一步研究以改善C-H键活化、催化剂再生能力和优化反应系统，以提高ODHP过程的效率。为解决这些挑战，可能的策略包括改进催化剂结构以促进产物传输，通过脱硅开发介孔结构来减轻失活，并探索更开放的孔结构架构。

    未来研究和发展的关键领域包括提高选择性和转化率、解决反应特性、改进催化剂稳定性和效率。这包括改进C-H键活化、增强催化剂再生能力和优化反应系统的方法。
