<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The recent advancements in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants have primarily focused on the creation of new catalyst systems. The effectiveness of these systems, which encompass transition metal/metal oxides and main group element metal oxides, has been scrutinized based on their selectivity and conversion rates. Persistent issues in this sector involve the corrosiveness of materials and the intrinsic characteristics of the reactions. Future research directions aim to enhance C-H bond activation, improve catalyst regeneration capabilities, and refine reaction systems to optimize the ODHP process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
