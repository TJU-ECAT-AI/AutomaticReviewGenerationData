<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Research has been conducted on the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) utilizing soft oxidants. Different catalyst systems, such as transition metals and their oxides along with main group elements and their oxides, have been evaluated based on their efficiency. Challenges faced in this area involve corrosion resistance and the nature of the reactions themselves. Future research directions are centered around enhancing the activation of carbon-hydrogen bonds, improving the recyclability of catalysts, and refining the reaction systems to better optimize the ODHP processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
