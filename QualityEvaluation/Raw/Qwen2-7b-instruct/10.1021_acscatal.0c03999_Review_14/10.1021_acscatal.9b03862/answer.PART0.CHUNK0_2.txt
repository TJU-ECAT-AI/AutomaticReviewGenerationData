<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Recent advancements in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants demonstrate their potential in chemical synthesis. Catalyst systems, like transition metal/metal oxides and main group element metal oxides, show distinct performance characteristics. Transition metal/metal oxide systems typically exhibit higher yields in terms of selectivity and conversion rates compared to other catalysts.

2. Challenges encountered in ODHP processes include corrosiveness due to reaction conditions and the inherent properties of the reaction, which can impact the efficiency and durability of catalysts.

3. Future research directions focus on enhancing C-H bond activation, improving catalyst regenerability, and refining reaction systems to optimize ODHP processes and control product selectivity and yield more effectively.
