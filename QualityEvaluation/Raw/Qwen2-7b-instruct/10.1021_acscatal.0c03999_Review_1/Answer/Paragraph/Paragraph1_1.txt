        The study by \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\] elucidates that among various metals tested, chromium (Cr) on MCM-41-DHT exhibits the highest activity, followed by gallium (Ga), nickel (Ni), vanadium (V), iron (Fe), manganese (Mn), and cobalt (Co) for the CO2 dehydrogenation of propane. The most promising Cr-MCM-41-DHT catalyst demonstrates a 17.0% propane conversion and 15.9% propene yield under specific conditions. The Cr species in tetrahedral coordination forms an active monochromate species, which is reduced to a less active octahedral coordination species during the reaction. The regeneration of deactivated catalysts through oxygen treatment facilitates the reoxidation of Cr(III) species to Cr(VI), showcasing the dynamic evolution of chromium species.

        In another investigation, \[10.1016/j.apcata.2018.03.020\], it was found that the addition of CO2 to the oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) reaction of propane over Cr/ZrO2 catalysts significantly improves the yield of olefins. The use of different metal oxides as supports for Cr2O3, as explored in \[10.1006/jcat.2001.3276\], was found to influence the catalytic performance, with chromium content having a notable impact on alkane conversion and olefin selectivity.

        The study by \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\] highlights the role of nickel (Ni) as a promoter, which makes CO2 a more effective oxidant and regenerates reduced Cr2+ states back to active Cr3+ states, enhancing the catalytic stability of propylene in the ODHP reaction. This finding underscores the synergistic effect of Ni in improving the performance of supported Cr2O3 catalysts.

        The work in \[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\] reveals that the surface area-to-metal loading ratio determines the formation of vanadium oxide species, with monomeric species predominating below 1.2 V/nm2, polymeric species between 1.2 and 4.4 V/nm2, and crystalline V2O5 species at higher loadings. The reducibility of vanadium species depends on their molecular structure, with VOV and VO bonds being more easily reduced than VO-support bonds. The presence of hydroxyl groups on the support surface aids dispersion and anchors vanadium precursors, thus influencing the catalytic performance.

        Further insights from \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2019.04.016\] indicate that chromium-doped mesoporous silica spheres prepared by metal vapor synthesis demonstrate high efficiency for propane dehydrogenation in CO2 presence, with the performance of Cr2O3 catalysts varying with chromium loading and support properties. The evolution of chromium species within supported Cr2O3 catalysts is critical for achieving high activity and selectivity in CO2-ODHP.

        The study by \[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.05.002\] introduces hydrothermally prepared Cr2O3-ZrO2 catalysts as an innovative approach for dehydrogenating propane with CO2, emphasizing the role of chromium species dispersion on silica surfaces in determining catalytic performance.

        The analysis in \[10.1021/cr5002436\] underscores the importance of understanding the nature of catalytically active chromium sites and the preparation of highly dispersed chromium oxide catalysts supported on mesoporous silica for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane.         基于\[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\]参考文献的深度分析。从\[10.1016/j.apcata.2018.03.020\]、\[10.1006/jcat.2001.3276\]、\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]、\[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\]、\[10.1016/j.micromeso.2019.04.016\]、\[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.05.002\]、\[10.1021/cr5002436\]、\[10.1016/j.molcata.2004.12.006\]和\[10.1021/cs5003417\]的参考文献中获得的综合理解。

        对于CO2-ODHP的金属氧化物催化剂的比较评估强调了在支持的Cr2O3催化剂中Cr物种演化的关键作用，受到负载和支撑材料属性的影响。这一分析强调了这些催化剂的结构和活性之间的复杂关系，特别是为了增强活性而采取的增强策略。

        \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\]研究揭示了在测试的各种金属中，Cr在MCM-41-DHT上的活动性最高，随后是Ga、Ni、V、Fe、Mn和Co，用于丙烷的CO2脱氢。最有效的Cr-MCM-41-DHT催化剂在特定条件下表现出17.0%的丙烷转化率和15.9%的丙烯产率。Cr物种以四面体配合物形式形成活性单铬酸盐物种，在反应过程中被还原为八面体配合物形式的较不活跃多铬酸盐物种。通过氧气处理再生失活的催化剂导致Cr(III)物种重新氧化为Cr(VI)，展示了Cr物种演化的动态性。

        \[10.1016/j.apcata.2018.03.020\]的研究表明，向Cr/ZrO2催化剂中的丙烷氧化脱氢（ODH）反应添加CO2显著提高了烯烃的产率。使用不同金属氧化物作为Cr2O3的支持，如在\[10.1006/jcat.2001.3276\]中所研究的，对催化性能产生了影响，尤其是Cr含量对烷基转化和烯烃选择性的影响。

        \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]的工作揭示了镍（Ni）作为促进剂的作用，这使CO2成为更有效的氧化剂，并将还原的Cr2+状态重新氧化回活性Cr3+状态，从而增强丙烯在ODHP反应中的催化稳定性。这一发现强调了Ni在提高支持Cr2O3催化剂性能方面的协同作用。

        \[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\]的研究表明，表面面积与金属负载比率决定了钒氧化物物种的形成，其中单体物种在低于1.2 V/nm2时占主导地位，聚集体物种位于1.2至4.4 V/nm2之间，而结晶V2O5物种则在较高负载时出现。VOV和VO键比VO-支持键更容易还原。支持表面的羟基团有助于分散并锚定钒前体，从而影响催化性能。

        \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2019.04.016\]的工作指出，通过金属蒸汽合成制备的铬掺杂介孔二氧化硅球显示出高效率，用于CO2存在的丙烷脱氢，催化剂性能的差异取决于铬负载和支撑性质。支持Cr2O3催化剂中Cr物种的演变对于实现CO2-ODHP的高活性和选择性至关重要。

        \[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.05.002\]引入了水热法准备的Cr2O3-ZrO2催化剂作为脱氢丙烷与CO2的新颖方法，强调了在二氧化硅表面上分散的铬物种对催化性能的重要性。

        \[10.1021/cr5002436\]的分析强调了理解活性铬位点的性质和通过金属蒸汽合成制备Cr-MCM-41催化剂用于CO2-ODHP的重要性。它比较了各种金属氧化物催化剂的性能，重点关注了支持Cr2O3催化剂中Cr物种的演变作为负载和支撑属性的函数。

        \[10.1016/j.molcata.2004.12.006\]的工作提供了有关活性铬位点的性质和通过金属蒸汽合成制备Cr-MCM-41催化剂用于CO2-ODHP的见解，证明了具有65%转化率和超过90%选择性的苯乙烯的生产。该假设表明，Cr(VI)物种在反应过程中从四面体到八面体的演变，最初作为活性单铬酸盐物种，然后减少为较不活跃的多铬酸盐物种。

        总之，对于CO2-ODHP的金属氧化物催化剂的比较评估强调了在支持的Cr2O3催化剂中Cr物种演化的关键作用，受到负载和支撑材料属性的影响。\[10.1021/cs300198u\]和\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_1\]的研究进一步强调了理解催化性能与基本因素之间的关系的重要性，如金属氧化物分散性和支持特性，以提高活性。

        集成的理解与未来展望，考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现，有探索新兴领域和创新方法的机会。未来的研究努力可能会集中在开发优化的结构-活性关系的新型催化剂，设计促进金属物种分散和演化的高级支撑，以及整合计算方法来预测和理解催化剂系统内的复杂相互作用。此外，多金属氧化物的开发或金属氧化物与其他功能性材料的结合也可能导致多功能催化剂的创造，这些催化剂具有增强的性能，用于CO2-ODHP和其他可持续化学转换。
