Chromium oxide catalysts, notably those supported on mesoporous silica or ZrO2, demonstrate high activity and selectivity for the conversion of propane to propylene, with chromium species evolving in relation to loading and support properties. Vanadium oxide catalysts also show promise, with the formation of active monochromate species in tetrahedral coordination and less active polychromate species in octahedral coordination during the reaction. The surface area-to-metal loading ratio plays a crucial role in determining the type of species that form, influencing catalytic performance.

            The role of support properties is also paramount, affecting the dispersion and evolution of chromium species, thereby determining intrinsic activity. For instance, hydrothermally prepared Cr2O3-ZrO2 catalysts show high activity in the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene, indicating the importance of support characteristics in enhancing catalytic performance. Metal vapor synthesis techniques enable the optimization of systems in terms of activity per Cr atom, allowing for the preparation of highly dispersed chromium species on the silica surface.

            The study of metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP emphasizes the importance of understanding the structure-activity relationships, particularly in relation to chromium species evolution. This understanding can guide the development of more efficient catalysts with improved activity and selectivity, potentially revolutionizing the production of valuable chemicals from hydrocarbons through the utilization of CO2 as a mild oxidant.

            这些研究的关键发现强调了金属氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODHP过程中的比较活性和选择性，特别是铬氧化物（Cr2O3）和钒氧化物催化剂。支持在硅胶或ZrO2上的铬氧化物催化剂显示出高活性和选择性，将丙烷转化为丙烯，铬物种根据加载量和支撑属性演化。钒氧化物催化剂也表现出潜力，其中形成活性单铬酸盐在四面体协调和较不活跃的多铬酸盐在八面体协调期间反应。表面面积与金属负载比在确定形成的物种类型中起着关键作用，影响催化性能。

            支撑特性的重要性也至关重要，影响铬物种的分散和演化，从而决定内在活性。例如，通过直接水热合成制备的Cr2O3-ZrO2催化剂在乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯时显示出高活性，这表明支撑特性在提高催化性能方面的作用。金属蒸汽合成技术允许优化系统中每Cr原子的活性，允许在硅胶表面上准备高度分散的铬物种。

            对金属氧化物催化剂进行CO2-ODHP的评估强调了理解结构-活性关系的重要性，特别是在铬物种演化的背景下。这种理解可以指导开发更高效的催化剂，具有改进的活性和选择性，可能彻底改变通过将CO2用作温和氧化剂来生产有价值的化学品的过程。
