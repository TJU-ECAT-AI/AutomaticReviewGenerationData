<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study focuses on the preparation of highly dispersed chromium oxide catalysts supported on mesoporous silica for the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane using CO2, aiming to understand the nature of catalytically active chromium sites.
2. The investigation examines how different metal oxide catalysts compare in terms of their activity and selectivity for CO2-oxidative dehydrogenation (ODHP), considering factors such as the evolution of chromium species within supported chromium(III) oxide catalysts based on their loading and support properties.
