<quotes>
The nature of the support is important in affecting the dispersion and evolution of Cr species, therefore determining the intrinsic activity. Among support materials, metal oxides have been widely used, such as Al2O3, ZrO2, CeO2, TiO2, and SiO2. For better Cr dispersion, ordered, mesoporous materials, such as SBA-1/SBA-15/MCM-41, and carbon-based supports with tunable surface functional groups are also promising candidates.
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
支撑材料的性质对Cr物种的分散性和演化有重要影响，从而决定了内在活性。在支撑材料中，广泛使用了如Al2O3、ZrO2、CeO2、TiO2和SiO2等金属氧化物。为了更好地分散Cr，有序、介孔材料，如SBA-1/SBA-15/MCM-41，以及具有可调表面功能团的碳基支撑材料也是有前景的选择。
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
