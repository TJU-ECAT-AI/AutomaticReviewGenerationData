<quotes>
[1] "The oxidative dehydrogenation process (ODHP) is an interesting approach for the production of valuable chemicals from hydrocarbons."
[2] "The addition of CO2 in the ODHP over supported Cr2O3 catalysts has been studied, aiming to improve the yield of olefins."
[3] "The use of different metal oxides as supports for Cr2O3 was investigated to evaluate their effect on the catalytic performance."
[4] "The evolution of chromium species in the catalysts was influenced by the loading and support properties."
[5] "A comparison of the activity and selectivity for CO2-ODHP among various metal oxide catalysts was conducted."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines how various metal oxide catalysts affect the activity and selectivity in the CO2-oxidative dehydrogenation process (CO2-ODHP) of propane. The focus is on the impact of different support materials and chromium loading on the catalytic performance, particularly the transformation of chromium species within the supported Cr2O3 catalysts. This investigation aims to optimize the process for enhanced yield of olefins from hydrocarbons.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
