1. The addition of CO2 in the oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) reaction of propane over Cr/ZrO2 catalysts enhances the conversion of propane and significantly improves the selectivity towards propene. 
2. The catalytic performance of the Cr/ZrO2 catalysts is influenced by the type of metal oxide catalyst, loading, and support properties, with a particular focus on the evolution of chromium species in supported Cr2O3 catalysts.
