<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The primary challenges in PDH for industrial propylene production involve achieving high-temperature stability, maintaining high propylene selectivity, preventing side reactions, and overcoming thermodynamic limitations. Researchers are addressing these needs by developing catalysts with improved stability and selectivity, utilizing metals with strong interaction capabilities, and enhancing confinement effects through zeolite supports.

2. The economic attractiveness and environmental benefits of PDH increase with the availability of abundant shale gas containing light alkanes. Improving the activity of Pt-based catalysts, reducing the use of precious metals like platinum, and lowering catalyst costs are crucial goals in PDH research.

3. Introducing a second metal species with a strong interaction has been shown to significantly enhance the activity and stability of Pt species in PDH reactions. This approach aims to optimize catalyst performance and efficiency.

4. The migration of subnanometric Pt species during PDH reactions has been identified as a factor contributing to the deactivation of Pt-Sn catalysts. Understanding this mechanism helps in designing catalysts that minimize Pt species movement and enhance overall stability.

5. By leveraging the known activity of Pt and Pt-Zn in PDH reactions and the confinement effect provided by Si-Beta zeolite, researchers have designed Pt-Zn/Si-Beta catalysts. These catalysts offer a promising solution for industrial PDH processes due to their ability to achieve high propylene production rates, selectivities, and deactivation rates.

6. The optimized Pt-Zn/Si-Beta catalyst achieves remarkable performance, including a high propylene production rate of 4.11 mol/hr, a high propylene selectivity of 98%, and a sustainable deactivation rate of 0.02 h−1 at 823 K.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. PDH工业丙烯生产的首要挑战包括高温稳定性、高丙烯选择性、防止副反应和克服热力学限制。研究人员通过开发具有改进稳定性和选择性的催化剂、利用具有强相互作用能力的金属以及通过沸石支持增强限制效应，来应对这些需求。

2. 由于丰富的轻烷烃富含的页岩气的存在，PDH在经济性和环境性方面的吸引力增加。提高铂基催化剂的活性、减少对贵重金属如铂的使用以及降低催化剂成本是PDH研究的关键目标。

3. 引入与强大相互作用的第二金属物种已被证明可以显著促进PDH反应中铂物种的活性和稳定性。这种方法旨在优化催化剂性能和效率。

4. 在PDH反应过程中，亚纳米级铂物种的迁移被确定为Pt-Sn催化剂失活的因素之一。理解这一机制有助于设计能够最小化铂物种移动并增强整体稳定性的催化剂。

5. 通过利用铂和铂锌在PDH反应中的已知活性以及Si-Beta沸石提供的限制效应，研究人员设计了铂锌/Si-Beta催化剂。这些催化剂因能够实现高丙烯生产速率、选择性和失活率而为工业PDH过程提供了一种有前景的解决方案。

6. 最优的铂锌/Si-Beta催化剂实现了令人瞩目的性能，包括4.11 mol/hr的高丙烯生产速率、98%的高丙烯选择性以及在823 K下的可持续失活率为0.02 h−1。
