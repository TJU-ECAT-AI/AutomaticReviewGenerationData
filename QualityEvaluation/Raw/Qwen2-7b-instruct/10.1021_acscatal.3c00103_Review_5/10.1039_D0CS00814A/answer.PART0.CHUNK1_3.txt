<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. The primary challenges in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) for industrial propylene production include achieving high conversion, maintaining high selectivity, and ensuring stability at high temperatures. Researchers are focusing on developing catalysts with optimized structures to address these issues. 

2. Thermodynamic limitations pose a challenge for PDH, particularly for direct dehydrogenation routes. Oxygen-assisted dehydrogenation (ODH) could improve efficiency, but it requires overcoming overoxidation of propylene to COx. 

3. Alternative catalysts, such as metal oxides and transition metal nitrides and carbides, are being explored as economic alternatives to traditional noble metal catalysts. 

4. Identifying the active sites and mechanisms under reaction conditions is critical. Operando/in situ characterization techniques are essential for advancing understanding. 

5. Theoretical calculations are used to aid in interpreting experimental results, but establishing a universal relationship between structure and activity remains a challenge.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1. PDH工业丙烯生产的主要挑战包括实现高转化率，保持高选择性，并在高温下确保稳定性。研究人员正在集中精力开发具有优化结构的催化剂来解决这些问题。

2. 热力学限制对PDH构成挑战，特别是对于直接脱氢路径。氧辅助脱氢（ODH）可以提高效率，但需要克服丙烯过氧化为COx的问题。

3. 作为传统贵金属催化剂的经济替代品，正在探索金属氧化物和过渡金属氮化物和碳化物等替代催化剂。

4. 确定反应条件下的活性位点和机制至关重要。原位/操作性表征技术对于推进理解是必不可少的。

5. 理论计算用于帮助解释实验结果，但在结构与活性之间的建立普遍关系仍然是一个挑战。
