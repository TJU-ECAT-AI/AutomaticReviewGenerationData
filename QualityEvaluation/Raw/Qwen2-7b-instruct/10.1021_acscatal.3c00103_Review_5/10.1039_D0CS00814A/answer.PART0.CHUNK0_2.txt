<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The research team at Tianjin University, led by Professor Jinlong Gong, includes several PhD candidates and researchers focused on the development of catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. This includes work on nanostructured catalysts, alloy catalyst design, and the application of theoretical approaches to guide the creation of catalysts. Key contributors include Sai Chen, Xin Chang, Guodong Sun, Tingting Zhang, and Chunlei Pei. These individuals specialize in different aspects of the project, such as alkane dehydrogenation, chemical looping reforming, and the use of advanced materials like carbon oxides and nanostructured materials. Professor Jinlong Gong himself has a background in chemical engineering, with a PhD from the University of Texas at Austin, and has conducted postdoctoral research at Harvard University. His expertise spans the areas of catalytic energy conversion, carbon oxide utilization, and the synthesis and application of nanostructured materials. This team is working at the forefront of developing highly efficient and selective catalysts for the production of propylene through propane dehydrogenation.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
天津大学由金龙教授领导的研究团队包括多位博士研究生和研究人员，专注于丙烷脱氢催化剂的研发。这涵盖了纳米结构催化剂、合金催化剂设计以及理论指导下的催化剂开发等多个方面。关键参与者包括蔡赛、常欣、孙国栋、张婷婷和裴春雷等人。他们分别在烷烃脱氢、循环重整等领域有着各自的专长。金龙教授拥有化学工程背景，博士学位来自德克萨斯大学奥斯汀分校，并曾在哈佛大学担任乔治·M·怀特塞兹教授的博士后研究员。他的研究兴趣集中在绿色能源的催化转换、碳氧化物的利用以及纳米结构材料的合成与应用。这个团队正致力于开发高效、高选择性的催化剂，用于通过丙烷脱氢生产丙烯。
