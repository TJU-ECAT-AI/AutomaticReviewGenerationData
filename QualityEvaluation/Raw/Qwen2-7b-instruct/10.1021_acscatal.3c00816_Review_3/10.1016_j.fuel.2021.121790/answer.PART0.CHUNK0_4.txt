<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation by enabling rapid formation of metal clusters within the zeolite framework. This ensures a more homogeneous distribution of metals and facilitates improved catalytic performance, specifically in the context of propane conversion. The strategy leads to significant effects on the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, enhancing their efficiency in propane conversion compared to traditional synthesis methods.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
