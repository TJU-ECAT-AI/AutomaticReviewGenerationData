The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation. This method has been thoroughly investigated for its impact on the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, particularly concerning propane conversion.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
