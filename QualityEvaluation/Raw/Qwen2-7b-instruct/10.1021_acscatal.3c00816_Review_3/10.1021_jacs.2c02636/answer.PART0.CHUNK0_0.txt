The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges encountered in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation. This method notably influences the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, enhancing their efficiency and selectivity in propane conversion.
