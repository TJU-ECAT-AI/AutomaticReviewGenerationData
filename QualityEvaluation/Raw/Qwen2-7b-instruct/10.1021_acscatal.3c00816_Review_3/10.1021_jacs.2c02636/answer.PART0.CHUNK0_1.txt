The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges encountered in direct synthesis methods for metal encapsulation. This strategy influences the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, particularly in relation to their efficiency in propane conversion.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
