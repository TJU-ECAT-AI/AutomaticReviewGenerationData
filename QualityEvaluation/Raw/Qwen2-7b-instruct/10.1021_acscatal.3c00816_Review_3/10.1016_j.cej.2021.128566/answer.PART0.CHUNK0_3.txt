<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1.Yu et al. introduced an innovative ultrafast zeolite crystallization method to tackle the time mismatch issues in direct metal encapsulation synthesis processes. This technique enables swift zeolite structure formation, drastically cutting down the crystallization duration. The study focused on the impact of this ultrafast technique on the creation, architecture, and catalytic capabilities of PtSn clusters embedded in ZSM-5 zeolites, particularly in relation to propane transformation. The objective was to elevate the catalyst's efficiency and selectivity in this context.
