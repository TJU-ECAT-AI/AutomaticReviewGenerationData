<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. addresses the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation. It enables the rapid synthesis of metal-containing materials, including PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites. This method led to enhanced catalyst activity and selectivity in the context of propane conversion. Specifically, the formation of well-dispersed PtSn clusters with optimized geometries improved the catalytic performance of ZSM-5 zeolites.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
