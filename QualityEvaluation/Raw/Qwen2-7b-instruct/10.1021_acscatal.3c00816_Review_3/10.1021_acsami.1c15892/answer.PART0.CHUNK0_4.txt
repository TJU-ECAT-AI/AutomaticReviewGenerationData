The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges encountered in direct synthesis methods for metal encapsulation. This approach significantly impacts the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, showcasing high efficiency and coking resistance when applied to propane conversion.
