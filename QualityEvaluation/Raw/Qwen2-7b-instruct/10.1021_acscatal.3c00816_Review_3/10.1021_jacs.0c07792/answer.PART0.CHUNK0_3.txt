<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the issue of time-scale mismatch in direct synthesis methods for metal encapsulation by enabling quick zeolite structure formation. This is essential for the creation of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites. These catalysts, when applied to propane conversion, can enhance efficiency and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
