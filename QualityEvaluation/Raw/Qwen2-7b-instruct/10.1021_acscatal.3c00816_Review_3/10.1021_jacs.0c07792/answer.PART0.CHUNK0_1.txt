The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation. This strategy significantly impacts the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, thereby enhancing their efficiency and selectivity in propane conversion.
