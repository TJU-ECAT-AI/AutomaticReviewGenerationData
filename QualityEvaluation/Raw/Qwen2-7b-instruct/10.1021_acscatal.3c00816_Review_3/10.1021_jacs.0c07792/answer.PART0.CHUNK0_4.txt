The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. addresses the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation by accelerating the formation of the zeolite matrix, thereby enabling faster encapsulation of metal clusters. This strategy leads to the formation of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites that exhibit improved structural stability and catalytic performance, particularly in the context of propane conversion.
