<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. addresses the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation by enabling the direct synthesis of metal-encapsulated ZSM-5 zeolites. This approach significantly influences the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites. Specifically, the encapsulation of PtSn clusters within the ZSM-5 zeolite framework provides a stable and well-defined microenvironment for the metal nanoparticles, leading to enhanced catalytic activity for propane conversion.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
