<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. addresses the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation by allowing for the rapid formation of zeolite structures. This facilitates the incorporation of metal species, leading to the formation of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites. These clusters enhance the catalytic performance, specifically improving the efficiency of propane conversion through dehydrogenation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
由Yu等人开发的超快速沸石结晶策略解决了直接合成过程中金属封装的时间尺度不匹配挑战，通过快速形成沸石结构来实现这一目标。这使得金属物种的整合成为可能，从而在ZSM-5沸石中形成了PtSn簇。这些簇提高了催化剂性能，特别是通过脱氢提高了丙烷转化的效率。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
