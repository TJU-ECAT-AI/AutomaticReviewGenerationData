<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. tackles the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation. This approach facilitates the formation of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites, which are vital for propane conversion. By employing this technique, the researchers were able to regulate the size and structure of the PtSn clusters, thereby improving their catalytic performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
