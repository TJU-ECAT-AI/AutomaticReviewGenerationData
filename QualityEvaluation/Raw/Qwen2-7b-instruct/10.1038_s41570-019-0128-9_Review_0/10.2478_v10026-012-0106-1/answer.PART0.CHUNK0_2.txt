<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The aim of the research was to explore the potential uses of propane dehydrogenation products besides propene, based on thermodynamic analysis and catalytic testing. The study also proposed various integration pathways of the DHP-CO2 process with existing chemical processes that use synthesis gas.

2. Different strategies for integrating the DHP-CO2 process with commercial chemical processes that utilize synthesis gas were suggested.

3. Thermodynamic data for the substances were sourced from Chemcad II Version 2.60.

4. The CO2 interaction with the catalyst surfaces was assessed through FTIR spectroscopy.

5. The unfavorable impact of CO2 on the propane dehydrogenation on the Cr-O/Al2O3 catalyst was attributed to the characteristics of γ-Al2O3.

6. Thermodynamic modeling and experimental tests revealed that the synthesis gas composition (CO/H2 ratio) could be easily adjusted by modifying the CO2:C3H8 molar ratio in the feed.

7. A low CO/H2 ratio suitable for direct synthesis gas application in chemical processes could be achieved using a Cr-O/SiO2 catalyst with a low CO2 concentration in the feed.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 研究的目标是基于热力学分析和催化剂测试探讨丙烷脱氢产物除了丙烯之外的应用可能性。研究还提出了将DHP-CO2过程与使用合成气的现有化学过程相整合的不同策略。

2. 提出了几种将DHP-CO2过程与利用合成气的商用化学过程相集成的方法。

3. 物质的热力学数据来源于Chemcad II Version 2.60数据库。

4. 通过傅里叶变换红外光谱法（FTIR）研究了CO2与催化剂表面的相互作用。

5. 在Cr-O/Al2O3催化剂上观察到的CO2对丙烷脱氢的不利影响归因于γ-Al2O3的特性。

6. 简化模型的热力学计算和实验测试表明，可以通过调整进料中CO2:C3H8的摩尔比轻松控制合成气组成（CO/H2比例）。

7. 使用低CO2浓度的进料，Cr-O/SiO2催化剂可以获得适用于直接合成气应用的低CO/H2比例。
