<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Chromium-based catalysts have been found to be the most effective for CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions compared to others like SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2.
2. Fe5-Cr10/Zr, Mn5-Cr10/Zr, and Co5-Cr10/Zr nano-composite catalysts showed higher ethylene selectivity than the Ni5-Cr10/Zr nano-composite catalyst, though with lower CO2 and ethane conversions.
3. The type of modifier used impacts the catalyst's textural and structural properties, distribution of chromium species and oxygen species, and acid/base properties, collectively determining its overall performance in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation.
4. At 650°C, the catalytic activity order for modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts, based on ethylene yield, was Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 铬基催化剂在二氧化碳氧化脱氢反应中被发现是最有效的，与SiO2、MCM-41、H-ZSM-5、Al2O3、ZrO2和CeO2等其他催化剂相比。
2. Fe5-Cr10/Zr、Mn5-Cr10/Zr和Co5-Cr10/Zr纳米复合催化剂的乙烷选择性高于Ni5-Cr10/Zr纳米复合催化剂，但其二氧化碳和乙烷转化率较低。
3. 添加剂的类型影响催化剂的纹理和结构性质、铬物种和氧物种的分布以及催化剂表面的酸/碱性质，这些共同决定了使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂的乙烷脱氢过程中催化剂的整体性能。
4. 在650°C下，基于乙烯产量，修改后的Cr2O3/ZrO2纳米复合催化剂的催化活性顺序为Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
