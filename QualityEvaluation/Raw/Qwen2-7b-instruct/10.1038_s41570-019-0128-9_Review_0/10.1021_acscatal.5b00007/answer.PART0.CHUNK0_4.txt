Various support materials like SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2, along with CO2 partial pressure, temperature, and reaction time, have significant impacts on the activity, selectivity, stability, and mechanistic pathways of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.
