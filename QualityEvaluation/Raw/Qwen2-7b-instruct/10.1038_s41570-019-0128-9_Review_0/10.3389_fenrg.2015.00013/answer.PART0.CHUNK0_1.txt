<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The activity, selectivity, stability, and mechanistic pathways of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions are influenced by the use of different support materials including SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2. Additionally, the CO2 partial pressure has an impact on the overall performance of these catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
