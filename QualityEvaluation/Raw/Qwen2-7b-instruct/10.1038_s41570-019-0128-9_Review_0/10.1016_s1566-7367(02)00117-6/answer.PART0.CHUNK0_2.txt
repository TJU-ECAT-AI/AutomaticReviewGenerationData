1. Various support materials, including SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2, were used in the preparation of chromium oxide catalysts. Reaction conditions such as CO2 partial pressure significantly influence the activity, selectivity, stability, and mechanistic pathways of these catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

2. The activity, selectivity, stability, and mechanistic pathways of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions are influenced by factors such as the type of support material and reaction conditions. Key challenges include optimizing overall performance, considering trade-offs between activity, selectivity, and stability.

3. The W =F ratio plays a crucial role in determining the yield, selectivity, and rate of ethylene formation. Lower W =F ratios lead to lower yields and higher selectivities, while increasing the rate of ethylene formation.

4. The maximum rate of ethylene formation reached 0.516 mmol g-catalyst1 min1 at 923 K. Ethylene selectivity was over 90% at this rate.

5. The Cr/H-ZSM-5 SiO2=Al2O3 P 190 catalyst demonstrated the highest performance in the dehydrogenation of ethane using CO2 as an oxidant.

6. Carbon dioxide maintains the activity of the catalyst by removing coke from its surface, contributing to the catalyst's stability over time.

7. TPR profiles indicate the presence of high oxidation state Cr species such as Cr(VI) on the catalyst, which are believed to contribute to the higher dehydrogenation activity.

8. Cr species with high oxidation states are essential for achieving the observed catalyst performances in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

9. Challenges in optimizing the performance of chromium oxide catalysts include balancing the effects of different support materials and reaction conditions on activity, selectivity, stability, and mechanistic pathways.

1. 各种支持材料，包括SiO2、MCM-41、H-ZSM-5、Al2O3、ZrO2和CeO2，用于制备铬氧化物催化剂。反应条件如二氧化碳分压对这些催化剂在二氧化碳氧化脱氢反应中的活性、选择性、稳定性和机制路径有显著影响。

2. 铬氧化物催化剂在二氧化碳氧化脱氢反应中的活性、选择性、稳定性和机制路径受支持材料类型和反应条件的影响。关键挑战在于优化整体性能，并考虑活动性、选择性和稳定性之间的权衡。

3. W =F 比率在决定乙烷形成率、选择性和速率方面起着关键作用。较低的W =F 比率导致较低的产率和更高的选择性，同时增加乙烷形成速率。

4. 在923 K时达到的最大乙烷形成速率为0.516 mmol g-catalyst1 min1。乙烷选择性在该速率下超过90%。

5. Cr/H-ZSM-5 SiO2=Al2O3 P 190 催化剂在使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂的乙烷脱氢中表现出最高性能。

6. 二氧化碳通过去除催化剂表面的焦炭来维持活性，有助于催化剂的长期稳定性。

7. TPR轮廓表明催化剂上存在高氧化态Cr物种，如Cr(VI)，被认为是提高脱氢活性的关键。

8. 高氧化态的Cr物种对于实现CO2氧化脱氢反应过程中观察到的催化剂性能至关重要。

9. 优化铬氧化物催化剂性能的挑战包括平衡不同支持材料和反应条件对活性、选择性、稳定性和机制路径的影响。

10. 在CO2氧化脱氢过程中，需要仔细考虑各种因素以实现所需结果，从而实现催化剂性能参数的优化。
