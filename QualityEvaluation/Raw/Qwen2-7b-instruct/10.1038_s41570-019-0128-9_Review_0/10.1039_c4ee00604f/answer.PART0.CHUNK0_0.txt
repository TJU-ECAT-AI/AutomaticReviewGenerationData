<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The direct conversion of methane to ethylene involves two steps. Firstly, methane reacts with half of oxygen to form ethane and water, releasing 42.4 kJ/mol of energy. Secondly, methane reacts fully with oxygen to produce ethylene and water, releasing 67.4 kJ/mol of energy.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
