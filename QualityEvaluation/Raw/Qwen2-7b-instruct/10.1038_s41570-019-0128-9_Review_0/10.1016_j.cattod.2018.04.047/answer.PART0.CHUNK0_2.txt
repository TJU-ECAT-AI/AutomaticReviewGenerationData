<quotes>
[1] "The nature of the support influences the oxidation state of chromium species."
[2] "CrOx/γ-Al2O3 catalyst is characterized by formation of ethylene during the DDH accompanied by the RWGS."
[3] "Both selective ODH and DDH are the possible reaction pathways over CrOx/ZrO2 catalyst."
[4] "Unlike CrOx/CeO2 catalyst, the catalyst supported on CexZr(1-x)O2 contains chromium mainly in a high valence state (Cr V/VI) according to TPR-H2 and Raman results, and no α-Cr2O3 phase is detected."
[5] "The side reactions for CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 are similar to CrOx/ZrO2, i.e., non-selective oxidation or/and dry-reforming reactions."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The support material affects the oxidation state of chromium species in the catalyst.
2. The CrOx/γ-Al2O3 catalyst forms ethylene via direct dehydrogenation (DDH) accompanied by the reverse water-gas shift (RWGS).
3. Both selective oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) and direct dehydrogenation are possible pathways over the CrOx/ZrO2 catalyst.
4. The catalyst supported on CexZr(1-x)O2 contains chromium mainly in a high valence state (Cr V/VI) and lacks the α-Cr2O3 phase.
5. The side reactions for the CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 catalysts are similar to those of the CrOx/ZrO2 catalyst, involving non-selective oxidation or dry-reforming reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 支持材料影响催化剂中的铬物种的氧化状态。
2. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 催化剂通过直接脱氢（DDH）伴随逆水煤气变换（RWGS）形成乙烯。
3. CrOx/ZrO2 催化剂可能存在选择性氧化脱氢（ODH）和直接脱氢两种可能的反应途径。
4. 在CexZr(1-x)O2支持下的催化剂主要含有高价态的铬（Cr V/VI），并且没有检测到α-Cr2O3相。
5. CrOx/CeO2 和 CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 催化剂的副反应与CrOx/ZrO2催化剂相似，包括非选择性氧化或干式重整反应。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
