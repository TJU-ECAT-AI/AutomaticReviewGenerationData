<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The activity, selectivity, and stability of the chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions are significantly influenced by the chromium content. Optimization of chromium content is essential for enhancing the overall performance of the catalyst.
2. Various support materials including SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2 were found to have an impact on the catalytic properties of the chromium oxide catalysts.
3. The CO2 partial pressure was recognized as a critical factor affecting the catalytic performance in these reactions.
