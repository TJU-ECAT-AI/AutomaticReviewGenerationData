<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. This research focuses on the CO2-oxidative dehydrogenation of ethane to ethylene using a Cr/MCM-41 nanocatalyst synthesized through hydrothermal/impregnation methods. The effect of chromium content on catalytic properties and performance was studied. Multiple support materials including SiO2, MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2 were utilized. Various CO2 partial pressures were applied to optimize the process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
