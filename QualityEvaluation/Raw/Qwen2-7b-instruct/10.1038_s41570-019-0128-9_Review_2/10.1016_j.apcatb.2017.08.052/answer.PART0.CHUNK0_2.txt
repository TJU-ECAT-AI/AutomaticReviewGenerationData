<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The advanced in situ and ex situ analytical techniques, including XRD, XANES, EXAFS, and ADF-STEM with EELS, synergistically contribute to our understanding of the structural properties, oxidation states, spatial distribution, and morphological characteristics of active sites in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 catalysts. These techniques provide valuable information on the catalyst's structure, which is crucial for understanding its performance. Key differences in the structural properties, such as the spatial arrangement of metals and their oxidation states, influence the catalyst's performance and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这些先进的原位和离线分析技术，包括XRD、XANES、EXAFS以及ADF-STEM结合EELS，协同作用于我们对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Fe1Ni3/CeO2催化剂中活性位点的结构性质、氧化态、空间分布和形态特征的理解。这些方法提供了催化剂结构的重要信息，这对于理解其性能至关重要。结构性质的关键差异，如金属的空间排列及其氧化态，影响催化剂的性能和选择性。
