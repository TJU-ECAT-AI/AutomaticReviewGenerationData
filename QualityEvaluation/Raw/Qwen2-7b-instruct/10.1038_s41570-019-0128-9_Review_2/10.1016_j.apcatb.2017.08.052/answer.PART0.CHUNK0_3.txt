<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The advanced in situ and ex situ analytical techniques, including XRD, XANES, EXAFS, and ADF-STEM with EELS, contribute significantly to our understanding of the structural properties, oxidation states, spatial distribution, and morphological characteristics of active sites in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 catalysts. These techniques provide detailed insights that elucidate the key differences in the structural properties and catalytic activities between the two catalysts, which are essential for their performance and design.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这些高级原位和离线分析技术，包括X射线衍射（XRD）、X射线吸收近边结构（XANES）、扩展X射线吸收精细结构（EXAFS）以及校正的扫描透射电子显微镜（ADF-STEM）配备能量色散X射线光谱仪（EELS），对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Fe1Ni3/CeO2催化剂中的活性位点的结构性质、氧化状态、空间分布和形态特征提供了全面的理解。这些技术提供的详细信息揭示了两种催化剂之间关键的结构特性和催化活性差异，这对于它们的性能和设计至关重要。
