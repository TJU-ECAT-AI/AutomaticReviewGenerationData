<EnglishVersionAnswer>
These advanced in situ and ex situ analytical techniques, including XRD, XANES, EXAFS, and ADF-STEM with EELS, synergistically contribute to our understanding of the structural properties, oxidation states, spatial distribution, and morphological characteristics of active sites in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 catalysts. XRD provides insights into the crystal structure and lattice parameters of the catalysts. XANES data reveal the oxidation states of Ni and Fe, offering information on their electronic properties and reactivity. EXAFS fitting allows for the determination of coordination environments around the metal centers, indicating the type of alloys present and their sizes. ADF-STEM with EELS enables high-resolution imaging of the catalyst morphology and chemical composition, revealing differences in the spatial distribution of active sites between Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2. Key differences include the presence of a thin layer of FeOx on CeO2 particles in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts, which contributes to the unique active sites for CO2 dissociation and C2H6 activation. These findings highlight the importance of surface chemistry and the interfacial interactions between metals and supports in determining catalyst performance and selectivity for CO2-ODH and DR processes.

这些先进的原位和离线分析技术，包括同步辐射X射线衍射（XRD）、近边吸收结构（XANES）数据、扩展X射线吸收精细结构（EXAFS）和原子衍射扫描透射电子显微镜（ADF-STEM）结合能量损失光谱（EELS），协同促进了我们对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Fe1Ni3/CeO2催化剂中活性位点的结构性质、氧化态、空间分布和形态特征的理解。XRD提供了催化剂晶体结构和晶格参数的见解。XANES数据揭示了Ni和Fe的氧化态，提供关于它们电子特性和反应性的信息。EXAFS拟合允许确定金属中心周围的配位环境，指示存在的合金类型及其大小。ADF-STEM结合EELS实现了高分辨率的催化剂形态和化学成分成像，揭示了Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Fe1Ni3/CeO2催化剂中活性位点空间分布的差异。关键差异包括Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂中CeO2粒子上存在一层薄薄的FeOx层，这为CO2分解和C2H6激活贡献了独特的活性位点。这些发现强调了表面化学和金属与支持物之间界面相互作用在决定CO2-ODH和DR过程催化剂性能和选择性中的重要性。
