<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Advanced in situ and ex situ analytical techniques such as XRD, XANES, EXAFS, and ADF-STEM with EELS were used to understand the structural properties, oxidation states, spatial distribution, and morphological characteristics of active sites in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 catalysts. These techniques helped in comprehending the intricate relationship between the catalyst structure and its activity, and the synergy between these methods allowed researchers to elucidate the complex interactions within the catalyst materials. Comparative studies showed that subtle differences in composition significantly affected the catalyst's performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
