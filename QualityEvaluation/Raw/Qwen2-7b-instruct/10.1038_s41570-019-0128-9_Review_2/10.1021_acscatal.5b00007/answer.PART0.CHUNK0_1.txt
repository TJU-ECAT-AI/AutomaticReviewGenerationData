<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Advanced in situ and ex situ analytical techniques, including XRD, XANES, EXAFS, and ADF-STEM with EELS, have been utilized to gain insights into the structural properties, oxidation states, spatial distribution, and morphological characteristics of active sites in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 catalysts. These techniques reveal distinct crystalline phases, provide detailed information on the electronic structure and coordination environment, enable visualization of atomic structure and chemical composition, and highlight significant differences in structural properties that influence the catalytic activity and selectivity towards olefin formation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
先进的原位和离线分析技术，包括XRD、XANES、EXAFS以及ADF-STEM结合EELS，已被用来深入了解Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Fe1Ni3/CeO2催化剂中活性位点的结构特性、氧化态、空间分布和形态特征。这些技术揭示了不同的晶体相、提供了电子结构和配位环境的详细信息、使原子结构和化学组成可视化，并强调了结构性质的显著差异，这些差异影响了催化剂向烯烃形成的催化活性和选择性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
