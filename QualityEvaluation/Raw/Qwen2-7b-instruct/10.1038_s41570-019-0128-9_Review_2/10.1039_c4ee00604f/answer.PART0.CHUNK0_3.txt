Answer: In the oxidative coupling of methane (OCM) reaction, two methane molecules (2CH4) react with 0.5 oxygen molecule (0.5O2) to form ethane (CH3CH3) and water (H2O), releasing 67.4 kJ per mole of reactants at 298 K.
