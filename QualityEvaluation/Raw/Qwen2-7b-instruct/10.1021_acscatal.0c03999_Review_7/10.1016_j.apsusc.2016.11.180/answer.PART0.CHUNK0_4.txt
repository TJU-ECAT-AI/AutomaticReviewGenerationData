<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) is influenced by their reducibility and the support materials used. Specifically, Fe2O3/AC, which exhibits high reducibility, proved to be an efficient catalyst for this process. The proposed reaction mechanism involving the redox cycle of Fe oxide includes the following steps: reduction, adsorption of reactants, activation, and oxidation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
