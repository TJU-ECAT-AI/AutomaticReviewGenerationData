<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Fe oxide-based catalysts were studied for their effectiveness in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP).
2. Catalyst reducibility had a significant impact on catalytic activity.
3. The support materials, especially in the case of Fe2O3/AC, were essential in boosting the catalytic efficiency.
4. A redox cycle of Fe oxide was proposed as the mechanism underlying the reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
