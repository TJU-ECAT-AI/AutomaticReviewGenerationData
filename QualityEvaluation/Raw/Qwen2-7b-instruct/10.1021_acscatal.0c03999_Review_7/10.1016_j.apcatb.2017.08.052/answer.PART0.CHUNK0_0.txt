<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The Fe oxide-based catalysts were assessed for their effectiveness in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP).
2. The ability of the catalyst to be reduced was observed to have a significant effect on its catalytic efficiency.
3. The support material used, especially in the context of Fe2O3/AC, was found to affect the catalytic activity.
4. The reaction mechanism entails a redox cycle of Fe oxide, resulting in the production of propylene.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 使用Fe氧化物基催化剂对CO2辅助丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）进行了评估。
2. 催化剂的可还原性被发现显著影响其催化活性。
3. 支持材料，特别是在Fe2O3/AC中的使用，影响了催化活性。
4. 反应机制涉及Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，导致丙烯的形成。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
