        The performance and behavior of Fe oxide-based catalysts, particularly those of the form Fe2O3/AC, in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) have been extensively studied. These catalysts demonstrate enhanced performance through improved reducibility and optimized support materials. The redox cycle of Fe oxide is pivotal in the reaction mechanism, driving the dehydrogenation process efficiently. Catalyst reducibility and the synergy between the catalyst and support material, such as activated carbon (AC), significantly influence catalytic activity.

        The superior activity of Fe2O3/AC is attributed to ceria's (CeO2) oxygen storage capacity, enabling CO2 dissociation into CO and active oxygen species that migrate to the Fe oxide surface, maintaining high reactivity. Zirconia (ZrO2) increases ceria's activity, facilitating CO2 activation and palladium-ceria phase cooperation, which enhances the catalyst's efficiency. DRIFTS experiments further confirm the ceria-zirconia material's role in reoxidizing itself through CO2 dissociation post-reducing treatment, accelerating the catalyst's recovery of activity.

        In summary, Fe oxide-based catalysts, notably Fe2O3/AC, excel in CO2-ODHP, showing enhanced performance and reducibility compared to other catalysts. The redox cycle of Fe oxide plays a pivotal role in the catalytic process, contributing to the superior activity of these catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane.

        Fe氧化物基催化剂，特别是Fe2O3/AC形式，在CO2辅助下丙烷的氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中的性能已经进行了广泛研究。这些催化剂通过提高可还原性以及优化支持材料表现出增强的性能。Fe氧化物的还原循环在反应机制中起着关键作用，有效地驱动了丙烷的脱氢过程。催化剂的可还原性和催化剂和支持材料之间的协同作用（如活性炭（AC））显著影响催化活性。

        Fe2O3/AC的优越活性归因于CeO2的氧存储能力，使CO2分解为CO和活性氧物种，这些物种迁移到Fe氧化物表面，维持高活性。ZrO2增加了CeO2的活性，促进了CO2激活和钯-CeO2相合作，从而提高了催化剂的效率。DRIFTS实验进一步证实了CeO2-ZrO2材料在去还原处理后通过CO2分解自我氧化的作用，加速了催化剂活性的恢复。

        总之，Fe氧化物基催化剂，特别是Fe2O3/AC，在CO2-ODHP中表现出卓越的性能和可还原性，与其他催化剂相比。Fe氧化物的还原循环在催化过程中发挥关键作用，有助于这些催化剂在CO2辅助下对丙烷的高效脱氢。
