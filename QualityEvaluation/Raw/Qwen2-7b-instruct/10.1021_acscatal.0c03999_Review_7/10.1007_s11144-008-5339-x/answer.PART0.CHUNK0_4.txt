<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP), the study highlights the significant impact of catalyst reducibility and support materials, notably in Fe2O3/AC, on catalytic activity. The reaction mechanism proposed for this process involves the redox cycle of Fe oxide.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
分析铁氧化物基催化剂在二氧化碳辅助丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中的性能，研究强调了催化剂可还原性和支持材料，特别是在Fe2O3/AC中的显著影响催化活性。提出的反应机制涉及铁氧化物的氧化还原循环。
