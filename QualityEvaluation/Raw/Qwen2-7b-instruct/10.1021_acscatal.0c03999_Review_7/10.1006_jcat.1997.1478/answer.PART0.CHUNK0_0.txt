[1] "Fe oxide-based catalysts are widely used in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP)."
[2] "The reducibility of Fe oxide plays a crucial role in determining the catalytic activity."
[3] "Support materials, such as activated carbon (AC), can enhance the catalytic performance of Fe oxide catalysts."

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Fe oxide-based catalysts are commonly utilized in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP).
2. The ability of Fe oxide to be reduced significantly influences its catalytic effectiveness.
3. Materials like activated carbon (AC) can improve the catalytic performance of Fe oxide catalysts.
4. The reaction mechanism includes a redox cycle of Fe oxide, wherein Fe(III) is reduced to Fe(II) and subsequently oxidized back to Fe(III).
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
