<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The decomposition of methane on Mo2C at 973 K led to the formation of surface carbon. This carbon, upon reaction with hydrogen above 800 K, converted back to methane. No other products resulting from the hydrogenation of this carbon were detected.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
