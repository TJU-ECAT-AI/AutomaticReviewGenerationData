<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Analyzing the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) reveals that catalyst reducibility and the type of support material, especially in the case of Fe2O3/AC, significantly impact catalytic activity. A proposed reaction mechanism involving the redox cycle of Fe oxide is also outlined.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
