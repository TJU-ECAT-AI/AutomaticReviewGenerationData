The goal is to achieve high utilization and exposure of active Pt with minimal Pt content to improve catalytic activity.

3. The use of nanocarbon materials in PDH reactions faces challenges in regeneration processes. Despite advantages in catalytic properties, these catalysts suffer from intrinsic low activity, loss of active components, and coke deposition, posing significant barriers to industrial application.
