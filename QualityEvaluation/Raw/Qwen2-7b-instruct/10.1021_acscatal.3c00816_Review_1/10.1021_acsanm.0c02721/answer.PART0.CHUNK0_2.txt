<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on evaluating the performance of Metal-Ordered Mesoporous Silica (OMS) catalysts, specifically MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx, in Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions. It compares these catalysts' structural properties and catalytic behavior against traditional metal oxide supports, detailing their advantages, limitations, and the underlying reaction mechanisms. The research also explores how factors such as lattice oxygen species and hydrogen (H2) pretreatment affect catalyst performance and deactivation during PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

研究主要关注评估金属有序介孔硅（OMS）催化剂，特别是MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx，在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的性能。它将这些催化剂的结构特性和催化行为与传统金属氧化物支持进行比较，并详细描述它们的优势、局限性以及背后的反应机制。研究还探讨了诸如晶格氧物种和氢（H2）预处理等因素如何影响催化剂在PDH反应中的性能和失活。
