The structural properties and catalytic behavior of these OMS-supported catalysts were compared to traditional metal oxide supports, highlighting their distinct advantages and limitations, along with the intricate reaction mechanisms involved. The factors influencing catalyst performance and deactivation in PDH reactions, such as lattice oxygen species and hydrogen (H2) pretreatment, were meticulously analyzed.
