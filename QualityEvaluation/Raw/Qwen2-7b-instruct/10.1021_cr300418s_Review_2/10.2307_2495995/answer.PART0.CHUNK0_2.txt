<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Soviet Russian State is described as a solid textbook designed for undergraduate courses focusing on Soviet politics. It covers the historical background of the contemporary system comprehensively, offering balanced treatment across various aspects of the Soviet system with commendable depth. However, the author notes that while this approach highlights discrepancies between political reality and mythology, it might lead to dwelling on the party's monopoly of power. Despite some overgeneralizations or dubious assertions, Professor Wesson successfully achieves his goal, providing a valuable resource for students seeking to understand Soviet politics.
