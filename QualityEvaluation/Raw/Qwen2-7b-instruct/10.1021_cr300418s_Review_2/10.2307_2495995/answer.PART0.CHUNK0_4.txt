The review highlights that Robert G. Wesson's work on the Soviet Russian State and The Soviet State: An Aging Revolution employs a historical-descriptive approach, drawing parallels between the Soviet and tsarist regimes, as well as earlier imperial orders. Despite some oversimplifications and questionable assertions, Wesson successfully delivers a comprehensive and detailed textbook that integrates themes effectively, providing useful illustrations. This makes it an appealing resource for students seeking a well-rounded understanding of Soviet politics.
