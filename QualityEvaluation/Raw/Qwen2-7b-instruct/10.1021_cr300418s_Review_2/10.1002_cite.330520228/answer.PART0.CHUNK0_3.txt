<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The book aims to present quantum chemical calculations and results on gas adsorption onto metal, semiconductor, oxide, and zeolite surfaces from the perspective of experimental chemists. Molecular methods receive priority over solid-state methods because they consider the interaction between molecular species and a small section of the solid surface as crucial for adsorption. When describing complex adsorption phenomena, finding suitable models might be just as important as choosing the approximation levels of the computational methods being applied.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
