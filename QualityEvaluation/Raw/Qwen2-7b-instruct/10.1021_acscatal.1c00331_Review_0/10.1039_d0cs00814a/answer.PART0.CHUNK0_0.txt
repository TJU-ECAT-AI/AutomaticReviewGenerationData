1. The PDH process has led to the development and updating of dehydrogenation technologies and catalysts. Technologies such as Catofin, Oleflex, FBD-4, PDH, STAR, ADHO, FCDh, and K-PROt have been commercialized and are in use today.
2. The Catofin process utilizes counter-current flow technology, changing the flow direction of reactive materials, air downward, and hydrocarbons directly upward, which results in the production of more products with fewer raw materials, thereby reducing investment and operating costs.
3. The Catofin process comprises four sections: propane dehydrogenation to propylene, reactor discharge compression, product recovery, and refining units.
4. The Oleflex process converts propane into propylene through catalytic dehydrogenation under moving bed reactors, having been commercialized since 1990.
5. Activating the first CH bond of propane is recognized as the rate-determining step that influences the catalyst activity in the PDH process.
6. Many studies have outlined the roles of Sn, but the exact active structures under reaction conditions are still under debate.
7. Currently, the Oleflex and Catofin processes have numerous industrial applications among PDH technologies.

1. PDH过程促进了脱氢技术与催化剂的开发和更新。Catofin、Oleflex、FBD-4、PDH、STAR、ADHO、FCDh和K-PROt等技术已经商业化并在使用中。
2. Catofin过程利用逆流流动技术，改变反应材料、空气向下流动和烃类直接向上流动的方向，从而以较少的原材料获得更多的产品，降低投资和运营成本。
3. Catofin过程分为四个部分：丙烷脱氢为丙烯、反应器排出压缩、产品回收和精炼单元。
4. Oleflex过程在移动床反应器下通过催化脱氢将丙烷转化为丙烯，并于1990年开始商业化。
5. 激活丙烷的第一个CH键被认为是决定催化剂活性的关键步骤。
6. 虽然许多研究确定了锡（Sn）的作用，但在反应条件下的确切活性结构仍在持续辩论中。
7. 目前，Oleflex和Catofin过程在PDH技术中具有广泛的应用。
8. Catofin过程采用逆流流动技术，使空气向下流动，烃类直接向上流动，从而增加产品产量，同时减少原材料使用，降低投资和运营成本。
9. Oleflex过程使用可回收的铂基氧化铝催化剂，提供低生产成本、高投资回报率和低环境影响。
10. 激活丙烷的第一个CH键被承认是影响PDH过程中催化剂活性的关键步骤。
11. 关于在反应条件下催化剂的精确活性结构，特别是锡（Sn）的作用，仍然存在争议。
12. 目前，Oleflex和Catofin过程在PDH技术中具有广泛的应用。
