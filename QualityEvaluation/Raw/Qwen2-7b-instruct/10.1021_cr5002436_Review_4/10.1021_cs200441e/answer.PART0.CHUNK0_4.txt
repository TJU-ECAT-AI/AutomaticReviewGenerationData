<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The research on Zn-modified ZSM-5 zeolites highlighted the importance of clustered cationic Zn complexes in determining their catalytic properties. These complexes significantly affect the activity and selectivity of the catalysts. Various factors, including temperature and surface chemistry, play crucial roles in influencing the performance of these catalysts. The study's outcomes offer promising avenues for the development of novel and efficient carbon-based catalysts.
