<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Carbon black demand is forecasted to grow dynamically until 2019, with the global market volume projected to increase by over 3.1 million tonnes from 2011 levels. Tyres account for the largest share of consumption, with >7.8 million tonnes consumed in 2011. Asia Pacific is the primary driver of growth, with consumption and production volumes expected to rise by more than 4.5% annually. China leads the market, consuming over 30% of the total global carbon black volume. The region's capacity expansions are anticipated to transform it into a net exporter in the future.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
