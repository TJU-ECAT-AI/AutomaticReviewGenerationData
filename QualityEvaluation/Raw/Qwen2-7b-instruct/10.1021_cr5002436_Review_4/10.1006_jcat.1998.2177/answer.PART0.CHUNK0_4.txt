JOURNAL OF CATALYSIS 179, 192202 (1998)

In situ Zn K-edge X-ray absorption studies have shown that Zn in Zn/H-ZSM5 (Si/Al = 14.5, ion exchange) is present as isolated ZnOx species, most likely located at zeolitic exchange sites, with about four oxygen nearest neighbors and without Zn next nearest neighbors.
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
