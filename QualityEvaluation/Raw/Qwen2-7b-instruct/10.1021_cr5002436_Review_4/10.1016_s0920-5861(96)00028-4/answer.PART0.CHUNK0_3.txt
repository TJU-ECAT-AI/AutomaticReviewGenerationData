<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. Various carbon-based materials like coke deposits, carbon nanofibers, and hybrid nanocarbons display surprising catalytic capabilities in alkane dehydrogenation processes. 

2. The underlying mechanisms are thought to involve the activation of C-H bonds and the disposal of hydrogen atoms generated during the reactions.

3. Temperature and surface chemistry are key factors affecting these catalytic activities.

4. The significance of this phenomenon lies in the opportunity to innovate and optimize carbon-based catalysts for practical applications.
