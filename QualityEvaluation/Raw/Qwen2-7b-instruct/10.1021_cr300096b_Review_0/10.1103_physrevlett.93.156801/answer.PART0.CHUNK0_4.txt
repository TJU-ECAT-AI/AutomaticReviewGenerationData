<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The role of strain and ligand effects in modifying the electronic and chemical properties of bimetallic surfaces has been explored in recent studies. This research contributes to understanding the unique properties of bimetallic catalysts, which are crucial in bridging the "materials gap" and "pressure gap" between model surfaces and industrially relevant supported catalysts.
