Bimetallic catalysts have evolved significantly since their commercial introduction, with unique electronic and chemical properties that bridge the 'materials gap' and 'pressure gap' between model surfaces and industrially relevant supported catalysts. These properties contribute to their effectiveness in both fundamental research and practical applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
