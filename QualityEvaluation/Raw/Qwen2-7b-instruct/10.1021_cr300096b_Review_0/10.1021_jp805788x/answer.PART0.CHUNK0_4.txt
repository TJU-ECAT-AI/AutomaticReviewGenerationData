<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The evolution of bimetallic catalysts in heterogeneous catalysis since their commercial introduction has been marked by advancements in controlling their composition and synthesis methods, leading to the development of near-monodisperse NiCu bimetallic nanocrystals. These catalysts possess unique electronic and chemical properties that enable them to effectively bridge the 'materials gap' and 'pressure gap' between model surfaces and industrial catalysts, enhancing their performance in both fundamental research and practical applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
