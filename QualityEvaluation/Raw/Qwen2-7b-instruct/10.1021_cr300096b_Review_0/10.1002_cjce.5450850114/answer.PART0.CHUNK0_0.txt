The book Fundamentals of Industrial Catalytic Processes (Second Edition) serves as a comprehensive instructional and reference text for chemical science and engineering students and professionals. It offers an academic and industrial perspective on industrial catalysis, addressing the needs of both practicing chemists and engineers. The text is structured into two sections: one focusing on reaction engineering and catalysis fundamentals, and another presenting industrial examples of heterogeneously catalyzed processes. This format makes it suitable for academic courses and as a quick reference for industrial applications.
