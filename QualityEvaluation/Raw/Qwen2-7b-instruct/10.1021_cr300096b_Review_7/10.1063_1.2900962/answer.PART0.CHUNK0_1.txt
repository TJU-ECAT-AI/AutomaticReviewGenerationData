<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on the thermodynamics and kinetics of oxygen-induced segregation of 3d metals in Pt3dPt (111) and Pt3dPt (100) bimetallic structures. It employs density functional theory calculations to predict the stability of subsurface 3d metal in Pt(111) and Pt(100) in oxygen. The research also discusses the effect of crystal plane on the oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) activity and the stability of the subsurface structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
