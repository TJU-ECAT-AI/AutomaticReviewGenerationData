[1] "The oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) has been studied over alumina-supported vanadia catalysts."
          [2] "The use of sulfur-based oxidants, such as hydrogen sulfide (H2S), disulfide (S2), and sulfur dioxide (SO2), was compared with conventional oxidants."
          [3] "The impact of these oxidants on propylene yield was assessed."
          [4] "Challenges associated with each oxidant, including byproduct formation, were discussed."
          [5] "Key areas for future research to improve catalyst design and optimize the overall process were identified."
