<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The manufacturing of polymers such as PVC, PU, and PC generates HCl as a by-product, which is typically recycled through oxychlorination in the balanced VCM process. Catalytic HCl oxidation is considered the most energy-efficient method. In 2012, Amrute et al. discovered that bulk CeO2 performs exceptionally well in HCl oxidation. Despite advancements in catalyst stability, challenges persisted in applying these catalysts to industrial processes since their inception in 1870.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Vinyl chloride (VCM)，第三大生产的塑料聚氯乙烯的单体，通过乙炔的氢氯化或通过能耗高的二氯乙烷（EDC）的热脱氯化来生产。乙烯氧氯化用于VCM制造，能从平衡VCM过程中的脱氯化步骤回收HCl。在乙烯氧氯化中，催化剂表面包含CeOCl，而主体相保持或演变成为CeO2。优化反应条件显示，提高温度或接触时间对VCM选择性有利，同时增加不希望的1,2-二氯乙烷（DCE）的形成。调整主体CeO2的表面积，也影响其酸性，或者工程CeO2-ZrO2混合氧化物可以产生高达25%的VCM产率。
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
