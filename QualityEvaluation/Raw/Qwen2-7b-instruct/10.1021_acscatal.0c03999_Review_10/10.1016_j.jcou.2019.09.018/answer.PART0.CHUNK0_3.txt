<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on the investigation of Cr-containing Beta zeolites as catalysts for propane dehydrogenation in the presence of carbon dioxide. Dealumination of Beta zeolite and subsequent chromium dispersion through a simple impregnation procedure results in highly effective catalysts for CO2-PDH.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这项研究专注于研究含Cr的Beta沸石作为在二氧化碳存在下丙烷脱氢反应的催化剂。通过去除Beta沸石中的铝并随后通过简单的浸渍过程分散铬，获得了高度有效的CO2-PDH催化剂。
