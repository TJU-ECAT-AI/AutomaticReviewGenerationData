<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Comparing the use of sulfur-based oxidants (H2S, S2, SO2) with conventional oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) to propylene, the study found differences in propylene yield and byproduct formation. Challenges associated with each oxidant include specific byproducts formed during the reaction. Future research aims to enhance catalyst design and optimize the overall process efficiency.
