<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study compares the effectiveness of different sulfur-based oxidants (H2S, S2, SO2) with conventional oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) to propylene. It evaluates the propylene yield, discusses the challenges associated with each oxidant including byproduct formation, and highlights the areas for future research to enhance catalyst design and optimize the process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
