<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, particularly using bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, contributes to various industrial and research applications such as fine chemical synthesis and cellulosic biomass conversion.
2. Methodologies employed to study, optimize, and enhance the selectivity and efficiency of this process include CO chemisorption, temperature-programmed reduction (TPR), X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), differential scanning calorimetry (DSC), gas chromatography coupled with mass spectrometry (GC-MS), reaction kinetics studies, and catalyst characterization using powder X-ray diffraction (XRD).
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
