<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, utilizing bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, significantly contributes to industrial and research areas including fine chemical synthesis and cellulosic biomass conversion.
2. The selectivity and efficiency of this process can be studied and optimized through theoretical and experimental methods.
3. Theoretical calculations predict reaction pathways and guide catalyst design.
4. Experimental studies involve testing catalyst performance under different conditions and analyzing product yields and purity.
5. Optimization strategies include tailoring catalyst composition, support, and operational parameters to improve selectivity.
6. Regenerating deactivated catalysts, especially those with distinctive structures, enhances process sustainability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
