<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The engineering of cobalt-doped platinum nanocatalysts is aimed at achieving ultraselective hydrogenation. This process is crucial for various industrial and research applications, notably in fine chemical synthesis and the conversion of cellulosic biomass. The effectiveness and selectivity of the catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, especially when using bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, are enhanced through both theoretical calculations and experimental studies.
