<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Electronic and geometric structures of supported platinum, gold, and platinum-gold catalysts were studied. The catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, especially with bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, plays a significant role in industrial and research applications, including fine chemical synthesis and cellulosic biomass conversion. Various methodologies, combining theoretical and experimental approaches, are used to investigate, refine, and improve the selectivity and efficiency of this process.
