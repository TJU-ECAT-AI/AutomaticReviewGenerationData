    <English>
        The catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, particularly using bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, significantly contributes to various industrial and research applications such as fine chemical synthesis and cellulosic biomass conversion. This process allows for enhanced selectivity and efficiency in converting feedstocks into desired products. Studies employing methodologies such as diffuse reflectance Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy and temperature programmed reduction have revealed that during the reaction, segregation of metals like Pt and Re occurs on the catalyst surface, increasing the availability of active sites for the decomposition of molecules like methane, thus improving conversion rates over time. For instance, in the case of Pt-Re supported on Ce0.52 Zr0.48 O2, the bimetallic catalyst demonstrated comparable methane conversion efficiency to a monometallic catalyst after 20 hours of reaction. 

        Similarly, in the hydrogenation of furfural to furfuryl alcohol, bimetallic PtSn catalysts, especially those with the lowest tin content, show high selectivity and efficiency, allowing for a high yield of furfuryl alcohol with selectivities ranging from 96% to 98%. Even when used in consecutive hydrogenation reactions, these catalysts showed a certain deactivation (25% between the first and third cycle), but they still maintain high selectivity to furfuryl alcohol.

        The introduction of tin notably increases both the reaction rate and the selectivity of crotylalcohol (SCO), as seen in the liquid-phase hydrogenation of cinnamaldehyde. This trend indicates that tin significantly enhances the reaction rate and leads to a pronounced increase in selectivity.

        The behavior of catalysts shows a very pronounced dependence on the CO partial pressure, highlighting the importance of optimizing this parameter for maximum efficiency. These findings not only advance the field of chemical engineering but also open avenues for the development of more efficient and selective catalysts, contributing to sustainable chemical production methods.

        <Chinese>
            使用双金属催化剂如Pt-Ni系统催化氢化C═O键在工业和研究领域有显著贡献，特别是在精细化学合成和木质素生物质转化方面。这一过程通过提高选择性和效率，能够将原料高效转化为所需产品。通过使用差示反射傅立叶变换红外光谱和温度程序还原法等方法研究发现，在反应过程中，铂和铼等金属在催化剂表面的分布，增加了活性位点的数量，从而提高了甲烷分解速率。例如，Ce0.52 Zr0.48 O2上负载的Pt-Re双金属催化剂在20小时反应后，其甲烷转化率与单金属催化剂相当。

            同样，在糠醛转化为糠醇的过程中，PtSn双金属催化剂（尤其低锡含量）表现出高选择性和效率，使得糠醇的产率达到96%-98%，即使在连续氢化反应中，这些催化剂显示出一定的失活（第一轮和第三轮之间为25%），但仍然保持高选择性到糠醇。

            锡的引入显著提高了反应速率和选择性，尤其是在顺式-丁烯醇（SCO）的液相氢化中，类似的趋势在肉桂醛的氢化中也被观察到，这表明锡显著增强了反应速率并导致了选择性的显著提高。

            催化剂的行为显示出对CO分压的强烈依赖，强调了优化此参数以达到最大效率的重要性。这些发现不仅推进了化工工程领域的发展，也为开发更高效、选择性更强的催化剂提供了途径，推动了可持续化学生产技术的进步。
