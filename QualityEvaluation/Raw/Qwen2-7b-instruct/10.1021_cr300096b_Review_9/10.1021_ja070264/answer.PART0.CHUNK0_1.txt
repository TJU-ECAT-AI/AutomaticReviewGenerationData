<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, particularly using bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, contributes significantly to various industrial and research applications. This includes fine chemical synthesis and the conversion of cellulosic biomass. These processes are optimized and enhanced through the architecture of bimetallic surface structures. The selectivity and efficiency of the hydrogenation process are improved through detailed theoretical calculations and experimental studies, leading to more efficient methods for chemical synthesis and biomass conversion.
