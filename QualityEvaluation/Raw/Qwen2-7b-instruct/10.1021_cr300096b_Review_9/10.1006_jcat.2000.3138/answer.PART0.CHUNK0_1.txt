<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Catalytic hydrogenation of C═O bonds, using bimetallic catalysts like Pt-Ni systems, significantly aids in industrial and research applications including the synthesis of fine chemicals and the conversion of cellulosic biomass.
2. Various methodologies are utilized to investigate, refine, and boost the selectivity and efficiency of this process within both theoretical and practical frameworks. These methodologies encompass density functional theory calculations, kinetic analyses, and catalyst characterization.
3. For PtCu bimetallic catalysts, copper plays a critical role in reducing nitrate to nitrite through a redox process. The interaction between copper and platinum is paramount in keeping copper in a metallic state, facilitated by hydrogen adsorption on platinum.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
