<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Isolated metal centers on oxide materials facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by acting as either Brnsted or Lewis acids. Brnsted acid sites are generated when tetravalent Si4+ is replaced with trivalent ions like Al3+ or Ga3+. Lewis acidity comes from oxo cations GaO+ on exchange positions of the framework. 

2. The principle of using these isolated metal centers involves the activation of C-H bonds through acid-base reactions. In the Mars van Krevelen mechanism, a Brnsted acid abstracts a proton from a C-H bond, leading to the formation of a carbocation. This carbocation can then undergo further reactions like alkyne addition or ring-opening reactions. In H-abstraction, a Lewis acid abstracts a hydrogen atom from a C-H bond, forming a radical.

3. In industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, these principles are applied by designing catalysts with the appropriate metal centers and acidic sites. For instance, the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride uses a catalyst that selectively oxidizes butane by activating the C-H bonds through acid-base reactions, followed by further oxidation steps.

Answer:
1. 隔离在氧化物材料上的金属中心通过充当Brnsted或Lewis酸来促进C-H键激活，在异相催化中。当四价Si4+被三价离子如Al3+或Ga3+取代时，会产生Brnsted酸位点。框架中的氧基GaO+作为Lewis酸作用于交换位置。 

2. 这些孤立金属中心的原理涉及通过酸碱反应激活C-H键。在Mars van Krevelen机制中，Brnsted酸从C-H键抽象质子，导致形成碳正离子。该碳正离子可以进一步进行如炔烃加成或环开裂等反应。在H抽象中，Lewis酸从C-H键抽象氢原子，形成自由基。

3. 在烷烃的选择性氧化工业应用中，这些原则通过设计具有适当金属中心和酸性位点的催化剂来实现。例如，将丁烷转化为马来酸酐使用一种催化剂，该催化剂选择性地通过酸碱反应激活丁烷的C-H键，随后进行进一步的氧化步骤。
