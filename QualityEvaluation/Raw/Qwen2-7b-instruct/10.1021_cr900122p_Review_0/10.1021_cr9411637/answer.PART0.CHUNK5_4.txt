<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The isolated metal centers on oxide materials play a crucial role in facilitating C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis. This process often involves mechanisms such as Mars van Krevelen and H-abstraction. In Mars van Krevelen, the metal center abstracts a proton from the molecule, leading to the formation of a carbocation intermediate. This intermediate can then undergo further reactions to achieve the desired transformation. H-abstraction involves the removal of a hydrogen atom from the molecule, typically facilitated by a metal center, leading to the formation of a radical or a carbocation. These mechanisms are particularly important in the selective oxidation of alkanes, where the goal is to convert saturated hydrocarbons into functionalized products.

For instance, in the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, the selective oxidation of butane requires precise control over the activation of the C-H bonds and the subsequent selective formation of the double bond and the anhydride functionality. The isolated metal centers, due to their ability to abstract protons or hydrogen atoms, are well-suited for this task. They can selectively activate the desired C-H bonds, leading to the formation of maleic anhydride rather than other potential products. The efficiency and selectivity of this conversion can be influenced by the nature of the metal center, its dispersion on the support material, and the acidity of the surrounding environment.

孤立的金属中心在异相催化中对C-H键激活起着关键作用。这个过程通常涉及Mars van Krevelen和H-抽象机制。在Mars van Krevelen机制中，金属中心从分子中提取质子，形成一个碳正离子中间体。这个中间体可以进一步反应以实现所需转化。H-抽象涉及到从分子中去除一个氢原子，通常在金属中心的帮助下，形成一个自由基或碳正离子。这些机制在选择性氧化烷烃中特别重要，目标是将饱和烃转化为功能性产物。

例如，在将丁烷转化为马来酸酐的过程中，选择性氧化丁烷需要精确控制C-H键的活化以及随后形成双键和酸酐功能性的选择性。由于能够提取质子或氢原子的能力，孤立的金属中心非常适合这一任务。它们可以选择性地活化所需的C-H键，导致马来酸酐的形成而不是其他潜在产品。这种转换的效率和选择性可以受到金属中心的性质、支持材料上的分散程度以及周围环境酸性的影响。
