<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The isolated metal centers on oxide materials facilitate C-H bond activation through mechanisms such as the Mars van Krevelen and H-abstraction processes. These principles are applied in industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, exemplified by the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
