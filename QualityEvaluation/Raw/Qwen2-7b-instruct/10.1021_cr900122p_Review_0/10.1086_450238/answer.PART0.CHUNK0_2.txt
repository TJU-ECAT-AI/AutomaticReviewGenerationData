<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Isolated metal centers on oxide materials contribute significantly to C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis. This process often employs the Mars-van-Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction. These mechanisms break C-H bonds under controlled circumstances, vital for further chemical reactions. Industrially, these concepts are utilized to effectively convert butane into maleic anhydride.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
