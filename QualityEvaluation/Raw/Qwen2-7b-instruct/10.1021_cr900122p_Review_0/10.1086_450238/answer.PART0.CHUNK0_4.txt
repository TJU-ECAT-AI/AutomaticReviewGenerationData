<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Isolated metal centers on oxide materials enable C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction are fundamental processes in these reactions.
2. These catalytic principles are utilized industrially, particularly in the selective oxidation of alkanes, exemplified by the conversion of butane into maleic anhydride.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 这些催化原理在工业上得到了应用，特别是在烷烃的选择性氧化中，如将丁烷转化为马来酸酐的例子。
