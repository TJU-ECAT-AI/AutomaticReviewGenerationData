<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Isolated metal centers on oxide materials play a crucial role in facilitating the activation of C-H bonds in heterogeneous catalysis.
2. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the abstraction of a hydrogen atom from a hydrocarbon molecule, leading to the formation of a radical and an alkyne.
3. In the specific case of converting butane to maleic anhydride, the Mars van Krevelen mechanism is pivotal.
4. Hydrogen abstraction from butane results in the formation of a butyl radical.
5. This butyl radical then undergoes a series of reactions, including addition of oxygen, culminating in the formation of maleic anhydride.
6. The role of isolated metal centers ensures the selective activation of the C-H bond, guiding the process towards the desired product.
7. The H-abstraction mechanism is implemented in industrial selective oxidation of alkanes by selecting appropriate catalysts that promote this process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

3. 在将丁烷转化为马来酸酐的具体情况下，Mars van Krevelen机制至关重要。
4. 从丁烷中抽象氢原子导致形成一个丁基自由基。
5. 这个丁基自由基随后经历一系列反应，包括氧的加成，最终形成马来酸酐。
6. 隔离的金属中心的作用确保了C-H键的有选择性激活，引导过程朝向所需产物进行。
7. H抽象机制通过选择促进这一过程的适当催化剂，在工业中应用于烷烃的选择性氧化。
