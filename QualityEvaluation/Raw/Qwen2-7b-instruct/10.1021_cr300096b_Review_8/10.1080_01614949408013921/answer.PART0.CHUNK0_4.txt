<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The review discusses various aspects of aromatic hydrogenation catalysis, encompassing the types of aromatic compounds found in petroleum fractions, their thermodynamic properties, kinetic studies on model compounds and industrial feedstocks, the nature of catalysts and their active sites, especially in sulfur-based and sulfur-tolerant noble metal catalysts, and concludes with an overview of industrial applications and insights into predicting hydrogenation activity through a combined theoretical and experimental approach.
