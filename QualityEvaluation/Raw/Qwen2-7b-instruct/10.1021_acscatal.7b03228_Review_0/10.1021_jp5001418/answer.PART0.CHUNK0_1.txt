<EnglishVersionAnswer>
To predict and compare the bond strengths between carbon monoxide (CM) and oxygen (OM) molecules adsorbed on transition metal surfaces, the study focuses on the synergistic effects of geometric and electronic properties in transition-metal alloys. This interplay aims to develop innovative strategies for designing more efficient, selective, and tunable catalytic systems that surpass traditional monometallic catalysts.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
