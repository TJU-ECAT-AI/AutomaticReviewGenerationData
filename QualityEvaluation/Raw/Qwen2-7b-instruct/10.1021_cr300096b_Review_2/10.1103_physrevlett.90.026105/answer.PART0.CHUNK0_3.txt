<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves scanning tunneling microscopy (STM), high-resolution X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (HR-XPS), and density functional theory (DFT) calculations. This process is crucial for structural modeling because it provides insights into the atomic arrangement and structure formation.
2. DFT calculations are pivotal for predicting the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces, which are indispensable for advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 对Ni/Pt双金属表面的表征涉及扫描隧道显微镜（STM）、高分辨率X射线光电子能谱（HR-XPS）和密度泛函理论（DFT）计算。这个过程对于结构建模至关重要，因为它能够提供表面原子排列和结构形成的具体信息。
2. DFT计算对于预测这些双金属表面的电子和化学性质至关重要，这对于高级表面科学和催化研究是不可或缺的。
