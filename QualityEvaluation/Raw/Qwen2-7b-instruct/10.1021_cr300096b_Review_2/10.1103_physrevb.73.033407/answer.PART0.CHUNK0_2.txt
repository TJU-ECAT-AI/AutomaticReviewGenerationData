<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves synthesizing these surfaces by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is essential for structural modeling, DFT calculations, and gaining a thorough understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these surfaces, which is critical for advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
