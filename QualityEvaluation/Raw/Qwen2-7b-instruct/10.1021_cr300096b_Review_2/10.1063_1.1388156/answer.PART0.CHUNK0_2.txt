<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces is performed through detailed atomic-scale studies focusing on the interface structure and magnetic properties. This method is essential for structural modeling, density functional theory (DFT) calculations, and gaining a thorough understanding of the electronic and chemical characteristics of these surfaces within the scope of advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
