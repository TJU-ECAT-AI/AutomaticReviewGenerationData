<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is crucial because it enables detailed structural modeling, DFT calculations, and a comprehensive understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces, which are pivotal in advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
