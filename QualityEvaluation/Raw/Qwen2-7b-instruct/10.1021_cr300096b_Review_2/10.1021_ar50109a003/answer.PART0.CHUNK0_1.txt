<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces synthesized by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates is essential for understanding their electronic and chemical properties. This process enables us to explore the interactions between the two metals and how they affect each other's characteristics. It plays a vital role in the advancement of new catalysts and surface science applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
