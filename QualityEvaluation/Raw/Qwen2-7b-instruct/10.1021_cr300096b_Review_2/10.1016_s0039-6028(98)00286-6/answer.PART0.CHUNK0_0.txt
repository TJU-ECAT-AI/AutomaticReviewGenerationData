<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces, created by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates, is vital for creating accurate structural models, conducting Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations, and gaining a thorough understanding of the electronic and chemical characteristics of these surfaces within the realm of advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
对在单晶Pt(111)或聚晶Pt基底上沉积Ni单层形成的Ni/Pt双金属表面进行表征，对于构建精确的结构模型、执行密度泛函理论（DFT）计算以及在先进表面科学和催化研究领域深入了解这些双金属表面的电子和化学性质至关重要。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
