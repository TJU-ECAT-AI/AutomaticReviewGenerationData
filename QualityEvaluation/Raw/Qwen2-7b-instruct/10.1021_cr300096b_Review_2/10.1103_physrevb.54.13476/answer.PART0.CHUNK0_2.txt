<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces is conducted using scanning tunneling microscopy (STM), atomic force microscopy (AFM), and X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS). This process is crucial for structural modeling because it enables researchers to determine the precise arrangement of atoms on the surface, which affects the electronic and chemical properties of the bimetallic surfaces.
2. DFT calculations are performed to gain deeper insights into the electronic structure and bonding characteristics of the bimetallic surfaces, which helps in understanding their reactivity and catalytic performance.
3. A thorough understanding of these properties is vital for advancing surface science and catalysis research, facilitating the creation of more efficient catalysts and materials for industrial use.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. Ni/Pt双金属表面的表征通过扫描隧道显微镜（STM）、原子力显微镜（AFM）和X射线光电子能谱（XPS）进行。这个过程对于结构建模至关重要，因为它使研究人员能够确定表面上原子的确切排列方式，这影响双金属表面的电子和化学性质。
2. 进行第一性原理计算（DFT）以深入理解双金属表面的电子结构和键合特性，有助于理解它们的反应性和催化性能。
3. 对这些性质的全面了解对于推进表面科学和催化研究至关重要，有助于开发更高效的催化剂和材料以用于工业应用。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
