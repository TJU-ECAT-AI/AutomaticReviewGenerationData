<quotes>

[2] "This approach enables the creation of a variety of bimetallic surfaces with tailored properties for specific applications in catalysis, electronics, and materials science."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
2. By depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates, researchers can generate a bimetallic interface with distinctive electronic and chemical properties, which are not present in either pure metal individually. This method facilitates the production of specialized bimetallic surfaces for targeted uses in catalysis, electronics, and materials science.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 通过在Pt(111)单晶或多晶Pt基板上沉积Ni单层，研究人员可以产生具有与单一纯金属中均未发现的独特电子和化学性质的双金属界面。这种方法允许制造具有特定应用（催化、电子学和材料科学）所需特性的各种双金属表面。
