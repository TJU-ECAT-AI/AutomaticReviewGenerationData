<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves synthesizing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is crucial because it allows for structural modeling and DFT calculations, providing insights into the electronic and chemical properties of these surfaces. These studies are essential for advancing surface science and catalysis research, as they help understand the behavior of bimetallic surfaces under various conditions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
