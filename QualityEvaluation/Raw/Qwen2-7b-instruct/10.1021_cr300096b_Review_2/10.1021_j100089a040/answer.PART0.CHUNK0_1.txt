<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is essential for structural modeling, DFT calculations, and gaining a thorough understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these surfaces within the field of advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
