<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves depositing a Ni monolayer on a Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is crucial for structural modeling and DFT calculations because it allows for the detailed examination of the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces. It provides insights into their behavior in advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
