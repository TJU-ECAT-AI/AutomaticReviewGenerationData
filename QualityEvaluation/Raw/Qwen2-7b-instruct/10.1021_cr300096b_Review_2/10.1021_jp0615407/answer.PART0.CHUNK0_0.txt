<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves depositing a Ni monolayer on either Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is crucial for structural modeling, DFT calculations, and gaining insights into the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces in the field of advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
