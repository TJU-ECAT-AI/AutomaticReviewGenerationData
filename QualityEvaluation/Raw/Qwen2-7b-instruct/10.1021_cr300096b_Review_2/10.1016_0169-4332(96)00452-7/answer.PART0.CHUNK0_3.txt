<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. This process is crucial for structural modeling, DFT calculations, and gaining insights into the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces in advanced surface science and catalysis research.
2. The experiments were conducted at the National Synchrotron Radiation Facility MAX-LAB in Lund, Sweden, using a modified SX-700 monochromator and a 200 mm radius hemispherical analyser of Scienta type.
3. Tin deposition was performed using a tungsten filament evaporator.
4. An excellent p(2 X 2)R30 LEED pattern was achieved after depositing about 1/3 monolayer (ML) of tin and annealing at 600°C for 3 minutes.
5. Atomic hydrogen was introduced onto the surface at -80°C, with the sample positioned approximately 5 cm away from the heated tungsten filament.
6. Hydrogen adsorption did not alter the LEED pattern or the shape of the Sn 4d spectrum.
7. Two surface peaks were necessary to fit the Pt 4f,,Z core level spectrum: one shifted -0.22 eV relative to the bulk peak with 26% relative intensity (S,) and another shifted 0.59 eV relative to the bulk peak with 24% relative intensity (S,).
8. The experimental surface core level shift for the clean Pt(ll1) surface and the Pt(1 1 1x6 X fi)R30-Sn surface alloy was -0.37 and -0.25 eV, respectively.
9. The value used for the Pt,Sn system was - 52 kJ/mol, as tabulated.
10. Binding energies were computed as differences between the energies of the H-surface system and the neutral species, specifically hydrogen and the surface at infinite separation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. Ni/Pt双金属表面的表征涉及在Pt(111)单晶或多晶Pt基底上沉积Ni单层。这一过程对于结构建模、DFT计算以及在先进表面科学和催化研究中获取这些双金属表面的电子和化学性质至关重要。
2. 实验在瑞典隆德的国家同步辐射光设施MAX-LAB进行，使用修改后的SX-700单色仪和Scintia类型的200毫米半径球面分析仪。
3. 锡的沉积是通过钨丝蒸发器完成的。
4. 在沉积大约1/3单层（ML）的锡并随后在600°C下退火3分钟后，获得了出色的p(2 X 2)R30 LEED图案。
5. 在-80°C时，通过加热的钨丝灯丝向表面引入了原子氢，样品位于灯丝约5cm处。
6. 原子氢吸附没有改变LEED模式或Sn 4d谱的形状。
7. Pt 4f,,Z核心级谱需要两个表面峰来拟合数据：一个相对于基体峰值偏移-0.22 eV且相对强度为26%（S,），另一个偏移0.59 eV且相对强度为24%（S,）。
8. 清洁的Pt(ll1)表面和Pt(1 1 1x6 X fi)R30-Sn表面合金的实验表面核心能级位移分别为-0.37和-0.25 eV。
9. Pt,Sn系统的值被设置为- 52 kJ/mol，这是从文献中找到的。
10. 绑定能被计算为H-表面系统和中性物种（即氢和无穷远分离的表面）能量之间的差异。
