<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces involves utilizing X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), high-resolution transmission electron microscopy (HRTEM), and low-energy electron microscopy (LEEM). These procedures are essential for building structural models, performing density functional theory (DFT) calculations, and gaining a thorough understanding of the electronic and chemical characteristics of these bimetallic surfaces within the realm of advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
