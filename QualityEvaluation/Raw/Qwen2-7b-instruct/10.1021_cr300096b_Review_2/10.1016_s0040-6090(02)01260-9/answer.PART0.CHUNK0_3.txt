<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Ultrathin Pd and Pt films can be grown on W substrates without causing substantial strain.
2. The DFT calculations permit us to propose the most energetically favorable structures.
3. The computational outcomes do not rule out the presence of dilute alloys that are not characterized by the 1-to-1 structure.
4. Allowing lateral motion reduces the energy difference between the most favorable alloy and the elemental film by 0.04 eV per surface unit cell, resulting in an energy difference of 0.18 eV per surface unit cell when compared to 0.14 eV per surface unit cell without lateral motion.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
