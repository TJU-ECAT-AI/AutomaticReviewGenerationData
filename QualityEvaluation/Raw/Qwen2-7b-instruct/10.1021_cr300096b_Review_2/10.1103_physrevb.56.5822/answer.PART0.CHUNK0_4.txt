<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces, synthesized by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates, is conducted through phase diagrams for surface alloys. This process is crucial for structural modeling, Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations, and our comprehensive understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces. These studies are essential for advancements in both advanced surface science and catalysis research.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
Ni/Pt双金属表面的表征通过表面合金相图进行，通过在Pt(111)单晶或多晶Pt基体上沉积一层Ni单层来合成。这一过程对于结构建模、密度泛函理论（DFT）计算以及对这些双金属表面的电子和化学性质的全面理解至关重要。这些研究对于推进表面科学和催化研究具有重要价值。
