This synthesis method is crucial for conducting structural modeling and performing density functional theory (DFT) calculations, enabling a comprehensive understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces. The characterization process often involves the utilization of synchrotron radiation, specifically the SA73b beamline with a TGM7 monochromator, for experiments carried out in a stainless-steel ultra-high vacuum (UHV) chamber equipped with various spectroscopic tools like photoelectron spectroscopy (PES), Auger electron spectroscopy (AES), and low-energy electron diffraction (LEED).

Key aspects of the characterization include:
1. Obtaining clean Cu(100) single-crystal surfaces through cycles of argon-ion sputtering and annealing at high temperatures.
2. Studying the Pt 4f core-level photoemission line, which helps in understanding the structure and composition of the bimetallic surface.
3. Observing the formation of an extended Pt-Cu surface alloy, characterized by distinct peaks in the spectra corresponding to Pt deposition.
4. Analyzing the effects of annealing on the alloy structure, such as changes in peak intensities and shifts in binding energies.
5. Investigating the influence of substrate topography on bonding and reactivity, often through the adsorption of molecules like ethylene.

Understanding these properties is essential for the development of catalysts and materials with enhanced performance. The detailed analysis of the surface structure and properties through various spectroscopic techniques provides insights into the electronic states of the surface atoms, influenced by the presence of subsurface Ni atoms. This information is critical for the design and optimization of catalysts and other materials.

        尼/铂双金属表面的表征，通过在铂（111）单晶或多晶铂基底上沉积一层镍单层进行合成，对于先进的表面科学和催化研究具有至关重要的作用。这一合成过程对于结构建模和密度泛函理论（DFT）计算至关重要，能够为这些双金属表面的电子和化学性质提供全面的理解。表征过程通常涉及使用同步辐射，具体是SA73b光束线搭配TGM7单色器，在不锈钢超高真空（UHV）腔室中进行实验，该腔室配备有各种光谱工具，如光电发射光谱（PES）、阿勒尔电子能谱（AES）和低能电子衍射（LEED）。

表征的关键方面包括：
1. 通过氩离子溅射和高温退火制备清洁的Cu（100）单晶表面。
2. 研究铂4f核心能级光发射线，以理解双金属表面的结构和组成。
3. 观察铂-Cu表面合金的形成，其在光谱中对应于铂沉积的不同峰。
4. 分析热处理对合金结构的影响，如峰强度的变化和峰位向较低结合能移动。
5. 探索基底表面拓扑对键合和反应性的影响，通常通过乙烯分子的吸附来实现。

了解这些属性对于催化剂和材料性能的提升至关重要。通过各种光谱技术对表面结构和性质的详细分析提供了关于表面原子电子状态的信息，这些状态受亚表面镍原子存在的影响。这些信息对于设计和优化催化剂和其他材料至关重要。
