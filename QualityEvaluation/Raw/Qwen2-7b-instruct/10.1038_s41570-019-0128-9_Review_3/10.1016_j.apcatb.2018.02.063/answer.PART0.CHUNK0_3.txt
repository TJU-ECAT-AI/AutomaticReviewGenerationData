<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalyst outperformed the Ni3Pt1/CeO2 catalyst in terms of activity and selectivity in the CO2-ODH reaction, while the latter showed superior stability during the CO2-DRP process. Both catalysts displayed notable synergistic effects, improving their overall performance in both reactions. The experimental findings were corroborated by DFT calculations, which shed light on the underlying reaction mechanisms. These bimetallic catalysts represent a viable solution for the efficient transformation of CO2 into useful chemicals, thereby facilitating CO2 emission reduction and advancing green chemistry practices.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
