<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The research utilized density functional theory (DFT) to explore the process of carbon dioxide (CO2) activation and dissociation on ceria (CeO2) (110) surfaces. The study also delved into the electronic properties and catalytic effectiveness of CeO2 as a catalyst for CO2 activation. Furthermore, the investigation encompassed the analysis of CO2 dissociation on surfaces of reduced CeO2 (110).
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
