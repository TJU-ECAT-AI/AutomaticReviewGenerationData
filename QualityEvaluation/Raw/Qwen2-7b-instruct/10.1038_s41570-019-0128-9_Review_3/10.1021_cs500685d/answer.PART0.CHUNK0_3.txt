<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study focused on the impact of gallium (Ga) loading and reaction parameters such as the CO2/C2H6 feed ratio, temperature, and space velocity on the performance, deactivation, and regeneration of the catalysts.
2. There is a clear interrelation between ethane conversion and ethene selectivity; higher ethane conversion leads to lower ethene selectivity.
3. The simplicity and effectiveness of the catalyst regeneration process suggest the potential for creating a dynamic process involving alternating reaction and regeneration cycles.
