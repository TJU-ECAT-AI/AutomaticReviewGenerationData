<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The Fe5-Cr10/Zr nano-composite catalyst showed an outstanding performance in the dehydrogenation of ethane with CO2, yielding 50.05% ethylene at 53.72% ethane conversion at 650°C.
2. The study aimed to enhance ethylene selectivity in the dehydrogenation of ethane with CO2 on Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts by adding Fe, Ni, Mn, and Co oxides.
3. According to ethylene yield, the catalytic activity of modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts at 650°C ranked as follows: Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 研究旨在通过添加铁、镍、锰和钴氧化物来提高Cr2O3/ZrO2纳米复合催化剂在二氧化碳存在下的乙烷脱氢过程中乙烯选择性。
