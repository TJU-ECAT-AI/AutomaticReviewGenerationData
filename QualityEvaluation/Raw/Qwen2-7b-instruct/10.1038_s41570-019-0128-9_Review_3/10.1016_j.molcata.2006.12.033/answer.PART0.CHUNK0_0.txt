<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The study aimed to enhance the ethylene selectivity in the dehydrogenation of ethane with CO2 on Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts by incorporating Fe, Ni, Mn, and Co oxides.

2. The catalytic activity of modified Cr2O3/ZrO2 nano-composite catalysts at 650°C ranked as follows: Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr.

3. The ethylene selectivity of Fe5-Cr10/Zr, Mn5-Cr10/Zr, and Co5-Cr10/Zr nano-composite catalysts was notably higher than that of Ni5-Cr10/Zr, indicating a preference for reforming and cracking reactions.

4. The type of modifier influenced the catalyst's textural and structural properties, the distribution of chromium and oxygen species, and the acid/base properties of the catalyst surface, collectively determining its activity in ethane dehydrogenation with CO2.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 研究旨在通过添加Fe、Ni、Mn和Co氧化物，提高Cr2O3/ZrO2纳米复合催化剂中乙烷与CO2脱氢生成乙烯的选择性。

2. 修改后的Cr2O3/ZrO2纳米复合催化剂在650°C下的催化活性按以下顺序排列：Fe5-Cr10/Zr > Mn5-Cr10/Zr > Co5-Cr10/Zr > Ni5-Cr10/Zr。

3. Fe5-Cr10/Zr、Mn5-Cr10/Zr和Co5-Cr10/Zr纳米复合催化剂的乙烯选择性明显高于Ni5-Cr10/Zr，这主要促进了重整和裂解反应。

4. 添加物的性质影响了催化剂的纹理和结构特性，铬和氧物种的分布，以及催化剂表面的酸/碱性质，共同决定了其在CO2作为氧化剂的乙烷脱氢过程中的活性。
