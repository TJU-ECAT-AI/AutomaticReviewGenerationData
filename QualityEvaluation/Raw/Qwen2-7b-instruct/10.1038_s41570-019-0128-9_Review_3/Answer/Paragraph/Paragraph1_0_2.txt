        The comparative analysis of bimetallic catalysts Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane has been comprehensively explored through experimental and theoretical (DFT) studies. These catalysts, featuring bimetallic systems with either precious metals (Pt or Pd) or non-precious metals (Fe, Co, or Ni) supported on CeO2, have demonstrated distinct performances in alkene production and CO2 emissions reduction.

        The selectivity and activity of these catalysts can be assessed by plotting CO2 conversion against CO selectivity. Unlike typical oxidation processes with oxygen leading to CO2 production due to over-oxidation, the CO2-ODH reaction results in a net reduction of CO2, as CO2 is consumed rather than produced. In situ XANES measurements have shown that the Ni3Pt catalyst consists of metallic Pt, while both Fe3Ni and Ni3Pt catalysts contain metallic Ni under reaction conditions.

        The results of steady-state flow reactions have identified Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 as promising catalysts for CO2-ODH and DRP of propane, respectively. The binding configurations of all intermediates on Fe3Ni(111) and Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) surfaces are similar, with all intermediates binding more strongly to the Fe3Ni(111) surface. Thermogravimetric analysis and in situ XRD measurements indicate that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 does not readily coke or experience surface metal agglomeration under reaction conditions, suggesting its stability and potential for environmentally benign catalysis.

        DFT calculations performed on bulk-terminated-Fe3Ni(111) and Pt-terminated-Ni3Pt(111) surfaces have further elucidated the potential reaction pathways for the oxidative cleavage of propane's CH and CC bonds. The DFT results reveal that the CC bond cleavage pathway is preferred on Ni3Pt(111), while bulk-terminated-Fe3Ni(111) favors the CH bond cleavage pathway.

        Fe3Ni shows a synergistic effect, demonstrating higher propylene yield compared to monometals when used as a catalyst for CO2-ODHP. The apparent activation energy for propane CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation over Fe3Ni is 115 kJ mol−1, while for Ni3Pt over the same process, it is 119 kJ mol−1. The Fe3Ni catalyst maintains stability with minimal mass loss, indicating a low likelihood of deactivation due to coking.

        The integrative understanding and prospective outlook suggest that the advancements in the field of bimetallic catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2, offer significant potential for enhancing global alkene production and reducing CO2 emissions. The synergistic effects observed between these catalysts and their superior selectivity and stability underscore their importance in catalytic processes for sustainable chemical synthesis.

        Future research endeavors might focus on exploring the synergistic effects in greater depth, optimizing reaction conditions, and investigating the practical applications of these catalysts in industrial-scale processes. Additionally, there is an opportunity to leverage these findings to develop new methodologies for the production of alkenes from CO2, contributing to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions and the advancement of environmentally benign catalysis.

        The insights gleaned from this study not only highlight the potential of bimetallic catalysts but also pave the way for innovative approaches in the chemical industry, emphasizing the role of catalysis in achieving sustainable chemical production and environmental stewardship.

        在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中使用丙烷时，对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2这两种双金属催化剂的比较性分析已经通过实验和理论（DFT）研究进行了全面探讨。这些由贵金属（Pt或Pd）和非贵金属（Fe、Co或Ni）组成的双金属系统，以及CeO2作为载体，已经在丙烯生产及减少CO2排放方面展现出不同的性能。

        这些催化剂的选择性和活性可以通过绘制CO2转化率与CO选择性之间的关系来评估。与典型的氧气氧化过程不同，该过程会产生CO2，而CO2-ODH反应则导致CO2净减少，因为CO2被消耗而不是产生。在反应条件下进行的原位XANES测量表明，Ni3Pt催化剂由金属Pt组成，而Fe3Ni和Ni3Pt催化剂都包含金属Ni。

        稳态流动反应的结果将Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2识别为CO2-ODH和DRP丙烷反应中的有前景的催化剂。所有中间体在Fe3Ni(111)和Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)表面上的结合配置相似，所有中间体均更强烈地结合到Fe3Ni(111)表面。根据热重分析和原位XRD测量，Fe3Ni1/CeO2在反应条件下不易结焦或发生表面金属聚集，表明其稳定性及其对环境友好催化潜力。

        对于Fe3Ni(111)和Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)表面进行的密度泛函理论（DFT）计算进一步揭示了丙烷的CH和CC键裂解潜在的反应途径。DFT结果表明，CC键裂解途径在Ni3Pt(111)上更优选，而Fe3Ni(111)倾向于CH键裂解途径。

        Fe3Ni显示了协同效应，证明了与单金属相比，在CO2-ODHP过程中丙烯产量更高。Fe3Ni在丙烷CO2氧化脱氢化过程中的显热能为115 kJ mol−1，而Ni3Pt的显热能为119 kJ mol−1。Fe3Ni催化剂仅损失其原始质量的不到一半，这表明由于结焦导致的主要失活路径的可能性很小。

        整合的理解和前瞻性的展望表明，双金属催化剂尤其是Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2的发展，对于增加全球烯烃生产并减少CO2排放具有重要意义。这些催化剂之间观察到的协同效应及其优越的选择性和稳定性强调了它们在催化过程中的重要性，以实现可持续化学合成。

        将来研究努力可能集中在更深入地探索协同效应，优化反应条件，并调查这些催化剂在工业规模过程中的实际应用。此外，还有机会利用这些发现开发新的方法来从CO2生产烯烃，从而促进温室气体减排并推动环境友好的催化技术发展。
