    <English>
        Comparative analysis of the performance, mechanisms, and characteristics of bimetallic catalysts in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane, as studied through experimental and theoretical (DFT) approaches, reveals insightful comparisons between Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2. The Fe3Ni1/CeO2 system exhibits promising selectivity for propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway, while Ni3Pt1/CeO2 is active for DRP with high selectivity towards CO. This indicates a synergistic effect in the bimetallic Fe3Ni1 catalyst, enhancing propylene yield compared to monometallic counterparts. The activation barriers for CO2-ODH and DRP are 115 kJ mol−1 and 119 kJ mol−1, respectively, over Fe3Ni1 and Ni3Pt, highlighting the energetic requirements for these processes.

        Both catalysts demonstrate stability under reaction conditions, with Fe3Ni1/CeO2 showing minimal mass loss during thermogravimetric analysis, suggesting a low risk of deactivation due to coking. In situ XANES measurements confirm that under reaction conditions, Ni3Pt1 consists of metallic Pt, while both Fe3Ni1 and Ni3Pt contain metallic Ni. This information contributes to our understanding of the active sites and their role in the reaction mechanism.

        The binding configurations of intermediates on Fe3Ni(111) and Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) are similar, indicating comparable interaction strengths with the catalyst surfaces. However, all intermediates bind more strongly to the Fe3Ni(111) surface, suggesting that this facet may play a crucial role in the reaction pathway.

        Density functional theory (DFT) calculations provide deeper insights into the potential reaction pathways. These simulations predict that the CC bond cleavage pathway is preferred on Ni3Pt(111), while bulk-terminated-Fe3Ni(111) favors the CH bond cleavage pathway. This distinction aligns with the observed differences in selectivity and efficiency of the two catalysts.

        These findings underscore the potential of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 in achieving environmentally benign catalysis for global alkene production and CO2 emissions reduction. The synergy in the bimetallic systems and their ability to selectively convert CO2 and propane into valuable products with lower energy barriers and enhanced stability offer promising avenues for future research and development in green chemistry.

        Looking ahead, the insights gained from these studies suggest a future direction for exploring the synergistic effects of bimetallic catalysts in CO2 utilization. Potential areas of investigation could include optimizing the composition and support materials for enhanced selectivity and activity, exploring the effects of different reaction conditions on catalyst performance, and developing novel methodologies for the design and synthesis of bimetallic catalysts tailored for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions.

        The development of such catalysts could significantly contribute to reducing CO2 emissions and promoting sustainable alkene production, thereby addressing pressing environmental concerns while advancing the field of catalysis science.

    <Chinese>
        对于在丙烷（C3H8）和丙烯（C4H10）上的二氧化碳氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）和分解反应（DRP），使用实验和理论（DFT）方法对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2两种双金属催化剂进行了比较分析。Fe3Ni1/CeO2系统在通过CO2-ODHP途径选择性生成丙烯方面表现出色，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2则在DRP中具有高CO选择性，这表明了双金属Fe3Ni1催化剂的协同效应，其丙烯产率相较于单金属催化剂显著提高。CO2-ODH和DRP的活化能分别为115 kJ mol−1和119 kJ mol−1，分别在Fe3Ni1和Ni3Pt上，这显示了这些过程的能量需求。

        两个催化剂均在反应条件下表现出稳定性，其中Fe3Ni1/CeO2在热重分析中的质量损失极小，暗示其主要失活路径不是由于结焦。原位XANES测量表明，在反应条件下，Ni3Pt1由金属态Pt组成，而Fe3Ni1和Ni3Pt都包含金属态Ni。这些信息有助于我们理解活性位点及其在反应机制中的作用。

        中间体在Fe3Ni(111)和Pt终止的Ni3Pt(111)表面的结合方式相似，显示了相似的与催化剂表面的相互作用强度。然而，所有中间体在Fe3Ni(111)表面上的结合力更强，这表明该晶面可能在反应途径中起关键作用。

        密度泛函理论（DFT）计算提供了深入的潜在反应途径见解。这些模拟预测CC键断裂途径在Ni3Pt(111)上占优势，而Fe3Ni(111)晶面倾向于CH键断裂途径。这一差异与两种催化剂观察到的选择性和效率相吻合。

        这些发现强调了Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2在实现环境友好催化以减少CO2排放和促进全球烯烃生产方面的潜力。双金属体系的协同作用和它们在选择性转化CO2和丙烷成有价值的产物时较低的能量壁垒以及增强的稳定性为未来研究和开发绿色化学提供了有前景的方向。

        从这些研究中获得的洞察提示了探索双金属催化剂在CO2利用方面的协同效应的未来方向。可能的研究领域包括优化组成和支持材料以提高选择性和活性、探索不同反应条件对催化剂性能的影响，并开发设计用于CO2-ODH和DRP反应的双金属催化剂的新方法。

        这样发展催化剂将有助于显著减少CO2排放并推动催化科学领域的可持续烯烃生产，同时解决环境挑战。
