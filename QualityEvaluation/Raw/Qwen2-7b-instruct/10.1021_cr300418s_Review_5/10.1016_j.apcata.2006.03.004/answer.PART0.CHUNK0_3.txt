<p>The presence of Au+ and Au3+ species has also been evidenced by XPS analysis.</p>
<p>FTIR spectra of CO adsorption at 90 K on the Au/CeO2 samples after in situ CO oxidation reaction at 273 K are shown in Fig. 2.</p>
<p>The concentration of the different gold species observed in the Au/CeO2 catalysts during catalytic CO oxidation, Table 1, evidence how oxidized gold species are stabilized on these samples during reaction.</p>

4. Table 1 details the concentration of various gold species in the Au/CeO2 catalyst during CO oxidation, highlighting the stabilization of oxidized gold species.
