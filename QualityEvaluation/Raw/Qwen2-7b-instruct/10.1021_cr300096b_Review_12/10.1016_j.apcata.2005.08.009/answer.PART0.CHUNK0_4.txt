<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The addition of Re to both Pt/TiO2 and Pd/TiO2 catalysts increases WGS activity. The effect is more remarkable in Pd/TiO2 catalysts than in Pt/TiO2 catalysts, and the effect is more dependent on the reducing temperature for Pt/TiO2 catalysts.
2. In Pt catalysts, the metal particle sizes increase with the addition of Re, leading to an increase in the formation rate of H2 despite saturation at a Pt:Re ratio of 2:2. The addition of Re forms a certain bimetallic cluster between Pt and Re.
3. Obvious acceleration was observed in PdRe(1:1)/TiO2 catalysts, becoming several times larger over PdRe(1:10)/TiO2 catalysts. The initial acceleration of H2 formation rate may be due to the formation of bimetallic clusters, but a different addition manner was suggested for the later acceleration effect over PdRe(1:10)/TiO2 catalysts.
4. Different types of CO adsorption occur with the addition of Re, which are observed in the IR spectra taken after WGS reactions.
5. WGS reactions proceed via the formation of surface-formate anions over Re-added Pt and Pd catalysts, with the formation step of formate being the rate-determining step of the WGS reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 添加Re到Pt/TiO2和Pd/TiO2催化剂中可以提高水煤气变换反应的活性。Pd/TiO2催化剂中的效果更为明显，且这种效果在Pt/TiO2催化剂中更多地依赖于还原温度。
2. 在Pt催化剂中，随着Re含量的增加，金属颗粒尺寸增大，导致H2形成速率增加，尽管在Pt:Re比例为2:2时达到饱和。Re的存在形成了一种Pt和Re之间的二元合金簇。
3. 明显加速发生在PdRe(1:1)/TiO2催化剂上，其H2形成速率比PdRe(1:10)/TiO2催化剂高几倍。初始加速可能由二元合金簇的形成引起，但对于PdRe(1:10)/TiO2催化剂来说，后来的加速作用方式有所不同。
4. 通过添加Re，不同类型的CO吸附发生在催化剂上，这些现象在WGS反应后的红外光谱中被观察到。
5. WGS反应通过在Pt和Pd催化剂表面形成的表面-甲酸盐离子进行，形成步骤是WGS反应的决定性步骤。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
