3. Results and discussion
"Based on this Pt dispersion, the exposed Pt surface area was calculated for Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2, Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2 and Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2. The WGS rate was observed to be proportional to the exposed Pt surface area for the Pt-Re and Pt catalysts; however, the rate per m2 of exposed Pt for Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2 was higher than that of Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2 or Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
3. Calculated based on the Pt dispersion, the exposed Pt surface areas were determined for Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2, Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2, and Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2. The WGS rate was found to correlate with the exposed Pt surface area for both Pt-Re and Pt catalysts, yet the rate per square meter of exposed Pt was notably higher for Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2 compared to Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2 or Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
3. 根据Pt分布计算，暴露的Pt表面积被确定为Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2、Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2和Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2。对于Pt-Re和Pt催化剂，WGS速率与暴露的Pt表面积成正比；然而，对于Pt-Re/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2的每平方米暴露的Pt速率明显高于Pt/Ce0.46Zr0.54O2或Pt/Ce0.6Zr0.4O2。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
