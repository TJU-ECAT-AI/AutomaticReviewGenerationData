This study focuses on the structure-induced enhancement in electrooxidation of trimetallic FePtAu nanoparticles, revealing how the structure of these nanoparticles significantly impacts their electrocatalytic activity. The results suggest that the findings might correlate with model surfaces and supported catalysts, and future UHV studies could help address the limitations in understanding Pt-based bimetallic surfaces.
