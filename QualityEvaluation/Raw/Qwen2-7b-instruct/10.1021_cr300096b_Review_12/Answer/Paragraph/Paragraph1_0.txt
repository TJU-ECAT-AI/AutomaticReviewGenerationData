As documented in \[10.1038/nmat2359\], the ternary PtRhSnO2 electrocatalyst, for instance, exhibits exceptional efficiency in ethanol oxidation to CO2, demonstrating the synergy between its constituent metals.

The challenges in UHV studies provide a fertile ground for innovation, driving the development of new experimental techniques and theoretical frameworks that could revolutionize our understanding of catalysis at the atomic level.
