<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Bimetallic catalysts, especially those based on Pt, show enhanced activity for preferential CO oxidation in hydrogen environments compared to their monometallic counterparts. This is crucial for efficient CO conversion to CO2.
2. Water gas shift reactions and methane conversions are influenced by the surface chemistry of these catalysts, highlighting their role in various chemical processes.
3. Future UHV studies aim to better understand and correlate the findings of catalyst performance with model surfaces and supported catalysts, despite existing limitations.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
