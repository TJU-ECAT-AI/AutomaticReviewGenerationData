<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Heterogeneous photocatalyst materials, such as oxides with metal cations in d0 and d10 configurations, metal (oxy)sulfides, and metal (oxy)nitrides, have been extensively studied for water splitting into H2 and O2. This rich library of photocatalysts provides valuable insights into the factors influencing photocatalytic performance and the design of new materials.
