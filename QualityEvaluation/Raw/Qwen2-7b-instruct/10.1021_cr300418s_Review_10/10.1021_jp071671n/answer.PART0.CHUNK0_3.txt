<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The literature discusses the effects of various metal dopants on the oxygen vacancy formation energy (OVFE) in ZnO(1010) surfaces. Alkali metals (Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs), Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, and Cd decrease OVFE and prefer to substitute a Zn atom at the surface of ZnO. On the other hand, elements like Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, and Pb increase OVFE. Among those that increase OVFE, Ca, Sr, Ba, La, Ce, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Sn, and Pb prefer to substitute a Zn atom at the surface while the rest are more likely to substitute a Zn atom in the bulk.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
