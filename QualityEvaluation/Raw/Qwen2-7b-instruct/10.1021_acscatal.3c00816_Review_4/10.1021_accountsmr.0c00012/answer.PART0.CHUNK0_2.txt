<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Pt-based catalysts are more environmentally friendly than CrOx-based catalysts.

2. The PDH reaction involves several steps: propane adsorption, CH bond cleavage, C3H6 desorption, and site recovery.

3. The PDH process is endothermic and requires high temperatures which can limit catalyst development.

4. Four steps are recognized that occur on the catalyst surface during the PDH reaction.

5. The CH bond cleavage of propane can be categorized into α-type (terminal methyl group) and β-type (central carbon).

6. Geometrically dispersed and electron-rich Pt sites show higher propylene selectivity.

7. Disordered PtCu alloy exhibits higher propylene selectivity and lower deactivation compared to pure Pt.

8. Core-shell nanostructures reduce the usage of noble metals and increase propylene selectivity.

9. Subnanometric metal species can form within zeolite pores, enhancing catalyst performance.

10. PtSn clusters remain stable after H2 pretreatment at 600°C.

11. The presence of TiO2 shifts desorption peaks of propylene to lower temperatures, reducing adsorption.

12. Adding TiO2 on Al2O3 supports increases the electron density of Pt, improving propylene selectivity and stability.

13. Nanocarbons, due to their abundance in surface sites, attract attention as catalyst supports.

14. Reductive treatment introduces coordinatively unsaturated M cations in MO sites, enhancing CH bond activation.

15. DRIFTs show a shift in the position of CH vibration peaks upon adding TiO2, indicating changes in the chemical environment.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. Pt催化剂比CrOx催化剂更环保。

2. PDH反应涉及四个步骤：丙烷吸附、CH键断裂、C3H6脱附以及活性位点恢复。

3. PDH过程是吸热的，需要高温，这可能限制催化剂的发展。

4. 认识到在催化剂表面发生的四个步骤：丙烷吸附、CH键断裂、C3H6脱附和位点恢复。

5. 烯烃的CH键断裂可以分为α型（末端甲基）和β型（中央碳）。

6. 几何分散和电子丰富的Pt位点显示出更高的丙烯选择性。

7. 杂乱的PtCu合金在丙烯选择性和活性上优于纯Pt。

8. 核壳纳米结构通过构建3dTM@贵金属纳米结构来减少贵金属使用并提高丙烯选择性。

9. 在沸石孔中形成亚纳米金属物种，增强催化剂性能。

10. PtSn簇在600°C的H2预处理后保持稳定。

11. 添加TiO2后，丙烯脱附峰向更低温度移动，减少了吸附。

12. 在Al2O3支持上添加适量的TiO2增加了Pt的电子密度，提高了丙烯选择性和催化稳定性。

13. 由于表面存在丰富位点，纳米碳吸引作为催化剂支撑的关注。

14. 通过还原处理引入MO位点中的配位不饱和M离子，增强CH键活化。

15. DRIFTs显示在添加TiO2后，CH振动峰的位置发生变化，表明化学环境的变化。
