<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology significantly impact the performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions. Specifically, the use of BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate zeolites improves propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability. These improvements can be attributed to the specific framework structure and ion-exchange properties of the zeolites, which facilitate the desired chemical transformations while minimizing unwanted side reactions. Enhanced conversion rates and catalyst stability are achieved through optimized surface area, acidity, and basicity profiles tailored to the PDH process conditions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
准备方法、组成和沸石拓扑结构对沸石基催化剂在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的性能有显著影响。特别是，使用BEA拓扑结构和硅铝磷酸盐沸石可以提高丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性。这些改进归因于沸石的特定框架结构和离子交换性质，它们促进了所需的化学转换，同时减少了不必要的副反应。通过优化表面面积、酸性和碱性特征，使材料适应PDH工艺条件，从而实现增强的转化率和催化剂稳定性。
