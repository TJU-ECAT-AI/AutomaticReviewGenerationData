<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology play a critical role in determining the performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions. BEA topologic zeolites, known for their large internal surface area and high porosity, provide superior propylene selectivity due to their efficient separation and activation of alkane molecules. Silicoaluminophosphate zeolites (SAPOs), with their tailored pore size and shape, enhance conversion rates by optimizing the reaction pathways for alkane dehydrogenation. Key factors contributing to improved propylene selectivity include the precise tuning of the zeolite's pore size and acidity, which guide the preferential activation of CH bonds leading to propylene formation over side reactions. Catalyst stability is also influenced by the zeolite's resistance to sintering and the protection of active sites against coke formation, often achieved through the strategic inclusion of promoters or the optimization of the zeolite's structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
准备方法、组成和沸石拓扑结构在决定基于沸石的催化剂在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的性能方面起着关键作用。具有大内部表面积和高孔隙率的BEA类型沸石，由于能够有效地分离和激活烷烃分子，提供出色的丙烯选择性。硅铝磷酸盐沸石（SAPOs），以其定制的孔径大小和形状，通过优化烷烃脱氢的反应路径来增强转化率。提高丙烯选择性的关键因素包括精确调整沸石的孔径大小和酸性，这指导了优先激活CH键，从而形成丙烯而不是副反应。催化剂稳定性还受到沸石抵抗聚集和保护活性位点免受焦炭形成的影响，通常通过策略性地包含促进剂或优化沸石的结构来实现。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
