For propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions, the performance of zeolite-based catalysts is significantly influenced by their preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology, particularly when focusing on BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate (SAPO) zeolites.

        The ALD process notably enhances the activity of Y zeolite, increasing its conversion rate to 1116% at 550°C when prepared through 13 cycles of ZnO ALD (ref. [10.1039/C5CP05043J]). The nanolith catalytic system outperforms conventional powders in oxidative dehydrogenation of cyclohexane, demonstrating both efficiency and reduction in over-oxidation (refs. [10.1021/acs.iecr.9b00032], [10.1039/D1SC05866E]). The ALD process generates high-density ZnO sites on the support, boosting catalytic activity but also risking coke formation and crystallization of catalytic phases, leading to accelerated catalyst deactivation (refs. [10.1039/C5CP05043J], [10.1039/D1SC05866E]).

        The micropore size of Y zeolite is notably larger than that of DEZ, allowing precursor molecules to easily enter the micropores during the first three ALD cycles (ref. [10.1039/C5CP05043J]). Post-stability testing, the catalyst color transitions from white to black, indicating potential deactivation mechanisms (ref. [10.1039/C5CP05043J]).

        Incorporation of phosphorus (P) to a nickel-phosphorus (NiP) catalyst significantly improves its performance in isobutane dehydrogenation (ref. [10.1021/acs.iecr.9b00032]). The BEA topological structure of the zeolite enhances catalytic activity in propane dehydrogenation (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]), while SAPO zeolites show better stability and selectivity towards propylene compared to other types of zeolites (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]). Propane dehydrogenation reactions are influenced by the preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology of the catalyst (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]). Improved propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability are attributed to specific features of the BEA and SAPO zeolites (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]).

        The Ga+-chabazite zeolite was found to be a highly selective catalyst for non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]). Preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology significantly influence the performance of various zeolite-based catalysts (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]). BEA topologic and SAPO zeolites showed improved propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability (ref. [10.1021/jacs.2c03941]).

        Questions focus on the impact of zeolite topology, preparation methods, and composition on the performance of zeolite-based catalysts in PDH, particularly regarding the role of BEA topology and SAPO zeolites. The discussion centers on how indium species, especially in the form of [InH2]+, affect the catalytic activity, selectivity, and stability of the catalysts (ref. [10.1039/D1SC05866E]). The relevance is evident in the exploration of how factors like indium loading, support materials, and the structure of the zeolite influence the catalyst's efficiency in converting propane to propylene (ref. [10.1039/D1SC05866E]).

        The high-temperature stability of the subnanometric Pt species in Pt@MCM-22 has been demonstrated through oxidation-reduction treatments at 650°C, resulting in the formation of small Pt nanoparticles with sizes around 1nm (ref. [10.1038/nmat4757]). These nanoparticles are found in Pt@MCM-22 after one cycle of treatment at this temperature (ref. [10.1038/nmat4757]). Considering the exceptional stability of Pt@MCM-22, its catalytic activity for dehydrogenating propane has been tested (ref. [10.1038/nmat4757]).

        Continuous global oil depletion provokes the urgent demand to find an alternative way to produce propylene (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). Direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) to propylene is thus of great significance in the chemical industry, upgrading low-priced and abundant propane to value-added propylene (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). Supported CrOx and Pt catalysts used in the PDH industry exhibit very high catalytic activity, but suffer from various problems such as high price, environmental hostility, and fast deactivation (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). Alternatively, several transition metals, void of shortcomings typical of CrOx and Pt catalysts, have been investigated for the PDH reaction (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). Unfortunately, they achieved much lower catalytic activity or propylene selectivity for PDH (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). The earth abundance of Co bodes well for long-term utility, and it has been reported to show a certain degree of catalytic activity for PDH (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). CoOx within dealuminated Beta (SiBeta) zeolite developing into ultrasmall-sized Co particles under H2 or reactant gas is highly efficient for catalyzing direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) to propylene reaction (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). The existence of highly dispersed CoOx species is identified, and the easily reducible CoOx species are confined in the created T-sites (silanols) of SiBeta support (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). CoSiBeta catalysts with or without prereduction show similar catalytic performance but different induction periods (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). The CoSiBeta catalysts with 0.5 wt% showed state-of-the-art propylene productivity with propane conversion of about 72% and propylene selectivity of over 92% at 600°C (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]). This work not only affords a green and very promising nonprecious dehydrogenation catalyst but gives new insight into the active Co phase for the PDH reaction (ref. [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]).

        从参考文献[10.1039/C5CP05043J]中得到深入分析，通过参考文献[10.1021/acs.iecr.9b00032]、[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]和[10.1039/D1SC05866E]进行综合理解。在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中，通过其制备方法、组成和沸石拓扑结构影响了沸石基催化剂的性能，特别是关注于BEA拓扑和硅铝磷酸盐（SAPO）沸石。

        ALD过程显著提高了Y沸石的活性，将其转化率提高至550°C时的1116%，这是在通过13周期的ZnO ALD制备的沸石中实现的（参考文献[10.1039/C5CP05043J]）。纳米晶催化系统在氧化脱氢环己烷中优于常规粉末，在效率和过氧化减少方面均表现出色（参考文献[10.1021/acs.iecr.9b00032]、[10.1039/D1SC05866E]）。ALD过程在支持物上生成高密度ZnO位点，提升了催化活性，但也有助于焦炭物种的生成和催化相的结晶，从而加速了催化剂的失效（参考文献[10.1039/C5CP05043J]、[10.1039/D1SC05866E]）。

        Y沸石的微孔尺寸明显大于DEZ，允许前体分子在前三个ALD周期内容易进入微孔（参考文献[10.1039/C5CP05043J]）。稳定性测试后，催化剂的颜色从白色转变为黑色，这表明可能存在失效机制（参考文献[10.1039/C5CP05043J]）。

        向镍磷（NiP）催化剂中添加磷（P）显著改善了其在异丁烷脱氢中的性能（参考文献[10.1021/acs.iecr.9b00032]）。BEA拓扑结构的沸石提高了丙烷脱氢的催化活性（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]），而SAPO沸石在稳定性和丙烯选择性方面比其他类型的沸石更好（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。丙烷脱氢反应受催化剂的制备方法、组成和沸石拓扑的影响（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。改进的丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性归因于BEA和SAPO沸石的特定特性（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。

        Ga+-chabazite沸石被发现是无氧化的丙烷脱氢的高选择性催化剂（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。制备方法、组成和沸石拓扑对各种沸石基催化剂的性能产生了显著影响（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。BEA拓扑和SAPO沸石展示了提高的丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性（参考文献[10.1021/jacs.2c03941]）。

        问题集中在沸石拓扑、制备方法和组成对沸石基催化剂在PDH中的性能的影响上，特别是关于BEA拓扑和SAPO沸石的作用。讨论集中在以[InH2]+形式存在的铟物种如何影响催化剂的活性、选择性和稳定性（参考文献[10.1039/D1SC05866E]）。这种相关性体现在探索诸如铟负载、支撑材料和沸石结构等因素如何影响催化剂将丙烷转化为丙烯的效率（参考文献[10.1039/D1SC05866E]）。

        在650°C下通过氧化还原处理证明了Pt@MCM-22中亚纳米Pt物种的高稳定性，形成了大小约为1nm的小Pt纳米颗粒（参考文献[10.1038/nmat4757]）。这些纳米颗粒在经过一次氧化还原处理后出现在Pt@MCM-22中（参考文献[10.1038/nmat4757]）。考虑到Pt@MCM-22的出色稳定性，对其脱氢丙烷的催化活性进行了测试（参考文献[10.1038/nmat4757]）。

        全球石油持续枯竭激发了寻找替代方法生产丙烯的迫切需求（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。直接将丙烷（PDH）转化为丙烯对于化学工业具有重要意义，将低价且丰富的丙烷升级为附加值较高的丙烯（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。在PDH行业中使用的CrOx和支持Pt催化剂显示出非常高的催化活性，但存在高成本、环境敌对性和快速失活等问题（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。相反，几种避免了CrOx和Pt催化剂典型缺点的过渡金属已被研究用于PDH反应（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。不幸的是，它们的PDH活性或丙烯选择性都大大降低（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。钴的地球丰度为长期使用提供了良好的前景，并且据报道它在PDH中显示了一定程度的催化活性（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。在去铝化Beta（SiBeta）沸石中形成的超小钴粒子在H2或反应气体下高效催化直接脱氢丙烷（PDH）至丙烯反应（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。高度分散的CoOx物种的存在已被确认，易于还原的CoOx物种被限制在SiBeta支持的T-位点（硅醇）中（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。CoSiBeta催化剂无论是否经过预还原，均显示相似的催化性能但不同的诱导期（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。CoSiBeta催化剂中的0.5 wt%显示了最先进的丙烯生产力，丙烷转化率为约72%，丙烯选择性超过92%在600°C（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。这项工作不仅提供了一个绿色且非常有前途的非贵金属脱氢催化剂，而且还为PDH反应中的活性Co相提供了新的见解（参考文献[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]）。

        整合理解和前瞻展望部分将考虑文件中讨论的进展和发现，强调进一步研究探索新型沸石结构和组成的重要性，以及优化制备方法以增强催化剂性能的可能性。未来方向可能包括新型金属前体的调查、更有效支撑材料的发展，以及通过设计先进的沸石拓扑来增强催化剂稳定性的优化。此外，结合高级表征技术和计算方法可以提供对沸石基PDH催化剂活性和稳定性的机制的更深理解。
