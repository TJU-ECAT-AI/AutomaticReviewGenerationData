<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study investigated how the structure and chemistry of bimetallic nanoparticle catalysts change under reaction conditions using ambient-pressure XPS in the Torr pressure range. The gases used were chosen due to their roles in energy conversion and pollution control processes. The transformations in structure and chemistry of these catalysts under reaction conditions highlight the significance of in situ studies for comprehending catalytic mechanisms at the atomic level.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
