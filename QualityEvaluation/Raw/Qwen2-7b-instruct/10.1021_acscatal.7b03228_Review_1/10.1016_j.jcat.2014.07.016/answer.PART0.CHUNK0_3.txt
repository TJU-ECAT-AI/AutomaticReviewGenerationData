Answer: Single-atom alloys, especially Pd atoms embedded in Cu surfaces, boost catalytic efficiency in selective hydrogenation processes. Designing effective SAA catalysts requires consideration of reaction conditions, metal characteristics, and advantages over conventional catalysts. These catalysts often defy scaling laws and enhance selectivity in a multitude of systems.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
