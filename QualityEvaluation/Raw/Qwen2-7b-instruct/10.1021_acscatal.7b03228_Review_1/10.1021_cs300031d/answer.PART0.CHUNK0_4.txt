<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Single-atom alloys, particularly Pd atoms embedded in Cu surfaces, enhance catalytic performance due to their unique electronic structure and atomic arrangement. The key criteria for designing successful SAA catalysts involve selecting appropriate metals, employing effective preparation methods, and utilizing suitable support materials. These catalysts often outperform traditional catalysts by breaking scaling rules and enhancing selectivity across various systems.
