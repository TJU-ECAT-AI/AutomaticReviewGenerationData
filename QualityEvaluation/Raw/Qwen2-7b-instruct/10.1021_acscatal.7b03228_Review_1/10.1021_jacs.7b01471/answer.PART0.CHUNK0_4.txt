<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The formation of single-atom alloys (SAAs) in intermetallic nanostructures provides a unique opportunity to precisely control the electronic structure of transition metals, leading to high catalytic selectivity for semihydrogenation reactions.
2. For the design of successful SAA catalysts, critical factors include the choice of metal, surface chemistry, and reaction conditions. Metal properties, such as electronic structure and reactivity, play a crucial role in determining the catalytic activity and selectivity.
3. Specific advantages of SAAs over traditional catalysts include improved selectivity, enhanced stability, and the ability to break scaling rules, offering exceptional selectivity towards alkene formation and outperforming conventional catalysts in semihydrogenation reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在互金属纳米结构中形成的单原子合金（SAAs）为精确控制过渡金属的电子结构提供了独特的机会，从而在半氢化反应中展现出高催化选择性。
2. 设计成功的SAA催化剂的关键因素包括金属的选择、表面化学和反应条件。金属的性质，如电子结构和反应性，在确定催化活性和选择性方面起着关键作用。
3. SAAs相对于传统催化剂的特定优势包括提高选择性、增强稳定性，并能够打破缩放规则，提供在半氢化反应中对烯烃形成高度选择性的优势，超越传统的催化剂性能。
