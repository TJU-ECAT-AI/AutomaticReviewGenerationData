            The multidimensional nature of the oxidative dehydrogenation (ODHP) reaction mechanism over boron-based catalysts, incorporating both surface and gas-phase radical reactions, significantly contributes to our understanding of the overall reaction network. These mechanisms emphasize the importance of considering both surface chemistry and gas-phase dynamics in the catalytic system. The synergistic effects between the surface reaction and gas radical reaction play a crucial role in achieving high activity and selectivity during the ODHP process. By integrating the surface reaction mechanism with the gas radical reaction mechanism, researchers gain insights into the complex interplay between surface and gas-phase processes, which govern the selective formation of products like propene and other olefins.

            The implications of these synergistic effects are profound for catalyst performance and design optimization. By harmonizing surface and gas-phase reactions, the catalyst system can efficiently manage competing pathways involved in the conversion of syngas to light olefins. This leads to higher selectivity for desired products, reduced formation of byproducts like methane, and improved stability over time. The use of a partially reduced oxide surface for CO activation, combined with the zeolite's confined acidic pores for carbon-carbon (CC) coupling, enables a flexible approach to adjusting CO conversion and olefin selectivity simultaneously. This opens new avenues for the development of syngas-to-olefin technologies, potentially enabling the efficient utilization of coal- and biomass-derived syngas with varying hydrogen-to-carbon (H2/CO) ratios.

            The composite catalyst's high selectivity and stability, as demonstrated in the 110-hour test, showcase its potential for industrial applications. The results indicate that the catalyst can maintain high C2C4 selectivity (94%) and low methane content (2%) even under prolonged operation, highlighting its robustness and efficiency. Additionally, the catalyst's performance in converting ketene directly to light olefins through the mesoporous silica component further underscores the importance of the catalyst's multifunctionality and the interplay between its components in achieving optimal reaction outcomes.

            Achieving the synergy of the surface reaction and gas radical reaction is of considerable significance to enhance the understanding of the ODHP reaction mechanism and to design a more effective boron-based catalytic system. The ability to optimize both surface and gas-phase reactions allows for the efficient conversion of propane into propylene with high selectivity, making boron-based catalysts a promising tool for the synthesis of valuable olefinic feedstocks from renewable and fossil resources.

            Looking ahead, the field of ODHP catalysis holds numerous opportunities for future exploration. Potential areas of focus include the development of more selective catalysts for the production of specific olefinic products, the improvement of catalyst stability under industrially relevant conditions, and the integration of advanced materials and technologies to enhance reaction efficiency and product purity. With continued research and innovation, the ODHP reaction mechanism over boron-based catalysts promises to play a pivotal role in the sustainable production of olefins, supporting the growth of industries ranging from petrochemicals to biorefineries.

            在硼基催化剂上进行氧化脱氢反应（ODHP）的多维反应机制，包括表面和气体相自由基反应，对整体反应网络的理解贡献显著。这些机制强调了在催化系统中同时考虑表面化学和气体相动力学的重要性。表面反应与气体自由基反应之间的协同效应在实现ODHP过程中的高活性和选择性方面起着关键作用。通过整合表面反应机制与气体自由基反应机制，研究人员获得了关于表面和气体相过程之间复杂相互作用的洞察，这调控了产物如丙烯和其他烯烃的选择性形成。

            这些协同效应的含义深远，对于催化剂性能和设计优化具有重大影响。通过协调表面和气体相反应，催化剂系统可以有效地管理合成轻烯烃过程中竞争路径的管理。这导致了对所需产品的更高选择性、甲烷等副产品的减少形成，并且在长时间运行下提高了稳定性。使用部分还原的氧化物表面进行二氧化碳激活，结合介孔二氧化硅的受限酸性孔进行碳碳（CC）耦合，允许灵活调整二氧化碳转化率和烯烃选择性。这为开发合成从可再生和化石资源中获取轻质烯烃的技术开辟了新的途径，有可能高效利用煤炭-和生物质衍生的合成气。

            复合催化剂的高选择性和稳定性，在110小时测试中得到了证明，展示了其工业应用潜力。结果表明，即使在长时间运行下，催化剂仍能保持高达94%的C2C4选择性和仅2%的甲烷含量，凸显了其稳健性和效率。此外，通过介孔二氧化硅组件直接将酮烯转化为轻烯烃的能力进一步强调了催化剂的多功能性和其组成部分间的相互作用在实现最佳反应结果中的重要性。

            实现表面反应和气体自由基反应之间的协同效应对增强ODHP反应机制的理解和设计更有效的硼基催化系统具有重要意义。优化表面和气体相反应的能力允许高效地将丙烷转化为丙烯并保持高选择性，使得硼基催化剂成为从可再生能源到化石资源中生产有价值的烯烃原料的有前景的工具。

            展望未来，ODHP催化领域提供了许多研究机会。关注领域包括开发更选择性的催化剂以生产特定的烯烃产品、改善在工业相关条件下催化剂的稳定性以及集成先进的材料和技术以提高反应效率和产品纯度。随着持续的研究和创新，ODHP反应机制在硼基催化剂上的作用将在可持续生产烯烃方面发挥核心作用，支持石油化工和生物精炼等行业的发展。
