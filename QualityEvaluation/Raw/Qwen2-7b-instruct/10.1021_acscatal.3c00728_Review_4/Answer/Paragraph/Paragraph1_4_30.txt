            <div>
                <p>The multidimensional nature of the oxidative dehydrogenation (ODHP) reaction mechanism over boron-based catalysts, encompassing both surface and gas-phase radical reactions, significantly enhances our understanding of the overall reaction network. This synergy among different reaction pathways has profound implications for catalyst performance and design optimization, allowing for more efficient propane conversion and improved product yield.</p>
                <p>Key insights include:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>The interplay between surface and gas-phase reactions elucidates the complex chemistry governing the ODHP process.</li>
                    <li>Surface-mediated formation of radicals over metastable sites, followed by sequential propagation in the gas phase, rationalizes the remarkable product distribution.</li>
                    <li>Optimizing the structure of chromium oxide particles on the support surface can lead to more efficient catalysts, enhancing both selectivity and activity.</li>
                    <li>Quantum chemical calculations provide a theoretical foundation for understanding the catalytic mechanisms and predicting the behavior of the catalysts under various conditions.</li>
                    <li>The ability to tailor catalysts for optimal performance in the ODHP reaction involves balancing the surface and gas-phase reactions to achieve high olefin selectivity and reduced methane formation.</li>
                </ul>
            </div>
            <div>
                <p>Looking ahead, the research into boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction opens new avenues for industrial applications. The development of catalysts capable of efficiently converting propane into propylene with high selectivity and stability over long periods is crucial for the sustainable production of olefins. The integration of both surface and gas-phase chemistry in the design of these catalysts holds promise for the utilization of coal- and biomass-derived syngas with varying hydrogen-to-carbon (H2/CO) ratios, expanding the feedstock options for petrochemical industries.</p>
                <p>Future research directions might include:</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>Further investigation into the catalysts' performance under diverse operating conditions to enhance robustness and efficiency.</li>
                    <li>Development of advanced computational models to predict catalyst behavior and optimize reaction pathways.</li>
                    <li>Exploration of alternative catalyst materials and combinations that could offer improved performance or environmental benefits.</li>
                    <li>Integration of the ODHP process into existing industrial infrastructure to assess economic feasibility and scalability.</li>
                    <li>Investigation of the ODHP process in continuous flow reactors to improve product purity and throughput.</li>
                </ul>
            </div>

    <Chinese> 
            <div>
                <p>硼基催化剂上的氧化脱氢化（ODHP）反应机制的多维性质，包含表面和气相自由基反应，极大地增强了我们对整体反应网络的理解。这些不同反应路径之间的协同作用对于催化剂性能和设计优化具有深远的影响，能够实现更高效的丙烷转化并提高产品产率。</p>
                <p>关键见解包括：</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>表面和气相反应之间的相互作用揭示了ODHP过程的复杂化学反应。</li>
                    <li>通过表面介导在不稳定位点形成自由基，并在气相中进行顺序传播，可以解释出奇的产物分布。</li>
                    <li>优化铬氧化物颗粒在支撑表面的结构可以导致更高效的催化剂，增强选择性和活性。</li>
                    <li>量子化学计算为理解催化机制提供理论基础，并预测催化剂在各种条件下的行为。</li>
                    <li>设计催化剂以实现最优的ODHP反应性能涉及平衡表面和气相反应，以实现高烯烃选择性并减少甲烷生成。</li>
                </ul>
            </div>
            <div>
                <p>展望未来，对硼基催化剂的研究为工业应用开辟了新的途径。能够高效地将丙烷转化为丙烯并保持稳定性的催化剂对于石油化学行业的可持续生产至关重要。整合表面和气相化学在催化剂设计中的作用，为利用煤炭和生物质衍生的合成气（H2/CO比例不同）提供了可能性，从而扩大了原料选项。</p>
                <p>未来研究方向可能包括：</p>
                <ul>
                    <li>进一步研究催化剂在不同操作条件下的性能，以提高其稳定性和效率。</li>
                    <li>开发高级计算模型来预测催化剂行为并优化反应路径。</li>
                    <li>探索替代催化剂材料和组合，以提供改进的性能或环境效益。</li>
                    <li>评估ODHP过程集成到现有工业基础设施的经济可行性和规模性。</li>
                    <li>研究连续流反应器中的ODHP过程，以改善产品纯度和吞吐量。</li>
                </ul>
            </div>
