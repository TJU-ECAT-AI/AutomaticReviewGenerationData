<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The multidimensional nature of the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction mechanism over boron-based catalysts involves both surface and gas-phase radical reactions, which significantly contribute to our understanding of the entire reaction network. This understanding implies that the interactions between these different dimensions play crucial roles in catalyst performance and can guide the optimization of catalyst design for improved efficiency.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
