The multidimensional nature of the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reaction mechanism on boron-based catalysts, which includes both surface and gas-phase radical reactions, significantly enhances our understanding of the overall reaction network. This synergy between surface and gas-phase reactions contributes to improved catalyst performance and offers opportunities for optimizing catalyst design.
