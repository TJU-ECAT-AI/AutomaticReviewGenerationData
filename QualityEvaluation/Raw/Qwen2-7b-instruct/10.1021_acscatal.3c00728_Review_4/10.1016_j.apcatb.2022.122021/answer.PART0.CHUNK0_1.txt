<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The vapor migration of boron-containing species from pBN to surfaces of Ni NPs is suggested to achieve the Strong Metal-Support Interaction (SMSI) between Ni NPs and pBN.
2. The encapsulation of metal NPs with support-derived material overlayers is considered a typical characteristic of the SMSI effect in oxide-supported metal catalysts.
3. Both high reaction temperature and the generated water product play crucial roles in the formation and vapor migration of BOx or B(OH)x species from pBN onto Ni NPs.
4. This study unveils a new mechanism for the formation of encapsulation overlayers on metal nanoparticles under the SMSI state.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 建议从pBN到Ni NPs表面的硼化合物蒸气迁移实现了Ni NPs和pBN之间的强金属-支撑相互作用（SMSI）。
2. 金属NP与氧化物支持金属催化剂中的衍生材料层封装是SMSI效应的典型特征。
3. 高反应温度和生成的水产物对BOx或B(OH)x物种从pBN迁移到Ni NPs是必不可少的。
4. 本研究揭示了在SMSI状态下金属纳米颗粒上形成封装层的新机制。
