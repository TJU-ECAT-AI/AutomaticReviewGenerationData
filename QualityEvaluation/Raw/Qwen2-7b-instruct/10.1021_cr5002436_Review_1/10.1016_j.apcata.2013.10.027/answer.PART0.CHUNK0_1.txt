<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Effect of -Al2O3 hydrothermal treatment on the formation and properties of platinum sites in Pt/-Al2O3 catalysts. The hydrothermal treatment process involves the use of high-pressure water under elevated temperatures to modify the structure of the catalyst. The results showed that the hydrothermal treatment significantly improved the dispersion of platinum on the alumina support, enhancing its activity and selectivity. In comparison to other metal oxides such as CrOx, gallium, indium, vanadium, zinc, and molybdenum oxides, Pt/-Al2O3 catalysts exhibit superior performance in the nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins due to their unique properties. Recent developments in the field have focused on improving the stability and longevity of these catalysts while maintaining their efficiency. Industrial applications of Pt/-Al2O3 catalysts have expanded, particularly in the petrochemical industry for the production of high-value chemicals like aromatics.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
-Al2O3水热处理对铂位点在Pt/-Al2O3催化剂形成和性质的影响。水热处理过程涉及使用高压水在高温度下改变催化剂的结构。结果表明，水热处理显著提高了铂在氧化铝支持物上的分散性，增强了其活性和选择性。与CrOx、镓、铟、钒、锌和钼氧化物等其他金属氧化物相比，Pt/-Al2O3催化剂在轻烯烃非氧化脱氢中的性能更优，由于它们独特的性质。近年来，该领域的研究重点集中在提高这些催化剂的稳定性和寿命的同时保持其效率。工业应用中，Pt/-Al2O3催化剂的应用范围已经扩大，特别是在石油化学工业中生产如芳烃等高价值化学品。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
