The literature does not provide specific comparisons between noble metal-based catalysts, particularly Platinum, and metal oxide-based catalysts used in the nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins. It focuses on the effect of reduction methods on the activity of PtSn/SiO2 catalysts for the dehydrogenation of propane.
