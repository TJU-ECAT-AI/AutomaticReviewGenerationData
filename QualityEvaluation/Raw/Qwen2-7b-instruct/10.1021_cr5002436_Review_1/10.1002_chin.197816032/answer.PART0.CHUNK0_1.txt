<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines the changes in dispersion of supported noble metal catalysts (Pt, Ir, Rh) on A1203 under reducing (H2) and oxidizing (O2) atmospheres varying temperature (250-800°C). In O2, thermal stability decreases in the sequence Rh > Pt > Ir, whereas in H2, the sequence is Ir > Rh > Pt. An increase in dispersion is observed for Pt and Ir catalysts in O2. Relative stabilities are compared qualitatively based on a sintering mechanism.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
