<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study investigates changes in dispersion of supported metal catalysts (platinum, iridium, and rhodium) on A1203 carriers under reducing (H2) and oxidizing (O2) atmospheres depending on temperature (250-800°C). In oxygen, thermal stability decreases in the order Rh > Pt > Ir, whereas in hydrogen, the order is Ir > Rh > Pt. An increase in dispersion is observed for platinum and iridium catalysts in oxygen. Relative stabilities are compared qualitatively based on a sintering mechanism.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
