<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on the sintering of supported metal catalysts, specifically platinum (Pt), iridium (Ir), and rhodium (Rh) catalysts supported on aluminum oxide (Al2O3). The research examines how these catalysts change in dispersion under reducing (H7) and oxidizing (O2) atmospheres at temperatures ranging from 250 to 800 degrees Celsius. It also discusses the thermal stability of these catalysts, noting that in oxygen (O2), the stability decreases in the sequence Rh > Pt > Ir, whereas in hydrogen (H), the sequence is Ir > Rh > Pt. Additionally, the study observes an increase in dispersion for Pt- and Ir-catalysts in O2 and compares relative stabilities using qualitative predictions based on a sintering mechanism.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

研究专注于支持金属催化剂的烧结，特别是铂（Pt）、铱（Ir）和铑（Rh）催化剂在氧化铝（Al2O3）上的支持。该研究考察了这些催化剂在还原（H7）和氧化（O2）气氛下，温度从250到800摄氏度时的分散变化情况。它还讨论了这些催化剂的热稳定性，指出在氧气（O2）中，稳定性按顺序为Rh > Pt > Ir递减，在氢气（H）中，则为Ir > Rh > Pt。此外，研究观察到了铂（Pt）和铱（Ir）催化剂在O2中的分散增加，并基于烧结机制对相对稳定性进行了定性的预测比较。
