<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study investigates the changes in dispersion of supported noble metal catalysts (Pt, Ir, Rh) and metal oxide catalysts (A1203) under reducing (H2) and oxidizing (O2) atmospheres, varying temperature (250-800°C). In O2, thermal stability decreases in the order Rh > Pt > Ir, while in H2, the order is Ir > Rh > Pt. An increase in dispersion is observed for Pt and Ir catalysts in O2. Relative stabilities are compared qualitatively based on sintering mechanisms.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
