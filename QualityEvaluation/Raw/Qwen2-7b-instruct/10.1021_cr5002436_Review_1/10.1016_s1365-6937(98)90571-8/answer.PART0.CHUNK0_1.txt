The power plant is scheduled to begin construction later in the year and achieve commercial operation in the summer of 2000.

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Elwood Energy LLC, a joint venture between Peoples Energy Resources Corp and Dominion Energy Inc., chose General Electric as its contractor. According to the agreement, GE will supply two 150 MW Model PG723 1 FA combustion turbines for the planned merchant electricity facility. These additional turbines will raise the facility's total planned capacity from 300 MW to 600 MW upon installation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
