The scientific literature discusses the general trend of adsorbate-induced segregation of subsurface metal atoms in bimetallic surfaces. It examines how thermodynamic preferences and reaction conditions impact the stability, surface segregation, and structural configuration of Pt-based systems, specifically focusing on Pt-3d alloys. The study reveals that these factors significantly influence the catalytic properties and performance of such materials in various applications.
