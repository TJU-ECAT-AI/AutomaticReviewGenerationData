<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The research elucidates how thermodynamic preferences and reaction conditions impact the stability, surface segregation, and structural configuration of bimetallic catalysts, specifically focusing on Pt-based systems like Pt-3d alloys. The study employs computational and experimental methodologies to analyze the Ni/Pt(111) system, comparing the outcomes of non-equilibrium path (NEB) calculations and molecular dynamics (MD) simulations with experimental findings. This comparative analysis provides insights into the effects on the catalytic properties and performance of these materials across diverse applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这项研究揭示了热力学偏好和反应条件如何影响双金属催化剂的稳定性、表面偏析和结构配置，特别是针对像Pt-3d合金这样的Pt基系统。该研究采用计算和实验方法来分析Ni/Pt(111)系统，比较非平衡路径（NEB）计算和分子动力学（MD）模拟的结果与实验数据，并进行对比分析。这种对比分析为我们提供了这些材料在不同应用中的催化性质和性能变化的见解。
