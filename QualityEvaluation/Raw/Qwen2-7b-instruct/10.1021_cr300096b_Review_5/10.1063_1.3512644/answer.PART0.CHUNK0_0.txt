<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The thermodynamic preferences and reaction conditions significantly impact the stability, surface segregation, and structural configuration of bimetallic catalysts, particularly in Pt-based systems such as Pt-3d alloys. The study utilized numerical methods, including NEB calculations and MD simulations, to elucidate the formation pathways and dynamic behavior of these bimetallic structures. By comparing experimental data with computational results, researchers gained valuable insights into how these factors affect the catalytic properties and performance of the materials, guiding the optimization of their use in various applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
热力学偏好和反应条件对双金属催化剂，特别是Pt基体系如Pt-3d合金的稳定性和表面聚集状态以及结构配置有着显著影响。研究通过分子动力学（MD）模拟和实验方法来理解Ni和Pt在Ni/Pt(111)双金属催化剂中的混合情况。利用NEB计算法研究了不同结构形成过程中的活化能垒，通过MD模拟探索了在不同条件下Ni/Pt系统的动态行为及其演化。通过将实验结果与计算结果进行对比，研究人员获得了有关这些因素如何影响催化性质和性能的见解，从而指导材料在各种应用中的优化使用。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
