<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Thermodynamic preferences and reaction conditions play crucial roles in influencing the stability, surface segregation, and structural configuration of bimetallic catalysts, particularly in Pt-based systems such as Pt-3d alloys. The computational methods employed in this study, including Density Functional Theory (DFT) calculations and the Monte Carlo method, helped elucidate how these factors affect the catalysts' properties and performance in different applications.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
