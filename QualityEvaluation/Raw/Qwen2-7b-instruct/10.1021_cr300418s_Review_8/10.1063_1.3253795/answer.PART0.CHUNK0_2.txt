The study conducted B3LYP calculations on cerium oxides, focusing on the Ce2O3 cluster. It compared large-core and small-core basis sets for cerium within the Ce2O3 cluster. The electronic structure of bulk Ce2O3 and CeO2 was explored, alongside cell parameters and elastic constants of CeO2, as well as cell parameters of Ce2O3.
