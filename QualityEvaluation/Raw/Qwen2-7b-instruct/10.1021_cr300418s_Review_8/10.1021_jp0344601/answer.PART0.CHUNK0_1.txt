<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines the structural characteristics of ceria-titania and vanadia/ceria-titania mixed oxides, focusing on their Raman and XPS analyses. The mixed oxide is prepared via a coprecipitation method and then impregnated with a nominal 5 wt % vanadia. The samples are subjected to thermal treatments ranging from 773 to 1073 K, followed by characterization using X-ray diffraction (XRD), Raman spectroscopy (RS), and X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS) techniques. 

The results suggest that the CeO2TiO2 mixed oxide calcined at 773 K consists of poorly crystalline CeO2 and TiO2-anatase phases. Higher calcination temperatures lead to better crystallization of these oxides, with some incorporation of titanium into the ceria lattice. Impregnation of vanadia enhances the crystallization of CeO2 and TiO2, although no crystalline V2O5 is observed. Instead, the dispersed vanadia forms a stable CeVO4 compound at higher calcination temperatures, interacting preferentially with the ceria portion of the mixed oxide. XPS results confirm the presence of Ce(IV), Ti(IV), and V(V) in the mixed oxides, with the formation of Ce(III) noted at all temperatures. The study provides insights into the formation and evolution of CeVO4 in mixed oxide catalysts, influenced by factors such as temperature and dopant type.

该研究探讨了氧化铈-二氧化钛和氧化钒/氧化铈-二氧化钛混合氧化物的结构特性，重点是它们的拉曼光谱和XPS分析。混合氧化物通过共沉淀法制备，并随后用5 wt％的氧化钒浸渍。样品在773至1073 K的温度下进行热处理，随后使用X射线衍射（XRD）、拉曼光谱（RS）和X射线光电子能谱（XPS）技术进行表征。

结果显示，773 K下煅烧的CeO2TiO2混合氧化物主要由非晶态CeO2和TiO2-α相组成。随着煅烧温度的升高，这些氧化物的结晶性得到改善，并且钛离子部分地进入铈化物晶格中。氧化钒的添加促进了CeO2和TiO2的结晶化，但没有观察到结晶的V2O5。相反，在较高煅烧温度下形成的分散的氧化钒与混合氧化物中的铈部分形成稳定的CeVO4化合物。XPS结果确认了混合氧化物中存在的Ce(IV)、Ti(IV)和V(V)，并注意到所有温度下都形成了Ce(III)。研究提供了有关CeVO4在混合氧化物催化剂中形成和演变的信息，这些信息受到诸如温度和掺杂剂类型等因素的影响。
