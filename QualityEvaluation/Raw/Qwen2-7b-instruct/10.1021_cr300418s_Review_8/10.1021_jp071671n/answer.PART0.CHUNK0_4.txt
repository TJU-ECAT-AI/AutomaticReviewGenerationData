<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Dopants like Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, and Cd reduce the oxygen vacancy formation energy (OVFE) and prefer to substitute a Zn atom at the surface of ZnO. On the contrary, dopants including Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, and Pb increase OVFE and tend to substitute Zn atoms in the bulk, except for Ca, Sr, Ba, La, Ce, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Sn, and Pb which prefer to substitute a Zn atom at the surface.

2. By altering the OVFE, doping an oxide modifies its oxidative power, potentially enhancing its catalytic activity in oxidation and oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

3. The computational methodology employed in this study involves density functional theory (DFT) calculations using a plane wave basis set implemented in the Vienna ab initio simulation package (VASP).

4. The results indicate that alkali metal dopants and certain transition metals (Ag and Au) significantly weaken the bond of surface oxygen to the oxide, suggesting they could improve catalytic properties in oxidation reactions. However, other dopants either cause a minimal change in OVFE or strengthen the bond between surface oxygen and the oxide, thus not enhancing the Marsvan Krevelen mechanism for catalytic oxidation by oxides.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, 和Cd这些掺杂剂可以降低氧空位形成能（OVFE），并且倾向于在ZnO的表面替代一个Zn原子。相反，Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, 和Pb等掺杂剂会增加OVFE，并倾向于在内部晶格中替代Zn原子，除了Ca, Sr, Ba, La, Ce, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Sn, 和Pb，它们更倾向于在表面替代一个Zn原子。

2. 通过改变OVFE，掺杂氧化物可以有效调整其氧化性能，可能提高其在氧化和氧化脱氢反应中的催化活性。

3. 这一研究使用密度泛函理论（DFT）计算，结合平面波基集，在维也纳原初模拟包（VASP）中实现。

4. 结果表明，碱金属掺杂剂以及某些过渡金属（如Ag和Au）显著减弱表面氧与氧化物之间的键合，这可能改善催化性能以促进氧化反应。然而，其他掺杂剂要么仅造成轻微的OVFE变化，要么加强表面氧与氧化物之间的键合，从而不增强Marsvan Krevelen机制对于氧化物的催化氧化作用。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
