<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. This study evaluates the effectiveness of density functional theory methods in understanding the electronic structure and reactivity of cerium (Ce) and zirconium (Zr)-doped titanium dioxide (TiO2), which impacts the catalytic properties of various metal oxides. Insights are gained from both computational and experimental analyses regarding how these dopants affect performance in different catalytic applications.
2. The reliability of computational approaches in predicting the behavior of dopants in metal oxides is a significant challenge, particularly when considering the complex interactions between dopants and the host oxide's electronic structure, including Lewis acid-base interactions and the reducibility or irreducibility of the oxide.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 本研究评估了密度泛函理论方法在理解铈（Ce）和锆（Zr）掺杂的二氧化钛（TiO2）的电子结构和反应性方面的可靠性，进而影响不同金属氧化物的催化性质。通过计算和实验分析获得了这些掺杂剂如何影响各种催化应用性能的见解。
2. 在预测金属氧化物中掺杂行为的可靠性方面存在重大挑战，特别是考虑到掺杂剂与宿主氧化物电子结构的复杂相互作用，包括拉氏酸碱相互作用以及氧化物的可还原性或不可还原性。
