These studies have shed light on how dopants alter the electronic structure and reactivity of metal oxides, particularly in relation to factors such as Lewis acid-base interactions and the nature of the host oxide (reducible vs. irreducible). However, formulating general predictive rules for dopant behavior remains challenging due to the intricate nature of these factors and the variability in dopant effects across different host systems.

        Insights gained from these investigations have shown that dopants can significantly influence the energy of oxygen vacancy formation (OVFE), a key factor in determining the catalytic efficiency of metal oxides. For instance, SVDs and HVDs can either decrease or increase the OVFE depending on their interaction with the host oxide and their electronic properties. Among these, Au and Ag have been found to cause a substantial lowering of OVFE, comparable to that caused by alkali dopants, while most HVDs that increase OVFE prefer to substitute Zn atoms in the bulk of the host oxide, except for certain divalent and trivalent dopants that prefer substitution at the surface.

This is particularly true for dopants whose bonds with oxygen are weaker than those in the host oxide, making them more favorable for surface placement.

        这些见解表明，掺杂剂可以显著影响氧空位形成能（OVFE），这是决定金属氧化物催化效率的关键因素。例如，SVDs和HVDs可以通过降低或增加OVFE来影响，具体取决于它们与宿主氧化物的相互作用及其电子性质。其中，Au和Ag被发现能够显著降低OVFE，与碱金属掺杂剂相当；而大多数增加OVFE的HVDs倾向于在宿主氧化物的主体中替换Zn原子，除非某些二价和三价掺杂剂更偏好在表面取代。

        该模型识别了两个弱点：相关计算仅适用于分散催化剂的平滑面，以及难以将掺杂剂属性与OVFE效应相联系。尽管存在这些局限性，但研究已经强调了理解特定掺杂剂对氧化物电子结构和氧空位形成能动力学的影响对于优化催化应用的重要性。

        该模型表明，减少OVFE的掺杂剂倾向于与氧形成较少的键，导致在氧化物表面放置时损失较少的结合能量。这尤其适用于其与氧的键强度低于宿主氧化物的掺杂剂，使它们更适合位于表面。

        展望未来，有潜力探索新兴领域和创新方法来改进金属氧化物中掺杂剂的使用。未来的研究可能重点研究在不同条件下的更全面的理解，包括掺杂剂与宿主氧化物之间的作用以及反应动力学、稳定性和活性的影响。这可以为设计具有特定应用（如丙烷脱氢）的高效和多功能催化剂提供指导，通过利用对掺杂剂对金属氧化物催化性能影响的深入研究。
