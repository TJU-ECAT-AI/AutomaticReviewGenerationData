        The in-depth exploration of dopant behavior in metal oxides reveals a complex interplay between the properties of the dopants and the catalytic characteristics of the host materials. This review delves into the influence of both same-valence dopants (SVDs) and high-valence dopants (HVDs) on the catalytic properties of metal oxides, with a focus on their impact on propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Key insights are drawn from a series of studies, including:

        1. **Dopant Localization and Energy of Oxygen Vacancy Formation (OVFE):** Dopants that decrease OVFE tend to localize at the surface of zinc oxide (ZnO) rather than in the bulk, with gold (Au) and silver (Ag) standing out for their substantial reduction in OVFE, comparable to alkali metal dopants. Conversely, HVDs that increase OVFE prefer to substitute Zn atoms in the bulk of the host oxide, except for certain elements like calcium (Ca), strontium (Sr), barium (Ba), lanthanum (La), cerium (Ce), iron (Fe), cobalt (Co), tin (Sn), and lead (Pb), which favor substitution at the surface.

        2. **Lewis Acid-Base Interactions and Host Oxide Nature:** The nature of the host oxide, whether reducible or irreducible, plays a crucial role in determining dopant behavior. For instance, La2O3, being an irreducible oxide, does not induce La atoms to change their valence under chemical events at the surface, highlighting the importance of considering the host oxide's properties.

        3. **Predictive Rules and Challenges:** There is a notable challenge in formulating general predictive rules for dopant behavior due to the complexity and variability of factors such as ionization energy, electronegativity, chemical hardness, and atomic radii. Despite this, some rationalizations are possible, such as the observation that dopants at the surface form fewer bonds with oxygen than those in the bulk, potentially reducing the energy loss upon substitution.

        4. **Model Limitations:** The models used in these studies often contain weak points, such as the relevance of calculations to flat facets of highly dispersed catalysts, which may not fully capture the behavior on the complex morphologies of real catalyst surfaces.

        **Prospective Outlook:**
        Looking ahead, the field of metal oxide catalysis offers exciting avenues for innovation. The integration of advanced computational methods with experimental techniques promises deeper insights into dopant effects. Future research might focus on:

        - **Exploring Emerging Fields:** Investigating the role of dopants in novel catalyst designs, such as supported catalysts with tailored morphology, could unlock new functionalities.
        
        - **Innovative Methodologies:** Developing predictive models that incorporate machine learning algorithms to better understand and predict dopant behavior across a wide range of conditions.
        
        - **Broadening Horizons:** Expanding the scope to include dopants beyond traditional metal oxides, exploring the catalytic potential of non-metal dopants or hybrid materials.

        The future of this field is promising, with the potential to engineer catalysts with optimized properties for specific catalytic applications, thereby enhancing efficiency and selectivity in industrial processes.

        同价掺杂剂（SVD）和高价掺杂剂（HVD）对金属氧化物的催化性质产生了复杂的影响，本文深入探讨了这些影响，并以丙烷脱氢催化剂为例进行了分析。关键见解源自一系列研究，包括：

        1. **掺杂剂定位与氧空位形成能（OVFE）：**降低OVFE的掺杂剂倾向于在锌氧化物（ZnO）的表面而非内部进行定位，其中金（Au）和银（Ag）对OVFE的显著降低类似于碱金属掺杂剂。相比之下，增加OVFE的高价掺杂剂通常偏好在宿主氧化物的内部替换Zn原子，除非是某些元素如钙（Ca）、锶（Sr）、钡（Ba）、镧（La）、铈（Ce）、铁（Fe）、钴（Co）、锡（Sn）和铅（Pb），它们倾向于在表面替代一个Zn原子。

        2. **路易斯酸-碱相互作用和宿主氧化物性质：**宿主氧化物的性质，无论是可还原还是不可还原，对于确定掺杂剂行为至关重要。例如，La2O3作为不可还原氧化物，在表面化学事件下不会诱导La原子改变其价态，这突显了考虑宿主氧化物性质的重要性。

        3. **预测规则与挑战：**形成一般性预测规则对于掺杂剂行为存在挑战，这主要是由于离子化能、电负性、化学硬度和原子半径等因素的复杂性和变化性。尽管如此，可以进行一些合理的解释，比如表面的掺杂剂与氧气形成的键少于内部掺杂剂，可能减少了掺杂时的能量损失。

        4. **模型局限性：**所使用的模型往往包含弱点，例如对高度分散催化剂复杂形态的平滑面的计算结果的相关性，这可能无法完全捕捉实际催化剂表面的行为。

        **前景展望：**
        未来的领域充满了创新的机会。高级计算方法与实验技术的整合将为深入理解掺杂剂效应提供新的见解。未来研究可能集中在：

        - **探索新兴领域：**研究新型催化剂设计中的掺杂剂作用，通过定制形态可能揭示新的功能。

        - **创新方法：**开发结合机器学习算法的预测模型，以更好地预测不同条件下的掺杂剂行为。

        - **拓宽视野：**探索超越传统金属氧化物的掺杂剂，或研究混合材料的催化潜力。

        这个领域的未来充满希望，通过优化特定催化应用的催化剂性质，有望提高效率和选择性，从而推动工业过程的发展。
