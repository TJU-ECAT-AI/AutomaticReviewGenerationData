[1] "Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane to propylene in the presence of HCl catalyzed by CeO2 and NiO-modified CeO2 nanocrystals."
[2] "The reactivity of propane in these processes differs significantly from that of other alkanes such as methane and ethane."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study focuses on the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane to propylene using CeO2 and NiO-modified CeO2 nanocrystals as catalysts in the presence of HCl. This process contrasts significantly with the reactivity of propane compared to other alkanes like methane and ethane.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
该研究专注于在HCl的存在下，使用CeO2和NiO改性的CeO2纳米晶作为催化剂，对丙烷进行氧化脱氢以生成丙烯。这一过程与丙烷与其他烷烃（如甲烷和乙烷）的反应性存在显著差异。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
