2. Metal iodides were utilized to develop oxidative dehydrogenation processes to address limitations in direct hydrocarbon iodination.

3. The exact mechanisms of metal iodides in the dehydrogenation process are not fully understood.

4. Metal iodides are thought to function as mediators, absorbing and releasing I2 during the dehydrogenation.
