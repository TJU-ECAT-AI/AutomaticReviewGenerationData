<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The main causes of deactivation in metal oxide catalysts, particularly those based on Cr and VOx, include sintering, poisoning, and overoxidation.
2. Reoxidation with air is the most frequently employed method for regenerating vanadium-based catalysts.
3. The temperature range for reoxidation varies by catalyst type, typically between 400°C and 600°C.
4. Overoxidation during the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane with VOx catalysts can result in catalyst deactivation.
5. Adding CO2 has been suggested as a strategy to mitigate overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions.
6. Air and O2 regeneration are more efficient than CO2 regeneration, as they facilitate faster reoxidation.
7. Nevertheless, high temperatures used in these regeneration methods can adversely affect the catalytic performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 金属氧化物催化剂，特别是Cr和VOx基催化剂失效的主要原因包括烧结、中毒和过氧化。
2. 空气再生是再生钒基催化剂最常用的手段。
3. 再生温度范围因催化剂类型而异，通常在400°C至600°C之间。

6. 空气和O2再生比CO2再生更有效，因为它们能促进更快的再生。
7. 然而，在这些再生方法中使用的高温会负面影响催化性能。
