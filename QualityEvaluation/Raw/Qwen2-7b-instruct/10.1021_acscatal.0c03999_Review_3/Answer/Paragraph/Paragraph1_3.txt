        The primary causes of metal oxide catalyst deactivation in oxidative dehydrogenation processes, particularly for Cr-based and VOx catalysts, encompass overoxidation, sintering, and poisoning. Regeneration methods, including reoxidation with air, CO2, or O2, vary in their effectiveness and require different temperature thresholds. CO2 addition is recognized for mitigating overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions. Air reoxidation is effective but necessitates high temperatures, typically above 500°C, whereas O2 reoxidation is more efficient in restoring full activity but also demands high temperatures. CO2 reoxidation, while less effective in fully re-oxidizing to V2O5 state, assists in preserving VOx dispersion and enhancing propylene selectivity. CO2 addition in the feed gas helps reduce surface molecular oxygen species, neutralizing overoxidation sites. For Cr-based catalysts, coke deposition and reduction of active sites are major causes of deactivation. High temperatures, around 550-650°C, are commonly applied for regeneration methods, with air or CO2 being effective for most of the activity and selectivity recovery. Oxygen at 600°C for 5 minutes can fully restore the activity of CrOx/ZrO2, and this recovery is repeatable after 4 cycles. However, using CO2 as an oxidant only allows a complete recovery in the second cycle and fails to prevent further deactivation. Adding O2 in CO2ODHP helps remove coke but decreases C3H6 selectivity and C2C3 olefin selectivity. A complete recovery of active sites is challenging but can be achieved with well-dispersed Cr species. The formation of inactive α-Cr2O3 at higher Cr loadings limits activity and stability. To overcome this, high surface area CrSiO2 catalysts can be prepared through direct one-pot hydrothermal synthesis. Controlling the Cr loading to maintain the unique pore structure sacrifices activity, and proper synthetic methods provide solutions to this issue. For VOx-based catalysts, overoxidation impedes achieving high C3H6 selectivity. CO2 addition in the feed gas helps reduce surface molecular oxygen species, neutralizing overoxidation sites. Four types of VOx species are present during the reaction: monovanadate, oligomeric vanadate, polyvanadate, and V2O5 crystallite. Alternative redox cycles, such as the V5+ V4+ cycle on an oxidized diamond-supported VOx catalyst, have been identified. Mesoporous silica supports are desirable for VOx catalysts, offering short-range, ordered mesopores and high dispersion of VOx species. 
        The integrative understanding and prospective outlook suggest that future research should focus on exploring emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Potential pathways and pioneering research opportunities may include the development of new synthetic methods for catalyst preparation, the design of novel mesoporous supports, and the implementation of advanced regeneration strategies. Future endeavors might concentrate on optimizing catalyst performance under various operating conditions, mitigating overoxidation effects, and enhancing the stability of Cr-based and VOx catalysts. The utilization of CO2 as a regenerating agent could be further investigated for its potential in reducing overoxidation and improving catalyst efficiency. Additionally, the integration of CO2 capture and utilization technologies alongside oxidative dehydrogenation processes could lead to significant environmental benefits and economic advantages. Future work might also explore the synergistic effects of CO2 addition in conjunction with other process improvements to achieve higher propylene yields and selectivities while minimizing energy consumption and waste generation.

        在氧化脱氢过程中，金属氧化物催化剂的主要失效原因，特别是Cr基和VOx催化剂，包括过氧化、烧结和中毒。再生方法，如空气、CO2或O2的再氧化，其有效性和所需温度阈值各不相同。CO2添加被认识到可以减轻VOx催化反应中的过氧化作用。空气再氧化是有效的，但需要高于500°C的高温；而O2再氧化更高效地恢复了全活性，但也要求高温。虽然CO2再氧化仅能部分重新氧化至V2O5状态，但它有助于保持VOx分散并提高丙烯选择性。在CO2添加到丙烷气流中时，表面分子氧物种的减少有助于中和过氧化作用位点。对于Cr基催化剂，焦炭沉积和活性位点的减少是主要的失效原因。通常应用550-650°C的高温进行再生方法，其中空气或CO2对于大多数活动性和选择性的恢复都是有效的。氧气在600°C下5分钟可完全恢复CrOx/ZrO2的活性，并且这种恢复在4个周期后是可重复的。然而，使用CO2作为氧化剂仅允许在第二个周期内完成完全恢复，并且无法防止催化剂进一步失效。在CO2ODHP中添加O2有助于去除焦炭，但降低了C3H6选择性和C2C3烯烃选择性。实现活性位点的完整恢复具有挑战性，但可以通过分散良好的Cr物种来实现。在较高Cr负载下形成的无活性α-Cr2O3限制了活性并损害了稳定性。为克服这一问题，可以通过直接一锅水热合成制备高表面积CrSiO2催化剂。控制Cr负载以维持独特的孔结构牺牲了活性，而合适的合成方法提供了解决这个问题的方法。对于VOx基催化剂，过氧化阻碍了高C3H6选择性的实现。在CO2添加到丙烷气流中时，表面分子氧物种的减少有助于中和过氧化作用。反应期间存在的四种类型VOx物种包括单钒酸盐、寡聚钒酸盐、多聚钒酸盐和V2O5晶体。替代还原循环，如氧化金刚石支持的VOx催化剂上的V5+ V4+循环，已经被识别。短程有序介孔硅支撑对于VOx催化剂是理想的，提供短程有序介孔和VOx物种的高分散度。
