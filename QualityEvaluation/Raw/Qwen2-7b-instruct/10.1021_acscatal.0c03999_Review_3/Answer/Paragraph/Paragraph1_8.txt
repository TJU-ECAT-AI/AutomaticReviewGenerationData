        Metal oxide catalysts, particularly chromium (Cr)-based and vanadium oxide (VOx) catalysts, often suffer deactivation in oxidative dehydrogenation processes. Overoxidation is the primary cause of catalyst deactivation. Various regeneration methods, including reoxidation with air, carbon dioxide (CO2), or oxygen (O2), have been evaluated. Air reoxidation is effective but requires high temperatures, typically above 500°C. CO2 reoxidation is less effective, only oxidizing to V2O4, confirming the inability to fully re-oxidize to V2O5 state. Oxygen reoxidation efficiently restores full activity but also demands high temperatures. CO2 addition can mitigate overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions, helping to preserve VOx dispersion and improve propylene selectivity. 
        Coke deposition and reduction of active sites are major reasons for deactivation of Cr-based catalysts. Regeneration in air or CO2 at high temperatures (550-650°C) is widely applied, through which most of the activity and selectivity are usually recoverable. Oxygen at 600°C for 5 minutes can fully restore the activity of CrOx/ZrO2, with this recovery being repeatable after 4 cycles. Using CO2 as an oxidant only allows a complete recovery in the second cycle, failing to prevent further deactivation. Adding O2 in CO2ODHP helps remove coke but decreases C3H6 selectivity and C2C3 olefin selectivity. A complete recovery of active sites is challenging but can be achieved with well-dispersed Cr species. The formation of inactive α-Cr2O3 at higher Cr loadings limits activity and stability. To overcome this, CrSiO2 catalysts with high surface area can be prepared through direct one-pot hydrothermal synthesis. Controlling the Cr loading to maintain the unique pore structure sacrifices activity. Proper synthetic methods provide solutions to this issue.
        VOx is another redox-active metal oxide that has been extensively studied in the O2ODHP reaction. Overoxidation still impairs the effort to achieve high C3H6 selectivity. CO2 addition in the feed gas helps reduce surface molecular oxygen species, neutralizing overoxidation sites. Four types of VOx species are present during the reaction: monovanadate, oligomeric vanadate, polyvanadate, and V2O5 crystallite. Alternative redox cycles, such as the V5+ V4+ cycle on an oxidized diamond-supported VOx catalyst, have been identified. Mesoporous silica supports are desirable for VOx catalysts, offering short-range, ordered mesopores and high dispersion of VOx species.

Future research endeavors might focus on utilizing more sophisticated synthesis techniques to tailor the catalyst's structure and composition for enhanced stability and activity, exploring novel regeneration strategies beyond traditional reoxidation methods, and optimizing process conditions to minimize overoxidation. The integration of CO2 utilization could be further investigated for sustainable dehydrogenation processes, aiming to reduce environmental impact while enhancing product selectivity. Future work could also delve into the development of catalysts with improved resistance to coke formation, possibly through the design of new materials or modification of existing ones to better withstand the harsh conditions of oxidative dehydrogenation.

        特别是铬(Cr)-基和二氧化钒(VOx)的金属氧化物催化剂在氧化脱氢过程中经常发生失活。过氧化是催化剂失活的主要原因。各种再生方法，包括空气、二氧化碳(CO2)或氧气(O2)的再氧化，已经被评估。空气再氧化有效，但需要高温，通常在500°C以上。CO2再氧化效果较差，只能氧化到V2O4，确认无法完全重新氧化到V2O5状态。氧气再氧化效率高，可以恢复全部活性，但也需要高温。CO2添加可以减轻VOx催化反应中的过氧化作用，帮助保持VOx分散性和提高丙烯选择性。
        焦炭沉积和活性位点的减少是Cr基催化剂失活的主要原因。在550-650°C的空气中或CO2中进行再生，大多数活性和选择性通常可以恢复。在600°C下用氧气进行5分钟的再氧化可以完全恢复CrOx/ZrO2的活性，并且这种恢复在4个周期后可以重复。使用CO2作为氧化剂只允许在第二个循环中完全恢复，之后无法阻止催化剂进一步失活。在CO2ODHP中添加O2有助于去除焦炭，但会降低C3H6选择性和C2C3烯烃选择性。通过适当的方法制备分散良好的Cr物种可以获得活性位点的完整恢复。在更高Cr负载下形成的无活性α-Cr2O3限制了活性并损害了稳定性。为了解决这个问题，可以通过直接一锅水热合成制备具有高表面积的CrSiO2催化剂。控制Cr负载以维持独特的孔结构牺牲了活性。适当的合成方法提供了克服此问题的解决方案。
        另外，二氧化钒(VOx)是一种高度可逆的金属氧化物，在O2ODHP反应中被广泛研究。过氧化仍然阻碍了高C3H6选择性的实现。在气体流中添加CO2有助于减少表面分子氧物种，从而中和过氧化作用位点。在反应中存在四种类型的VOx物种：单钒酸盐、寡聚钒酸盐、多聚钒酸盐和V2O5结晶体。在氧化金刚石支持的VOx催化剂上的V5+ V4+循环已被识别为替代还原循环。介孔硅支持是VOx催化剂的理想选择，提供短程、有序的介孔结构和VOx物种的高分散度。
