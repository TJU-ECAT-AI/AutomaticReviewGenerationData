[1] "Oxygen Activation in Oxidative Coupling of Methane on Calcium Oxide"
</quotes>
<EnglishVersionAnswer>
This study focuses on the oxygen activation process during the oxidative coupling of methane on calcium oxide, exploring its role in the reaction mechanism and comparing it with oxygen scrambling activity. The authors use a pulsed isotope exchange technique to investigate oxygen scrambling on polycrystalline calcium oxide under relevant temperatures and pressures for the oxidative coupling of methane.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
