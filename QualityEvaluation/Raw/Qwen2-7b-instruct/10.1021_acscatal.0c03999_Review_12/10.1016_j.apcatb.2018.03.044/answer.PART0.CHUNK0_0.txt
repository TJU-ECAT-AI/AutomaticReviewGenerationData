<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study examines the molecular structure and surface chemistry of supported K2O/WO3/Al2O3 catalysts, comparing various catalysts including V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters. It explores the mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration in oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, focusing on the roles of traditional oxidants and CO2. The effectiveness of different catalysts in activating CO2 for oxidative dehydrogenation of propane is assessed through the proposed mechanisms and energy barriers for CO2 dissociation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
