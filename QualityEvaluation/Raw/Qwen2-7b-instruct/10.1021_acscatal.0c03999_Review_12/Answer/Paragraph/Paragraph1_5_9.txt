        Comparative analysis of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration mechanisms in the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, focusing on the utilization of CO2 as an alternative oxidant and the role of redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts. This review encompasses the evaluation of V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, with an emphasis on the energy barriers for CO2 dissociation and the effectiveness of these catalysts in promoting alkane dehydrogenation. The study reveals that redox-active catalysts, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, effectively utilize oxygen as a strong oxidant to facilitate the breaking of C-H bonds, whereas redox-inactive catalysts like CeO2 might require additional conditions or additives for the effective activation of CO2. Transition metal clusters also contribute to the process, albeit with differing mechanisms and effectiveness.

        CO2 plays a pivotal role in oxidative dehydrogenation as an alternative oxidant, offering advantages such as avoiding deep oxidation of alkanes, reducing coke formation, and facilitating propane conversion through its participation in the reverse water-gas shift reaction. The use of CO2 as a soft oxidant enables the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes with enhanced efficiency and selectivity.

        A series of iron-doped ceria (Fe-CeO2) catalysts demonstrated enhanced stability and promising catalytic performance for propane oxidative dehydrogenation using CO2 as an oxidant. The synthesis parameters, particularly calcination temperature, significantly impact the catalyst's activity and stability. Calcination at 600°C yields a higher specific surface area and forms a Fe-Ce solid solution, rather than two separated cerianite-hematite phases, which is crucial for the catalyst's performance in oxidative dehydrogenation.

        The review underscores the importance of catalyst design and synthesis conditions in optimizing the oxidative dehydrogenation process, highlighting the potential of metal oxide catalysts, especially those incorporating redox-active and redox-inactive elements, in achieving efficient alkane dehydrogenation with CO2 as an alternative oxidant.
        
        * * *
        An integrative understanding of the reviewed content suggests several future directions. With the growing emphasis on sustainable and environmentally friendly chemical processes, the integration of CO2 as an alternative oxidant in oxidative dehydrogenation presents a promising avenue for the development of green chemistry. The focus on metal oxide catalysts, particularly those capable of efficiently activating CO2, offers a robust foundation for advancing this technology. Future research might explore the optimization of catalyst structures and composition to enhance the selectivity and efficiency of CO2 activation. Additionally, the investigation of novel catalyst systems, such as metal clusters and layered double hydroxides, could further expand the scope of CO2 utilization in oxidative dehydrogenation. The development of scalable synthesis methods for these catalysts and the understanding of their underlying reaction mechanisms are critical for the practical implementation of CO2-based oxidative dehydrogenation processes. These advancements could lead to significant improvements in the economic viability and environmental sustainability of alkane dehydrogenation technologies.

        比较分析了在烷烃氧化脱氢反应中C-H键激活和催化剂再生机制，重点探讨了二氧化碳作为替代氧化剂的应用及其对活性氧和非活性氧金属氧化物催化剂的作用。本综述评估了V2O5、VOx/SiO2、CeO2和过渡金属簇在丙烷氧化脱氢中的作用，特别关注了二氧化碳脱附的能量壁垒以及这些催化剂促进烷烃脱氢的有效性。研究显示，活性氧催化剂，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，有效地利用氧气作为强氧化剂来促进C-H键的断裂，而非活性氧催化剂如CeO2可能需要额外的条件或添加剂以有效地激活二氧化碳。过渡金属簇也对过程有所贡献，尽管其机制和有效性各不相同。

        二氧化碳在氧化脱氢中的作用至关重要，作为替代氧化剂，它提供了避免深氧化烷烃、减少焦炭形成并通过参与逆水煤气变换反应促进丙烷转化的优点。使用二氧化碳作为软氧化剂能够实现烷烃的高效和选择性氧化脱氢。

        铁掺杂铈氧化物（Fe-CeO2）催化剂系列显示出增强的稳定性及使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂时的有前景的催化性能。合成参数，特别是煅烧温度，显著影响催化剂的活性和稳定性。在600°C下煅烧可获得更高的比表面积，并形成Fe-Ce固溶体，而不是分离的铈亚铁相，这对氧化脱氢过程中的催化剂性能至关重要。

        该综述强调了催化剂设计和合成条件的重要性，以优化氧化脱氢过程，突出了活性氧和非活性氧金属氧化物催化剂在使用二氧化碳作为替代氧化剂实现高效烷烃脱氢方面的潜力。

        对综述内容的综合理解表明，未来的几个方向值得关注。随着可持续和环境友好化学过程的日益重视，将二氧化碳作为氧化剂应用到氧化脱氢中提供了一个有前景的途径来推动绿色化学的发展。聚焦于金属氧化物催化剂，尤其是那些能够高效激活二氧化碳的催化剂，为这一技术的进步奠定了坚实的基础。未来研究可以探索催化剂结构和组成的优化，以提高二氧化碳激活的选择性和效率。此外，对新型催化剂系统的探索，如金属簇和双层羟基化物，可以进一步扩展二氧化碳在氧化脱氢中的应用范围。开发这些催化剂的大规模合成方法和理解其基本反应机制对于实现二氧化碳基氧化脱氢过程的实用实施至关重要。这些进展可能带来经济可行性和环境可持续性方面的重要改进，从而在烷烃脱氢技术中取得显著进步。
