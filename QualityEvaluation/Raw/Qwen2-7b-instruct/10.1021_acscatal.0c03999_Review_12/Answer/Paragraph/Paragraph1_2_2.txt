        A comprehensive review of the mechanisms of C-H bond activation, catalyst regeneration, and oxidant utilization in oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes has been synthesized from several studies focusing on both redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts, notably V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters. These catalysts play distinct roles in activating CO2 for the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, highlighting the importance of traditional oxidants alongside CO2 as a versatile alternative.

        Redox-active metal oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, are instrumental in the oxidative dehydrogenation process, effectively utilizing oxygen as a strong oxidant to facilitate the breaking of C-H bonds. Their redox cycle involves oxidation by the alkane followed by reduction by a traditional oxidant, leading to the regeneration of the active surface. On the other hand, redox-inactive catalysts like CeO2 require additional conditions or additives for effective CO2 activation, often relying on weaker oxidants or employing a combination of thermal energy and chemical activation to promote the reaction. Transition metal clusters also contribute to the process, although their specific mechanisms and effectiveness may vary.

        Traditional oxidants and CO2 are central to the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, significantly influencing the energy barriers for CO2 dissociation. The activation energy for CO2 activation varies across different catalysts, with redox-active catalysts typically requiring less activation energy compared to redox-inactive catalysts. This disparity highlights the efficiency of redox-active catalysts in CO2 activation.

        The use of CO2 as an alternative oxidant holds considerable potential. It can help mitigate deep oxidation of alkanes and alkynes, reduce coke formation, and facilitate propane conversion by participating in the reverse water-gas shift reaction (RWGS). This property makes CO2 a valuable alternative oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, offering a pathway to enhance sustainability and efficiency in chemical processes.

        Notably, iron-doped ceria (Fe-CeO2) has demonstrated enhanced stability and promising catalytic performance for the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane using CO2 as a soft oxidant. The synthesis parameters, including calcination temperature, play a crucial role in achieving optimal catalytic activity and stability. At 600°C, calcination leads to a higher specific surface area and the formation of a Fe-Ce solid solution, instead of two separated cerianite-hematite phases, further enhancing the catalyst's performance.

        Considering the advancements and findings detailed in the referenced studies, future research endeavors might focus on exploring emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Potential avenues for investigation could include the development of novel catalyst materials, optimization of reaction conditions, and the integration of CO2 utilization in a broader context of sustainable chemical processes. The integration of CO2 as an alternative oxidant presents a promising direction for enhancing the efficiency and environmental impact of oxidative dehydrogenation processes, paving the way for a more sustainable chemical industry.

        对于烷烃的氧化脱氢过程中的C-H键活化、催化剂再生及氧化剂利用机制的深入综述，基于多个研究，重点关注红氧化性金属氧化物催化剂如V2O5、VOx/SiO2、CeO2以及过渡金属簇等在丙烷氧化脱氢中的作用。这些催化剂在CO2激活方面表现出不同特性，对于丙烷的氧化脱氢具有重要意义。

        红氧化性金属氧化物催化剂，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，在氧化脱氢过程中发挥关键作用，有效利用氧气作为强氧化剂促进C-H键的断裂。其红氧化循环涉及由烷烃氧化然后通过传统氧化剂还原，以实现活性表面的再生。相比之下，红氧化性不活跃的催化剂如CeO2可能需要额外条件或添加剂来有效激活CO2，往往依赖较弱的氧化剂或结合热能和化学激活促进反应。过渡金属簇也对过程有所贡献，但其具体机制和有效性可能有所不同。

        传统的氧化剂和CO2是烷烃氧化脱氢的关键因素，显著影响着CO2的分解能垒。不同催化剂激活CO2的能量存在差异，红氧化性催化剂通常比红氧化性不活跃的催化剂要求更低的激活能量，这反映了红氧化性催化剂在CO2激活方面的高效性。

        将CO2用作替代氧化剂具有重大潜力。它有助于减轻烷烃和烯烃的深层氧化，减少焦炭形成，并通过参与逆水煤气变换反应（RWGS）促进丙烷转化，从而增强CO2作为替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢中的价值，为更可持续的化学过程提供了途径。

        特别值得注意的是，铁掺杂的铈氧化物（Fe-CeO2）在使用CO2作为软氧化剂进行丙烷氧化脱氢时表现出增强的稳定性和有前景的催化性能。合成参数，特别是煅烧温度，在实现最佳催化活性和稳定性方面起着关键作用。在600°C的煅烧下，可以获得更高的比表面积，并形成Fe-Ce固溶体，而不是分离的铈亚铁和氧化铁相，进一步增强了催化剂的性能。

        考虑到上述研究中概述的进展，未来的研究重点可能包括探索新兴领域和创新方法。潜在的研究方向可以包括新型催化剂材料的发展、反应条件的优化以及CO2利用在更广泛可持续化学过程中的整合。将CO2用作替代氧化剂代表了增强氧化脱氢过程效率和环境影响的一个有前景的方向，为更可持续的化学工业开辟了道路。
