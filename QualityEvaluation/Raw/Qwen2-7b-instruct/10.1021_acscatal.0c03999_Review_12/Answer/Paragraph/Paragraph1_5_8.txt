        <p>
            In the context of oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, redox-active metal oxide catalysts such as vanadia (V2O5) and vanadyl oxide (VOx/SiO2) effectively utilize oxygen as a strong oxidant to facilitate the breaking of C-H bonds. These catalysts involve a redox cycle where they are oxidized by the alkane and then reduced by a traditional oxidant, leading to the regeneration of the active surface. In contrast, redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts like ceria (CeO2) might require additional conditions or additives for effective CO2 activation. Transition metal clusters also play a role in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, although their specific mechanisms and effectiveness may vary.
        </p>
        <p>
            Traditional oxidants and CO2 are central to the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, influencing the energy barriers for CO2 dissociation. For instance, in the case of n-butane oxidative dehydrogenation over Ni-Mo/Al2O3 catalysts, the activation energy (EA) determined experimentally with carbon dioxide (CO2) and nitrous oxide (N2O) as oxidants was 161 and 112 kJ mol-1, respectively, which aligns well with the theoretical prediction of 144 kJ mol-1 for the abstraction of the first hydrogen atom from butane, indicating that this is the rate-determining step. Butadiene emerged as the most abundant product with productivity reaching up to 0.89 g butadiene per gram of catalyst per hour (gcat-1 h-1) when using N2O as an oxidant, 0.61 g butadiene per gram of catalyst per hour when using CO2 as an oxidant, and 0.21 g butadiene per gram of catalyst per hour when using oxygen (O2) as an oxidant.
        </p>
        <p>
            The use of CO2 as an alternative oxidant has the potential to mitigate deep oxidation of alkanes and alkynes, reduce coke formation, and favor propane conversion due to the participation of the produced hydrogen in the reverse water-gas shift reaction (RWGS). However, traditional chromium-based catalysts suffer from severe and rapid deactivation within a short time, regardless of sample preparation, reaction conditions, and reactor design. In this regard, a series of iron-doped ceria (Fe-CeO2) catalysts have shown enhanced stability and promising catalytic performance for the propane oxidative dehydrogenation using CO2 as a soft oxidant. The synthesis of these Fe-doped ceria catalysts involves a co-precipitation method followed by calcination at various temperatures, with calcination at 600°C being particularly advantageous due to its ability to yield a higher specific surface area and form a Fe-Ce solid solution, rather than two separated cerianite-hematite phases, thus ensuring a stable and active surface for the reaction.
        </p>
        <p>
            The integrative understanding and prospective outlook of the aforementioned studies suggests the potential for further exploration into the development of more efficient and stable catalysts for oxidative dehydrogenation processes. Future research might focus on optimizing the synthesis conditions for metal oxide catalysts, improving the selectivity and productivity of the oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, and investigating the synergistic effects of different catalysts and oxidants to enhance the overall efficiency of the process. Additionally, the role of metal clusters and the utilization of CO2 as an alternative oxidant could be explored to broaden the applicability of oxidative dehydrogenation techniques in the chemical industry, aiming to reduce environmental impact and improve energy efficiency.
        </p>

        <p>
            在烷烃氧化脱氢的背景下，活性金属氧化物催化剂如五氧化二钒(V2O5)和五氧化二钒/二氧化硅(VOx/SiO2)有效地利用氧气作为强氧化剂来促进C-H键的断裂。这些催化剂涉及一个红ox循环，在此期间它们由烷烃氧化并随后通过传统的氧化剂还原，导致活性表面的再生。相比之下，不活性的金属氧化物催化剂如铈(CeO2)可能需要额外的条件或添加剂以有效地激活CO2。过渡金属簇也参与丙烷的氧化脱氢，尽管其具体机制和有效性可能有所不同。
        </p>
        <p>
            传统氧化剂和CO2是烷烃氧化脱氢的关键因素，影响着CO2的分解能垒。例如，在Ni-Mo/Al2O3催化剂上进行丁烷氧化脱氢时，使用二氧化碳(CO2)和一氧化二氮(N2O)作为氧化剂确定的激活能量(EA)分别为161和112 kJ mol-1，这与理论预测的144 kJ mol-1一致，用于从丁烷中提取第一个氢原子，表明这是决定性步骤。丁二烯成为最丰富的产物，其生产力可达每克催化剂每小时0.89克丁二烯(gcat-1 h-1)，当使用N2O作为氧化剂时，为每克催化剂每小时0.61克丁二烯，当使用CO2作为氧化剂时为每克催化剂每小时0.21克丁二烯，当使用氧气(O2)作为氧化剂时为每克催化剂每小时0.21克丁二烯。
        </p>
        <p>
            使用CO2作为替代氧化剂有潜力避免深度氧化烷烃和烯烃，减少焦炭形成，并促进丙烷转化，得益于生成氢气参与逆水煤气变换反应(RWGS)。然而，传统铬基催化剂在短时间内遭受严重的快速失活，无论样品制备、反应条件还是反应器设计如何。与此相反，铁掺杂的铈(Fe-CeO2)催化剂系列显示了增强的稳定性和具有前景的催化性能，用于丙烷氧化脱氢，使用CO2作为软氧化剂。铁掺杂铈催化剂的合成采用共沉淀法，然后在不同温度下煅烧，其中在600°C的煅烧特别有利，因为它能够提供更高的比表面积并形成Fe-Ce固溶体，而不是两个分离的cerianite-hematite相，从而确保反应过程中的稳定和活性表面。
        </p>
        <p>
            上述研究的综合理解和前瞻展望表明，对于氧化脱氢过程的进一步探索具有潜在价值。未来的研究可能集中在优化金属氧化物催化剂的合成条件、提高氧化脱氢反应的选择性和生产力，以及调查不同催化剂和氧化剂的协同效应，以提高整个过程的效率。此外，金属簇的作用以及CO2作为替代氧化剂的利用可以被探索，以扩大氧化脱氢技术在化学工业中的应用范围，旨在减少环境影响并提高能源效率。
        </p>
