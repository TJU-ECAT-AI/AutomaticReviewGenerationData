<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Catalytic mixed conducting ceramic membrane reactors are primarily utilized in methane conversion reactions including partial oxidation of methane (POM), steam reforming of methane (SRM), dry reforming of methane (DRM), oxidative/nonoxidative coupling of methane (OCM/NCM), methane aromatization (MA), and bi-functional membrane reactors. The review discusses the performance, benefits, and difficulties associated with these reactors across different reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
