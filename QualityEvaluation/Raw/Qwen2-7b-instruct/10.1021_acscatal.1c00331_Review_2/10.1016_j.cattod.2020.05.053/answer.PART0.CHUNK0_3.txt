The research focuses on understanding how hydrogen cofeeding affects the stability of the catalyst, coke formation, and the reaction mechanisms during propane dehydrogenation. It examines the influence on energy barriers, coke accumulation patterns, acid site conversions, and graphitization. The study aims to determine the implications of these effects on the overall efficiency and performance of the process.
