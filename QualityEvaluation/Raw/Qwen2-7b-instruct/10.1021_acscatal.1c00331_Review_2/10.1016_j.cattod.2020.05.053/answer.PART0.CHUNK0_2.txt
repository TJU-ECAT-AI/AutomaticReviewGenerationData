<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. This study focuses on the catalytic stability of solgel derived SnOx/SiO2 catalysts and compares their side reactions with K-PtSn/Al2O3 towards propane dehydrogenation.
2. The research considers the influence of these reactions on energy barriers, patterns of coke accumulation, conversion of acid sites, and graphitization.
3. The implications of these effects on the overall efficiency and performance of the process are being explored.
