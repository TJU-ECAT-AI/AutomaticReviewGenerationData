<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Several C3 species were found to be carbon deposits on the surface of the PtSn alloy, and their production pathways were studied through DFT calculations. These deposits can be converted to propylene during the propane dehydrogenation (PDH) process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 几种C3物种被发现是PtSn合金表面的碳沉积物，并通过DFT计算研究了它们的产生途径。这些沉积物可以在PDH过程中转化为丙烯。

</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
2. The presence of hydrogen in the PDH process can lead to the hydrogenation of coke to propylene, which may contribute to improved yield and stability of the catalyst.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2. 在PDH过程中加入氢气可能导致焦炭的氢化转化为丙烯，这可能有助于提高催化剂的产率和稳定性。

</ChineseVersionAnswer>
