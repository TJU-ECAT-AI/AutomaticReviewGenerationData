It alters energy barriers, coke accumulation patterns, acid site conversions, and mitigates graphitization, impacting overall process efficiency and performance. This results in enhanced catalyst stability and improved selectivity towards propylene. The hydrogen effect is critical in optimizing the PDH process, as it can minimize coke buildup and maintain optimal reaction conditions.
