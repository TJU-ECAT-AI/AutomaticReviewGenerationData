<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Density Functional Theory (DFT) was employed to investigate the adsorption of propane and propene on Pt(111) and PtSn alloy surfaces.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study aimed to understand the effects of hydrogen cofeeding in the propane dehydrogenation (PDH) process on coke formation, catalyst stability, and reaction mechanisms.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>

</EnglishVersionAnswer>
