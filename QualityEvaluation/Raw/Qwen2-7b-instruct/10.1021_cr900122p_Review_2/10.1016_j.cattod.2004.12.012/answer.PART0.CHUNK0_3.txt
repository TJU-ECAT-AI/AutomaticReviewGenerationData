<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Research groups of Lunsford and Somorjai demonstrated that applying nitrous oxide over supported MoO3 and V2O5 oxides significantly enhanced the selectivity of methane oxidation to methanol and formaldehyde, especially at low conversion levels.
2. In 1983, Iwamoto et al. pioneered the use of nitrous oxide for benzene oxidation, achieving superior selectivity compared to oxygen oxidation.
3. The late 1970s to early 1980s saw significant interest in using nitrous oxide as a novel oxidant for selective methane transformation.
4. Nitrous oxide exhibits a unique specificity related to the formation of anion radical oxygen species Oa on a-sites, which cannot be formed from oxygen.
5. High selectivities, over 90%, were observed in the hydroxylation of chlorobenzene, fluorobenzene, phenol, and biphenyl.
6. With alkane and alkene substrates, almost no hydroxylated products were obtained, except for a few exceptions noted in the literature.
7. Nitrous oxide was effectively utilized in the epoxidation of propylene to propylene oxide.
8. Researchers aim to neutralize functional groups on zeolite surfaces while preserving the a-sites to prevent side transformations and coking during catalytic reactions at elevated temperatures.
9. The initial period of the hydroxylation of phenol by nitrous oxide showed that the hydroquinone (HQ) fraction comprised 85 mol%.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. Lunsford和Somorjai研究小组展示了，将N2O应用于MoO3和V2O5氧化物上显著提高了甲烷氧化为甲醇和甲醛的选择性，尤其是在低转化率时。
2. 1983年，Iwamoto等人首次使用N2O进行苯氧化，与氧气氧化相比，实现了更高的选择性。
3. 在20世纪70年代末至80年代初，人们开始关注使用N2O作为选择性甲烷转化的新氧化剂。
4. N2O的独特特性在于其在a位点上形成离子型氧物种Oa的能力，而这些能力不能从氧气形成。
5. 在氯苯、氟苯、酚和联苯的羟基化反应中观察到了高达90%以上的高选择性。
6. 对于烷烃和烯烃，几乎没有获得羟基化产物，除了文献中提到的一些例外情况。
7. N2O被有效用于丙烯氧化为过氧化物。
8. 研究人员试图在高温下催化反应时通过中和沸石表面的各种功能团，同时保持a位点的完整性，以防止副反应和结焦。
9. 研究表明，在用N2O进行苯酚羟基化反应的初始阶段，对羟基醌（HQ）的摩尔比例达到了85%。
