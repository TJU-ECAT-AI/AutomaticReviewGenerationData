The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts, including supported vanadium oxides and metal-exchanged zeolites, significantly influence the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations. Key factors contributing to their industrial significance include:

1. **Mechanism of Alkane Transformations**: The mechanisms, such as oxidative dehydrogenation and zeolite-catalyzed reactions, are influenced by the specific composition and structure of catalysts. Quantum chemical studies provide insights into the reaction pathways, identifying the roles of metal sites, acidity, and confinement effects.

2. **Dehydrogenation Role**: Metal-exchanged zeolites, especially gallium-modified ZSM-5 catalysts, exhibit promotional effects by introducing new reaction pathways and modifying the nature of active sites. The dehydrogenation role of extralattice Al species is suggested by theoretical studies.

3. **Activation Energies**: Calculated activation energies for reactions like H/D exchange, hydride transfer, cracking, and dehydrogenation are crucial for understanding reaction rates and efficiency. These energies compare with experimental findings, validating theoretical models.

4. **Zeolite Size Influence**: Theoretical studies, such as those using larger zeolite clusters, highlight the importance of local geometry and long-range electrostatic effects on the stability of alkoxide intermediates. This impacts the efficiency of alkane transformations.

The industrial significance of these catalysts stems from their ability to selectively transform alkanes into more valuable feedstocks like aromatics and olefins, offering economic benefits and environmental advantages in petrochemical industries.

金属基催化剂，包括支持的钨氧化物和金属交换沸石，其组成、结构和反应条件显著影响烷烃转化的机制和效率。对工业意义的关键贡献因素包括：

1. **反应机制**：特定组成和结构的催化剂决定了氧化脱氢和沸石催化反应的机制。量子化学研究提供了有关反应途径的见解，识别出金属位点、酸性和孔隙效应的作用。

2. **脱氢作用**：特别是镓改性的ZSM-5催化剂，通过引入新的反应路径和修改活性位点性质，显示出促进效果。理论研究表明，额外晶格外铝物种可能在脱氢过程中发挥作用。

3. **活化能**：计算的活化能，如H/D交换、氢转移、裂解和脱氢，对于理解反应速率和效率至关重要。这些能量与实验结果相比较，验证了理论模型的有效性。

4. **沸石尺寸的影响**：使用更大尺寸的沸石簇的理论研究表明，局部几何形状和长程电静电力效应对烷氧中间体的稳定性有重要影响，这影响了烷烃转化的效率。

这些催化剂在工业上的重要性在于它们能够选择性地将烷烃转化为更有价值的原料，如芳香族化合物和烯烃，从而带来经济利益和环境优势，在石油化工行业具有显著价值。
