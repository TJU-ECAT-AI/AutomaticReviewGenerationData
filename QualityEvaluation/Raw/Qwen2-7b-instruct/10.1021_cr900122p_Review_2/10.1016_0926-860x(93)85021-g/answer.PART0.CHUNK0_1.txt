<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The direct oxidation of benzene to phenol over Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites and their ferrisilicate analogs achieved the best results.
2. The unique effectiveness of these catalytic systems stems from the particular form of surface oxygen produced during dinitrogen monoxide decomposition.
3. The simplicity, high selectivity, and ease of regenerating the catalyst make this method an ecologically safe and potentially appealing approach for phenol manufacturers.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
