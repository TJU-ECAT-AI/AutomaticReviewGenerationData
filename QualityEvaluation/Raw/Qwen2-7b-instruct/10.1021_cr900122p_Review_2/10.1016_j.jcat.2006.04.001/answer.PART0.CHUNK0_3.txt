<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The erratum in the Journal of Catalysis corrects the presence of ethane instead of ethene in the following sections of the paper: the abstract on page 478, the second paragraph on the right column of page 482, and the conclusion on line 18 of page 485.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
