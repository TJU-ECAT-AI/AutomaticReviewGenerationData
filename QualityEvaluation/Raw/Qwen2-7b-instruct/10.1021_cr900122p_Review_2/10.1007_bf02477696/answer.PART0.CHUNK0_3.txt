<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The direct conversion of natural gas to more valuable compounds, such as liquid hydrocarbons, is a globally intriguing and challenging endeavor.
2. Methane conversion under non-oxidizing conditions is thermodynamically unfavorable, yet high selectivity can still be achieved.
3. The selective conversion of methane to benzene over Mo-containing zeolites at 973 K has been documented, along with a suggested carbenium ion mechanism for methane activation over a bifunctional Mo/HZSM-5 catalyst.
4. The present research focuses on the direct conversion of methane to aromatics under non-oxidizing conditions at lower temperatures of 773-873K, initiated by higher hydrocarbon additives in the feed and special coke on Mo-containing ZSM-5 catalysts.
5. The results of methane and natural gas reactions over Mo/HZSM-5 catalysts are detailed in Table 2.
6. X-ray diffraction (XRD), Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FT-IR), and surface area measurements indicate that Mo species are evenly distributed within the zeolite's channels. This similarity applies to MoW-ZSM-5 catalysts as well.
7. We proposed that natural gas containing higher hydrocarbons is converted to aromatics through a specific reaction mechanism.
8. In this process, C2 + carbenium ions are more easily formed compared to CH carbenium ions.
9. Partially coked Mo/HZSM-5 and Mo-W/HZSM-5 catalysts are produced after six hours of methane flow at 973 K.
10. Despite carbonization of the catalysts on the methane stream being feasible at 873 K, the coked catalysts at this temperature show minimal activity for pure methane conversion.
11. The phase transformation process during methane aromatization involves a change from gas phase methane to solid phase coke to gas phase aromatics.
12. High methane conversion is achieved at lower temperatures without encountering thermodynamic limitations.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 直接将天然气转化为更有价值的化合物，如液态烃，是一个全球性的引人入胜且具有挑战性的项目。
2. 在非氧化条件下进行甲烷转换是热力学上不利的，但仍然可以获得高选择性。
3. 在973 K的温度下，已经报告了通过钼含有的沸石催化剂选择性地将甲烷转化为苯，并提出了甲烷在双功能钼/沸石-5催化剂上的激活机制。
4. 当前的研究关注的是在较低温度（773-873K）下直接将甲烷转化为芳烃的非氧化条件下的转换，这由喂料中的较高碳氢化合物添加剂和钼含有的沸石-5催化剂上的特殊焦炭引发。
5. 表2详细列出了甲烷和天然气（含部分C2+添加剂）在钼/沸石-5催化剂上的反应结果。
6. X射线衍射（XRD）、傅里叶变换红外光谱（FT-IR）和表面积测量表明，钼物种均匀分布在沸石的通道中。这种相似性也适用于钼钨沸石-5催化剂。
7. 我们提出，含较高碳氢化合物的天然气可以转化为芳烃通过特定的反应机制。
8. 在这个过程中，C2 + 碳正离子比CH碳正离子更容易形成。
9. 在973 K的条件下，经过六小时的甲烷流，部分焦化的钼/沸石-5和钼钨/沸石-5催化剂被生产出来。
10. 尽管在873 K的条件下对催化剂进行碳化是可行的，但在此温度下的焦化催化剂对纯甲烷转换表现出几乎无活性。
11. 甲烷芳香化过程中的相转变过程涉及从气体相甲烷转变为固体相焦炭再到气体相芳烃的变化。
12. 在较低温度下，没有遇到热力学限制，实现了高的甲烷转化率。
