<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. There is considerable interest in activating methane and higher alkanes through specific catalysts. 
2. A silica-grafted zirconium hydride complex activates the C-H bonds of cyclooctane and methane, leading to the formation of corresponding cyclooctyl- and methyl-zirconium complexes.
3. Under moderate conditions with pressure of 5.3 x 10^4 Pa and temperature of 423 K, the supported zirconium hydride complex is also reactive towards methane.
