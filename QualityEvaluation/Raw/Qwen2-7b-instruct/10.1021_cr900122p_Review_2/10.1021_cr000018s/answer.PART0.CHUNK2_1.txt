<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts, including supported vanadium oxides and metal-exchanged zeolites, significantly impact the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations. These catalysts are essential in processes like oxidative dehydrogenation and zeolite-catalyzed reactions due to their unique properties and high activity. Their industrial significance is attributed to factors such as selectivity, stability, and the capability to function under mild conditions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
