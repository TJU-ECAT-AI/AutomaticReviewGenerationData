<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts, including supported vanadium oxides and metal-exchanged zeolites, play a pivotal role in the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations, particularly in processes such as oxidative dehydrogenation and zeolite-catalyzed reactions. These catalysts are industrially significant due to their ability to enhance yield, selectivity, and overall efficiency in petrochemical processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
