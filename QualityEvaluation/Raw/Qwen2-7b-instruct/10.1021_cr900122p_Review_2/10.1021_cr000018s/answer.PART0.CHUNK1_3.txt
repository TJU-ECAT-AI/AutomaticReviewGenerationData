<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The molecular sensitizers in photochemical CO2 reduction can be replaced with semiconductor electrodes or particles for light harvesting purposes.
2. The typical turnover frequencies for CO or HCOO- are between 1 and 10 h−1, and turnover numbers are usually 100 or less.
3. In the most efficient photochemical CO2 reduction systems, the total quantum yield for all reduced products can reach 40%.
4. The mechanism for cleaving CO bonds as CO2 is reduced is not well understood.
5. The formation of CC bonds between olefins + CO2 and alkynes + CO2 involves initial coordination of the olefin or alkyne followed by CO2 attack at an activated carbon atom.
6. The factors controlling regio- and stereoselectivity in metal-centered CO2 transformations are not clearly defined.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 在光化学CO2还原中，上述分子敏化剂可以被半导体电极或颗粒取代以实现光能捕获。
2. CO或HCOO-的典型周转频率在1到10 h−1之间，周转数通常为100以下。
3. 在最高效的光化学CO2还原系统中，所有还原产物的总量子产率可达40%。
4. CO键的断裂作为CO2的减少机制不清楚。
5. 芳烃+CO2和炔烃+CO2形成CC键的过程涉及初始烯烃或炔烃的配位，随后CO2攻击活化的碳原子。
6. 控制金属中心CO2转化的区域选择性和立体选择性的因素尚不明确。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
