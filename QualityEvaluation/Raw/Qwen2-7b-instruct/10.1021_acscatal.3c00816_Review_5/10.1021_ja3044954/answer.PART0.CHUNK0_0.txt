1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Main types of hierarchical zeolites include those with well-defined pore structures and high surface areas.
3. Synthesis methods involve calcination, ion exchange, and templating techniques.
4. These materials improve PDH reaction performance by increasing surface area and pore volume.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit higher catalytic activity and stability.
