<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials are: (a) mesoporous zeolites, (b) macroporous zeolites, and (c) hybrid zeolites with both micropore and mesopore/macropore structures.
3. Synthesis methods include: (a) template-directed synthesis, (b) sol-gel method, and (c) thermal treatment.
4. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites by offering larger surface area, increased pore volume, and enhanced selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 这些材料的主要类型包括：(a) 介孔沸石，(b) 大孔沸石，以及(c) 具有微孔和介孔/大孔结构的混合沸石。
3. 合成方法包括：(a) 模板导向合成，(b) 溶胶-凝胶法，以及(c) 热处理。
4. 这些材料与传统沸石相比，通过提供更大的表面积、增加的孔体积和增强的选择性，改善了PDH反应的性能。
