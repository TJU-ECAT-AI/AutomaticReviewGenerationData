<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to augment the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary categories of hierarchical zeolites consist of MCM-41, SBA-15, and SBA-11.
3. These materials' synthesis typically employs template-directed self-assembly techniques.
4. In comparison to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites elevate the PDH reaction performance by offering more accessible sites for reactants and products.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
