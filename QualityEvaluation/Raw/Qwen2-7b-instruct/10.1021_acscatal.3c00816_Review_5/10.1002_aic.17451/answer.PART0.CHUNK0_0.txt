<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to improve accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Key types are MFI, MEL, and BEA zeolites.
3. Synthesis techniques include template-directed synthesis and sol-gel methods.
4. Enhanced performance over traditional zeolites due to increased surface area and pore interconnectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔、介孔和大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 主要类型包括MFI、MEL和BEA沸石。
3. 合成方法涉及模板导向合成和溶胶-凝胶过程。
4. 相比传统沸石，性能提升得益于增加的表面积和孔隙连通性。
