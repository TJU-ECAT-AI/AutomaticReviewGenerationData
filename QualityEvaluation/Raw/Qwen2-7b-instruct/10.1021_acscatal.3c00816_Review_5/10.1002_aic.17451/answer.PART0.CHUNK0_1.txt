<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and either mesopores or macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions. 
2. Main types of these materials encompass mesoporous-silica-zeolite hybrids and macroporous-zeolite composites.
3. Synthesis methodologies typically involve the infiltration of metal salts into the microporous structure, followed by calcination.
4. These advanced zeolite materials enhance PDH performance by offering a greater surface area and improved molecular sieving capabilities.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit superior reactivity, selectivity, and stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 级联型沸石结合微孔和介孔或大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 这些材料的主要类型包括介孔二氧化硅沸石杂化体和大孔沸石复合物。
3. 合成方法通常涉及将金属盐渗透到微孔结构中，然后进行煅烧。
4. 这些先进的沸石材料通过提供更大的表面积和改进的分子筛分能力来提高PDH性能。
5. 相比传统沸石，级联型沸石表现出更高的活性、选择性和稳定性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
