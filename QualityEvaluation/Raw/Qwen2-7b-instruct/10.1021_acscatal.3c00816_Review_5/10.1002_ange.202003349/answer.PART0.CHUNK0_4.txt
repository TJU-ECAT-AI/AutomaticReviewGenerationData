<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions, making them ideal for anchoring small metal nanoparticles (MNPs).

2. Zeolites featuring ordered microporous structures and exceptional thermal stability are recognized as optimal supports for attaching small MNPs.

3. A straightforward ligand-protected direct hydrogen reduction technique was devised to encapsulate subnanometer bimetallic PtZn clusters inside silicalite-1 zeolites.

4. The developed PtZn@S-1 catalysts exhibit extraordinary efficiency in PDH conversions.

5. The inclusion of zinc species notably boosts the stability of the subnanometer Pt clusters.

6. The PtZn4@S-1-H catalyst displays a remarkable specific activity in propylene formation (65.5mol gPt1h1) at a weight hourly space velocity (WHSV) of 108h1, along with a high propylene selectivity (99.3%) at 550C.

7. The catalyst shows no discernible degradation even after 13000 hours of operation without supplementary hydrogen feeding (WHSV=3.6h1), achieving the highest stability among current Pt-based heterogeneous catalysts for PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性，被认为是锚定小金属纳米颗粒的理想支持。

2. 具有有序微孔结构和卓越热稳定性的沸石被公认为是固定小纳米颗粒的最佳支持材料。

3. 开发了一种简便的配体保护直接氢还原方法，用于将亚纳米双金属铂锌簇封装到硅酸铝沸石中。

4. 所开发的PtZn@S-1催化剂在PDH转化中表现出极高的效率。

5. 锌元素的引入显著提高了亚纳米铂簇的稳定性。

6. PtZn4@S-1-H催化剂在108小时的重量小时空间速度（WHSV）下，展现出65.5mol/g铂1小时的丙烯形成特定活性，并在550°C时具有99.3%的高丙烯选择性。

7. 催化剂在不补充氢气喂料的情况下（WHSV=3.6小时1），即使运行长达13000小时，也未显示出明显的降解，实现了当前Pt基异质催化剂中PDH反应的最高稳定性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
