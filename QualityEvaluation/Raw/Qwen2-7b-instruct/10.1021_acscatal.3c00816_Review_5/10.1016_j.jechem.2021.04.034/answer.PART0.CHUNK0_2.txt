2. Zeolite S-1 was synthesized using a conventional hydrothermal process.
3. Zeolites with varying zinc loadings, named Zn-x@S-1, were created through a similar hydrothermal technique, with zinc precursor added during the synthesis.
4. The reference Zn-4/S-1 sample was prepared using an incipient wetness impregnation method.
5. Zn-4@S-1 catalyst demonstrates excellent dehydrogenation ability, high propylene selectivity, and regeneration capacity in PDH reactions.
