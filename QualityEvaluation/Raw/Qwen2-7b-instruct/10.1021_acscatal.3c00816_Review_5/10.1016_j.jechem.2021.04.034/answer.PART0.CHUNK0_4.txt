<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The synthesized Zn@S-1 zeolites serve as effective PDH catalysts with zinc as the sole active component.
3. Optimized catalyst formulations and reaction conditions enable the Zn-4@S-1 catalyst to showcase desirable dehydrogenation activity, high propylene selectivity, and regeneration capacity in PDH reactions.
4. Achieving a propylene STY of 24 and a selectivity of 97% at 853 K indicates the significant industrial applicability of these catalysts for PDH processes.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
