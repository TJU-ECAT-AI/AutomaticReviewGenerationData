<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions.
2. The key types of hierarchical zeolites encompass dealuminated zeolite beta, featuring isolated platinum (Pt) atoms within SiOZnOH nests.
3. Dealuminated zeolite beta enhances the performance of PDH reactions by leveraging its distinctive pore architecture to facilitate better accessibility.
