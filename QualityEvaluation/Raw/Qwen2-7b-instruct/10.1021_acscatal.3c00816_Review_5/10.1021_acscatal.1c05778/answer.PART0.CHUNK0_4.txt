<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Main types of hierarchical zeolites encompass ZSM-5, Beta, MFI, and MEL.
3. Synthesis methods include hydrothermal treatment, sol-gel processes, and template-directed synthesis.
4. These materials elevate PDH reaction performance by augmenting surface area and porosity.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit superior stability and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
