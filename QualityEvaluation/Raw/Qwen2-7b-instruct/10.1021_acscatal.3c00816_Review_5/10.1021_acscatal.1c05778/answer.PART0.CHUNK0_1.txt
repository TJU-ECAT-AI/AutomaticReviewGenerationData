<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48. Synthesis methods typically involve template-directed assembly of organic molecules followed by removal of the template. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1. 分级沸石通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。分级沸石的主要类型包括SBA-15、MCM-41和MCM-48。合成方法通常涉及使用有机分子的模板导向组装，随后去除模板。这些材料与传统沸石相比，由于增加了表面积和可控的孔径分布，从而提高了PDH反应的性能。
