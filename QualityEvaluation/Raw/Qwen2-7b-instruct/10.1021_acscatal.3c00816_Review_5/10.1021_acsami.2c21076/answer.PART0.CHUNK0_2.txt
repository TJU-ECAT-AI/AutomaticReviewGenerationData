1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites consist of MFI, MCM-41, and SBA-15.
3. Synthesis techniques include template-directed assembly, sol-gel processes, and hydrothermal treatments.
4. These materials elevate PDH reaction performance by offering more active sites and improving diffusion.
5. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit superior selectivity and efficiency in PDH reactions.
