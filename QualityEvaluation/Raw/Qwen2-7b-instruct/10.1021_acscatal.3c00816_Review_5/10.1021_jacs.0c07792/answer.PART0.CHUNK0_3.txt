<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types include Y-type, Beta-type, and MFI-type zeolites. These materials are synthesized using template agents and high-temperature treatments. Hierarchical zeolites demonstrate improved stability and activity in PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
