<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types of these materials include MCM-41, SBA-15, and SBA-16. Synthesis methods usually involve template-directed assembly and post-synthesis modification. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by offering enhanced surface area and controlled pore structure. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites demonstrate higher selectivity and activity in PDH reactions due to their unique pore architecture.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
