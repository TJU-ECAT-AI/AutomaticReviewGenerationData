<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores or macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites consist of mesoporous-silica-supported zeolites, zeolite imidazolate frameworks (ZIFs), and metal-organic frameworks (MOFs).
3. Synthesis methods usually employ template agents, sol-gel processes, and hydrothermal treatments.
4. These materials enhance PDH reaction performance by offering a greater surface area and more accessible active sites, resulting in higher yields and selectivities.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石通过结合微孔和介孔或大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 主要类型的分级沸石包括二氧化硅支持的介孔沸石、杂环框架沸石（ZIFs）和金属有机框架（MOFs）。
3. 合成方法通常涉及模板剂、溶胶-凝胶过程和水热处理。
4. 这些材料通过提供更大的表面积和更易接近的活性位点，从而提高PDH反应的性能，导致更高的产率和选择性。
