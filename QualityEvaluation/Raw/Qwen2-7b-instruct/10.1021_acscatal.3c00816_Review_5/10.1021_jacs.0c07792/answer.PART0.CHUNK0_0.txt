<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites enhance accessibility for PDH reactions by combining micropores and mesopores/macropores.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites are MCM-41, SBA-15, and SBA-16.
3. These materials are synthesized through template-directed assembly or sol-gel processes.
4. Hierarchical zeolites outperform traditional zeolites in PDH reactions due to their enhanced pore structure and increased surface area.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
