<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites are MFI, MEL, and BEA structures.
3. Synthesis methods of hierarchical zeolites usually involve hydrothermal treatment and template-directed synthesis.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites improve the performance of PDH reactions by offering a larger surface area and better thermal stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
