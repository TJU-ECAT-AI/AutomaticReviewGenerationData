<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions by combining micropores and mesopores/macropores. The main types of these materials include mesoporous zeolites, macroporous zeolites, and hybrid zeolites. Synthesis methods for these materials involve template-directed assembly, sol-gel processing, and ion-exchange techniques. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit higher activity and selectivity due to their enhanced porosity and pore connectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

层状沸石通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。这些材料的主要类型包括介孔沸石、大孔沸石和混合沸石。这些材料的合成方法包括模板导向组装、溶胶-凝胶处理和离子交换技术。与传统沸石相比，层状沸石由于其增强的孔隙度和孔隙连通性，表现出更高的活性和选择性。
