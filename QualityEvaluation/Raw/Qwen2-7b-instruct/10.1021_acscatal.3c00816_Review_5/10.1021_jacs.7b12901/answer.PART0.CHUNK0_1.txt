<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include mesoporous-microporous hybrids, macroporous-microporous hybrids, and mixed-macroporous-mesoporous-microporous structures.
3. These materials are synthesized through various methods such as the incorporation of organic templates during the synthesis process, the use of microemulsion techniques, and the utilization of metal organic frameworks (MOFs) as precursors.
4. Hierarchical zeolites improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution, which facilitates better adsorption and desorption kinetics.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 层级沸石通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 层级沸石的主要类型包括介孔-微孔混合物、大孔-微孔混合物和混合大孔-介孔-微孔结构。
3. 这些材料通过在合成过程中使用有机模板、使用微乳液技术以及利用金属有机框架（MOFs）作为前体等方法进行合成。
4. 与传统沸石相比，层级沸石提高了PDH反应的性能，由于其增强的表面积和可控的孔径分布，这有利于更好的吸附和脱附动力学。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
