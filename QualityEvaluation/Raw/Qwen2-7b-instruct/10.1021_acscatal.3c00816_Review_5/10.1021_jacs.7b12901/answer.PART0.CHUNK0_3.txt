<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The key types of hierarchical zeolites are H-ZSM-5, H-USY, and H-MOR.
3. These materials are produced via hydrothermal treatments, template-directed synthesis, and sol-gel processes.
4. Hierarchical zeolites outperform traditional zeolites in PDH reactions due to their improved pore structure and accessibility.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
