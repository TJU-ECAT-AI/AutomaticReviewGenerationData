<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. 
2. Metal nanoparticles (NPs) with sizes in the range of 1 to 2nm were encapsulated into various zeolites, including Sodalite (SOD), Analcime (ANA), and Gismondine (GIS).
3. A novel method was introduced to create highly stable subnanometric metal catalysts suitable for high-temperature catalytic reactions in pure siliceous zeolites.
4. The atomic structures of Pt@MCM-22 were analyzed using transmission electron microscopy (TEM) and high-angle annular dark-field detector high-resolution scanning TEM imaging (HAADF-HRSTEM imaging).
5. The particle size distribution of subnanometric Pt species was determined by counting over 300 particles.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 多级孔结构的沸石通过集成微孔和介孔/大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 尺寸在1到2nm范围内的金属纳米颗粒被封装进多种沸石，包括Sodalite（SOD）、Analcime（ANA）和Gismondine（GIS）。
3. 引入了一种新方法，用于制备高度稳定的亚纳米级金属催化剂，适用于纯硅基沸石中的高温催化反应。
4. Pt@MCM-22的原子结构通过透射电子显微镜（TEM）和高角环形暗场检测器高分辨率扫描透射电子显微镜成像（HAADF-HRSTEM成像）进行了分析。
5. 通过对超过300个粒子的计数确定了亚纳米级Pt物种的粒度分布。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
