<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility of substrates for PDH reactions. Main types of hierarchical zeolites include MFI, MEL, and BEA structures, synthesized through methods like template-directed synthesis, sol-gel processing, and microwave-assisted synthesis. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites feature enhanced surface area, pore size diversity, and improved selectivity, leading to superior performance in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
