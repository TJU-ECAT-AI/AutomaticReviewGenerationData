<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions. 
2. The principal categories of hierarchical zeolites consist of materials featuring a mix of micropores and mesopores/macropores.
3. Synthesizing these materials employs methods such as sol-gel processes, hydrothermal synthesis, and template-directed synthesis.
4. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites enhance PDH reaction performance by offering a greater surface area and a more accessible pore structure.
5. This results in higher conversion rates and selectivities for propene production.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
