<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. These materials outperform traditional zeolites in PDH reactions' performance.
2. Sn-incorporated SBA-15 mesoporous materials were synthesized through one-pot hydrothermal synthesis, offering high specific surface areas.
3. The catalysts prepared with Sn-incorporated SBA-15 were compared to those made using a conventional impregnation method.
4. Excessive Sn negatively impacts interactions among Pt species, Sn species, and the support, reducing catalytic activity.
5. The Pt catalysts supported on Sn-incorporated SBA-15 demonstrated superior catalytic activities and stabilities in propane dehydrogenation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，增强PDH反应的可接近性。与传统沸石相比，这些材料在PDH反应性能方面表现出更优。
2. 通过一锅法合成制备了含有Sn的SBA-15介孔材料，具有高比表面积。
3. 以Sn-掺杂SBA-15为载体的催化剂与采用传统浸渍法制备的催化剂进行了性能比较。
4. 过量的Sn削弱了Pt物种、Sn物种和支持物之间的相互作用，导致催化活性下降。
5. Pt催化剂支持在Sn-掺杂SBA-15上，在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出卓越的催化活性和稳定性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
