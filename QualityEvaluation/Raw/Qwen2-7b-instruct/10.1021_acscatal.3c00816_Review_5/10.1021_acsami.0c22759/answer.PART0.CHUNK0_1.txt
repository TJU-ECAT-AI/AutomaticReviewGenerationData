<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions by combining micropores and mesopores/macropores.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include Type A, Type B, Type C, etc.
3. These materials are synthesized through various methods such as template-assisted synthesis, sol-gel method, microwave-assisted synthesis, etc.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit enhanced stability, activity, selectivity, and efficiency in PDH reactions.
