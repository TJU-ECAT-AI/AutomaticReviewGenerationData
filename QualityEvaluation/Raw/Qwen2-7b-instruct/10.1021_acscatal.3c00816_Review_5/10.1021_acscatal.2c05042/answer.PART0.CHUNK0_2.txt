<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include SBA-15, MCM-41, and mesoporous silica.
3. Synthesis methods for these materials usually involve template-directed self-assembly processes.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites demonstrate better performance in PDH reactions due to their increased surface area and pore structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
