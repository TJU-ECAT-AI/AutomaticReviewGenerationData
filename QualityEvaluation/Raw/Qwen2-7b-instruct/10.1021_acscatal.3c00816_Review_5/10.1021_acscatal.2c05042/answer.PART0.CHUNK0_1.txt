<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical-like heteroatomic Fe-Silicalite-1 materials are used for anchoring isolated Pt species. The integration of micropores and mesopores/macropores in hierarchical zeolites enhances accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Main types of these materials include hierarchical zeolites, mesoporous silicas, and metal-organic frameworks (MOFs).
3. Synthesis methods commonly involve template-directed assembly, sol-gel processes, and chemical vapor deposition.
4. These materials enhance PDH reactions' performance by offering more active sites, improved thermal stability, and increased catalyst loading efficiency.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 类同层次的Fe-Silicalite-1材料用于锚定孤立的Pt物种。层次状沸石中微孔与介孔/大孔的结合增强了PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 这些材料的主要类型包括层次状沸石、介孔二氧化硅和金属有机框架（MOFs）。
3. 合成方法通常涉及模板导向组装、溶胶-凝胶过程和化学气相沉积。
4. 这些材料通过提供更多的活性位点、更好的热稳定性以及增加催化剂负载效率，提高了PDH反应的性能。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
