<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical-like heteroatomic Fe-Silicalite-1 materials combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites are silicalite-1, chabazite, and mordenite.
3. Synthesis methods of these materials typically involve hydrothermal treatment and template-directed assembly.
4. Hierarchical zeolites improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
