<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions.
2. Main types of hierarchical zeolites encompass metal-organic frameworks (MOFs), covalent organic frameworks (COFs), and metal-organic porous networks (MOPNs).
3. Synthesis methods of hierarchical zeolites usually involve sol-gel processes, template-directed self-assembly, and chemical vapor deposition.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, these materials enhance PDH reaction performance thanks to their elevated surface area, superior thermal stability, and better selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔来增强丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可接近性。
2. 分级沸石的主要类型包括金属有机框架（MOFs）、共价有机框架（COFs）和金属有机多孔网络（MOPNs）。
3. 分级沸石的合成方法通常包括溶胶-凝胶过程、模板导向自组装和化学气相沉积。
4. 与传统沸石相比，这些材料通过提高表面积、改善热稳定性以及提升选择性，增强了PDH反应的性能。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
