<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions.
2. The principal types of hierarchical zeolites are MFI, MEL, and BEA.
3. Synthesis methodologies usually consist of hydrothermal treatment and template-assisted processes.
4. Compared to conventional zeolites, these materials enhance PDH reaction performance due to their increased surface area and pore structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

3. 这些材料的合成方法通常涉及水热处理和模板辅助过程。
