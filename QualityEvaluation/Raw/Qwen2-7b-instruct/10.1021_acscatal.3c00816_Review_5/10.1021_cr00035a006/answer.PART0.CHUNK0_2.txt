<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions. 
2. Main types of hierarchical zeolites encompass nanostructured zeolites, templated zeolites, and zeolite composites.
3. Synthesis techniques include sol-gel processes, ionothermal synthesis, and microwave-assisted synthesis.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, these materials enhance PDH reaction performance through increased surface areas, improved selectivity, and minimized coke formation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
