<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Key types are nanostructured, porous, and hierarchical zeolites.
3. Synthesis techniques include template-directed assembly, sol-gel processes, and hydrothermal synthesis.
4. These materials enhance PDH reaction performance through increased surface area and pore volume.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
