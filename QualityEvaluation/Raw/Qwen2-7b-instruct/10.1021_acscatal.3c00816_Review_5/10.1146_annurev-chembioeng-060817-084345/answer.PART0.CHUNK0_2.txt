<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The principal types of hierarchical zeolites encompass mesoporous molecular sieves, hierarchical mesoporous materials, and templated mesoporous materials.
3. Synthesis methodologies for these materials usually entail the employment of template molecules to establish ordered pore structures during the preparation process.
4. These materials enhance the performance of PDH reactions relative to traditional zeolites due to their elevated surface area and superior accessibility for reactants and products.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔来提高PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 主要的分级沸石类型包括微孔分子筛、分级介孔材料和模板介孔材料。
3. 这些材料的合成方法通常涉及使用模板分子在制备过程中建立有序孔结构。
4. 相对于传统沸石，这些材料通过其更高的表面积和对反应物和产物的更优可接近性，提升PDH反应性能。
