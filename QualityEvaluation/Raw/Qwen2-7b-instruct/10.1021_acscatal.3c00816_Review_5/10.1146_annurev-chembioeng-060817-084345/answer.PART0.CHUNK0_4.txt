<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Key types of hierarchical zeolites encompass SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48.
3. Synthesis methodologies involve the self-assembly of organic molecules under a template, followed by calcination to eliminate the template.
4. These materials enhance PDH reactions by offering a greater accessible surface area and optimized pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
