<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions. Main types include ZSM-5, MFI, MEL, and BEA. Synthesis methods encompass template-directed synthesis, sol-gel process, and ion-exchange technique. These materials enhance performance through better diffusion pathways, increased surface area, and improved stability. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit higher activity and selectivity in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
