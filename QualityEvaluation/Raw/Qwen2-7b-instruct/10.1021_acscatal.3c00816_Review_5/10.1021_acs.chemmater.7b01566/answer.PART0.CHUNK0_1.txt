<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The hierarchical Ga-MFI catalysts for propane dehydrogenation combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials are hierarchical Ga-MFI catalysts, synthesized through template-directed methods followed by calcination.
3. Compared to traditional zeolites, these materials show improved performance due to increased surface area and pore size, resulting in enhanced catalytic activity and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
