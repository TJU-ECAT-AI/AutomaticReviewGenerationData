<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical Ga-MFI catalysts for propane dehydrogenation have been developed, which combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials include Ga-MFI zeolites, Ga-MCM-41, and Ga-SBA-15.
3. Synthesis methods typically involve sol-gel processes, template-directed assembly, and ion-exchange techniques.
4. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced surface area, controlled pore size distribution, and improved thermal stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 已开发出用于丙烷脱氢的分级Ga-MFI催化剂，这些催化剂通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔来增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 这些材料的主要类型包括Ga-MFI沸石、Ga-MCM-41和Ga-SBA-15。
3. 合成方法通常涉及溶胶-凝胶过程、模板导向组装和离子交换技术。
4. 与传统沸石相比，这些材料在PDH反应中的性能得到改善，由于它们增强了表面积、孔径分布控制以及提高了热稳定性。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
