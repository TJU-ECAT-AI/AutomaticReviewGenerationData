<quotes>
[1] "Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions."
[2] "These materials are synthesized through a combination of bottom-up and top-down methods."
[3] "Hierarchical zeolites improve PDH reaction performance by providing a larger surface area and better pore connectivity."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores, mesopores, and macropores to optimize accessibility for PDH reactions. These materials are fabricated using a blend of bottom-up and top-down methodologies. Their implementation enhances PDH reaction efficiency by augmenting surface area and improving pore interconnectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔、介孔和大孔，以提高PDH反应的可接近性。这些材料通过底向上和顶向下方法的组合进行合成。它们的使用通过增加表面积并改善孔隙连通性来优化PDH反应性能。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<quotes>
[1] "Some examples of hierarchical zeolites include SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48."
[2] "SBA-15 is synthesized by a sol-gel process, while MCM-41 and MCM-48 are typically created through a template-directed synthesis."
[3] "These hierarchical zeolites offer superior catalytic performance in PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their unique pore architecture."
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
2. Examples of hierarchical zeolites encompass SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48. SBA-15 is synthesized via a sol-gel process, whereas MCM-41 and MCM-48 are generally crafted through a template-directed synthesis. These hierarchical zeolites demonstrate superior catalytic capabilities in PDH reactions relative to conventional zeolites, thanks to their distinctive pore structure.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
2. 包括SBA-15、MCM-41和MCM-48在内的例子是分层沸石。SBA-15通过溶胶-凝胶过程合成，而MCM-41和MCM-48通常通过模板导向合成制造。这些分层沸石在PDH反应中展现出与传统沸石相比更优越的催化性能，得益于其独特的孔结构。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
