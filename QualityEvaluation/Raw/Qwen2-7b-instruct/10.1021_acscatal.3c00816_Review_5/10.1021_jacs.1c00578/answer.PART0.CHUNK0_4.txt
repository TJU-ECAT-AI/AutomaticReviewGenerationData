<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary categories of these materials consist of mesoporous, macroporous, and hierarchical zeolites.
3. Synthesis techniques include hydrothermal treatment, sol-gel process, and template-directed synthesis.
4. Hierarchical zeolites enhance PDH reactions by offering greater surface area and improved accessibility for reactants.
5. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites demonstrate superior activity and durability in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
