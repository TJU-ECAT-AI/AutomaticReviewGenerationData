<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to significantly enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include self-pillared zeolite nanosheets.
3. Synthesis methods for these materials often involve layer-by-layer assembly processes.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites demonstrate improved performance in PDH reactions due to their enhanced pore architecture.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
