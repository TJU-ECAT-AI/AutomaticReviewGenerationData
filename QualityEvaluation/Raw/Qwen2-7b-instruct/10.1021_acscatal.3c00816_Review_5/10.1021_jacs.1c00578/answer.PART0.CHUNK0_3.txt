<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The key types of hierarchical zeolites encompass MFI, MCM-41, and SBA-15.
3. Synthesis methodologies for these materials involve template-directed assembly, sol-gel processing, and hydrothermal synthesis.
4. These materials enhance the performance of PDH reactions by offering more accessible sites for reactants.
5. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites feature superior surface area and pore structure, which results in increased catalytic activity and selectivity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
