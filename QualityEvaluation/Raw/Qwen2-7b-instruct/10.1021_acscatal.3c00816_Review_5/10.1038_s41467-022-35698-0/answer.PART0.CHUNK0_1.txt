<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites consist of SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48.
3. These materials are fabricated using template-directed methods, like the Stöber method for SBA-15.
4. They surpass traditional zeolites in PDH reaction performance due to enhanced surface areas and more accessible pore structures.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
