<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. These materials are generally fabricated through processes like hydrothermal synthesis, sol-gel methodologies, and templating approaches.
3. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites typically show enhanced catalytic performance, selectivity, and durability in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
