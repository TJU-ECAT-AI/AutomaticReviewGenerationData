<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Sn was doped into SBA-15 and applied as a support (SSF) to disperse the active phases Pt.
2. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
3. The main types of hierarchical zeolites are SBA-15, MCM-41, and SBA-MCM hybrids.
4. Synthesis methods of these materials include hydrothermal synthesis and template-directed assembly.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites improve PDH reaction performance by offering enhanced surface areas and controlled pore sizes, which facilitate better reactant diffusion and product separation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1.Sn被掺入到SBA-15中，并作为载体（SSF）分散活性组分Pt。
2.分级沸石将微孔和介孔/大孔结合在一起，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
3.主要的分级沸石类型包括SBA-15、MCM-41和SBA-MCM混合物。
4.这些材料的合成方法包括水热合成和模板导向组装。
5.与传统沸石相比，分级沸石通过提供增强的比表面积和可控的孔径尺寸，改善了PDH反应性能，从而促进了更好的反应物扩散和产物分离。
