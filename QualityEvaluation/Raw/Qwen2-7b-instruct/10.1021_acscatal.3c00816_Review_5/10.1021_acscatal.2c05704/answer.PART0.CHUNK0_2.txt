<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. Main types include MCM-41, SBA-15, and H-ZSM-5. Synthesis methods involve sol-gel processes, template-directed assembly, and hydrothermal treatments. These materials improve PDH reaction performance by providing better control over pore size distribution and enhanced surface area.
