<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types include SBA-15, MCM-41, and MCM-48, synthesized through sol-gel, template-directed assembly, and post-synthetic modification methods, respectively. These materials improve PDH reaction performance compared to traditional zeolites because of their enhanced surface area, controlled pore size distribution, and improved mass transfer properties.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
