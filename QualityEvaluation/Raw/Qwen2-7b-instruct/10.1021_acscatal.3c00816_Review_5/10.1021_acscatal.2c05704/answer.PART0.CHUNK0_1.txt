<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores or macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Key types of hierarchical zeolites are MFI, MCM-41, and SBA-15.
3. Synthesis techniques encompass template-directed assembly, sol-gel methodologies, and microwave-assisted synthesis.
4. These materials enhance PDH reaction performance through increased surface area and improved pore architecture.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
