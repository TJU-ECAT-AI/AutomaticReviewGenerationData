Main types include MFI, MEL, and BEA, synthesized via hydrothermal processes, sol-gel methods, and template-directed synthesis. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution, offering superior activity and selectivity in PDH processes. Key to their effectiveness, hierarchical zeolites provide improved pore structures that facilitate better diffusion pathways for reactants and products, leading to enhanced catalytic performance.
