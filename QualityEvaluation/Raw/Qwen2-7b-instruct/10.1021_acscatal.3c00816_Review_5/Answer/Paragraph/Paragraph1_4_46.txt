Key types of hierarchical zeolites include MFI, MEL, BEA, ZSM-5, MCM-41, SBA-15, SBA-16, and others. These materials offer superior performance in PDH reactions due to their enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution, surpassing traditional zeolites.

        The presence of specific elements like gallium (Ga) and cerium (Ce) in hierarchical zeolites further improves their catalytic properties, enabling higher activity and selectivity in PDH reactions. Moreover, the introduction of cesium (Cs+) ions can improve the regeneration stability of the catalysts, maintaining unchanged catalytic activity after multiple cycles.

        The integration of hierarchical zeolites with metal nanoparticles, such as Pt, Sn, and ZnOx, further enhances their performance in PDH reactions. This design allows for the creation of small, controllable metal nanoparticles, promising for enhanced catalytic activity in PDH reactions.

        The development of hierarchical zeolites with improved pore architectures has led to significant advancements in PDH reactions.         These findings highlight the potential of hierarchical zeolites as highly selective and efficient catalysts for PDH reactions.         在特定元素如镓（Ga）和铈（Ce）的存在下，层次化沸石的催化性能得到了进一步提升，展现出更高的活性和选择性。此外，引入铯（Cs+）离子可以提高催化剂的再生稳定性，维持催化活性不变，经过多次循环后。

        层次化沸石与铂（Pt）、锡（Sn）和ZnOx等金属纳米粒子的结合进一步提升了其在PDH反应中的性能。这些材料利用了微孔的形状选择性和介孔材料的运输效率，其中介孔在层次化支持中充当纳米空间，限制金属纳米粒子的自由迁移，防止高温下的严重聚集。这一设计允许制造小尺寸、可控的金属纳米粒子，为PDH反应提供了增强的催化活性。

        层次化沸石的开发及其改进的孔结构架构引领了PDH反应领域的重大进步。这些材料作为可持续和环保的丙烷脱氢技术的替代品，提供了一种更安全的选择，克服了传统催化剂存在的毒性问题和生态危害。原位形成的ZnOx物种进一步增强了这些催化剂的催化效率，导致更长周期内较高的丙烷转化率。

        这些研究结果强调了层次化沸石作为PDH反应高效选择性催化剂的潜力。结合微孔和介孔/大孔结构的材料增强了其催化性能，使其成为开发高效、环境友好的PDH技术的一个有前景的途径。
