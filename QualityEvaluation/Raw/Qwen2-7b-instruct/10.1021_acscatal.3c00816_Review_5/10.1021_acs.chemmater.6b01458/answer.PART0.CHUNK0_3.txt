<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. Main types of these materials include SAPO-34, MFI, and BEA structures. Synthesis methods involve hydrothermal treatment, sol-gel process, and ion-exchange technique. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by providing better selectivity and conversion efficiency compared to traditional zeolites.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
