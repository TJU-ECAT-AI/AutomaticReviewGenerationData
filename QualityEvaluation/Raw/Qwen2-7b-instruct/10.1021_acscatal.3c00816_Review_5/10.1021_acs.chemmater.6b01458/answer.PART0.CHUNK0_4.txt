<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types of these materials include SAPO-34, MFI, and ZSM-5 zeolites. Synthesis methods typically involve hydrothermal treatment, sol-gel processes, and ion-exchange techniques. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by providing a higher surface area and better pore structure, leading to improved selectivity and conversion rates.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

这些分级沸石将微孔和介孔/大孔结合在一起，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。这些材料的主要类型包括SAPO-34、MFI和ZSM-5沸石。合成方法通常包括水热处理、溶胶-凝胶过程和离子交换技术。这些材料通过提供更高的比表面积和更好的孔结构来改善PDH反应的性能，从而提高选择性和转化率。
