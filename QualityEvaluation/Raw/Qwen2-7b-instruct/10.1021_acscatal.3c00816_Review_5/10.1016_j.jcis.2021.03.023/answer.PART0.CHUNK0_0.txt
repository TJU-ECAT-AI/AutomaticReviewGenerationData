<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. They are typically synthesized by incorporating mesoporous or macroporous materials into the zeolite framework. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by providing additional pathways for reactants and products, thus enhancing the efficiency of the catalytic process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
