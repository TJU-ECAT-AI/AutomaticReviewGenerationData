<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include MCM-41, SBA-15, and MCM-48.
3. These materials are synthesized through template-directed methods, such as the Stöber process for SBA-15.
4. Hierarchical zeolites improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced pore architecture, which allows for better diffusion of reactants and products.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 级联型沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 级联型沸石的主要类型包括MCM-41、SBA-15和MCM-48。
3. 这些材料通过模板导向方法合成，例如Stöber过程用于合成SBA-15。
4. 与传统沸石相比，级联型沸石在PDH反应中的性能得到改善，这是由于其增强的孔结构，这使得反应物和产物的扩散更好。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
