<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost access in PDH reactions.
2. Major types encompass MFI, MEL, and BEA structures.
3. Synthesis techniques include hydrothermal synthesis, sol-gel processing, and template-directed assembly.
4. These materials enhance performance by offering increased surface area and regulated pore size distribution, promoting reactant diffusion and product expulsion.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 分级沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，以增强PDH反应的可访问性。
2. 主要类型包括MFI、MEL和BEA结构。
3. 合成方法涉及水热合成、溶胶-凝胶加工和模板导向组装。
4. 这些材料通过提供更大的表面积和调节的孔径分布，促进反应物扩散和产物排出，从而提高性能。
