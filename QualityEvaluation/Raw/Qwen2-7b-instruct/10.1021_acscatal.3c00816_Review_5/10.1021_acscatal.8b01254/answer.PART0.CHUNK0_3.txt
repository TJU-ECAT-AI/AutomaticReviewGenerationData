<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include MFI, MEL, and BEA.
3. Synthesis methods involve template-directed assembly, vapor-phase exchange, and sol-gel processes.
4. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by increasing surface area and porosity.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites offer better selectivity and stability in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

3. 合成方法包括模板导向组装、气相交换和溶胶凝胶过程。
