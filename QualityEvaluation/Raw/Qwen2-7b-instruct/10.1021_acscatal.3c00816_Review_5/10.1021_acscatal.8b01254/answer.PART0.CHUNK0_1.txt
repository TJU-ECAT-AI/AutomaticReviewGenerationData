<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites consist of H-ZSM-5, H-USY, and H-MFI.
3. Synthesis techniques typically employ vapor-phase exchange of conventional zeolites with metal salts such as GaCl3.
4. These materials enhance the performance of PDH reactions through improved surface area and pore architecture.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
