<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites are H-ZSM-5, H-Y, H-MFI, and H-MOR.
3. Synthesis methods usually consist of template-directed assembly or vapor-phase exchange.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, these materials boost PDH reaction performance due to their enhanced surface area and pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
