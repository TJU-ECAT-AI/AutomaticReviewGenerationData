<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites are chabazite, faujasite, and mordenite.
3. Synthesis techniques encompass template-directed assembly, sol-gel processes, and hydrothermal treatments.
4. These materials elevate PDH reaction performance by offering a greater surface area and more accessible sites for reactants.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit superior selectivity and efficiency in PDH reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
