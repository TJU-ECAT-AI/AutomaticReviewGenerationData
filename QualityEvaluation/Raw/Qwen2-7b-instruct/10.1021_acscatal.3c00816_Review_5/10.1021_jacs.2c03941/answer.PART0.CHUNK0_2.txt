1. Ga+-Chabazite zeolite functions as an effective catalyst for non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation (PDH).
2. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost the accessibility for PDH reactions.
3. Key types of hierarchical zeolites encompass mesoporous zeolites and macroporous zeolites.
4. Synthesis techniques often employ templates, sol-gel methodologies, or hydrothermal treatments.
5. These materials enhance PDH reaction performance through offering a greater surface area and optimized pore structure.
6. In comparison to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit superior selectivity and activity in PDH reactions.
