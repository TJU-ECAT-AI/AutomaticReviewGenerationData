<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance the accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials encompass mesoporous-silica-based zeolites, metal-organic frameworks (MOFs), and hybrid organic-inorganic materials (HOIMs).
3. Synthesis methods commonly used include template-directed assembly, sol-gel processes, and covalent organic framework (COF) strategies.
4. These materials outperform traditional zeolites in PDH reactions by providing larger surface areas, better thermal stability, and enhanced coking resistance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
1. 级联沸石将微孔和介孔/大孔结合在一起，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。
2. 这些材料的主要类型包括介孔二氧化硅基沸石、金属有机框架（MOFs）和杂化有机无机材料（HOIMs）。
3. 合成方法通常涉及模板导向组装、溶胶-凝胶过程和共价有机框架（COF）策略。
4. 这些材料在PDH反应中的性能优于传统沸石，提供更大的比表面积、更好的热稳定性以及提高的结焦抵抗性。
