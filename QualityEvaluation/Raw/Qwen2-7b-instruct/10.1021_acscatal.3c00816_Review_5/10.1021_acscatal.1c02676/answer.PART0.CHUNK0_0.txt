<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include TS-1, ZSM-5, and Beta zeolites.
3. Synthesis methods of hierarchical zeolites typically involve hydrothermal treatment or template-directed synthesis.
4. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions by providing more active sites and better diffusion channels for reactants and products.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites show higher activity and selectivity in PDH reactions due to their unique pore structures.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

3. 级联沸石的合成方法通常涉及水热处理或模板导向合成。

5. 相比传统沸石，级联沸石在PDH反应中的活性和选择性更高，这是由于它们独特的孔结构。
