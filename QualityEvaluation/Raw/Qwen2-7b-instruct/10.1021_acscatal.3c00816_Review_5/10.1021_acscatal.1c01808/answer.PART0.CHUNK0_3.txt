<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials include mesoporous zeolites and macroporous zeolites.
3. Synthesis methods for these materials typically involve template-directed assembly or sol-gel processes.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites exhibit improved performance in PDH reactions due to enhanced accessibility and better thermal stability.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
