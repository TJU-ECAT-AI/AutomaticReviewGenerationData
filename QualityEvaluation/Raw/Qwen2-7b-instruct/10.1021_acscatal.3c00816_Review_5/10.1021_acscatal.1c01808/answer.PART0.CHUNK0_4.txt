<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. Main types of these materials are MCM-41, SBA-15, and SBA-16.
3. Synthesis methods include template-directed assembly and post-synthetic modification.
4. Compared to traditional zeolites, these materials enhance PDH reaction performance thanks to their increased surface area and controlled pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
