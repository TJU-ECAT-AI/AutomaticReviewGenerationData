<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. They can be synthesized using methods such as template-directed synthesis or sol-gel processing. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites by providing a larger surface area and increased porosity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
