<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include MFI, MEL, and BEA structures, and their synthesis methods often involve template-directed assembly or sol-gel processes. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to enhanced surface area and controlled pore size distribution.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
层次型沸石结合微孔和介孔/大孔，以增强PDH反应的可接近性。层次型沸石的主要类型包括MFI、MEL和BEA结构，其合成方法通常涉及模板导向组装或溶胶-凝胶过程。这些材料与传统沸石相比，由于提高了比表面积和可控的孔径分布，从而改善了PDH反应的性能。
