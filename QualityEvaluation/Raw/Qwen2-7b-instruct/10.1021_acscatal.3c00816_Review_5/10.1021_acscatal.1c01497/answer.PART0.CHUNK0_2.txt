<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to boost accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary types of hierarchical zeolites are H-ZSM-5, H-USY, and H-Y.
3. Synthesis methods include template-directed synthesis, sol-gel process, and ion-exchange techniques.
4. Compared to conventional zeolites, these materials exhibit enhanced pore structure and surface area, leading to improved PDH reaction performance.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
