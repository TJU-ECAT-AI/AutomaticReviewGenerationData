<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of hierarchical zeolites include single-crystal house-of-cards-like ZSM-5.
3. Synthesis methods involve template-directed assembly and high-pressure crystallization.
4. These materials improve PDH reaction performance by increasing surface area and pore volume.
5. Compared to traditional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites offer enhanced catalytic activity and selectivity.
