<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Hierarchical zeolites integrate micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The primary categories of these materials are ordered mesoporous silica (OMS), mesoporous aluminosilicates, and metal-organic frameworks (MOFs).
3. Synthesis methodologies involve template-directed synthesis, sol-gel processes, and hydrothermal treatments.
4. These materials outperform traditional zeolites in PDH reaction performance due to increased surface area, controlled pore size, and enhanced catalytic activity.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
