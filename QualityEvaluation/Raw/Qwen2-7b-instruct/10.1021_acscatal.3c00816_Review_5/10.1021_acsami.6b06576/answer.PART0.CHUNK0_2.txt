1. Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions.
2. The main types of these materials include zeolite A, zeolite Y, and mesoporous silicas.
3. Synthesis methods involve hydrothermal treatment, sol-gel processes, and template-directed synthesis.
4. These materials improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites due to their enhanced surface area and pore structure.
