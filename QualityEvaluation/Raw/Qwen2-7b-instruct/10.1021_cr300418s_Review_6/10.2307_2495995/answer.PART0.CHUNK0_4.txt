The Soviet Russian State and The Soviet State: An Aging Revolution by Robert G. Wesson are textbooks aimed at undergraduate courses on Soviet politics. They explore the historical tensions between introducing Western technology and maintaining political control within the empire. These books are noted for their balanced coverage and depth, providing insightful information on various aspects of the Soviet system. The author, Professor Wesson, has achieved his goal of writing readable textbooks that are thematically integrated with useful illustrative details. The book Soviet Economists of the Twenties: Names to Be Remembered by Naum Jasny discusses the significant role of non-Bolshevik economists in the development of Soviet planning and economic thought, highlighting their collaboration with the new regime despite their political backgrounds and personal reasons for doing so.

《苏联俄罗斯国家》和《苏联国家：一个衰老的革命》是罗伯特·G·韦森为苏联政治本科生课程编写的教科书。它们探讨了引进西方技术与帝国内部维持政治控制之间的历史紧张关系。这些书籍因其平衡的覆盖范围和深度而受到赞誉，提供了对苏联制度各方面的重要信息。作者罗伯特·G·韦森成功地编写了可读性高、主题整合良好的教科书，包含了许多有用的说明细节。《二十世纪苏联经济学家：值得铭记的名字》一书由纳乌姆·贾斯尼撰写，讨论了非布尔什维克经济学家在苏联规划和经济思想发展中的关键作用，强调了他们在新政权下合作的原因以及他们的政治背景和个人理由。
