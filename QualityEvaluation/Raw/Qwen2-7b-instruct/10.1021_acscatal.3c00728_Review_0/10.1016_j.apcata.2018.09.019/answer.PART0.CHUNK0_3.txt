The significance of propene in the chemical industry has evolved significantly due to its increasing demand across various applications. This demand has led to the widespread adoption of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a preferred production method. PDH is favored because it provides economic advantages such as higher yield rates and lower costs compared to traditional methods. Additionally, it offers environmental benefits including reduced emissions and the potential for using renewable feedstocks.
