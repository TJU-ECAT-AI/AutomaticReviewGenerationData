<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Propene's significance in the chemical industry has evolved as its demand has increased, driving the adoption of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a preferred production method. This shift is primarily due to PDH's ability to produce propene with higher efficiency, resulting in lower costs and reduced environmental impact. Economically, PDH offers a competitive edge over traditional processes, while environmentally, it minimizes emissions and energy consumption, making it an attractive solution for meeting the growing demands for propene across various applications.
