<quotes>
The nature of active chromium species in Cr-catalysts for dehydrogenation of propane: New insights by a comprehensive spectroscopic study
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study explores the nature and distribution of chromium species in Cr-catalysts used for dehydrogenating propane. The catalysts are characterized using various techniques like N2-physisorption, X-ray diffraction (XRD), UV-Raman spectroscopy, scanning transmission electron microscopy-energy dispersive X-ray spectroscopy (STEMEDXS), and X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XANES and EXAFS).
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这项研究探讨了用于丙烷脱氢的Cr催化剂中活性铬物种的性质和分布。使用包括氮气吸附、X射线衍射（XRD）、紫外拉曼光谱、扫描透射电子显微镜-能量色散X射线光谱（STEMEDXS）以及X射线吸收光谱（XANES和EXAFS）在内的多种技术对催化剂进行了表征。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
It reveals that at 1 wt% Cr, SBA-15 contains exclusively uniform distribution of chromium as isolated Cr(VI) species in tetrahedral coordination, whilst on γ-Al2O3 a fraction of oligomers (including dimers) is present along with isolated species. At 5 wt% Cr, SBA-15 is dominated by crystalline α-Cr2O3 particles, besides a fraction of isolated Cr(VI) species in coordination. Remarkably, γ-Al2O3 contains mainly oligomers with different degrees of nuclearity and a fraction of isolated species but no Cr2O3 particles.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
它表明，在1 wt% Cr的情况下，SBA-15仅包含均匀分布的孤立Cr(VI)物种，其在四面体配位下存在；而在γ-Al2O3上，则有一部分寡聚体（包括二聚体）与孤立物种共存。在5 wt% Cr时，SBA-15主要由晶态α-Cr2O3颗粒组成，还有一部分在协调下存在的孤立Cr(VI)物种。值得注意的是，γ-Al2O3主要含有不同核度的寡聚体以及一部分孤立物种，但没有Cr2O3颗粒。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The intrinsic activity of these isolated Cr species is higher than those observed on γ-Al2O3 (1 wt% Cr). Interestingly, among xCrAl2O3, the catalysts dominated by oligomeric species show the highest activity, indicating the important role of the species in the reaction.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这些孤立的Cr物种的内在活性高于在γ-Al2O3上观察到的（1 wt% Cr）。有趣的是，在xCrAl2O3中，以寡聚物种为主的催化剂显示出最高的活性，这表明这些物种在反应中的重要作用。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The findings highlight the dependence of the nature and distribution of Cr species on the support, and how this affects their catalytic performance in propane dehydrogenation.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
研究结果强调了Cr物种的性质和分布依赖于支持物，并如何影响其在丙烷脱氢中的催化性能。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The study contributes to the understanding of the structure-reactivity relationships of Cr catalysts in dehydrogenation of alkanes and could guide the design of more efficient catalysts for similar reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>
这项研究有助于理解Cr催化剂在烷烃脱氢中的结构-反应关系，并可能指导设计更高效的类似反应催化剂。
</ChineseVersionAnswer>
