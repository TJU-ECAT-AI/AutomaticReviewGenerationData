    <English>
        The concept of tandem catalysis at the nanoscale, as exemplified by the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst for coupling catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, significantly advances the field of chemical engineering processes. This approach integrates multiple reaction steps, aiming to enhance energy efficiency and process optimization. It enables precise control over reactive intermediates, thereby facilitating the sequential execution of reactions such as oxidative dehydrogenation and selective hydrogen combustion. The net effect is a rapid and stable oxidative dehydrogenation of propane at high per-pass yields, surpassing the equilibrium conversion of non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This showcases the potential for improved chemical processing and reduced energy consumption.

        The In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst specifically demonstrates the potential of tandem catalysis by achieving high per-pass yields that exceed the PDH equilibrium. Beyond this result, further improvements and examples are anticipated from optimizing process conditions and independently tuning the identity and amount of dehydrogenation and selective hydrogen combustion (SHC) sites. This highlights the versatility and adaptability of tandem catalysis in addressing complex chemical processing challenges.

        Parallelly, the utilization of small metal clusters within protected channels of shape-selective zeolites, as seen in the Pt/Na-[Fe]ZSM5 catalyst, further exemplifies the power of tandem catalysis. These catalysts enable high turnover rates and stability in dehydrogenation reactions, particularly for alkanes like C3H8 and C2H6. By staging the introduction of oxygen, selective hydrogen removal is achieved, leading to enhanced alkene yields beyond equilibrium. This method markedly decreases process complexity and costs, optimizing energy efficiency and process integration compared to traditional multi-stage systems.

        The tandem catalysis approach, therefore, represents a significant advancement in chemical engineering processes, offering a promising pathway towards more efficient, cost-effective, and sustainable chemical production methods. Future research endeavors might focus on exploring new materials, optimizing existing ones, and developing innovative methodologies to further enhance the performance and applicability of tandem catalysis in various industrial settings.

    <Chinese>
        在纳米尺度上实施串联催化，以In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂为例，将催化丙烷脱氢与选择性H2燃烧耦合起来，极大地推进了化学工程过程领域的进展。这种策略整合了多个反应步骤，旨在提高能效和优化工艺流程。它允许对中间反应体进行精确控制，从而实现氧化脱氢和选择性H2燃烧等反应的有序执行。净效果是快速而稳定的丙烷氧化脱氢，在每次通过的高产率下超越非氧化丙烷脱氢（PDH）的平衡转化。这展示了改善化学处理和减少能耗的潜力。

        In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂具体展示串联催化原理，通过实现超过非氧化PDH平衡的高每次通过产率，证明了其优势。除此之外，进一步的改进和案例期待通过优化工艺条件和独立调整脱氢和选择性氢燃烧（SHC）站点的身份和数量来实现。这凸显了串联催化在解决复杂化学加工挑战方面的灵活性和适应性。

        同时，小金属簇在形状选择性沸石通道保护内的应用，如Pt/Na-[Fe]ZSM5催化剂所展示的，进一步体现了串联催化的力量。这些催化剂使得烷烃如C3H8和C2H6的脱氢反应具有高周转率和稳定性。通过分阶段引入氧气，实现了选择性氢去除，进而提高了超出平衡的烯烃产率。这种方法显著降低了工艺复杂性和成本，与传统的多级系统相比，优化了能源效率和工艺集成。

        因此，串联催化策略代表了化学工程过程领域的重大进步，为更高效、经济和可持续的化学品生产方法开辟了有前景的途径。未来的研究努力可能集中在探索新材料、优化现有材料和开发创新方法上，以进一步增强串联催化在各种工业环境中的性能和应用范围。
