"High C3H8 dehydrogenation rates with high alkene selectivities and yields and unprecedented catalyst stability were achieved using Pt clusters residing within shape-selective channels in Na-[Fe]ZSM5."
         "Staged O2 introduction led to the combustion of H2 with 90% selectivity on these catalysts, even when hydrocarbons were predominant components in reacting mixtures."
         "Alkene yields higher than equilibrium by a factor of 1.6 were achieved for C2H6 and C3H8 dehydrogenation at 773 and 723 K, respectively."
         "These enhancements are significantly higher than previously reported with a more complex three-stage selective H2 combustion scheme."
