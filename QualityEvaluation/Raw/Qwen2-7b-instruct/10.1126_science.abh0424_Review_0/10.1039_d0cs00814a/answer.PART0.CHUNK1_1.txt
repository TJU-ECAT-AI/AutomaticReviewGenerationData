<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The concept of tandem catalysis at the nanoscale addresses the challenges of integrating multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes by enabling efficient sequential reactions on the same catalyst surface. This approach allows for the coupling of catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, potentially leading to enhanced energy efficiency and process optimization. By utilizing a single catalyst system, tandem catalysis minimizes the need for separate reaction stages, reducing energy consumption and increasing process throughput. The In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst exemplifies this integration, demonstrating its potential for efficient energy use in the dehydrogenation process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
