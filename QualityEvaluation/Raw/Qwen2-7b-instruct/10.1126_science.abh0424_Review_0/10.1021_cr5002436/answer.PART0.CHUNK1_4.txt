<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Tandem catalysis at the nanoscale integrates multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes by sequentially executing them on a single catalyst support. This method enables the coupling of catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, aiming to enhance energy efficiency and process optimization. By combining these two processes on a single catalyst platform, it reduces the energy consumption and waste generation compared to traditional separation steps, leading to improved sustainability and economic benefits.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
