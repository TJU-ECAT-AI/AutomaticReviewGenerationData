            <p>Light olefins, such as propylene and ethylene, are pivotal to the chemical industry, serving as foundational components in the synthesis of polyolefins, styrene, ethylene dichloride, acrylonitrile, and isopropanol. Their production primarily hinges on processes like steam cracking, catalytic cracking, and dehydrogenation, with thermodynamic challenges arising from managing temperature, pressure, and alkane chain length effects.</p>
            <p>Olefin/paraffin separation, a cornerstone of the petrochemical industry, encounters difficulties due to the high energy demands of traditional methods such as cryogenic distillation. The adoption of membrane separation techniques presents an energy-efficient alternative. The development of polymer-inorganic nano composite materials aims to enhance the physical properties of polymer membranes, simultaneously improving selectivity and permeability. This innovation has the potential to revolutionize the utilization of membrane technology in the petrochemical industry, overcoming the limitations imposed by insufficient gas flux in conventional separation methods.</p>
            <p>The kinetic modeling of thermal cracking processes employs a free-radical reaction scheme with numerous reactions, typically following free-radical mechanisms. Constructing a kinetic model necessitates determining frequency factors and activation energies for each reaction. Transition-state theory simplifies the calculation of frequency factors by focusing on the standard entropy of the activated complex. Industrial reactors operate at high temperatures (approximately 850-900°C), enabling the application of transition-state theory. The symmetry number of molecules is determined through the examination of their spatial structure, with both methods agreeing for reactions with known symmetry numbers.</p>
            <p>While membrane separation processes offer an energy-efficient solution, they are often constrained by insufficient gas flux. Polymer-inorganic nano composite materials are being developed to improve the physical properties of polymer membranes, thereby enhancing both selectivity and permeability. This advancement could significantly augment the utilization of membrane technology in the petrochemical industry, addressing the limitations posed by conventional separation methods.</p>
            <p>Future research directions might explore the integration of emerging fields and innovative methodologies to further enhance the efficiency and applicability of membrane technologies. Potential areas of focus could include optimizing membrane design, developing more selective and permeable materials, and exploring novel separation processes that leverage advanced materials science and engineering principles.</p>

            <p>丙烯和乙烯等轻烯烃在化工行业至关重要，它们是生产聚烯烃、苯乙烯、乙二醇、丙烯腈和异丙醇等产品的基础原料。其主要生产方法包括蒸汽裂解、催化裂化和脱氢反应，而热力学挑战则主要来源于对温度、压力以及烷烃链长的影响控制。</p>
            <p>在石油化学工业中，烯烃/正构烷烃分离是一个关键过程，由于传统的分离方法如冷冻精馏对高能量需求，面临着诸多挑战。而膜分离技术的引入为实现更高效能的分离提供了可能。通过开发聚合物-无机纳米复合材料来改善聚合物膜的物理性质，同时提高选择性和渗透性，这将极大地推动膜技术在石油化学行业的应用，解决传统分离方法中由气体流量不足带来的限制问题。</p>
            <p>热裂解过程的动态模型通常基于自由基反应方案，包含数百个反应步骤，并且遵循自由基机制。构建动力学模型需要为每个反应确定频率因子和活化能。过渡态理论简化了频率因子的计算，将其归结为激活复杂体系标准熵的计算。工业反应器在约850-900°C的高温下运行，使得过渡态理论的应用成为可能。分子的对称数通过对其空间结构的详细研究来确定，在所有已知对称数的情况下，两种方法都是一致的。</p>
            <p>尽管膜分离技术提供了更节能的解决方案，但它们往往受限于气体流量不足的问题。通过开发聚合物-无机纳米复合材料来改进聚合物膜的物理性能，进而提升选择性和渗透性，这一进展有望显著增强膜技术在石油化学行业的应用，克服传统分离方法带来的局限。</p>
            <p>未来研究方向可能包括整合新兴领域和创新方法，进一步提高膜技术的效率和适用性。潜在的研究重点可能包括优化膜设计、开发更加选择性和渗透性的材料，以及探索利用先进的材料科学和工程原理的新型分离过程。</p>
