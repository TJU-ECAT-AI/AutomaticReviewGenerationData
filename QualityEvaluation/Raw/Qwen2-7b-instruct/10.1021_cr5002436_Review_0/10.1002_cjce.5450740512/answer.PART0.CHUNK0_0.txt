<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. Light olefins, such as propylene and ethylene, are vital in the chemical industry for their diverse applications, primarily in plastic, solvent, and lubricant production. 
2. Steam cracking is a primary method for olefin production, which is influenced by temperature and pressure. Higher temperatures promote cracking, whereas higher pressures can enhance the yield of lighter olefins.
3. Key thermodynamic challenges in olefin synthesis involve managing the balance between cracking and reforming reactions. The length of the alkane chain impacts the product distribution, favoring more olefins from shorter chains.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
