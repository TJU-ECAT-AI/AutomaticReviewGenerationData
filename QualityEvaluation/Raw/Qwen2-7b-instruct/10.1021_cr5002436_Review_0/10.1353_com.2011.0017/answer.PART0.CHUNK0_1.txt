<EnglishVersionAnswer>
Light olefins, including propylene and ethylene, significantly contribute to the chemical industry. They are widely utilized in petrochemicals, plastics, and fuels. The synthesis of these olefins employs methods such as steam cracking, thermal cracking, and catalytic processes. Temperature influences the cracking reaction, determining the amount of light olefins produced. Pressure impacts the effectiveness of these reactions, altering the selectivity of products. Additionally, the length of alkane chains affects the distribution of olefin products in the cracking process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
