The Ga/Al ratio, Si/Al ratio, and framework aluminum pair density in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts significantly influence the formation and nature of active gallium species, including gallium hydrides. These relationships reveal important insights into the structure-activity correlations of the catalysts in propane dehydrogenation reactions.
