<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Ga/Al ratio, Si/Al ratio, and framework aluminum pair density in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts influence the formation and nature of active gallium species, including gallium hydrides. These relationships are crucial for understanding the structure-activity correlations in propane dehydrogenation reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
