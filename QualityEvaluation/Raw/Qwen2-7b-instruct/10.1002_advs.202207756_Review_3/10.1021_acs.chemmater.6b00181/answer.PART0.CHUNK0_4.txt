The study investigates how the Ga/Al ratio, Si/Al ratio, and framework aluminum pair density affect the formation and characteristics of active gallium species in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts. These factors are crucial in understanding the structure-activity correlations of these catalysts during propane dehydrogenation reactions.
