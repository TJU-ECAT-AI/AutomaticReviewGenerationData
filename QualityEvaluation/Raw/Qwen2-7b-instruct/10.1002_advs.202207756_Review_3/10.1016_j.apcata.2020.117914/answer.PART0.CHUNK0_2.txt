<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The review discusses the influence of the Ga/Al ratio, Si/Al ratio, and framework aluminum pair density in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts on the formation and nature of active gallium species, including gallium hydrides, in the context of propane dehydrogenation reactions.
