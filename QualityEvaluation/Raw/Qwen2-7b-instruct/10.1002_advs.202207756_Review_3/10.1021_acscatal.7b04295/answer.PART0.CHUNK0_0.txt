<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Ga/Al ratio, Si/Al ratio, and framework aluminum pair density in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts significantly influence the formation and nature of active gallium species, particularly gallium hydrides. These relationships highlight the importance of these parameters in structuring the catalysts' activity for propane dehydrogenation reactions.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
