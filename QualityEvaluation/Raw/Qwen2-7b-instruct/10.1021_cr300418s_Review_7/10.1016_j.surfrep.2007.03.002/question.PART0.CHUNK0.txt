M. Ver

This is the motivation of numerous studies of partially reduced oxide surfaces. We review, discuss, and compare theoretical data for structural and electronic properties and energetic quantities related to the formation of oxygen defects at TM and RE oxide surfaces using TiO2, ZrO2, V2O5, and CeO2 as examples.
