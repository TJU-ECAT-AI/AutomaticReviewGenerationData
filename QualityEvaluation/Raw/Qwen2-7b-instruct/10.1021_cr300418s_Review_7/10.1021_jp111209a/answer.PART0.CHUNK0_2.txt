<quotes>
<p>The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism (8-10) for catalytic oxidation involves the formation and annihilation of oxygen vacancies on the surface of rutile TiO2(110).</p>
<p>The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) influences both the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface.</p>
</quotes>

<EnglishVersionAnswer>
1. The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism describes how oxygen vacancies are formed and annihilated on the surface of rutile TiO2(110) during catalytic oxidation processes.
2. The energy needed to create an oxygen vacancy (ΔEv) plays a crucial role in determining the catalytic efficiency and oxidative capability of the oxide surface.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
