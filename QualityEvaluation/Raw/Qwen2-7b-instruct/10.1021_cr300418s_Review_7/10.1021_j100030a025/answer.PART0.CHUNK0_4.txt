<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism operates in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions by utilizing the energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) on the oxide surface. This energy determines the catalytic activity and oxidizing ability of the oxide surface.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
