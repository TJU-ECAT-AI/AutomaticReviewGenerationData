<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism operates by utilizing the energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) on the oxide surface. This energy plays a crucial role in determining the catalytic activity and oxidizing ability of the oxide surface. A lower ΔEv indicates higher reactivity and efficiency in the oxidation process.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
