<EnglishVersionAnswer>
The Soviet Russian State is a textbook designed for undergraduate courses focusing on Soviet politics. It covers the historical background of the contemporary system through four chapters. Professor Wesson's approach is broadly historical-descriptive, highlighting similarities between the Soviet and tsarist regimes, and comparing them with earlier imperial orders. Despite some issues, Wesson successfully accomplished his objective.
</EnglishVersionAnswer>
