                    The application, structure, and performance of Metal-Ordered Mesoporous Silica (OMS) catalysts in Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions have been extensively studied, particularly focusing on MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx catalysts \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. These catalysts exhibit superior performance due to their unique structural properties, such as low surface acidity, high surface area, and ordered pores, which enhance reactive site dispersion and anti-coking \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. The surface acidity of the catalyst plays a critical role in coke formation, with higher acidity leading to increased coke \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Surface OH content on Al2O3 affects the polymeric degree of VOx species and its interaction with the support \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. The SMSI effect between the support and active center is essential for high conversion and selectivity \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Transition metals can optimize the electronic structure of Pt atoms, improving catalytic performance \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Zn anchoring sites enhance electron interaction and improve dispersion \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Sulfur addition promotes C3H6 desorption and anti-coking ability \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Na-doping regulates the interaction between Brnsted sites and Cr components, decreasing propane adsorption and inhibiting CH bond activation \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. The 5%Cr/Z-5(260) catalyst showcases remarkable performance with high propane conversion and propylene selectivity \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. In non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation, VOx/MCM-41 showed superior stability over four DH cycles, with activity fully recoverable by oxidative regeneration \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. Its VOx species remained unchanged, unlike CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3, which lost activity due to structural changes in active species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. Deactivation in a single cycle was mainly due to carbon deposits, while cycle-to-cycle activity loss was caused by metal/metal oxide species alterations \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. H2 pretreatment effects on DH properties were explored, revealing that VOx/MCM-41's stability is attributed to unchanged VOx species over cycles, contrasting with CrOx aggregation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in the application of Metal-Ordered Mesoporous Silica (OMS) catalysts in Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions, particularly focusing on MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx catalysts, have opened new avenues for exploration. The unique structural properties of these catalysts, such as low surface acidity, high surface area, and ordered pores, have been shown to enhance reactive site dispersion and anti-coking, leading to superior performance in PDH reactions. Future research efforts might focus on further optimizing the electronic structure of Pt atoms through the introduction of transition metals, enhancing electron interaction and dispersion through Zn anchoring sites, and promoting C3H6 desorption and anti-coking ability through sulfur addition. Additionally, the regulation of interactions between Brnsted sites and Cr components through Na-doping could be explored to decrease propane adsorption and inhibit CH bond activation. The study of the effects of lattice oxygen species and H2 pretreatment on catalyst performance and deactivation in PDH reactions remains a critical area for future investigation, as it could lead to the development of more stable and efficient catalysts.

                    金属有序介孔二氧化硅(OMS)催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的应用、结构和性能已得到广泛研究,特别是关注MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx催化剂\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。这些催化剂因其独特的结构特性,如低表面酸性、高表面积和有序孔隙,表现出优越的性能,这些特性增强了反应位点的分散和抗结焦能力\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。催化剂的表面酸性在结焦形成中起着关键作用,酸性越高,结焦越多\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Al2O3上的表面OH含量影响VOx物种的聚合度及其与支撑物的相互作用\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。支撑物与活性中心之间的SMSI效应对于高转化率和选择性至关重要\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。过渡金属可以优化Pt原子的电子结构,提高催化性能\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Zn锚定位点增强电子相互作用,改善分散性\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。硫的添加促进C3H6的解吸和抗结焦能力\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Na掺杂调节Brnsted位点与Cr组分之间的相互作用,减少丙烷吸附,抑制CH键活化\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。5%Cr/Z-5(260)催化剂展现出卓越的性能,具有高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。在非氧化丙烷脱氢中,VOx/MCM-41在四个DH循环中表现出优越的稳定性,活性完全可以通过氧化再生恢复\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。与CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3不同,其VOx物种保持不变,后者由于活性物种的结构变化而失去活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。单个循环中的失活主要是由于碳沉积,而循环间的活性损失是由金属/金属氧化物物种的改变引起的\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。对DH性质的H2预处理效应进行了探讨,揭示了VOx/MCM-41的稳定性归因于循环中不变的VOx物种,与CrOx的聚集形成对比\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中应用金属有序介孔二氧化硅(OMS)催化剂,特别是MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx催化剂,的进展开辟了新的探索途径。这些催化剂的独特结构特性,如低表面酸性、高表面积和有序孔隙,已被证明能增强反应位点的分散和抗结焦能力,从而在PDH反应中表现出优越的性能。未来的研究工作可能集中在通过引入过渡金属进一步优化Pt原子的电子结构,通过Zn锚定位点增强电子相互作用和分散性,以及通过硫的添加促进C3H6的解吸和抗结焦能力。此外,通过Na掺杂调节Brnsted位点与Cr组分之间的相互作用,以减少丙烷吸附和抑制CH键活化,也值得探索。研究晶格氧物种和H2预处理对催化剂性能和PDH反应中失活的影响仍然是未来研究的关键领域,因为这可能导致开发更稳定、更高效的催化剂。
