                    The application of Metal-Ordered Mesoporous Silica (OMS) catalysts, particularly MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx, in Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions has garnered significant attention due to their unique structural properties and catalytic behavior \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. These catalysts exhibit superior performance over traditional metal oxide supports, with isolated Cr(III) sites in xCrSBA-15 (1 wt% Cr) demonstrating higher activity and selectivity per mole of Cr than those dominated by crystalline α-Cr2O3 particles (5 wt% Cr) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. In contrast, for xCrAl2O3, oligomeric Cr species are more active and selective than isolated Cr sites \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. Active Cr sites are generated on-site during the reaction, typically in the (III) oxidation state \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\].

                    VOx/MCM-41 catalysts have shown notable onstream stability and high selectivity in PDH reactions, with isolated VOx species demonstrating superior intrinsic activity over oligomerized forms \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]. Unlike CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3, VOx/MCM-41 exhibited superior stability and fully recoverable activity through oxidative regeneration, with VOx species remaining unchanged over four DH cycles \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. Deactivation was primarily due to carbon deposits in a single cycle and structural changes in metal/metal oxide species over cycles \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\].

                    The literature lacks specific insights into how lattice oxygen species and H2 pretreatment affect catalyst performance and deactivation in PDH reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]. However, it is known that lattice oxygen species participate in PDH, detectable as CO, and are eliminated by H2 pretreatment \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]. As-prepared VOx/MCM-41 and CrOx/MCM-41 outperform prereduced versions in propylene productivity but have lower stability, with oxidized species more active than reduced ones \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]. Deactivation is linked to lattice oxygen depletion \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\].

                    Surface acidity significantly influences coke formation, with higher acidity leading to increased coke \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Surface OH on Al2O3 affects VOx species and its interaction with the support \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. The SMSI effect between the support and active center is critical for high conversion and selectivity \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Geometric effects are more significant than electronic effects in dehydrogenation activity \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Transition metals improve catalytic performance by altering the electronic structure of Pt atoms \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Zn anchoring enhances electron interaction and improves dispersion \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Sulfur addition promotes C3H6 desorption and anti-coking \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Na-doping regulates interactions between Brnsted sites and Cr components, decreasing propane adsorption and inhibiting CH bond activation \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Optimized catalysts achieve high activity, extreme propene selectivity, and long-term stability \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. The 5%Cr/Z-5(260) catalyst demonstrates remarkable performance with high propane conversion and propylene selectivity \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\].

                    金属有序介孔二氧化硅（OMS）催化剂，特别是MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx，在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的应用因其独特的结构特性和催化行为而受到广泛关注\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。这些催化剂在性能上优于传统的金属氧化物载体，其中xCrSBA-15（1 wt% Cr）中的孤立Cr(III)位点每摩尔Cr的活性和选择性高于由结晶α-Cr2O3颗粒（5 wt% Cr）主导的催化剂\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。相比之下，对于xCrAl2O3，寡聚Cr物种比孤立Cr位点更活跃和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。活性Cr位点在反应过程中生成，通常处于(III)氧化态\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。

                    VOx/MCM-41催化剂在PDH反应中表现出显著的在线稳定性以及高选择性，其中孤立的VOx物种的内在活性优于寡聚形式\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]。与CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3不同，VOx/MCM-41表现出优越的稳定性和完全可恢复的活性，通过氧化再生，VOx物种在四个DH循环中保持不变\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。失活主要是由于单个循环中的碳沉积和金属/金属氧化物物种结构变化导致的循环间活性损失\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。

                    文献中缺乏关于晶格氧物种和H2预处理如何影响PDH反应中催化剂性能和失活的具体见解\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]。然而，已知晶格氧物种参与PDH，可检测为CO，并通过H2预处理消除\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]。预处理的VOx/MCM-41和CrOx/MCM-41在丙烯生产力方面优于预还原版本，但稳定性较低，氧化物种比还原物种更活跃\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]。失活与晶格氧耗尽有关\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]。

                    表面酸度对焦炭形成有显著影响，酸度越高，焦炭形成越多\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Al2O3上的表面OH影响VOx物种及其与载体的相互作用\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。载体与活性中心之间的SMSI效应对于高转化率和选择性至关重要\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。几何效应在脱氢活性中比电子效应更重要\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。过渡金属通过改变Pt原子的电子结构来提高催化性能\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Zn锚定增强电子相互作用并提高分散性\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。硫添加促进C3H6解吸和抗焦化\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。Na掺杂调节Brnsted位点与Cr组分之间的相互作用，减少丙烷吸附并抑制CH键活化\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。优化的催化剂实现了高活性、极端丙烯选择性和长期稳定性\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。5%Cr/Z-5(260)催化剂表现出高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性的显著性能\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。
