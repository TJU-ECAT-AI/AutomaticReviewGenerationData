            <English> 
                    The application of Metal-Ordered Mesoporous Silica (OMS) catalysts, particularly MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx, in Propane Dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions has garnered significant attention due to their unique structural properties and catalytic behavior \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. These catalysts exhibit superior performance over traditional metal oxide supports, with isolated Cr(III) sites in xCrSBA-15 (1 wt% Cr) demonstrating higher activity and selectivity than those dominated by crystalline α-Cr2O3 (5 wt% Cr) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. In contrast, for xCrAl2O3, oligomeric Cr species show the highest activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. Active Cr sites are generated on-site during the reaction, typically in the (III) oxidation state \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. VOx/MCM-41 has shown superior stability and recoverable activity through oxidative regeneration, unlike CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3, which lost activity from cycle to cycle \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. VOx species on MCM-41 remained unchanged over four DH cycles, while CrOx aggregated \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. Carbon deposits were identified as the main deactivation cause in a single cycle, while structural changes in CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3 led to activity loss over cycles \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. H2 pretreatment effects and catalyst deactivation mechanisms were elucidated using in situ UV/Vis and temperature-programmed oxidation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. The study aimed to assess VOx/MCM-41's potential for PDH without gas-phase O2, focusing on long-term stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in the application of OMS catalysts in PDH reactions, particularly MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx, have opened new avenues for exploration. Future research might focus on optimizing the synthesis methods to enhance the dispersion of active sites and improve the stability of these catalysts under PDH conditions. The role of lattice oxygen species and H2 pretreatment in catalyst performance and deactivation remains a critical area for further investigation. Developing a deeper understanding of these factors could lead to the design of more efficient and stable catalysts for PDH reactions. Additionally, the exploration of novel metal-ordered mesoporous materials with tailored properties for PDH could potentially revolutionize the field, offering improved selectivity and activity.

                    金属有序介孔二氧化硅（OMS）催化剂，特别是MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx，在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中的应用因其独特的结构特性和催化行为而受到广泛关注\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。这些催化剂在性能上优于传统的金属氧化物支持，其中xCrSBA-15（1 wt% Cr）中的孤立Cr(III)位点表现出比由结晶α-Cr2O3（5 wt% Cr）主导的催化剂更高的活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。相比之下，对于xCrAl2O3，寡聚Cr物种显示出最高的活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。活性Cr位点在反应过程中生成，通常处于(III)氧化态\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。VOx/MCM-41显示出通过氧化再生恢复的优越稳定性和可回收活性，与从循环到循环失去活性的CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3不同\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。MCM-41上的VOx物种在四个DH循环中保持不变，而CrOx聚集\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。单循环中碳沉积被确定为主要的失活原因，而CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3的结构变化导致了循环中的活性损失\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。使用原位UV/Vis和温度程序氧化阐明了H2预处理效应和催化剂失活机制\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。该研究旨在评估VOx/MCM-41在无气相O2的PDH反应中的潜力，重点放在长期稳定性上\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在PDH反应中OMS催化剂，特别是MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx的应用进展，为探索开辟了新的途径。未来的研究可能集中在优化合成方法以增强活性位点的分散性和改善这些催化剂在PDH条件下的稳定性。晶格氧物种和H2预处理在催化剂性能和失活中的作用仍然是进一步研究的关键领域。对这些因素的深入理解可能导致设计更高效、更稳定的PDH反应催化剂。此外，探索具有定制性质的新型金属有序介孔材料用于PDH，可能彻底改变该领域，提供改进的选择性和活性。
