                    In PDH reactions, xCrSBA-15 catalysts with isolated Cr species exhibit higher activity and selectivity per mole of Cr than those dominated by crystalline α-Cr2O3 particles \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]. Isolated Cr(III) sites with a coordination number greater than four in xCrSBA-15 are more active, selective, and stable than Cr sites on the surface of crystalline Cr2O3. Conversely, for xCrAl2O3, oligomeric Cr species are more active and selective than isolated Cr sites. Active Cr sites are generated on-site during the reaction, typically in the (III) oxidation state.

                    VOx/MCM-41 catalysts exhibit superior stability and recoverable activity through oxidative regeneration in non-oxidative propane dehydrogenation, unlike CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3, which lose activity over cycles \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]. VOx species on MCM-41 remain unchanged, while CrOx aggregates. Deactivation is mainly due to carbon deposits in a single cycle and structural changes in CrOx and PtSn/Al2O3 over cycles. H2 pretreatment influences catalyst properties.

                    OMS catalysts like MCM-41 and SBA-15 supported VOx and CrOx exhibit unique properties affecting catalytic behavior compared to traditional metal oxides \[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]. Surface acidity correlates with coke formation, where higher acidity increases coke tendency. Surface OH on Al2O3 impacts VOx species' polymeric degree and interaction. The SMSI effect between support and active center is pivotal for high conversion and selectivity. Metal migration prevention and sintering agglomeration enhance stability. Geometric effects are more significant than electronic effects for dehydrogenation activity. Transition metals modify Pt atoms' electronic structure, improving performance. Zn anchoring sites constrain Pt, enhancing electron interaction and dispersion. Sulfur addition promotes C3H6 desorption and anti-coking. Na-doping regulates Brnsted sites and Cr components, reducing propane adsorption and CH bond activation. The 5%Cr/Z-5(260) catalyst demonstrates high propane conversion and propylene selectivity.

                    VOx/MCM-41 catalysts exhibit good onstream stability and high selectivity in PDH reactions, with isolated VOx species showing higher intrinsic activity than oligomerized ones \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]. The literature lacks specific discussion on the impact of lattice oxygen species and H2 pretreatment on catalyst performance and deactivation in PDH reactions.

                    OMS, synthesized via self-assembly, supports VOx and CrOx in PDH reactions, offering low acidity, high surface area, and ordered pores, enhancing reactive site dispersion and anti-coking \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]. VOx and CrOx on MCM-41 and SBA-15 show high propylene yields but quick deactivation. Lattice oxygen species participate in PDH, eliminated by H2 pretreatment. As-prepared catalysts yield more propylene but are less stable, with oxidized species more active than reduced ones. VOx/MCM-41 outperforms CrOx/MCM-41, achieving 0.2 kgC3H6/kgcat/h and 90% propylene selectivity.

Additionally, the exploration of transition metals and their impact on Pt atoms' electronic structure offers a promising avenue for improving catalytic activity. The integration of these findings into the design of next-generation PDH catalysts could potentially revolutionize the industry by achieving higher conversion rates and selectivity while minimizing deactivation and coke formation.

                    在PDH反应中，xCrSBA-15催化剂中孤立的Cr物种表现出比以结晶α-Cr2O3粒子为主的催化剂更高的每摩尔Cr的活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.11.014\]。在xCrSBA-15中，配位数大于四的孤立Cr(III)位点比结晶Cr2O3表面的Cr位点更活跃、更选择性、更稳定。相反，对于xCrAl2O3，寡聚Cr物种比孤立Cr位点更活跃和选择性。活性Cr位点在反应过程中原位生成，通常处于(III)氧化态。

                    VOx/MCM-41催化剂在非氧化性丙烷脱氢中表现出优越的稳定性和可恢复的活性，通过氧化再生，与CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3不同，后者在循环中失去活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.06.005\]。MCM-41上的VOx物种保持不变，而CrOx聚集。失活主要是由于单个循环中的碳沉积和CrOx和PtSn/Al2O3在循环中的结构变化。H2预处理影响催化剂性能。

                    诸如MCM-41和SBA-15支持的VOx和CrOx等OMS催化剂表现出与传统金属氧化物相比影响催化行为的独特性质\[10.1039/D2NR02208G\]。表面酸度与焦炭形成相关，酸度越高，焦炭倾向越大。Al2O3上的表面OH影响VOx物种的聚合度和相互作用。支持物和活性中心之间的SMSI效应对于高转化率和选择性至关重要。防止金属迁移和烧结聚集体增强稳定性。几何效应对于脱氢活性比电子效应更重要。过渡金属改变Pt原子的电子结构，提高性能。Zn锚定位点约束Pt，增强电子相互作用和分散。硫添加促进C3H6解吸和抗焦炭。Na掺杂调节Brnsted位点和Cr组分，减少丙烷吸附和CH键活化。5%Cr/Z-5(260)催化剂表现出高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性。

                    VOx/MCM-41催化剂在PDH反应中表现出良好的在线稳定性，具有高选择性，孤立的VOx物种比寡聚物表现出更高的内在活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.05.022\]。文献中缺乏关于晶格氧物种和H2预处理对催化剂性能和PDH反应失活影响的具体讨论。

                    通过自组装合成的OMS支持PDH反应中的VOx和CrOx，提供低酸度、高表面积和有序孔隙，增强反应位点分散和抗焦炭\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_1\]。MCM-41和SBA-15上的VOx和CrOx显示出高丙烯产量，但快速失活。晶格氧物种参与PDH，被H2预处理消除。制备的催化剂产生更多丙烯，但稳定性较差，氧化物种比还原物种更活跃。VOx/MCM-41优于CrOx/MCM-41，实现0.2 kgC3H6/kgcat/h和90%的丙烯选择性。
