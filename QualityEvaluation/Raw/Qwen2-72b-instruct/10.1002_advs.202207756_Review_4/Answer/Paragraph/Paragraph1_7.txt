                    The alkyl mechanism in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions on gallium-based catalysts is a complex process involving the activation of propane by a metal cation to form an alkyl gallium intermediate, followed by β-elimination and desorption of propylene and hydrogen \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. This mechanism applies to various Ga species structures, including Ga+, [GaH]+, and Ga2O22+, with the CH activation step proposed as the rate-determining step (RDS) due to the strong C-H bond breakage \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. Unlike the carbenium mechanism, which facilitates hydrogen recombination, the alkyl mechanism activates the CH bond in propane \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. The initial step involves oxidative addition of an alkane molecule to the Ga+ cation via an indirect heterolytic mechanism, with the overall activation barrier for the alkyl mechanism being lower than that for the carbenium mechanism \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. Ga+ is the most favorable species, followed by GaH2+ and GaH+2, with the reactivity of GaH+2 ions strongly dependent on the distance between stabilizing aluminum-occupied oxygen tetrahedra \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The alkyl mechanism in PDH reactions on gallium-based catalysts presents a rich field for further exploration, particularly in understanding the role of different Ga species and their influence on reaction kinetics. Future research could focus on the precise mechanisms by which GaH+2 ions' reactivity is modulated by the distance between stabilizing aluminum-occupied oxygen tetrahedra, potentially leading to the design of more efficient catalysts. Additionally, the development of advanced computational models could provide deeper insights into the RDS and the overall activation barrier, guiding the synthesis of catalysts with tailored properties for enhanced PDH activity.

                    在基于镓的催化剂上的丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中，烷基机制是一个复杂的过程，涉及通过金属阳离子激活丙烷形成烷基镓中间体，随后β-消除和丙烯和氢的脱附\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。这一机制适用于各种Ga物种结构，包括Ga+，[GaH]+和Ga2O22+，其中CH激活步骤被提议为速率决定步骤（RDS），因为需要断裂强C-H键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。与促进氢重组的碳正离子机制不同，烷基机制激活丙烷中的CH键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。初始步骤涉及通过间接异裂机制将烷烃分子氧化加成到Ga+阳离子，整体上，烷基机制的激活势垒低于碳正离子机制\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。Ga+是最有利的物种，其次是GaH2+和GaH+2，GaH+2离子的反应性强烈依赖于稳定铝占据的氧四面体之间的距离\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：基于镓的催化剂上的PDH反应中的烷基机制为更深入的探索提供了丰富的领域，特别是理解不同Ga物种的作用及其对反应动力学的影响。未来的研究可以集中在GaH+2离子的反应性如何由稳定铝占据的氧四面体之间的距离调节的精确机制上，这可能导向更有效催化剂的设计。此外，开发先进的计算模型可以为RDS和整体激活势垒提供更深入的见解，指导具有定制性能的催化剂的合成，以增强PDH活性。
