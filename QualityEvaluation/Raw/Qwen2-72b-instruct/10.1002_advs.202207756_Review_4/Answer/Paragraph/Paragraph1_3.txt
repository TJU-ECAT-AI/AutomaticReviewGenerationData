                    In PDH reactions on gallium-based catalysts, the alkyl mechanism is a predominant pathway for propane activation, involving the formation of an alkyl gallium intermediate followed by β-elimination and desorption of propylene and hydrogen \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. This mechanism applies to various Ga species structures, including Ga+, [GaH]+, and Ga2O22+, with the CH activation step proposed as the rate-determining step (RDS) due to the strong C-H bond breaking \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. Unlike the carbenium mechanism, which facilitates hydrogen recombination, the alkyl mechanism activates the CH bond in propane \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\].

                    The alkyl mechanism involves gallium acting as an acceptor of the alkyl group, starting with the oxidative addition of an alkane molecule to the Ga+ cation via an indirect heterolytic mechanism \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. The activation energy for the alkyl mechanism is 193 kJ/mol, higher than the 158 kJ/mol for GaH2+ species, but the overall activation barrier for the alkyl mechanism is lower \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. Ga+ is the most favorable Ga species, followed by GaH2+ and GaH+2, with the reactivity of GaH+2 ions strongly dependent on the distance between stabilizing aluminum-occupied oxygen tetrahedra \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\].

                    The CH activation step is the RDS due to the energy required to break the strong C-H bond \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. The alkyl mechanism applies to different Ga species structures, with lower overall free energy for the 4-membered ring cluster Z4a compared to carbenium activation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\]. Alkene desorption is a rate-limiting step for the alkyl activation cycle, regardless of the catalytic site's activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\]. A novel one-step H2 and C2H4 desorption pathway from gallium alkyl intermediate has been reported, involving a cyclic transition state with an activation barrier of 53.5 kcal/mol, setting a lower limit of about 55 kcal/mol for the apparent activation barrier \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\].

                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The alkyl mechanism in PDH reactions on gallium-based catalysts presents a complex yet intriguing pathway for hydrocarbon activation. The RDS at the CH activation step highlights the need for catalysts that can efficiently break the strong C-H bond. Future research might focus on developing gallium-based catalysts with tailored Ga species structures, particularly Ga+, GaH2+, and GaH+2, to optimize the activation barrier for the alkyl mechanism. Understanding the role of stabilizing aluminum-occupied oxygen tetrahedra in the reactivity of GaH+2 ions could lead to the design of catalysts with enhanced activity for initial CH bond cleavage. Moreover, exploring the one-step H2 and C2H4 desorption pathway could provide insights into improving the rate-limiting step of alkene desorption, potentially enhancing the overall efficiency of PDH reactions.

                    在基于镓的催化剂上的PDH反应中，烷基机制是丙烷活化的主要途径，涉及形成烷基镓中间体，随后β-消除和丙烯和氢的解吸\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。该机制适用于各种Ga物种结构，包括Ga+、[GaH]+和Ga2O22+，其中CH活化步骤被提议为速率决定步骤（RDS），因为需要断裂强C-H键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。与促进氢重组的碳正离子机制不同，烷基机制激活丙烷中的CH键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。

                    烷基机制涉及镓作为烷基基团的受体，从Ga+阳离子通过间接杂裂机制氧化加成烷烃分子开始\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。烷基机制的活化能为193 kJ/mol，高于GaH2+物种的158 kJ/mol，但烷基机制的总体活化势垒较低\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。Ga+是最有利的Ga物种，其次是GaH2+和GaH+2，GaH+2离子的反应性强烈依赖于稳定铝占据的氧四面体之间的距离\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。

                    CH活化步骤是RDS，因为需要断裂强C-H键\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。烷基机制适用于不同的Ga物种结构，与碳正离子活化相比，4元环簇Z4a的总自由能较低\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\]。无论催化位点的活性如何，烯烃解吸都是烷基活化循环的速率限制步骤\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\]。从镓烷基中间体报道了一种新型的一步H2和C2H4解吸途径，涉及具有53.5 kcal/mol活化势垒的环过渡态，为表观活化势垒设定了约55 kcal/mol的下限\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.11.032\]。

                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：基于镓的催化剂上的PDH反应中的烷基机制呈现出一个复杂但引人入胜的烃活化途径。CH活化步骤的RDS突出了对能够有效断裂强C-H键的催化剂的需求。未来的研究可能会集中在开发具有定制Ga物种结构的镓基催化剂，特别是Ga+、GaH2+和GaH+2，以优化烷基机制的活化势垒。理解稳定铝占据的氧四面体在GaH+2离子反应性中的作用，可能会导致设计出初始CH键裂解活性增强的催化剂。此外，探索一步H2和C2H4解吸途径可能会提供改善烯烃解吸的速率限制步骤的见解，可能提高PDH反应的整体效率。
