                The alkyl mechanism in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions on gallium-based catalysts is a complex process involving several key steps from propane activation to product formation \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. Initially, propane is activated by a metal cation, forming an alkyl gallium intermediate, followed by β-elimination and desorption of propylene and hydrogen. This mechanism applies to various Ga species structures, including Ga+, [GaH]+, and Ga2O22+. The CH activation step and alkyl metal intermediate formation are proposed as the rate-determining step (RDS) due to the strong C-H bond breakage \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]. Unlike the carbenium mechanism, where Ga species facilitate hydrogen recombination, the alkyl mechanism activates the CH bond in propane \[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\].

                The alkyl mechanism is characterized by gallium acting as an acceptor of the alkyl group, starting with the oxidative addition of an alkane molecule to the Ga+ cation via an indirect heterolytic mechanism \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. This mechanism is preferred over the carbenium mechanism, which forms a carbenium ion and a hydride ion. The activation energy for the alkyl mechanism (193 kJ/mol) is higher than for GaH2+ species (158 kJ/mol), but the overall activation barrier for the alkyl mechanism (233 kJ/mol) is lower than for the carbenium mechanism \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]. The CH activation step is proposed as the RDS due to the strong C-H bond breakage \[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\].

                The alkyl mechanism contrasts with the carbenium mechanism in that it involves CH bond activation by [HGaH]+, forming [H2GaC2H5], followed by H2 removal and alkyl desorption \[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\]. The initial CH activation step, with a high activation barrier, is the RDS, leading to a low intrinsic reaction rate \[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\]. This contrasts with the carbenium mechanism, which has a lower activation barrier for CH activation and hydrogen removal \[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\].

                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The alkyl mechanism in PDH reactions on gallium-based catalysts presents a rich field for further exploration. The understanding of the RDS and the role of different Ga species structures opens avenues for the design of more efficient catalysts. Future research might focus on the optimization of Ga species structures to enhance the catalytic activity and selectivity. Additionally, the comparison with the carbenium mechanism highlights the importance of CH bond activation, suggesting that modifications to the catalyst structure could potentially lower the activation barrier for this step. Theoretical studies and computational modeling could play a crucial role in predicting the behavior of different Ga species and their impact on the reaction mechanism. Moreover, the investigation of novel gallium-based catalysts with tailored properties could lead to breakthroughs in PDH reactions, potentially improving the overall efficiency and economics of propylene production.

                在基于镓的催化剂上的丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中，烷基机制是一个复杂的过程，涉及从丙烷活化到产物形成的关键步骤\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。最初，丙烷通过金属阳离子活化，形成烷基镓中间体，随后是β-消除和丙烯和氢的脱附。这一机制适用于各种Ga物种结构，包括Ga+，[GaH]+和Ga2O22+。CH活化步骤和烷基金属中间体的形成被提议为决定速率的步骤（RDS），因为需要断裂强C-H键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。与碳正离子机制不同，其中Ga物种促进氢的重组，烷基机制激活丙烷中的CH键\[10.1002/advs.202207756_4\]。

                烷基机制的特点是镓作为烷基基团的受体，从通过间接异裂机制将烷烃分子氧化加合到Ga+阳离子开始\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。这一机制优于碳正离子机制，后者形成碳正离子和氢化物离子。烷基机制的活化能（193 kJ/mol）高于GaH2+物种（158 kJ/mol），但烷基机制的总活化能障（233 kJ/mol）低于碳正离子机制\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。CH活化步骤被提议为RDS，因为需要断裂强C-H键\[10.1016/j.jcat.2006.03.011\]。

                烷基机制与碳正离子机制的对比在于它涉及CH键通过[HGaH]+的活化，形成[H2GaC2H5]，随后是H2的去除和烷基脱附\[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\]。初始CH活化步骤，具有高活化能障，是RDS，导致较低的内在反应速率\[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\]。这与碳正离子机制形成对比，后者具有较低的CH活化和氢去除的活化能障\[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.04.017\]。

                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：基于镓的催化剂上的PDH反应中的烷基机制为更深入的探索提供了一个丰富的领域。对RDS的理解和不同Ga物种结构的作用为设计更有效的催化剂开辟了途径。未来的研究可能集中在优化Ga物种结构以增强催化活性和选择性。此外，与碳正离子机制的比较突出了CH键活化的重要性，表明催化剂结构的修改可能降低这一步骤的活化能障。理论研究和计算建模可能在预测不同Ga物种的行为及其对反应机制的影响方面发挥关键作用。此外，新型镓基催化剂的调查，具有定制的特性，可能导致PDH反应的突破，可能改善丙烯生产的整体效率和经济效益。
