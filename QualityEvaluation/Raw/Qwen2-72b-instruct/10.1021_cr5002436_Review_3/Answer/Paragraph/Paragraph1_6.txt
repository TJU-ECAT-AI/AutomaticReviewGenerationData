The development and characterization of vanadium oxide-based catalysts as alternatives for paraffin dehydrogenation have been a focal point of research since the 1980s, driven by the limitations of Pt and CrOx catalysts \[10.1039/c3cy01083j\]. Vanadium oxide catalysts, notably VOx/MCM-41 with isolated VOx species, have demonstrated stable catalytic performance in dehydrogenation (DH) and regeneration cycles, outperforming CrOx/MCM-41 and PtSn/Al2O3 \[10.1039/c3cy01083j\]. This stability is attributed to VOx species' resistance to agglomeration. Research has evolved, focusing on the complex chemistry of vanadium species, influenced by carrier type, support surface area, and metal loading \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. Techniques like TPR and Raman spectroscopy, especially using multiple excitation wavelengths, have advanced understanding of vanadium species \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. Isolated VOx species dominate at low surface densities, polyvanadates coexist at intermediate densities, and V2O5 forms at high densities \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. Catalyst re-oxidation rates increase with vanadium loading \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. Supported VOx catalysts are highly active for dehydrogenation reactions, with initial dehydrogenation activity rising with surface VOx density but decreasing over time for densities above 1.2 V/nm2 due to coke formation \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. Catalysts with densities no higher than 1.2 V/nm2 maintain stable activity \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. At higher pressures, catalysts with higher densities show enhanced activity \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. The structure of VOx species can be restored by oxidation of coke deposits up to 873 K, but only high-loaded samples are fully restored \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]. After regeneration, the surface area of an 8%V catalyst was reduced by 50%, indicating transformations in the support nature \[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\].

The quest for vanadium oxide-based catalysts as alternatives for paraffin dehydrogenation has evolved since the 1980s, driven by the need to address catalyst deactivation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]. Research highlights the role of carrier type, support surface area, and metal loading on vanadium species, which can exist as isolated monomeric vanadia, polyvanadate domains, or V2O5 crystallites \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]. The structure of dispersed vanadia changes with water vapor exposure, and its redox properties are crucial for reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]. The nature of vanadia species, whether isolated or polymeric, impacts catalytic activity and selectivity for carbon deposition and dehydrogenation reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\].

Driven by the limitations of Pt and CrOx catalysts, research on vanadium oxide-based alternatives for paraffin dehydrogenation has evolved since the 1980s \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]. Carrier type, support surface area, and metal loading significantly influence the nature of vanadium species, affecting catalytic activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]. Carrier type determines the formation of vanadium species, including monomeric or dimeric vanadates, VOx chains, vanadia overlayers, or V2O5 crystallites \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]. Support surface area and metal loading impact dispersion and redox properties, crucial for optimal catalyst performance \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\].

自1980年代以来，由于Pt和CrOx催化剂的局限性，对钒氧化物基催化剂的开发和表征作为石蜡脱氢的替代品已成为研究的焦点\[10.1039/c3cy01083j\]。特别是VOx/MCM-41催化剂，具有孤立的VOx物种，已在脱氢(DH)和再生循环中显示出稳定的催化性能，优于CrOx/MCM-41和PtSn/Al2O3\[10.1039/c3cy01083j\]。这种稳定性归因于VOx物种对聚结的抵抗。研究已经发展，专注于钒物种的复杂化学，受载体类型、支撑表面积和金属负载的影响\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。TPR和拉曼光谱技术，特别是使用多种激发波长，已经推进了对钒物种的理解\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。在低表面密度下，孤立的VOx物种占主导，中间密度下，聚钒酸盐共存，高密度下，形成V2O5\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。钒负载量增加时，催化剂的再氧化速率增加\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。支撑VOx催化剂在脱氢反应中高度活跃，初始脱氢活性随表面VOx密度的增加而增加，但对密度高于1.2 V/nm2的样品，由于焦炭形成，随时间显著下降\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。密度不超过1.2 V/nm2的催化剂保持稳定的活性\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。在较高压力下，高密度催化剂表现出增强的活性\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。VOx物种的结构可以通过氧化焦炭沉积物恢复至873 K，但只有高负载样品完全恢复\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。再生后，8%V催化剂的表面积减少了50%，表明支撑性质的转变\[10.1002/9783527626113.ch15\]。

自1980年代以来，对石蜡脱氢的钒氧化物基催化剂的探索，由解决催化剂失活的需求驱动，已经发展\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]。研究强调了载体类型、支撑表面积和金属负载对钒物种的作用，这些物种可以作为孤立的单体钒酸盐、聚钒酸盐域或V2O5晶体存在\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]。分散的钒酸盐结构随水蒸气暴露而变化，其氧化还原性质对反应至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]。钒酸盐物种的性质，无论是孤立的还是聚集体，影响碳沉积和脱氢反应的催化活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.07.015\]。

由于Pt和CrOx催化剂的局限性，对石蜡脱氢的钒氧化物基替代品的研究自1980年代以来已经发展\[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]。载体类型、支撑表面积和金属负载对钒物种的性质有重大影响，影响催化活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]。载体类型决定了钒物种的形成，包括单体或二聚体钒酸盐、VOx链、钒酸盐覆盖层或V2O5晶体\[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]。支撑表面积和金属负载影响分散性和氧化还原性质，对最佳催化剂性能至关重要\[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.05.017\]。
