        <English> 
                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction has been achieved through the strategic doping of active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. Additionally, the exposed surface of bulk boride materials can also serve as 2-D dispersed active sites, offering a significant advantage over linearly dispersed edge sites in terms of active site density, which can be substantially increased \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. This unique structural feature has attracted extensive research interest due to its potential to enhance catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements in developing 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction, there is a promising avenue to explore the optimization of hosting materials and the precise control of boron oxide/boron hydroxyl unit doping. Future research endeavors might focus on the synthesis of novel boride materials with enhanced surface properties and the investigation of their impact on active site density and catalytic activity. Moreover, the development of computational models to predict the catalytic performance of 2-D dispersed active sites could provide valuable insights into the design of more efficient boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction.

                通过在各种承载材料表面战略性地掺杂活性硼氧化物或硼羟基单元，开发了用于丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的硼基催化剂的2-D分散活性位点\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。此外，块体硼化物材料的暴露表面也可以作为2-D分散活性位点，与线性分散边缘位点相比，在活性位点密度方面具有显著优势，可以大幅提高\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这一独特的结构特征因其在ODHP反应中增强催化性能的潜力而引起了广泛的研究兴趣\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到在开发用于ODHP反应的硼基催化剂的2-D分散活性位点方面的进展，有希望探索承载材料的优化和硼氧化物/硼羟基单元掺杂的精确控制。未来的研究努力可能集中在新型硼化物材料的合成，以增强表面性能，以及研究其对活性位点密度和催化活性的影响。此外，开发预测2-D分散活性位点催化性能的计算模型，可以为设计更高效的硼基催化剂用于ODHP反应提供有价值的见解。
