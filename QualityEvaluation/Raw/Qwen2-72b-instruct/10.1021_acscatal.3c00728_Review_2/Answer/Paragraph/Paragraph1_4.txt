                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction has been achieved through the strategic doping of active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. Additionally, the exposed surface of bulk boride materials can also serve as 2-D dispersed active sites, offering a significant advantage over linearly dispersed edge sites in terms of active site density, which can be substantially increased \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. This unique structural feature has attracted extensive research interest due to its potential to enhance catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in developing 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction have opened up new avenues for enhancing catalytic performance. Given the potential for increased active site density, future research might focus on optimizing the synthesis methods to achieve higher dispersion and stability of these sites. Moreover, exploring the synergistic effects of combining different hosting materials with boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units could lead to the discovery of more efficient catalysts. The field is ripe for the integration of computational modeling to predict and design catalysts with tailored properties, potentially revolutionizing the propane dehydrogenation process.

                用于丙烷氧化脱氢反应(ODHP)的硼基催化剂中二维分散活性位点的开发是通过将活性硼氧化物或硼羟基单元战略性地掺杂到各种宿主材料的表面实现的\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。此外，块体硼化物材料的暴露表面也可以作为二维分散活性位点，与线性分散边缘位点相比，在活性位点密度方面具有显著优势，可以大幅提高\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这一独特的结构特征因其在ODHP反应中增强催化性能的潜力而引起了广泛的研究兴趣\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在硼基催化剂中开发用于ODHP反应的二维分散活性位点的进展为提高催化性能开辟了新的途径。鉴于活性位点密度增加的潜力，未来的研究可能会集中在优化合成方法，以实现更高的分散性和稳定性。此外，探索不同宿主材料与硼氧化物或硼羟基单元的协同效应，可能会发现更有效的催化剂。该领域正适合整合计算建模，以预测和设计具有定制特性的催化剂，这可能彻底改变丙烷脱氢过程。
