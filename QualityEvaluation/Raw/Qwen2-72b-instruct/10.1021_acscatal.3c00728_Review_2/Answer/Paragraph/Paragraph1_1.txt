        <English> 
                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction represents a significant advancement in catalytic technology \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. These sites are engineered by doping active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials, or by utilizing the exposed surface of bulk boride materials as 2-D dispersed active sites. This approach markedly enhances the density of active sites compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, thereby improving catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. The unique structural features of these 2-D dispersed active sites have garnered extensive research interest due to their potential to revolutionize the efficiency and selectivity of propane dehydrogenation catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The innovative development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction opens up new avenues for enhancing catalytic performance and efficiency. Future research should focus on optimizing the synthesis methods to achieve higher density and stability of these active sites, exploring the use of novel hosting materials that can further improve the catalytic activity, and investigating the underlying mechanisms that govern the superior performance of 2-D dispersed active sites over linearly dispersed edge sites. Additionally, the integration of computational modeling with experimental studies could provide deeper insights into the structure-performance relationship, guiding the rational design of next-generation propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

                综合理解与未来展望: 用于ODHP反应的硼基催化剂中二维分散活性位点的创新开发为提高催化性能和效率开辟了新的途径。未来的研究应集中在优化合成方法以实现更高的活性位点密度和稳定性，探索使用可以进一步提高催化活性的新宿主材料，以及研究二维分散活性位点相对于线性分散边缘位点的优越性能的潜在机制。此外，将计算建模与实验研究相结合，可以为结构-性能关系提供更深入的见解，指导下一代丙烷脱氢催化剂的理性设计。
