                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction has emerged as a significant advancement in the field of catalysis \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. These sites are engineered by doping active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials, and the exposed surface of bulk boride materials can also serve as 2-D dispersed active sites. Compared with linearly dispersed edge sites, the density of active sites can be substantially increased, offering a significant advantage in terms of catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. This unique structural feature has attracted extensive research interest due to its potential to enhance catalytic activity in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The strategic development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts has opened new avenues for enhancing catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction. Given the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore the synthesis of novel boron nanomaterials with optimized structural features for improved active site density. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of advanced doping techniques to tailor the surface properties of hosting materials, aiming for higher catalytic efficiency and selectivity. Moreover, the integration of computational modeling with experimental studies could provide deeper insights into the reaction mechanisms on 2-D dispersed active sites, guiding the design of next-generation catalysts for propane dehydrogenation.

                综合理解与未来展望: 2-D分散活性位点在硼基催化剂中的战略性开发为提高ODHP反应的催化性能开辟了新的途径。鉴于文件中讨论的进展和发现，有机会探索新型硼纳米材料的合成，以优化结构特征，提高活性位点密度。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发先进的掺杂技术，以定制宿主材料的表面性质，旨在提高催化效率和选择性。此外，将计算建模与实验研究相结合，可以更深入地了解2-D分散活性位点上的反应机制，指导下一代丙烷脱氢催化剂的设计。
