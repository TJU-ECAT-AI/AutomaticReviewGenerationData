                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction has been achieved through the strategic doping of active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. Additionally, the exposed surface of bulk boride materials can also serve as 2-D dispersed active sites, offering a significant advantage over linearly dispersed edge sites in terms of active site density, which can be substantially increased \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. This unique structural feature has attracted extensive research interest due to its potential to enhance catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements in the development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction, there is a promising avenue to explore the optimization of these catalysts for industrial-scale applications. Future research endeavors might focus on the synthesis of novel boron-based materials with enhanced stability and selectivity, leveraging the unique structural features of 2-D dispersed active sites. Moreover, the investigation of the underlying mechanisms that govern the catalytic performance of these materials could provide insights into the design of more efficient catalysts for the ODHP reaction. The potential for integrating these catalysts into existing petrochemical processes, thereby improving the overall efficiency and sustainability, is a compelling direction for future studies.

                通过在各种承载材料表面战略性地掺杂活性硼氧化物或硼羟基单元，开发了用于丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的硼基催化剂的2-D分散活性位点\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。此外，块体硼化物材料的暴露表面也可以作为2-D分散活性位点，与线性分散边缘位点相比，在活性位点密度方面具有显著优势，可以大幅增加\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这一独特的结构特征因其在ODHP反应中增强催化性能的潜力而引起了广泛的研究兴趣\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到在硼基催化剂的ODHP反应中2-D分散活性位点的发展进展，有希望探索这些催化剂在工业规模应用中的优化。未来的研究努力可能集中在合成具有增强稳定性和选择性的新型硼基材料上，利用2-D分散活性位点的独特结构特征。此外，研究控制这些材料催化性能的潜在机制，可以为设计更有效的ODHP反应催化剂提供见解。将这些催化剂整合到现有的石化过程中，从而提高整体效率和可持续性，是一个引人注目的未来研究方向。
