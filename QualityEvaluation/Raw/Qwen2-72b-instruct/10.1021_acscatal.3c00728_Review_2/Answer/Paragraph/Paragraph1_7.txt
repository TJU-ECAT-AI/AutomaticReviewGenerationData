                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction has been achieved through the strategic doping of active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. Additionally, the exposed surface of bulk boride materials can also function as 2-D dispersed active sites, offering a significant advantage over linearly dispersed edge sites in terms of active site density, which can be substantially increased \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. This unique structural feature has attracted extensive research interest due to its potential to enhance catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in developing 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction have opened new avenues for catalytic efficiency enhancement. Given the potential for increased active site density and improved catalytic performance, future research might focus on the synthesis of novel boron-based materials with optimized structural features. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects of combining boron with other elements could lead to the discovery of more efficient catalysts for the ODHP reaction. The development of advanced characterization techniques to better understand the active site distribution and its impact on catalytic activity is also a promising direction for future studies.

                用于丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的硼基催化剂中2-D分散活性位点的开发是通过将活性硼氧化物或硼羟基单元战略性地掺杂到各种宿主材料的表面实现的\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。此外，块体硼化物材料的暴露表面也可以作为2-D分散活性位点，与线性分散边缘位点相比，在活性位点密度方面具有显著优势，可以大幅增加\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这一独特的结构特征因其在ODHP反应中增强催化性能的潜力而引起了广泛的研究兴趣\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在硼基催化剂中开发用于ODHP反应的2-D分散活性位点的进展为提高催化效率开辟了新的途径。鉴于活性位点密度增加和催化性能改善的潜力，未来的研究可能会集中在合成具有优化结构特征的新型硼基材料上。此外，探索硼与其他元素的协同效应可能会发现更高效的ODHP反应催化剂。开发先进的表征技术以更好地理解活性位点分布及其对催化活性的影响，也是未来研究的一个有希望的方向。
