                The development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the Oxidative Dehydrogenation of Propane (ODHP) reaction represents a significant advancement in catalytic technology \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. These sites are engineered by doping active boron oxide or boron hydroxyl units onto the surface of various hosting materials, or by utilizing the exposed surface of bulk boride materials as 2-D dispersed active sites. This approach offers a substantial increase in active site density compared to linearly dispersed edge sites, thereby enhancing the catalytic performance in the ODHP reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]. The unique structural features of these 2-D dispersed active sites have garnered extensive research interest due to their potential to revolutionize the efficiency and selectivity of propane dehydrogenation catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The innovative development of 2-D dispersed active sites in boron-based catalysts for the ODHP reaction has opened new avenues for enhancing catalytic performance and efficiency. Building upon the foundational research, future directions might focus on the optimization of hosting materials to further increase active site density and stability. Additionally, the exploration of novel boron-based nanomaterials with enhanced structural features could lead to the discovery of more efficient catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. The integration of computational modeling and experimental techniques will be crucial for understanding the underlying mechanisms of these catalysts and guiding the design of next-generation materials. Moreover, the investigation of the role of 2-D dispersed active sites in other catalytic reactions could broaden the application scope of boron-based catalysts in the chemical industry.

                用于丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的硼基催化剂中二维分散活性位点的开发代表了催化技术的重大进步\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这些位点通过在各种宿主材料表面掺杂活性硼氧化物或硼羟基单元，或利用大块硼化物材料的暴露表面作为二维分散活性位点来构建。与线性分散边缘位点相比，这种方法提供了活性位点密度的显著提高，从而增强了ODHP反应中的催化性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。这些二维分散活性位点的独特结构特征因其有可能彻底改变丙烷脱氢催化剂的效率和选择性而引起了广泛的研究兴趣\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_2\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 用于ODHP反应的硼基催化剂中二维分散活性位点的创新开发为提高催化性能和效率开辟了新的途径。在基础研究的基础上，未来方向可能集中在优化宿主材料以进一步增加活性位点密度和稳定性。此外，探索具有增强结构特征的新型硼基纳米材料可能会发现更有效的丙烷脱氢催化剂。计算建模和实验技术的整合对于理解这些催化剂的潜在机制以及指导下一代材料的设计至关重要。此外，研究二维分散活性位点在其他催化反应中的作用可能会扩大硼基催化剂在化工行业的应用范围。
