The escalating demand for propylene, forecasted to reach 191 megatons by 2030 \[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\], has catalyzed the adoption of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a pivotal production method \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, central to PDH, have been a subject of scientific debate regarding the active Ga species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]. Recent studies have shown that Ga speciation and PDH performance are influenced by Ga/Al ratios and Al distribution in zeolites \[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\], contributing to our understanding of these catalysts. The active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH remains a topic of debate, with recent studies pinpointing [GaH]2+ and a basic framework oxygen as critical for PDH reactions \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. The debate over active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts persists, with recent insights highlighting Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted acid pairs as pivotal for propane dehydrogenation, optimizing CH bond activation and hydrogen elimination \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\].

The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved, with a focus on active Ga species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]. Recent studies have identified coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ cations as key active species \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. The nature of active sites, including Lewis and Brnsted acid sites, remains a topic of discussion. Research on CO2's role in enhancing olefin yields and the benefits of promoters like potassium in reducing coke formation and increasing selectivity has advanced our understanding \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. Studies also explore the promotion of GaOx-based catalysts with metals such as Pt to aid hydrogen desorption and active site regeneration, reflecting ongoing efforts to optimize PDH processes \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

The active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH remains a subject of debate, with recent studies pinpointing [GaH]2+ and a basic framework oxygen at the Brnsted-acid site as active sites for PDH reactions \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. The debate over active Ga species in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts continues, with recent research highlighting the importance of Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted acid pairs for propane dehydrogenation, where heightened Lewis acidity aids CH bond activation and Brnsted acidity reduces the barrier for hydrogen elimination \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in understanding Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH, particularly the active Ga species, have opened new avenues for research and innovation. Future directions might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can further enhance the efficiency and selectivity of PDH processes. The ongoing debate over active Ga species suggests that there is room for deeper exploration into the nature of these sites and their role in PDH. The potential for synergistic effects between Ga species and other metal promoters, such as Pt, could be a promising area for investigation, aiming to optimize hydrogen desorption and active site regeneration. Additionally, the role of CO2 in enhancing olefin yields and the benefits of promoters like potassium in reducing coke formation and increasing selectivity could be further explored to improve catalyst performance and longevity. The development of GaOx-based catalysts with enhanced dispersion and stability, as well as the investigation of alternative supports for gallium species, could also contribute to the optimization of PDH processes. The integration of computational methods, such as DFT calculations, with experimental techniques could provide a more comprehensive understanding of the active sites and reaction mechanisms in PDH, guiding the design of more efficient catalysts. The exploration of renewable feedstocks for PDH, such as bio-propane, could also be a promising direction for future research, aligning with the principles of green chemistry and sustainability.

随着丙烯需求的激增，预计到2030年将达到191百万吨\[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\]，丙烷脱氢（PDH）作为关键生产方法的采用得到了催化\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂，作为PDH的核心，关于活性Ga物种的科学争论持续不断\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。最近的研究表明，Ga的物种和PDH性能受Ga/Al比率和沸石中Al分布的影响\[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\]，这增进了我们对这些催化剂的理解。Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中活性Ga物种的讨论仍在继续，最近的研究指出[GaH]2+和基本框架氧是PDH反应的关键\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。关于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中活性Ga物种的争论持续，最近的见解强调了Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted酸对在丙烷脱氢中的关键作用，优化了CH键的激活和氢的消除\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。

对于PDH的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的理解已经发展，重点放在活性Ga物种上\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。最近的研究确定了配位不饱和的Ga3+阳离子作为关键的活性物种\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。活性位点的性质，包括Lewis和Brnsted酸位点，仍然是讨论的话题。关于CO2在提高烯烃产量中的作用以及像钾这样的促进剂在减少焦炭形成和提高选择性方面的益处的研究增进了我们的理解\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。研究还探讨了使用金属如Pt促进GaOx基催化剂，以帮助氢的解吸和活性位点的再生，这反映了持续优化PDH过程的努力\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中活性Ga物种的讨论仍在继续，最近的研究指出[GaH]2+和Brnsted酸位点的基本框架氧是PDH反应的活性位点\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。关于Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中活性Ga物种的争论持续，最近的研究强调了Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted酸对在丙烷脱氢中的重要性，其中增强的Lewis酸性有助于CH键的激活，而Brnsted酸性降低了氢消除的障碍\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：关于PDH的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂，特别是活性Ga物种的理解进展，为研究和创新开辟了新的途径。未来的方向可能集中在开发可以进一步提高PDH过程效率和选择性的新型催化剂上。关于活性Ga物种的持续争论表明，对这些位点的性质及其在PDH中的作用进行更深入的探索还有空间。Ga物种与Pt等其他金属促进剂之间的潜在协同效应可能是一个有前景的研究领域，旨在优化氢的解吸和活性位点的再生。此外，CO2在提高烯烃产量中的作用以及像钾这样的促进剂在减少焦炭形成和提高选择性方面的益处可以进一步探索，以改善催化剂的性能和寿命。开发分散性和稳定性增强的GaOx基催化剂，以及对镓物种的替代载体的调查，也可能有助于PDH过程的优化。将DFT计算等计算方法与实验技术相结合，可以对活性位点和PDH中的反应机制提供更全面的理解，指导更有效催化剂的设计。探索PDH的可再生原料，如生物丙烷，也可能是一个有前景的研究方向，与绿色化学和可持续性的原则相一致。
