Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, central to PDH, have been in use since the 1970s for hydrocarbon processing \[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\]. The identity of the active Ga species in these catalysts remains a subject of debate, with recent research pinpointing [GaH]2+ and a basic framework oxygen as active sites \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. The debate on active Ga species persists, with recent studies highlighting the significance of Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted acid pairs in propane dehydrogenation \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]. Enhanced Lewis acidity facilitates CH bond activation, and Brnsted acidity reduces the barrier for hydrogen elimination \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]. Under reaction conditions, Ga+3 species reduce to monomeric hydride forms, which release H2 during propane dehydrocyclodimerization \[10.1006/jcat.1993.1079\]. Reduced Ga species reoxidize to Ga+3 at room temperature in H2, indicating that active Ga forms are present only under reaction conditions \[10.1006/jcat.1993.1079\].

H-ZSM5沸石中的Ga物种催化脱氢，促进H2的形成并抑制裂化\[10.1006/jcat.1993.1079\]。对于PDH的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的理解已经发展，关于活性Ga物种的争论\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。最近的研究表明，Ga物种化和PDH性能受到Ga/Al比率和沸石中Al分布的影响\[10.1002/advs.202207756_0\]。PDH中的活性Ga物种仍然是科学争论的主题，提出了各种Ga形式\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。最近的发现提出了[GaH]2+作为活性中心，独立于Ga/Al比率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。Ga+H+配对位点被报告为比单个Ga+位点更活跃\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。

对于PDH的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的活性Ga物种的理解仍然是科学争论的主题\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。最近的研究指出了[GaH]2+和Brnsted酸位点的基本框架氧对于PDH反应至关重要\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。对于PDH的Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的活性Ga物种的理解仍然是科学争论的主题\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。最近的研究强调了Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted酸对作为丙烷脱氢的活性位点\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。增强的Lewis酸性促进了异裂CH键的活化，而Brnsted酸性降低了氢消除的障碍\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。
