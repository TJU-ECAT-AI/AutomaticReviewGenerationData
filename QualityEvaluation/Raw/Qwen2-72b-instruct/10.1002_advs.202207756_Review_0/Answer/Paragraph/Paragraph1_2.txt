        <English> 
                The escalating demand for propylene, forecasted to grow at 2-3% annually until 2035, has catalyzed the development of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a pivotal production method \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]. PDH, alongside MTO and Fischer-Tropsch processes, offers a promising alternative to conventional methods. Central to PDH are Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, whose active Ga species have been a subject of ongoing debate. Recent studies highlight [GaH]2+ ions and a basic framework oxygen as critical active sites, more active than isolated Ga+ \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. Trace Pt modification has been shown to enhance catalytic efficiency by facilitating hydrogen desorption and recovering gallium hydride species \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]. DFT calculations reveal that Zrcus cations in ZrO2 lower activation barriers for H2 and C3H6 formation \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]. The active site of Ga2O3 is identified as the Lewis acid site provided by coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]. These findings contribute to our evolving understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in PDH.
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in understanding Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH, particularly the identification of [GaH]2+ ions and a basic framework oxygen as active sites, open avenues for further optimization. Future research could focus on developing more efficient catalysts by exploring the synergistic effects of different metals with Ga, such as Pt, to enhance hydrogen desorption and active site regeneration. Additionally, the role of Zrcus cations in ZrO2 in lowering activation barriers for H2 and C3H6 formation suggests a potential for developing novel catalysts with tailored active sites. DFT calculations and computational studies could be employed to predict and design catalysts with enhanced activity and selectivity. Moreover, the investigation of Ga2O3's Lewis acid site provided by coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ could lead to the development of new catalysts with optimized Lewis acidity for improved PDH performance. The evolving landscape of propylene production, driven by the growing demand for propylene, presents opportunities for innovative catalyst design and process optimization to meet the increasing global demand.

                综合理解与未来展望：在PDH中对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的理解进展，特别是[GaH]2+离子和基本框架氧作为活性位点的识别，为进一步优化开辟了途径。未来的研究可以集中在通过探索不同金属与Ga的协同效应，如Pt，来增强氢解吸和活性位点再生，以开发更有效的催化剂。此外，ZrO2中Zrcus阳离子在降低H2和C3H6形成活化能方面的作用表明了开发具有定制活性位点的新型催化剂的潜力。可以利用DFT计算和计算研究来预测和设计具有增强活性和选择性的催化剂。此外，对由配位不饱和Ga3+提供的Ga2O3的路易斯酸位点的调查可能导致开发具有优化路易斯酸性的新型催化剂，以改善PDH性能。由丙烯需求增长驱动的丙烯生产格局的演变，为创新催化剂设计和过程优化以满足全球日益增长的需求提供了机会。
