1.The growing demand for propylene has driven the rapid application and development of propane dehydrogenation technologies. The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved, with ongoing debate about the active Ga species. Recent research has identified Lewis(Ga+)-Brnsted acid pairs as the active sites for propane dehydrogenation over Ga/H-ZSM-5. The increased Lewis acidity facilitates heterolytic CH bond activation, and Brnsted acidity reduces the activation barrier for the elimination of hydrogen.
