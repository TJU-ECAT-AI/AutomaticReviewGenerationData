<English>
The utilization of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) has garnered significant attention due to its ability to enhance propylene selectivity compared to traditional oxidants [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. N2O's role in ODHP is multifaceted, as it slows the reoxidation of vanadium oxides (VOx), thereby reducing the formation of active oxygen species and curtailing the conversion of propene to carbon oxides [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. This effect is particularly pronounced when the vanadium coverage is below a monolayer, with selectivity plummeting as V2O5 nanocrystallites dominate [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. Non-lattice oxygen from O2 is more conducive to COx formation than N2O, which aids in maintaining higher propene selectivity [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. 

When employing iron-zeolites as catalysts in propane ODHP, N2O's monooxygen role and iron's reactive oxygen coordination have been shown to boost propene selectivity, achieving yields comparable to top-performing vanadium and molybdenum catalysts [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. However, coke-induced deactivation, particularly in Fe-beta zeolites, significantly curbs the yield [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. Deactivation stems from the loss of active sites, a decline in site quality, and restricted pore access [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 

In situ FTIR spectroscopy and temperature-programmed oxidation mass spectrometry (TEOMGC) have been instrumental in gaining insights into deactivation mechanisms [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]]. Advanced research is imperative to address the limitations of N2O-ODHP, focusing on catalyst stability and deactivation [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]].

※※※

Integrative Understanding and Prospective Outlook: The use of N2O in ODHP presents a promising avenue for enhancing propylene selectivity, yet the challenges of catalyst deactivation and stability remain significant hurdles. To advance the practical implementation of N2O-ODHP, future research should concentrate on developing catalysts with enhanced stability and resistance to coke formation. This could involve the synthesis of novel catalysts with tailored active sites and improved pore structures to prevent the accumulation of coke [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 

Moreover, the optimization of reaction conditions, such as temperature and N2O concentration, is crucial for maximizing propylene yield while minimizing deactivation [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. Advanced in situ spectroscopic techniques, such as FTIR and TEOMGC, should be employed to gain deeper insights into the deactivation mechanisms and guide the design of more robust catalysts [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]]. 

Additionally, the exploration of alternative regeneration methods, such as the use of N2O for partial regeneration and oxygen for full activity restoration, could offer a pathway to mitigate catalyst deactivation [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028\]]. Lastly, the development of cost-effective and scalable N2O sources is essential for the economic viability of N2O-ODHP [\[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005\]]. By addressing these challenges, N2O-ODHP could potentially become a more feasible and sustainable alternative for propylene production.

<Chinese>
N2O在丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)中的应用因其能显著提高丙烯选择性而受到广泛关注，与传统氧化剂相比，N2O具有独特优势 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. N2O在ODHP中的作用是多方面的，它能减缓钒氧化物(VOx)的再氧化，从而减少活性氧物种的形成，抑制丙烯转化为碳氧化物 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. 当钒覆盖度低于单层时，这种效果尤为显著，随着V2O5纳米晶体的主导，选择性会急剧下降 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. 非晶格氧从O2更有利于COx的形成，而N2O则有助于维持更高的丙烯选择性 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. 

在丙烷ODHP中使用铁沸石作为催化剂时，N2O的单氧作用和铁的反应性氧配位已被证明能提高丙烯选择性，其产量可与顶级的钒和钼催化剂相媲美 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 然而，焦炭诱导的失活，特别是在Fe-beta沸石中，显著降低了产量 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 失活源于活性位点的损失、位点质量的下降和孔隙通道的受限 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 

原位FTIR光谱和温度程序氧化质谱(TPOMS)在获得失活机制的见解方面发挥了重要作用 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]]. 为了应对N2O-ODHP的局限性，迫切需要进行先进的研究，重点放在催化剂稳定性和失活问题上 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]].

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：N2O在ODHP中的应用为提高丙烯选择性提供了一条有前景的途径，但催化剂失活和稳定性的问题仍然是重大障碍。为了推进N2O-ODHP的实际应用，未来的研究应集中于开发具有增强稳定性和抗焦炭形成能力的催化剂。这可能涉及合成新型催化剂，具有定制的活性位点和改进的孔结构，以防止焦炭的积累 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. 

此外，优化反应条件，如温度和N2O浓度，对于在最小化失活的同时最大化丙烯产量至关重要 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. 应用先进的原位光谱技术，如FTIR和TPOMS，以获得对失活机制的更深入理解，并指导更坚固催化剂的设计 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]]. 

此外，探索替代的再生方法，如使用N2O进行部分再生和氧气进行完全活性恢复，可能为缓解催化剂失活提供一条途径 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028\]]. 最后，开发成本效益高且可扩展的N2O来源对于N2O-ODHP的经济可行性至关重要 [\[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005\]]. 通过解决这些挑战，N2O-ODHP有可能成为一种更可行和可持续的丙烯生产替代方案。
