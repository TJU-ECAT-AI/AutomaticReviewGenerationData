<English>
The utilization of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) has garnered significant attention due to its ability to enhance propylene selectivity compared to traditional oxidants [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. N2O's role in slowing the reoxidation of vanadium oxides (VOx) reduces the formation of active oxygen species, thereby curtailing the conversion of propene to carbon oxides (COx) [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. This effect is particularly evident across various catalysts, where the selectivity for propene is maintained at lower monolayer vanadium coverage, while it drops significantly with the presence of V2O5 nanocrystallites, a condition that N2O helps mitigate [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. Non-lattice oxygen from O2 is known to promote more COx formation compared to N2O [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. The use of N2O halves propane conversion while doubling propene selectivity at 10% conversion, a critical factor for ODHP [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. However, the challenges associated with catalyst deactivation and stability remain significant hurdles for the practical implementation of N2O-ODHP [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.10.020\], \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_8\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.09.043\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.11.028\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\], \[10.1016/S0926-860X(03)00103-0\], \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032\], \[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.11.021\], \[10.1021/jp070747c\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028\]].

Catalyst deactivation, often exacerbated by the formation of coke, particularly in iron-based zeolites (Fe-zeolites), significantly impacts the yield and efficiency of N2O-ODHP [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]. The deactivation stems from the loss of active sites, degradation of site quality, and reduced pore access [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]. Advanced research is essential to address these limitations and make N2O-ODHP a more viable option for industrial application [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]].

※※※

Integrative Understanding and Prospective Outlook: The use of N2O in ODHP presents a promising avenue for enhancing propylene selectivity, yet the challenges of catalyst deactivation and stability must be overcome for its practical implementation. Advanced research focusing on the development of coke-resistant catalysts, optimization of reaction conditions, and understanding the mechanisms of deactivation and regeneration is crucial [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]. Future research endeavors might concentrate on the design of novel catalysts with enhanced stability and selectivity, the exploration of alternative oxidants that complement N2O, and the development of efficient regeneration methods to mitigate deactivation [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]. Additionally, in-depth studies on the nature of oxygen species from O2 and N2O on VOx/MCM-41 are required to optimize catalyst performance and stability [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]. The integration of advanced spectroscopy and analysis techniques could provide insights into the real-time monitoring of deactivation and regeneration processes, facilitating the development of more robust catalysts for N2O-ODHP [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]].

<Chinese>
N2O在丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)中作为氧化剂的应用因其能显著提高丙烯选择性而受到广泛关注，与传统氧化剂相比，N2O能减缓钒氧化物(VOx)的再氧化，减少活性氧物种的形成，从而抑制丙烯转化为碳氧化物(COx) [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]。这一效应在各种催化剂中尤为明显，丙烯的选择性在较低的单层钒覆盖下得以保持，而在V2O5纳米晶粒存在时显著下降，N2O有助于缓解这一情况 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]。非晶格氧从O2促进更多COx的形成，而N2O则不然 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]。N2O将丙烷转化率减半，同时将丙烯选择性提高一倍，在10%转化率时，这是ODHP的关键因素 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]。然而，催化剂失活和稳定性问题仍然是N2O-ODHP实际应用的重大障碍 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.10.020\], \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_8\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.09.043\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2005.11.028\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\], \[10.1016/S0926-860X(03)00103-0\], \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032\], \[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.11.021\], \[10.1021/jp070747c\], \[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028\]]。

催化剂失活，通常由铁基沸石(Fe-沸石)中焦炭的形成加剧，严重影响N2O-ODHP的产率和效率 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]。失活源于活性位点的损失、位点质量的退化和孔隙通路的减少 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]]。为了使N2O-ODHP成为工业应用的更可行选择，进行高级研究以解决这些限制至关重要 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：N2O在ODHP中的应用为提高丙烯选择性开辟了一条有希望的途径，但必须克服催化剂失活和稳定性的挑战，才能实现其实际应用。高级研究应侧重于开发抗焦炭催化剂、优化反应条件以及理解失活和再生机制，这是至关重要的 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]。未来的研究工作可能集中在设计具有增强稳定性和选择性的新型催化剂、探索补充N2O的替代氧化剂以及开发有效的再生方法以缓解失活 [\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\], \[10.1021/ie0493863\]]。此外，对VOx/MCM-41上O2和N2O氧物种性质的深入研究对于优化催化剂性能和稳定性至关重要 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]]。集成高级光谱学和分析技术可以提供对失活和再生过程实时监测的见解，促进N2O-ODHP更坚固催化剂的开发 [\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]]。
