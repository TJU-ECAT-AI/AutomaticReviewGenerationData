<English>
The utilization of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) has garnered significant attention due to its ability to boost propylene selectivity compared to traditional oxidants like molecular oxygen (O2) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. N2O's slower reoxidation of vanadia (VOx) species curbs the formation of carbon oxides (COx), thereby enhancing propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. This is particularly evident when using iron-zeolites and vanadium oxides as catalysts, where N2O outperforms O2 in propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. However, the use of N2O is not without challenges. Catalyst deactivation, primarily due to coke formation, is a significant hurdle that limits the practical implementation of N2O-ODHP \[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]. Fe-zeolites, while effective in N2O-ODHP, are prone to deactivation by coke, which can hinder propylene yield \[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]. The rate of deactivation varies, impacting the quality and accessibility of active sites \[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]. In situ studies, such as those employing temperature-programmed oxidation mass spectrometry (TEOM) and Fourier transform infrared spectroscopy (FTIR), have been instrumental in monitoring deactivation and understanding the mechanisms of coke formation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]. However, there is a need for advanced research to address the limitations of catalyst stability and reusability in N2O-ODHP \[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The use of N2O in ODHP presents a promising avenue for enhancing propylene selectivity, but the challenges of catalyst deactivation and stability must be addressed for practical implementation. Advanced research is needed to develop coke-resistant catalysts, optimize regeneration techniques, and understand the fundamental mechanisms of deactivation. Exploring the synergistic effects of N2O and O2 could potentially lead to the development of more stable and selective catalysts. Additionally, the investigation of alternative oxidants and the development of cost-effective N2O sources could further enhance the feasibility of N2O-ODHP. Future research endeavors might focus on the design of catalysts that can withstand the harsh conditions of N2O-ODHP while maintaining high propylene selectivity and stability over extended periods. The integration of in situ studies with advanced computational methods could provide deeper insights into the deactivation mechanisms, guiding the development of more robust catalysts. Moreover, the exploration of novel regeneration techniques, such as the use of steam or other mild oxidants, could mitigate the effects of deactivation and enhance catalyst reusability. The optimization of N2O concentration and the synergistic use of O2 could also be explored to maximize propylene selectivity while minimizing catalyst deactivation. Ultimately, the realization of N2O-ODHP as a viable industrial process will require a multidisciplinary approach, combining insights from catalysis, materials science, and chemical engineering.

<Chinese>
N2O在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）中的应用因其能显著提高丙烯选择性而受到广泛关注，与传统的氧化剂如氧气（O2）相比，N2O的VOx物种的较慢再氧化抑制了碳氧化物（COx）的形成，从而增强了丙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。特别是在使用铁沸石和钒氧化物作为催化剂时，N2O在丙烯选择性方面优于O2\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。然而，N2O的使用并非没有挑战。催化剂失活，主要是由于焦炭形成，是限制N2O-ODHP实际应用的重大障碍\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]。虽然Fe-沸石在N2O-ODHP中有效，但它们容易因焦炭而失活，这可能阻碍丙烯产量\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]。失活速率的变化影响了活性位点的质量和可及性\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035\]。原位研究，如采用温度编程氧化质谱（TEOM）和傅里叶变换红外光谱（FTIR）的监测，对于监控失活和理解焦炭形成机制至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]。然而，需要进行高级研究以解决N2O-ODHP中催化剂稳定性和可重用性的限制\[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：N2O在ODHP中的应用为提高丙烯选择性提供了一条有希望的途径，但必须解决催化剂失活和稳定性问题，以实现实际应用。需要进行高级研究来开发抗焦炭催化剂，优化再生技术，并理解失活的基本机制。探索N2O和O2的协同效应可能有助于开发更稳定和选择性的催化剂。此外，研究替代氧化剂和开发成本效益高的N2O来源可以进一步增强N2O-ODHP的可行性。未来的研究工作可能集中在设计能够在N2O-ODHP的恶劣条件下保持高丙烯选择性和稳定性的催化剂。将原位研究与先进的计算方法相结合，可以提供对失活机制的更深入理解，指导更坚固催化剂的开发。此外，探索新型再生技术，如使用蒸汽或其他温和氧化剂，可以缓解失活的影响，增强催化剂的可重用性。N2O浓度的优化和O2的协同使用也可以探索，以最大化丙烯选择性，同时最小化催化剂失活。最终，将N2O-ODHP作为可行的工业过程的实现将需要一个多学科的方法，结合催化、材料科学和化学工程的见解。
