                    The diverse properties and oxidation states of niobium oxides significantly influence their behavior as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. At low Nb content (3-7%), the apparent activation energy and intrinsic rate of ethane conversion decrease, enhancing catalytic sites' activity but reducing their number, leading to increased ethylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Beyond 15% Nb, the inactive NiNb2O6 phase forms, decreasing active phase content \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Ni0.85Nb0.15O yields optimal results due to reduced active phase particle size, preventing sintering \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Over time, all catalysts deactivate due to NiNb2O6 phase formation and reduced oxygen over-stoichiometry, eliminating active Ospecies \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Catalyst regeneration attempts have been unsuccessful \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Niobium oxides affect NiO catalysts for ethane ODH by altering redox properties, reducing NiO crystallite size, and enhancing selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. At Nb content >5 at.%, NbNiO composites are less active but more selective for ethane ODH, likely due to the Nb-rich phase covering unselective active oxygen species of NiO \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. High Nb loadings lead to significant catalyst deactivation, while 34 at.% Nb content yields the most efficient ODH catalysts with high activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Optimizing the preparation method could further improve NiO-catalyzed ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Challenges arise in reconciling theoretical predictions with experimental observations due to the complex interplay between niobium oxide properties and catalyst behavior \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The intricate role of niobium oxides in enhancing ethane ODH activity and selectivity in NiO catalysts presents a fertile ground for future research. The optimal doping level of niobium, around 15%, offers a balance between activity and selectivity, with the potential for further optimization through advanced preparation methods \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The challenges in catalyst regeneration and the formation of inactive phases suggest a need for innovative regeneration strategies and the development of more stable niobium-doped NiO catalysts \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The complex interplay between theoretical predictions and experimental observations highlights the importance of developing more accurate computational models and experimental techniques to better understand the catalytic behavior of niobium-doped NiO catalysts \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Future research might focus on the detailed characterization of surface morphology and the development of model systems to bridge the gap between theoretical predictions and experimental observations \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]. Additionally, exploring the effects of other dopants and their interactions with niobium could lead to the discovery of new catalysts with enhanced performance for ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. The field is exploratory, with promising results and much work remaining \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\].

                    钽氧化物的多样性质和氧化态显著影响其在NiO催化剂中作为乙烷氧化脱氢(ODH)助剂的行为\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在低Nb含量(3-7%)时，乙烷转化的表观活化能和内在速率降低，提高了催化位点的活性但减少了位点数量，导致乙烯选择性增加\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。超过15%的Nb，形成不活跃的NiNb2O6相，减少了活性相含量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。Ni0.85Nb0.15O由于减少了活性相粒子尺寸，防止了烧结，因此产生了最佳结果\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。随着时间的推移，所有催化剂由于NiNb2O6相的形成和氧过量减少，消除了活性O物种而失活\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。催化剂再生尝试未能成功\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。钽氧化物影响NiO催化剂用于乙烷ODH，通过改变氧化还原性质，减少NiO晶粒尺寸，提高选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。在Nb含量>5 at.%时，NbNiO复合物活性较低但对乙烷ODH的选择性较高，可能是因为Nb富集相覆盖了NiO的非选择性活性氧物种\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。高Nb负载导致催化剂显著失活，而34 at.%的Nb含量产生了最有效的ODH催化剂，具有高活性、选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。优化制备方法可以进一步提高NiO催化的乙烷ODH\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。由于钽氧化物性质和催化剂行为之间的复杂相互作用，将理论预测与实验观察相协调存在挑战\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 钽氧化物在增强NiO催化剂中乙烷ODH活性和选择性中的复杂作用为未来研究提供了肥沃的土壤。大约15%的最佳铌掺杂水平在活性和选择性之间提供了平衡，通过先进的制备方法有进一步优化的潜力\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。催化剂再生的挑战和不活跃相的形成表明需要创新的再生策略和开发更稳定的铌掺杂NiO催化剂\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。理论预测与实验观察之间的复杂相互作用突出了开发更准确的计算模型和实验技术以更好地理解铌掺杂NiO催化剂的催化行为的重要性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。未来的研究可能集中在表面形态的详细表征和模型系统的开发上，以弥合理论预测与实验观察之间的差距\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。此外，探索其他助剂及其与铌的相互作用，可能会发现具有增强性能的新催化剂，用于乙烷ODH\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。该领域是探索性的，有前景的结果和大量工作有待完成\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。
