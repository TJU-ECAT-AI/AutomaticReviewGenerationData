                    The diverse properties of niobium oxides, including their various stable forms and oxidation states, significantly influence their behavior as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. At low Nb content (3-7%), ethane conversion's apparent activation energy and intrinsic rate decrease, indicating more active but fewer catalytic sites, leading to increased ethylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Beyond 15% Nb, the inactive NiNb2O6 phase forms, reducing active phase content \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The optimal Ni0.85Nb0.15O compound yields the best results \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Surface interaction between NiO and amorphous Nb2O5 phases is crucial for efficiency \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Thermal decomposition of oxalate ions further influences catalytic properties \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The continuous reduction of NiO oxygen over-stoichiometry and NiNb2O6 phase formation over time cause deactivation, challenging theoretical predictions \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Niobium oxides, due to their redox properties and ability to limit NiO crystallite size, make NiO catalysts less active but more selective for ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. At Nb content >5 at.%, the Nb-rich phase covers unselective, non-stoichiometric, active oxygen species of NiO, enhancing selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. However, high Nb loadings lead to significant catalyst deactivation due to the close interaction between NiO and the Nb-rich mixed oxide layer \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. The most efficient ODH catalysts contain 34 at.% Nb, combining high activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Optimizing the catalyst preparation method could further improve NiO-catalyzed ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The properties and behavior of niobium oxides as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane ODH have been extensively studied, revealing the intricate role of Nb in enhancing ethylene selectivity while maintaining catalytic activity. The optimal doping level and the formation of specific phases, such as NiNb2O6, have been identified as critical factors in catalyst performance \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004, 10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Future research endeavors might focus on developing advanced catalyst preparation techniques to control the formation of these phases and optimize the Nb content for enhanced stability and activity. Additionally, the development of in situ characterization methods to monitor the evolution of the catalyst structure and composition under reaction conditions could provide deeper insights into the deactivation mechanisms and guide the design of more robust catalysts. The exploration of alternative niobium precursors and the influence of different preparation methods on the catalytic properties of NiO-NbOx systems could also be promising avenues for future research.

                    钽氧化物的多样化性质，包括其各种稳定形态和氧化态，显著影响其在NiO催化剂中作为乙烷氧化脱氢（ODH）的掺杂剂的行为\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在低Nb含量（3-7%）时，乙烷转化的表观活化能和内在速率降低，表明催化位点更加活跃但数量减少，从而提高了乙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。超过15%的Nb含量时，形成不活跃的NiNb2O6相，减少了活性相的含量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。最优的Ni0.85Nb0.15O化合物产生最佳结果\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。NiO与无定形Nb2O5相之间的表面相互作用对于效率至关重要\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。草酸盐离子的热分解进一步影响催化性能\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。NiO的氧过量随时间的持续减少和NiNb2O6相的形成导致催化剂失活，这给理论预测带来了挑战\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。由于钽氧化物的氧化还原性质和限制NiO晶粒尺寸的能力，NiO催化剂对于乙烷ODH变得不那么活跃但更具选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。在Nb含量>5 at.%时，Nb富集相覆盖了NiO的非选择性、非化学计量的活性氧物种，提高了选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。然而，高Nb负载导致催化剂显著失活，这是由于NiO与Nb富集的混合氧化层之间的紧密相互作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。最有效的ODH催化剂含有34 at.%的Nb，结合了高活性、选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。优化催化剂制备方法可以进一步改善NiO催化的乙烷ODH\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：钽氧化物的性质和行为作为NiO催化剂中乙烷ODH的掺杂剂已经得到了广泛的研究，揭示了Nb在提高乙烯选择性同时保持催化活性的复杂作用\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004, 10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。已经确定了最优的掺杂水平和特定相，如NiNb2O6的形成，是催化剂性能的关键因素\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004, 10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发先进的催化剂制备技术，以控制这些相的形成并优化Nb含量，以增强稳定性和活性。此外，开发原位表征方法来监测催化剂结构和组成在反应条件下的演变，可以提供对失活机制的更深入理解，并指导更坚固催化剂的设计。探索替代的铌前体和不同制备方法对NiO-NbOx系统催化性能的影响，也可能成为未来研究的有希望的方向。
