                    The diverse properties and oxidation states of niobium oxides significantly influence their behavior as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. At low Nb content (3-7%), the ethane conversion rate and selectivity to ethylene increase, attributed to modified active sites \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Beyond 15% Nb, the inactive NiNb2O6 phase forms, reducing active phase content \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The optimal activity is observed at 15% Nb, due to decreased active phase particle size and prevention of sintering \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Long-term stability studies reveal a continuous decrease in activity, attributed to the formation of NiNb2O6 and reduced oxygen over-stoichiometry, leading to a lack of Oactive species \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Catalyst regeneration attempts have been unsuccessful \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Niobium oxides influence NiO catalysts for ethane ODH by affecting redox properties, selectivity, and surface area \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. At Nb content >5 at.%, NbNiO composites are less active but more selective for ethane ODH, as the Nb-rich phase covers unselective active oxygen species of NiO \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. High Nb loadings lead to significant catalyst deactivation due to close interaction and possible restructuration under ODH conditions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. The most efficient ODH catalysts contain 34 at.% Nb, combining high activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Preparation method impacts the structural and catalytic properties of NbNiO nanocomposites, suggesting potential for further improvement in NiO-catalyzed ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Challenges arise in reconciling theoretical predictions with experimental observations due to the complex interplay between niobium oxides' properties and catalyst behavior \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The intricate relationship between niobium oxides' properties and their role as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane ODH presents a fertile ground for future research. The optimization of Nb content, aiming for the sweet spot of 15% to maximize ethylene yield while minimizing the formation of inactive phases, is a critical area for investigation. Understanding the mechanisms behind the long-term stability issues, particularly the formation of NiNb2O6 and the reduction of oxygen over-stoichiometry, could lead to the development of more robust catalyst regeneration strategies. The impact of preparation methods on the structural and catalytic properties of NbNiO nanocomposites suggests that tailoring synthesis techniques could unlock further improvements in catalyst performance. The discrepancies between theoretical predictions and experimental observations underscore the need for more sophisticated modeling approaches that can accurately capture the complex interplay between niobium oxides' properties and catalyst behavior. Future research should focus on bridging this gap, potentially through the development of advanced computational methods that can account for the non-linear kinetic parameters and complex reaction networks observed in these systems.

                    钽氧化物的多样性质和氧化态显著影响其作为NiO催化剂中乙烷氧化脱氢(ODH)的掺杂剂的行为\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在低Nb含量(3-7%)时，乙烷转化率和乙烯选择性增加，归因于活性位点的改变\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。超过15%Nb时，形成不活跃的NiNb2O6相，减少活性相含量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在15%Nb时观察到最佳活性，归因于活性相颗粒尺寸减小和防止烧结\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。长期稳定性研究表明活性持续下降，归因于NiNb2O6的形成和NiO中氧过量减少，导致缺乏O活性物种\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。催化剂再生尝试未获成功\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。钽氧化物影响NiO催化剂的乙烷ODH，通过影响氧化还原性质、选择性和表面积\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。在Nb含量>5 at.%时，NbNiO复合材料活性较低但对乙烷ODH的选择性较高，因为Nb富集相覆盖了NiO的非选择性活性氧物种\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。高Nb负载导致催化剂显著失活，由于在ODH条件下与NiO的紧密相互作用和可能的重构\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。最有效的ODH催化剂含有34 at.%Nb，结合高活性、选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。制备方法影响NbNiO纳米复合材料的结构和催化性质，表明NiO催化乙烷ODH有进一步改进的潜力\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。由于铌氧化物性质和催化剂行为之间复杂相互作用，理论预测与实验观察之间的协调存在挑战\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 钽氧化物的性质与其作为NiO催化剂中乙烷ODH掺杂剂的角色之间的复杂关系为未来研究提供了肥沃的土壤。优化Nb含量，旨在达到15%的甜蜜点以最大化乙烯产量同时最小化不活跃相的形成，是关键的研究领域。理解长期稳定性问题背后的机制，特别是NiNb2O6的形成和氧过量减少，可能导致开发更强大的催化剂再生策略。制备方法对NbNiO纳米复合材料的结构和催化性质的影响表明，定制合成技术可能解锁催化剂性能的进一步改进。理论预测与实验观察之间的差异强调了需要更先进的建模方法，能够准确捕捉铌氧化物性质与催化剂行为之间复杂相互作用的必要性。未来的研究应集中在弥合这一差距上，可能通过开发先进的计算方法，能够考虑到这些系统中观察到的非线性动力学参数和复杂反应网络。
