Niobium oxides, with their diverse properties and oxidation states, significantly influence NiO catalysts for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) by altering redox properties, reducing NiO crystallite size, and inhibiting NiO reducibility \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. This leads to less active but more selective catalysts, where the Nb-rich phase covers unselective, non-stoichiometric active oxygen species of NiO, enhancing selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. At optimal Nb content (3-4 at.%), catalysts exhibit high activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. However, high Nb loadings cause significant deactivation due to the close interaction between NiO and the Nb-rich mixed oxide layer \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. The preparation method's impact on NbNiO nanocomposites suggests potential for improved NiO-catalyzed ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Reconciling theoretical predictions with experimental observations in these complex systems presents challenges \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\].

Niobium oxides, with their diverse properties and oxidation states, enhance NiO catalysts for ethane ODH by reducing oxygen over-stoichiometry and electrophilic Ospecies concentration \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. At low Nb content (3-7%), the apparent activation energy and intrinsic rate of ethane conversion decrease, enhancing ethylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Beyond 15% Nb, the inactive NiNb2O6 phase forms, reducing active phase content \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The optimal Ni0.85Nb0.15O compound yields the best results due to reduced active phase particle size, preventing sintering \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Over time, all catalysts deactivate due to NiNb2O6 phase formation and reduced oxygen over-stoichiometry, leading to a lack of active Ospecies \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Regeneration attempts have been unsuccessful \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\].

Niobium oxides, due to their ionic radii, valence, and electron-donor properties, substitute nickel atoms in NiO catalysts, forming a NiNb solid solution \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. This substitution reduces non-stoichiometry and cationic defects, decreasing unselective oxygen radicals and enhancing ethane ODH activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. Higher dopant valence correlates with increased ethylene selectivity, as evidenced by NiNb exhibiting the highest selectivity (90%) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. The less mobile oxygen species in high valence doped NiO are more selective for ethane to ethylene conversion \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. The linear relationship between oxygen exchange and ethane consumption confirms the active role of oxygen species in ethane activation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. The clear correlation between non-stoichiometric oxygen and ethylene selectivity supports these findings, with higher dopant valence leading to lower oxygen species and higher ethylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\].

Niobium oxides, with their diverse properties and oxidation states, influence their behavior as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane ODH \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]. In the presence of gas-phase oxygen, the system's behavior becomes complex \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]. The method of catalyst preparation significantly impacts performance, with qualitative and quantitative differences observed \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]. The distinction between high- and low-valence dopants observed in calculations is also seen in experiments \[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]. Niobium oxides, with their diverse properties and oxidation states, significantly impact their role as dopants in NiO catalysts for ethane ODH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Doping with niobium decreases activation energy and eliminates electrophilic oxygen species, favoring ethylene production over total ethane oxidation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. The ODH reaction on both oxides follows a Marsvan Krevelen mechanism, but the replenishment of surface oxygen differs, affecting product distribution \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. On NiO, rapid gaseous oxygen dissociation leads to high electrophilic oxygen concentrations, while niobium fills vacancies, reducing active sites and promoting diffusion, resulting in nucleophilic oxygen species selective for ethylene \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Challenges in studying these systems arise from the non-linear kinetic parameters and complex reaction networks, complicating the alignment of theoretical predictions with experimental data \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\].

Niobium-doped NiO catalysts boost ethane ODH reactivity and ethene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Niobium improves nickel dispersion and CH bond activation, contracting the NiO lattice and reducing lattice constant up to a Nb/Ni ratio of 0.176 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Niobium's incorporation inhibits crystallization, leading to smaller grain sizes, until high Nb loading promotes NiO crystallization \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Substitution of Ni2+ with Nb5+ causes charge imbalance, altering mixed NiNbO oxide properties \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Acidity peaks at a specific Nb/Ni ratio, correlating with NiONb bond formation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Niobium reduces p+ concentration, limiting excess oxygen on the surface, which inversely affects ethene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. As an electron donor, niobium reduces p+ holes in NiO, consuming electrophilic oxygen species and enhancing selective ethane conversion to ethene \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\].

铌氧化物因其多样化的性质和氧化态，对NiO催化剂在乙烷氧化脱氢（ODH）中的性能有显著影响，通过改变NiO的还原性、降低NiO晶粒尺寸和抑制NiO的还原性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。这导致了活性较低但选择性更高的催化剂，其中Nb富集相覆盖了NiO的非选择性、非化学计量活性氧物种，提高了选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。在最佳Nb含量（3-4 at.%）下，催化剂表现出高活性、选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。然而，高Nb负载会导致催化剂显著失活，由于NiO与Nb富集混合氧化层之间的紧密相互作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。NbNiO纳米复合材料的制备方法对性能有影响，表明NiO催化的乙烷ODH有改进的潜力\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。在这些复杂系统中调和理论预测与实验观察存在挑战\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。

铌氧化物因其多样化的性质和氧化态，通过降低过量氧和电性氧物种浓度，显著增强了NiO催化剂在乙烷ODH中的性能\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在低Nb含量（3-7%）下，乙烷转化的表观活化能和内在速率降低，提高了乙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。超过15% Nb，不活跃的NiNb2O6相形成，减少了活性相含量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。最佳的Ni0.85Nb0.15O化合物由于减少了活性相颗粒尺寸，防止了烧结，因此效果最佳\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。随着时间的推移，所有催化剂都会因NiNb2O6相的形成和过量氧的减少而失活，导致活性氧物种的缺乏\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。再生尝试均未成功\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。

铌氧化物因其离子半径、价态和电子供体性质，取代了NiO催化剂中的镍原子，形成了NiNb固溶体\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。这种取代降低了非化学计量和阳离子缺陷，减少了非选择性氧自由基，提高了乙烷ODH的活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。较高价态的掺杂剂与乙烯选择性的增加相关，NiNb表现出最高的选择性（90%）\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。高价态掺杂NiO中较不移动的氧物种对乙烷到乙烯的转化更具选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。氧交换与乙烷消耗之间的线性关系确认了氧物种在乙烷活化中的积极作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。非化学计量氧与乙烯选择性之间的明确相关性支持了这些发现，较高价态的掺杂剂导致较低的氧物种和较高的乙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。

铌氧化物因其多样化的性质和氧化态，影响了它们在NiO催化剂中作为乙烷ODH掺杂剂的行为\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。Nb2O5和NbO的稳定性以及掺杂氧化物中Nb的氧化态，这取决于其环境，影响了反应性\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。Nb掺杂剂作为强路易斯碱，影响了表面氧的结合，阻碍了氧化\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。在气相氧的存在下，系统的反应变得复杂\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。催化剂的制备方法对性能有显著影响，观察到定性和定量的差异\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。掺杂催化剂中的较小颗粒具有更多缺陷，增强了化学活性\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。Nb掺杂减少了氢的吸收和氧的释放，表明表面氧反应性的变化\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。在计算中观察到的高价和低价掺杂剂之间的区别在实验中也得到了证实\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。掺杂通常会提高催化活性，如计算和实验所示\[10.1021/cr300418s_10\]。

铌氧化物因其多样化的性质和氧化态，显著影响了它们在NiO催化剂中作为乙烷ODH掺杂剂的作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。Nb掺杂降低了活化能，消除了电性氧物种，有利于乙烯的产生，而不是乙烷的完全氧化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。两种氧化物上的ODH反应遵循Marsvan Krevelen机制，但表面氧的补充不同，影响了产物分布\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。在NiO上，快速的气态氧解离导致高电性氧浓度，而铌填充了空位，减少了活性位点，促进了扩散，导致了对乙烯具有选择性的亲核氧物种\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。在研究这些系统时遇到的挑战源于非线性动力学参数和复杂的反应网络，这使得理论预测与实验数据的对齐变得复杂\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。

铌掺杂的NiO催化剂提高了乙烷ODH的活性和乙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。铌提高了镍的分散度和CH键的活化，收缩了NiO晶格，将Nb/Ni比降低至0.176\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。铌的掺入抑制了结晶，导致了较小的晶粒尺寸，直到高Nb负载促进了NiO的结晶\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。Ni2+被Nb5+取代导致了电荷不平衡，改变了混合NiNbO氧化物的性质\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。酸性在特定的Nb/Ni比下达到峰值，与NiONb键的形成相关\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。铌降低了p+浓度，限制了表面过量氧，这反过来影响了乙烯的选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。作为电子供体，铌减少了NiO中的p+空穴，消耗了电性氧物种，增强了乙烷到乙烯的有选择性转化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。
