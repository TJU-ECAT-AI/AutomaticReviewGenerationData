                The complex effects of dopants on the catalytic properties of metal oxides have been elucidated through computational and experimental studies, as detailed in reference \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. High-valence dopants (HVDs) exhibit a more intricate impact on the chemistry of the host oxide than low-valence dopants (LVDs), contingent on the reducibility of the host oxide. In irreducible oxides, HVDs can impede oxidation by the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, whereas in reducible oxides, they can donate electrons to reduce cations in the host oxide \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. Same-valence dopants (SVDs) generally enhance the performance of the host oxide in catalytic applications \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. However, formulating simple rules for the effects of SVDs and HVDs is challenging due to factors such as Lewis acid-base interactions and the nature of the host oxide \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. Computational and experimental studies have shown that HVDs can increase the energy needed for removing surface oxygen atoms in irreducible oxides, which can hinder oxidation \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. In reducible oxides, HVDs can reduce cations, affecting the redox properties of the oxide \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. SVDs have been shown to favorably affect the properties of the host oxide in various catalytic applications \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. Challenges persist in formulating general predictive rules for dopant behavior due to the complexity of interactions between dopants and host oxides \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. The behavior of flexible-valence dopants (FVDs) is particularly difficult to predict, especially in reducible host oxides \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]. More computational and experimental work is needed to clarify the behavior of these systems \[10.1021/cr300418s_8\].

                通过计算和实验研究，详细阐述了掺杂剂对金属氧化物催化性能的复杂影响，如参考文献\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]所示。高价掺杂剂（HVDs）对宿主氧化物的化学性质的影响比低价掺杂剂（LVDs）更为复杂，这取决于宿主氧化物的可还原性。在不可还原的氧化物中，HVDs可以通过Mars-van Krevelen机制阻碍氧化，而在可还原的氧化物中，它们可以向宿主氧化物中的阳离子捐赠电子\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。同价掺杂剂（SVDs）通常可以增强宿主氧化物在催化应用中的性能\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。然而，由于诸如Lewis酸碱相互作用和宿主氧化物的性质等因素，制定SVDs和HVDs效应的简单规则具有挑战性\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。计算和实验研究表明，HVDs可以增加在不可还原氧化物中去除表面氧原子所需的能量，这可能阻碍氧化\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。在可还原氧化物中，HVDs可以还原阳离子，影响氧化物的氧化还原性质\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。SVDs已被证明可以有利地影响宿主氧化物在各种催化应用中的性质\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。由于掺杂剂和宿主氧化物之间的复杂相互作用，制定掺杂剂行为的一般预测规则仍然存在挑战\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。在可还原宿主氧化物中，可变价掺杂剂（FVDs）的行为尤其难以预测\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。需要更多的计算和实验工作来阐明这些系统的性质\[10.1021/cr300418s_8\]。
