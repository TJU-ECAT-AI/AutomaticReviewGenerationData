                    The evolving significance of propene in the chemical industry is driven by its demand outpacing that of ethylene, particularly in Western Europe \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. This trend has led to the preference for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a production method, addressing the misalignment between current olefin production and market needs. PDH's economic viability and environmental friendliness are evident in its improved energy efficiency and process simplicity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. To meet the increasing demand, better olefin yields are essential. However, higher exothermic reactions in propane ODH lead to greater energy losses and fixed costs, including CO2 production \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Innovative technologies that combine catalyst performance with process design are key to addressing these challenges \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\].

                    Propene, a vital raw material for high-value-added chemicals and polymers, has seen increased demand due to the chemical industry's expansion \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_0\]. Propane dehydrogenation is favored for propene production due to its economic viability and environmental benefits over other methods, making it a preferred choice in meeting the growing needs for this key olefin \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_0\].

                    Propylene, a critical chemical industry feedstock, is predominantly used in thermoplastic polypropylene production, with a global demand exceeding $90 billion \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Three main production methods exist: steam cracking, catalytic cracking (FCC), and catalytic dehydrogenation. Steam cracking and FCC are energy-intensive and face issues with coke formation, making them less favorable. In contrast, oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) of propane to propylene is gaining prominence due to its lower operational costs and environmental benefits \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. The exothermic nature of ODH eliminates the need for additional energy, and the availability of propane in natural gas and refinery off-gases enhances sustainability \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Fluidized bed reactors are preferred for ODH due to their temperature control and catalyst regeneration capabilities \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Vanadium-based catalysts are crucial for high propylene yield, providing lattice oxygen for dehydrogenation while minimizing carbon oxide formation \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\].

                    Propene's demand has surged, outpacing conventional sources like ethane steam crackers and FCCs, leading to the preference for propane dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.103\]. This method offers higher propene yields, reduced energy use, and lower greenhouse gas emissions, making it a sustainable and cost-effective solution for growing needs in plastics, resins, and synthetic rubbers production \[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.103\].

                    Propene's significance in the chemical industry has grown, with a global production capacity of 110 million tons \[10.1021/acs.accounts.8b00330\]. Traditionally, propene was produced from naphtha cracking. However, the shift to abundant natural gas as a feedstock, specifically ethane, has mainly yielded ethylene, creating a gap in C3 and C4 olefin production. To meet market demand, on-purpose processes like propane dehydrogenation have emerged, offering economic and environmental advantages over traditional methods by producing propene more efficiently and with lower emissions \[10.1021/acs.accounts.8b00330\].

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in propane dehydrogenation, particularly in catalyst design and process optimization, have positioned it as a pivotal technology for meeting the growing demand for propene. Future research endeavors might focus on developing more efficient and selective catalysts, enhancing process design for better energy management, and exploring the integration of renewable energy sources to further reduce the carbon footprint of propene production. The development of novel catalysts that can balance lattice oxygen supply and minimize carbon oxide formation will be crucial for achieving high propylene selectivity. Additionally, the optimization of fluidized bed reactors for temperature control and catalyst regeneration will be essential for maximizing the benefits of oxidative dehydrogenation. The potential for scaling up propane dehydrogenation processes to achieve significant energy savings and impact propene production, considering its high demand and energy-intensive production, presents an exciting avenue for future exploration.

                    丙烯在化学工业中的重要性正在演变，其需求超过了乙烯，尤其是在西欧\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。这一趋势促使人们倾向于使用丙烷脱氢（PDH）作为生产方法，以解决当前烯烃生产与市场需求之间的不匹配。PDH的经济可行性和环境友好性体现在其改进的能源效率和过程简化\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。为了满足日益增长的需求，提高烯烃产量至关重要。然而，丙烷ODH中较高的放热反应会导致更大的能量损失和固定成本，包括CO2的产生\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。将催化剂性能与过程设计相结合的创新技术是解决这些挑战的关键\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。

                    丙烯，作为高附加值化学品和聚合物的重要原料，由于化学工业的扩张，需求量增加\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_0\]。由于其经济可行性和环境效益超过其他方法，丙烷脱氢被用于丙烯生产，成为满足这种关键烯烃日益增长需求的首选\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_0\]。

                    丙烯，一种关键的化学工业原料，主要用于热塑性聚丙烯生产，全球需求超过900亿美元\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。主要存在三种生产方法：蒸汽裂解、催化裂化（FCC）和催化脱氢。蒸汽裂解和FCC能耗大，存在焦炭形成问题，因此不太受欢迎。相比之下，丙烷到丙烯的氧化脱氢（ODH）由于其较低的运营成本和环境效益而日益受到关注\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。ODH的放热性质消除了对额外能量的需求，而丙烷在天然气和炼油厂副产品中的可用性增强了可持续性\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。流化床反应器因其温度控制和催化剂再生能力而被首选用于ODH\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。钒基催化剂对于高丙烯产量至关重要，它们提供晶格氧进行脱氢，同时最小化碳氧化物的形成\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。

                    丙烯的需求激增，超过了传统的乙烷蒸汽裂解器和FCCs，这导致了对丙烷脱氢的偏好\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.103\]。这种方法提供了更高的丙烯产量，减少了能源使用，降低了温室气体排放，使其成为满足塑料、树脂和合成橡胶生产日益增长需求的可持续和成本效益解决方案\[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.103\]。

                    丙烯在化学工业中的重要性正在增长，全球生产能力达到1.1亿吨\[10.1021/acs.accounts.8b00330\]。传统上，丙烯是通过石脑油裂解生产的。然而，向天然气作为原料的转变，特别是乙烷，主要生产乙烯，造成了C3和C4烯烃的生产缺口。为了满足市场需求，像丙烷脱氢这样的目标过程已经出现，与传统方法相比，它们在更高效地生产丙烯和降低排放方面提供了经济和环境优势\[10.1021/acs.accounts.8b00330\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 丙烷脱氢催化剂设计和过程优化的进展使其成为满足丙烯日益增长需求的关键技术。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发更高效、更选择性的催化剂，改进过程设计以更好地管理能源，以及探索可再生能源的整合，以进一步减少丙烯生产的碳足迹。开发能够平衡晶格氧供应和最小化碳氧化物形成的新型催化剂对于实现高丙烯选择性至关重要。此外，优化流化床反应器的温度控制和催化剂再生对于最大化氧化脱氢的好处至关重要。扩大丙烷脱氢过程的规模，以实现显著的能源节约，并影响丙烯生产，考虑到其高需求和能源密集型生产，为未来的探索提供了一个令人兴奋的途径。
