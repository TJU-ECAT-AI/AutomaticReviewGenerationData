                    Propene's significance in the chemical industry has escalated, driven by its role as a critical feedstock for polymers and derivatives, with a global demand exceeding $90 billion \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Traditional production methods, such as steam cracking and catalytic cracking (FCC), are energy-intensive and plagued by coke formation, making them less favorable \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Propane dehydrogenation (PDH), notably oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH), has emerged as a preferred method due to lower operational costs and minimal environmental impact \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. ODH's exothermic nature eliminates the need for additional energy, and the availability of propane in natural gas enhances sustainability \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Fluidized bed reactors offer advantages in temperature control and catalyst regeneration, crucial for ODH's commercial viability \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Vanadium-based catalysts are efficient in ODH reactions, providing lattice oxygen for dehydrogenation \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]. Designing catalysts that balance lattice oxygen supply and minimize carbon oxide formation is key to achieving high propylene selectivity \[10.1039/C6CY00078A\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in propane dehydrogenation, particularly in catalyst design and reactor technology, have positioned PDH as a sustainable and economically viable method for propene production. Future research should focus on developing more efficient and selective catalysts, optimizing reactor designs for better temperature control and catalyst regeneration, and exploring alternative feedstocks for PDH. The integration of PDH with renewable energy sources could further enhance its environmental benefits, potentially leading to a more sustainable chemical industry. Additionally, the development of novel catalytic materials that can control partial oxidation and prevent overoxidation to CO and CO2 will be crucial for the large-scale implementation of PDH.

                    丙烯在化工行业中的重要性日益凸显，作为聚合物和衍生物的关键原料，其全球需求已超过900亿美元\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。传统的生产方法，如蒸汽裂解和催化裂化（FCC），由于能耗高且易形成焦炭，逐渐失去优势\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。丙烷脱氢（PDH），尤其是氧化脱氢（ODH），因其较低的运营成本和环境影响小而成为首选方法\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。ODH的放热性质消除了对额外能源的需求，而天然气中丙烷的丰富性增强了其可持续性\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。流化床反应器在温度控制和催化剂再生方面具有优势，对ODH的商业化至关重要\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。基于钒的催化剂在ODH反应中效率高，提供晶格氧进行脱氢\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。设计能够平衡晶格氧供应并最小化碳氧化物形成的催化剂是实现高丙烯选择性的关键\[10.1039/C6CY00078A\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 丙烷脱氢催化剂设计和反应器技术的进步，使PDH成为一种可持续且经济可行的丙烯生产方法。未来的研究应集中在开发更高效、选择性更高的催化剂，优化反应器设计以实现更好的温度控制和催化剂再生，以及探索PDH的替代原料。将PDH与可再生能源的整合可以进一步增强其环境效益，可能引领更可持续的化工行业。此外，开发能够控制部分氧化、防止过度氧化为CO和CO2的新型催化材料，对于PDH的大规模实施至关重要。
