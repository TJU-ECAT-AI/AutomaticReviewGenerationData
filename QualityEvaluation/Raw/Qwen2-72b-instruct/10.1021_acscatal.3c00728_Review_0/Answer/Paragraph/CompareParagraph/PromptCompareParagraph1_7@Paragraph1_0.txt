Propene, the second most important olefin, is critical for producing high-value-added chemicals and polymers \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728\_0\]. Its demand has surged with the chemical industry's expansion and increased consumption of these products \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728\_0\]. Propane dehydrogenation is favored for propene production due to its economic viability and environmental benefits over other methods \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728\_0\]. Propene's role as a key feedstock for polymers, oxygenates, and derivatives has grown, driving demand \[10.1039/C8CC04604B\].
