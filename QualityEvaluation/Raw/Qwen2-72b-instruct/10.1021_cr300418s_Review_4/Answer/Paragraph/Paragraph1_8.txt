                    Comprehensive characterization methods, including synchrotron-based X-ray diffraction \[10.1021/jp103958u\], X-ray absorption spectroscopy \[10.1021/jp103958u\], Raman spectroscopy \[10.1021/jp103958u\], and density functional theory calculations \[10.1021/jp103958u\], were employed to verify dopant incorporation and distribution in Ce1xNixO2y nanosystems. These techniques confirmed Ni in a +2 oxidation state, indicated a distorted NiO first shell bond, suggested modest charge redistribution for Ce, and revealed frequency shifts and broadening for the F2g ceria peak, corroborating XRD and EXAFS data. Chemical reactivity tests using H2 and in situ XRD, XANES, and DRIFT data during the WGS reaction provided insights into catalytic performance. The solubility limit for Ni was closer to 10%, as indicated by similar shifts in samples containing 10% and 20% Ni \[10.1021/jp103958u\]. High Sensitivity-Low Energy Ion Scattering (HS-LEIS) is a pivotal technique for characterizing doped metal oxide catalysts, offering quantitative surface composition data \[10.1016/j.cattod.2008.10.012\]. It excels in specificity for the outermost atomic layer and straightforward quantification, surpassing XPS and SIMS in sensitivity and information depth. HS-LEIS identifies elements with masses 12-60 amu using 4He+ ions, with enhanced mass resolution for heavier atoms using 20Ne+ ions. Its sensitivity allows Static LEIS, minimizing sample surface sputtering. Quantification is achieved by correlating peak areas to surface atom fractions, with calibration plots for precise quantification. HS-LEIS's sensitivity and analysis of the outermost layer, where catalytic processes occur, are essential for understanding dopant effects on structure and catalytic performance \[10.1016/j.cattod.2008.10.012\].

                    采用包括基于同步加速器的X射线衍射\[10.1021/jp103958u\]、X射线吸收光谱\[10.1021/jp103958u\]、拉曼光谱\[10.1021/jp103958u\]和密度泛函理论计算\[10.1021/jp103958u\]在内的全面表征方法，验证了Ce1xNixO2y纳米系统中掺杂剂的掺入和分布。这些技术确认了Ni处于+2氧化态，表明NiO第一壳层键存在畸变，Ce的电荷重新分配适度，且F2g铈峰的频率偏移和展宽，证实了XRD和EXAFS数据。使用H2进行的化学反应性测试和WGS反应期间的原位XRD、XANES和DRIFT数据提供了对催化性能的见解。Ni的溶解度极限更接近10%，如含10%和20%Ni的样品中相似的偏移所示\[10.1021/jp103958u\]。高灵敏度-低能离子散射（HS-LEIS）是表征掺杂金属氧化物催化剂的关键技术，提供定量的表面组成数据\[10.1016/j.cattod.2008.10.012\]。它在最外层原子层的特异性方面表现出色，量化简便，与XPS和SIMS相比，在灵敏度和信息深度方面更胜一筹。HS-LEIS使用4He+离子识别12-60 amu质量的元素，使用20Ne+离子增强对较重原子的质量分辨率。其灵敏度允许静态LEIS，最大限度地减少样品表面溅射。通过将峰面积与表面原子分数相关联，实现量化，校准图用于精确量化。HS-LEIS的灵敏度和对外层的分析，对于理解掺杂剂对结构和催化性能的影响至关重要\[10.1016/j.cattod.2008.10.012\]。
