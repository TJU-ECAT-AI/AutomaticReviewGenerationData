                The multidimensional nature of the ODHP reaction mechanism over B-based catalysts, integrating surface and gas-phase radical reactions, significantly impacts catalyst performance and design optimization \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. The B[OHO(H)Si]2 structure in borosilicate zeolites acts as an efficient active center for the ODHP reaction, promoting dehydrogenation while hindering full boron hydrolysis \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. The synergistic effects of surface and gas-phase reactions suggest a complex reaction network, offering multiple pathways for propane conversion to propylene \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. The B2O3/SiO2@HC monolithic catalyst demonstrates high propylene productivity under oxidative dehydrogenation of propane conditions, attributed to the efficient dispersion of the catalytically active B2O3 layer and improved mass transfer \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. Balancing surface and gas-phase reactions maximizes propylene yield and minimizes CO2 formation \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. Optimizing catalyst design to manage these reactions could enhance propylene productivity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. The first coated SiO2 layer stabilizes active boron species, while the monolithic catalyst design effectively manages gas-phase reactions, suppressing overoxidation and achieving high olefin selectivity under high GHSV conditions \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The multidimensional nature of the ODHP reaction mechanism over B-based catalysts, integrating surface and gas-phase radical reactions, has revealed a complex reaction network that significantly impacts catalyst performance and design optimization. The B[OHO(H)Si]2 structure in borosilicate zeolites, acting as an efficient active center for the ODHP reaction, promotes dehydrogenation while hindering full boron hydrolysis. The synergistic effects of surface and gas-phase reactions offer multiple pathways for propane conversion to propylene, highlighting the importance of balancing these reactions to maximize propylene yield and minimize CO2 formation. Future research endeavors might focus on developing catalysts that can effectively manage both surface and gas-phase reactions, leading to enhanced propylene productivity and selectivity. The stabilization of active boron species by the first coated SiO2 layer and the effective management of gas-phase reactions by the monolithic catalyst design are promising strategies for suppressing overoxidation and achieving high olefin selectivity under high GHSV conditions. Further investigation into the synergistic effects between surface and gas-phase reactions could provide insights into the development of more efficient and selective catalysts for propane dehydrogenation.

                B基催化剂上的ODHP反应机制的多维性质，整合了表面和气相自由基反应，对催化剂性能和设计优化产生了重大影响\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。硼硅酸盐沸石中的B[OHO(H)Si]2结构作为ODHP反应的有效活性中心，促进了脱氢反应，同时抑制了硼的完全水解\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。表面和气相反应的协同效应表明了一个复杂的反应网络，为丙烷转化为丙烯提供了多种途径\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。B2O3/SiO2@HC多孔催化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢条件下表现出高丙烯生产力，归因于催化活性B2O3层的有效分散和质量传递的改善\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。平衡表面和气相反应可以最大化丙烯产量并最小化CO2的形成\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。优化催化剂设计以管理这些反应可以增强丙烯生产力和选择性\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。第一层涂覆的SiO2稳定了活性硼物种，而多孔催化剂设计有效地管理了气相反应，抑制了过度氧化，并在高GHSV条件下实现了高烯烃选择性\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: B基催化剂上的ODHP反应机制的多维性质，整合了表面和气相自由基反应，揭示了一个复杂的反应网络，对催化剂性能和设计优化产生了重大影响。硼硅酸盐沸石中的B[OHO(H)Si]2结构作为有效的活性中心，促进了脱氢反应，同时抑制了硼的完全水解。表面和气相反应的协同效应为丙烷转化为丙烯提供了多种途径，强调了平衡这些反应以最大化丙烯产量和最小化CO2形成的重要性。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发能够有效管理表面和气相反应的催化剂，从而增强丙烯生产力和选择性。第一层涂覆的SiO2稳定活性硼物种，以及多孔催化剂设计有效地管理气相反应，抑制过度氧化，并在高GHSV条件下实现高烯烃选择性，是抑制过度氧化和实现高烯烃选择性的有前途的策略。进一步研究表面和气相反应之间的协同效应可能为开发更高效和选择性的丙烷脱氢催化剂提供见解。
