1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials can facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis through mechanisms such as the Mars van Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction. In the Mars van Krevelen mechanism, the metal center oxidizes the hydrocarbon substrate, while the oxide support reduces the metal center, regenerating the active site. H-abstraction involves the direct removal of a hydrogen atom from the hydrocarbon by the metal center. These principles are applied in industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, where the metal center selectively oxidizes the alkane to the desired product while minimizing over-oxidation.

1.在异质催化中，氧化物材料上的孤立金属中心可以通过Mars van Krevelen机制和H-抽象等机制促进C-H键的活化。在Mars van Krevelen机制中，金属中心氧化烃类底物，而氧化物载体还原金属中心，再生活性位点。H-抽象涉及金属中心直接从烃类中移除氢原子。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，例如丁烷转化为马来酸酐，其中金属中心选择性氧化烷烃以产生所需产物，同时最小化过度氧化。
