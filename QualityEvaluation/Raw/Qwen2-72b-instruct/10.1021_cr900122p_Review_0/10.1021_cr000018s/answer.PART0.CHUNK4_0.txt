1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials can facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis through mechanisms such as the Mars van Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction. In the Mars van Krevelen mechanism, a metal oxide catalyst is reduced by the substrate, forming a metal-carbon bond and releasing CO2. In H-abstraction, a metal center abstracts a hydrogen atom from a hydrocarbon, forming a metal-hydride intermediate. These principles are applied in industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, where a metal oxide catalyst selectively abstracts hydrogen atoms from the butane molecule, leading to the formation of maleic anhydride through a series of oxidation steps.

1.在异质催化中，氧化物材料上的孤立金属中心可以通过Mars van Krevelen机制和H-抽象等机制促进C-H键的活化。在Mars van Krevelen机制中，金属氧化物催化剂被底物还原，形成金属-碳键并释放CO2。在H-抽象中，金属中心从烃类中抽象出一个氢原子，形成金属-氢化物中间体。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，例如丁烷转化为马来酸酐，其中金属氧化物催化剂选择性地从丁烷分子中抽象出氢原子，通过一系列氧化步骤形成马来酸酐。
