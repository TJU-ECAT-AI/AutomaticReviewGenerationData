The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves redox cycling and hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen, forming metal-oxo intermediates, while H-abstraction involves direct hydrogen atom transfer to the metal center \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. The rate-determining step in the selective oxidation of butane to maleic anhydride is the activation of the C-H bond, facilitated by mechanisms such as concerted hydrogen abstraction on VIVO species or H-abstraction by activated O2 coordinated to VIV centers \[10.1021/cr900122p\_0\].
