                    The pivotal role of isolated metal centers on oxide materials in facilitating C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis is underscored by their function as Lewis acid sites, enabling the Mars van Krevelen and H-abstraction mechanisms \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. These mechanisms are crucial for the selective oxidation of alkanes, as exemplified by the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, where the metal oxide catalysts selectively activate C-H bonds, promoting desired oxidation reactions while minimizing over-oxidation to CO2 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves redox cycling and hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen, forming metal-oxo intermediates, while H-abstraction involves direct hydrogen atom transfer from C-H bonds to the metal center \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. These principles are applied in industrial selective oxidation processes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, using metal-oxide catalysts with isolated metal centers \[10.1021/cr010333u\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights gained from the study of isolated metal centers on oxide materials in heterogeneous catalysis open up new avenues for the design of more efficient and selective catalysts. Future research could focus on the development of catalysts with tailored Lewis acidity and optimized metal-oxide interactions to enhance C-H bond activation. The exploration of novel metal-oxide combinations and the precise control of metal dispersion and oxidation states could lead to catalysts with improved selectivity and activity for alkane oxidation reactions. Moreover, the integration of computational modeling with experimental studies could provide a deeper understanding of the active sites and reaction mechanisms, guiding the rational design of catalysts for the selective oxidation of alkanes to high-value oxygenates.

                    隔离金属中心在氧化物材料上作为Lewis酸位点，通过Mars van Krevelen和H-抽提机制促进异相催化中的C-H键活化，其关键作用被强调 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]。这些机制对于选择性氧化烷烃至关重要，例如，丁烷转化为马来酸酐的过程，其中金属氧化物催化剂选择性地活化C-H键，促进所需的氧化反应，同时最小化氧化至CO2的副反应 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及通过晶格氧进行的氧化还原循环和氢抽提，形成金属-氧中间体，而H-抽提则涉及从C-H键直接转移氢原子到金属中心 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化过程中，例如，使用具有隔离金属中心的金属氧化物催化剂将丁烷转化为马来酸酐 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 从隔离金属中心在氧化物材料上异相催化研究中获得的见解，为设计更高效、更选择性的催化剂开辟了新的途径。未来的研究可以集中在开发具有定制Lewis酸性和优化金属-氧化物相互作用的催化剂，以增强C-H键的活化。探索新型金属-氧化物组合和精确控制金属分散度和氧化态，可以产生具有改善的选择性和活性的催化剂，用于烷烃氧化反应。此外，将计算建模与实验研究相结合，可以更深入地理解活性位点和反应机制，指导合理设计用于选择性氧化烷烃至高价值含氧化合物的催化剂。
