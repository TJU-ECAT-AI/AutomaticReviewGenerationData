                    The pivotal role of isolated metal centers on oxide materials in facilitating C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis is underscored by their function as Lewis acid sites, stabilizing the transition state for C-H bond cleavage \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. Mechanisms such as the Mars van Krevelen and H-abstraction are central to these processes, involving redox cycling and hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen to form metal-oxo intermediates \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. H-abstraction directly removes hydrogen atoms, creating metal-hydrogen bonds and carbon-centered radicals or cations \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, exemplified by the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, using supported metal oxide catalysts with isolated metal centers that selectively activate C-H bonds and promote desired oxidation reactions while minimizing over-oxidation to CO2 \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism is particularly dominant in the selective oxidation of butane to maleic anhydride over vanadium pentoxide catalysts, ensuring high selectivity for maleic anhydride with minimal byproduct formation \[10.1021/cr9411637\]. The catalytic behavior of V-P-based catalysts, used in this process, is influenced by the method of preparation, P/V ratio, presence of additives, and activation procedures \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The morphology of (VO)HP04.0.5H20 crystallites, affected by preparation conditions, impacts the exposure of the (001) plane and the reduction of V5+ \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. A P/V ratio higher than stoichiometric stabilizes the catalyst, preventing oxidation \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The rate-determining step in the selective oxidation of n-butane to maleic anhydride is the activation of the paraffin, facilitated by vanadylpyrophosphate with high selectivity \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed analysis of isolated metal centers on oxide materials and their role in C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis opens up new avenues for the development of more efficient and selective catalysts. Future research might focus on the optimization of catalyst preparation methods, P/V ratios, and activation procedures to enhance the selectivity and stability of V-P-based catalysts. The exploration of alternative supports and additives could also lead to the discovery of novel catalysts with improved performance in selective alkane oxidation. Moreover, the investigation of the effects of different preparation conditions on the morphology and reactivity of (VO)HP04.0.5H20 crystallites could provide insights into the design of catalysts with tailored properties for specific reactions. The understanding of the Mars van Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction in the selective oxidation of butane to maleic anhydride could be extended to other alkane oxidation reactions, potentially leading to the development of new industrial processes for the conversion of alkanes to valuable oxygenates.

                    隔离金属中心在氧化物材料上对C-H键活化在异相催化中的关键作用，体现在它们作为路易斯酸位点，稳定C-H键断裂的过渡态\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。火星-克雷维尔机制和H-抽提是这些过程的核心，涉及通过晶格氧的氢抽提和氧化还原循环形成金属-氧中间体\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。H-抽提直接移除氢原子，形成金属-氢键和碳中心的自由基或阳离子\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。这些原则应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，以丁烷转化为马来酸酐为例，使用具有隔离金属中心的金属氧化物催化剂，选择性活化C-H键，促进所需的氧化反应，同时最小化氧化至CO2\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。火星-克雷维尔机制在丁烷选择性氧化为马来酸酐的钒酸盐催化剂上尤其占主导地位，确保了对马来酸酐的高选择性，副产品形成最少\[10.1021/cr9411637\]。在这一过程中使用的V-P基催化剂的催化行为受到制备方法、P/V比、添加剂的存在和激活程序的影响\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。(VO)HP04.0.5H20晶体的形态，受制备条件的影响，影响(001)面的暴露和V5+的还原\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。P/V比高于化学计量比稳定催化剂，防止氧化\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。选择性氧化n-丁烷至马来酸酐的速率决定步骤是石蜡的活化，由钒氧磷酸盐以高选择性促进\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。
