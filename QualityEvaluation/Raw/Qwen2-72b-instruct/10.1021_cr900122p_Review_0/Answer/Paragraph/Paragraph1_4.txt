                The pivotal role of isolated metal centers on oxide materials in facilitating C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis is well-established \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. These centers act as Lewis acid sites, stabilizing the transition state for C-H bond activation. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism, involving redox cycling and hydrogen abstraction by lattice oxygen, forms a metal-oxo intermediate \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. The H-abstraction mechanism involves direct hydrogen atom transfer from the C-H bond to the metal center, creating a carbon-centered radical or cation \[10.1021/cr010333u\]. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as converting butane to maleic anhydride, by using supported metal oxide catalysts with isolated metal centers that selectively activate C-H bonds and promote desired oxidation reactions without over-oxidation to CO2 \[10.1021/cr010333u\].

                The Mars van Krevelen mechanism, involving alkane oxidation by metal oxides and subsequent reoxidation by molecular oxygen, is pivotal in the selective oxidation of alkanes \[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]. The conversion of butane to maleic anhydride exemplifies this industrial process, involving a 14-electron oxidation to sequentially form butenes, butadiene, and 1,4-dihydrofuran, which then becomes maleic anhydride \[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]. The rate-limiting step, butane's C-H bond activation, occurs via hydrogen abstraction on VIVO species or by activated O2 coordinated to VIV centers, leading to 2-butenes or 2-butanone \[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]. This process, despite its complexity, is commercially viable, achieving high selectivities up to 97% at 65% conversion \[10.1021/cr900122p_0\].

                Isolated metal centers on oxide materials, particularly V-P-based catalysts, facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis, enabling the selective oxidation of alkanes \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The mechanisms involved, including the Mars van Krevelen mechanism and H-abstraction, are crucial in the industrial process of converting butane to maleic anhydride \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The catalytic behavior of V-P-based catalysts is influenced by the method of preparation, the P/V ratio, the presence of additives, and the activation and conditioning procedures at high temperatures \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The optimal catalyst is characterized by microcrystalline (V0)HPO4.O.5H20 prepared in organic solvent, with a slight excess of phosphorus and defects in the stacking of platelets \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The morphology of the (VO)HP04.0.5H20 crystallites, influenced by various factors, affects the exposure of the (001) plane, creates strain in the crystallite, and influences the extent of reduction of V5+ \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The reaction pattern for the selective oxidation of n-butane to maleic anhydride involves several steps, including oxidative dehydrogenation, allylic oxidation, and electrophilic oxygen insertion, facilitated by the polyfunctional nature of the V-P catalysts \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]. The rate-determining step is the activation of the paraffin, which is facilitated by the vanadylpyrophosphate catalyst with high selectivity \[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\].

                隔离金属中心在氧化物材料上促进C-H键活化在异相催化中的关键作用已得到充分证实\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。这些中心作为路易斯酸位点，稳定C-H键活化的过渡态。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及氧化还原循环和晶格氧的氢抽象，形成金属-氧中间体\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。H-抽象机制涉及C-H键直接向金属中心转移氢原子，生成碳中心的自由基或阳离子\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。这些原理应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，如将丁烷转化为马来酸酐，通过使用具有隔离金属中心的支撑金属氧化物催化剂，选择性活化C-H键，促进所需的氧化反应，避免过度氧化至CO2\[10.1021/cr010333u\]。

                Mars van Krevelen机制，涉及金属氧化物氧化烷烃和随后由分子氧重新氧化，是选择性氧化烷烃的关键\[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]。丁烷转化为马来酸酐是这一工业过程的典范，涉及14电子氧化，依次形成丁烯、丁二烯和1,4-二氢呋喃，然后转化为马来酸酐\[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]。速率限制步骤，丁烷的C-H键活化，通过VIVO物种的氢抽象或VIV中心协调的激活O2发生，导致2-丁烯或2-丁酮\[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]。尽管过程复杂，但商业上可行，选择性高达97%，转化率为65%\[10.1021/cr900122p_0\]。

                特别是V-P基催化剂上的隔离金属中心，促进C-H键活化在异相催化中，使烷烃的选择性氧化成为可能\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。涉及的机制，包括Mars van Krevelen机制和H-抽象，是丁烷转化为马来酸酐工业过程中的关键\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。V-P基催化剂的催化行为受制备方法、P/V比、添加剂的存在以及高温下的激活和调节程序的影响\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。最佳催化剂的特征是有机溶剂中制备的微晶(V0)HPO4.O.5H20，磷略有过量，板状堆积中存在缺陷\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。(VO)HP04.0.5H20晶粒的形态，受多种因素影响，影响(001)面的暴露，晶粒中产生应变，影响V5+还原的程度\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。n-丁烷选择性氧化为马来酸酐的反应模式涉及多个步骤，包括氧化脱氢、烯丙基氧化和亲电氧插入，由V-P催化剂的多功能性质促进\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。速率决定步骤是石蜡的活化，由钒氧磷酸盐催化剂以高选择性促进\[10.1039/9781847553232-00246\]。
