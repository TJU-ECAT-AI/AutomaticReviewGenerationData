1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials can facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by serving as active sites for hydrogen abstraction or by stabilizing transition states for C-H bond cleavage. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the metal center undergoing redox cycling between oxidation states, while the H-abstraction mechanism involves direct hydrogen atom transfer from the C-H bond to the metal center. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, by carefully controlling the metal type, support, and reaction conditions to achieve high selectivity for the desired product.

1.在多相催化中，氧化物材料上的孤立金属中心可以通过充当氢原子抽提的活性位点或稳定C-H键断裂的过渡态，促进C-H键的活化。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及金属中心在氧化态之间进行氧化还原循环，而H-抽提机制涉及C-H键直接向金属中心转移氢原子。这些原理被应用于工业上选择性氧化烷烃，例如丁烷转化为马来酸酐，通过仔细控制金属类型、载体和反应条件，以实现对所需产物的高选择性。
