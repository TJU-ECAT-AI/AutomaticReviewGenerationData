1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials can facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by serving as active sites for hydrogen abstraction reactions. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the transfer of oxygen from the oxide lattice to the organic substrate, followed by the regeneration of the oxide lattice through the incorporation of lattice oxygen. H-abstraction mechanisms involve the direct removal of hydrogen atoms from the organic substrate by the metal center, leading to the formation of reactive intermediates that can undergo further oxidation. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, by using metal-oxide catalysts that promote the selective oxidation of specific carbon-hydrogen bonds while minimizing the formation of undesired byproducts.

1.在多相催化中，氧化物材料上的孤立金属中心可以通过充当氢原子抽提反应的活性位点来促进C-H键的活化。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及从氧化物晶格向有机底物转移氧，随后通过掺入晶格氧来再生氧化物晶格。H-抽提机制涉及金属中心直接从有机底物中移除氢原子，形成可以进一步氧化的反应中间体。这些原理被应用于工业上选择性氧化烷烃，例如使用促进特定碳-氢键选择性氧化的金属氧化物催化剂，同时最小化不希望的副产物的形成，将丁烷转化为马来酸酐。
