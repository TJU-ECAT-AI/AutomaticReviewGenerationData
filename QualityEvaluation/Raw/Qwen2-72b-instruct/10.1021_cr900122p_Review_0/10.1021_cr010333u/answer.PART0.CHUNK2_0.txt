1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials can facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by acting as Lewis acid sites that stabilize the transition state for C-H bond cleavage. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the metal center undergoing redox cycling between oxidized and reduced states, while the H-abstraction mechanism involves direct hydrogen atom transfer from the C-H bond to the metal center. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, by using supported metal catalysts that can activate the C-H bonds selectively while minimizing over-oxidation to CO2. The metal centers promote the formation of reactive oxygen species that can oxidize the alkane to the desired product.

1.孤立的金属中心在氧化物材料上可以通过充当路易斯酸位点来促进C-H键在异相催化中的活化，稳定C-H键裂解的过渡态。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及金属中心在氧化态和还原态之间进行氧化还原循环，而H-抽象机制涉及C-H键直接向金属中心转移氢原子。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，例如使用支撑金属催化剂将丁烷转化为马来酸酐，这些催化剂可以选择性地活化C-H键，同时最大限度地减少氧化为CO2的过度氧化。金属中心促进形成可以将烷烃氧化为所需产物的活性氧物种。
