<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.Isolated metal centers on oxide materials facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by acting as Lewis acid sites that can coordinate and activate hydrocarbon molecules. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the abstraction of a hydrogen atom from the hydrocarbon, forming a metal-hydrogen bond and leaving behind a carbon-centered radical. This radical can then react further to form the desired products. In the H-abstraction mechanism, the metal center directly abstracts a hydrogen atom, forming a metal-hydrogen bond and a carbon-centered cation. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, by using metal-oxide catalysts that can activate the C-H bonds selectively and promote the desired oxidation reactions.

1.孤立的金属中心在氧化物材料上通过充当路易斯酸位点来促进异相催化中的C-H键活化，这些位点可以配位和活化烃分子。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及从烃中抽离一个氢原子，形成金属-氢键，留下一个碳中心的自由基。这个自由基然后可以进一步反应形成所需的产品。在H-抽离机制中，金属中心直接抽离一个氢原子，形成金属-氢键和一个碳中心的阳离子。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，例如丁烷转化为马来酸酐，通过使用可以有选择地活化C-H键并促进所需氧化反应的金属氧化物催化剂。
