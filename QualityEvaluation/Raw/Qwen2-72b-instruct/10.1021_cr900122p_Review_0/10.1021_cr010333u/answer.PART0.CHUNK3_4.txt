Answer: Isolated metal centers on oxide materials facilitate C-H bond activation in heterogeneous catalysis by acting as Lewis acid sites that can stabilize the transition state of the reaction. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism involves the metal center abstracting a hydrogen atom from the hydrocarbon, forming a metal hydride and a carbon-centered radical. This radical can then react with an oxygen molecule to form a peroxo intermediate, which subsequently decomposes to form the desired product. In the H-abstraction mechanism, the metal center directly abstracts a hydrogen atom from the hydrocarbon, forming a metal hydride and a carbocation. This carbocation can then react with an oxygen molecule to form the desired product. These principles are applied in the industrial selective oxidation of alkanes, such as the conversion of butane to maleic anhydride, by using a catalyst with isolated metal centers that can selectively activate the C-H bonds of the alkane and promote the desired oxidation reaction.

答：孤立的金属中心在氧化物材料上通过充当路易斯酸位点来促进异质催化中的C-H键活化，这些位点可以稳定反应的过渡态。Mars van Krevelen机制涉及金属中心从烃中抽象一个氢原子，形成金属氢化物和一个碳中心的自由基。这个自由基然后可以与一个氧分子反应，形成一个过氧中间体，随后分解形成所需的产品。在H-抽象机制中，金属中心直接从烃中抽象一个氢原子，形成金属氢化物和一个碳正离子。这个碳正离子然后可以与一个氧分子反应，形成所需的产品。这些原理被应用于工业选择性氧化烷烃，例如丁烷转化为马来酸酐，通过使用具有孤立金属中心的催化剂，这些中心可以选择性地活化烷烃的C-H键，并促进所需的氧化反应。
