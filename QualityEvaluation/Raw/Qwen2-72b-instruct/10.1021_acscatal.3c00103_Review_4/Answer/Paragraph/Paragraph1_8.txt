The influence of catalyst physicochemical properties on dehydrogenation reactions, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH), is profound, with chemical qualities and morphological characteristics playing pivotal roles in catalytic efficiency and selectivity \[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]. Acid strength and alloy formation are critical factors, with the addition of Ca to Al2O3 reducing acid centers and enhancing alloy formation, leading to improved propylene selectivity and stability \[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]. The Ca-promoted catalyst displays lower deactivation rates and higher residual activity at 600°C, indicating enhanced resistance to coke formation \[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]. This catalyst has a slightly higher activation energy, leading to reduced initial activity but superior performance in terms of activity and selectivity at steady state conditions \[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\].

Lewis acid sites from unsaturated AlIII in Al2O3 are crucial for dehydrogenation reactions, with γ-Al2O3-200 exhibiting the highest yield due to its high level of strong acid sites \[10.1039/D0CY00779J\]. Propane conversion positively correlates with the amount of Lewis acid sites, indicating their role as active sites \[10.1039/D0CY00779J\]. The regenerability of the γ-Al2O3-200 catalyst, allowing recovery after deactivation, makes it sustainable for industrial use \[10.1039/D0CY00779J\].

Catalyst properties, including chemical and morphological aspects, are crucial in dehydrogenation reactions. A diammonium-type bromide surfactant and sodium-free silica source synthesize zeolites with silanol nests, confirmed by spectroscopy \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. These nests enable the formation of Pt3Y alloy nanoparticles, which exhibit high catalytic durability, propane conversion, and propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. Pt3Y shows fully reversible hydrogen chemisorption at room temperature, unlike monometallic Pt \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br and PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-OH outperform PtY(0.5)/MZ-dg in durability, maintaining high propane conversion and propylene selectivity for 20 days \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. In contrast, PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br-NaCl deactivates rapidly \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. The spent PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br catalyst has 37 wt% coke deposition \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]. Support modification strategies, such as using specific surfactants and silica sources, optimize zeolite properties for enhanced catalytic efficiency and selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\].

CrOx on high-silica HZSM-5, synthesized by wet impregnation, exhibits high activity and stability for PDH, highlighting the role of Cr species and Brnsted acid sites \[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]. Zeolite supports, particularly HZSM-5, provide a unique environment for reactions, with Brnsted acid sites crucial for propane adsorption and activation \[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]. High surface area and well-defined pores in HZSM-5 enhance Cr species dispersion and stability \[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]. Cr loading amount and SiO2/Al2O3 ratio significantly affect catalytic performance \[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]. Zeolites, with tunable acidity and thermal stability, are versatile supports for catalytic reactions, making HZSM-5 an ideal support for PDH due to its suitable acidic sites \[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\].

催化剂的理化性质对脱氢反应，特别是丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的影响深远，化学品质和形态特征在催化效率和选择性中扮演着关键角色\[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]。酸强度和合金形成是重要因素，向Al2O3中添加Ca可以减少酸中心并增强合金形成，从而提高丙烯选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]。Ca促进的催化剂在600°C下表现出较低的失活率和较高的残余活性，表明对焦炭形成的抵抗能力增强\[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]。这种催化剂具有略高的活化能，导致初始活性降低，但在稳态条件下表现出更优的活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.cej.2020.126656\]。

Al2O3中未饱和AlIII的Lewis酸位点对脱氢反应至关重要，γ-Al2O3-200由于其高水平的强酸位点而表现出最高的产量\[10.1039/D0CY00779J\]。丙烷转化率与Lewis酸位点的数量呈正相关，表明它们作为活性位点的作用\[10.1039/D0CY00779J\]。γ-Al2O3-200催化剂的可再生性，允许在失活后恢复，使其适用于工业使用\[10.1039/D0CY00779J\]。

催化剂的性质，包括化学和形态方面，在脱氢反应中至关重要。使用二铵型溴化物表面活性剂和无钠硅源合成的沸石具有硅醇巢，通过光谱学得到证实\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。这些巢穴使Pt3Y合金纳米粒子的形成成为可能，这些粒子表现出高催化耐久性、丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。与单金属Pt不同，Pt3Y在室温下显示出完全可逆的氢化学吸附\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br和PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-OH在耐久性方面优于PtY(0.5)/MZ-dg，保持高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性长达20天\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。相比之下，PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br-NaCl迅速失活\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。PtY(0.5)/MZ-sf-Br催化剂的残余物中沉积了37 wt%的焦炭\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。使用特定表面活性剂和硅源的支撑修改策略优化了沸石的性质，以提高催化效率和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。

通过湿浸渍在高硅HZSM-5上制备的CrOx在PDH中表现出高活性和稳定性，突出了Cr物种和Brnsted酸位点的作用\[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]。特别是HZSM-5等沸石支撑提供了独特的反应环境，其中Brnsted酸位点对丙烷吸附和活化至关重要\[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]。HZSM-5中高表面积和明确定义的孔隙增强了Cr物种的分散和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]。Cr负载量和SiO2/Al2O3比率对催化性能有显著影响\[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]。沸石，具有可调酸性和热稳定性，是催化反应的多功能支撑，HZSM-5由于其适宜的酸性位点而成为PDH的理想支撑\[10.1016/j.jechem.2019.12.010\]。
