                    The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. 1-D active sites, particularly those found on the edges of 2-D h-BN sheets and BNNTs, exhibit higher propene selectivity than conventional metal oxide catalysts, with BNNTs showing higher propane consumption rates. The reaction mechanism involves hydrogen abstraction from propane, forming a surface-stabilized propyl intermediate and subsequent radical rebound to produce propene. Among the 1-D edge active sites, the zigzag B-terminated edge is more active \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Strategies to enhance 1-D BN edge active sites include preoxygen modification to reduce the induction period, increasing edge density by reducing BN nanosheet size, and aggregating active components into 1-D shaped materials. The stability of 1-D dispersed active sites can be improved by designing composite catalysts, such as h-BNxIn2O3, which can extend catalytic stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\].

                    B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度显著影响丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。1-D活性位点，特别是2-D h-BN片层和BNNT边缘上的位点，比传统金属氧化物催化剂具有更高的丙烯选择性，BNNT显示出更高的丙烷消耗率。反应机制涉及从丙烷中抽氢，形成表面稳定的丙基中间体，随后通过自由基反弹产生丙烯。在1-D边缘活性位点中，锯齿状B-终止边缘更活跃\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。增强1-D BN边缘活性位点的策略包括预氧改性以缩短诱导期，通过减小BN纳米片尺寸增加边缘密度，以及将活性成分聚集为1-D形状的材料。1-D分散活性位点的稳定性可以通过设计复合催化剂，如h-BNxIn2O3，来提高，这可以延长催化稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。
