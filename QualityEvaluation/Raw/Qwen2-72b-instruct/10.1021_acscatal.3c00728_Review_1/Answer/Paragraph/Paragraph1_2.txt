The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. 1-D active sites, particularly those found on the edges of hexagonal boron nitride (h-BN) sheets and boron nitride nanotubes (BNNTs), exhibit higher selectivity for propene than conventional metal oxide catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. These sites' high reactivity, selectivity, and stability enhance propene productivity, making them commercially viable \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. The reaction mechanism involves hydrogen abstraction from propane, forming a surface-stabilized propyl intermediate and subsequent radical rebound to produce propene \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. DFT calculations reveal that the zigzag B-terminated edge is more active than other 1-D edge types \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Strategies to improve 1-D BN edge active sites include preoxygen modification to shorten the induction period, increasing edge density by reducing BN nanosheet size, and aggregating active components into 1-D shapes \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. The stability of 1-D dispersed active sites can be enhanced by designing composite catalysts, such as h-BNxIn2O3, which strengthens interlayer forces and reduces oxygen affinity \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\].

Isolated boron species in B-based catalysts dynamically transform into aggregated ones, enhancing catalytic activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Aggregated BOx species, particularly BOH groups hydrogen bonded with adjacent SiOH groups, serve as active centers for efficient ODHP at low temperatures \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Aggregated boron species are more effective in propane CH bond activation than isolated species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Uniformly anchoring boron species to a hydroxyl-rich MFI-type zeolite matrix may construct highly active boron centers \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\].

Defective trigonal boron species (B[3]a and B[3]b) are crucial for propane oxidative dehydrogenation activity in B-based catalysts \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]. Understanding these 1-D edge active sites' properties and reaction mechanisms can lead to the design of more efficient catalysts for commercial ODHP applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in understanding the spatial distribution, classification, and performance of active sites in B-based catalysts for ODHP, with a focus on 1-D edge active sites, open up new avenues for catalyst design and reaction mechanism elucidation. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of innovative strategies to enhance the stability and reactivity of 1-D edge active sites, such as the use of novel precursors and synthesis methods. Additionally, the exploration of the synergistic effects between 1-D edge active sites and other active sites, such as 2-D and 3-D sites, could lead to the design of more efficient and selective catalysts for commercial ODHP applications. The integration of computational methods, such as DFT calculations, with experimental techniques could provide deeper insights into the reaction mechanisms and help guide the rational design of B-based catalysts.

B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度对丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应性能有显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。1-D活性位点，特别是在六方氮化硼(h-BN)片层和硼氮化纳米管(BNNTs)边缘的位点，对丙烯的选择性高于传统金属氧化物催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。这些位点的高反应性、选择性和稳定性提高了丙烯的生产力，使其具有商业可行性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。反应机制涉及从丙烷中抽氢，形成表面稳定的丙基中间体，随后通过自由基反弹产生丙烯\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。DFT计算表明，锯齿状B-终止边缘比其他1-D边缘类型更活跃\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。改进1-D BN边缘活性位点的策略包括预氧改性以缩短诱导期，通过减小BN纳米片尺寸增加边缘密度，以及将活性组分聚集为1-D形状\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。通过设计复合催化剂，如h-BNxIn2O3，可以增强1-D分散活性位点的稳定性，这种催化剂加强了层间力并降低了氧亲和力\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。

B基催化剂中的孤立硼物种动态转化为聚集态，增强了催化活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。聚集的BOx物种，特别是与相邻SiOH基团氢键结合的BOH基团，作为低温下高效ODHP的活性中心\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。聚集的硼物种在丙烷CH键活化方面比孤立物种更有效\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。将硼物种均匀锚定在富含羟基的MFI型沸石基质上可能构建出高度活性的硼中心\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。

缺陷三角硼物种(B[3]a和B[3]b)对B基催化剂中丙烷氧化脱氢活性至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。理解这些1-D边缘活性位点的特性和反应机制可以促进更高效催化剂的设计，用于商业ODHP应用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望: 在B基催化剂中，对ODHP活性位点的空间分布、分类和性能的深入理解，特别是对1-D边缘活性位点的聚焦，为催化剂设计和反应机制阐明开辟了新的途径。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发创新策略以增强1-D边缘活性位点的稳定性和反应性，例如使用新型前体和合成方法。此外，探索1-D边缘活性位点与其他活性位点，如2-D和3-D位点之间的协同效应，可能导致设计出更高效、更选择性的催化剂，用于商业ODHP应用。将DFT计算等计算方法与实验技术相结合，可以提供对反应机制的更深入见解，并有助于指导B基催化剂的理性设计。
