                    The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. 1-D edge active sites, particularly on h-BN sheets and BNNTs, exhibit higher propene selectivity than conventional metal oxide catalysts, with BNNTs showing higher mass-specific propane consumption rates. BN materials' high reactivity, selectivity, and stability enhance propene productivity, making them commercially viable for ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. The reaction mechanism involves hydrogen abstraction from propane, forming a surface-stabilized propyl intermediate and subsequent radical rebound to produce propene \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Strategies to improve 1-D BN edge active sites include preoxygen modification to reduce the induction period, increasing edge density by reducing BN nanosheet size, and aggregating active components into 1-D shapes \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Stability is enhanced by designing composite catalysts like h-BNxIn2O3, which strengthens interlayer forces and weakens BN bond affinity to oxygen \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Understanding the unique properties and mechanisms of 1-D edge active sites is pivotal for designing efficient catalysts for commercial ODHP applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in understanding the spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts for ODHP reactions, particularly the 1-D edge active sites, offer a fertile ground for future research. Innovations in catalyst design, such as the development of composite materials like h-BNxIn2O3, which enhance stability by strengthening interlayer forces and weakening BN bond affinity to oxygen, can lead to more efficient and stable catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Future research might focus on the optimization of 1-D edge active sites through strategies like preoxygen modification, increasing edge density, and aggregating active components into 1-D shapes \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Additionally, the exploration of novel boron-based materials with enhanced mass and heat transfer properties, as demonstrated by the h-BN/SiO2 nanofiber catalyst \[10.1002/cctc.202100476\], could open new avenues for improving catalyst performance. The design of catalysts that effectively utilize active sites while minimizing secondary reactions of deep oxidation, as suggested by the h-BN wash-coated cordierite monolithic catalyst \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028\], is another promising direction. The understanding of 1-D edge active sites' unique properties and reaction mechanisms, as evidenced by the oxygen-terminated armchair edge of BN (>BOON<) \[10.1126/science.aaf7885\], could further contribute to the development of more efficient catalysts for commercial ODHP applications.

                    B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度对丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的性能有显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。特别是在h-BN片和BNNTs上的1-D边缘活性位点，与传统金属氧化物催化剂相比，显示出更高的丙烯选择性，BNNTs表现出更高的丙烷消耗率\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。BN材料的高反应性、选择性和稳定性提高了丙烯生产力，使其在商业上适用于ODHP\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。反应机制涉及从丙烷中抽氢，形成表面稳定的丙基中间体，随后产生丙烯\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。改进1-D BN边缘活性位点的策略包括预氧改性以缩短诱导期，通过减小BN纳米片尺寸增加边缘密度，以及将活性成分聚集为1-D形状\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。通过设计像h-BNxIn2O3这样的复合催化剂，增强层间力，减弱BN对氧气的亲和力，可以提高稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。理解1-D边缘活性位点的独特性质和机制对于设计高效的商业ODHP应用催化剂至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在B基催化剂中，特别是1-D边缘活性位点，对ODHP反应的活性位点空间分布和维度的深入理解，为未来的研究提供了肥沃的土壤。像h-BNxIn2O3这样的复合材料的创新设计，通过增强层间力和减弱BN对氧气的亲和力来提高稳定性，可以导致更高效和稳定的催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。未来的研究可能集中在通过预氧改性、增加边缘密度和将活性成分聚集为1-D形状来优化1-D边缘活性位点\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。此外，探索具有增强质量和热传递性能的新型硼基材料，如h-BN/SiO2纳米纤维催化剂\[10.1002/cctc.202100476\]，可以为提高催化剂性能开辟新的途径。设计有效利用活性位点同时最小化深度氧化副反应的催化剂，如h-BN涂覆的堇青石整体催化剂\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028\]，是另一个有前景的方向。理解1-D边缘活性位点的独特性质和反应机制，如BN的氧终止扶手椅边缘(>BOON<)\[10.1126/science.aaf7885\]，可以进一步促进更高效商业ODHP应用催化剂的开发。
