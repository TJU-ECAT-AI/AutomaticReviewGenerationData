                    The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. 1-D active sites, found on the edges of 2-D h-BN sheets and BNNTs, exhibit higher selectivity for propene than conventional metal oxide catalysts, with BNNTs showing higher mass-specific propane consumption rates. These sites enable high reactivity, selectivity, and stability, enhancing propene productivity. The reaction mechanism involves hydrogen abstraction from propane, forming a surface-stabilized propyl intermediate and subsequent radical rebound to produce propene \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Strategies to improve 1-D BN edge active sites include preoxygen modification to reduce the induction period, increasing edge density by reducing BN nanosheet size, and aggregating active components into 1-D shapes \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Enhancing stability involves designing composite catalysts like h-BNxIn2O3, which can extend catalytic stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]. Understanding the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of these 1-D edge active sites is crucial for designing more efficient catalysts for commercial applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\].

                    B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度显著影响丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。1-D活性位点，位于2-D h-BN片层和BNNTs的边缘，对丙烯的选择性高于传统金属氧化物催化剂，BNNTs表现出更高的丙烷质量消耗率。这些位点使高反应性、选择性和稳定性得以实现，从而提高丙烯的生产力。反应机制涉及从丙烷中抽氢，形成表面稳定的丙基中间体，随后通过自由基反弹产生丙烯\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。改进1-D BN边缘活性位点的策略包括预氧改性以缩短诱导期，通过减小BN纳米片尺寸增加边缘密度，以及将活性组分聚集为1-D形状\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。增强稳定性涉及设计如h-BNxIn2O3等复合催化剂，可以延长催化稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1\]。理解这些1-D边缘活性位点的独特性质和反应机制对于设计更高效的商业应用催化剂至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。
