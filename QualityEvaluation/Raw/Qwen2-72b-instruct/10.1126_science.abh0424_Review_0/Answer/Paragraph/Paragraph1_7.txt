                    The concept of tandem catalysis at the nanoscale is introduced as a powerful approach to address the challenges of integrating multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes, enabling precise spatiotemporal control over reactive intermediates \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. This is exemplified by the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst, which couples catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, translating macroscale engineering strategies into a single nanoscale tandem catalyst particle for propane to propylene conversion \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. The proximity and hierarchy of catalytic sites within the tandem catalyst are crucial for regulating the transport of intermediates, facilitating the on-purpose production of propylene and breaking the thermodynamic limitation of nonoxidative catalytic reactions by instantly removing H2 through selective combustion \[10.1126/science.abh0424\]. The porous overcoating structure of the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst regulates the transport of hydrogen species, promoting selective H2 combustion, which in turn drives the catalytic propane dehydrogenation forward \[10.1126/science.abh0424\]. This tandem catalysis has implications for energy efficiency and process optimization in the chemical industry, potentially filling the supply-demand gap in the alkene market \[10.1126/science.abh0424\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in tandem catalysis at the nanoscale, as exemplified by the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst, open up new avenues for the development of high-performance, multifunctional nanostructured catalysts for multiple-step chemical reactions \[10.1038/nchem.1018\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the design of novel bimetallic catalysts and the combination of different catalytic functionalities on a single support to further enhance energy efficiency and process optimization \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. The synergistic effect of bimetallic catalysts, such as PtSn, can lead to higher selectivity and stability, reducing the need for regeneration and thus saving energy \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. The literature highlights the importance of careful catalyst design to minimize side reactions and maximize the desired product yield, which is a key goal of tandem catalysis \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. The potential for tandem catalysis to address the challenges of integrating multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes and improve energy efficiency and process optimization is significant, and further research in this area could lead to the development of more sustainable and economically viable processes for olefin production \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\].

                    纳米尺度上的串联催化概念被引入作为一种强大的方法，以解决化学工程过程中整合多个反应步骤的挑战，使对反应中间体的时空控制更加精确\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。这通过In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂得到例证，该催化剂将催化丙烷脱氢与选择性H2燃烧相结合，将宏观工程策略转化为单一纳米尺度串联催化剂粒子，用于丙烷到丙烯的转化\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。串联催化剂中催化位点的邻近性和层次性对于调节中间体的传输至关重要，促进了丙烯的直接生产，并通过即时去除H2进行选择性燃烧，打破了非氧化催化反应的热力学限制\[10.1126/science.abh0424\]。In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂的多孔包覆结构调节了氢物种的传输，促进了选择性H2燃烧，进而推动了催化丙烷脱氢的进行\[10.1126/science.abh0424\]。这种串联催化对化学工业中的能源效率和过程优化具有重要意义，可能填补烯烃市场供需缺口\[10.1126/science.abh0424\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 以In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂为例的纳米尺度串联催化进展，为开发高性能、多功能纳米结构催化剂用于多步化学反应开辟了新途径\[10.1038/nchem.1018\]。未来的研究可能集中在设计新型双金属催化剂和在单一载体上结合不同的催化功能，以进一步提高能源效率和过程优化\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。双金属催化剂如PtSn的协同效应可以导致更高的选择性和稳定性，减少再生需求，从而节省能源\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。文献强调了精心设计催化剂以最小化副反应和最大化所需产品产量的重要性，这是串联催化的一个关键目标\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。串联催化在解决化学工程过程中整合多个反应步骤的挑战、提高能源效率和过程优化方面的潜力巨大，对该领域的进一步研究可能导致开发更可持续和经济上可行的烯烃生产过程\[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]。
