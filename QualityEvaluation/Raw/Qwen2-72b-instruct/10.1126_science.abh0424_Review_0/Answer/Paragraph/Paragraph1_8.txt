                    The concept of tandem catalysis at the nanoscale is pivotal in addressing the challenges of integrating multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes, as it provides precise spatiotemporal control over reactive intermediates \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. This is exemplified by the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst, which couples catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, translating macroscale engineering strategies into a single nanoscale tandem catalyst particle for propane to propylene conversion \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. This tandem catalyst design overcomes the limitations of physical or temporal separation of dehydrogenation and combustion, leading to rapid and stable oxidative dehydrogenation of propane at high per-pass yields exceeding the PDH equilibrium \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. The nanoscale overcoating geometry of tandem catalysis offers opportunities for highly selective catalytic performance in grand challenge reactions, potentially improving energy efficiency and process optimization in chemical engineering \[10.1126/science.abd4441\]. The In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst combines the dehydrogenation activity of In2O3 with the hydrogen combustion activity of Pt, potentially improving energy efficiency and process optimization by coupling two reaction steps on a single catalyst \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. This approach could lead to more efficient use of resources and energy by simultaneously converting propane to propylene and utilizing the generated hydrogen in a separate reaction, thus potentially enhancing overall process efficiency \[10.1039/d0cs00814a\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in tandem catalysis, particularly the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst for coupling catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion, open up new avenues for the design of high-performance, multifunctional nanostructured catalysts. Future research might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can further optimize the process by improving selectivity and stability, reducing the need for regeneration, and saving energy. The synergistic effect of bimetallic catalysts, such as PtSn, in tandem catalysis could be explored to enhance catalytic activity and selectivity. Additionally, the use of advanced materials and fabrication techniques could lead to the creation of more efficient tandem catalysts with improved control over the proximity and hierarchy of catalytic sites, thus regulating the transport of intermediates more effectively. The potential for integrating tandem catalysis with other process engineering concepts, such as reactor cascades, membrane reactors, and chemical looping, could also be investigated to address the limitations of current processes. The ultimate goal is to achieve higher energy efficiency and better process optimization, potentially filling the supply-demand gap in the alkene market.

                    纳米尺度的串联催化概念在解决化学工程过程中整合多个反应步骤的挑战中起着关键作用，因为它提供了对反应中间体的精确时空控制\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。这在In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂中得到了体现，该催化剂将催化丙烷脱氢与选择性H2燃烧相结合，将宏观工程策略转化为单一的纳米尺度串联催化剂粒子，用于丙烷到丙烯的转化\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。这种串联催化剂设计克服了脱氢和燃烧物理或时间分离的局限性，导致丙烷氧化脱氢的快速和稳定，每过一次的产量超过PDH平衡\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。纳米尺度串联催化的过涂层几何结构为大挑战反应提供了高度选择性的催化性能的机会，可能在化学工程中提高能源效率和过程优化\[10.1126/science.abd4441\]。In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂将In2O3的脱氢活性与Pt的氢燃烧活性相结合，通过在单一催化剂上耦合两个反应步骤，可能提高能源效率和过程优化\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。这种方法可以通过同时将丙烷转化为丙烯并利用产生的氢在单独的反应中，从而可能提高整体过程效率，更有效地利用资源和能源\[10.1039/d0cs00814a\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望：在串联催化，特别是In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂用于耦合催化丙烷脱氢与选择性H2燃烧的进展，为设计高性能、多功能纳米结构催化剂开辟了新的途径。未来的研究可能集中在开发新型催化剂，通过提高选择性和稳定性，减少再生需求，节省能源，进一步优化过程。PtSn等双金属催化剂在串联催化中的协同效应可以被探索，以增强催化活性和选择性。此外，使用先进材料和制造技术可以导致更有效的串联催化剂的创建，具有改善的控制催化位点的接近性和层次性，从而更有效地调节中间体的传输。将串联催化与反应器级联、膜反应器和化学循环等其他过程工程概念的集成的潜力也可以被研究，以解决当前过程的局限性。最终目标是实现更高的能源效率和更好的过程优化，可能填补烯烃市场供需缺口。
