<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.Tandem catalysis integrates multiple reactions, promising to enhance chemical processing by minimizing separations, coupling reactions to drive transformations forward, and controlling selectivity in reaction networks. However, precise spatiotemporal control over reactive intermediates has been challenging.
2.The concept of tandem catalysis at the nanoscale was applied to develop a single catalyst particle for oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) to propylene, addressing the challenge of integrating multiple reaction steps in chemical engineering processes.
3.The In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 tandem catalyst was designed for selective catalytic dehydrogenation of propane to propylene, demonstrating the potential of nanoscale tandem catalysis for energy efficiency and process optimization.
4.The net effect of the tandem catalysis is rapid and stable oxidative dehydrogenation of propane at high per-pass yields exceeding the PDH equilibrium, showcasing the potential for enhanced energy efficiency and process optimization.
5.Tandem and cooperative catalysis have the potential to revolutionize chemical production by minimizing separations, coupling reactions to drive transformations forward, and controlling selectivity in reaction networks, as exemplified by the In2O3-Pt/Al2O3 catalyst for coupling catalytic propane dehydrogenation with selective H2 combustion.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.串联催化将多个反应集成在一起，承诺通过最小化分离、耦合反应以推动转化向前发展或控制反应网络中的选择性来增强化学加工。然而，对反应中间体的精确时空控制一直难以捉摸。
2.在纳米尺度上串联催化的概念被应用于开发单一催化剂粒子，用于氧化脱氢丙烷(ODHP)至丙烯，解决了化学工程过程中整合多个反应步骤的挑战。
3.设计了In2O3-Pt/Al2O3串联催化剂用于选择性催化丙烷脱氢至丙烯，展示了纳米尺度串联催化在能源效率和过程优化方面的潜力。
4.串联催化的净效应是丙烷的快速和稳定的氧化脱氢，其高每过一次的产量超过了PDH平衡，展示了增强能源效率和过程优化的潜力。
5.串联和协同催化有可能通过最小化分离、耦合反应以推动转化向前发展以及控制反应网络中的选择性来彻底改变化学生产，正如In2O3-Pt/Al2O3催化剂将催化丙烷脱氢与选择性H2燃烧耦合所展示的那样。
