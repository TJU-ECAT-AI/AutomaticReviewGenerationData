                The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy developed by Yu et al. \[10.1002/anie.202007044\] addresses the time-scale mismatch challenges in direct synthesis procedures for metal encapsulation by expediting the crystallization of zeolites to synchronize with the rapid formation of nanoclusters. This strategy leads to the ultrafast in situ encapsulation of metal nanoclusters into zeolites within just a few minutes \[10.1002/anie.202007044\]. The effects on the formation, structure, and catalytic performance of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites are significant. The resultant Pt/Sn-ZSM-5 exhibits excellent stability and activity in the dehydrogenation of propane to propene, with exceptional selectivity and stability even after multiple cycles of reaction and regeneration at high temperatures \[10.1002/anie.202007044\]. The role of Sn in modulating the interaction between the Pt precursor and the zeolite precursor affects the size and dispersion of the Pt nanoclusters, leading to improved catalytic performance in propane conversion \[10.1002/anie.202007044\].

                The ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy allows the in situ encapsulation of PtSn clusters with approximately 1 nm size inside high-silica ZSM-5 zeolites through a rapid one-pot synthesis procedure \[10.1039/D0CS01260B\]. This results in a Pt/Sn-ZSM-5 catalyst with superior catalytic stability, as evidenced by its propane conversion remaining at 45% after 24 hours in the PDH reaction at 600 degrees Celsius \[10.1039/D0CS01260B\].

                Yu et al. developed an ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategy by combining precursor age, seed additives, and a fast-heating tubular reactor, which allows complete crystallization of ZSM-5 zeolites within 5 minutes at 170 C, or 2 minutes at 190 C \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_3\]. This ultrafast crystallization rate enables the full encapsulation of PtSn clusters within ZSM-5 zeolites. The Pt loadings in the silica matrix increase with increasing zeolite crystallinity, indicating the formation of Pt nanoclusters. The Sn species transform into amorphous SnO2 initially and then into Td Sn species within the zeolite framework, which improves Pt dispersion. By fixing the Pt loading at 0.32 wt % and increasing Sn loadings from 0 to 1.2 wt %, the size of PtSn clusters decreases from 4 nm to 1 nm. The optimized PtSn-ZSM-5 catalyst shows an initial propane conversion of 65%, significantly higher than that of impregnated Pt/Sn-ZSM-5 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_3\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in ultrafast zeolite crystallization strategies for metal encapsulation, as demonstrated by Yu et al., have opened new avenues for the development of highly efficient and stable catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. The ability to synchronize the rapid formation of nanoclusters with zeolite crystallization has led to the creation of Pt/Sn-ZSM-5 catalysts with superior catalytic performance and stability. Future research endeavors might focus on exploring the potential of this strategy in the encapsulation of other metal clusters and in different zeolite frameworks to expand the scope of applications in various catalytic processes. Additionally, the investigation of the underlying mechanisms of the ultrafast encapsulation process and the role of Sn in modulating the interaction between the Pt precursor and the zeolite precursor could provide further insights into the optimization of catalyst design for enhanced catalytic performance.

                尤等人开发的超快速沸石结晶策略\[10.1002/anie.202007044\]通过加快沸石的结晶以与纳米团簇的快速形成同步，解决了直接合成金属封装的时间尺度不匹配问题。这一策略实现了在几分钟内将金属纳米团簇原位封装到沸石中的超快速过程\[10.1002/anie.202007044\]。PtSn团簇在ZSM-5沸石中的形成、结构和催化性能的影响显著。所得的Pt/Sn-ZSM-5在丙烷脱氢到丙烯中表现出优异的稳定性和活性，具有出色的选择性和稳定性，即使在高温下多次反应和再生后也是如此\[10.1002/anie.202007044\]。Sn在调节Pt前体与沸石前体之间的相互作用，影响Pt纳米团簇的大小和分散，从而提高丙烷转化中的催化性能方面起着关键作用\[10.1002/anie.202007044\]。

                超快速沸石结晶策略允许在高硅ZSM-5沸石中通过快速一锅合成程序原位封装大约1 nm大小的PtSn团簇\[10.1039/D0CS01260B\]。这导致Pt/Sn-ZSM-5催化剂具有优越的催化稳定性，其丙烷转化率在600摄氏度的PDH反应中24小时后仍保持在45%\[10.1039/D0CS01260B\]。

                尤等人通过结合前体年龄、种子添加剂和快速加热管式反应器开发了超快速沸石结晶策略，使ZSM-5沸石在170 C下5分钟内或190 C下2分钟内完全结晶\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_3\]。这种超快速结晶速率使PtSn团簇完全封装在ZSM-5沸石中成为可能。随着沸石结晶度的增加，硅酸盐基质中的Pt负载量增加，表明Pt纳米团簇的形成。Sn物种最初完全接枝到硅酸盐基质中，随后转化为沸石框架内的无定形SnO2，然后转化为Td Sn物种，从而改善Pt的分散。通过将Pt负载固定在0.32 wt %，并将Sn负载从0增加到1.2 wt %，PtSn团簇的大小从4 nm减小到1 nm。优化的PtSn-ZSM-5催化剂的初始丙烷转化率为65%，显著高于浸渍的Pt/Sn-ZSM-5\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_3\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 尤等人展示的超快速沸石结晶策略在金属封装中的进展为开发高效稳定的丙烷脱氢催化剂开辟了新的途径。将纳米团簇的快速形成与沸石结晶同步的能力导致了具有优越催化性能和稳定性的Pt/Sn-ZSM-5催化剂的创造。未来的研究努力可能集中在探索这一策略在其他金属团簇的封装和不同沸石框架中的潜力，以扩大在各种催化过程中的应用范围。此外，对超快速封装过程的潜在机制以及Sn在调节Pt前体与沸石前体之间相互作用中的作用的调查可能为优化催化剂设计以提高催化性能提供进一步的见解。
