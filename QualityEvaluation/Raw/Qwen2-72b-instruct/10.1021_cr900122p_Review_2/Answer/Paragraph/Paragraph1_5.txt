<English>
    Metal-based catalysts, particularly vanadium oxides, play a pivotal role in alkane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) processes, with optimal performance observed at intermediate vanadium oxide (VOx) loadings and domain sizes, enhancing olefin selectivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. The efficiency of these catalysts is influenced by the feed's redox properties, which affect the surface state of vanadium, impacting selectivity \[10.1021/cr00083a003\]. Surface reduction, facilitated by N2O over O2, boosts propylene yield \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Among supports, silica exhibits superior selectivity over alumina and titania \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Additives, such as phosphorus (P), potassium (K), calcium (Ca), and nickel (Ni), modify the acid-base and redox properties of the catalyst, significantly impacting its activity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Alkali dopants, while reducing activity, increase propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. The isolation of active sites is critical, with microporous and mesoporous materials proving effective \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Molybdenum-based catalysts, although less active than vanadium oxides, exhibit enhanced reactivity with increased molybdenum density \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Alkali metals inhibit the reduction of MoOx, slowing down carbon-hydrogen (CH) bond activation, yet enhance propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\].
    ※※※
    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in metal-based catalysts for alkane transformations, particularly in oxidative dehydrogenation and zeolite-catalyzed processes, have opened up new avenues for exploration. Future research efforts might focus on the development of novel catalysts with enhanced selectivity and activity, leveraging the insights gained from the study of vanadium oxides and metal-exchanged zeolites. The optimization of reaction conditions, such as temperature, pressure, and feed composition, will be crucial for maximizing efficiency. Moreover, the integration of computational methods, including density functional theory (DFT) and molecular dynamics simulations, can provide deeper insights into the catalytic mechanisms and guide the design of more efficient catalysts. The exploration of alternative supports and additives, as well as the engineering of catalysts with tailored pore structures, could further enhance the performance of these systems. Additionally, the investigation of the role of surface chemistry and the development of strategies for the controlled synthesis of active sites with specific functionalities will be essential for advancing the field. The pursuit of sustainable and environmentally friendly processes, such as the direct conversion of methane to higher-value products, will also be a significant area of focus, driven by the need to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and optimize the use of natural resources.

    金属基催化剂，尤其是钒氧化物，在烷烃氧化脱氢（ODH）过程中扮演着关键角色，其中在中间钒氧化物（VOx）负载量和域尺寸下观察到最佳性能，增强了烯烃选择性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。这些催化剂的效率受到原料的氧化还原性质的影响，这些性质影响了钒的表面状态，进而影响了选择性\[10.1021/cr00083a003\]。表面还原，通过N2O而非O2促进，提高了丙烯产量\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。在支撑材料中，二氧化硅的优选性优于氧化铝和二氧化钛\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。添加剂，如磷（P）、钾（K）、钙（Ca）和镍（Ni），通过修改催化剂的酸碱性和氧化还原性质，显著影响其活性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。碱金属掺杂剂，虽然降低了活性，但增加了丙烯的选择性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。活性位点的隔离至关重要，微孔和介孔材料被证明是有效的\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。钼基催化剂，尽管活性低于钒氧化物，但随着钼密度的增加，表现出增强的反应性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。碱金属抑制了MoOx的还原，减缓了碳氢（CH）键的活化，但增强了丙烯的选择性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。
    ※※※
    综合理解与未来展望: 金属基催化剂在烷烃转化，特别是在氧化脱氢和沸石催化过程中的进展，为探索开辟了新的途径。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发具有增强选择性和活性的新型催化剂上，利用从研究钒氧化物和金属交换沸石中获得的见解。反应条件的优化，如温度、压力和原料组成，对于效率最大化至关重要。此外，密度泛函理论（DFT）和分子动力学模拟等计算方法的整合，可以提供对催化机制的更深入理解，并指导更有效催化剂的设计。探索替代支撑材料和添加剂，以及具有定制孔结构的催化剂的工程，可以进一步增强这些系统的性能。此外，表面化学的作用的探究，以及具有特定功能的活性位点的受控合成策略的发展，对于推动该领域的发展至关重要。追求可持续和环境友好的过程，如直接将甲烷转化为高价值产品，也将是一个重要的关注领域，由减少温室气体排放和优化自然资源利用的需求驱动。
