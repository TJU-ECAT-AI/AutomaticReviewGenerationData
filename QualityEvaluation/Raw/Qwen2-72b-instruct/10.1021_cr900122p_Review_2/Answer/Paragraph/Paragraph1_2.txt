Metal-based catalysts, particularly vanadium and molybdenum oxides, are pivotal in alkane oxidative dehydrogenation processes \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. The efficiency of these catalysts is intricately linked to the clustering of metal cations, the acid-base properties, the valence state, and the electronic features of the catalysts. In propane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH), the activity is enhanced with increasing V2O5 loading, peaking at intermediate VOx domain sizes for optimal surface access and reactivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Selectivity to olefins is maximized at intermediate active phase loadings, associated with a monolayer structure \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Surface reduction significantly boosts propylene selectivity, with N2O proving to be more effective than O2 in this regard \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. The choice of support also plays a critical role, with V2O5-ZrO2 showing the highest reactivity, followed by V2O5-Al2O3 and V2O5-SiO2 \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Additives can tweak the acid-base and redox properties of the catalysts, significantly impacting their performance \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Alkali and alkaline earth dopants, while reducing activity, can enhance propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Mo-based catalysts, though less active than vanadia, exhibit peak propane ODH rates at a density of 4.5 Mo/nm2 \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. The presence of alkali can alter the MoOx structure and properties, reducing turnover rates with a higher dopant/Mo ratio and increased basicity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\].

基于金属基催化剂，特别是钒和钼氧化物，在烷烃氧化脱氢过程中的关键作用\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]，催化剂的效率紧密关联于金属阳离子的聚集、酸碱性质、价态以及电子特性。在丙烷氧化脱氢(ODH)中，随着V2O5负载量的增加，活性得到提升，当VOx域尺寸适中时，表面接触和反应性达到最佳\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。烯烃的选择性在中间活性相负载量时达到最大，与单层结构相关\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。表面还原显著提高了丙烯的选择性，N2O在这方面比O2更有效\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。载体的选择也起着关键作用，V2O5-ZrO2表现出最高的活性，其次是V2O5-Al2O3和V2O5-SiO2\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。添加剂可以调整催化剂的酸碱和氧化还原性质，对性能产生重大影响\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。碱金属和碱土金属掺杂剂，虽然降低了活性，却能提高丙烯的选择性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。基于钼的催化剂，尽管活性低于钒，但在4.5 Mo/nm2的密度下，丙烷ODH速率达到峰值\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。碱金属的存在可以改变MoOx的结构和性质，随着掺杂剂/Mo比率的增加和碱性的增强，周转率降低\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。
