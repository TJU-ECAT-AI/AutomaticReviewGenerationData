<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The catalytic conversion of methane to more valuable liquid fuels is still a challenging problem in the field of catalysis.
2.The direct partial oxidation of methane to C1 oxygenates, formaldehyde or methanol, has been one of the most studied routes since it could potentially replace the conventional three-step industrial process.
3.The nature and dispersion of the metal oxide phase have a significant influence on the catalyst's behavior.
4.Banares et al. reported selectivities above 90% at methane conversion of 0.6% using a MoO3/SiO2 catalyst.
5.The results of the partial oxidation of methane to formaldehyde over W/HZSM-5 catalysts using molecular oxygen as an oxidant are reported in this paper.
6.Formaldehyde selectivity appears to be related to the dispersion of the tungsten oxide phase, which is influenced by the metal loading.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

2.直接部分氧化甲烷为C1氧酸、甲醛或甲醇是过去几年中研究最多的路线之一，因为它有可能取代传统的三步工业过程。

6.甲醛选择性似乎与钨氧化物相的分散度有关，而钨氧化物相的分散度又受金属负载量的影响。
