1.The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, low selectivity towards propylene, and reliance on dwindling petroleum reserves. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient and selective route for propylene production. The use of oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP presents its own set of advantages and limitations. Oxygen is a cheap and abundant oxidant, but its use can lead to overoxidation and the formation of undesired byproducts such as carbon oxides. The presence of oxygen can also cause hotspots in the catalyst bed, leading to temperature control issues. To mitigate these challenges, the development of suitable catalysts and reactor designs is crucial for the successful implementation of ODHP.

1.当前丙烯生产方法，如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化，主要面临高能耗、丙烯选择性低以及对日益减少的石油储备的依赖等问题。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供更节能、选择性更高的丙烯生产途径，解决了这些问题。在ODHP中使用氧气作为氧化剂具有其自身的一系列优势和局限性。氧气是一种廉价且丰富的氧化剂，但其使用可能导致过度氧化和产生不希望的副产品，如碳氧化物。氧气的存在也可能导致催化剂床层中出现热点，从而引发温度控制问题。为了缓解这些挑战，开发合适的催化剂和反应器设计对于ODHP的成功实施至关重要。
