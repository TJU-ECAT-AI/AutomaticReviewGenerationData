1.The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, low selectivity towards propylene, and reliance on dwindling petroleum reserves. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient process with higher selectivity for propylene production. However, ODHP presents its own set of advantages and limitations, particularly in the context of oxidant selection. Using oxygen as an oxidant can lead to overoxidation and the formation of undesired carbon oxides, while using molecular oxygen can result in higher selectivity but requires additional separation steps. The specific challenges associated with using oxygen include the need for precise temperature control to prevent hotspots and the potential for coke formation, which can lead to catalyst deactivation.

1.当前丙烯生产方法，如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化，主要面临高能耗、丙烯选择性低以及对日益枯竭的石油资源的依赖等问题。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供更节能的工艺和更高的丙烯选择性，解决了这些问题。然而，ODHP也存在其自身的优势和局限性，特别是在氧化剂选择方面。使用氧气作为氧化剂可能导致过度氧化和不希望的碳氧化物的形成，而使用分子氧虽然可以提高选择性，但需要额外的分离步骤。使用氧气的具体挑战包括需要精确控制温度以防止热点的形成，以及可能的焦炭形成，这可能导致催化剂失活。
