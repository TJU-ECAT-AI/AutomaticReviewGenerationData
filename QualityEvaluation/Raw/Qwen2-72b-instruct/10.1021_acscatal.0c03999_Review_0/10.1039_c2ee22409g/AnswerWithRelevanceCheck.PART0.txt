      <English>
          The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, high capital and operating costs, environmental issues due to greenhouse gas emissions, and the production of byproducts that reduce the overall yield of propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a potentially more energy-efficient, cost-effective, and selective process for propylene production. The use of carbon dioxide (CO2) as an oxidant in ODHP can further reduce costs, lower greenhouse gas emissions, save energy compared to traditional steam cracking, and promote selective dehydrogenation without the risk of over-oxidation. However, the use of oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP presents challenges due to the over-oxidation of propane to carbon dioxide, which decreases the selectivity for propylene. The choice of oxidant is crucial in determining the selectivity and yield of propylene in ODHP processes.

          当前丙烯生产方法，如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化，主要挑战包括高能耗、高资本和运营成本、温室气体排放导致的环境问题以及副产品的产生，这些副产品会降低丙烯的整体产量。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供一条可能更节能、成本效益更高、更选择性的丙烯生产过程来解决这些问题。在ODHP中使用二氧化碳(CO2)作为氧化剂，与传统的蒸汽裂解相比，可以进一步降低成本，降低温室气体排放，节省能源，促进选择性脱氢而不会过度氧化的风险。然而，使用氧气作为ODHP中的氧化剂会带来由于丙烷过度氧化为二氧化碳而导致的丙烯选择性降低的问题。氧化剂的选择对于确定ODHP过程中丙烯的选择性和产量至关重要。
