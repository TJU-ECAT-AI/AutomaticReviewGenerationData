<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, high capital and operating costs, and environmental issues due to greenhouse gas emissions. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a potentially more energy-efficient and cost-effective route to propylene. The use of carbon dioxide (CO2) as an oxidant in ODHP can further reduce costs, lower greenhouse gas emissions, and save energy compared to traditional steam cracking. However, the use of oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP presents challenges due to the over-oxidation of propane to carbon dioxide, which decreases the selectivity for propylene. The answer to this problem may be the use of CO2 as an oxidant, which avoids the over-oxidation issue and maintains high selectivity for propylene.

1.当前丙烯生产方法，如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化，主要挑战包括高能耗、高资本和运营成本以及温室气体排放导致的环境问题。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供一条可能更节能、成本效益更高的丙烯生产途径来解决这些问题。在ODHP中使用二氧化碳(CO2)作为氧化剂，与传统的蒸汽裂解相比，可以进一步降低成本，降低温室气体排放，节省能源。然而，使用氧气作为ODHP中的氧化剂会带来由于丙烷过度氧化为二氧化碳而导致的丙烯选择性降低的问题。这个问题的解决方案可能是使用CO2作为氧化剂，避免过度氧化问题，同时保持高丙烯选择性。
