1.The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, high capital costs, and the production of by-products that reduce the overall yield of propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more energy-efficient and selective process for propylene production. However, ODHP presents its own set of advantages and limitations, particularly in the context of oxidant selection. Using oxygen as an oxidant can lead to over-oxidation and the formation of undesired by-products, such as carbon dioxide and water, which can decrease the selectivity for propylene. Carbon dioxide, on the other hand, can serve as a mild oxidant that promotes the selective dehydrogenation of propane to propylene without the risk of over-oxidation. However, the use of carbon dioxide as an oxidant can also result in lower conversion rates and may require higher temperatures and pressures to achieve comparable yields of propylene.

1.当前丙烯生产方法的主要挑战包括蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂解等方法的高能耗、高资本成本以及副产品的产生，这些副产品会降低丙烯的整体产量。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供更节能和选择性的丙烯生产过程来解决这些问题。然而，ODHP在氧化剂选择方面具有自身的一系列优势和局限性。使用氧气作为氧化剂可能导致过度氧化和副产品如二氧化碳和水的形成，这会降低丙烯的选择性。另一方面，二氧化碳可以作为温和的氧化剂，促进丙烷选择性脱氢生成丙烯，而不会过度氧化的风险。然而，使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂也可能导致转化率降低，可能需要更高的温度和压力才能达到类似的丙烯产量。
