                    Propylene production is currently marred by demand-supply mismatches and the thermodynamic constraints of dehydrogenation, leading to catalyst deactivation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) directly produces propylene with improved energy efficiency due to its exothermal nature \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. However, ODHP's use of oxygen results in higher energy losses and fixed costs, with CO2 production adding environmental and economic concerns \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Current propylene production methods are energy-intensive, emit greenhouse gases, and suffer from co-product dilution. ODHP offers a cleaner, more direct route, with oxygen boosting propylene yields but risking deep oxidation and requiring costly oxygen separation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. The oxidant choice is pivotal, affecting reaction pathway, product selectivity, and catalyst stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\].

Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can suppress over-oxidation while enhancing propylene selectivity. The exploration of alternative oxidants, such as nitrous oxide (N2O) and carbon dioxide (CO2), presents a promising direction for mitigating the challenges associated with oxygen use. Additionally, the design of advanced reactor systems that can precisely control reaction conditions and optimize the use of oxidants could significantly enhance the efficiency and selectivity of ODHP processes. The integration of ODHP with other chemical processes, such as CO2 utilization and hydrogen production, could also offer synergistic benefits and further improve the overall sustainability of propylene production.

                    当前丙烯生产受到供需失衡和脱氢的热力学限制的影响，导致催化剂失活\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)直接产生丙烯，由于其放热性质，提高了能源效率\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。然而，ODHP使用氧气导致更高的能源损失和固定成本，二氧化碳生产增加了环境和经济问题\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。当前丙烯生产方法能源密集，排放温室气体，并受到副产品稀释的影响。ODHP提供了一条更清洁、更直接的路线，氧气可以提高丙烯产量，但存在深度氧化的风险，需要昂贵的氧气分离\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。氧化剂的选择至关重要，影响反应路径、产品选择性和催化剂稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现，我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新型催化剂，可以抑制过度氧化，同时提高丙烯选择性。探索替代氧化剂，如一氧化二氮(N2O)和二氧化碳(CO2)，为缓解与氧气使用相关的挑战提供了一个有希望的方向。此外，设计先进的反应器系统，可以精确控制反应条件，优化氧化剂的使用，可以显著提高ODHP过程的效率和选择性。将ODHP与其它化学过程，如二氧化碳利用和氢气生产，的集成，也可能提供协同效益，进一步提高丙烯生产的整体可持续性。
