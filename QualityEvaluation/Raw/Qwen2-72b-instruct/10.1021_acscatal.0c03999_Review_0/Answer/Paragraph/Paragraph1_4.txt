                    The current landscape of propylene production is dominated by methods like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking (FCC), which are energy-intensive and face thermodynamic constraints, leading to rapid catalyst deactivation \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. These methods struggle to meet the growing demand for propylene, particularly in Western Europe, due to their high energy consumption, reduced yield, and environmental issues \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Direct dehydrogenation of propane (DDP) is also limited by high energy requirements and thermodynamic constraints \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].

                    Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) emerges as a promising alternative, offering improved energy efficiency as an exothermal process \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. ODHP operates at lower temperatures, reducing coke formation and energy use \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. However, the choice of oxidant is critical. Oxygen, while enhancing propene selectivity, risks deep oxidation to CO2 and requires energy-intensive separation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. Nitrous oxide (N2O) improves propene selectivity and reduces deep oxidation risk, but may lower activity and increase production costs \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. Carbon dioxide (CO2) as a mild oxidant offers a safer, milder solution, enhancing process safety and selectivity \[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

                    The oxidant significantly impacts the reaction pathway, product selectivity, and catalyst stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]. Oxygen's specific challenges in ODHP, including overoxidation and temperature control issues, underscore the need for further research on oxidant selection \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90228-7\]. Suitable catalysts and reactor designs are crucial for ODHP's success, requiring precise temperature control to prevent catalyst deactivation \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\].

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on developing catalysts that can activate propane under mild conditions while suppressing over-oxidation and managing the exothermic nature of the reaction to prevent overheating and ensure safety \[10.1002/ente.201900622\]. The use of CO2 as a mild oxidant presents a promising direction for mitigating over-oxidation and enhancing selectivity to propylene, offering a safer and more sustainable alternative to oxygen \[10.1039/C5CY00998G\]. Additionally, the development of efficient reactor designs that can precisely control reaction conditions and prevent over-oxidation is essential for the commercial success of ODHP \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The potential of using CO2 as an oxidant in ODHP could also contribute to carbon capture and utilization strategies, addressing environmental concerns while improving the efficiency of propylene production \[10.1039/c2ee22409g\].

                    当前丙烯生产的格局主要由蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化（FCC）等方法主导，这些方法能耗高，面临热力学限制，导致催化剂快速失活\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。这些方法在满足丙烯日益增长的需求方面，尤其是在西欧，由于其高能耗、产量降低和环境问题而显得力不从心\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。直接丙烷脱氢（DDP）也受限于高能耗要求和热力学限制\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。

                    丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）作为一种有前景的替代方案出现，提供了一种作为放热过程的改进能源效率\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。ODHP在较低温度下运行，减少了焦炭形成和能源使用\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。然而，氧化剂的选择至关重要。氧气虽然提高了丙烯的选择性，但存在深度氧化至CO2的风险，并且需要能耗高的分离\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。一氧化二氮（N2O）提高了丙烯的选择性，减少了深度氧化的风险，但可能降低了活性并增加了生产成本\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。二氧化碳（CO2）作为一种温和的氧化剂，提供了一个更安全、更温和的解决方案，增强了过程安全性和选择性\[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]。

                    氧化剂对反应路径、产品选择性和催化剂稳定性有重大影响\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010\]。氧气在ODHP中的具体挑战，包括过度氧化和温度控制问题，凸显了进一步研究氧化剂选择的必要性\[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90228-7\]。适合的催化剂和反应器设计对于ODHP的成功至关重要，需要精确的温度控制以防止催化剂失活\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望：考虑到讨论中的进展和发现，有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发能够在温和条件下激活丙烷的催化剂，同时抑制过度氧化，管理反应的放热性质，以防止过热并确保安全\[10.1002/ente.201900622\]。使用CO2作为温和氧化剂为缓解过度氧化和提高丙烯的选择性提供了一个有希望的方向，提供了一个比氧气更安全、更可持续的替代方案\[10.1039/C5CY00998G\]。此外，开发能够精确控制反应条件并防止过度氧化的高效反应器设计对于ODHP的商业成功至关重要\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。使用CO2作为ODHP中的氧化剂的潜力也可能有助于碳捕获和利用策略，解决环境问题，同时提高丙烯生产的效率\[10.1039/c2ee22409g\]。
