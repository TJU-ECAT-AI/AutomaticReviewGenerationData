1.The primary challenges in current propylene production methods, such as steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking, include high energy consumption, significant greenhouse gas emissions, and the production of a wide range of co-products, which can dilute the yield of propylene. Oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) addresses these issues by offering a more direct route to propylene production with potentially lower energy requirements and emissions. However, ODHP presents its own set of advantages and limitations, particularly in the context of oxidant selection. Using oxygen as an oxidant in ODHP can lead to higher propylene yields due to the direct conversion of propane to propylene and carbon dioxide. However, the use of oxygen also poses challenges, such as the risk of deep oxidation to carbon dioxide and the need for oxygen separation from air, which can be energy-intensive. In contrast, using nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant can improve propene selectivity and reduce the risk of deep oxidation, but it may result in lower activity due to slower reoxidation of the catalyst.

1.当前丙烯生产方法的主要挑战，如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化，包括高能耗、大量温室气体排放以及产生一系列副产品，这会稀释丙烯的产量。丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)通过提供一条直接的丙烯生产途径，可能具有较低的能源需求和排放，从而解决了这些问题。然而，ODHP本身具有一系列优势和局限性，特别是在氧化剂选择的背景下。在ODHP中使用氧气作为氧化剂可以导致更高的丙烯产量，因为丙烷可以直接转化为丙烯和二氧化碳。然而，使用氧气也带来了挑战，如深氧化至二氧化碳的风险以及从空气中分离氧气的需要，这可能是能源密集型的。相比之下，使用一氧化二氮(N2O)作为氧化剂可以提高丙烯的选择性，降低深度氧化的风险，但可能会导致活性降低，因为催化剂的再氧化速度较慢。
