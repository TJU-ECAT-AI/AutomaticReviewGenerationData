                    The catalytic performance of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks for propane dehydrogenation is significantly enhanced by structural characteristics that provide numerous sites for B species and regulate radical reaction behavior \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. These materials, featuring high specific surface areas and porous structures, offer improved mass transport and molecular diffusion, crucial for catalytic applications. Incorporation methods, such as the two-step wash-coat technique \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\], effectively integrate B components into these structures, enhancing catalytic performance for propane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODHP). The effectiveness of these methods is influenced by the specific pore structure and B2O3 loading, affecting the apparent propane reaction order and catalytic activity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2023.114048\].

                    Defect boron sites, especially those hydrogen-bonded to silanols, are key for low-temperature ODHP \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Aggregated boron species, not isolated B(OSi)3 units, prove to be active for propane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Isolated boron in zeolite frameworks, without oligomers, shows high activity and selectivity for ODHP, with BOSiOx linkages preventing hydrolysis and leaching, ensuring durability \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. Spectroscopic evidence confirms the presence of isolated boron species with tetrahedral and trigonal coordination, crucial for catalysis \[10.1126/science.abe7935\].

                    Layered borosilicate zeolites, synthesized hydrothermally, exhibit predominantly microporous structures with minor mesopores, confirmed by type-I N2 adsorption-desorption isotherms \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]. Defective trigonal boron structures (BO3) act as active sites for propane oxidative dehydrogenation to propylene \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]. Embryonic borosilicate zeolites, with their defective sites and large porosity, effectively catalyze propane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031\]. Solid-state NMR confirms the presence of open-coordinate boron sites, ensuring ample active center exposure \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031\].

                    B-based materials with bowl-like structures offer large surface areas and porous frameworks, enhancing catalytic performance and environmental remediation potential \[10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743\]. Boron doping of carbon nanofibers (CNFs) boosts selectivity to propene in propane oxidative dehydrogenation, influenced by boron loading, calcination temperature, and the type of B precursor \[10.1002/cssc.201402363\]. Using boric acid as the precursor maximizes selectivity at isoconversion \[10.1002/cssc.201402363\]. Silicon boride, a boron-rich material, exhibits high-temperature oxidation resistance, making it a promising catalyst for light alkanes' oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]. Its antioxidant ability ensures catalytic stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\].

                    h-BN/SiO2 nanofiber catalysts, prepared by electrospinning, exhibit superior catalytic performance in propane conversion to propylene, achieving 12.4% conversion and 85.1% selectivity, with olefins productivity 5 times higher than bulk ODHP catalysts \[10.1002/cctc.202100476\]. The one-dimensional structure facilitates active site exposure, enhancing catalytic efficiency \[10.1002/cctc.202100476\]. Boron oxide, as an electron-deficient promoter, sustains selective oxygen species on the catalyst surface by inhibiting O2 adsorption, even at a low 0.003wt% loading \[10.1002/anie.200901826\]. Maximum propene productivity is achieved at 0.01wt% B2O3 \[10.1002/anie.200901826\].

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in B-based materials for propane dehydrogenation catalysis, grounded in the structural characteristics and catalytic advantages of 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks, open avenues for exploring innovative methodologies and emerging fields. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel synthesis techniques that enhance the dispersion and stability of B species within porous structures, aiming to further improve catalytic activity and selectivity. The exploitation of nonmetal catalysts like B2O3, with its low cost and eco-friendly properties, is a promising trend for high-temperature applications. The synthesis of materials with controlled, uniform boron oxide speciation is necessary to understand active site formation and optimize catalytic performance. The investigation of the dynamic nature of boron sites under reaction conditions and the identification of the active site(s) for propane oxidative dehydrogenation remain critical challenges. The development of advanced characterization techniques to monitor these dynamic changes in real-time could provide valuable insights. Moreover, the exploration of B-based materials in other catalytic applications, such as environmental remediation and energy storage, could broaden their impact and utility.

                    3-D分散活性位点的B基材料在多孔框架中，通过结构特性提供大量B物种位点，调节自由基反应行为，显著增强了丙烷脱氢的催化性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。这些材料具有高比表面积和多孔结构，提供改善的质量传输和分子扩散，对催化应用至关重要。两步洗涤涂层技术等掺入方法\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]有效地将B组分整合到这些结构中，增强了丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)的催化性能。这些方法的有效性受特定孔结构和B2O3负载的影响，影响表观丙烷反应级数和催化活性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2023.114048\]。

                    缺陷硼位点，特别是与硅醇氢键结合的位点，是低温度ODHP的关键\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。聚合硼物种，而非孤立的B(OSi)3单元，证明在丙烷氧化脱氢中是活性的\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。在没有寡聚物的沸石框架中孤立的硼显示出高活性和选择性，BOSiOx连接阻止了水解和浸出，确保了耐用性\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。光谱证据确认了具有四面体和三角形配位的孤立硼物种的存在，对催化至关重要\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。

                    水热合成的层状硼硅酸盐沸石主要显示微孔结构，有少量介孔，通过I型N2吸附-解吸等温线确认\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。缺陷三角硼结构(BO3)作为丙烷氧化脱氢至丙烯的活性位点\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021\]。具有缺陷位点和大孔隙度的胚胎硼硅酸盐沸石有效地催化丙烷氧化脱氢(ODH)\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031\]。固体态NMR确认了开放配位硼位点的存在，确保了充足的活性中心暴露\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031\]。

                    具有碗状结构的B基材料提供大表面积和多孔框架，增强了催化性能和环境修复潜力\[10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743\]。硼掺杂的碳纳米纤维(CNFs)提高了丙烷氧化脱氢中丙烯的选择性，受硼负载、煅烧温度和B前体类型的影响\[10.1002/cssc.201402363\]。使用硼酸作为前体在等转化率下最大化选择性\[10.1002/cssc.201402363\]。富硼材料硅化硼具有高温氧化抗性，使其成为轻烷氧化脱氢的有希望的催化剂\[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]。其抗氧化能力确保了催化稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]。

                    通过电纺丝制备的h-BN/SiO2纳米纤维催化剂在丙烷转化为丙烯方面表现出优越的催化性能，实现了12.4%的转化率和85.1%的选择性，与块体ODHP催化剂相比，烯烃生产力提高了5倍\[10.1002/cctc.202100476\]。一维结构促进了活性位点的暴露，增强了催化效率\[10.1002/cctc.202100476\]。作为电子不足的促进剂，硼氧化物在催化剂表面维持选择性氧物种，即使在低至0.003wt%的负载下也能抑制O2吸附\[10.1002/anie.200901826\]。在0.01wt% B2O3时达到最大丙烯生产力\[10.1002/anie.200901826\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望：基于3-D分散活性位点在多孔框架中的结构特性和催化优势的B基材料在丙烷脱氢催化方面的进展，为探索创新方法和新兴领域开辟了道路。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发增强B物种在多孔结构中分散和稳定性的新型合成技术上，旨在进一步提高催化活性和选择性。利用像B2O3这样的非金属催化剂，具有低成本和环保特性，是高温应用的有希望的趋势。合成具有受控、均匀硼氧化物规格化的材料对于理解活性位点的形成和优化催化性能是必要的。在反应条件下硼位点的动态性质的探究和丙烷氧化脱氢的活性位点(s)的识别仍然是关键挑战。开发先进的表征技术来实时监测这些动态变化可能提供有价值的见解。此外，B基材料在环境修复和能源存储等其他催化应用中的探索可能会扩大其影响和效用。
