                    The structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks significantly enhance catalytic performance in propane dehydrogenation applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Aggregated boron species, not isolated B(OSi)3 units, are crucial for enhancing CH bond activation in propane oxidative dehydrogenation (ODHP) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Defect boron sites, particularly those hydrogen-bonded to silanols, are critical for low-temperature ODHP \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Isolated boron in a zeolite framework without oligomers shows high activity and selectivity for ODHP, with BOSiOx linkage preventing full hydrolysis and enhancing durability \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. The presence of hydroxyl groups is confirmed as crucial for the reaction \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites feature porous structures and high specific surface areas, providing numerous sites for B species and regulating radical reaction behavior \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. Loading B components on 3-D porous surfaces or doping them into frameworks enhances catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. Honeycomb cordierite's straight channels enable high gas hourly space velocity (GHSV) and effective pore diffusion \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. The B2O3/SiO2@HC monolithic catalyst demonstrates superior catalytic activity at low boron oxide loading, achieving high propylene and olefin selectivity, negligible CO2 production, and a high propylene space time yield under high GHSV \[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]. Silicon boride, a B-rich material, shows promise as a catalyst for oxidative dehydrogenation of light alkanes due to its high-temperature oxidation resistance and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]. The catalytic activity is attributed to BOH groups formed from BO species with steam assistance \[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]. B2O3@BPO4, synthesized via a one-step template-free method, exhibits enhanced catalytic performance for propane oxidative dehydrogenation due to its 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\]. Melted B2O3, supported by BPO4, acts as an active species, contributing to the catalyst's excellent productivity and high stability at 550°C \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\]. The exploitation of nonmetal catalysts like B2O3 is a growing trend due to their low cost and eco-friendly properties \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\].

                    具有3-D分散活性位点的B基材料的结构特性在多孔框架中显著增强了丙烷脱氢应用中的催化性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。在丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)中，不是孤立的B(OSi)3单元，而是聚集的硼物种对于增强CH键活化至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。特别是与硅醇氢键结合的缺陷硼位点对于低温ODHP至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。在没有寡聚物的沸石框架中孤立的硼显示出高活性和选择性用于ODHP，BOSiOx链接防止完全水解并增强耐用性\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。证实了羟基的存在对于反应至关重要\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。具有3-D分散活性位点的B基材料具有多孔结构和高比表面积，为B物种提供了大量位点并调节了自由基反应行为\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。在3-D多孔表面上加载B组分或将其掺入框架中增强了催化性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。蜂窝状堇青石的直通道使高气体小时空间速度(GHSV)和有效孔扩散成为可能\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。B2O3/SiO2@HC单体催化剂在低硼氧化物负载下表现出优越的催化活性，实现了高丙烯和烯烃选择性，几乎无CO2产生，并在高GHSV下实现了高丙烯空间时间产量\[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040\]。富硼材料硅化硼由于其高温氧化抗性和稳定性，作为轻烷氧化脱氢的催化剂显示出前景\[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]。催化活性归因于从BO物种与蒸汽辅助形成的BOH基团\[10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014\]。通过一步无模板方法合成的B2O3@BPO4在多孔框架中由于其3-D分散活性位点而表现出增强的催化性能，用于丙烷氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\]。由BPO4支持的熔融B2O3作为活性物种，为催化剂的优异生产力和550°C下的高稳定性做出了贡献\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\]。像B2O3这样的非金属催化剂的开发是一种趋势，因为它们成本低且环保\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028\]。
