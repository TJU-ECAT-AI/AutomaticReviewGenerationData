                    The structural characteristics of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks significantly enhance catalytic performance for propane dehydrogenation, as evidenced by reference \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Aggregated boron species, particularly BOH groups hydrogen-bonded to SiOH, are crucial for low-temperature oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Defect boron sites in BMFI catalysts with varied crystallinity enrich the active sites, boosting catalytic activity and olefin productivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]. Isolated boron in zeolite frameworks without oligomers shows high activity and selectivity for ODHP, with superior durability due to the BOSiOx linkage that hinders full hydrolysis and leaching in humid conditions \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. The 2D 11B1H HETCOR spectrum and FTIR analysis reveal the spatial proximities and coordination of boron species, confirming the presence of isolated boron species with tetrahedral and trigonal coordination \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. NMR and IR results suggest that boron sites within the MFI zeolite framework can hydrolyze to form B[OHO(H)Si]2 groups, enhancing catalysis, and reverse to SiOB linkages \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. Hydroxyl groups are confirmed to be crucial by isotope-labeled experiments \[10.1126/science.abe7935\]. B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites feature porous structures and high specific surface areas, providing numerous sites for B species and regulating mass transport \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. Loading B components on 3-D porous surfaces or doping them into frameworks enhances catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. h-BN/cordierite monoliths with a 3-D straight-channel structure improve mass transport and catalytic efficiency \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. SBA-15 zeolite's 3-D mesoporous structure anchors boron oxide active sites, boosting propene selectivity at low temperatures \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. B2O3/SiO2@cordierite honeycomb ceramics achieve high olefin selectivity and negligible CO2 production \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. Doped B species in zeolites create 3-D dispersed active sites for propane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. 3-D ordered macroporous boron phosphate, synthesized using a hard template method, has rich pores and a high surface area, improving molecular diffusion and heat transfer \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]. 3-D h-BN bowl foam, with a large surface area and numerous mesopores, shows excellent activity for propane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in the synthesis and characterization of B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks for propane dehydrogenation catalysis open up new avenues for the development of highly efficient and selective catalysts. Future research endeavors might focus on the optimization of synthesis methods to achieve precise control over the dispersion and coordination of boron species, as well as the exploration of novel boron-containing materials with unique structural features that can further enhance catalytic performance. The integration of advanced characterization techniques, such as in-situ spectroscopy and microscopy, will be crucial for understanding the dynamic behavior of boron active sites under reaction conditions. Moreover, the development of theoretical models and computational simulations can provide insights into the catalytic mechanisms and guide the rational design of catalysts. The exploitation of nonmetal catalysts like B2O3, with its low cost and eco-friendly properties, is an emerging trend in catalysis that holds promise for sustainable and environmentally friendly chemical processes.

                    基于\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]参考文献的深度分析，B基材料中3-D分散活性位点在多孔框架中的结构特性显著提高了丙烷脱氢的催化性能。特别是氢键连接SiOH的BOH基团等聚集硼物种对于低温丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。不同结晶度的BMFI催化剂中的缺陷硼位点丰富了活性位点，提高了催化活性和烯烃产量\[10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017\]。不含寡聚物的沸石框架中的孤立硼表现出高活性和选择性，由于BOSiOx键联，即使在潮湿条件下也能防止完全水解和浸出，从而具有优异的耐用性\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。2D 11B1H HETCOR光谱和FTIR分析揭示了硼物种的空间邻近性和配位，确认了四面体和三角配位的孤立硼物种的存在\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。NMR和IR结果表明，MFI沸石框架内的硼位点可以水解形成B[OHO(H)Si]2基团，增强催化作用，然后逆转为SiOB键\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。通过同位素标记实验确认了羟基的重要性\[10.1126/science.abe7935\]。B基材料中3-D分散活性位点具有多孔结构和高比表面积，为B物种提供了大量位点，并调节了质量传递\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。将B组分加载到3-D多孔表面或掺入框架中增强了催化性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。具有3-D直通道结构的h-BN/堇青石单体改善了质量传递和催化效率\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。SBA-15沸石的3-D介孔结构锚定了氧化硼活性位点，提高了低温下丙烯的选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。B2O3/SiO2@堇青石蜂窝陶瓷实现了高烯烃选择性和几乎无CO2产生\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。掺杂沸石中的B物种在沸石框架中创建了3-D分散活性位点，用于丙烷氧化脱氢\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。使用硬模板方法合成的3-D有序大孔硼酸盐具有丰富的孔隙和高比表面积，改善了分子扩散和热传递\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。具有大比表面积和大量介孔的3-D h-BN碗泡沫对丙烷氧化脱氢表现出优异的活性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：B基材料中3-D分散活性位点在多孔框架中用于丙烷脱氢催化作用的合成和表征的进展为开发高效和选择性催化剂开辟了新的途径。未来的研究工作可能集中在优化合成方法以实现对硼物种分散和配位的精确控制，以及探索具有独特结构特征的新型含硼材料，以进一步提高催化性能。将先进的表征技术，如原位光谱学和显微镜，与反应条件下的动态行为相结合，对于理解硼活性位点至关重要。此外，理论模型和计算模拟的发展可以为催化机制提供见解，并指导催化剂的合理设计。利用B2O3等非金属催化剂，具有低成本和环保特性，是催化领域的一种新兴趋势，为可持续和环境友好的化学过程提供了前景。
