<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The literature does not provide a direct comparison of various metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP. However, it does state that b-Ga2O3 supported chromium oxide materials are active and selective for dehydrogenation of propane. The high catalytic activity of these catalysts arises from the dehydrogenating properties of both gallium and chromium oxides. Deposition of chromium oxide on the surface of b-Ga2O3 enhances the resistance to coke deposition, which is caused probably by decreasing the surface acidity. The evolution of chromium species in supported Cr2O3 catalysts as a function of loading and support properties is not discussed in detail, but it is suggested that deposition of chromium oxide generates surface Cr3+ species highly active in the dehydrogenation process.

1.文献没有直接比较各种金属氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的活性和选择性。但是，它确实指出，b-Ga2O3支持的铬氧化物材料对丙烷脱氢具有活性和选择性。这些催化剂的高催化活性源于镓和铬氧化物的脱氢性质。在b-Ga2O3表面沉积铬氧化物增强了对焦炭沉积的抵抗力，这可能是由于降低了表面酸性。在支持的Cr2O3催化剂中，铬物种的演变作为负载和支撑性质的函数没有详细讨论，但建议沉积铬氧化物生成表面Cr3+物种，在脱氢过程中具有高活性。
