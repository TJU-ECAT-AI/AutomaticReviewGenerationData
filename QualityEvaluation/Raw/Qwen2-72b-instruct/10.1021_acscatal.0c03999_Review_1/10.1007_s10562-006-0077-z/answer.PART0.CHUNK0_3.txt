1.The literature does not provide a direct comparison of various metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP. However, it does state that b-Ga2O3 supported chromium oxide materials are active and selective for dehydrogenation of propane. The high catalytic activity is attributed to the dehydrogenating properties of both gallium and chromium oxides. Chromium oxide deposition enhances resistance to coke deposition, possibly due to decreased surface acidity. The effect of chromium loading and support properties on the evolution of chromium species is not explicitly discussed.

1.文献没有直接比较各种金属氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的活性和选择性。但是，它确实指出b-Ga2O3支持的铬氧化物材料在丙烷脱氢中是活性和选择性的。高催化活性归因于镓和铬氧化物的脱氢性质。铬氧化物的沉积增强了对焦炭沉积的抵抗力，这可能是由于表面酸性降低。铬的负载和支持物性质对铬物种演化的具体影响没有被明确讨论。
