            Comprehensive evaluation of metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP: From initial screening to in-depth analysis of chromium-based catalysts, focusing on performance factors, species evolution, and structure-activity relationships. \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\] \[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\] \[10.1016/j.tca.2008.02.017\] \[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\] \[10.1016/S0167-2991(98)80785-4\] \[10.1007/s10562-006-0077-z\] \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.07.008\] \[10.1039/C5CY00998G\] \[10.1016/S0920-5861(98)00245-4\] \[10.1007/s10562-004-3749-6\] \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_1\] \[10.1016/S0167-2991(04)80271-4\] \[10.1016/j.catcom.2006.08.005\] \[10.1016/j.molcata.2004.12.006\] \[10.1039/C9CY01990A\] \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2012.05.011\] \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\] \[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\] \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.05.037\] \[10.1016/j.molcata.2011.08.019\] \[10.1021/cr5002436\] \[10.1016/S0021-9517(03)00278-1\] \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.026\] \[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]

            ※※※

            Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive evaluation of metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP has revealed the pivotal role of chromium-based catalysts, with a particular emphasis on the evolution of chromium species as a function of loading and support properties. This in-depth analysis has highlighted the critical factors influencing catalytic performance, including the dispersion and oxidation state of chromium, the nature of the support, and the interaction between chromium species and the support. 

            Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can further enhance the selectivity and stability of CO2-ODHP processes. This could involve the exploration of new support materials that can optimize the dispersion and stability of chromium species, as well as the investigation of alternative chromium precursors that can facilitate the formation of active chromium species under milder conditions. Additionally, the use of advanced characterization techniques, such as in-situ spectroscopy and microscopy, could provide deeper insights into the dynamic evolution of chromium species during CO2-ODHP, enabling the rational design of more efficient catalysts.

            Moreover, the integration of CO2-ODHP processes with other chemical processes, such as the reverse water-gas shift reaction, could offer synergistic benefits and enhance the overall efficiency of CO2 utilization. This could be achieved through the development of bifunctional catalysts that can simultaneously catalyze CO2-ODHP and the reverse water-gas shift reaction, leading to a more sustainable and economically viable approach to CO2 conversion.

            ※※※

            综合理解与未来展望：对CO2-ODHP金属氧化物催化剂的全面评估揭示了铬基催化剂的关键作用，特别强调了铬物种随负载量和支持物性质变化的演变。这一深入分析突出了影响催化性能的关键因素，包括铬的分散度和氧化态、支持物的性质以及铬物种与支持物之间的相互作用。

            未来的研究工作可能集中在开发能够进一步提高CO2-ODHP过程选择性和稳定性的新型催化剂上。这可能涉及探索新的支持材料，以优化铬物种的分散度和稳定性，以及研究替代的铬前体，以在更温和的条件下促进活性铬物种的形成。此外，使用先进的表征技术，如原位光谱学和显微镜，可以提供对CO2-ODHP过程中铬物种动态演变的更深入见解，从而实现更有效催化剂的理性设计。

            此外，将CO2-ODHP过程与其他化学过程，如逆水煤气变换反应，相结合，可以提供协同效益，提高CO2利用的总体效率。这可以通过开发能够同时催化CO2-ODHP和逆水煤气变换反应的双功能催化剂来实现，从而为CO2转化提供更可持续和经济上可行的方法。
