                    Comprehensive evaluation of metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP: From initial screening to in-depth analysis of chromium-based catalysts, focusing on performance factors, species evolution, and structure-activity relationships. The pivotal role of Cr3+ in catalytic activity is underscored, with Ni enhancing Cr3+ regeneration and thus stability and selectivity for propylene \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. Cr and Ga oxides exhibit high activity and selectivity in CO2-ODHP, with CO2 boosting propene yield \[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]. Cr2O3 catalysts peak at 5 wt.% Cr for optimal activity and selectivity, with higher loadings forming inactive Cr2O3 \[10.1016/j.tca.2008.02.017\]. The evolution of chromium species, influenced by loading and support, significantly impacts catalytic performance \[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\]. CO2 sustains the partially oxidized state of Cr2O3 on silica, enhancing propene yield and preventing deactivation \[10.1016/S0167-2991(98)80785-4\]. Cr2O3 on b-Ga2O3 exhibits high CO2-ODHP activity and selectivity, with Cr3+ playing a key role \[10.1007/s10562-006-0077-z\]. Cr2O3 catalysts with 6.8 wt% Cr maximize propene yield in CO2-ODHP, with CO2 enhancing yield via redox and reverse water-gas shift \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.07.008\]. Cr/SiO2 catalysts show stable performance, with surface chromates and Brnsted acids present, and a 40% propene yield \[10.1039/C5CY00998G\]. Cr2O3/SiO2 boosts CO2-ODHP activity by 40%, with CO2 sustaining the partially oxidized state of Cr2O3 \[10.1016/S0920-5861(98)00245-4\]. CrOx/SiO2 boosts propene yield compared to CrOx/Al2O3, with chromium loading modulating CO2's effect \[10.1007/s10562-004-3749-6\]. In CO2-ODHP, Cr2O3 on SiO2/Al2O3 excels, with Cr species evolving from Cr6+ to α-Cr2O3 with loading, peaking at monolayer Cr for maximum activity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_1\]. Cr-MCM-41 excels in CO2-ODHP, achieving >15% propene yield and >90% selectivity, with Cr content boosting C3H8 conversion \[10.1016/S0167-2991(04)80271-4\]. Cr/MSU-x excels in CO2-ODHP, achieving 36.8% propane conversion and 89.1% propylene selectivity \[10.1016/j.catcom.2006.08.005\]. Cr-MCM-41 in CO2-ODHP yields 30% propane conversion to propene with 90% selectivity, with Cr(VI)O4 tetrahedra reducing to Cr(III)O6 octahedra, reoxidized by CO2 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\]. Cr2O3 excels in CO2-ODHP, with Cr(II) and Cr(III) species vital for performance, peaking at 2 wt% Cr \[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\]. Cr catalysts on SiO2/diamond, with 1.7-8.0 wt% Cr, excel in CO2-ODHP, with activity linked to dispersed chromates and oxidation state \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.05.037\]. Cr, Ga excel in CO2-ODHP, with Cr/silica's activity hinging on Cr states, species, and Cr-silica link \[10.1016/j.molcata.2011.08.019\]. Cr-based CO2-ODHP catalysts exhibit high olefin selectivity and activity, with Cr3+ as active sites, and performance influenced by Cr2O3 loading and support properties \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. Cr-MCM-41 via DHT/TIE impacts CO2-ODHP, with DHT boosting formaldehyde selectivity and TIE varying Cr species \[10.1016/S0021-9517(03)00278-1\]. Cr3+ from Cr6+ reduction boosts CO2-ODHP activity, with CO2 oxidizing Cr2+ to Cr3+/Cr6+, aiding propylene formation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.026\]. 7% Cr-TUD-1 excels in CO2-ODHP, with 45% propane conversion and 75% propylene selectivity, with Cr6+ to Cr3+/2+ conversion and high Cr dispersion in TUD-1 key \[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\].

                    对CO2-ODHP中金属氧化物催化剂的全面评估：从初步筛选到深入分析铬基催化剂，重点关注性能因素、物种演变和结构-活性关系。Ni通过增强Cr3+的再生，从而提高丙烯的稳定性和选择性，突显Cr3+在催化活性中的核心作用\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。Cr和Ga氧化物在CO2-ODHP中表现出高活性和选择性，CO2促进丙烯产量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]。Cr2O3催化剂在5 wt.% Cr时达到最佳活性和选择性，更高负载形成非活性Cr2O3\[10.1016/j.tca.2008.02.017\]。铬物种的演变，受负载和载体影响，对催化性能有显著影响\[10.1016/j.cej.2010.11.070\]。CO2维持Cr2O3在二氧化硅上的部分氧化状态，增强丙烯产量并防止失活\[10.1016/S0167-2991(98)80785-4\]。Cr2O3在b-Ga2O3上表现出高CO2-ODHP活性和选择性，Cr3+发挥关键作用\[10.1007/s10562-006-0077-z\]。6.8 wt% Cr的Cr2O3催化剂在CO2-ODHP中最大化丙烯产量，CO2通过氧化还原和逆水煤气变换增强产量\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.07.008\]。Cr/SiO2催化剂表现出稳定性能，表面存在铬酸盐和Brnsted酸，丙烯产量达40%\[10.1039/C5CY00998G\]。Cr2O3/SiO2将CO2-ODHP活性提高40%，CO2维持Cr2O3的部分氧化状态\[10.1016/S0920-5861(98)00245-4\]。CrOx/SiO2相比CrOx/Al2O3提高丙烯产量，铬负载调节CO2的效果\[10.1007/s10562-004-3749-6\]。在CO2-ODHP中，Cr2O3在SiO2/Al2O3上表现出优异性能，Cr物种从Cr6+演变为α-Cr2O3，单层Cr达到最大活性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_1\]。Cr-MCM-41在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，丙烯产量>15%，选择性>90%，Cr含量促进C3H8转化\[10.1016/S0167-2991(04)80271-4\]。Cr/MSU-x在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，丙烷转化率36.8%，丙烯选择性89.1%\[10.1016/j.catcom.2006.08.005\]。Cr-MCM-41在CO2-ODHP中，丙烷转化为丙烯的转化率为30%，选择性为90%，Cr(VI)O4四面体还原为Cr(III)O6八面体，通过CO2再氧化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.03.014\]。Cr2O3在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，Cr(II)和Cr(III)物种对性能至关重要，峰值在2 wt% Cr\[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\]。1.7-8.0 wt% Cr的Cr催化剂在SiO2/diamond上，在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，活性与分散的铬酸盐和氧化态相关\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.05.037\]。Cr和Ga在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，Cr/silica的活性取决于Cr状态、物种和Cr-silica的链接\[10.1016/j.molcata.2011.08.019\]。Cr基CO2-ODHP催化剂表现出高烯烃选择性和活性，Cr3+作为活性位点，性能受Cr2O3负载和载体性质影响\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。Cr-MCM-41通过DHT/TIE影响CO2-ODHP，DHT提高甲醛选择性，TIE改变Cr物种\[10.1016/S0021-9517(03)00278-1\]。Cr3+从Cr6+还原增强CO2-ODHP活性，CO2将Cr2+氧化为Cr3+/Cr6+，促进丙烯形成\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.026\]。7% Cr-TUD-1在CO2-ODHP中表现出色，丙烷转化率45%，丙烯选择性75%，Cr6+到Cr3+/2+的转化和TUD-1中高Cr分散是关键\[10.1007/s10562-017-2282-3\]。
