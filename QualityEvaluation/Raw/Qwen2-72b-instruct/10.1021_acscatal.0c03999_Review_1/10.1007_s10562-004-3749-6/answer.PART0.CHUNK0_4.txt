1.The literature does not provide a direct comparison of various metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP, nor does it detail the evolution of chromium species in supported Cr2O3 catalysts as a function of loading and support properties. However, it does mention that the nature of the support (SiO2 vs Al2O3) significantly affects the catalytic performance in the dehydrogenation of propane with CO2. Over CrOx/SiO2, CO2 enhances propene yield, whereas over CrOx/Al2O3, the yield and selectivity of propene decrease in the presence of CO2. The effect of CO2 and steam on the acidity/basicity of the CrOx/Al2O3 catalyst is suggested as a possible reason for its low activity in the dehydrogenation of propane with CO2. The chromium loading level also influences the effect of CO2, with the ratio of yield in He to yield in CO2 (YHe/YCO2) increasing over CrOx/SiO2 with higher chromium loading, and decreasing over CrOx/Al2O3 with higher loading.

1.文献没有直接比较各种金属氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的活性和选择性，也没有详细描述支撑Cr2O3催化剂中铬物种的演变与负载量和支持物性质的关系。但是，它确实提到，支持物的性质（SiO2与Al2O3）对在CO2存在下丙烷脱氢的催化性能有重大影响。在CrOx/SiO2上，CO2增强了丙烯的产量，而在CrOx/Al2O3上，CO2存在时丙烯的产量和选择性显著下降。CO2和蒸汽对CrOx/Al2O3催化剂的酸碱性的影响被建议为该催化剂在CO2存在下丙烷脱氢活性低的可能原因。铬的负载量也影响CO2的效果，CrOx/SiO2上He与CO2的产量比（YHe/YCO2）随着更高的铬负载量而增加，而在CrOx/Al2O3上，随着负载量的增加，CO2的中毒效应降低。
