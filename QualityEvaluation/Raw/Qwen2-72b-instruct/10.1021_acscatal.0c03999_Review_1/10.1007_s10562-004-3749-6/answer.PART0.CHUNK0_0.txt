1.The literature does not provide a direct comparison of various metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP, nor does it detail the evolution of chromium species in supported Cr2O3 catalysts as a function of loading and support properties. However, it does discuss the effect of support type (SiO2 vs Al2O3) on the catalytic performance of CrOx-based catalysts in the dehydrogenation of propane with CO2. Over CrOx/SiO2, CO2 enhances propene yield, while over CrOx/Al2O3, the yield and selectivity of propene in the presence of CO2 decrease. The acidity/basicity of the oxide support is suggested to play a significant role in the catalytic properties of the supported chromium catalysts.
