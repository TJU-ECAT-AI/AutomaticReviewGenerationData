1.The literature does not provide a direct comparison of various metal oxide catalysts for CO2-ODHP, nor does it detail the evolution of chromium species in supported Cr2O3 catalysts as a function of loading and support properties. However, it does mention that the nature of the support (SiO2 vs Al2O3) significantly influences the catalytic performance in the dehydrogenation of propane with CO2. Over CrOx/SiO2, CO2 enhances propene yield, whereas over CrOx/Al2O3, the yield and selectivity of propene in the presence of CO2 decrease. The effect of CO2 and/or steam on the acidity/basicity of the CrOx/Al2O3 catalyst is suggested as a possible reason for its low activity in the dehydrogenation of propane with CO2.
