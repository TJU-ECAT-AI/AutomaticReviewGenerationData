        <English> 
                Zeolites, such as HZSM-5 with a high Si/Al ratio, are recognized as promising support materials for CO2-ODHP catalysts due to their high catalytic activity and stability in the dehydrogenation of propane to propene in the presence of CO2 \[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\]. Dealumination and weakening methods are employed to modify the acidity of zeolites, which is crucial for optimizing catalytic performance by reducing the amount of strong acid sites that can cause catalyst deactivation through side reactions like oligomerization and cracking \[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\]. The intrinsic properties of the supports, their interactions with gallium oxide, and the balance between enhancing catalyst activity and preventing catalyst deactivation are key considerations in selecting and implementing these strategies for different types of zeolites used as supports \[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The optimization of zeolite-supported catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions involves a deep understanding of the properties and acidity modification techniques that influence catalytic performance. Future research should focus on developing novel dealumination and weakening methods that can precisely control the acidity of zeolites, leading to enhanced selectivity and stability. The exploration of zeolites with unique pore structures and tunable acidity, such as ZSM-48 and Beta zeolites, offers promising avenues for improving the efficiency of CO2-ODHP catalysts. Moreover, the integration of advanced characterization techniques and computational modeling can provide insights into the structure-activity relationships of zeolite-supported catalysts, guiding the design of more efficient and selective catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions.

                HZSM-5等具有高Si/Al比的沸石被公认为是CO2-ODHP催化剂的有前途的支撑材料，因为它们在CO2存在下将丙烷脱氢为丙烯时具有高催化活性和稳定性\[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\]。脱铝和弱化方法用于修改沸石的酸性，这对优化催化性能至关重要，通过减少强酸位的数量，可以防止催化剂因副反应如寡聚和裂解而失活\[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\]。在为不同类型的沸石选择和实施这些策略时，需要考虑支撑材料的内在性质、它们与镓氧化物的相互作用以及增强催化剂活性与防止催化剂失活之间的平衡\[10.1016/S0167-2991(07)80962-1\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：CO2-ODHP反应中沸石支撑催化剂的优化涉及对影响催化性能的性质和酸性修改技术的深入了解。未来的研究应集中在开发新的脱铝和弱化方法上，这些方法可以精确控制沸石的酸性，从而提高选择性和稳定性。探索具有独特孔结构和可调酸性的沸石，如ZSM-48和Beta沸石，为提高CO2-ODHP催化剂的效率提供了有希望的途径。此外，高级表征技术和计算建模的整合可以提供沸石支撑催化剂的结构-活性关系的见解，指导设计更高效和选择性的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂。
