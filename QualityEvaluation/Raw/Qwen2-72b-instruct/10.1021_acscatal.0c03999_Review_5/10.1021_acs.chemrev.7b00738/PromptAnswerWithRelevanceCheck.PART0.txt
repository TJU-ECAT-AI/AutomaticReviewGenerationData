Dealumination can be achieved through thermal treatment, steaming, or hydrothermal aging, while weakening methods involve the introduction of bulky organic molecules or the use of fluoride ions to reduce the acidity. The choice of modification technique should be based on the specific requirements of the catalytic process and the properties of the zeolite support.
