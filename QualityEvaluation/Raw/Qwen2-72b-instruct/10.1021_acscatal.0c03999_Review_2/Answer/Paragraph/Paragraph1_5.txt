        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for addressing the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters significantly enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is essential for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as the co-feeding of O2, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts; however, they often result in a significant decrease in the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. In contrast, promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer a distinct advantage by activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The strategic introduction of promoters, especially Ni-based catalysts, in chromium-based catalytic systems has opened new avenues for enhancing CO2 activation and oxidizing ability. This approach not only overcomes the limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness but also maintains a stable selectivity for propylene, offering a superior alternative to O2 co-feeding. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel promoters that can further lower the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, improve the reoxidation efficiency of CrOx species, and enhance the stability and selectivity of the catalytic performance under various reaction conditions. Additionally, the exploration of synergistic effects between different promoters and chromium-based catalysts could lead to the discovery of more efficient and selective catalytic systems for propane dehydrogenation.

                在铬基催化体系中使用促进剂对于解决CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些促进剂显著增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2的共喂入，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率；然而，这通常会导致C3H6反应选择性的显著下降\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。相比之下，促进剂，特别是基于Ni的催化剂，通过在苛刻的反应条件下激活CO2，降低CO2解离的活化能，并生成可以再氧化还原CrOx物种的活化氧（O*ads），而不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性，提供了一种独特的优势\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 尤其是基于Ni的催化剂在铬基催化体系中的战略性引入，为增强CO2活化和氧化能力开辟了新的途径。这种方法不仅克服了CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的限制，而且保持了对丙烯的稳定选择性，提供了一个优于O2共喂入的优越替代方案。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新型促进剂上，这些促进剂可以进一步降低CO2解离的活化能，提高CrOx物种的再氧化效率，并在各种反应条件下提高催化性能的稳定性和选择性。此外，探索不同促进剂与铬基催化剂之间的协同效应，可能会发现更高效、更选择性的丙烷脱氢催化体系。
