        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for addressing the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters significantly enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is critical for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as O2 co-feeding, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts, but they come with the significant drawback of decreasing the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. In contrast, promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer a distinct advantage by activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. 
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The introduction of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems has opened new avenues for enhancing CO2 activation and oxidizing ability, leading to improved performance and selectivity in propane dehydrogenation. Future research should focus on the development of novel promoters that can further lower the activation energy for CO2 dissociation and increase the efficiency of O*ads generation. Additionally, the exploration of synergistic effects between different promoter elements could lead to the design of more efficient and selective catalysts. The integration of computational methods, such as ab initio calculations, with experimental studies will be crucial for understanding the fundamental mechanisms of CO2 activation and for guiding the rational design of catalysts. Moreover, the investigation of the long-term stability and recyclability of these promoted catalysts under industrial conditions is essential for their practical application.

                在铬基催化体系中使用助剂对于解决CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些助剂显著增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2共喂养，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活率，但它们显著降低了C3H6反应的选择性\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。相比之下，助剂，特别是基于Ni的催化剂，通过在恶劣反应条件下激活CO2，降低CO2解离的活化能，并生成可再氧化还原CrOx物种的活化氧（O*ads），而不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性，提供了独特的优势\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在铬基催化体系中引入助剂为增强CO2活化和氧化能力，提高丙烷脱氢性能和选择性开辟了新的途径。未来的研究应集中在开发新型助剂上，这些助剂可以进一步降低CO2解离的活化能，提高O*ads生成的效率。此外，探索不同助剂元素之间的协同效应，可以设计出更高效、更选择性的催化剂。将计算方法，如ab initio计算，与实验研究相结合，对于理解CO2活化的基本机制和指导催化剂的合理设计至关重要。此外，研究这些助剂催化剂在工业条件下的长期稳定性和可回收性，对于其实际应用至关重要。
