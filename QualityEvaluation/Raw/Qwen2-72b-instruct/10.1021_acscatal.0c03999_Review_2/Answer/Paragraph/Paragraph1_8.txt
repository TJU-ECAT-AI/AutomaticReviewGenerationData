        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for overcoming the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is essential for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as O2 co-feeding, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\], but they significantly decrease the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. In contrast, promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer the advantage of activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\], and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The role of promoters in enhancing CO2 activation and oxidizing ability in chromium-based catalytic systems is a promising avenue for improving the performance and selectivity of propane dehydrogenation. The use of Ni-based promoters has shown significant potential in lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation and generating O*ads, which can reoxidize reduced CrOx species. Future research should focus on the development of novel promoters that can further enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2 while maintaining high selectivity and stability. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects of different promoter combinations could lead to the discovery of more efficient and selective catalysts. The integration of computational methods, such as ab initio calculations, could provide insights into the mechanisms of CO2 activation and the role of promoters, guiding the rational design of catalysts for propane dehydrogenation.

                在铬基催化体系中使用促进剂对于克服CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些促进剂增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2共喂入，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]，但它们显著降低了C3H6反应的选择性\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。相比之下，促进剂，特别是基于Ni的催化剂，提供了在苛刻反应条件下激活CO2的优势，降低了CO2解离的活化能\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]，并生成了可以再氧化还原CrOx物种的活化氧（O*ads），同时不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在铬基催化体系中，促进剂在增强CO2活化和氧化能力方面的作用是提高丙烷脱氢性能和选择性的一个有希望的方向。基于Ni的促进剂的使用在降低CO2解离的活化能和生成O*ads方面显示出巨大潜力，可以再氧化还原CrOx物种。未来的研究应集中在开发新型促进剂上，这些促进剂可以进一步增强CO2的氧化能力，同时保持高选择性和稳定性。此外，探索不同促进剂组合的协同效应可能会导致发现更高效、更选择性的催化剂。将计算方法，如ab initio计算，与实验研究相结合，可以提供关于CO2活化机制和促进剂作用的见解，指导丙烷脱氢催化剂的合理设计。
