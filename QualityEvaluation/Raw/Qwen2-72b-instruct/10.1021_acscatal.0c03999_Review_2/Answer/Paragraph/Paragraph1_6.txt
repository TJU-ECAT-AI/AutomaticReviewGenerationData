        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for addressing the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is essential for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as O2 co-feeding, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\], but they significantly decrease the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. Promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer a distinct advantage by activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The introduction of promoters, especially Ni-based catalysts, in chromium-based catalytic systems has opened new avenues for enhancing CO2 activation and oxidizing ability. This strategy not only overcomes the limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness but also maintains a stable selectivity for propylene. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel promoters that can further lower the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, improve the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species, and enhance the stability and selectivity of the catalytic performance under more severe reaction conditions. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects of different promoters and their interaction mechanisms with chromium-based catalysts could lead to the design of more efficient and selective catalytic systems for propane dehydrogenation.

                在铬基催化体系中使用促进剂对于解决CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些促进剂增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2共喂，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]，但会显著降低C3H6反应的选择性\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。特别是基于Ni的催化剂，通过在苛刻反应条件下激活CO2，降低CO2解离的活化能，并生成可再氧化还原CrOx物种的活化氧（O*ads），同时不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性，提供了独特的优势\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 尤其是基于Ni的催化剂在铬基催化体系中引入促进剂，为增强CO2激活和氧化能力开辟了新的途径。这一策略不仅克服了CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的限制，而且保持了对丙烯的稳定选择性。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发新型促进剂上，这些促进剂可以进一步降低CO2解离的活化能，改善还原CrOx物种的再氧化，并在更恶劣的反应条件下提高催化性能的稳定性和选择性。此外，探索不同促进剂的协同效应及其与铬基催化剂的相互作用机制，可能会导致设计出更高效、更选择性的催化体系，用于丙烷脱氢。
