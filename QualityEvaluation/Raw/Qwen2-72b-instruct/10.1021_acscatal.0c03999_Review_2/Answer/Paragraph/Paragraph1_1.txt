        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for addressing the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is essential for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as O2 co-feeding, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts, but they come with a significant drawback: a decrease in the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. In contrast, promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer a distinct advantage by activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The introduction of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems has opened new avenues for enhancing CO2 activation and improving the oxidizing ability of CO2. This strategy not only overcomes the limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness but also maintains a stable selectivity for propylene, offering a superior alternative to O2 co-feeding \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_2\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel promoters that can further lower the activation energy for CO2 dissociation and enhance the stability of the catalytic performance under more severe reaction conditions. Additionally, the exploration of synergistic effects between different promoters and chromium-based catalysts could lead to the discovery of more efficient and selective catalytic systems for propane dehydrogenation.

                在铬基催化体系中使用助剂对于解决CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些助剂增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2共喂入，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率，但它们伴随着一个显著的缺点：C3H6反应选择性的降低\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。相比之下，助剂，特别是基于Ni的催化剂，通过在恶劣反应条件下激活CO2，降低CO2解离的活化能，并生成可以再氧化还原CrOx物种的活化氧（O*ads），而不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性，提供了一种独特的优势\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在铬基催化体系中引入助剂为增强CO2激活和提高CO2的氧化能力开辟了新的途径。这一策略不仅克服了CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的限制，而且保持了对丙烯的稳定选择性，提供了一个优于O2共喂入的优越替代方案\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_2\]。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新型助剂，以进一步降低CO2解离的活化能，并在更恶劣的反应条件下提高催化性能的稳定性。此外，探索不同助剂与铬基催化剂之间的协同效应，可能会发现更高效、更选择性的丙烷脱氢催化体系。
