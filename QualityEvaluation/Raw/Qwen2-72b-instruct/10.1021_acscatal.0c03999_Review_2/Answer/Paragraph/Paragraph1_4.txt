        <English> 
                The utilization of promoters in chromium-based catalytic systems is pivotal for addressing the inherent limitations of CO2's weak oxidation potential and inertness \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. These promoters significantly enhance the oxidizing ability of CO2, which is essential for the reoxidation of reduced CrOx species. Alternative strategies, such as O2 co-feeding, can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\], but they often result in a significant decrease in the selectivity of the reaction for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. In contrast, promoters, particularly Ni-based catalysts, offer a distinct advantage by activating CO2 under harsh reaction conditions, lowering the activation energy for CO2 dissociation, and generating activated oxygen (O*ads) that can reoxidize reduced CrOx species without compromising the selectivity and stability of the catalytic performance \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The role of promoters in enhancing CO2 activation and oxidizing ability in chromium-based catalytic systems is a promising avenue for improving the performance and selectivity of propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Future research should focus on the development of novel promoters that can further lower the activation energy for CO2 dissociation and increase the efficiency of O*ads generation. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects of different promoter combinations could lead to the discovery of more effective catalysts. The integration of computational methods, such as ab initio calculations, with experimental studies will be crucial for understanding the fundamental mechanisms of CO2 activation and for guiding the design of next-generation catalysts. Moreover, the investigation of the long-term stability and recyclability of these catalysts under industrial conditions will be essential for their practical application.

                在铬基催化体系中使用促进剂对于解决CO2氧化潜力弱和惰性的固有限制至关重要\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这些促进剂显著增强了CO2的氧化能力，这对于还原CrOx物种的再氧化至关重要。替代策略，如O2共喂入，可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]，但通常会导致C3H6反应选择性显著下降\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。相比之下，促进剂，特别是基于Ni的催化剂，通过在苛刻反应条件下激活CO2，降低CO2解离的活化能，并生成可再氧化还原CrOx物种的活性氧（O*ads），而不损害催化性能的选择性和稳定性，提供了独特的优势\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 在铬基催化体系中增强CO2活化和氧化能力的促进剂作用是提高丙烷脱氢催化剂性能和选择性的有希望的途径。未来的研究应集中在开发新型促进剂上，这些促进剂可以进一步降低CO2解离的活化能，提高O*ads生成的效率。此外，探索不同促进剂组合的协同效应可能会导致更有效催化剂的发现。将计算方法，如ab initio计算，与实验研究相结合，对于理解CO2活化的基本机制和指导下一代催化剂的设计至关重要。此外，在工业条件下对这些催化剂的长期稳定性和可回收性进行研究，对于它们的实际应用至关重要。
