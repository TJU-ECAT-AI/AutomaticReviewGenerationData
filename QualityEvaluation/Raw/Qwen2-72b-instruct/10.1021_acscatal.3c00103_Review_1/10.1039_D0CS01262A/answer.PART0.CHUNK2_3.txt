<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) involves three steps: (1) dissociative adsorption of propane on the metal surface, (2) β-H extraction from the adsorbed propane to form a propyl intermediate, and (3) desorption of propylene from the surface. The ongoing debate concerns whether the dissociative adsorption process or the β-H extraction is the rate-controlling step in this reaction. Some studies suggest that the first CH bond activation is the rate-determining step on certain surfaces, while others propose that the second CH bond activation is the rate-determining step on other surfaces. This uncertainty impacts our understanding of the PDH process by complicating the identification of the most active and selective catalysts for PDH. Without a clear consensus on the rate-controlling step, it is challenging to design catalysts that can efficiently promote the desired reaction pathway while suppressing side reactions.

1.丙烷脱氢（PDH）的逆向Horiuti-Polanyi机制涉及三个步骤：（1）丙烷在金属表面的解离吸附，（2）从吸附的丙烷中提取β-H形成丙基中间体，（3）从表面解吸丙烯。关于反应速率控制步骤的持续争议在于解离吸附过程还是β-H提取。一些研究表明，在某些表面上，首次CH键活化是速率决定步骤，而其他研究则提出，在其他表面上，第二次CH键活化是速率决定步骤。这种不确定性影响了我们对PDH过程的理解，因为它使我们难以确定最有效和选择性的PDH催化剂。如果没有关于速率控制步骤的明确共识，就很难设计出能够有效促进所需反应途径同时抑制副反应的催化剂。
