1.The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) involves three steps: (1) dissociative adsorption of propane on the metal surface, (2) β-H extraction from the adsorbed propane to form a propyl species, and (3) desorption of propylene from the surface. There is ongoing debate regarding whether the dissociative adsorption process or the β-H extraction is the rate-controlling step in this reaction. Some studies suggest that the first CH bond activation is the rate-determining step on certain metal surfaces, while others propose that the second CH bond activation is the rate-determining step on other surfaces. This uncertainty impacts our understanding of the PDH process by complicating the identification of the true rate-determining step and hindering the development of more effective catalysts. Recent studies using microkinetic analysis combined with DFT calculations have shed light on the reaction mechanism and rate-determining steps, but further research is needed to fully resolve the debate.

1.丙烷脱氢（PDH）的逆向Horiuti-Polanyi机制涉及三个步骤：（1）丙烷在金属表面的解离吸附，（2）从吸附的丙烷中提取β-H形成丙基物种，（3）从表面解吸丙烯。关于解离吸附过程还是β-H提取是该反应的速率控制步骤，存在持续的争论。一些研究认为，第一个CH键的激活在某些金属表面上是速率决定步骤，而其他研究则提出，在其他表面上，第二个CH键的激活是速率决定步骤。这种不确定性通过使我们难以确定真正的速率决定步骤，从而阻碍了对PDH过程的理解，阻碍了更有效催化剂的开发。最近使用微动力学分析结合DFT计算的研究为反应机制和速率决定步骤提供了见解，但需要进一步的研究来完全解决这一争论。
