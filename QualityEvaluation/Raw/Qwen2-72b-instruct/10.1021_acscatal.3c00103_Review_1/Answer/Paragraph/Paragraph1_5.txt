                The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH), proposed in 1934, is widely acknowledged for describing the kinetics of the PDH process \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]. It divides the process into three stages: (i) dissociative adsorption of the alkane, where one hydrogen atom is eliminated; (ii) extraction of the β-H from the adsorbed species, leading to the formation of H2 and a CC double bond; and (iii) desorption of the alkene and H2. The ongoing debate regarding whether the dissociative adsorption process or the β-H extraction is the rate-controlling step in the PDH reaction remains unresolved \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]. This uncertainty impacts our understanding of the PDH process by leaving a critical aspect of the reaction kinetics unresolved, which is essential for optimizing the reaction conditions and catalyst design.

                Some studies suggest that the CH cleavage steps are the rate-limiting step, while others propose that the dissociative adsorption of the paraffin is the rate-controlling step \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. This uncertainty affects the interpretation of experimental results and the development of theoretical models. For example, DFT calculations on Pt, Pt3Sn, and Pt2Sn (111) surfaces during the PDH reaction have revealed that the dissociative adsorption of the alkane is the rate-limiting step \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. However, the positive effect of Sn on catalytic activity is explained by the facile desorption of propylene, which hinders further dehydrogenation of the adsorbed propylene. This suggests that the rate-controlling step may depend on the catalyst and reaction conditions.

                Propane dehydrogenation over platinum and over the alloys occurs via the same reaction mechanism, namely, dissociative chemisorption of propane on a single platinum atom to which two adsorption sites are associated, one of which carries a hydrogen atom \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]. The subsequent conversion of the propyl radical into π-bonded propene via β-hydrogen elimination appears to be rate determining. The last step, desorption of π-bonded propene, has a comparatively low activation energy. The difference in negative reaction order, with respect to hydrogen, between platinum and its diluted alloys reflects a lower steady-state θH on Pt atoms surrounded by Au atoms vis--vis Pt atoms on a Pt surface \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The unresolved debate over the rate-controlling step in the PDH process highlights the need for further research to elucidate the reaction kinetics. Future studies might focus on developing more accurate theoretical models and advanced experimental techniques to determine the rate-controlling step under various reaction conditions and catalyst compositions. This could lead to the design of more efficient catalysts and the optimization of PDH reaction conditions. Additionally, exploring the role of alloying elements, such as Sn, in modifying the reaction pathway and the rate-controlling step could provide insights into the development of novel catalysts for PDH and other dehydrogenation reactions.

        <Chinese> 
                1934年提出的逆Horiuti-Polanyi机制广泛被认可为描述丙烷脱氢(PDH)过程动力学的机制\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]。它将过程分为三个阶段：(i)烷烃的解离吸附，其中一个氢原子被消除；(ii)从吸附物种中提取β-H，形成H2和CC双键；(iii)烯烃和H2的解吸。关于PDH反应中解离吸附过程或β-H提取是否为速率控制步骤的持续争论仍未解决\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]。这种不确定性影响我们对PDH过程的理解，因为它使反应动力学的一个关键方面未得到解决，这对于优化反应条件和催化剂设计至关重要。

                一些研究表明，CH裂解步骤是速率限制步骤，而其他研究则提出，烷烃的解离吸附是速率控制步骤\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。这种不确定性影响实验结果的解释和理论模型的开发。例如，在PDH反应中，对Pt、Pt3Sn和Pt2Sn (111)表面进行的DFT计算表明，烷烃的解离吸附是速率限制步骤\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。然而，Sn对催化活性的正面影响可以通过丙烯的易解吸来解释，这阻碍了吸附丙烯的进一步脱氢。这表明速率控制步骤可能取决于催化剂和反应条件。

                丙烷在铂和合金上的脱氢通过相同的反应机制进行，即丙烷在单个铂原子上解离化学吸附，该原子与两个吸附位点相关联，其中一个携带氢原子\[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]。随后，通过β-氢消除将丙基自由基转化为π-键合丙烯似乎具有速率决定性。最后一步，π-键合丙烯的解吸，具有相对较低的活化能。铂和其稀释合金之间关于氢的负反应级数的差异反映了Au原子包围的Pt原子上的较低稳态θH与Pt表面上的Pt原子相比\[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：PDH过程中速率控制步骤的未解之谜强调了进一步研究以阐明反应动力学的必要性。未来的研究可能集中在开发更准确的理论模型和先进的实验技术，以确定在不同的反应条件和催化剂组成下速率控制步骤。这可能导致设计更有效的催化剂和优化PDH反应条件。此外，探索合金元素，如Sn，在修改反应途径和速率控制步骤中的作用，可以为开发新型PDH和其他脱氢反应的催化剂提供见解。
