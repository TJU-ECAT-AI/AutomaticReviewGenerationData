                The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH), a widely acknowledged framework since 1934 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\], delineates the PDH process into three sequential steps: (i) dissociative adsorption of propane, where one hydrogen atom is eliminated; (ii) β-H extraction, leading to the formation of H2 and a CC double bond; and (iii) desorption of the alkene and H2 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]. An ongoing debate persists regarding whether the dissociative adsorption process or the β-H extraction is the rate-controlling step in the PDH reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]. This ambiguity significantly influences our comprehension of the PDH process, leaving a critical aspect of the reaction kinetics unresolved, which is pivotal for optimizing reaction conditions and catalyst design \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\].

                Some studies propose that the CH cleavage steps are the rate-limiting step \[10.1021/cr5002436\], while others posit that the dissociative adsorption of the paraffin is the rate-controlling step \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. DFT calculations on Pt, Pt3Sn, and Pt2Sn (111) surfaces during the PDH reaction have revealed that the dissociative adsorption of the alkane is the rate-limiting step \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. However, the positive effect of Sn on catalytic activity is attributed to the facile desorption of propylene, which impedes further dehydrogenation of the adsorbed propylene, suggesting that the rate-controlling step may be contingent on the catalyst and reaction conditions \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

                Propane dehydrogenation over platinum and its alloys occurs through the same mechanism, involving dissociative chemisorption of propane on a single platinum atom to which two adsorption sites are associated, one carrying a hydrogen atom. The conversion of the propyl radical into π-bonded propene via β-hydrogen elimination appears to be rate determining, while the desorption of π-bonded propene has a relatively low activation energy \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]. The difference in negative reaction order with respect to hydrogen between platinum and its diluted alloys reflects a lower steady-state θH on Pt atoms surrounded by Au atoms compared to Pt atoms on a Pt surface \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\].

                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The unresolved debate over the rate-controlling step in the PDH process highlights the need for advanced experimental techniques and theoretical models to elucidate the reaction kinetics. Future research endeavors might focus on developing catalysts that can selectively enhance either the dissociative adsorption or the β-H extraction step, depending on which is identified as the rate-controlling step. Additionally, the exploration of novel alloy compositions and surface modifications could offer insights into how the catalyst structure influences the rate-controlling step. The integration of machine learning algorithms with DFT calculations could also provide a more comprehensive understanding of the PDH mechanism, potentially leading to the design of more efficient catalysts.

                自1934年以来广受认可的逆Horiuti-Polanyi机制将丙烷脱氢（PDH）过程细分为三个连续步骤：（i）丙烷的解离吸附，其中一个氢原子被消除；（ii）β-H提取，导致H2的形成和CC双键的产生；以及（iii）烯烃和H2的脱附 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]。关于PDH反应中解离吸附过程还是β-H提取是速率控制步骤的争论持续存在 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]。这种不确定性显著影响我们对PDH过程的理解，留下反应动力学的关键方面未解决，这对于优化反应条件和催化剂设计至关重要 \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00103_1\]。

                一些研究提出CH断裂步骤是速率限制步骤 \[10.1021/cr5002436\]，而其他研究则认为烷烃的解离吸附是速率控制步骤 \[10.1021/cr5002436\]。在PDH反应中，对Pt、Pt3Sn和Pt2Sn（111）表面的DFT计算揭示，烷烃的解离吸附是速率限制步骤 \[10.1021/cr5002436\]。然而，Sn对催化活性的正面影响归因于丙烯的容易脱附，这阻碍了吸附丙烯的进一步脱氢，表明速率控制步骤可能取决于催化剂和反应条件 \[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

                丙烷在铂及其合金上的脱氢通过相同的机制发生，涉及在单个铂原子上丙烷的解离化学吸附，该原子与两个吸附位点相关联，其中一个携带氢原子。通过β-氢消除将丙基自由基转化为π-键合丙烯似乎决定了速率，而π-键合丙烯的脱附具有相对较低的活化能 \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]。铂和其稀释合金之间关于氢的负反应级数的差异反映了被Au原子包围的Pt原子上的较低稳态θH，与Pt表面上的Pt原子相比 \[10.1016/0021-9517(77)90010-0\]。

                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：PDH过程中速率控制步骤未解决的争论凸显了需要先进的实验技术和理论模型来阐明反应动力学。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发能够选择性增强解离吸附或β-H提取步骤的催化剂上，具体取决于哪一个被确定为速率控制步骤。此外，探索新型合金组成和表面改性可能提供催化剂结构如何影响速率控制步骤的见解。将机器学习算法与DFT计算相结合，也可能提供PDH机制的更全面理解，可能导向更高效催化剂的设计。
