1.In light of the growing global demand for propylene, the key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production are its ability to address the challenges faced by conventional production methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. PDH technology specifically addresses these challenges by utilizing propane, a widely available feedstock, and achieving high efficiency and selectivity in the conversion of propane to propylene. This makes PDH a more sustainable and economically viable option for propylene production compared to traditional methods.
