1.The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology that make it the most promising method for propylene production include its high efficiency, selectivity, and the ability to use a wide range of feedstocks. PDH specifically addresses the challenges faced by conventional production methods by reducing the dependency on oil-based feedstocks, improving process efficiency through selective catalysts, and increasing product selectivity for propylene over other byproducts. This technology offers a more sustainable and economically viable route to propylene production, which is crucial to meet the growing global demand for this important chemical intermediate.
