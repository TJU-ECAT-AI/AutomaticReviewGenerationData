            <English> 
                    Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology has emerged as a promising and advantageous method for propylene production, addressing the growing global demand in the context of limitations faced by conventional methods \[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]. PDH leverages the abundant and cheap shale gas feedstock to efficiently produce propylene, thereby outperforming traditional methods \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. This technology utilizes high-temperature endothermic processes, which, although limiting selectivity, can be optimized with nano-catalysts to enhance adsorption and efficiency \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]. PDH's economic viability, high selectivity, and utilization of abundant propane further enhance its efficiency \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. The use of cleaner gas in PDH boosts propylene output while addressing environmental issues, expanding its role in the industry \[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\]. PDH's ability to balance ethylene-propylene production and excel in feedstock use, efficiency, and selectivity makes it a superior alternative \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH technology, particularly the development of efficient and selective catalysts, have positioned it as a leading method for meeting the global demand for propylene. Future research endeavors might focus on the optimization of catalysts for enhanced selectivity and stability, the exploration of eco-friendly catalysts to address health and cost issues associated with conventional catalysts, and the development of low-temperature processes to improve energy efficiency. Additionally, the integration of PDH with renewable energy sources could further enhance its sustainability and competitiveness in the global market.

            <Chinese> 
                    丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术作为丙烯生产的一种有前景且有利的方法，解决了全球日益增长的需求，同时考虑到了传统方法面临的局限性\[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]。PDH利用丰富且廉价的页岩气原料高效生产丙烯，从而超越了传统方法\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。该技术利用高温吸热过程，虽然限制了选择性，但通过优化纳米催化剂可以增强吸附和效率\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]。PDH的经济可行性、高选择性以及对丰富丙烷的利用进一步提高了其效率\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。PDH使用更清洁的气体提高丙烯产量，同时解决环境问题，扩大了其在行业中的作用\[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\]。PDH在平衡乙烯-丙烯生产、提高原料使用、效率和选择性方面表现出色，使其成为更优的选择\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: PDH技术，尤其是高效和选择性催化剂的发展，使其成为满足全球丙烯需求的领先方法。未来的研究努力可能集中在优化催化剂以提高选择性和稳定性，探索环保催化剂以解决与传统催化剂相关的健康和成本问题，以及开发低温过程以提高能源效率。此外，将PDH与可再生能源的整合可以进一步增强其可持续性和在全球市场的竞争力。
