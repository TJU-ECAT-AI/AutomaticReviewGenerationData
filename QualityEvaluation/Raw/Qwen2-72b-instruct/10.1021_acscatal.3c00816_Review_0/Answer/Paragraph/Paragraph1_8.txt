            <English> 
                    The emergence of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) as a promising and advantageous technology to address the challenges of meeting the growing global demand for propylene production, in the context of limitations faced by conventional methods, is substantiated by a plethora of studies \[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\], \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\], \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]. PDH, using propane, taps shale gas for cost-effective, efficient propylene production, outperforming traditional methods \[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]. It boosts propylene production efficiency and selectivity, overcoming high-temperature challenges via nanostructured catalyst advancements \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. PDH boosts propylene production, enhancing efficiency, selectivity, and feedstock use, addressing demand and resource limits \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH technology, particularly the use of nanostructured catalysts, have significantly enhanced the efficiency and selectivity of propylene production. This has positioned PDH as a leading method to address the growing global demand for propylene, outperforming conventional methods in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of more sustainable and cost-effective catalysts, the optimization of reaction conditions for higher conversion rates, and the integration of PDH processes with renewable energy sources to further reduce the environmental impact. The exploration of novel catalysts that can maintain high activity and selectivity under milder conditions could also be a promising direction, potentially leading to lower energy consumption and improved economic viability.

                    丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术作为一项有前景且具优势的技术，旨在解决全球丙烯需求增长的挑战，同时克服传统方法的局限性，这一点得到了大量研究的证实\[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\], \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\], \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]。PDH利用丙烷，挖掘页岩气资源，实现成本效益高、效率高的丙烯生产，超越传统方法\[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]。它通过纳米结构催化剂的进展，提高了丙烯生产效率和选择性，克服了高温挑战\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。PDH提高了丙烯生产效率，增强了效率、选择性和原料利用，解决了需求和资源限制问题\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: PDH技术的进展，特别是纳米结构催化剂的使用，显著提高了丙烯生产的效率和选择性，使PDH成为解决全球丙烯需求增长的主要方法，与传统方法相比，在原料可用性、过程效率和产品选择性方面表现出色。未来的研究可能集中在开发更可持续和成本效益更高的催化剂，优化反应条件以提高转化率，以及将PDH过程与可再生能源源集成，以进一步减少环境影响。探索在较温和条件下保持高活性和选择性的新型催化剂也可能是一个有前景的方向，可能带来更低的能源消耗和改善的经济可行性。
