The use of Pt/CrOx catalysts in PDH boosts propylene yield, tackling the challenges of high temperatures and enhancing selectivity for global demand \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. This direct, economical method of propylene production is particularly advantageous in the face of petroleum depletion, as it ensures a steady supply of propylene \[10.3390/catal9020174\].
