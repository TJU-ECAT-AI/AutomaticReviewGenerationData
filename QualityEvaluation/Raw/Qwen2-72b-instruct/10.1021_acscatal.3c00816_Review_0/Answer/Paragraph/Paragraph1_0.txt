            <English> 
                    The emergence of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) as a promising and advantageous technology to address the challenges of meeting the growing global demand for propylene production, in the context of limitations faced by conventional methods, is substantiated by a plethora of studies \[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\], \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\], \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]. PDH, using propane, efficiently meets propylene demand, leveraging shale gas, offering single-product focus and cost benefits \[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]. It boosts propylene yields, addressing feedstock, efficiency, and selectivity issues with Pt- and CrOx catalysts, optimized by nanostructured adsorption for enhanced efficiency \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]. PDH boosts propylene production, economically converting propane directly, outshining conventional methods amid resource scarcity \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH technology, as highlighted by the studies, offer a promising avenue for the future of propylene production. The use of shale gas as a feedstock, the development of efficient and selective catalysts, and the optimization of process conditions to enhance propylene yields and selectivity are key areas of focus. Future research endeavors might concentrate on the development of more sustainable and cost-effective catalysts, the exploration of alternative feedstocks, and the integration of PDH processes with other chemical production pathways to create a more integrated and efficient chemical manufacturing landscape.

                    丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术的出现，作为一种有前景且具优势的技术，旨在解决全球丙烯需求增长背景下传统方法面临的挑战，这一点得到了大量研究的证实\[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\], \[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\], \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]。PDH利用丙烷，通过利用页岩气，提供单一产品焦点和成本优势，高效满足丙烯需求\[10.1016/j.mcat.2021.112081\]。它通过Pt和CrOx催化剂提升丙烯产量，通过增强效率的纳米结构吸附优化，解决原料、效率和选择性问题\[10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012\]。PDH通过直接经济地将丙烷转化为丙烯，超越了资源稀缺背景下传统方法\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62805-5\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 根据研究中突出的PDH技术进展，为丙烯生产的未来提供了一条有希望的途径。页岩气作为原料的使用、高效和选择性催化剂的开发，以及优化工艺条件以提高丙烯产量和选择性，是关注的关键领域。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发更可持续和成本效益更高的催化剂、探索替代原料，以及将PDH过程与其他化学生产路径整合，以创建一个更加集成和高效的化学制造景观。
