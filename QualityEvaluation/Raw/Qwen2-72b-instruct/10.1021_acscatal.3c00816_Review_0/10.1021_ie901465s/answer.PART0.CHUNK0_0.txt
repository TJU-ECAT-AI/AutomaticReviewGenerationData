1.Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology is advantageous for propylene production due to its ability to utilize cheap and readily available propane and butane as feedstocks, which are produced by various petrochemical processes. This technology does not require significant investment as existing production facilities can be easily retrofitted. PDH technology offers higher selectivity for propylene production compared to conventional methods, making it a promising method for meeting the growing global demand for propylene.
