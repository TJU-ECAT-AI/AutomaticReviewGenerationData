1.Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) provides an alternative route to non-petroleum based propylene with significant industrial relevance. PDH, employing Pt- or Cr-based catalysts, represents an emerging industrial route for propylene production. The as-prepared Zn@S-1 catalysts exhibit good dehydrogenation activity, high propylene selectivity, and regeneration capability in PDH reaction under employed conditions. The in-situ partial reduction of zinc species is observed and the partially reduced zinc cations are definitely identified as the active sites for PDH reaction.
