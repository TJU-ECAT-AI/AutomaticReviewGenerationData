<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) provides an alternative route to non-petroleum based propylene with significant industrial relevance. PDH, employing Pt- or Cr-based catalysts, represents an emerging industrial route for propylene production. The as-prepared Zn@S-1 catalysts exhibit good dehydrogenation activity, high propylene selectivity, and regeneration capability in PDH reaction under employed conditions. Zn-4@S-1 catalyst exhibits good dehydrogenation activity, high propylene selectivity and regeneration capability in PDH reaction. Typically, propylene STY of 24 and selectivity of 97% can be achieved at 853 K, demonstrating the great potential of such catalyst for industrial PDH process.
