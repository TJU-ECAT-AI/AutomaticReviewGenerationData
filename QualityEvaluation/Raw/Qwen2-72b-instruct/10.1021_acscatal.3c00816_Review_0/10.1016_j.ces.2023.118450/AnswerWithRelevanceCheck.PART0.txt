      <English>
          The literature highlights that propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology is a promising method for the industrial-scale production of propylene due to the heavy demand in the feedstock market. PDH has gained increasing attention as an on-purpose way to produce propylene from propane, suggesting it may offer advantages in terms of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity compared to conventional methods. PDH can provide efficient CH bond activation rather than CC bond, longer reactive longevity, and environmental friendliness. The use of Pt-based catalysts in PDH can improve propylene selectivity and catalytic stability.
