However, it does mention that the increasing world demand for propylene and the current low cost of propane derived from shale gas have renewed interest in PDH technologies. The literature focuses on the investigation of catalysts for PDH, particularly the use of indium on silica, alumina, and zeolite chabazite (CHA) with a range of In/Al ratios and Si/Al ratios. It highlights the formation of extra-framework In+ species in CHA zeolites and their superior stability and selectivity for propane dehydrogenation compared to other supports.
