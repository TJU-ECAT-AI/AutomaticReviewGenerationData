1.The preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology significantly influence the performance of various zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of ZnO onto ZSM-5 and Y zeolites results in highly dispersed ZnO species, which improves the activities of both zeolites in propane conversion. The type and strength of acid sites on the parent and the ALD ZnO modified zeolites play a crucial role in the reaction pathways. Incorporation of ZnO into Y zeolite by ALD completely eliminates the Brnsted acid sites and increases the number of strong Lewis acid sites, while on ALD ZnO modified ZSM-5, the Brnsted acid sites are only partially removed. The isolated Zn(OH)+ species formed on both zeolites after the first cycle of ZnO ALD are more effective for the conversion of propane to propylene and aromatics. The pore size and topology of the zeolites also affect the distribution of ZnO species and the accessibility of reactants to the active sites. For ZSM-5, the precursor molecules could only infiltrate the micropores during the first ALD cycle, while for Y zeolite, the precursor molecules could infiltrate the micropores during the first three cycles of ZnO ALD. The key factors contributing to improved propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability include the dispersion and form of ZnO species, the type and strength of acid sites, and the pore structure of the zeolites.

1.制备方法、组成和沸石拓扑结构对各种沸石基催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的性能有重大影响。通过原子层沉积(ALD)将ZnO沉积到ZSM-5和Y沸石上，形成高度分散的ZnO物种，从而提高了这两种沸石在丙烷转化中的活性。母体和ALD ZnO改性沸石上酸位点的类型和强度在反应途径中起着关键作用。通过ALD将ZnO掺入Y沸石中，完全消除了Brnsted酸位点，增加了强Lewis酸位点的数量，而在ALD ZnO改性的ZSM-5上，Brnsted酸位点仅部分被去除。在ZnO ALD的第一周期后，在这两种沸石上形成的孤立Zn(OH)+物种对丙烷转化为丙烯和芳烃更有效。沸石的孔径和拓扑结构也影响ZnO物种的分布和反应物到达活性位点的可及性。对于ZSM-5，前体分子只能在第一个ALD周期中渗透到微孔中，而对于Y沸石，前体分子可以在ZnO ALD的前三个周期中渗透到微孔中。提高丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性的关键因素包括ZnO物种的分散和形式、酸位点的类型和强度以及沸石的孔结构。
