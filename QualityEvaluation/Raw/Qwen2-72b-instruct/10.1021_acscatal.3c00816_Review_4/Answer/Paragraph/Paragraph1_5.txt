The performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions is significantly influenced by preparation methods, composition, and topology \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. A PtSn-based catalyst supported on SAPO-34, prepared by sequential impregnation, achieved 94% propylene selectivity and >97% total olefins selectivity \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. Dealuminated β zeolite supports small ZnO nanoclusters, enhancing PDH catalysis with high initial propane conversion (~53%) and superior propylene selectivity (~93%), alongside stability and reusability \[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]. Sn-Beta zeolite, with single-site Sn in the BEA framework, excels in PDH, activating propane's C-H bond at high temperatures with high selectivity and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of ZnO onto ZSM-5 and Y zeolites enhances catalytic activity for propane conversion in PDH reactions, improving propylene selectivity and conversion rates \[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]. In PDH reactions, the location of Pt clusters on X-zeolite significantly impacts catalyst performance \[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]. Zeolite-based catalysts' performance is influenced by preparation methods, composition, and topology \[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Preparation methods and zeolite topology significantly impact PDH catalyst performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. The performance of PtSn-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation is influenced by interactions between Pt, Sn, and the support \[10.1021/ie901465s\]. An improved post-synthesis strategy for PDH catalysts involves dealumination and metal impregnation \[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]. In PDH reactions, VOx catalysts on dealuminated Beta zeolite (SiBeta) exhibit varying catalytic activities depending on V loading \[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]. Encapsulating subnanometric clusters in zeolite pores boosts stability and catalytic applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]. In PDH reactions, CoOx confined in dealuminated Beta (SiBeta) zeolite forms ultrasmall Co particles under H2 or reactant gas, enhancing PDH efficiency \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]. In PDH reactions, PtSnNa/SUZ-4 outperforms PtSnNa/ZSM-5 due to a higher external Pt quantity, minimizing deactivation by carbon deposition \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in zeolite-based catalysts for PDH reactions, as detailed in the file, highlight the pivotal role of preparation methods, composition, and topology in achieving optimal performance. The exploration of novel preparation techniques, such as ALD and dealumination, alongside the utilization of various metal species and zeolite types, has opened new avenues for enhancing propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of more efficient post-synthesis strategies to further improve the dispersion of active metal species and the stability of zeolite frameworks. The integration of computational methods, such as theoretical calculations and molecular simulations, could provide deeper insights into the catalytic mechanisms and guide the design of advanced catalysts. Moreover, the investigation of novel metal-zeolite combinations and the optimization of reaction conditions could lead to the discovery of catalysts with superior performance under industrially relevant conditions. The exploration of emerging fields, such as the encapsulation of subnanometric clusters and the use of earth-abundant metals like Co, holds promise for the development of cost-effective and sustainable PDH catalysts.

基于\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]参考文献的深度分析，我们了解到在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中，沸石基催化剂的性能受到制备方法、组成和拓扑结构的显著影响。通过顺序浸渍制备的PtSn基催化剂，以SAPO-34为载体，实现了94%的丙烯选择性和>97%的总烯烃选择性\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]。脱铝β沸石支持的小ZnO纳米簇，通过消除晶格Al原子，抑制了强酸位点，增强了PDH催化作用，实现了高初始丙烷转化率（~53%）和优异的丙烯选择性（~93%），并具有高稳定性和可重复使用性\[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]。Sn-Beta沸石，具有BEA框架中的单位点Sn，在PDH中表现出色，能够在高温下激活丙烷的C-H键，具有高选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。ZnO通过原子层沉积（ALD）到ZSM-5和Y沸石上，增强了PDH反应中丙烷转化的催化活性，提高了丙烯选择性和转化率\[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]。在PDH反应中，Pt簇在X-沸石上的位置对催化剂性能有显著影响\[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]。沸石基催化剂的性能受到制备方法、组成和拓扑结构的影响\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。制备方法和沸石拓扑结构对PDH催化剂性能有重大影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。PtSn基催化剂在丙烷脱氢中的性能受到Pt、Sn和载体之间相互作用的影响\[10.1021/ie901465s\]。PDH催化剂的改进后合成策略涉及脱铝和金属浸渍\[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]。在PDH反应中，VOx催化剂在脱铝Beta沸石（SiBeta）上的催化活性取决于V负载\[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]。将亚纳米级簇封装在沸石孔中，提高了稳定性和催化应用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]。在PDH反应中，CoOx在脱铝Beta（SiBeta）沸石中被限制，形成超小Co颗粒，在H2或反应气体下，增强了PDH效率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]。在PDH反应中，PtSnNa/SUZ-4的外部Pt量高于PtSnNa/ZSM-5，最小化了由碳沉积引起的失活\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：文件中详述的PDH反应中沸石基催化剂的进展，突出了制备方法、组成和拓扑结构在实现最佳性能中的关键作用。ALD和脱铝等新型制备技术的探索，以及各种金属物种和沸石类型的利用，为提高丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性开辟了新的途径。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发更有效的后合成策略，以进一步提高活性金属物种的分散性和沸石框架的稳定性。将计算方法，如理论计算和分子模拟，与实验研究相结合，可以提供对催化机制的更深入理解，并指导先进催化剂的设计。此外，新型金属-沸石组合的探索和反应条件的优化，可能会发现在工业相关条件下性能更优的催化剂。新兴领域，如亚纳米级簇的封装和地球丰富金属（如Co）的使用，为开发成本效益高且可持续的PDH催化剂提供了前景。
