                    The performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions is significantly influenced by preparation methods, composition, and topology\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. PtZn-containing zeolites Beta and mordenite with nSi/nAl ratios >8.7 and 2.5 wt.% zinc showed the highest and most stable propane conversions, achieving thermodynamic equilibrium for propane dehydrogenation to propene (XPr 29%, T = 555 C, p = 0.1 MPa) over 63 h time-on-stream (TOS) with >90% propene selectivity\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Zinc loading through impregnation or ion exchange influenced activity and selectivity, with ion exchange inhibiting zinc loss\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Large-pore zeolites with lower nSi/nAl ratios (≤ 3.4) showed similar catalytic behavior regardless of zinc incorporation method\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Preparation methods, composition, and topology significantly impact zeolite-based catalysts in PDH reactions\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Zn ion-exchange followed by Pt impregnation boosts Pt dispersion and reduces Zn loss, enhancing propylene selectivity and conversion\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Optimized 0.5Pt/2.6Zn-Beta catalysts achieve high propane conversion and propylene selectivity\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Dealumination to Si-Beta zeolites and Na+ doping of Sn-Beta catalysts improve PDH performance\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Zn/Si-Beta catalysts outperform Zn/Al-Beta in propane conversion and propylene selectivity\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Co/Si-Beta catalysts show higher propylene formation TOF\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Direct PDH mechanism on -Sn-(OSi)4 sites is energetically favorable\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Encapsulated Pt species in Si-Beta channels due to strong PtSn interactions lead to high propane conversion, propylene selectivity, and stability\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Encapsulated Pt@MCM-22 retains 90% activity after cycles, unlike conventional Pt/MCM-22\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Zn deposition on Y zeolites eliminates Bronsted acid sites and increases Lewis acid sites\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. Isolated Ga+/In+ sites enhance PDH reaction rates\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. PtSn/SAPO-34 exhibits higher propylene selectivity and yield than PtSn/ZSM-5 due to better coke-resistance\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in zeolite-based catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) have revealed the critical role of preparation methods, composition, and topology in enhancing catalytic performance. The optimization of these factors has led to significant improvements in propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel synthesis techniques that further refine the distribution and interaction of active metal species within the zeolite framework, aiming to achieve even higher selectivity and stability. Additionally, the exploration of new zeolite topologies and compositions that can provide enhanced shape selectivity and acidity could open new avenues for PDH catalyst design. The integration of computational methods to predict and optimize catalyst properties based on theoretical calculations could also be a promising direction, allowing for the rational design of catalysts with tailored active sites for PDH reactions.

                    基于\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]参考文献的深度分析，我们了解到在丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应中，基于沸石的催化剂的性能受到制备方法、组成和拓扑结构的显著影响。含PtZn的Beta和mordenite沸石，具有nSi/nAl比率>8.7和2.5 wt.%锌，显示出最高和最稳定的丙烷转化率，实现了丙烷脱氢至丙烯的热力学平衡（XPr 29%，T = 555 C，p = 0.1 MPa），在63小时的时间流（TOS）内，丙烯选择性超过90%\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。通过浸渍或离子交换进行锌负载影响了活性和选择性，其中离子交换抑制了锌的损失\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。具有较低nSi/nAl比率（≤ 3.4）的大孔沸石，无论锌掺入方法如何，都表现出相似的催化行为\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。制备方法、组成和拓扑结构对PDH反应中的沸石基催化剂有显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。Zn离子交换后Pt浸渍提高了Pt分散度，减少了Zn损失，增强了丙烯选择性和转化率\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。优化的0.5Pt/2.6Zn-Beta催化剂实现了高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。脱铝至Si-Beta沸石和Sn-Beta催化剂的Na+掺杂改善了PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。Zn/Si-Beta催化剂在丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性方面优于Zn/Al-Beta\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。Co/Si-Beta催化剂显示出更高的丙烯形成TOF\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。直接PDH机制在-Sn-(OSi)4位点上是能量上有利的\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。由于强烈的PtSn相互作用，封装在Si-Beta通道中的Pt物种导致高丙烷转化率、丙烯选择性和稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。封装的Pt@MCM-22在循环后保留了90%的活性，与传统的Pt/MCM-22不同\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。Y沸石上的Zn沉积消除了Bronsted酸位点，增加了Lewis酸位点\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。孤立的Ga+/In+位点增强了PDH反应速率\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。PtSn/SAPO-34由于更好的焦炭抗性，显示出比PtSn/ZSM-5更高的丙烯选择性和产量\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在丙烷脱氢（PDH）的沸石基催化剂的进展中，我们认识到制备方法、组成和拓扑结构在增强催化性能中的关键作用。这些因素的优化导致了丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性的显著提高。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的合成技术，进一步细化活性金属物种在沸石框架内的分布和相互作用，旨在实现更高的选择性和稳定性。此外，探索新的沸石拓扑结构和组成，可以提供增强的形状选择性和酸性，可能为PDH催化剂设计开辟新的途径。将计算方法的整合用于基于理论计算预测和优化催化剂性质，也可能是一个有希望的方向，允许根据PDH反应设计具有定制活性位点的催化剂。
