<English>
The performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions is significantly influenced by preparation methods, composition, and topology \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. PtSn/SAPO-34 catalysts, prepared by sequential impregnation, achieved 94% propylene selectivity and >97% total olefins selectivity, surpassing PtSn/ZSM-5 due to SAPO-34's larger surface area, shape selectivity, and suitable acidity \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. Dealuminated β zeolite, with anchored ZnO nanoclusters, serves as a high-performance catalyst for PDH, achieving 53% initial propane conversion and 93% propylene selectivity, with high stability and reusability \[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]. Sn-Beta zeolite, with single-site Sn in the BEA framework, excels in PDH, achieving 40% propane conversion and 92% propylene selectivity, maintaining stability under harsh conditions for 72 hours \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of ZnO onto ZSM-5 and Y zeolites enhances catalytic activity for propane conversion in PDH reactions, improving propylene selectivity and conversion rates \[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]. The location of Pt clusters on X-zeolite significantly affects catalytic performance in PDH reactions \[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]. PtZn catalysts on Beta and mordenite zeolites with nSi/nAl ratios >8.7 and 2.5 wt.% zinc showed the highest stable propane conversion rates, achieving thermodynamic equilibrium for propene production (XPr 29%, T = 555 C, p = 0.1 MPa) for 63 h with >90% selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Preparation methods, composition, and topology significantly impact zeolite-based catalysts' performance in PDH reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. The performance of PtSn-based catalysts in PDH is influenced by interactions between Pt, Sn, and the support, with the support stabilizing activity \[10.1021/ie901465s\]. An improved post-synthesis strategy for Pt/Sn-Si-beta catalysts in PDH involves dealumination of H-beta and metal impregnation, leading to Sn incorporation into tetrahedral coordination states of Si-beta \[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]. In PDH reactions, VOx catalysts on dealuminated Beta zeolite (SiBeta) exhibit varying catalytic activities based on V loading \[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]. Encapsulating subnanometric clusters in zeolite pores boosts stability and catalytic applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]. In PDH reactions, CoOx confined in SiBeta zeolite's T-sites forms ultrasmall Co particles under H2 or reactant gas, enhancing PDH efficiency \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]. In PDH reactions, PtSnNa/SUZ-4 outperforms PtSnNa/ZSM-5 due to superior Pt distribution on external surfaces, minimizing deactivation by carbon deposition \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\].

基于\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]参考文献的深度分析，PtSn/SAPO-34催化剂通过顺序浸渍制备，实现了94%的丙烯选择性和>97%的总烯烃选择性，其性能超越了PtSn/ZSM-5，归因于SAPO-34更大的表面积、形状选择性和适宜的酸性\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]。脱铝β沸石，带有锚定的ZnO纳米簇，作为高性能催化剂用于丙烷脱氢，实现了53%的初始丙烷转化率和93%的丙烯选择性，具有高稳定性和可重复使用性\[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]。Sn-Beta沸石，BEA框架中的单位点Sn，在丙烷脱氢中表现出色，实现了40%的丙烷转化率和92%的丙烯选择性，能在72小时内保持稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。ZnO通过原子层沉积(ALD)到ZSM-5和Y沸石上，增强了丙烷转化的催化活性，提高了丙烯选择性和转化率\[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]。X-沸石上Pt簇的位置对丙烷脱氢反应的催化性能有显著影响\[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]。在nSi/nAl比率>8.7和2.5 wt.%锌的Beta和丝光沸石上，PtZn催化剂显示了最高的稳定丙烷转化率，实现了丙烯生产的热力学平衡(XPr 29%，T = 555 C，p = 0.1 MPa)，63小时内>90%的选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。制备方法、组成和拓扑结构对沸石基催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中的性能有显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。PtSn基催化剂在丙烷脱氢中的性能受Pt、Sn和载体之间相互作用的影响，载体稳定了活性\[10.1021/ie901465s\]。Pt/Sn-Si-beta催化剂的改进后合成策略涉及H-beta的脱铝和金属浸渍，导致Sn被纳入Si-beta的四面体配位状态\[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]。在丙烷脱氢反应中，VOx催化剂在脱铝Beta沸石(SiBeta)上的催化活性根据V负载量的不同而变化\[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]。将子纳米级簇封装在沸石孔中可提高稳定性和催化应用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]。在丙烷脱氢反应中，CoOx在SiBeta沸石的T位点下形成超小Co颗粒，通过H2或反应气体增强PDH效率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]。在丙烷脱氢反应中，PtSnNa/SUZ-4优于PtSnNa/ZSM-5，因为外部Pt的分布更优，减少了由碳沉积引起的失活\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\]。
