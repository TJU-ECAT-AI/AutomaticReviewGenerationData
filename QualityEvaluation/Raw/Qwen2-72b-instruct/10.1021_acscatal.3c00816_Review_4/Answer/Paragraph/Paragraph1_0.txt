<English>
The performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) is significantly influenced by preparation methods, composition, and topology, particularly for BEA and silicoaluminophosphate types \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. PtSn-based catalysts supported on SAPO-34, prepared by sequential impregnation, achieved 94% propylene selectivity and >97% total olefins selectivity, outperforming PtSn/ZSM-5 due to SAPO-34's larger surface area, shape selectivity, and suitable acidity \[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]. ZnO nanoclusters on dealuminated β zeolite serve as a high-performance catalyst for PDH, achieving initial propane conversion of 53% and propylene selectivity of 93% \[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]. Sn-Beta zeolite, with single-site Sn in the BEA framework, excels in PDH, achieving high selectivity and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Atomic layer deposition (ALD) of ZnO onto ZSM-5 and Y zeolites boosts PDH activity and propylene selectivity, with isolated Zn(OH)+ species proving more effective than larger clusters \[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]. The location of Pt clusters on zeolite significantly affects catalyst performance, with exterior Pt clusters enhancing TOF and propylene selectivity \[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]. PtZn-containing zeolites Beta and mordenite with nSi/nAl ratios >8.7 and 2.5 wt.% zinc content showed the highest and most stable conversion levels \[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]. Preparation methods, composition, and topology significantly impact zeolite-based catalysts in PDH \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]. The performance of PtSn-based catalysts in PDH is influenced by interactions between Pt, Sn, and the support, with the support stabilizing activity \[10.1021/ie901465s\]. An improved post-synthesis strategy for Pt/Sn-Si-beta catalysts in PDH involves dealumination and metal impregnation, leading to enhanced performance \[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]. VOx catalysts on dealuminated Beta zeolite (SiBeta) exhibit catalytic activities dependent on V loading \[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]. Encapsulating subnanometric clusters in zeolite pores boosts stability and catalytic applications \[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]. In PDH reactions, CoSiBeta catalysts, particularly those with 0.5 wt% Co, demonstrate state-of-the-art propylene productivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]. PtSnNa/SUZ-4 outperforms PtSnNa/ZSM-5 due to a higher external Pt quantity, minimizing deactivation by carbon deposition \[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\].
※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in zeolite-based catalysts for PDH, particularly focusing on BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate zeolites, have revealed the critical role of preparation methods, composition, and topology in enhancing propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability. Future research endeavors might focus on developing novel synthesis strategies that further optimize the dispersion of active metal species within the zeolite framework, aiming to achieve higher catalytic activity and selectivity while minimizing deactivation pathways. The exploration of advanced characterization techniques could provide deeper insights into the dynamic behavior of catalysts under reaction conditions, guiding the design of more robust and efficient catalysts. Additionally, the integration of computational methods with experimental findings could offer a more comprehensive understanding of the reaction mechanisms and the role of active sites, facilitating the rational design of next-generation PDH catalysts. The development of sustainable and cost-effective catalysts, capable of operating under milder conditions, could also be a promising direction for future research, addressing the challenges of energy efficiency and environmental impact.

基于\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]参考文献的深度分析，我们了解到在丙烷脱氢反应中，沸石基催化剂的性能受到制备方法、组成和拓扑结构的显著影响，特别是对于BEA和硅铝磷酸盐类型。PtSn基催化剂支持在SAPO-34上，通过顺序浸渍制备，实现了94%的丙烯选择性和>97%的总烯烃选择性，优于PtSn/ZSM-5，归因于SAPO-34更大的表面积、形状选择性和适宜的酸性\[10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008\]。脱铝化β沸石上的ZnO纳米簇作为PDH的高性能催化剂，实现了53%的初始丙烷转化率和93%的丙烯选择性\[10.1002/cctc.201801708\]。Sn-Beta沸石，具有BEA框架中的单位点Sn，在PDH中表现出高选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。ZnO通过原子层沉积（ALD）到ZSM-5和Y沸石上，提高了PDH活性和丙烯选择性，孤立的Zn(OH)+物种比大簇更有效\[10.1039/C5CP05043J\]。Pt在沸石上的位置显著影响催化剂性能，外表面的Pt簇提高了TOF和丙烯选择性\[10.1039/D1CY00738F\]。含PtZn的Beta和mordenite沸石，具有nSi/nAl比率>8.7和2.5 wt.%锌含量，显示了最高和最稳定的转化水平\[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028\]。制备方法、组成和拓扑结构对PDH中的沸石基催化剂性能有显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4\]。PtSn基催化剂在PDH中的性能受到Pt、Sn和支撑物之间相互作用的影响，支撑物稳定了催化剂的活性\[10.1021/ie901465s\]。Pt/Sn-Si-beta催化剂在PDH中的改进后合成策略涉及脱铝化和金属浸渍，导致性能增强\[10.1039/C9CY01907C\]。脱铝化Beta沸石（SiBeta）上的VOx催化剂的催化活性依赖于V负载\[10.1016/S1872-2067(19)63444-3\]。在沸石孔中封装亚纳米簇提高了稳定性和催化应用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022\]。在PDH反应中，CoSiBeta催化剂，特别是那些含有0.5 wt% Co的催化剂，展示了最先进的丙烯生产力\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037\]。PtSnNa/SUZ-4优于PtSnNa/ZSM-5，因为外部Pt的数量更高，最小化了由碳沉积引起的失活\[10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62750-5\]。
※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 在PDH中，特别是关注BEA拓扑和硅铝磷酸盐沸石的沸石基催化剂的进展，揭示了制备方法、组成和拓扑结构在提高丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性方面的重要作用。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的合成策略，进一步优化活性金属物种在沸石框架内的分散，旨在实现更高的催化活性和选择性，同时最小化失活途径。探索先进的表征技术可以提供对催化剂在反应条件下的动态行为的更深入理解，指导更坚固和高效催化剂的设计。此外，将计算方法与实验发现相结合，可以提供对反应机制和活性位点作用的更全面理解，促进下一代PDH催化剂的理性设计。开发可持续和成本效益高的催化剂，能够在更温和的条件下运行，也可能是一个有前景的研究方向，解决能源效率和环境影响的挑战。
