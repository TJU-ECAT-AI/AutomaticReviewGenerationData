                Comparative analysis of CO and H2 chemisorption techniques for characterizing active metal sites and metal dispersion in bimetallic catalysts reveals the predominance of CO chemisorption for quantifying active sites, as detailed in reference \[10.1021/cr300096b_4\]. This method, utilizing specialized instruments such as the AMI-200ip, involves loading a known quantity of calcined catalyst into a quartz tube, reducing it with a hydrogen-containing gas mixture at a specific temperature, cooling it to room temperature, and then pulsing CO over the catalyst. The effluent CO is measured using a thermal conductivity detector (TCD), and the reaction rates are normalized by the CO uptake values for proper comparison among different catalysts. CO chemisorption can also estimate metal dispersion assuming a 1:1 M/CO stoichiometry, although this is an oversimplification. H2 chemisorption is also used to determine active metal sites, but caution is needed as hydrogen can desorb from some bimetallic surfaces at room temperature \[10.1021/cr300096b_4\]. The literature does not provide a direct comparison between CO and H2 chemisorption techniques for characterizing bimetallic catalysts, but it does mention the use of CO chemisorption to determine metal dispersion and CO uptake, which provides a quantitative comparison of the number of active sites among the two series of catalysts \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.01.019\]. CO chemisorption is used to estimate the exposed metal fraction (dispersion) of different catalysts to avoid complications that may arise with hydrogen chemisorption, such as spillover \[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.11.032\].

                对CO和H2化学吸附技术在双金属催化剂中表征活性金属位点和金属分散度的比较分析显示，参考文献\[10.1021/cr300096b_4\]详细介绍了CO化学吸附在量化活性位点方面的主导地位。该方法使用如AMI-200ip等专业仪器，涉及将已知数量的煅烧催化剂装入石英管中，用含氢气体混合物在特定温度下还原，冷却至室温，然后向催化剂脉冲CO。使用热导检测器（TCD）测量流出的CO，通过CO吸收值对不同催化剂的反应速率进行归一化，以进行适当的比较。CO化学吸附还可以通过假设1:1的M/CO化学计量比来估计金属分散度，尽管这是一个过度简化的假设。H2化学吸附也用于确定活性金属位点，但需要注意的是，氢气在室温下可以从某些双金属表面解吸\[10.1021/cr300096b_4\]。文献没有直接比较CO和H2化学吸附技术在表征双金属催化剂方面的应用，但确实提到了使用CO化学吸附来确定金属分散度和CO吸收，这为两系列催化剂中活性位点数量的定量比较提供了依据\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.01.019\]。为了避免与氢化学吸附相关的并发症，如溢流，CO化学吸附被用来估计不同催化剂的暴露金属分数（分散度）\[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.11.032\]。
