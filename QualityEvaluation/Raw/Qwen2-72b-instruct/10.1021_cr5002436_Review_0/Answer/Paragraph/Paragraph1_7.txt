                    The escalating demand for light olefins, notably ethylene and propylene, underscores their pivotal role in the chemical industry \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. These olefins are predominantly produced through steam cracking, fluid-catalytic-cracking, and catalytic dehydrogenation, with the latter gaining prominence due to thermodynamic constraints in conventional processes \[10.1002/9783527610044.hetcat0162\]. The direct conversion of ethane and propane to chemical intermediates, facilitated by efficient catalysts, could revolutionize the industry by enhancing conversion activity and product selectivity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Synthesizing light olefins is thermodynamically challenging, requiring higher temperatures for dehydrogenation of light paraffins than heavy ones; propane needs at least 580°C for 40% conversion, while dodecane requires only 450°C \[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in catalytic dehydrogenation, particularly in the design of dehydrogenation catalysts with high activity and selectivity, offer a promising pathway for the efficient production of light olefins \[10.1007/s11244-012-9912-1\]. Future research endeavors might focus on developing novel catalysts that can operate under milder conditions, thereby reducing energy consumption and minimizing side reactions. Additionally, the exploration of alternative feedstocks and innovative processes, such as oxydehydrogenation, could provide new avenues for the sustainable production of light olefins while addressing safety and process control challenges \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\].

                    轻烯烃，尤其是乙烯和丙烯，需求量的激增凸显了它们在化工行业中的核心地位\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。这些烯烃主要通过蒸汽裂解、流化催化裂化和催化脱氢生产，其中催化脱氢因传统工艺的热力学限制而日益受到重视\[10.1002/9783527610044.hetcat0162\]。高效的催化剂促进的乙烷和丙烷直接转化为化学中间体，有可能通过提高转化活性和产品选择性来彻底改变行业\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。合成轻烯烃在热力学上具有挑战性，轻烷烃脱氢所需的温度高于重烷烃；丙烷至少需要580°C才能达到40%的转化率，而十二烷只需450°C\[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 催化脱氢技术的进步，特别是在设计具有高活性和选择性的脱氢催化剂方面，为轻烯烃的高效生产提供了一条有希望的途径\[10.1007/s11244-012-9912-1\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发能在更温和条件下运行的新型催化剂，从而降低能耗并减少副反应。此外，探索替代原料和创新工艺，如氧脱氢，可能为轻烯烃的可持续生产开辟新途径，同时解决安全和过程控制问题\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。
