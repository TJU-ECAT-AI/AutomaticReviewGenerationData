            <English> 
                    The industrial significance of light olefins, particularly ethylene and propylene, is underscored by their extensive applications in the chemical industry \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. These olefins are predominantly produced through steam cracking, fluid-catalytic-cracking, and catalytic dehydrogenation \[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]. The direct conversion of alkanes to olefins, a process that could revolutionize the chemical industry, hinges on the effectiveness of the catalyst for alkane conversion and selectivity to desired products \[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]. Synthesizing light olefins is thermodynamically challenging, requiring higher temperatures for light paraffins' dehydrogenation compared to heavy paraffins \[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]. Propane dehydrogenation, for instance, needs at least 580°C for 40% conversion, while dodecane requires only 450°C \[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\].

                    ※※※

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in catalytic dehydrogenation, particularly in the design of dehydrogenation catalysts that balance chemical and mass transport properties for high activity and selectivity \[10.1007/s11244-012-9912-1\], open up new avenues for the chemical industry. Future research endeavors might focus on developing more efficient and selective catalysts that can operate under milder conditions, thereby reducing energy consumption and environmental impact. Additionally, the exploration of alternative feedstocks and innovative processes, such as oxydehydrogenation using carbon dioxide as an oxidant \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\], could lead to safer and more sustainable production methods for light olefins.

                    轻烯烃，特别是乙烯和丙烯，在化学工业中的工业重要性由其广泛的应用所凸显\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。这些烯烃主要通过蒸汽裂解、流化催化裂化和催化脱氢生产\[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]。直接将烷烃转化为烯烃，一个可能彻底改变化学工业的过程，取决于催化剂对烷烃转化和对所需产品的选择性效率\[10.1016/j.cattod.2007.05.009\]。合成轻烯烃在热力学上具有挑战性，对于轻石蜡的脱氢，需要比重石蜡更高的温度\[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]。例如，丙烷脱氢至少需要580°C才能达到40%的转化率，而十二烷只需要450°C\[10.1007/s11244-012-9917-9\]。

                    ※※※

                    综合理解与未来展望: 催化脱氢领域的进展，特别是在设计脱氢催化剂以平衡化学和质量传递性能，实现高活性和选择性\[10.1007/s11244-012-9912-1\]，为化学工业开辟了新的途径。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发更高效、更选择性的催化剂，能够在更温和的条件下运行，从而降低能源消耗和环境影响。此外，探索替代原料和创新工艺，如使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂的氧脱氢\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]，可能导致更安全、更可持续的轻烯烃生产方法。
