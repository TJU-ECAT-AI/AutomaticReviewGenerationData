1.The literature does not provide a comparison of synthesis methods for supported bimetallic catalysts. It does, however, describe two methods used to prepare monometallic cobalt and bimetallic CoPt catalysts: classical impregnation and an ammonia method. The ammonia method results in the formation of finely dispersed Co3O4 on both SiO2 and synthetic kenyaite supports. The classical impregnation method leads to the formation of relatively larger Co3O4 particles. The ammonia method is advantageous for achieving higher catalytic activity in the reaction of complete benzene oxidation due to the finer dispersion of easily reducible Co3O4.
