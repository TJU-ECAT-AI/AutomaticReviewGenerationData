<English>
Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 have emerged as promising catalysts for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane, respectively \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits 58% C3H6 selectivity with good stability \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\], while Ni3Pt/CeO2 is a highly active DRP catalyst with 96% selectivity for CO \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. The C3H6 yield of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 surpasses that of monometallic catalysts, indicating a synergistic effect \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Ni3Pt/CeO2 features metallic Pt, whereas Fe3Ni/CeO2 includes metallic Ni and oxidized Fe \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. DFT studies suggest Fe3Ni(111) favors CH over CC scission, unlike Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni(111) binds oxygen strongly, aiding selective CH scission and C3H6 formation, whereas Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) binds oxygen moderately, enabling rapid oxygenated intermediate formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\].

Fe3Ni1/CeO2 demonstrates high selectivity for propylene production via CO2-ODHP, achieving 58.2% C3H6 selectivity \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\], while Ni3Pt1/CeO2 is active for DRP with 39% CO2 conversion and 88% CO selectivity \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 shows a synergistic effect, with higher propylene yield than monometallics \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. DFT studies confirm that Fe3Ni favors CH bond cleavage for propylene production, and Ni3Pt prefers CC bond cleavage for CO formation \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 is stable, losing <0.5% mass, unlikely due to coking \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\], whereas Ni3Pt1/CeO2 loses 8% mass without sintering \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 contains oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 comprises metallic Pt and Ni \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].

Fe3Ni1/CeO2 showed 2.6% CO2 and 1.1% C3H8 conversion in CO2-ODH, with 32% C3H6 and 65% CO selectivities \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Ni3Pt1/CeO2 exhibited higher activity and stability in DRP, with an activation barrier of 119 kJmol-1, lower than Co3Pt1/CeO2's 126 kJmol-1 \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Ni3Pt1/CeO2 had an 8% TGA weight loss, while Co3Pt1/CeO2 lost 4% \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Both Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and DRP Fe1Ni3/CeO2 consisted of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Fe-Co bimetallic catalysts favored propylene formation but had low performance due to deactivation \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Fe3Co1/CeO2 had less Fe oxidation than Fe3Ni1/CeO2 \[10.1002/aic.16670\], and Co3Ni1/CeO2 consisted of metallic Ni and Co under reaction conditions \[10.1002/aic.16670\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comparative analysis of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane reveals their distinct advantages and mechanisms. Fe3Ni1/CeO2's high selectivity for propylene production and stability, coupled with Ni3Pt1/CeO2's activity for CO formation, highlight the potential of bimetallic catalysts in environmentally benign catalysis for global alkene production and CO2 emissions reduction. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel bimetallic catalysts with enhanced selectivity, stability, and activity for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions. The exploration of advanced characterization techniques and theoretical calculations could provide deeper insights into the synergistic effects and reaction mechanisms of bimetallic catalysts. Moreover, the integration of bimetallic catalysts into industrial processes could pave the way for sustainable and efficient alkene production, contributing to the reduction of CO2 emissions and the mitigation of climate change.

Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2分别在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中与丙烷反应显示出前景，这已被确认\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2表现出58%的C3H6选择性，稳定性良好\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]，而Ni3Pt/CeO2是一种高活性的DRP催化剂，对CO的选择性为96%\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2的C3H6产量超过单金属催化剂，表明存在协同效应\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Ni3Pt/CeO2具有金属Pt，而Fe3Ni/CeO2包含金属Ni和氧化Fe\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。DFT研究表明Fe3Ni(111)倾向于CH而不是CC断裂，与Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)不同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni(111)强烈结合氧气，有助于选择性CH断裂和C3H6的形成，而Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)适度结合氧气，使快速氧化中间体的形成成为可能\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。

Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODHP中对丙烯生产显示出高选择性，达到58.2%的C3H6选择性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2在DRP中活性高，CO2转化率为39%，CO选择性为88%\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2显示出协同效应，丙烯产量高于单金属催化剂\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。DFT研究证实Fe3Ni倾向于CH键断裂以生产丙烯，而Ni3Pt倾向于CC键断裂以形成CO\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2稳定，质量损失小于0.5%，不太可能由于焦化\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2损失8%的质量，没有烧结\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2含有氧化Fe和金属Ni，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2包含金属Pt和Ni\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。

Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH中显示出2.6%的CO2和1.1%的C3H8转化率，C3H6和CO的选择性分别为32%和65%\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Ni3Pt1/CeO2在DRP中表现出更高的活性和稳定性，活化势垒为119 kJmol-1，低于Co3Pt1/CeO2的126 kJmol-1\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Ni3Pt1/CeO2的TGA质量损失为8%，而Co3Pt1/CeO2损失4%\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2和DRP Fe1Ni3/CeO2均包含金属Ni和氧化Fe\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe-Co双金属催化剂倾向于形成丙烯，但由于失活而性能较低\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Co1/CeO2的Fe氧化程度低于Fe3Ni1/CeO2\[10.1002/aic.16670\]，而Co3Ni1/CeO2在反应条件下由金属Ni和Co组成\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。

综合理解与未来展望：Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中与丙烷的比较分析揭示了它们独特的优点和机制。Fe3Ni1/CeO2对丙烯生产的高选择性和稳定性，加上Ni3Pt1/CeO2对CO形成的活性，突出了双金属催化剂在环境友好的催化作用中用于全球烯烃生产和CO2排放减少的潜力。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发具有增强选择性、稳定性和活性的新型双金属催化剂，用于CO2-ODH和DRP反应。探索先进的表征技术和理论计算可以为双金属催化剂的协同效应和反应机制提供更深入的见解。此外，将双金属催化剂整合到工业过程中可以为可持续和高效的烯烃生产铺平道路，有助于减少CO2排放和缓解气候变化。
