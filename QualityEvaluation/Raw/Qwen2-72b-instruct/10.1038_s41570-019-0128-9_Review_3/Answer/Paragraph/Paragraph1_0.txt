                Comparative analysis of bimetallic catalysts' performance, mechanisms, and characteristics in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane: An experimental and theoretical study focusing on Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 are promising catalysts for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane, respectively \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits 58% C3H6 selectivity with good stability, while Ni3Pt/CeO2 is a highly active DRP catalyst with 96% CO selectivity \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2's C3H6 yield exceeds monometallic catalysts, indicating synergy \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Ni3Pt/CeO2 features metallic Pt, Fe3Ni/CeO2 includes metallic Ni and oxidized Fe \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. DFT studies suggest Fe3Ni(111) favors CH over CC scission, unlike Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni(111) binds oxygen strongly, aiding selective CH scission and C3H6 formation, whereas Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) binds oxygen moderately, allowing rapid oxygenated intermediate formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 demonstrates high selectivity for propylene production via CO2-ODHP, achieving 58.2% C3H6 selectivity \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Ni3Pt1/CeO2 is active for DRP with 39% CO2 conversion and 88% CO selectivity \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni demonstrates a synergistic effect, with higher propylene yield than monometallics \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni is stable, losing <0.5% mass, unlikely due to coking \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Ni3Pt loses 8% mass without sintering \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. DFT reveals CC bond cleavage on Ni3Pt(111) and CH bond cleavage on Fe3Ni(111) \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Experimental results align with DFT, showing Fe3Ni's selectivity for propylene and Ni3Pt's activity for CO \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 was active in CO2-ODH, achieving 2.6% CO2 and 1.1% C3H8 conversion, with 32% C3H6 and 65% CO selectivities \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Ni3Pt1/CeO2 excelled in DRP, with higher activity and stability than Pt and Pd counterparts \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and DRP Fe1Ni3/CeO2 contained metallic Ni and oxidized Fe \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Fe-Co bimetallics favored propylene formation but had low performance due to deactivation \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. Fe3Co1/CeO2 had less Fe oxidation than Fe3Ni1/CeO2 \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. DRP Co3Ni1/CeO2 consisted of metallic Ni and Co under reaction conditions \[10.1002/aic.16670\].

Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel bimetallic catalysts with enhanced selectivity and stability for CO2-ODH and DRP reactions. The synergistic effects observed in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 suggest that the design of catalysts with tailored metal combinations could lead to improved performance. Theoretical studies, such as DFT calculations, can play a crucial role in predicting the behavior of new catalysts and guiding experimental efforts. Moreover, the investigation of the deactivation mechanisms of bimetallic catalysts, particularly in Fe-Co systems, could provide insights into strategies for enhancing catalyst longevity. The potential for environmentally benign catalysis in the context of global alkene production and CO2 emissions reduction highlights the importance of developing catalysts that not only perform well but also contribute to sustainable chemical processes. The exploration of alternative reaction conditions, such as the use of renewable energy sources for heating or the integration of CO2 capture and conversion technologies, could further enhance the environmental benefits of these catalytic systems.

                Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中与丙烷的比较分析：实验和理论研究聚焦Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2的性能、机制和特性\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2分别是CO2-ODH和DRP反应中与丙烷反应的有希望的催化剂\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2表现出58%的C3H6选择性，稳定性良好，而Ni3Pt/CeO2是一种高活性的DRP催化剂，CO选择性为96%\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2的C3H6产量超过单金属催化剂，表明存在协同效应\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Ni3Pt/CeO2具有金属Pt，Fe3Ni/CeO2包含金属Ni和氧化Fe\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。DFT研究表明Fe3Ni(111)倾向于CH而不是CC裂解，与Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)不同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni(111)强烈结合氧，有助于选择性CH裂解和C3H6的形成，而Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)适度结合氧，允许快速形成氧化中间体\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODHP中表现出对丙烯生产的高选择性，达到58.2%的C3H6选择性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Ni3Pt1/CeO2在DRP中活性高，CO2转化率为39%，CO选择性为88%\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni表现出协同效应，丙烯产量高于单金属催化剂\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni稳定性好，质量损失小于0.5%，不太可能是由于焦化\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Ni3Pt失去8%的质量，但没有烧结\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。DFT显示Ni3Pt(111)上CC键裂解和Fe3Ni(111)上CH键裂解\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。实验结果与DFT一致，显示Fe3Ni对丙烯的选择性和Ni3Pt对CO的活性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH中活性高，CO2转化率为2.6%，C3H8转化率为1.1%，C3H6选择性为32%，CO选择性为65%\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Ni3Pt1/CeO2在DRP中表现出比Pt和Pd对应物更高的活性和稳定性\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2和DRP Fe1Ni3/CeO2包含金属Ni和氧化Fe\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe-Co双金属催化剂有利于丙烯的形成，但由于失活而性能低\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Co1/CeO2的Fe氧化程度低于Fe3Ni1/CeO2\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。DRP Co3Ni1/CeO2在反应条件下由金属Ni和Co组成\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。
