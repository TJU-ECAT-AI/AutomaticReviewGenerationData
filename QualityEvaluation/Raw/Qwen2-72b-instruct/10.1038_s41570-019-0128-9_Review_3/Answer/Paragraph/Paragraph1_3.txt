                    Comparative analysis of bimetallic catalysts' performance, mechanisms, and characteristics in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane: An experimental and theoretical study focusing on Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 are promising for CO2-ODH and DRP with propane, respectively. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 shows 58% C3H6 selectivity and stability, while Ni3Pt/CeO2 is active for DRP with 96% CO selectivity \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Synergy between Fe and Ni in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 boosts C3H6 yield. Ni3Pt/CeO2 features metallic Pt, Fe3Ni/CeO2 includes metallic Ni and oxidized Fe. DFT studies suggest Fe3Ni(111) favors CH scission, unlike Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111). Fe3Ni(111) binds oxygen strongly, promoting selective CH scission and C3H6 formation, whereas Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111) binds oxygen moderately, facilitating rapid formation of oxygenated intermediates \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits high selectivity for propylene production via CO2-ODHP, achieving 58.2% C3H6 selectivity, while Ni3Pt1/CeO2 is active for DRP with 39% CO2 conversion and 88% CO selectivity. Fe3Ni demonstrates a synergistic effect, with higher propylene yield than monometallics, and is stable with minimal mass loss, suggesting resistance to coking. Ni3Pt loses 8% mass but shows no sintering. DFT studies confirm that Fe3Ni favors CH bond cleavage for propylene production, whereas Ni3Pt prefers CC bond cleavage for CO formation \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].

                    Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中与丙烷的比较分析：一项以Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2为重点的实验和理论研究\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2分别对CO2-ODH和DRP与丙烷有前景。Fe3Ni1/CeO2显示出58%的C3H6选择性和稳定性，而Ni3Pt/CeO2在DRP中活性高，CO选择性为96%\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe和Ni在Fe3Ni1/CeO2中的协同作用提高了C3H6的产量。Ni3Pt/CeO2具有金属Pt，Fe3Ni/CeO2包括金属Ni和氧化Fe。DFT研究表明Fe3Ni(111)有利于CH裂解，与Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)不同。Fe3Ni(111)强烈结合氧气，促进选择性CH裂解和C3H6的形成，而Pt-终止的Ni3Pt(111)适度结合氧气，促进快速形成氧化中间体\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODHP中对丙烯生产具有高选择性，达到58.2%的C3H6选择性，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2在DRP中活性高，CO2转化率为39%，CO选择性为88%。Fe3Ni表现出协同效应，丙烯产量高于单金属，且稳定性高，质量损失极小，表明抗结焦性。Ni3Pt损失8%的质量，但无烧结现象。DFT研究证实Fe3Ni有利于丙烯生产的CH键断裂，而Ni3Pt倾向于CO形成的CC键断裂\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。
