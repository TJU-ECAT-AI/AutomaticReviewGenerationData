                    Comparative analysis of noble metal-based and metal oxide-based catalysts for nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins reveals the unique properties of Pt-based catalysts, specifically their superior activation of paraffinic CH bonds and low CC cleavage activity \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. The Pacol process, utilizing alumina-supported Pt, was the pioneering industrial alkane dehydrogenation technology in 1968, and UOP's Oleflex process, an advancement, targets butadiene and propylene production \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. Metal oxide-based catalysts, notably CrOx, have industrial applications and show promise, with gallium, indium, vanadium, zinc, and molybdenum oxides also demonstrating success in testing \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. A novel Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O catalyst demonstrates high activity and selectivity for ethene and propene formation from ethane and propane, respectively, in nonoxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. The PtGa alloys within the catalyst enhance performance by diluting Pt ensembles and inhibiting product alkene adsorption, suppressing methane formation and coke deposition \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Optimal Ga/Pt ratios achieve peak activity and selectivity, with selectivity reaching nearly 100% at Ga/Pt = 5.4 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Hydrogen addition to the feed boosts alkene formation due to enhanced hydrogen-assisted dehydrogenation, but excessive hydrogen can lead to alkene hydrogenation, reducing dehydrogenation rates \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Hydrogen addition has minimal impact on alkene selectivity, which remains near 100% \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. The presence of Ga suppresses coke formation, contributing to lower deactivation and higher stability, making this catalyst advantageous for industrial light olefin production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Gallium-modified Pt/Al2O3 catalysts show enhanced selectivity to propylene, reduced deactivation, and carbon formation compared to pure Pt/Al2O3 \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]. Gallium slightly affects the acidity function but significantly alters the metallic phase structure, improving performance in propane dehydrogenation \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]. This suggests that metal oxide-based catalysts, when combined with noble metals, offer advantages in activity and stability for dehydrogenation processes \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed, there lies an opportunity to explore the synergistic effects of combining noble metals with metal oxides for enhanced performance in nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins. Future research endeavors might focus on developing bimetallic catalysts that optimize the balance between activity, selectivity, and stability. The exploration of novel metal oxide supports that can further suppress coke formation and improve catalyst longevity is also a promising avenue. Additionally, the investigation of hydrogen addition as a means to enhance dehydrogenation rates while minimizing alkene hydrogenation could lead to more efficient processes. The development of catalysts that maintain high activity and selectivity over long periods is crucial for economical processes, highlighting the need for continuous catalyst regeneration technology and the design of catalysts with intrinsic resistance to deactivation.

                    对于非氧化脱氢制轻烯烃的贵金属基和金属氧化物基催化剂的比较分析揭示了Pt基催化剂的独特性质，特别是它们对石蜡CH键的优异活化能力和低CC裂解活性\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。1968年，Pacol工艺，使用氧化铝支持的Pt，是开创性的工业烷烃脱氢技术，而UOP的Oleflex工艺，作为一项进步，针对丁二烯和丙烯的生产\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。金属氧化物基催化剂，尤其是CrOx，具有工业应用并显示出前景，而镓、铟、钒、锌和钼氧化物在测试中也表现出成功\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。一种新型的Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O催化剂在非氧化脱氢中表现出从乙烷和丙烷分别形成乙烯和丙烯的高活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。催化剂内的PtGa合金通过稀释Pt集合体和抑制产物烯烃吸附来增强性能，抑制甲烷形成和焦炭沉积\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。最优的Ga/Pt比率实现了峰值活性和选择性，选择性在Ga/Pt = 5.4时接近100%\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。向进料中添加氢气由于增强的氢辅助脱氢而促进烯烃形成，但过量的氢气会导致烯烃氢化，降低脱氢速率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。氢气添加对烯烃选择性的影响最小，选择性保持在接近100%\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。镓的存在抑制了焦炭形成，有助于降低失活和提高稳定性，使这种催化剂在工业轻烯烃生产中具有优势\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。镓改性的Pt/Al2O3催化剂与纯Pt/Al2O3相比，显示出对丙烯的增强选择性、降低的催化剂失活和碳形成\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。镓对酸性功能的影响微小，但显著改变了金属相结构，提高了丙烷脱氢的性能\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。这表明，当与贵金属结合时，金属氧化物基催化剂在脱氢过程中提供了活性和稳定性的优势\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到讨论的进展和发现，有机会探索将贵金属与金属氧化物结合以增强非氧化脱氢制轻烯烃的性能的协同效应。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发双金属催化剂，以优化活性、选择性和稳定性的平衡。探索可以进一步抑制焦炭形成并提高催化剂寿命的新型金属氧化物载体也是一个有希望的方向。此外，研究氢气添加作为增强脱氢速率同时最小化烯烃氢化的方法，可能会导致更有效的过程。开发在长时间内保持高活性和选择性的催化剂对于经济过程至关重要，这突出了连续催化剂再生技术和设计具有内在抗失活能力的催化剂的必要性。
