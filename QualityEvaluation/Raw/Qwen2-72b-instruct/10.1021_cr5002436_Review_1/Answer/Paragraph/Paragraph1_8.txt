                    Comparative analysis of noble metal-based and metal oxide-based catalysts for nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins reveals that Pt is the sole noble metal catalyst used commercially due to its superior activation of paraffinic CH bonds and low CC cleavage activity \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. The Pacol process (1968) and UOP's Oleflex process, which produces butadiene and propylene, exemplify its industrial application \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. Metal oxide catalysts, notably CrOx, have industrial use and show promise, with gallium, indium, vanadium, zinc, and molybdenum oxides also demonstrating success \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. A novel Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O catalyst demonstrates high activity and selectivity for ethene and propene formation from ethane and propane, respectively, in nonoxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. The PtGa alloys within the catalyst enhance performance by diluting Pt ensembles and inhibiting product alkene adsorption, suppressing undesired products like methane. Ga addition also reduces coke formation, improving stability. Recent developments focus on optimizing the Ga/Pt ratio for peak activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. The catalyst's high activity, selectivity, and stability are advantageous for industrial light olefin production. Hydrogen addition to the feed boosts alkene formation due to enhanced hydrogen-assisted dehydrogenation, with selectivity remaining near 100% \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Gallium-modified Pt/Al2O3 catalysts exhibit enhanced selectivity to propylene, reduced catalyst deactivation, and decreased carbon formation compared to pure Pt/Al2O3 \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]. Gallium's effect on the metallic phase structure improves performance in propane dehydrogenation, suggesting advantages in industrial applications for light olefin production \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\].

                    比较分析表明，铂（Pt）是唯一商业上使用的贵金属催化剂，因其对石蜡CH键的优异活化能力和较低的CC断裂活性而被广泛采用\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。Pacol工艺（1968年）和UOP的Oleflex工艺，用于生产丁二烯和丙烯，是其工业应用的典范\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。金属氧化物催化剂，尤其是CrOx，具有工业应用价值并展现出潜力，而镓、铟、钒、锌和钼的氧化物也显示出成功\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。一种新型的Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O催化剂在非氧化脱氢过程中表现出对乙烯和丙烯形成的高活性和选择性，分别从乙烷和丙烷中生成\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。催化剂内的PtGa合金通过稀释Pt集合体和抑制产物烯烃吸附，抑制了不需要的产物如甲烷的生成。镓的添加还减少了焦炭的形成，提高了稳定性。近期的发展集中在优化Ga/Pt比例以达到最佳活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。催化剂的高活性、选择性和稳定性对工业轻烯烃生产具有优势。向进料中添加氢气通过增强氢辅助脱氢作用提高了烯烃的生成，选择性保持在接近100%\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。镓改性的Pt/Al2O3催化剂显示出对丙烯的增强选择性，催化剂失活减少，碳形成减少，与纯Pt/Al2O3相比\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。镓对金属相结构的影响提高了丙烷脱氢过程中的性能，表明在轻烯烃生产中的工业应用具有优势\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。
