Comparative analysis of noble metal-based and metal oxide-based catalysts for nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins: composition, effectiveness, and industrial applications.

Noble metal-based catalysts, particularly Platinum (Pt), have been pivotal in catalytic dehydrogenation since the 1940s, with high activity for dehydrogenating paraffins to olefins \[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]. In the 1960s, Pt-based catalysts were developed for selective dehydrogenation of long-chain linear paraffins to internal mono-olefins, leading to the UOP PacolTM process for linear olefin production for biodegradable detergents \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]. By 1999, over 30 commercial Pt-catalyzed dehydrogenation units were operational for detergent alkylate production \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]. Continuous catalyst regeneration (CCR) technology, introduced in the 1970s, enabled noble metal catalysts to maintain stable activity for years, leading to the UOP OleflexTM process for producing light olefins, particularly propylene and isobutylene, at high selectivities under superatmospheric pressures \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\].

Metal oxide-based catalysts, including CrOx, gallium, indium, vanadium, zinc, and molybdenum oxides, have been used in the oxydehydrogenation of ethane to ethylene \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]. At temperatures of 300-500°C, reducible metal oxide catalysts containing vanadium are used, with selectivity to ethylene around 70% at 70% conversion of ethane \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]. At higher temperatures, above 600°C, non-reducible and reducible metal oxide catalysts evolved from methane oxidative coupling investigations are used, producing ethylene as the primary C2 product \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\].

A novel Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O catalyst demonstrates high activity and selectivity for ethene and propene production from ethane and propane, respectively, in nonoxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. The performance enhancement is attributed to PtGa alloys, which dilute Pt ensembles and inhibit product alkene adsorption, suppressing undesired methane formation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Ga addition also reduces coke formation, leading to higher stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. Recent developments focus on optimizing the Ga/Pt ratio for peak activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\].

Gallium-modified Pt/Al2O3 catalysts outperform pure Pt/Al2O3, enhancing propylene selectivity, reducing deactivation, and minimizing carbon formation \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]. Gallium subtly alters the acidity function and significantly modifies the metallic phase structure, improving propane dehydrogenation performance \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]. This highlights metal oxide-based catalysts' potential to boost activity and stability when combined with noble metals, offering industrial advantages in light olefin production \[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\].

A novel Pt/Mg(In)(Al)O catalyst demonstrates high activity and selectivity for ethane and propane dehydrogenation, with maximum performance at a bulk In/Pt ratio of 0.48 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\]. Alloying Pt with In significantly reduces coke deposition, enhancing stability and selectivity to nearly 100% for alkene production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\]. This catalyst's high performance and stability make it a promising candidate for industrial applications in light olefin production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\].

Pt catalysts have high activity and selectivity for dehydrogenation, while metal oxide-based catalysts offer potential for lower cost and improved stability under high-temperature conditions \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]. Recent developments in metal oxide-based catalysts aim to improve selectivity and stability for dehydrogenation of light paraffins to olefins \[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\].

比较分析了用于轻烯烃非氧化脱氢的贵金属基和金属氧化物基催化剂：组成、有效性和工业应用。

自20世纪40年代以来，特别是铂（Pt）的贵金属基催化剂在催化脱氢中起着关键作用，对将石蜡脱氢为烯烃具有高活性\[10.1021/cr5002436_1\]。20世纪60年代，基于Pt的催化剂被开发用于选择性地将长链线性石蜡脱氢为内部单烯烃，导致UOP PacolTM工艺用于生产生物可降解洗涤剂的线性烯烃\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。到1999年，超过30个商业Pt催化脱氢装置正在运行，用于生产洗涤剂烷基化物\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。20世纪70年代引入的连续催化剂再生（CCR）技术使贵金属催化剂能够保持多年的稳定活性，导致UOP OleflexTM工艺用于在超大气压下以高选择性生产轻烯烃，特别是丙烯和异丁烯\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。

包括CrOx、镓、铟、钒、锌和钼氧化物在内的金属氧化物基催化剂已被用于将乙烷脱氢为乙烯\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。在300-500°C的温度下，使用含钒的可还原金属氧化物催化剂，乙烷选择性为70%，乙烷转化率为70%\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。在更高的温度下，超过600°C，从甲烷氧化偶联研究中演变而来的不可还原和可还原金属氧化物催化剂被使用，产生乙烯作为主要的C2产品\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。

一种新型的Pt/Mg(Ga)(Al)O催化剂在非氧化脱氢中表现出从乙烷和丙烷生产乙烯和丙烯的高活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。性能增强归因于PtGa合金，它们稀释了Pt集合体并抑制了产物烯烃的吸附，抑制了不需要的甲烷形成\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。Ga的添加也减少了焦炭的形成，导致更高的稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。最近的发展集中在优化Ga/Pt比率以达到峰值活性和选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。

镓改性的Pt/Al2O3催化剂优于纯Pt/Al2O3，提高了丙烯选择性，减少了失活，最小化了碳形成\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。镓微妙地改变了酸性功能，并显著改变了金属相结构，改善了丙烷脱氢性能\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。这突出了金属氧化物基催化剂与贵金属结合时的潜力，可以提高活性和稳定性，为轻烯烃生产提供工业优势\[10.1016/s0926-860x(99)00058-7\]。

一种新型的Pt/Mg(In)(Al)O催化剂在乙烷和丙烷脱氢中表现出高活性和选择性，当In/Pt的总比率为0.48时，性能最佳\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\]。将Pt与In合金化显著减少了焦炭沉积，增强了稳定性和选择性，几乎为100%，用于烯烃生产\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\]。这种催化剂的高性能和稳定性使其成为轻烯烃生产工业应用的有前途的候选者\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.06.008\]。

Pt催化剂对脱氢具有高活性和选择性，而金属氧化物基催化剂在高温条件下具有潜在的低成本和改进的稳定性\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。金属氧化物基催化剂的最新发展旨在提高轻石蜡脱氢为烯烃的选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/s0926-860x(01)00816-x\]。

综合理解和未来展望：考虑到文件中讨论的进展和发现，有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发结合了贵金属和金属氧化物优势的双金属催化剂，旨在在高温条件下提高活性、选择性和稳定性。优化金属比率，如Ga/Pt和In/Pt，可能会导致发现性能优越的新型催化剂，用于轻烯烃生产。此外，新型金属氧化物及其与贵金属的相互作用的调查可能会为催化剂设计开辟新的途径，可能提供较低的成本和在脱氢过程中改进的稳定性。将连续催化剂再生（CCR）技术与金属氧化物基催化剂的集成也可能是一个有希望的方向，使催化剂能够在没有停机的情况下保持长期的稳定活性。
