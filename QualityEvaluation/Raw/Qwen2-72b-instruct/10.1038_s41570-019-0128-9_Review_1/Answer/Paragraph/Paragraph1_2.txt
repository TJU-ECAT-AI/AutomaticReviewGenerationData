                Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Vanadium oxide catalysts, such as V2O5, exhibit activity and selectivity that are significantly influenced by the nature of the support, which impacts reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion. Supported VOx catalysts are more selective than bulk V2O5 due to the stabilization of particularly active VOx surface structures by support interactions \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. DFT calculations and isotopic labeling experiments suggest that both one-step and two-step pathways are possible for CO2-ODH over V-based catalysts \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism is common in V-based catalysts, and increased participation of oxygen atoms from the support, such as CeO2, improves alkene selectivity \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. CeO2 inclusion enhances oxygen mobility and vacancy concentration compared to pure ZrO2 \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Mixed-oxide supports have better activity due to greater acid-base bifunctionality \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Acidic supports interact weakly with V2O5, leading to poorly dispersed crystallites \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Polymeric V species result in low alkene yield, highlighting dispersion's role in controlling selectivity \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. DFT studies show that the optimal size of V2O5 on supports like SiO2 or CeO2 differs \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Dimers have a kinetic preference for the rate-determining H-abstraction step \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres can achieve 90% propylene selectivity, but conversion decreases from 60% to 20% within one cycle \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. This performance is maintained for eight reaction-regeneration cycles \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. The development of V2O5 catalysts is promising, but acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns regarding V2O5 dust remain challenges \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts exhibit high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in CO2-ODH reactions, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 has high CO selectivity (>98%) through the dry reforming (DR) pathway \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. The selectivities remain stable at comparable C2H6 conversions, indicating intrinsic kinetic differences \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. C2H4 is the primary product over Fe3Ni1/CeO2, suggesting its potential as a CO2-ODH catalyst \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Ni/CeO2 is an excellent reforming catalyst, whereas Fe/CeO2 is less effective \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. The high selectivity of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 for CO2-ODH is attributed to distinct active sites involving Fe-Ni interaction \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\].

                对FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性，以及支持材料和组成对反应机制和产物形成的影响\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。钒氧化物催化剂，如V2O5，其活性和选择性受到支持材料性质的显著影响，这影响了还原性、酸碱性质和分散性。支持VOx催化剂比纯V2O5更具选择性，因为支持材料的相互作用可以稳定特别活跃的VOx表面结构\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。DFT计算和同位素标记实验表明，CO2-ODH在V基催化剂上可能同时存在一步法和两步法途径\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。大多数V基催化剂通过Mars van Krevelen机制运行，而来自支持材料（如CeO2）的氧原子的增加参与可以提高烯烃的选择性\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。CeO2的加入相对于纯ZrO2提高了氧的流动性和空位浓度\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。混合氧化物支持材料由于具有更大的酸碱双功能而具有更好的活性\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。如果支持材料仅具有酸性，它们只能与V2O5弱相互作用，导致该氧化物形成分散性差的结晶体\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。聚V物种导致低烯烃产率，再次表明分散性在控制选择性中的关键作用\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。DFT研究表明，V2O5在SiO2或CeO2等支持材料上的最优尺寸不同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。二聚体在决定性的H-抽提步骤中具有动力学偏好\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5可以达到90%的丙烯选择性，但转化率在一个循环内从60%下降到20%\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。这种性能仅在八个反应-再生循环中得以维持\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。V2O5催化剂的发展前景看好，但促进烯烃裂解的酸性位点和V2O5粉尘的健康问题仍然是挑战\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中表现出高C2H4选择性（>75%），而Fe1Ni3/CeO2通过干重整（DR）途径具有高CO选择性（>98%）\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。在可比较的C2H6转化率下，选择性几乎没有变化，表明差异来自内在动力学\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。C2H4是Fe3Ni1/CeO2上的主要产物，表明其作为CO2-ODH催化剂的潜力\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。Ni/CeO2是一种优秀的重整催化剂，而Fe/CeO2的效果较差\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH中表现出的高选择性可归因于涉及Fe-Ni相互作用的特定活性位点\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]。
