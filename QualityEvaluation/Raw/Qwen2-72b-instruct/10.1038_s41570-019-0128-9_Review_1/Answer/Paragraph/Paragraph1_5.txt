                Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]. Vanadium oxide catalysts, such as V2O5, exhibit activity and selectivity that are significantly influenced by the nature of the support, which impacts reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion. Supported VOx catalysts are more selective than bulk V2O5 due to the stabilization of particularly active VOx surface structures by support interactions. DFT calculations and isotopic labeling experiments suggest that both one-step and two-step pathways are possible for CO2-ODH over V-based catalysts. The Mars van Krevelen mechanism is common in V-based catalysts, and increased participation of oxygen atoms from the support, such as CeO2, improves alkene selectivity. CeO2 inclusion enhances oxygen mobility and vacancy concentration compared to pure ZrO2. Mixed-oxide supports have better activity due to greater acid-base bifunctionality. Acidic supports interact weakly with V2O5, leading to poorly dispersed crystallites. Polymeric V species result in low alkene yield, highlighting dispersion's role in controlling selectivity. DFT studies show that the optimal size of V2O5 on supports like SiO2 or CeO2 differs. Dimers have a kinetic preference for the rate-determining H-abstraction step. V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres can achieve 90% propylene selectivity, but conversion decreases from 60% to 20% within one cycle. This performance is maintained for eight reaction-regeneration cycles. The development of V2O5 catalysts is promising, but acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns regarding V2O5 dust remain challenges. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts exhibit high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in CO2-ODH reactions, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 has high CO selectivity (>98%) through the dry reforming (DR) pathway. The selectivities remain stable at comparable C2H6 conversions, indicating intrinsic kinetic differences. C2H4 is the primary product over Fe3Ni1/CeO2, suggesting its potential as a CO2-ODH catalyst. Ni/CeO2 is an excellent reforming catalyst, whereas Fe/CeO2 is less effective. The high selectivity of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 for CO2-ODH is attributed to distinct active sites involving Fe-Ni interaction.

                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the reference \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\], there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on the optimization of support materials to enhance the dispersion and stability of active sites, particularly in V-based catalysts. The development of novel mixed-oxide supports with tailored acid-base properties could lead to improved catalyst performance and selectivity. Moreover, the investigation of alternative metal combinations in FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts could reveal new active sites that promote CO2-ODH reactions with higher efficiency and selectivity. The use of advanced characterization techniques, such as in situ spectroscopy and microscopy, could provide deeper insights into the reaction mechanisms and the role of support materials in controlling product formation. Additionally, the exploration of sustainable and health-friendly alternatives to V2O5, addressing the health concerns associated with V2O5 dust, could open new avenues for the design of safer and more environmentally friendly catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions.

                基于\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1\]参考文献的深度分析。比较分析FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能、选择性，以及支持材料和组成对反应机制和产物形成的影响。钒氧化物催化剂，如V2O5，其活性和选择性受到支持材料性质的显著影响，这影响了还原性、酸碱性质和分散性。支持VOx催化剂比纯V2O5更具选择性，因为支持材料的相互作用稳定了特别活跃的VOx表面结构。DFT计算和同位素标记实验表明，V基催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中可能同时存在一步法和两步法途径。大多数V基催化剂通过Mars van Krevelen机制运行，支持材料（如CeO2）中氧原子的参与增加可以提高烯烃的选择性。CeO2的加入提高了氧的流动性和空位浓度，与纯ZrO2相比。混合氧化物支持材料由于具有更大的酸碱双功能而具有更好的活性。酸性支持材料与V2O5的相互作用较弱，导致结晶体分散性差。聚集体V物种导致烯烃产率低，突出了分散性在控制选择性中的作用。DFT研究表明，V2O5在SiO2或CeO2等支持材料上的最优尺寸不同。二聚体在决定性的氢抽提步骤中具有动力学偏好。分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5可以实现90%的丙烯选择性，但转化率在一个循环内从60%下降到20%。这种催化性能仅维持了八个反应再生循环（64小时）。V2O5催化剂的开发前景光明，但促进烯烃裂解的酸性位点和V2O5粉尘的健康问题仍然是挑战。Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中表现出高C2H4选择性（>75%），而Fe1Ni3/CeO2通过干重整（DR）途径具有高CO选择性（>98%）。选择性在可比的C2H6转化率下几乎不变，表明差异来自内在动力学。C2H4是Fe3Ni1/CeO2上的主要产物，表明其作为CO2-ODH催化剂的潜力。Ni/CeO2是一种优秀的重整催化剂，而Fe/CeO2的效果较差。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH中表现出的高选择性可归因于涉及Fe-Ni相互作用的特定活性位点。
