                    The evolution of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a promising alternative for propylene production has been significantly influenced by the development of innovative catalyst design strategies aimed at addressing catalyst deactivation challenges \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. These strategies have been pivotal in enhancing the stability and recyclability of PDH catalysts, particularly in the context of increasing industrial demand and changing global energy landscapes. The synthesis of mesoporous Al2O3 via CADH (controlled aqueous decomposition of hydrates) has been instrumental in boosting the performance of VOx/Al2O3 catalysts \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. Optimal V loading (12 wt%) increases the concentration of V3+ species, while P modification reduces VOx polymerization and Mg modification acts as an anti-coking agent, significantly improving catalytic performance \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. The direct co-assembly of V-DMSNs (vanadium-doped mesoporous silica nanoparticles) achieves 18.1% propane conversion, 91% propene selectivity, and a space-time yield (STY) of 22 molC3H6 kg-1cat h-1 \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. VOx/ZrO2 is found to be 5 times more active (STY 9.2 molC3H6 kg-1cat h-1) than VOx/Al2O3 \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. Preparation methods play a crucial role in tuning the active V species, impacting the overall performance of V-based catalysts \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. However, V-based catalysts' STYs are approximately 34 times less than those of Pt catalysts \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH catalyst design, particularly the development of VOx-based catalysts with enhanced stability and recyclability, have opened up new avenues for efficient propylene production. Future research endeavors might focus on the optimization of preparation methods to further refine the active V species, aiming for higher STYs that approach those of Pt catalysts. Additionally, the exploration of alternative supports and modification strategies, such as the use of ZrO2 and Mg for anti-coking, could lead to the development of more robust and cost-effective PDH catalysts. The integration of PDH processes with renewable energy sources, such as solar or wind, could also be a promising direction, aligning with the global shift towards sustainable energy landscapes.

                    丙烷脱氢(PDH)作为丙烯生产的一种有前景的替代方案，其发展受到了旨在解决催化剂失活挑战的创新催化剂设计策略的显著影响\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。这些策略在增强PDH催化剂的稳定性和可回收性方面发挥了关键作用，特别是在工业需求增加和全球能源格局变化的背景下。CADH(控制水解法)合成的介孔Al2O3对VOx/Al2O3催化剂性能的提升起到了重要作用\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。最优的V负载量(12 wt%)增加了V3+物种的浓度，而P改性减少了VOx的聚合，Mg改性则作为抗焦剂，显著提高了催化性能\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。直接共组装的V-DMSNs(含钒介孔二氧化硅纳米颗粒)实现了18.1%的丙烷转化率，91%的丙烯选择性，以及22 molC3H6 kg-1cat h-1的空间时间产率(STY)\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。VOx/ZrO2的活性是VOx/Al2O3的5倍(STY 9.2 molC3H6 kg-1cat h-1)\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。制备方法在调节活性V物种方面起着至关重要的作用，影响V基催化剂的整体性能\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。然而，V基催化剂的STY大约是Pt催化剂的1/34\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: PDH催化剂设计的进展，特别是具有增强稳定性和可回收性的VOx基催化剂的发展，为高效丙烯生产开辟了新的途径。未来的研究努力可能集中在优化制备方法上，以进一步细化活性V物种，目标是提高STY，接近Pt催化剂的水平。此外，探索替代载体和改性策略，如使用ZrO2和Mg抗焦，可能会导致开发出更坚固、更具成本效益的PDH催化剂。将PDH过程与可再生能源，如太阳能或风能，相结合，也可能是一个有希望的方向，与全球向可持续能源格局转变的趋势相一致。
