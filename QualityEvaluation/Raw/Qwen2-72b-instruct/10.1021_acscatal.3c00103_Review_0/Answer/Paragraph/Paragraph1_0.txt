            <English> 
                    The evolution of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a promising alternative for propylene production has been significantly influenced by the development of innovative catalyst design strategies to address catalyst deactivation challenges \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. These strategies have been pivotal in enhancing stability and recyclability, particularly in the context of increasing industrial demand and changing global energy landscapes. VOx-based catalysts have shown remarkable improvements through CADH for Al2O3, P and Mg modifications for stability, V/SiO2 for high propene yield, V-DMSNs for high STY, and VOx/ZrO2 for enhanced activity \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. ZnO catalysts have also emerged as a viable alternative, tripling propene yield and addressing the toxicity and regeneration issues associated with Cr/Pt catalysts \[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]. Innovations in PDH have not only focused on enhancing propene production but also on tackling the high temperature issues through the use of VOx, NiOx, and supported oxides, as well as membrane reactors and doped ceria for improved selectivity \[10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016\]. The MVK mechanism, involving reactant-catalyst oxygen interaction, is central to oxidative PDH, and advanced design strategies have been developed to boost efficiency \[10.1039/D0CS01262A\]. 
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH catalyst design, particularly the VOx-based catalysts and the innovative ZnO catalysts, have opened new avenues for sustainable propylene production. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of single-atom, alloy, and supported metal catalysts to further minimize deactivation and coking, thereby enhancing stability and recyclability \[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]. The exploration of novel catalysts, such as those based on V-based catalysts on SiO2, ZrO2, and Al2O3, could lead to the discovery of materials with superior stability and recyclability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\]. Moreover, the investigation of coke reduction strategies, including reductive gas, Pt-Sn alloy, support tweaks, Pt size control, and single atom catalysts, could significantly enhance the stability and recyclability of PDH catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]. The use of HZSM-5/ZnO in PDH could also be a promising direction for enhancing stability and recyclability \[10.1016/j.mcat.2019.110508\]. The optimization of Cr Beta zeolites for CO2-PDH could further improve catalytic stability and efficiency \[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\]. These directions could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and sustainable PDH processes, addressing the increasing industrial demand for propylene and aligning with the evolving global energy landscapes.

                    丙烷脱氢（PDH）作为生产丙烯的有前途的替代方法，其发展受到创新催化剂设计策略的显著影响，这些策略旨在解决催化剂失活挑战\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。这些策略在提高稳定性和可回收性方面发挥了关键作用，特别是在工业需求增加和全球能源格局变化的背景下。基于VOx的催化剂通过CADH为Al2O3、P和Mg的修改以提高稳定性、V/SiO2以获得高丙烯产量、V-DMSNs以获得高STY以及VOx/ZrO2以增强活性，显示出显著的改进\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。ZnO催化剂也作为可行的替代品出现，丙烯产量提高了三倍，并解决了与Cr/Pt催化剂相关的毒性和再生问题\[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]。PDH的创新不仅集中在提高丙烯生产上，还集中在通过使用VOx、NiOx和支撑氧化物以及膜反应器和掺杂的铈来解决高温问题，以提高选择性\[10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016\]。MVK机制，涉及反应物-催化剂氧气的相互作用，是氧化PDH的核心，先进的设计策略已经开发出来以提高效率\[10.1039/D0CS01262A\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: VOx基催化剂和创新ZnO催化剂在PDH催化剂设计方面的进步为可持续丙烯生产开辟了新的途径。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发单原子、合金和支撑金属催化剂上，以进一步减少失活和焦炭形成，从而提高稳定性和可回收性\[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]。基于V基催化剂在SiO2、ZrO2和Al2O3上的新型催化剂的探索可能会导致发现具有优越稳定性和可回收性的材料\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\]。此外，焦炭减少策略的调查，包括还原气体、Pt-Sn合金、支撑修改、Pt尺寸控制和单原子催化剂，可以显著提高PDH催化剂的稳定性和可回收性\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]。在PDH中使用HZSM-5/ZnO也可能是一个有希望的方向，以增强稳定性和可回收性\[10.1016/j.mcat.2019.110508\]。Cr Beta沸石的CO2-PDH优化可以进一步提高催化稳定性和效率\[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\]。这些方向可能最终导致更有效和可持续的PDH过程的发展，以解决工业对丙烯日益增长的需求，并与不断变化的全球能源格局保持一致。
