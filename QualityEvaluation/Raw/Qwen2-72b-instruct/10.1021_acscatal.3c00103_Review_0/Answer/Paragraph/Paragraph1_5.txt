            <English> 
                    Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) has emerged as a promising alternative for propylene production, addressing the limitations of traditional methods and the increasing industrial demand for propylene \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. The evolution of PDH catalysts has been pivotal in this context, with a focus on overcoming catalyst deactivation challenges through innovative design strategies to enhance stability and recyclability \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. Vanadium-based catalysts, such as VOx/Al2O3, have been optimized via CADH to boost Lewis acid sites, while P modifications reduce VOx polymerization and Mg modifications fight coking \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. The use of V/SiO2, including V-DMSNs, has shown high activity due to V5+ interaction with silica \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. VOx/ZrO2 has been found to be 5 times more active than VOx/Al2O3, with a rich V3+ species from VO reduction \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. Preparation methods and the nature of the support play a critical role in tuning the V species for optimal PDH performance \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. 

                    ZnO catalysts have also shown promise, outperforming Cr/Pt analogues in propene productivity by a factor of three, addressing deactivation issues \[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]. Cobalt catalysts, optimized by active site isolation, have demonstrated high PDH performance, achieving 40% propane conversion and >97% propene selectivity without deactivation over 10 hours \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.03.023\]. Vanadium oxide catalysts have been designed to manage hydroxyl groups, suppressing coke formation and enhancing stability and recyclability \[10.1002/anie.201800123\]. Single-atom, alloy, and supported metal catalysts have been developed to enhance stability and recyclability, resisting coking and withstanding high temperatures \[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]. V-based catalysts on mesoporous supports, such as VOX-SiO2 and VOX/MCM-41, have shown stable PDH performance, with full recovery via oxidative regeneration, making them viable for commercial PDH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\].

                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH catalyst design have significantly contributed to addressing the challenges of deactivation and enhancing stability and recyclability. The use of vanadium-based catalysts, ZnO, cobalt catalysts, and innovative design strategies for single-atom, alloy, and supported metal catalysts have shown promise in meeting the increasing industrial demand for propylene. Future research efforts might focus on further refining the preparation methods for vanadium-based catalysts to optimize the V species for enhanced PDH performance. The exploration of novel supports and the development of more efficient regeneration techniques could also be pivotal in improving the recyclability of PDH catalysts. Moreover, the integration of PDH processes with renewable energy sources could offer a sustainable pathway for propylene production, aligning with the evolving global energy landscape.

                    丙烷脱氢（PDH）作为丙烯生产的一种有前景的替代方案，解决了传统方法的局限性和丙烯日益增长的工业需求\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。PDH催化剂的演变在此背景下至关重要，重点在于通过创新设计策略克服催化剂失活挑战，以增强稳定性和可回收性\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。例如，VOx/Al2O3催化剂通过CADH优化，以增强Lewis酸位点，而P改性减少了VOx聚合，Mg改性对抗了焦炭形成\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。V/SiO2的使用，包括V-DMSNs，由于V5+与二氧化硅的相互作用而显示出高活性\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。VOx/ZrO2被发现比VOx/Al2O3活性高5倍，从VO还原中获得了丰富的V3+物种\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。制备方法和载体的性质在调整V物种以实现最佳PDH性能方面起着关键作用\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。

                    ZnO催化剂也显示出潜力，其丙烯生产力是Cr/Pt类似物的三倍，解决了失活问题\[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]。通过活性位点隔离优化的钴催化剂在PDH性能方面表现出色，实现了40%的丙烷转化率和>97%的丙烯选择性，10小时内无失活\[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.03.023\]。通过管理羟基群，抑制焦炭形成，增强稳定性和可回收性，设计了钒氧化物催化剂\[10.1002/anie.201800123\]。开发了单原子、合金和支撑金属催化剂，以增强稳定性和可回收性，抵抗焦炭形成，承受高温\[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]。基于介孔载体的V基催化剂，如VOX-SiO2和VOX/MCM-41，显示出稳定的PDH性能，通过氧化再生完全恢复，使其适用于商业PDH\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\]。

                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: PDH催化剂设计的进步在很大程度上有助于解决失活挑战，增强稳定性和可回收性。使用钒基催化剂、ZnO、钴催化剂以及单原子、合金和支撑金属催化剂的创新设计策略在满足丙烯日益增长的工业需求方面显示出潜力。未来的研究工作可能集中在进一步完善制备方法，以优化V基催化剂的V物种，以增强PDH性能。探索新型载体和开发更有效的再生技术也可能在提高PDH催化剂的可回收性方面发挥关键作用。此外，将PDH过程与可再生能源源的整合可以为丙烯生产提供一条可持续的途径，与不断变化的全球能源格局相一致。
