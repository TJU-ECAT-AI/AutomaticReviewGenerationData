            <English> 
                    The evolution of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) as a promising alternative for propylene production has gained significant traction, particularly in the context of increasing industrial demand and the limitations of traditional production methods \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. This review highlights the advancements in PDH catalysts, with a focus on addressing catalyst deactivation challenges through innovative design strategies to enhance stability and recyclability. VOx/Al2O3 catalysts have been pivotal, with CADH boosting STY and V3+ species playing a key role \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. Phosphorus and magnesium modifications have been shown to enhance stability and anti-coking properties \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. VOx/ZrO2 and V/SiO2 catalysts have also demonstrated high STY, with the role of V species requiring further study \[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]. ZnO catalysts have emerged as an eco-friendly alternative, tripling propene productivity compared to toxic Cr/Pt alternatives \[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]. The use of ZrO2 catalysts has been optimized for phase composition, enhancing activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012\]. Cobalt catalysts, optimized by active site isolation, have achieved high propane conversion and propene selectivity without deactivation \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.03.023\]. VOx catalysts have been designed to control surface structure, with hydroxyl groups playing a crucial role in suppressing coke formation \[10.1002/anie.201800123\]. Single-atom, alloy, and supported metal catalysts have been employed to minimize coke and enhance selective dehydrogenation \[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]. V-based catalysts on mesoporous supports have offered high stability and recyclability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\]. Pt catalyst deactivation has been mitigated through coke reduction strategies, including reductive gases, Pt alloying, support tweaks, size control, and single atom catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]. Cobalt catalysts supported on high-silica HZSM-5 zeolites have enhanced stability and recyclability \[10.1016/j.mcat.2019.110508\]. Cr Beta zeolites have been used to tackle deactivation, enhancing stability through dealumination and optimized acidity \[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH catalyst design have significantly contributed to addressing the challenges of catalyst deactivation, enhancing stability and recyclability. The use of eco-friendly ZnO catalysts and cobalt catalysts optimized for active site isolation has shown promise in boosting propene productivity and selectivity. The role of hydroxyl groups in VOx catalysts for suppressing coke formation and the employment of single-atom, alloy, and supported metal catalysts for minimizing coke and enhancing selective dehydrogenation have opened new avenues for catalyst design. The optimization of V-based catalysts on mesoporous supports for high stability and recyclability and the mitigation of Pt catalyst deactivation through coke reduction strategies have been crucial in enhancing PDH efficiency. The use of Cr Beta zeolites for tackling deactivation and enhancing stability through dealumination and optimized acidity has shown potential for CO2-PDH. Future research efforts might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can further enhance stability and recyclability, the optimization of reaction conditions for improved selectivity and efficiency, and the exploration of alternative feedstocks for PDH processes. The integration of PDH processes with other chemical processes for the production of value-added chemicals and the development of sustainable and eco-friendly PDH processes are also promising areas for future research.

            <Chinese> 
                    丙烷脱氢（PDH）作为丙烯生产的一种有前景的替代方案，特别是在工业需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的背景下，已经获得了显著的进展\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。本综述强调了PDH催化剂的进展，重点是通过创新设计策略解决催化剂失活挑战，以增强稳定性和可回收性。VOx/Al2O3催化剂在其中发挥了关键作用，CADH提高了STY，V3+物种起着核心作用\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。磷和镁的改性被证明可以增强稳定性和抗焦性能\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。VOx/ZrO2和V/SiO2催化剂也显示出高STY，V物种的作用需要进一步研究\[10.1039/D1GC03700E\]。ZnO催化剂作为一种环保替代品出现，与有毒的Cr/Pt替代品相比，丙烯生产力提高了三倍\[10.1038/s41586-021-03923-3\]。ZrO2催化剂的相组成得到了优化，增强了活性、选择性和稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2019.02.012\]。通过活性位点隔离优化的钴催化剂实现了高丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性，没有失活\[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.03.023\]。VOx催化剂被设计来控制表面结构，其中羟基在抑制焦炭形成中起着关键作用\[10.1002/anie.201800123\]。单原子、合金和支撑金属催化剂被用于最小化焦炭并增强选择性脱氢\[10.1039/C5SC01227A\]。基于V的催化剂在介孔支撑上提供了高稳定性和可回收性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2017.12.004\]。Pt催化剂失活通过焦炭减少策略得到了缓解，包括还原气体、Pt合金化、支撑调整、尺寸控制和单原子催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331\]。高硅H-ZSM-5沸石支撑的钴催化剂增强了稳定性和可回收性\[10.1016/j.mcat.2019.110508\]。Cr Beta沸石被用于解决失活问题，通过脱铝和优化酸性增强稳定性\[10.1016/j.jcou.2019.09.018\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: PDH催化剂设计的进展在很大程度上有助于解决催化剂失活的挑战，增强稳定性和可回收性。使用环保的ZnO催化剂和通过活性位点隔离优化的钴催化剂在提高丙烯生产力和选择性方面显示出潜力。VOx催化剂中羟基在抑制焦炭形成中的作用以及单原子、合金和支撑金属催化剂在最小化焦炭和增强选择性脱氢中的应用为催化剂设计开辟了新的途径。基于V的催化剂在介孔支撑上优化以实现高稳定性和可回收性，以及通过焦炭减少策略缓解Pt催化剂失活，对于提高PDH效率至关重要。Cr Beta沸石用于解决失活问题，通过脱铝和优化酸性增强稳定性，为CO2-PDH显示了潜力。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发新型催化剂，以进一步增强稳定性和可回收性，优化反应条件以提高选择性和效率，以及探索PDH过程的替代原料。将PDH过程与其他化学过程集成以生产高附加值化学品，以及开发可持续和环保的PDH过程，也是未来研究的有希望的领域。
