<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, PDH techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route for propylene production. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of single-atom catalysts (SACs) with well-defined local structures to establish structure-reactivity relationships, the modification of acid-base properties of catalysts through alkali metal additives, and the control of the ratio of different oxygen sites on the catalyst surface to balance activity and selectivity. Additionally, the use of metal-oxide interfaces and the tuning of oxygen activity on oxides are explored to reduce the formation of byproducts and improve selectivity. The development of these catalysts is supported by computational methods, such as density functional theory (DFT) calculations and microkinetic modeling, which help in understanding the effects of geometrical and electronic structures on the activation of C-H bonds and in screening potential catalyst candidates.

1.在工业对丙烯需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的背景下，PDH技术通过提供丙烯生产的替代途径来应对这些挑战。为了克服失活问题、增强稳定性和提高循环利用性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，这些策略适用于氧化和非氧化PDH过程。这些策略包括使用具有明确定义的局部结构的单原子催化剂（SACs），以建立结构-反应性关系，通过碱金属添加剂修改催化剂的酸碱性质，以及通过控制催化剂表面不同氧位点的比例来平衡活性和选择性。此外，还探索了金属-氧化物界面的使用和氧化物上氧活性的调节，以减少副产品的形成并提高选择性。这些催化剂的开发得到了计算方法的支持，如密度泛函理论（DFT）计算和微动力学建模，这些方法有助于理解几何和电子结构对C-H键活化的影响，并筛选潜在的催化剂候选者。
