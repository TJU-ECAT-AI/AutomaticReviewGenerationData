1.In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, PDH techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route for propylene production. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of single-atom catalysts (SACs) with well-defined local structures to establish structure-reactivity relationships, the modification of acid-base properties of catalysts using alkali metal additives, and the control of the ratio of different oxygen sites on the catalyst surface to balance activity and selectivity. The design of new catalysts with improved selectivity is guided by the understanding that the ratio of the terminal VO to the other two oxygen sites is a key parameter. Sub-monolayer VOx catalysts can provide a high propylene yield by compromising between activity and selectivity. The use of metal oxides with high redox properties and less acidic metal oxides is also being explored to achieve higher catalytic activity and propylene yields, respectively. The introduction of CO2 as a mild oxidant can reactivate deactivated redox catalysts and overcome the problems of over-oxidation and low propylene selectivity. The development of thermally stable single-atom catalysts (SACs) and the use of strong metal-support interactions (SMSI) to prevent single atoms from sintering on the surface are also being investigated. Theoretical studies using density functional theory (DFT) calculations and microkinetic modeling are providing insights into the effects of geometrical and electronic structures on the activation of C-H bonds of light alkanes and the discovery of new and more effective catalysts for light alkane conversion.

1.在工业对丙烯需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的背景下，PDH技术通过提供丙烯生产的替代途径来应对这些挑战。为了克服失活问题、提高稳定性和改善循环利用性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，这些策略适用于氧化和非氧化PDH过程。这些策略包括使用具有明确定义的局部结构的单原子催化剂（SACs），以建立结构-反应性关系，使用碱金属添加剂修改催化剂的酸碱性质，以及控制催化剂表面不同氧位点的比例，以平衡活性和选择性。设计具有改进选择性的新催化剂的指导原则是，终端VO与另外两种氧位点的比例是一个关键参数。亚单层VOx催化剂可以通过在活性和选择性之间妥协来提供高丙烯产量。正在探索使用具有高氧化还原性能的金属氧化物和酸性较小的金属氧化物，以分别实现更高的催化活性和丙烯产量。将CO2作为温和的氧化剂引入可以重新激活失活的氧化还原催化剂，并克服过度氧化和丙烯选择性低的问题。正在研究热稳定的单原子催化剂（SACs）的发展和使用强金属-支撑相互作用（SMSI）来防止表面单原子烧结。理论研究使用密度泛函理论（DFT）计算和微动力学建模正在提供关于轻烷烃C-H键激活的几何和电子结构效应以及轻烷烃转化的有效新催化剂发现的见解。
