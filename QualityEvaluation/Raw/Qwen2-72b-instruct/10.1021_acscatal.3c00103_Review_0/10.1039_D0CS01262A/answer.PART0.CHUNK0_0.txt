1.In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route to produce propylene from propane. PDH processes can be either oxidative or nonoxidative. In oxidative PDH, propane is converted to propylene and water in the presence of a catalyst and oxygen. In nonoxidative PDH, propane is directly dehydrogenated to propylene without the need for oxygen. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of bimetallic catalysts, the incorporation of promoters, and the development of novel catalyst supports. Bimetallic catalysts can improve the selectivity and stability of the PDH process by providing multiple active sites for the dehydrogenation reaction. Promoters can enhance the activity and stability of the catalyst by modifying the electronic properties of the active metal. Novel catalyst supports can improve the recyclability of the catalyst by providing a stable and reusable platform for the active metal. These strategies are being developed to improve the efficiency and sustainability of PDH processes and to meet the increasing industrial demand for propylene.

1.在工业对丙烯需求日益增长和传统生产方法局限性的背景下，丙烷脱氢(PDH)技术通过提供从丙烷生产丙烯的替代途径来应对这些挑战。PDH过程可以是氧化的，也可以是非氧化的。在氧化PDH中，丙烷在催化剂和氧气的存在下转化为丙烯和水。在非氧化PDH中，丙烷直接脱氢生成丙烯，无需氧气。为了克服失活问题，提高稳定性和改善可回收性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，这些策略适用于氧化和非氧化PDH过程。这些策略包括使用双金属催化剂、加入促进剂和开发新型催化剂载体。双金属催化剂可以通过为脱氢反应提供多个活性位点来提高PDH过程的选择性和稳定性。促进剂可以通过改变活性金属的电子性质来提高催化剂的活性和稳定性。新型催化剂载体可以通过为活性金属提供稳定和可重复使用的平台来提高催化剂的可回收性。这些策略正在开发中，以提高PDH过程的效率和可持续性，以满足工业对丙烯日益增长的需求。
