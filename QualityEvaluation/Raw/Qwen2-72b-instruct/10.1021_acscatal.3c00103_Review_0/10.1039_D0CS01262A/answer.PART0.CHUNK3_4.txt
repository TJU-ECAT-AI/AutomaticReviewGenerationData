1.In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, PDH techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route for propylene production. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of single-atom catalysts (SACs) with well-defined local structures to establish structure-reactivity relationships, the modification of acid-base properties of catalysts using alkali metal additives, and the tuning of oxygen activity on metal oxides to reduce CC bond cleavage and oxidation to COx. The design of new catalysts with improved selectivity involves compromising cluster size between activity and selectivity, with sub-monolayer VOx providing a high propylene yield. The ratio of terminal VO to other oxygen sites is a key parameter in catalyst design, with small cluster VOx increasing propylene selectivity at the expense of activity, while polymeric VOx decreases selectivity. The use of SACs on metal oxides has received much attention in catalyzing PDH, with single metal atoms deposited onto transition metal, graphene, and metal oxides. The addition of a second metal to Pt-based alloys can lower olefin desorption energy, increase olefin selectivity, and lower coking potential, although at the expense of lower activity. The modification of electronic properties of active sites through the use of various metals, alloys, single atoms, metal-oxide interfaces, and locally coordinated oxygen in oxides can improve the catalytic performance of alkane conversion.

1.在工业对丙烯需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的背景下，PDH技术通过提供丙烯生产的替代途径来应对这些挑战。为了克服失活问题、提高稳定性和改善氧化和非氧化PDH过程的可回收性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略。这些策略包括使用具有明确定义的局部结构的单原子催化剂（SACs）来建立结构-反应性关系，使用碱金属添加剂来修改催化剂的酸碱性质，以及通过调节金属氧化物上的氧活性来减少CC键断裂和氧化为COx。设计具有改进选择性的新型催化剂涉及在活性和选择性之间折衷簇大小，其中亚单层VOx提供高丙烯产量。终端VO与其它氧位点的比例是催化剂设计中的关键参数，小簇VOx通过牺牲活性来增加丙烯选择性，而聚合VOx则降低选择性。在金属氧化物上使用SACs在催化PDH方面受到了广泛关注，单金属原子沉积在过渡金属、石墨烯和金属氧化物上。在Pt基合金中添加第二种金属可以降低烯烃脱附能，增加烯烃选择性，并降低焦化潜力，尽管以较低的活性为代价。通过使用各种金属、合金、单原子、金属-氧化物界面和氧化物中局部配位的氧来修改活性位点的电子性质，可以改善烷烃转化的催化性能。
