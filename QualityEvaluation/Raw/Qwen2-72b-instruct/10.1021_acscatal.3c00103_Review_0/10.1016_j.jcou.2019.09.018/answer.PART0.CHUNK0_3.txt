1.Increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods have led to the development of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of chromium-containing Beta zeolite catalysts, which have shown high activity and selectivity in PDH processes. Dealumination of Beta zeolite and subsequent chromium dispersion by simple impregnation procedures have resulted in excellent catalysts for PDH. The well correlation between the number of redox Cr sites and propene yield up to 2wt % of Cr has been determined. Moreover, in switch operation mode between propane dehydrogenation in presence and absence of CO2 the formation rate of propene raises in mode with CO2 only over Cr-containing SiBeta zeolite catalyst, while over Cr-containing AlBeta zeolite catalyst a negative effect on the rate of propene formation was observed.

1.工业上对丙烯需求的增加和传统生产方法的局限性导致了丙烷脱氢(PDH)技术的发展。为了克服PDH过程中催化剂失活问题，提高催化剂的稳定性和可回收性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略。这些策略包括使用含铬的Beta沸石催化剂，这种催化剂在PDH过程中表现出高活性和选择性。通过硝酸处理Beta沸石进行脱铝，然后通过简单的浸渍程序进行铬分散，已经得到了PDH的优秀催化剂。已经确定了Cr的红ox位点数量与丙烯产量之间的良好相关性，直到Cr含量达到2wt%。此外，在丙烷脱氢存在和不存在CO2的切换操作模式下，仅在Cr含量的SiBeta沸石催化剂上存在CO2时，丙烯的形成速率提高，而在Cr含量的AlBeta沸石催化剂上，CO2对丙烯形成速率有负面影响。
