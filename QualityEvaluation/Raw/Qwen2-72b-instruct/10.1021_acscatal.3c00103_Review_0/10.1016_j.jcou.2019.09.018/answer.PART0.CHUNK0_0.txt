1.Increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods are addressed by propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques. Innovative catalyst design strategies being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes include the use of chromium-containing Beta zeolite catalysts. Dealumination of Beta zeolite and subsequent chromium dispersion by simple impregnation procedures give excellent catalysts for CO2-PDH. The well correlation between the number of redox Cr sites and propene yield up to 2wt % of Cr has been determined. Comparing of catalytic activity of Cr-containing Beta zeolite catalysts with the identical Cr content (2wt % of Cr) supported on SiBeta and AlBeta revealed that the dealumination has great impact on catalytic properties. Low acidity of SiBeta is suitable for achieving high selectivity to propene (above 80 %) while high acidity of starting AlBeta promotes propane cracking to lighter hydrocarbons. Moreover, in switch operation mode between propane dehydrogenation in presence and absence of CO2 the formation rate of propene raises in mode with CO2 only over Cr-containing SiBeta zeolite catalyst, while over Cr-containing AlBeta zeolite catalyst a negative effect on the rate of propene formation was observed.

1.为了应对丙烯工业需求的增加和传统生产方法的局限性，丙烷脱氢(PDH)技术应运而生。为了克服失活问题，提高稳定性和改善循环利用性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，这些策略适用于氧化和非氧化PDH过程。其中包括使用含铬的Beta沸石催化剂。Beta沸石的脱铝和随后通过简单的浸渍程序进行的铬分散，为CO2-PDH提供了出色的催化剂。已经确定了Cr位点的还原数量与丙烯产量之间的良好相关性，直到Cr含量达到2wt%。将Cr含量相同(2wt%Cr)的Cr含Beta沸石催化剂在SiBeta和AlBeta上的催化活性进行比较，发现脱铝对催化性能有重大影响。SiBeta的低酸性适合获得高丙烯选择性(超过80%)，而AlBeta的高酸性会促进丙烷裂解为更轻的烃类。此外，在丙烷脱氢存在和不存在CO2的切换操作模式下，仅在Cr含SiBeta沸石催化剂上存在CO2时，丙烯的生成速率才会提高，而在Cr含AlBeta沸石催化剂上，CO2对丙烯生成速率有负面影响。
