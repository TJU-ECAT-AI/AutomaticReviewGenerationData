1.In the context of increasing industrial demand for propylene and the limitations of traditional production methods, PDH techniques address these challenges by providing an alternative route for propylene production from propane, a readily available feedstock. Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in both oxidative and nonoxidative PDH processes. These strategies include the use of high-silica HZSM-5 zeolites as supports for ZnO catalysts, which have been shown to improve the dispersion of Zn species, reduce side reactions, and minimize coke deposition. The high-silica HZSM-5 zeolite, with its good textural properties, well-defined structure, and well-adjusted acidity, is helpful for dispersing Zn species and reducing side reactions. Moreover, it is proposed that the small ZnO clusters directly connected with the framework of HZSM-5 zeolite are active phases, which are massively produced upon the zeolite support with fine structure condition and sparse Al sites. The ZnO catalysts supported on high-silica HZSM-5 are low-cost, toxicity-free, and reusable, thus representing a promising approach for industrial propylene production.

1.在工业对丙烯需求日益增长以及传统生产方法局限性的背景下，PDH技术通过从丙烷这一易得原料中提供丙烯生产的替代途径来应对这些挑战。为了克服失活问题、增强稳定性和提高循环利用性，正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，适用于氧化和非氧化PDH过程。这些策略包括使用高硅HZSM-5沸石作为ZnO催化剂的载体，已被证明可以改善Zn物种的分散，减少副反应，并最小化焦炭沉积。高硅HZSM-5沸石，具有良好的纹理性质、明确定义的结构和调整得当的酸性，有助于Zn物种的分散和副反应的减少。此外，有人提出，直接与HZSM-5沸石框架相连的小ZnO簇是活性相，当沸石载体具有精细结构条件和稀疏的Al位点时，这些簇大量产生。支持在高硅HZSM-5上的ZnO催化剂成本低廉、无毒且可重复使用，因此代表了工业丙烯生产的一种有前景的方法。
