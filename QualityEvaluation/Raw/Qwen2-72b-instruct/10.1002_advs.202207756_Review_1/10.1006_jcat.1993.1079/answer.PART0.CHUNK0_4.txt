1.The primary challenges in elucidating Ga speciation in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts under propane dehydrogenation (PDH) conditions include understanding the dynamic changes in the chemical state of Ga, which can exist in multiple oxidation states and coordination environments. In situ or operando characterization techniques, such as X-ray absorption spectroscopy, are crucial for overcoming these challenges by providing real-time information on the chemical form of Ga under reaction conditions. This approach helps in advancing our understanding of structure-activity relationships in these catalytic systems by revealing the active species and their role in the catalytic cycle.
