                    The catalytic direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) is a pivotal process for propene production, characterized by its endothermic nature and the challenges of thermodynamic equilibrium and coking \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]. Pt-based catalysts, despite their high activity, are prone to coking, which significantly impacts their efficiency and selectivity \[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]. Smaller Pt particles (3nm) exhibit higher activity but lower propene selectivity and higher coking rates compared to larger particles (21nm), which favor dehydrogenation over cracking \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]. The presence of coke, paradoxically, can act as a promoter by deactivating cracking sites, thus enhancing propene selectivity \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\].

                    Strategies to mitigate coking on Pt-based catalysts include the use of promoters such as Sn, which can extend the catalyst's lifetime \[10.1002/cctc.202001640\], and the implementation of coking-resistant catalysts and regeneration techniques \[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]. The addition of Cu to Pt-based catalysts can suppress coking, enhancing stability and selectivity \[10.1039/c4nr02143f\]. Moreover, the use of single-atom Pt catalysts on Cu supports and doped graphene can significantly improve selectivity and stability \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03286\].

                    The design of Pt surfaces, alloying with metals like Sn, and the use of supports like CeO2 and dopants can prevent sintering and suppress side reactions, thus reducing coking \[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]. Core-shell catalysts and TiOx supports can also enhance stability and selectivity \[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]. The optimization of reaction conditions, such as hydrogen co-feeding, can suppress coke precursors and reduce deactivation \[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\].

                    ZrO2 has emerged as an alternative to Pt-based catalysts, offering enhanced C-H bond cleavage and higher propene selectivity while minimizing coke formation \[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\]. Ceria's redox properties aid in the use of water vapor, which can significantly reduce coke formation on PtSn catalysts \[10.1002/anie.201701115\].

                    The industrial application of PDH is driven by the favorable propane-propylene price gap and the demand for pure olefins \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. The use of PtSn catalysts at 1-3 bar and 525-705°C ensures continuous operation and a lifespan of 2-3 years, with temperature adjustments sustaining activity \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. However, high temperatures foster coke formation, necessitating frequent regeneration \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH catalysts and strategies to mitigate coking have opened up new avenues for research and development. Future efforts might focus on the design of novel catalysts with enhanced stability and selectivity, such as the use of single-atom catalysts and the development of more efficient regeneration techniques. The exploration of alternative catalysts like ZrO2 and the optimization of reaction conditions to further reduce coke formation and improve catalyst performance are promising directions. Additionally, the integration of PDH processes with other chemical processes for the co-production of valuable chemicals could enhance the overall efficiency and economic viability of propene production.

                    催化直接脱氢丙烷（PDH）是生产丙烯的关键过程，其特征是吸热性质和热力学平衡与积碳的挑战\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。基于Pt的催化剂，尽管活性高，但容易积碳，这显著影响了其效率和选择性\[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]。较小的Pt颗粒（3nm）表现出更高的活性，但丙烯选择性较低，积碳率高于较大颗粒（21nm），后者更倾向于脱氢而非裂解\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。积碳，反常地，可以作为促进剂，通过使裂解位点失活，从而提高丙烯选择性\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。

                    减轻基于Pt催化剂积碳的策略包括使用Sn等促进剂，可以延长催化剂的寿命\[10.1002/cctc.202001640\]，以及实施抗积碳催化剂和再生技术\[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]。向基于Pt的催化剂中添加Cu可以抑制积碳，提高稳定性和选择性\[10.1039/c4nr02143f\]。此外，使用Cu支持的单原子Pt催化剂和掺杂石墨烯可以显著提高选择性和稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03286\]。

                    Pt表面的设计、与Sn等金属的合金化以及CeO2和掺杂剂的使用可以防止烧结和抑制副反应，从而减少积碳\[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]。核壳催化剂和TiOx支持也可以增强稳定性和选择性\[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]。反应条件的优化，如氢气共进料，可以抑制积碳前体，减少失活\[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\]。

                    ZrO2作为一种替代基于Pt的催化剂，提供了增强的C-H键断裂和更高的丙烯选择性，同时最小化积碳形成\[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\]。铈的氧化还原性质有助于水蒸气的使用，可以显著减少PtSn催化剂上的积碳形成\[10.1002/anie.201701115\]。

                    PDH的工业应用受到丙烷-丙烯价格差的有利影响和对纯烯烃的需求驱动\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。在1-3 bar和525-705°C下使用PtSn催化剂确保了连续运行和2-3年的使用寿命，温度调整维持了活性\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。然而，高温促进了积碳的形成，需要频繁的再生\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：PDH催化剂的进展和减轻积碳的策略为研究和开发开辟了新的途径。未来的工作可能集中在设计具有增强稳定性和选择性的新型催化剂上，例如使用单原子催化剂和开发更有效的再生技术。探索替代催化剂如ZrO2和优化反应条件以进一步减少积碳和提高催化剂性能是很有前景的方向。此外，将PDH过程与其他化学过程集成以共同生产有价值的化学品可以提高丙烯生产的整体效率和经济可行性。
