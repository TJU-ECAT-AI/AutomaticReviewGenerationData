                    The catalytic direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) for propene production is a critical process in the chemical industry, characterized by its endothermic nature and the challenges of cracking and coke formation \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]. The efficiency of this process is significantly influenced by the catalyst type and reaction conditions, with Pt-based catalysts being pivotal but susceptible to deactivation due to coke formation \[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]. Particle size plays a crucial role in the catalytic performance of Pt catalysts, with smaller particles (3 nm) exhibiting higher activity but lower selectivity and increased coking compared to larger particles (21 nm) \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]. Research efforts have been directed towards enhancing the dehydrogenation rate (DHP) while minimizing cracking and prolonging catalyst life \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\].

                    The formation of coke on Pt-based catalysts significantly impacts the process efficiency, acting as a double-edged sword by both hindering the reaction and potentially enhancing selectivity by deactivating cracking sites \[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]. Strategies to mitigate coke formation include modifying the catalyst surface to improve selectivity, weakening propylene adsorption, and controlling reaction conditions \[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]. Alloying, the use of specific supports, and the addition of dopants such as Sn and CeO2 have been shown to enhance stability, reduce coke formation, and improve efficiency \[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]. ZrO2 has also emerged as an alternative to Pt-based catalysts, offering high efficiency, durability, and mitigating coke formation \[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\].

                    Water vapor, facilitated by ceria's redox properties, has been demonstrated to effectively cut coke formation on PtSn catalysts, significantly impacting conversion rates \[10.1002/anie.20170115\]. Oxidation has been employed to regenerate spent catalysts, preserving efficiency without the need for chlorine \[10.1002/anie.20170115\]. The optimization of hydrogen and steam conditions, hydrocarbon pressure, space time, and linear velocity has been identified as critical for slowing coking and prolonging regeneration time \[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]. Single-atom catalysts, particularly Pt/Cu SAAs, have been shown to prevent coke formation, boost propylene yield, and maintain high selectivity and stability \[10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8\].

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in PDH for propene production have opened up opportunities for exploring emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can further mitigate coke formation while enhancing selectivity and stability. The use of single-atom catalysts and the optimization of reaction conditions to prolong catalyst life and reduce regeneration frequency are promising avenues. Moreover, the integration of advanced materials such as graphene and the exploration of alternative catalysts like ZrO2 could lead to significant improvements in efficiency and sustainability. The application of machine learning and computational modeling to predict catalyst performance and optimize reaction conditions holds potential for enhancing the PDH process. Additionally, the investigation of novel regeneration techniques and the development of more environmentally friendly processes could contribute to the overall advancement of PDH technology.

                    丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）生产丙烯是化工行业中的关键过程，其特征是吸热性质和裂解、焦炭形成等挑战\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。该过程的效率受催化剂类型和反应条件的显著影响，其中基于铂（Pt）的催化剂至关重要，但易因焦炭形成而失活\[10.1039/d0cp04669h\]。铂催化剂的粒径在催化性能中扮演着核心角色，较小的粒子（3纳米）表现出更高的活性，但选择性较低，焦炭形成增加，与较大的粒子（21纳米）相比\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。研究工作已朝着提高脱氢率（DHP）同时最小化裂解和延长催化剂寿命的方向发展\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。

                    铂基催化剂上的焦炭形成对过程效率有重大影响，它是一把双刃剑，既阻碍反应，又可能通过钝化裂解位点来提高选择性\[10.1016/j.catcom.2007.08.015\]。缓解焦炭形成的策略包括修改催化剂表面以提高选择性，削弱丙烯吸附，并控制反应条件\[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]。合金化、使用特定载体以及添加锡（Sn）和CeO2等掺杂剂已被证明能增强稳定性，减少焦炭形成，提高效率\[10.1039/d0cs01262a\]。ZrO2作为一种替代铂基催化剂的选项，提供了高效率、耐用性，并缓解了焦炭形成\[10.1038/s41467-018-06174-5\]。

                    水蒸气，通过铈（ceria）的氧化还原性质促进，已被证明能有效减少铂锡催化剂上的焦炭形成，对转化率产生显著影响\[10.1002/anie.20170115\]。氧化已被用于再生失活催化剂，无需氯气即可保持效率\[10.1002/anie.20170115\]。氢气和蒸汽条件、烃类压力、停留时间和线速度的优化已被确定为减缓焦炭形成和延长再生时间的关键\[10.1039/d0cs00983k\]。单原子催化剂，特别是Pt/Cu SAAs，已被证明能防止焦炭形成，提高丙烯产量，同时保持高选择性和稳定性\[10.1038/s41467-018-06967-8\]。

                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：丙烯PDH生产的发展为探索新兴领域和创新方法开辟了机会。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新型催化剂，以进一步缓解焦炭形成，同时提高选择性和稳定性。使用单原子催化剂和优化反应条件以延长催化剂寿命和减少再生频率是充满希望的方向。此外，先进材料如石墨烯的集成以及锆（ZrO2）等替代催化剂的探索，可能会显著提高效率和可持续性。将机器学习和计算建模应用于预测催化剂性能和优化反应条件，具有增强PDH过程的潜力。此外，新型再生技术的调查和更环保过程的开发，可能对PDH技术的总体进步做出贡献。
