<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The catalytic direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) is a key industrial reaction for the production of propylene. It is normally catalyzed by PtM alloys such as PtSn, PtZn, PtGa, PtIn, PtGe, and PtCu. The reaction conditions typically involve atmospheric pressure, temperatures around 520°C, and a propane-to-nitrogen ratio of 1:1. The selectivity and conversion of propane to propylene are crucial considerations. Coke formation on Pt-based catalysts can lead to deactivation and reduced efficiency. Strategies to mitigate coke formation include using single atom alloys (SAAs) like Pt/Cu, which can maintain high propylene selectivity and stability even after 120 hours on stream. The regeneration of the catalyst can be achieved by successive oxidation-reduction cycles, which can largely restore the initial activity.

1.催化直接脱氢丙烷（PDH）是生产丙烯的关键工业反应。它通常由PtM合金催化，如PtSn、PtZn、PtGa、PtIn、PtGe和PtCu。反应条件通常涉及大气压，温度约为520°C，丙烷与氮气的比例为1:1。丙烷转化为丙烯的选择性和转化率是关键考虑因素。Pt基催化剂上的焦炭形成会导致失活和效率降低。缓解焦炭形成的策略包括使用单原子合金（SAAs），如Pt/Cu，即使在连续120小时的运行后，也能保持高丙烯选择性和稳定性。催化剂的再生可以通过连续的氧化还原循环实现，这可以很大程度上恢复初始活性。
