<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The catalytic direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) is an important industrial reaction for the production of propylene. It is normally catalyzed by PtM alloys such as PtSn, PtZn, PtGa, PtIn, PtGe, and PtCu. The key considerations regarding reaction conditions include temperature, pressure, and the propane-to-nitrogen ratio. The catalyst types play a crucial role in determining the selectivity and activity of the reaction. The formation of coke on Pt-based catalysts can lead to deactivation and reduced selectivity for propylene. Strategies to mitigate coke formation include the use of single atom alloys (SAAs) like Pt/Cu, which can maintain high propylene selectivity and stability even under high-temperature conditions. The regeneration of the catalyst can be achieved through successive oxidation-reduction cycles, which can largely restore the initial activity of the catalyst.

1.催化直接脱氢丙烷（PDH）是生产丙烯的重要工业反应。通常使用PtM合金如PtSn、PtZn、PtGa、PtIn、PtGe和PtCu作为催化剂。关于反应条件的关键考虑因素包括温度、压力和丙烷与氮气的比例。催化剂类型在决定反应的选择性和活性方面起着关键作用。Pt基催化剂上形成的焦炭会导致失活和丙烯选择性降低。减轻焦炭形成的策略包括使用单原子合金（SAAs）如Pt/Cu，即使在高温条件下也能保持高丙烯选择性和稳定性。催化剂的再生可以通过连续的氧化还原循环实现，这可以很大程度上恢复催化剂的初始活性。
