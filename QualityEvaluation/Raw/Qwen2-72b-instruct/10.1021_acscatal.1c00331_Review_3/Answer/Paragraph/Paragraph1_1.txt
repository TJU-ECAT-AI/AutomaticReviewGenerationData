                    The comprehensive influence of catalyst support pore structure characteristics on coke formation, accommodation, and overall catalyst performance in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions is multifaceted and critical. Hierarchical micro-/mesoporous structures in siliceous MFI zeolites significantly boost catalytic performance by facilitating diffusion and enhancing metal nanoparticle dispersion \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. Incorporating La and Y into mesoporous zeolite supports improves Pt dispersion, stabilizing it as small nanoparticles, which results in enhanced PDH catalytic performance, including activity, selectivity, and durability \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. The pore network structure significantly influences coke formation and accommodation, affecting overall catalyst performance. Non-uniform coke distribution, influenced by pore structure and reaction conditions, complicates the coking process and leads to sudden changes in macroscopic properties like effective diffusivity and reaction rates \[10.1002/aic.16410\]. Encapsulating PtZn clusters within S-1 zeolite enhances stability and propylene selectivity, with pore structure playing a critical role in hydrogen generation rates and catalyst stability \[10.1002/anie.202003349\]. Non-acidic supports minimize coke formation by reducing cracking and polymerization reactions, while adding tin to platinum catalysts shifts coke from the metal to the support, enhancing coke tolerance and performance \[10.1016/j.jiec.2010.10.011\]. Pore size significantly impacts coke yield, with smaller pores leading to lower coke formation \[10.1007/s10562-009-0196-4\]. The pore geometry of catalyst supports, including size, structure, and connectivity, significantly influences activity, selectivity, and coke formation \[10.1016/j.micromeso.2009.05.031\]. Hierarchical pore modifications in ZSM-5 reduce acidity, inhibiting coke formation and enhancing coke accommodation \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_3\]. Hierarchical-pore structures in ZQ zeolite significantly enhance coke accommodation and catalyst performance compared to conventional ZSM-5 zeolite \[10.1002/jctb.4686\]. The pore structure of MFI zeolite supports plays a significant role in accommodating structural changes of PtSn clusters, influencing coke formation and catalyst performance \[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The detailed analysis of the influence of catalyst support pore structure characteristics on coke formation, accommodation, and overall catalyst performance in PDH reactions reveals the pivotal role of pore structure in optimal catalyst design. The hierarchical micro-/mesoporous structures in siliceous MFI zeolites, the pore network structure, and the pore geometry of catalyst supports significantly impact PDH catalytic performance and coke management \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4, 10.1002/aic.16410, 10.1016/j.micromeso.2009.05.031\]. Future research endeavors might focus on developing advanced characterization techniques to better understand the intrinsic influence of pore geometry on PDH, exploring innovative methods to modify pore structures for enhanced coke accommodation and stability, and designing catalysts with optimal pore size, connectivity, uniformity, and hierarchical arrangements for improved performance and durability in PDH reactions.

                    催化剂载体孔结构特性对丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中焦炭形成、容纳和整体催化剂性能的全面影响是多方面的且至关重要。硅酸盐MFI沸石中的分级微/介孔结构通过促进扩散和增强金属纳米粒子分散显著提高催化性能\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。将La和Y掺入介孔沸石载体中改善了Pt分散，将其稳定为小纳米粒子，从而显著提高了PDH催化性能，包括活性、选择性和耐用性\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。孔网络结构对焦炭形成和容纳有重大影响，影响整体催化剂性能。受孔结构和反应条件影响的不均匀焦炭分布使焦化过程复杂化，并导致宏观性质如有效扩散率和反应速率的突然变化\[10.1002/aic.16410\]。将PtZn簇封装在S-1沸石中增强了稳定性和丙烯选择性，孔结构在氢生成速率和催化剂稳定性中起关键作用\[10.1002/anie.202003349\]。非酸性载体通过减少裂解和聚合反应来最小化焦炭形成，而向铂催化剂中添加锡将焦炭从金属转移到载体，提高了焦炭耐受性和性能\[10.1016/j.jiec.2010.10.011\]。孔径大小显著影响焦炭产量，小孔径导致较低的焦炭形成\[10.1007/s10562-009-0196-4\]。催化剂载体的孔几何结构，包括尺寸、结构和连通性，对活性、选择性和焦炭形成有显著影响\[10.1016/j.micromeso.2009.05.031\]。在ZSM-5中引入分级孔结构降低了酸性，抑制了焦炭形成并增强了焦炭容纳\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_3\]。ZQ沸石中的分级孔结构与传统ZSM-5沸石相比显著提高了焦炭容纳和催化剂性能\[10.1002/jctb.4686\]。MFI沸石载体的孔结构在容纳PtSn簇的结构变化中起着重要作用，影响焦炭形成和催化剂性能\[10.1038/s41929-020-0472-7\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 对催化剂载体孔结构特性对PDH反应中焦炭形成、容纳和整体催化剂性能影响的详细分析揭示了孔结构在最优催化剂设计中的核心作用。硅酸盐MFI沸石中的分级微/介孔结构、孔网络结构和催化剂载体的孔几何结构对PDH催化性能和焦炭管理有重大影响\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4, 10.1002/aic.16410, 10.1016/j.micromeso.2009.05.031\]。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发先进的表征技术以更好地理解孔几何结构对PDH的内在影响，探索创新方法来修改孔结构以增强焦炭容纳和稳定性，以及设计具有最优孔径、连通性、均匀性和分级排列的催化剂，以提高PDH反应中的性能和耐用性。
