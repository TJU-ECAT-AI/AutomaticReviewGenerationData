The effect of CO2 on the yield of propene depends on the catalyst nature, with CO2 having a promoting effect on Cr-O/SiO2 and a negative effect on Cr-O/Al2O3. The thermodynamic calculations and catalytic tests indicate that the synthesis gas composition (molar ratio of CO/H2) can be controlled by changing the molar ratio of CO2:C3H8 in the feed.
