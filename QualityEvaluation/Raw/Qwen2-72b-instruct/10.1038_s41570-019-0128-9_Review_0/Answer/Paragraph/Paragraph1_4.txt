                    Chromium oxide catalysts supported on SiO2 and Al2O3 exhibit good performance in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) of propane \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. CO2 has a promoting effect on Cr-O/SiO2 and a negative effect on Cr-O/Al2O3 for propene yield. Synthesis gas composition can be controlled by adjusting the CO2:C3H8 ratio \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 show high activity, with CrOx/γ-Al2O3 favoring direct dehydrogenation and CrOx/ZrO2 selective oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/ZrO2 deactivates rapidly at high temperatures due to sintering or inactive chromium species formation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2, while less active, is the most stable due to the formation of α-Cr2O3, with selective oxidative dehydrogenation limited by surface re-oxidation difficulties \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 have high stability due to low ethylene formation activity and CO2 removal of carbon deposits \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CO2 enhances the yield of propylene and suppresses deactivation of the Cr2O3/SiO2 catalyst \[10.1246/cl.1996.973\]. CrOx/SiO2 activity and C3H6 selectivity increase with CO2 partial pressure, whereas CrOx/Al2O3 activity decreases \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CO2 adsorption on Al2O3 inhibits C3H8 adsorption, reducing Cr/Al2O3 activity \[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\]. The redox cycle between Cr(III)O6 and Cr(VI)O4 is pivotal for C3H6 dehydrogenation over both supports \[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\]. CO2 oxidizes Cr(III) to Cr(VI) under reaction conditions, enabling oxidative dehydrogenation of propane over Cr/SiO2 \[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature reveals that support materials significantly impact the performance, stability, and mechanistic behavior of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions. SiO2 and Al2O3 supports show good performance, with CO2 promoting Cr-O/SiO2 and hindering Cr-O/Al2O3 for propene yield. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 exhibit high activity, but CrOx/ZrO2 faces rapid deactivation at high temperatures. CrOx/CeO2 is less active but more stable. CO2 partial pressure boosts CrOx/SiO2 activity and C3H6 selectivity, whereas CrOx/Al2O3 activity decreases. Future research might focus on developing catalysts with enhanced stability at high temperatures, optimizing the CO2:C3H8 ratio for desired product selectivity, and understanding the role of CO2 in promoting dehydrogenation over specific supports. Exploring the redox cycle between Cr(III)O6 and Cr(VI)O4 could lead to the design of catalysts with improved activity and selectivity. Investigating the effects of other support materials, such as MCM-41, H-ZSM-5, and CeO2, could provide insights into the role of support properties in catalyst performance.

                    支撑材料为SiO2和Al2O3的铬氧化物催化剂在CO2氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）丙烷反应中表现出良好的性能\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CO2对Cr-O/SiO2有促进作用，而对Cr-O/Al2O3的丙烯产率有负面影响。通过调整CO2:C3H8的比例可以控制合成气的组成\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2表现出高活性，CrOx/γ-Al2O3倾向于直接脱氢，CrOx/ZrO2则促进选择性氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/ZrO2在高温下迅速失活，原因是烧结或形成不活跃的铬物种\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2虽然活性较低，但稳定性最高，归因于α-Cr2O3的形成，选择性氧化脱氢受限于表面再氧化的困难\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2和CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2具有高稳定性，归因于低乙烯形成活性和CO2去除碳沉积\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CO2提高了Cr2O3/SiO2催化剂的丙烯产量，抑制了催化剂的失活\[10.1246/cl.1996.973\]。CrOx/SiO2的活性和C3H6选择性随CO2分压的增加而增加，而CrOx/Al2O3的活性则降低\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。Al2O3上的CO2吸附抑制了C3H8的吸附，降低了Cr/Al2O3的活性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\]。Cr(III)O6和Cr(VI)O4之间的氧化还原循环对于两个支撑材料上的C3H6脱氢至关重要\[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\]。CO2在反应条件下将Cr(III)氧化为Cr(VI)，使Cr/SiO2能够进行丙烷的氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.cattod.2011.10.028\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 文献显示，支撑材料对铬氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能、稳定性和机制行为有重大影响。SiO2和Al2O3支撑材料表现出良好的性能，CO2促进Cr-O/SiO2，而对Cr-O/Al2O3的丙烯产率有负面影响。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2表现出高活性，但CrOx/ZrO2在高温下迅速失活。CrOx/CeO2活性较低，但稳定性最高。CO2分压提高了CrOx/SiO2的活性和C3H6选择性，而CrOx/Al2O3的活性则降低。未来的研究可能集中在开发在高温下具有增强稳定性的催化剂，优化CO2:C3H8比例以获得所需的产品选择性，以及理解CO2在特定支撑材料上促进脱氢的作用。探索Cr(III)O6和Cr(VI)O4之间的氧化还原循环可能导致设计出具有改进的活性和选择性的催化剂。研究其他支撑材料，如MCM-41、H-ZSM-5和CeO2，可能提供关于支撑材料性质在催化剂性能中的作用的见解。
