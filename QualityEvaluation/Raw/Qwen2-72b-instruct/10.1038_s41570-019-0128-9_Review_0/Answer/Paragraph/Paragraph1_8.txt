                    The performance, stability, and mechanistic behavior of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) reactions are significantly influenced by support materials and reaction conditions \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. Chromium oxide supported on SiO2 and Al2O3 exhibits good performance, with CO2 having a promoting effect on Cr-O/SiO2 and a negative effect on Cr-O/Al2O3 for propene yield \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 are highly active, with CrOx/γ-Al2O3 favoring direct dehydrogenation and CrOx/ZrO2 promoting selective oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/ZrO2 deactivates rapidly at high temperatures due to sintering or inactive chromium species formation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2, while less active, is the most stable due to the formation of α-Cr2O3, with selective oxidative dehydrogenation being minor due to surface re-oxidation difficulties \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 have high stability and remove carbon deposits by CO2 due to basic sites, with side reactions similar to CrOx/ZrO2, involving non-selective oxidation or dry-reforming \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CO2 partial pressure boosts CrOx/SiO2 activity and C3H6 selectivity, whereas CrOx/γ-Al2O3 activity drops with increased CO2 \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CO2 adsorption on γ-Al2O3 competes with C3H8 adsorption and alters CrOx/γ-Al2O3's acid-base properties \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrVI centers in MCM-41 activate CO2, and an 8wt% Cr content maximizes CrVI:CrIII ratios and alkane conversion \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. Excessive Cr content leads to agglomeration and inactive polychromate formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrVI=O species rapidly dehydrogenate alkanes, but CrIII reoxidation lags, causing deactivation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the literature reveal a complex interplay between support materials, reaction conditions, and the performance of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions. The unique properties of different supports, such as SiO2, Al2O3, γ-Al2O3, ZrO2, CeO2, and MCM-41, significantly impact the activity, stability, and selectivity of CrOx catalysts. The role of CO2 partial pressure in enhancing the performance of CrOx/SiO2 while negatively affecting CrOx/γ-Al2O3 highlights the need for a nuanced understanding of the CO2-CrOx-support interactions. Future research should focus on developing catalysts that can maintain high activity and selectivity under varying CO2 partial pressures, while also addressing the challenges of deactivation and side reactions. The exploration of novel support materials that can promote the redox cycle of CrOx without leading to rapid deactivation or sintering is a promising avenue. Additionally, the optimization of Cr content to balance activity and stability, as well as the investigation of the role of Cr speciation in the catalytic performance, are critical areas for future studies. The development of catalysts that can efficiently remove carbon deposits and prevent reoxidation, such as CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2, should be prioritized to enhance overall performance and stability in CO2-ODH reactions.

                    CO2氧化脱氢反应中，铬氧化物催化剂的性能、稳定性和机理行为受到支撑材料和反应条件的显著影响\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。在SiO2和Al2O3支撑下的铬氧化物表现出良好的性能，CO2对Cr-O/SiO2有促进作用，而对Cr-O/Al2O3的丙烯产率有负面影响\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2具有高活性，CrOx/γ-Al2O3倾向于直接脱氢，CrOx/ZrO2促进选择性氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/ZrO2在高温下迅速失活，原因是烧结或非活性铬物种的形成\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2虽然活性较低，但稳定性最高，由于α-Cr2O3的形成，选择性氧化脱氢受到表面再氧化的限制\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2和CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2具有高稳定性，通过CO2去除碳沉积物，由于基本位点，与CrOx/ZrO2相似的副反应涉及非选择性氧化或干重整\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CO2分压提高CrOx/SiO2的活性和C3H6选择性，而CrOx/γ-Al2O3的活性随着CO2的增加而下降\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CO2在γ-Al2O3上的吸附与C3H8的吸附竞争，并改变了CrOx/γ-Al2O3的酸碱性质\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。MCM-41中的CrVI中心激活CO2，8wt%的Cr含量最大化CrVI:CrIII比率和烷烃转化\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。过量的Cr含量导致聚结和非活性多铬酸盐的形成\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CrVI=O物种迅速脱氢烷烃，但CrIII再氧化滞后，导致失活\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 文献中的发现揭示了支撑材料、反应条件和CO2-ODH反应中铬氧化物催化剂性能之间复杂的相互作用。不同支撑材料，如SiO2、Al2O3、γ-Al2O3、ZrO2、CeO2和MCM-41的独特性质，对CrOx催化剂的活性、稳定性和选择性有重大影响。CO2分压在提高CrOx/SiO2性能的同时对CrOx/γ-Al2O3产生负面影响，突出了对CO2-CrOx-支撑相互作用的细微理解的需要。未来的研究应集中在开发能够在变化的CO2分压下保持高活性和选择性的催化剂，同时解决失活和副反应的挑战。探索能够促进CrOx的氧化还原循环而不导致快速失活或烧结的新支撑材料是一个有希望的方向。此外，优化Cr含量以平衡活性和稳定性，以及研究Cr物种在催化性能中的作用，是未来研究的关键领域。优先开发能够有效去除碳沉积物并防止再氧化的催化剂，如CrOx/CeO2和CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2，以增强CO2-ODH反应的整体性能和稳定性，是至关重要的。
