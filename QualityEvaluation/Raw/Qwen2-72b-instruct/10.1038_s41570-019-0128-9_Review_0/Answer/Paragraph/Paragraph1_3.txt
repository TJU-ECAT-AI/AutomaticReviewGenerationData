                    Chromium oxide catalysts supported on SiO2 and Al2O3 exhibit good performance in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) reactions \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. The effect of CO2 on propene yield varies, promoting Cr-O/SiO2 and hindering Cr-O/Al2O3 \[10.1007/s10562-004-3749-6\]. Synthesis gas composition can be controlled by adjusting the CO2:C3H8 ratio \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 show high activity, with CrOx/γ-Al2O3 favoring direct dehydrogenation and CrOx/ZrO2 selective oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/ZrO2 can deactivate rapidly at high temperatures due to sintering or inactive chromium species formation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2, while less active, is the most stable due to the formation of α-Cr2O3, with selective oxidative dehydrogenation limited by surface re-oxidation difficulties \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 have high stability and remove carbon deposits by CO2 due to basic support sites, with side reactions similar to CrOx/ZrO2 \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CO2's promoting effect on SiO2-supported Cr2O3 catalysts enhances propylene yield and suppresses deactivation \[10.1246/cl.1996.973\]. CrOx catalysts show peak activity and selectivity on SiO2, MCM-41, and H-ZSM-5, though excess inactive polychromates favor undesired products \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 are most active but follow distinct pathways; the former uses DDE and RWGS, prone to deactivation by coke, while the latter operates in a single step, deactivating through sintering or Cr speciation changes \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrOx/CeO2 is less active but more stable due to coke consumption \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrOx/SiO2's activity and C3H6 selectivity rise with CO2 partial pressure, unlike CrOx/γ-Al2O3 \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CO2 adsorption on γ-Al2O3 competes with C3H8 adsorption and affects CrOx/γ-Al2O3's acid-base properties \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrVI centers in MCM-41 activate CO2, and an 8wt% Cr content maximizes CrVI:CrIII ratios and alkane conversion \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. Excessive Cr content leads to agglomeration and inactive polychromate species \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]. CrVI=O species rapidly dehydrogenate alkanes, but CrIII sites' inactivity and coking eventually deactivate even highly dispersed CrOx catalysts \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature reveals that support materials significantly influence the performance, stability, and mechanistic behavior of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions. SiO2 and Al2O3 supports show contrasting effects on propene yield, with CO2 promoting Cr-O/SiO2 and hindering Cr-O/Al2O3. The synthesis gas composition can be controlled by adjusting the CO2:C3H8 ratio, offering a means to tailor the product distribution. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 exhibit high activity, with distinct pathways and stability issues. CrOx/CeO2, while less active, is the most stable due to the formation of α-Cr2O3 and the ability to remove carbon deposits by CO2. Future research might focus on developing catalysts with enhanced stability and selectivity, exploring the role of support materials in promoting specific mechanistic pathways, and optimizing reaction conditions to maximize the beneficial effects of CO2. The challenge lies in balancing high conversion and selectivity while maintaining stability and minimizing side reactions. Innovations in catalyst design, such as the use of mixed oxides and the incorporation of novel support materials, could lead to improved performance in CO2-ODH reactions.

                    二氧化硅和氧化铝支持的铬氧化物催化剂在CO2氧化脱氢(CO2-ODH)反应中表现出良好的性能\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CO2对丙烯产量的影响因催化剂而异，促进Cr-O/二氧化硅，但阻碍Cr-O/氧化铝\[10.1007/s10562-004-3749-6\]。通过调整CO2:C3H8比率可以控制合成气组成\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2表现出高活性，CrOx/γ-Al2O3倾向于直接脱氢，而CrOx/ZrO2促进选择性氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/ZrO2在高温下可能迅速失活，由于烧结或非活性铬物种的形成\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2虽然活性较低，但最稳定，由于形成α-Cr2O3，选择性氧化脱氢受到表面再氧化的限制\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2和CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2具有高稳定性，由于CO2去除碳沉积物，与CrOx/ZrO2的副反应相似\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CO2对二氧化硅支持的Cr2O3催化剂的促进作用增强了丙烯产量并抑制了失活\[10.1246/cl.1996.973\]。CrOx催化剂在二氧化硅、MCM-41和H-ZSM-5上表现出峰值活性和选择性，尽管过量的非活性多铬酸盐有利于不希望的产品\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2是最活跃的，但遵循不同的途径；前者使用DDE和RWGS，容易因焦炭而失活，而后者在一步中运行，通过烧结或Cr物种变化而失活\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CrOx/CeO2活性较低，但更稳定，由于焦炭消耗\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CrOx/二氧化硅的活性和C3H6选择性随着CO2分压的升高而增加，与CrOx/γ-Al2O3不同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CO2在γ-Al2O3上的吸附与C3H8吸附竞争，影响CrOx/γ-Al2O3的酸碱性质\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。MCM-41中的CrVI中心激活CO2，8wt%的Cr含量最大化CrVI:CrIII比率和烷烃转化\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。过量的Cr含量导致聚集和非活性多铬酸盐物种\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。CrVI=O物种迅速脱氢烷烃，但CrIII位点的不活动和焦化最终使即使高度分散的CrOx催化剂失活\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_0\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 文献揭示，支持材料对铬氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能、稳定性和机制行为有重大影响。二氧化硅和氧化铝支持显示出对丙烯产量的对比影响，CO2促进Cr-O/二氧化硅，但阻碍Cr-O/氧化铝。通过调整CO2:C3H8比率可以控制合成气组成，为定制产品分布提供了一种手段。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2表现出高活性，具有不同的途径和稳定性问题。CrOx/CeO2虽然活性较低，但最稳定，由于形成α-Cr2O3和CO2去除碳沉积物的能力。未来的研究可能集中在开发具有增强稳定性和选择性的催化剂，探索支持材料在促进特定机制途径中的作用，以及优化反应条件以最大化CO2的有益效果。挑战在于在保持稳定性和最小化副反应的同时平衡高转化率和选择性。催化剂设计的创新，如使用混合氧化物和引入新型支持材料，可能导致CO2-ODH反应中性能的改善。
