            <English> 
                    The performance, stability, and mechanistic behavior of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) reactions are significantly influenced by support materials and reaction conditions \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. Chromium oxide catalysts supported on SiO2 and Al2O3 exhibit good performance in CO2-ODH reactions, with CO2 promoting propene yield on Cr-O/SiO2 but negatively affecting Cr-O/Al2O3 \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. By adjusting the CO2:C3H8 ratio, the synthesis gas composition can be controlled, presenting a key challenge in achieving high conversion and selectivity while maintaining stability and minimizing side reactions \[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]. CrOx/γ-Al2O3 and CrOx/ZrO2 are highly active, with CrOx/γ-Al2O3 favoring direct dehydrogenation of ethane and CrOx/ZrO2 promoting selective oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/ZrO2 can deactivate rapidly at high temperatures due to sintering or the formation of inactive chromium species \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. CrOx/CeO2, while less active, is the most stable due to the formation of α-Cr2O3, which is inactive in direct dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. Its stability and low activity for ethylene formation contribute to the removal of carbon deposits by CO2 \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]. The side reactions for CrOx/CeO2 and CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2 are similar to CrOx/ZrO2, involving non-selective oxidation or dry-reforming reactions \[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature reveals that support materials and reaction conditions play a pivotal role in the performance of chromium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions. The unique properties of SiO2 and Al2O3, as well as the distinct behaviors of CrOx/γ-Al2O3, CrOx/ZrO2, and CrOx/CeO2, highlight the need for tailored catalyst design to optimize activity, selectivity, and stability. Future research efforts should focus on developing novel support materials that can enhance the dispersion of chromium oxide and improve its reactivity with CO2, while also addressing the challenges of deactivation and side reactions. Exploring the synergistic effects of mixed oxides, such as CeO2 and ZrO2, could lead to the development of more stable and active catalysts. Additionally, the investigation of reaction conditions, including CO2 partial pressure and the CO2:C3H8 ratio, is crucial for controlling the synthesis gas composition and achieving high conversion and selectivity. The optimization of these parameters could pave the way for more efficient and sustainable CO2-ODH processes.

                    在CO2氧化脱氢(CO2-ODH)反应中，铬氧化物催化剂的性能、稳定性和机理行为受到支撑材料和反应条件的显著影响\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。在SiO2和Al2O3支撑的铬氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中表现出良好的性能，CO2促进Cr-O/SiO2的丙烯产量，但对Cr-O/Al2O3产生负面影响\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。通过调整CO2:C3H8比例，可以控制合成气的组成，实现高转化率和选择性，同时保持稳定性和最小化副反应是关键挑战\[10.2478/v10026-012-0106-1\]。CrOx/γ-Al2O3和CrOx/ZrO2具有高活性，CrOx/γ-Al2O3有利于直接脱氢，CrOx/ZrO2促进选择性氧化脱氢\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/ZrO2在高温下可能迅速失活，原因是烧结或形成不活性铬物种\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2虽然活性较低，但最稳定，由于形成α-Cr2O3，它在直接脱氢中不活跃\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。其稳定性和低乙烯形成活性有助于CO2去除碳沉积物\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。CrOx/CeO2和CrOx/CexZr(1-x)O2的副反应与CrOx/ZrO2类似，涉及非选择性氧化或干重整反应\[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.04.047\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 文献表明，支撑材料和反应条件在CO2-ODH反应中铬氧化物催化剂的性能中起着关键作用。SiO2和Al2O3的独特性质，以及CrOx/γ-Al2O3、CrOx/ZrO2和CrOx/CeO2的不同行为，强调了为优化活性、选择性和稳定性而定制催化剂设计的必要性。未来的研究工作应集中在开发新型支撑材料上，这些材料可以增强铬氧化物的分散性，提高其与CO2的反应性，同时解决失活和副反应的挑战。探索CeO2和ZrO2等混合氧化物的协同效应，可能导致开发出更稳定和更活性的催化剂。此外，研究反应条件，包括CO2分压和CO2:C3H8比例，对于控制合成气组成和实现高转化率和选择性至关重要。这些参数的优化可能为更高效和可持续的CO2-ODH过程铺平道路。
