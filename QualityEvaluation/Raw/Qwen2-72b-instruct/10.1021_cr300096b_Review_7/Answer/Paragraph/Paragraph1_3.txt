                    Cyclohexene, due to its sensitivity to the surface termination of Pt or Ni in bimetallic catalysts \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\], serves as an ideal probe molecule for studying hydrogenation reactions. Its multiple reaction pathways, particularly the disproportionation pathway, provide insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]. The unique hydrogenation pathway observed on the PtNiPt(1 1 1) subsurface structure, which is not present on either of the parent metal surfaces \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\], demonstrates the enhanced catalytic properties of bimetallic systems compared to monometallic materials. Cyclohexene's hydrogenation activity and selectivity can provide insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation reactions in industrial processes \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. The study of cyclohexene hydrogenation on bimetallic surfaces can guide the rational design of catalytic materials for industrial hydrogenation processes \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. The unique hydrogenation pathway observed on the PtNiPt(1 1 1) subsurface structure, which results from the weaker bonding of atomic hydrogen and cyclohexene, provides insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity, guiding the rational design of novel bimetallic catalytic materials for hydrogenation reactions in industrial processes \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. The hydrogenation of cyclohexene was used as a probe reaction to demonstrate the enhanced hydrogenation activity toward the CC bond on the bimetallic catalysts, as predicted from the surface science studies \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.08.016\]. The PtNi bimetallic catalysts showed higher activity for cyclohexene hydrogenation than PtCo catalysts, consistent with predictions from surface science studies \[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.08.016\].

                    由于其对Pt或Ni在双金属催化剂表面终止的敏感性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\],环己烯作为研究氢化反应的理想探针分子。其多种反应途径,特别是歧化途径,提供了对双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性影响的见解\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]。在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的独特氢化途径,在任一母金属表面上均不存在\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\],展示了双金属系统与单金属材料相比的增强催化性能。环己烯的氢化活性和选择性可以提供对双金属催化剂在工业过程中氢化反应影响的见解\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。在双金属表面上研究环己烯氢化可以指导工业氢化过程催化材料的合理设计\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的独特氢化途径,源自原子氢和环己烯的较弱结合,提供了对双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性影响的见解,指导了工业过程中氢化反应新型双金属催化材料的合理设计\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。环己烯的氢化被用作探针反应,以证明双金属催化剂上CC键的增强氢化活性,这与表面科学研究的预测一致\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.08.016\]。PtNi双金属催化剂对环己烯氢化的活性高于PtCo催化剂,与表面科学研究的预测一致\[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.08.016\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 利用环己烯作为研究氢化反应的探针分子,为双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性的影响提供了宝贵的见解。在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的独特氢化途径,在任一母金属表面上均不存在,展示了双金属系统与单金属材料相比的增强催化性能。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发工业过程中氢化反应的新型双金属催化材料,由从在双金属表面上研究环己烯氢化获得的见解指导。工业氢化过程催化材料的合理设计可以由在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的独特氢化途径指导,该途径源自原子氢和环己烯的较弱结合。在双金属催化剂上通过环己烯氢化证明的CC键增强氢化活性,可以指导更高效和选择性催化剂的开发,用于工业应用。
