                    The utilization of cyclohexene as an ideal probe molecule for investigating hydrogenation reactions, with emphasis on its industrial relevance, multiple reaction pathways, and its role in elucidating the effects of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity, is well-established \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]. Cyclohexene's sensitivity to the surface termination of Pt or Ni in bimetallic catalysts, particularly through the disproportionation pathway, provides insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]. Its unique hydrogenation pathway observed on the PtNiPt(1 1 1) subsurface structure, which is not present on either of the parent metal surfaces, reveals the enhanced catalytic properties of bimetallic systems compared to monometallic materials \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. The weaker bonding of atomic hydrogen and cyclohexene on this surface leads to a low-temperature hydrogenation pathway, demonstrating the enhanced catalytic properties of bimetallic systems \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. Comparative studies of cyclohexene's multiple reaction pathways, including decomposition, dehydrogenation, disproportionation (self-hydrogenation), and hydrogenation, provide insights into how the formation of bimetallic bonds affects hydrogenation activity and selectivity in industrial processes \[10.1021/cr300096b_7\]. The unique activity of the PtNiPt(111) subsurface monolayer toward cyclohexene, confirmed by HREELS and DFT calculations, suggests that the subsurface structure is more effective in the hydrogenation of cyclohexene than the surface structure \[10.1016/j.surfrep.2008.02.001\]. The correlation of self-hydrogenation activity with the surface d-band center of MBS structures is further confirmed by experimental results, providing insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity in industrial processes \[10.1016/j.surfrep.2008.02.001\].

                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The study of cyclohexene hydrogenation on bimetallic surfaces can guide the rational design of catalytic materials for industrial hydrogenation processes. The unique hydrogenation pathway observed on the PtNiPt(1 1 1) subsurface structure, which results from the weaker bonding of atomic hydrogen and cyclohexene, provides insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity, guiding the rational design of novel bimetallic catalytic materials for hydrogenation reactions in industrial processes \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of more efficient and selective catalysts for industrial applications by exploring the distinct reaction pathways of cyclohexene on different catalysts, revealing how bimetallic surfaces modify the catalytic properties, leading to enhanced or altered hydrogenation activity and selectivity \[10.1016/j.susc.2004.03.032\]. The different reaction pathways of cyclohexene allow researchers to understand the catalytic hydrogenation and dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons, providing insights into the influence of bimetallic catalysts on hydrogenation activity and selectivity in industrial processes \[10.1016/j.susc.2004.03.032\].

                    基于\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]参考文献，利用环己烯作为研究氢化反应的理想探针分子，强调其工业相关性，多种反应途径，以及其在阐明双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性影响方面的作用。环己烯对双金属催化剂中Pt或Ni表面终止的敏感性，特别是通过歧化途径，提供了双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性影响的见解\[10.1016/j.apcata.2008.01.024\]。在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的环己烯的独特氢化途径，不在任一金属表面存在，揭示了双金属系统与单金属材料相比的增强催化性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。原子氢和环己烯在该表面上的较弱结合导致低温氢化途径，展示了双金属系统的增强催化性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。环己烯的多种反应途径，包括分解、脱氢、歧化（自氢化）和氢化，的比较研究提供了双金属键的形成如何影响工业过程中氢化活性和选择性的见解\[10.1021/cr300096b_7\]。PtNiPt(111)亚表面单层对环己烯的独特活性，通过HREELS和DFT计算确认，表明亚表面结构在环己烯的氢化中比表面结构更有效\[10.1016/j.surfrep.2008.02.001\]。自氢化活性与MBS结构的表面d带中心值之间的相关性通过实验结果进一步确认，提供了双金属催化剂对工业过程中氢化活性和选择性影响的见解\[10.1016/j.surfrep.2008.02.001\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 研究环己烯在双金属表面上的氢化可以指导工业氢化过程催化材料的合理设计。在PtNiPt(1 1 1)亚表面结构上观察到的独特氢化途径，源自原子氢和环己烯的较弱结合，提供了双金属催化剂对氢化活性和选择性影响的见解，指导了工业过程中氢化反应新型双金属催化材料的合理设计\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.03.007\]。未来的研究努力可能会集中在通过探索环己烯在不同催化剂上的独特反应途径，揭示双金属表面如何修改催化性能，导致增强或改变的氢化活性和选择性，来开发更高效和选择性的工业应用催化剂\[10.1016/j.susc.2004.03.032\]。环己烯的不同反应途径使研究人员能够理解烃类的催化氢化和脱氢，提供了双金属催化剂对工业过程中氢化活性和选择性影响的见解\[10.1016/j.susc.2004.03.032\]。
