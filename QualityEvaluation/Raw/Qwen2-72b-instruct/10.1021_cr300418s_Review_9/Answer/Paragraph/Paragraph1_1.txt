                    The preparation, properties, and catalytic applications of doped nickel oxide materials in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, with emphasis on the effects of dopants and vacancies on electronic structure, theoretical modeling challenges, and process optimization considerations, have been extensively studied \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\], \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Niobium-doped nickel oxide catalysts, prepared through various methods, exhibit enhanced catalytic performance in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, such as ethane to ethylene, due to altered electronic structures and oxygen species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. The presence of a thin niobium-rich mixed oxide layer inhibits NiO reducibility, affecting redox properties and leading to changes in activity and selectivity for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Low-temperature treatment of pure NiO creates a non-stoichiometric phase with cationic deficiency, and upon Nb doping, two phases emerge: a NiO phase with Nb cations forming a NiNb solid solution, and a distorted Nb-rich phase, precursor to NiNb2O6 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]. The interaction of niobium ions with structural defects in NiO enhances ethylene selectivity in ethane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]. Niobium doping reduces activation energy and the exchange rate of labeled oxygen, eliminating electrophilic active oxygen species responsible for ethane's total oxidation to CO2 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Theoretical modeling and process optimization challenges include understanding complex reaction mechanisms, predicting dopant and vacancy effects on electronic structure and reactivity, optimizing conditions for yield and selectivity, and ensuring safe operation at high temperatures and pressures \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in the preparation and application of doped nickel oxide catalysts for oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, particularly in ethane to ethylene conversion, have opened new avenues for research and development. The intricate relationship between dopant concentrations, vacancy formation, and electronic structure has been elucidated, highlighting the critical role of niobium in enhancing catalytic performance \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel preparation methods that can further optimize the electronic structure and catalytic properties of these materials. Additionally, there is a need for advanced theoretical modeling techniques that can accurately predict the effects of dopants and vacancies on the electronic structure and reactivity of nickel oxide catalysts, addressing the challenges of process optimization and safety \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]. The exploration of other dopants and their synergistic effects with niobium could also be a promising direction, potentially leading to the discovery of more efficient and selective catalysts for oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

                    在氧化脱氢反应中，特别是乙烷转化为乙烯，掺杂镍氧化物材料的制备、性质和催化应用，特别是掺杂剂和空位对电子结构的影响、理论建模挑战和过程优化考虑，已经得到了广泛研究\[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\], \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\], \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。铌掺杂的镍氧化物催化剂，通过各种方法制备，由于电子结构和氧物种的改变，在氧化脱氢反应中，如乙烷转化为乙烯，表现出增强的催化性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。铌富集的混合氧化层的存在抑制了NiO的还原性，影响了氧化还原性质，导致乙烷氧化脱氢活性和选择性的变化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。低温处理纯NiO产生具有阳离子缺陷的非化学计量相，铌掺杂后，出现两个相：一个NiO相，Nb离子形成NiNb固溶体，以及一个扭曲的Nb富集相，是NiNb2O6的前体\[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]。铌离子与NiO结构缺陷的相互作用增强了乙烷氧化脱氢中乙烯的选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]。铌掺杂降低了活化能和标记氧的交换率，消除了对乙烷完全氧化为CO2负责的亲电活性氧物种\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。理论建模和过程优化的挑战包括理解复杂的反应机制，预测掺杂剂和空位对电子结构和反应性的影响，优化产量和选择性的条件，以及在高温和高压下确保安全操作\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在乙烷转化为乙烯的氧化脱氢反应中，掺杂镍氧化物催化剂的制备和应用的进展为研究和开发开辟了新的途径。掺杂浓度、空位形成和电子结构之间的复杂关系已经阐明，突出了铌在增强催化性能中的关键作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的制备方法，可以进一步优化这些材料的电子结构和催化性质。此外，需要先进的理论建模技术，可以准确预测掺杂剂和空位对镍氧化物催化剂的电子结构和反应性的影响，解决过程优化和安全的挑战\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.002\]。探索其他掺杂剂及其与铌的协同效应也可能是一个有希望的方向，可能发现更有效和选择性的氧化脱氢反应催化剂。
