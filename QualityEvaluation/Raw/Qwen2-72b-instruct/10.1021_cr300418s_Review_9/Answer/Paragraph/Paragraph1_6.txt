                    The preparation, properties, and catalytic applications of doped nickel oxide materials in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, particularly ethane to ethylene, are significantly influenced by dopant concentrations and vacancy formation. Niobium-doped nickel oxide catalysts, such as NiNbO with varying Nb contents, have been extensively studied for ethane oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Optimal ethane conversion (33%) and selectivity to ethylene (78%) are achieved at 15% Nb content, with efficiency linked to surface interaction between NiO and amorphous Nb2O5, reducing NiO oxygen over-stoichiometry \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. Oxalate ions, present due to the niobium precursor, influence catalytic properties, and above 15% Nb, the inactive NiNb2O6 phase forms, leading to deactivation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]. The preparation method, involving citric acid to form a nickel-rich NbNi gel, impacts the electronic structure and catalytic performance \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Niobium-rich mixed oxide layers inhibit NiO reducibility, affecting redox properties, and ethane oxidative dehydrogenation activity and selectivity correlate with NiO crystallite size and Nb-rich phase coverage \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. High Nb loadings cause significant deactivation, whereas 34 at.% Nb yields high activity, selectivity, and stability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]. Low-temperature treatment of pure NiO creates a non-stoichiometric phase with cationic deficiency, and upon Nb doping, two phases emerge: a NiO phase with Nb cations forming a NiNb solid solution, and a distorted Nb-rich phase, precursor to NiNb2O6 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]. Niobium reduces structural defects, correlating with high ethylene selectivity in ethane ODH, as vacancies form strong oxidizing oxygen species, leading to ethane's total oxidation to CO2 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]. Dopant valence in nickel oxide catalysts modifies lattice structure, impacting oxygen species and ethane conversion to ethylene \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. Lower valence dopants increase non-stoichiometric oxygen, favoring ethylene production, while higher valence dopants enhance initial selectivity to the desired product \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]. Dopant concentrations and preparation methods significantly affect electronic structure and catalytic performance in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]. Doping with Fe, Zr, V, and Ga increases surface area, enhancing catalytic activity \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]. Low-valence dopants facilitate oxide reduction, improving performance, and Zr-doped NiO exhibits improved selectivity to ethylene \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]. Niobium-doped nickel oxide catalysts show reduced activation energy and exchange rate of labeled oxygen, eliminating electrophilic active oxygen species that cause total ethane oxidation to CO2 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Niobium fills cationic vacancies, decreasing active sites for oxygen dissociation, favoring diffusion and promoting nucleophilic oxygen species selective for ethylene production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Theoretical modeling challenges include understanding complex reaction mechanisms and dopant effects on electronic structure and reactivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]. Process optimization must balance efficiency, selectivity, and safety at high temperatures and pressures \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\].

The integration of advanced characterization techniques, such as in situ spectroscopy and microscopy, could provide deeper insights into the dynamic changes in the catalyst structure and electronic properties under reaction conditions, facilitating the development of more robust and efficient catalytic systems.

                    镍氧化物材料的掺杂、性质和催化应用在氧化脱氢反应中，特别是乙烷到乙烯的转化，受到掺杂浓度和空位形成的影响。铌掺杂的镍氧化物催化剂，如NiNbO，具有不同的铌含量，已被广泛研究用于乙烷氧化脱氢 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。在15%的铌含量下，实现了最佳的乙烷转化率（33%）和乙烯选择性（78%），效率与NiO和无定形Nb2O5之间的表面相互作用有关，减少了NiO的过量氧 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。由于铌前体的存在，草酸盐离子影响催化性能，超过15%的铌，不活跃的NiNb2O6相形成，导致失活 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2010.10.004\]。制备方法，涉及柠檬酸形成镍丰富的NbNi凝胶，影响电子结构和催化性能 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。铌丰富的混合氧化物层抑制NiO的还原性，影响氧化还原性质，乙烷氧化脱氢的活性和选择性与NiO晶粒大小和铌丰富相的覆盖相关 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。高铌负载导致显著的失活，而34 at.%的铌产生高活性、选择性和稳定性 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2011.10.005\]。纯NiO的低温处理产生具有阳离子缺陷的非化学计量相，铌掺杂后，出现两个相：一个NiO相，Nb阳离子形成NiNb固溶体，和一个扭曲的铌丰富相，是NiNb2O6的前体 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]。铌减少了结构缺陷，与乙烷ODH中高乙烯选择性相关，空位形成强氧化氧物种，导致乙烷完全氧化为CO2 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030\]。镍氧化物催化剂中的掺杂价态改变了晶格结构，影响氧物种和乙烷转化为乙烯 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。低价态掺杂剂增加了非化学计量氧，有利于乙烯生产，而高价态掺杂剂增强了对所需产品的初始选择性 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004\]。掺杂浓度和制备方法对氧化脱氢反应中的电子结构和催化性能有显著影响 \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]。用Fe、Zr、V和Ga掺杂增加了表面积，提高了催化活性 \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]。低价态掺杂剂促进了氧化物还原，提高了性能，Zr掺杂的NiO表现出对乙烯的改进选择性 \[10.1021/cr300418s_9\]。铌掺杂的镍氧化物催化剂显示出降低的活化能和标记氧的交换率，消除了导致乙烷完全氧化为CO2的亲电活性氧物种 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。铌填充了阳离子空位，减少了氧解离的活性位点，有利于扩散，促进了对乙烯生产有选择性的亲核氧物种 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。理论建模的挑战包括理解复杂的反应机制和掺杂剂对电子结构和反应性的影响 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。过程优化必须在高温和高压下平衡效率、选择性和安全性 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.11.003\]。
