1.The preparation methods, dopant concentrations, and vacancy formation in nickel oxide-based catalysts significantly influence their electronic structure and catalytic performance in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions. The study found that the incorporation of dopants with different valences affects the non-stoichiometry of nickel oxides, leading to changes in the electrophilic oxygen species responsible for the total oxidation of ethane to CO2 on pure NiO. Lower valence dopants increase the non-stoichiometric oxygen, while higher valence dopants reduce it, enhancing the selective conversion to ethylene. The lability of the oxygen species is also affected, with high valence dopants resulting in more strongly bonded oxygen species. Theoretical modeling, process optimization, and balancing efficiency, selectivity, and safety in these catalytic systems remain key challenges.

1.镍氧化物基催化剂的制备方法、掺杂浓度和空位形成对其电子结构和氧化脱氢反应(如乙基苯转化为苯乙烯和乙烷转化为乙烯)的催化性能有重大影响。研究发现,不同价态的掺杂剂影响镍氧化物的非化学计量性,导致对乙烷在纯NiO上完全氧化为CO2负责的亲电氧物种发生变化。低价掺杂剂增加非化学计量氧,而高价掺杂剂减少非化学计量氧,增强选择性转化为乙烯。氧物种的活性也受到影响,高价掺杂剂导致氧物种更牢固地结合。在这些催化体系中,理论建模、过程优化以及效率、选择性和安全性之间的平衡仍然是主要挑战。
