                    The interplay between gallium species, zeolite composition, and framework aluminum pairs in Ga-modified zeolite catalysts significantly influences the formation and nature of active gallium species, including gallium hydrides, and reveals structure-activity correlations in propane dehydrogenation reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]. At lower Si/Al ratios, paired Brnsted acid sites (BAS) are more prevalent, leading to the preferential formation of Ga+H+ pair sites through ion-exchange. These Ga+H+ pair sites are capable of forming gallium hydrides (GaHx) upon reduction, which are crucial for the catalytic activity in propane dehydrogenation reactions. In contrast, at higher Si/Al ratios, BAS are mostly isolated, resulting in the formation of isolated Ga+ species that do not form stable GaHx species at high temperatures \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]. The ability to form GaHx species is directly related to the presence of paired BAS, which are more abundant at lower Si/Al ratios. These relationships reveal that the structure of the catalyst, particularly the distribution and density of BAS, plays a critical role in determining the nature and reactivity of the active gallium species \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]. The presence of Ga+H+ pair sites versus isolated Ga+ species could have significant implications for the catalyst's performance in propane dehydrogenation reactions, as they may exhibit different catalytic properties \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\].

                    Ga改性沸石催化剂中镓物种、沸石组成和骨架铝对之间的相互作用对活性镓物种（包括镓氢化物）的形成和性质有显著影响，揭示了丙烷脱氢反应中的结构-活性相关性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。在较低的Si/Al比下，配对的Brnsted酸位点（BAS）更为普遍，通过离子交换优先形成Ga+H+对位点。这些Ga+H+对位点在还原后能够形成镓氢化物（GaHx），对丙烷脱氢反应的催化活性至关重要。相比之下，在较高的Si/Al比下，BAS大多被隔离，导致形成孤立的Ga+物种，这些物种在高温下不能形成稳定的GaHx物种\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。形成GaHx物种的能力直接与配对BAS的存在相关，而配对BAS在较低的Si/Al比下更为丰富。这些关系揭示了催化剂的结构，特别是BAS的分布和密度，在确定活性镓物种的性质和反应性方面起着关键作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。Ga+H+对位点与孤立Ga+物种的存在可能对丙烷脱氢反应中催化剂的性能有重大影响，因为它们可能表现出不同的催化性质\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.11.004\]。
