          The thermodynamic stability of specific bimetallic structures, particularly in Pt-3d systems with subsurface 3d atoms, contributes to the strong correlation between model surfaces and supported catalysts in hydrogenation reactions. This is evidenced by the unique low-temperature hydrogenation pathway displayed by the subsurface configuration of the Ni/Pt(111) monolayer bimetallic system, PtNiPt(111), which is not present on either Pt(111) or Ni(111). The origin of this pathway is correlated to the low binding energy of cyclohexene on PtNiPt(111), which is weaker than on either of the parent metal surfaces. The electronic and catalytic properties of bimetallic surfaces depend on the admetal location, with the structure having admetal on the top-most layer, designated as 3d-Pt(111), moving the d-band center closer to the Fermi level, while the structure with admetal in the subsurface region, designated as Pt-3d-Pt(111), shifts the d-band center away from the Fermi level. The d-band center correlates well with adsorbate binding energy and thus catalytic and electrocatalytic activity. The surface structure binds adsorbates strongly and is effective for decomposition reactions, while the subsurface structure has a weak adsorbate binding energy and is active for hydrogenation. The stability of metal overlayers on different substrates is also discussed, with carbides being a better diffusion barrier for admetals than Pt. The similar electronic and chemical properties of Pt and WC suggest that WC can potentially replace Pt in bimetallic systems, producing alternative bimetallic catalysts with lower cost and higher thermal stability. Understanding the electronic and catalytic properties of bimetallic surfaces and their stability on different substrates can guide the design of more effective catalysts.

          特定双金属结构的热力学稳定性，特别是在具有亚表面3d原子的Pt-3d系统中，对模型表面和支撑催化剂在氢化反应中的强相关性有贡献。这在Ni/Pt(111)单层双金属系统的亚表面配置，即PtNiPt(111)，显示的独特低温氢化途径中得到了证明，这种途径在Pt(111)或Ni(111)上并不存在。这种途径的起源与PtNiPt(111)上环己烯的低结合能有关，这种结合能比任一母金属表面的结合能都弱。双金属表面的电子和催化性质取决于吸附金属的位置，顶部层具有吸附金属的结构，标记为3d-Pt(111)，将d带中心移近费米能级，而具有吸附金属在亚表面区域的结构，标记为Pt-3d-Pt(111)，将d带中心移离费米能级。d带中心与吸附物结合能有很好的相关性，因此与催化和电催化活性相关。表面结构强烈结合吸附物，对分解反应有效，而亚表面结构具有弱的吸附物结合能，对氢化有效。文献还讨论了不同基底上金属覆盖层的稳定性，与Pt相比，碳化物是吸附金属更好的扩散屏障。Pt和WC的相似电子和化学性质表明WC可以潜在地替代Pt中的双金属系统，产生成本更低、热稳定性更高的替代双金属催化剂。理解双金属表面的电子和催化性质及其在不同基底上的稳定性可以指导更有效催化剂的设计。
