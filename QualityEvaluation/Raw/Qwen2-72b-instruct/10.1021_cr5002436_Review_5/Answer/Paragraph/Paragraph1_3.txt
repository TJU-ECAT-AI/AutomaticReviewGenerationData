                The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism, a cornerstone in catalytic dehydrogenation reactions, involves four principal steps: adsorption, hydrogen dissociation, hydrogen migration, and desorption \[10.1021/jp909163z\]. The identity of the rate-limiting step remains a subject of debate due to the mechanism's complexity and the influence of various factors \[10.1021/jp909163z\]. Despite nearly a century of study, its significance endures, as it underpins hydrocarbon dehydrogenation, a critical process in the petrochemical industry \[10.1021/jp909163z\].

                The mechanism's application to ethane dehydrogenation over Cr/Al2O3 exemplifies its four steps: end-on, dissociative adsorption of ethane on a Cr3+O active site, followed by ethene and hydrogen desorption \[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\]. Ethane adsorption is proposed as the rate-determining step, supported by transient kinetic data and the absence of isotopic scrambling, indicating no involvement of coke deposits \[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\]. The ongoing debate over the rate-limiting step contributes to the mechanism's scientific interest and controversy \[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\].

                Computational studies using DFT highlight the Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for dehydrogenation reactions, identifying the cleavage of the first CH bond upon alkane adsorption as the rate-determining step \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]. Selectivity towards alkene production is attributed to higher barriers for third hydrogen atom cleavage and low alkene binding energies \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]. Adsorbed H atoms facilitate alkene desorption over further dehydrogenation \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]. Substituting Sn in Pt clusters enhances catalyst activity by lowering the activation barrier for the rate-limiting step \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]. Understanding factors influencing alkene versus coke precursor formation remains a topic of scientific interest and controversy \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\].

                该机制在Cr/Al2O3上对乙烷脱氢的应用展示了四个步骤：在Cr3+O活性位点上端到端的解离吸附，随后是乙烯和氢的解吸\[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\]。乙烷吸附被提议为速率决定步骤，这一观点得到了瞬态动力学数据的支持，并且由于没有同位素重排，表明没有焦炭沉积的参与\[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\]。关于速率控制步骤的持续争论增加了机制的科学兴趣和争议\[10.1007/s10562-005-6521-7\]。

                使用DFT的计算研究表明了Horiuti-Polanyi机制在脱氢反应中的作用，确定了烷烃吸附时第一个CH键的裂解是速率决定步骤\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。对第三氢原子裂解的高障碍和低烯烃结合能导致了对烯烃生产的偏好\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。吸附的H原子促进了烯烃的解吸，而不是进一步的脱氢\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。在Pt簇中用Sn取代增强了催化剂的活性，通过降低速率决定步骤的活化能\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。理解影响烯烃与焦炭前体形成因素仍然是科学兴趣和争议的话题\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。

                综合理解与未来展望：Horiuti-Polanyi机制在催化脱氢反应中的持续相关性强调了需要先进的计算方法和实验技术来确定速率控制步骤的身份。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发具有定制活性位点的催化剂，以增强脱氢反应的选择性和效率。将机器学习算法与DFT计算相结合，可能为催化剂结构与反应动力学之间的复杂相互作用提供新的见解。此外，探索表面中间体在CC键断裂中的作用可能为设计优化烯烃生产和焦炭前体形成平衡的催化剂开辟途径，可能彻底改变石化过程。
