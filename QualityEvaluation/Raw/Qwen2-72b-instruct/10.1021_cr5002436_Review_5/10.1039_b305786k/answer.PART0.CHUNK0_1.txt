1.The literature does not provide information on the Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for catalytic dehydrogenation reactions or the ongoing debate regarding which step is considered rate-limiting. The focus of the literature is on the development of a novel experimental set-up for characterizing supported metal oxide catalysts under working conditions, combining product analysis using on-line mass spectrometry with catalyst characterization by time-resolved operando UV-VIS and Raman spectroscopy. The potentials of this approach are illustrated by the propane dehydrogenation over a chromium oxide on alumina catalyst. The study provides operando spectroscopic information on the short- and long-term deactivation process of the catalyst.
