        <English> 
                Fe oxide-based catalysts have emerged as promising candidates for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\], given their established role in catalyzing the oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons such as ethylbenzene and isobutane. Among the various catalysts tested, Fe2O3 supported on activated carbon (Fe2O3/AC) has demonstrated superior performance in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield, with a C3H6 selectivity comparable to that of bare Fe2O3 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC, as evidenced by H2-TPR results, is a critical factor contributing to its high catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. This is in stark contrast to Fe2O3 supported on γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts, where the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support significantly hinders reducibility \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The redox cycle of Fe oxide, involving the oxidation of C3H8 to C3H6 on lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4 and the subsequent reoxidation of reduced Fe oxide by CO2, is proposed to play a pivotal role in CO2-ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings underscore the critical role of support materials in influencing the reducibility and, by extension, the catalytic activity of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP. The superior performance of Fe2O3/AC highlights the importance of exploring novel support materials that can further enhance the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide catalysts. Future research efforts might focus on developing advanced support materials with tailored properties to optimize the redox cycle of Fe oxide, thereby improving the efficiency and selectivity of CO2-ODHP. Additionally, the investigation of alternative redox-active metals and their interaction with support materials could open new avenues for enhancing the catalytic performance in oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

                基于\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]的参考文献，Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中展现出潜力，因为它们能够催化诸如乙苯和异丁烷等烃类的氧化脱氢。在测试的催化剂中，Fe2O3/AC（Fe2O3负载于活性炭上）在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面表现出色，C3H6选择性与裸Fe2O3相当\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。H2-TPR结果表明，Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性是其高催化性能的关键因素\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。这与Fe2O3负载于γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂形成鲜明对比，后者由于Fe氧化物与载体之间的强相互作用，还原性受到抑制\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，包括C3H8在Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧上的氧化以及CO2对还原Fe氧化物的再氧化，被提议在CO2-ODHP中发挥核心作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 研究结果强调了载体材料在影响Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和催化活性中的关键作用，特别是在CO2-ODHP中。Fe2O3/AC的卓越性能突显了探索新型载体材料以进一步增强Fe氧化物催化剂的还原性和稳定性的必要性。未来的研究工作可能集中于开发具有定制特性的先进载体材料，以优化Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，从而提高CO2-ODHP的效率和选择性。此外，研究替代的氧化还原活性金属及其与载体材料的相互作用，可能开辟增强氧化脱氢反应催化性能的新途径。
