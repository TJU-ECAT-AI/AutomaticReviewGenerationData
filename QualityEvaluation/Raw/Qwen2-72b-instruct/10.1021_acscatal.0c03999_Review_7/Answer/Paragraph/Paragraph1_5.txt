        <English> 
                Fe oxide-based catalysts have emerged as potential candidates for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) due to their ability to catalyze the oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons, such as ethylbenzene and isobutane \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. Among the tested catalysts, Fe2O3 supported on activated carbon (Fe2O3/AC) exhibits superior performance in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield, with comparable C3H6 selectivity to bare Fe2O3 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC, as evidenced by H2-TPR results, is a key factor in its high catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. This is in stark contrast to Fe2O3 supported on γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts, where the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support retards reducibility \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The redox cycle of Fe oxide, involving the oxidation of C3H8 to C3H6 on lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4 and the subsequent reoxidation of reduced Fe oxide by CO2, is proposed to play a crucial role in CO2-ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings underscore the pivotal role of support materials in modulating the reducibility and, consequently, the catalytic performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP. The superior activity of Fe2O3/AC can be attributed to its enhanced reducibility, which facilitates the redox cycle of Fe oxide. Future research efforts might focus on the development of novel support materials that can further improve the reducibility of Fe oxides, potentially leading to catalysts with even higher activity and selectivity. Additionally, the exploration of alternative redox-active metals and their interaction with CO2 as a soft oxidant could open new avenues for the design of efficient catalysts for CO2-ODHP and other oxidative dehydrogenation reactions.

                基于\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]的参考文献，Fe氧化物基催化剂由于其催化氧化脱氢烃类（如乙苯和异丁烷）的能力，已成为CO2辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）的潜在候选者。在测试的催化剂中，负载在活性炭上的Fe2O3（Fe2O3/AC）在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面表现出色，与裸Fe2O3的C3H6选择性相当\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。H2-TPR结果表明，Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性是其高催化性能的关键因素\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。这与Fe2O3负载在γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂上形成鲜明对比，后者由于Fe氧化物与载体之间的强相互作用而减缓了还原性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，涉及C3H8在Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧上的氧化以及随后由CO2还原的Fe氧化物的再氧化，被提议在CO2-ODHP中起着关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 研究结果强调了载体材料在调节Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的还原性和随后的催化性能中的核心作用。Fe2O3/AC的优越活性可归因于其增强的还原性，这促进了Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发新型载体材料，以进一步提高Fe氧化物的还原性，可能产生活性和选择性更高的催化剂。此外，探索替代的氧化还原活性金属及其与CO2作为软氧化剂的相互作用，可能为CO2-ODHP和其他氧化脱氢反应的高效催化剂设计开辟新的途径。
