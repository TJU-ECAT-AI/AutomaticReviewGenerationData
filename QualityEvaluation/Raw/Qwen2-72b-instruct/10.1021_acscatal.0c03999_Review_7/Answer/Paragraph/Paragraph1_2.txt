        <English> 
                Fe oxide-based catalysts have emerged as potential candidates for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\], due to their established role in catalyzing oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons such as ethylbenzene and isobutane. Among the various catalysts tested, Fe2O3 supported on activated carbon (Fe2O3/AC) has demonstrated superior performance in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield, with comparable C3H6 selectivity to bare Fe2O3 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC, as evidenced by H2-TPR results, is a key factor in its high catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. This is in stark contrast to Fe2O3 supported on γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts, where the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support retards reducibility \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The redox cycle of Fe oxide, involving the oxidation of C3H8 to C3H6 on lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4 and the subsequent reoxidation of reduced Fe oxide by CO2, is proposed to play a crucial role in CO2-ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings underscore the pivotal role of support materials in modulating the reducibility and, by extension, the catalytic activity of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP. Future research efforts might focus on the development of novel support materials that can further enhance the reducibility of Fe oxides, potentially leading to improved catalytic performance. Additionally, a deeper understanding of the redox cycle of Fe oxide and its interaction with CO2 could open new avenues for the design of more efficient catalysts for CO2-ODHP and related processes. The exploration of alternative redox-active metals and their interaction with CO2 as a soft oxidant could also be a promising direction for future studies.

        <Chinese> 
                基于\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]的参考文献，Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中展现出潜力，因为它们能够催化诸如乙苯和异丁烷等烃类的氧化脱氢。在测试的催化剂中，负载在活性炭上的Fe2O3（Fe2O3/AC）在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面表现出色，与裸Fe2O3的C3H6选择性相当\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性，由H2-TPR结果证实，是其高催化性能的关键因素\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。这与Fe2O3负载在γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂上形成鲜明对比，其中Fe氧化物与载体的强相互作用阻碍了还原性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，涉及C3H8在Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧上的氧化成C3H6，以及随后由CO2还原的Fe氧化物的再氧化，被提议在CO2-ODHP中起着关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 研究结果突出了载体材料在调节Fe氧化物催化剂的还原性以及间接地催化活性中的核心作用。未来的研究可能集中于开发新型载体材料，以进一步增强Fe氧化物的还原性，可能带来催化性能的提升。此外，对Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环及其与CO2的相互作用的更深入理解，可能为设计更高效的CO2-ODHP和相关过程的催化剂开辟新途径。探索替代的氧化还原活性金属及其与CO2作为软氧化剂的相互作用，也可能成为未来研究的有希望的方向。
