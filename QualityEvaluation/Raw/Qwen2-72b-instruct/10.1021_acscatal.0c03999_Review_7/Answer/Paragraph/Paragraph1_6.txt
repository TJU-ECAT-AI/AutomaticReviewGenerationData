                Fe oxide-based catalysts are potential candidates for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) due to their ability to catalyze oxidative dehydrogenation of hydrocarbons, such as ethylbenzene and isobutane \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. Among the tested catalysts, Fe2O3 supported on activated carbon (Fe2O3/AC) shows superior performance in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield, with comparable C3H6 selectivity to bare Fe2O3 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC, as evidenced by H2-TPR results, is a key factor in its high catalytic performance \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. This is in contrast to Fe2O3 supported on γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts, where the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support retards reducibility \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]. The redox cycle of Fe oxide, involving the oxidation of C3H8 to C3H6 on lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4 and the subsequent reoxidation of reduced Fe oxide by CO2, is proposed to play a crucial role in CO2-ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Taking into consideration the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel support materials that can further enhance the reducibility of Fe oxide-based catalysts, potentially leading to improved catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP. Additionally, the investigation of alternative redox-active metals and their interaction with Fe oxide could provide insights into the design of more efficient catalysts for propane dehydrogenation. The role of CO2 as a soft oxidant in the redox cycle of Fe oxide opens up possibilities for the utilization of other green oxidants in similar catalytic processes, paving the way for sustainable chemical production.

                基于\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]参考文献，Fe氧化物基催化剂是CO2辅助丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）的潜在候选者，因为它们能够催化诸如乙苯和异丁烷等烃类的氧化脱氢。在测试的催化剂中，负载在活性炭上的Fe2O3（Fe2O3/AC）在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面表现出优越性能，与裸Fe2O3具有可比的C3H6选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。H2-TPR结果证明Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性是其高催化性能的关键因素\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。这与Fe2O3负载在γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂上形成鲜明对比，其中Fe氧化物与载体的强相互作用阻碍了还原性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，涉及从Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧氧化C3H8至C3H6，随后由CO2还原氧化的Fe氧化物，被提议在CO2-ODHP中起关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现，我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能会集中在开发新型载体材料，以进一步增强Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性，可能带来CO2-ODHP中催化性能的提升。此外，研究替代的氧化还原活性金属及其与Fe氧化物的相互作用，可能为设计更高效的丙烷脱氢催化剂提供见解。CO2作为Fe氧化物氧化还原循环中的软氧化剂的作用，为在类似的催化过程中利用其他绿色氧化剂开辟了可能性，为可持续化学生产铺平了道路。
