1.Analyze the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP), explaining how catalyst reducibility and support materials (particularly in Fe2O3/AC) influence catalytic activity, and describe the proposed reaction mechanism involving the redox cycle of Fe oxide.
The literature does not provide information on Fe oxide-based catalysts or their performance in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane. The study focuses on the catalytic activity and stability of Ru doped CeO2 and Ru doped ZrO2 for the partial oxidation of propane using carbon dioxide as an oxidant. The major products of propane partial oxidation were CO and H2 when CO2 is used as an oxidant, and the two catalysts had comparable activity below 450 C. Neither catalyst showed measurable selectivity for oxidative dehydrogenation activity of propane-to-propylene with CO2 or CO. Coke formation will limit the utility of either catalyst for dry reforming of propane in practice.

文献没有提供关于Fe氧化物基催化剂或其在二氧化碳辅助丙烷氧化脱氢中的性能的信息。该研究的重点是Ru掺杂CeO2和Ru掺杂ZrO2在使用二氧化碳作为氧化剂的丙烷部分氧化中的催化活性和稳定性。当CO2用作氧化剂时，丙烷部分氧化的主要产物是CO和H2，两种催化剂在450 C以下具有可比较的活性。两种催化剂在CO2或CO存在下均未显示出可测量的丙烷至丙烯氧化脱氢活性的选择性。焦炭形成将限制这两种催化剂用于丙烷干重整的实用性。
