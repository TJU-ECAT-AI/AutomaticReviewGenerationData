However, it does mention that the preparation method (ion-exchange and steam activation) and the framework composition (FeAlSi, FeGaSi, and FeGeSi) can induce different iron speciation and acidic properties in the zeolites, which in turn affect their catalytic performance. The key performance metrics that demonstrate the effectiveness of the zeolites under different reaction conditions include the initial propylene yield, the rate of deactivation, and the amount of coke formed.
