                The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions: Impact of synthesis methods on catalytic performance and evaluation through key performance metrics \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Steam treatment of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites results in a C3H8 conversion of 48%, N2O conversion of 96%, and C3H6 selectivity of 45% at 450 degrees Celsius. Solid-state ion exchange or liquid exchange methods of synthesizing Fe-ZSM-5 also show good catalytic performance, with C3H8 conversion ranging from 20% to 60%, C3H6 selectivity from 25% to 70%, and C3H6 yield from 14% to 30% in the temperature range of 377 to 527 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. The study investigated the oxidation of propane using oxygen, nitrous oxide, or a mixture of both over ZSM-5 zeolites with low iron concentrations (30-1200 ppm) treated under various conditions at 450 C. The highest propane conversion (51.7%) and selectivity to propene (39.7%) and to the sum of aromatics and oxygenates (11.5%, mainly C1C3 alcohols) was achieved when a mixture of oxygen and nitrous oxide was used as the oxidant over the steamed FeH-ZSM-5 \[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]. The selectivity and yield of propene were highest over Fe-ZSM-5 with low Al contents and reduced acidity. The initial propene yield over Fe-ZSM-5 was significantly higher than that of FeSiO2 due to the presence of weak and/or medium acid sites together with oligonuclear iron species and iron oxides on the ZSM-5, which enhance the N2O activation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]. Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared with the solid-state method have comparable activity and better stability towards coking compared to Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared by liquid ion exchange and hydrothermal synthesis methods \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\].

                铁掺杂ZSM-5沸石作为催化剂在N2O辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应中的有效性和性能:合成方法对催化性能的影响及通过关键性能指标的评估\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。铁掺杂ZSM-5沸石的蒸汽处理导致C3H8转化率为48%，N2O转化率为96%，C3H6选择性为45%，在450摄氏度下。固态离子交换或液相交换方法合成Fe-ZSM-5也显示出良好的催化性能，C3H8转化率在20%到60%之间，C3H6选择性在25%到70%之间，C3H6产率在14%到30%之间，在377到527摄氏度的温度范围内\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。研究调查了在450 C下，使用氧气、一氧化二氮或两者的混合物在低铁浓度(30-1200 ppm)的ZSM-5沸石上氧化丙烷的情况。当使用氧气和一氧化二氮的混合物作为氧化剂在蒸气处理的FeH-ZSM-5上时，实现了最高的丙烷转化率(51.7%)和对丙烯(39.7%)以及对芳香族和氧化物(11.5%，主要是C1C3醇)的总选择性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]。丙烯的选择性和产率在低Al含量和降低酸性的Fe-ZSM-5上最高。Fe-ZSM-5上初始丙烯产率显著高于FeSiO2，这是由于ZSM-5上弱和/或中等酸位点的存在，以及寡核铁物种和铁氧化物，它们增强了N2O的活化\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。采用固态方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石与采用液相离子交换和水热合成方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石相比，具有相当的活性和更好的抗焦炭稳定性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。
