                    The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions have been extensively studied \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Steam treatment of these zeolites results in a C3H8 conversion of 48%, N2O conversion of 96%, and C3H6 selectivity of 45% at 450 degrees Celsius. Solid-state ion exchange or liquid exchange methods of synthesizing Fe-ZSM-5 also show good catalytic performance, with C3H8 conversion ranging from 20% to 60%, C3H6 selectivity from 25% to 70%, and C3H6 yield from 14% to 30% in the temperature range of 377 to 527 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. The study of reference \[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\] investigated the oxidation of propane using oxygen, nitrous oxide, or a mixture of both over ZSM-5 zeolites with low iron concentrations (30-1200 ppm) treated under various conditions at 450 C. The highest propane conversion (51.7%) and selectivity to propene (39.7%) and to the sum of aromatics and oxygenates (11.5%, mainly C1C3 alcohols) was achieved when a mixture of oxygen and nitrous oxide was used as the oxidant over the steamed FeH-ZSM-5. The catalytic activity was attributed to extra-framework Fe-oxo complexes generated by hydrolysis of framework SiOFe bonds followed by extraction of iron to extra-framework positions. Fe-zeolites with the highest Fe content steamed in the flow of water vapor at 600 C showed the highest catalytic activity. In addition to propene, other valuable products such as ethene, methanol, ethanol, and acetone were formed \[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]. Synthesized comprehension stemming from references \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\] and \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010\] reveals that the selectivity and yield of propene were highest over Fe-ZSM-5 with low Al contents and reduced acidity. The initial propene yield over Fe-ZSM-5 was significantly higher than that of FeSiO2 due to the presence of weak and/or medium acid sites together with oligonuclear iron species and iron oxides on the ZSM-5, which enhance the N2O activation. The coking of Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts could be controlled by reducing the surface acidity of ZSM-5 and using O2 in addition to N2O as the oxidant. Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared with the solid-state method have comparable activity and better stability towards coking compared to Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared by liquid ion exchange and hydrothermal synthesis methods \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]. Steaming of FeH-zeolites changes the structure of Fe species, resulting in a significant decrease in TOF to 30% of the original value. The initial TOFC3H8 of both FeH-ZSM-5 zeolites, regardless of the crystal size, were similar and exhibited similar decrease with TOS. These initial values were also not significantly affected by the steaming of both zeolites, although the structure of Fe species was markedly changed. But the TOFC3H8 and propane conversion over FeH-ZSM-5 decreased with TOS much more compared to the steamed zeolites. On contrary, the initial propene yields were substantially higher for the steamed zeolites compared to non-steamed ones, and moreover, much more stable values with TOS were found. This finding might indicate much lower propene oligomerization/aromatization and oxidation of these side products as a result of much lower concentration of Brnsted sites in the steamed zeolites \[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010\].

The exploration of alternative iron sources and the investigation of the role of other metal ions in the catalytic process could also be promising directions for future research.

                    铁掺杂ZSM-5分子筛在N2O辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应中的有效性和性能已得到广泛研究\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。蒸汽处理这些分子筛导致C3H8转化率为48%，N2O转化率为96%，C3H6选择性为45%，在450摄氏度。固态离子交换或液相交换方法合成Fe-ZSM-5也显示出良好的催化性能，C3H8转化率在20%到60%之间，C3H6选择性在25%到70%之间，C3H6产率在14%到30%之间，在377到527摄氏度的温度范围内\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。参考文献\[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]研究了在450 C下，使用氧气、一氧化二氮或两者的混合物，对低铁浓度(30-1200 ppm)的ZSM-5分子筛进行不同条件处理，以氧化丙烷。当使用氧气和一氧化二氮的混合物作为氧化剂时，最高丙烷转化率(51.7%)和丙烯选择性(39.7%)以及芳香族和氧化物(11.5%，主要是C1C3醇)的总和达到最高。催化活性归因于框架SiOFe键的水解后生成的框架外Fe-氧复合物，随后铁被提取到框架外位置。在600 C的水蒸气流中蒸制的最高Fe含量的Fe-分子筛显示出最高的催化活性。除了丙烯，还形成了其他有价值的产物，如乙烯、甲醇、乙醇和丙酮\[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]。从参考文献\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]和\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010\]中获得的综合理解表明，丙烯的选择性和产率在低Al含量和降低酸性的Fe-ZSM-5上最高。Fe-ZSM-5上的初始丙烯产率显著高于FeSiO2，这是由于ZSM-5上弱和/或中等酸位点以及寡核铁物种和铁氧化物的存在，增强了N2O的活化。通过降低ZSM-5的表面酸度和使用O2作为N2O的氧化剂，可以控制Fe-ZSM-5催化剂的焦化。采用固态方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5分子筛与采用液相离子交换和水热合成方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5分子筛相比，具有相当的活性和更好的抗焦化稳定性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。FeH-分子筛的蒸制改变了Fe物种的结构，导致TOF显著下降至原始值的30%。无论晶粒尺寸如何，两个FeH-ZSM-5分子筛的初始TOFC3H8相似，并且随TOS表现出相似的下降。这些初始值也没有受到两种分子筛蒸制的显著影响，尽管Fe物种的结构发生了显著变化。但是，FeH-ZSM-5上的TOFC3H8和丙烷转化率随TOS比蒸制分子筛下降得更多。相反，蒸制分子筛的初始丙烯产率比非蒸制分子筛高得多，而且，随着TOS的稳定值也高得多。这一发现可能表明，由于蒸制分子筛中Brnsted位点浓度低，丙烯的聚合作用/芳构化和这些副产品的氧化要低得多\[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010\]。
