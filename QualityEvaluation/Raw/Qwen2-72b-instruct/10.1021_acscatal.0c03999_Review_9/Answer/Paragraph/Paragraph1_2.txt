            <English> 
                    The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are significantly influenced by the synthesis methods employed \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange methods all impact the catalytic activity and selectivity, with C3H8 conversion ranging from 20% to 60%, N2O conversion from 96% to 100%, and C3H6 selectivity from 25% to 70% \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. The highest catalytic activity is observed in Fe-zeolites with the highest Fe content steamed in the flow of water vapor at 600°C, achieving propane conversion of 51.7% and selectivity to propene of 39.7% \[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]. The selectivity and yield of propene are highest over Fe-ZSM-5 with low Al contents and reduced acidity, attributed to the presence of weak and/or medium acid sites together with oligonuclear iron species and iron oxides on the ZSM-5, which enhance N2O activation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]. Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared with the solid-state method exhibit comparable activity and better stability towards coking compared to those prepared by liquid ion exchange and hydrothermal synthesis methods \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The synthesis methods of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites play a pivotal role in their catalytic performance in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane. Steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange methods each offer unique advantages in terms of catalytic activity, selectivity, and stability. Future research should focus on optimizing these synthesis methods to enhance the stability and selectivity of Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts, particularly by controlling the surface acidity and iron species distribution. Additionally, exploring the use of oxygen in conjunction with N2O as an oxidant could further improve the catalytic performance and control coking. The development of novel synthesis techniques that can precisely tune the acidity and iron content of Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites holds promise for advancing the efficiency and selectivity of propane dehydrogenation processes.

                    铁掺杂ZSM-5沸石作为催化剂在N2O辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应中的有效性和性能受到所采用合成方法的显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。蒸汽处理、固态离子交换和液态交换方法均影响催化活性和选择性，C3H8转化率在20%到60%之间，N2O转化率在96%到100%之间，C3H6选择性在25%到70%之间\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。在600°C的水蒸气流中蒸汽处理的Fe-沸石中观察到最高的催化活性，丙烷转化率达到51.7%，丙烯选择性达到39.7%\[10.1016/j.apcata.2003.12.020\]。丙烯的选择性和产率在低Al含量和减少酸性的Fe-ZSM-5上最高，归因于ZSM-5上弱和/或中等酸位点的存在，以及寡核铁物种和铁氧化物，这些增强了N2O的活化\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。通过固态方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石与通过液态离子交换和水热合成方法制备的沸石相比，表现出相当的活性和更好的抗焦炭稳定性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 铁掺杂ZSM-5沸石的合成方法在N2O辅助的丙烷氧化脱氢中起着关键作用。蒸汽处理、固态离子交换和液态交换方法在催化活性、选择性和稳定性方面各自具有独特的优势。未来的研究应集中在优化这些合成方法以增强Fe-ZSM-5催化剂的稳定性和选择性，特别是通过控制表面酸性和铁物种分布。此外，探索将氧气与N2O结合使用作为氧化剂，可以进一步提高催化性能和控制焦炭形成。开发能够精确调整Fe-ZSM-5沸石酸性和铁含量的新合成技术，对于提高丙烷脱氢过程的效率和选择性具有前景。
