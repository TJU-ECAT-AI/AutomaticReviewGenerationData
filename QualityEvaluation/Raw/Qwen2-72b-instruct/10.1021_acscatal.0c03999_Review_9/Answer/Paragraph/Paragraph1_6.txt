            <English> 
                    The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are significantly influenced by the synthesis methods employed \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange methods all impact the catalytic properties, with C3H8 conversion, N2O conversion, C3H6 selectivity, and C3H6 yield serving as key performance metrics \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Steam treatment, in particular, results in a C3H8 conversion of 48%, N2O conversion of 96%, and C3H6 selectivity of 45% at 450 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. Solid-state ion exchange or liquid exchange methods of synthesizing Fe-ZSM-5 also show good catalytic performance, with C3H8 conversion ranging from 20% to 60%, C3H6 selectivity from 25% to 70%, and C3H6 yield from 14% to 30% in the temperature range of 377 to 527 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]. The selectivity and yield of propene are highest over Fe-ZSM-5 with low Al contents and reduced acidity \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]. The initial propene yield over Fe-ZSM-5 is significantly higher than that of FeSiO2 due to the presence of weak and/or medium acid sites together with oligonuclear iron species and iron oxides on the ZSM-5, which enhance the N2O activation \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]. Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared with the solid-state method have comparable activity and better stability towards coking compared to Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared by liquid ion exchange and hydrothermal synthesis methods \[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The synthesis methods of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites significantly impact their catalytic performance in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane reactions. Steam treatment, solid-state ion exchange, and liquid exchange methods all influence the C3H8 conversion, N2O conversion, C3H6 selectivity, and C3H6 yield, which are key performance metrics. The selectivity and yield of propene are highest over Fe-ZSM-5 with low Al contents and reduced acidity. The initial propene yield over Fe-ZSM-5 is significantly higher than that of FeSiO2 due to the presence of weak and/or medium acid sites together with oligonuclear iron species and iron oxides on the ZSM-5, which enhance the N2O activation. Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared with the solid-state method have comparable activity and better stability towards coking compared to Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites prepared by liquid ion exchange and hydrothermal synthesis methods. Future research might focus on optimizing the synthesis methods to further enhance the catalytic performance and stability of Fe-ZSM-5 zeolites in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane reactions.

                    铁含量ZSM-5沸石作为催化剂在N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷(ODHP)反应中的有效性和性能受到所采用合成方法的显著影响\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。蒸汽处理、固态离子交换和液态交换方法都会影响催化性质，C3H8转化率、N2O转化率、C3H6选择性和C3H6产率作为关键性能指标\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。特别是蒸汽处理，在450摄氏度下，C3H8转化率为48%，N2O转化率为96%，C3H6选择性为45%\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。固态离子交换或液态交换方法合成Fe-ZSM-5也表现出良好的催化性能，在377至527摄氏度的温度范围内，C3H8转化率在20%到60%之间，C3H6选择性在25%到70%之间，C3H6产率在14%到30%之间\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9\]。丙烯的选择性和产率在低Al含量和减少酸性的Fe-ZSM-5上最高\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。Fe-ZSM-5上初始丙烯产率显著高于FeSiO2，这是由于ZSM-5上存在弱和/或中等酸位点以及寡核铁物种和铁氧化物，增强了N2O的活化\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。采用固态方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石与采用液态离子交换和水热合成方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石相比，具有相当的活性和更好的抗焦稳定性\[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 铁含量ZSM-5沸石的合成方法对其在N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷反应中的催化性能有重大影响。蒸汽处理、固态离子交换和液态交换方法都会影响C3H8转化率、N2O转化率、C3H6选择性和C3H6产率，这些是关键性能指标。丙烯的选择性和产率在低Al含量和减少酸性的Fe-ZSM-5上最高。Fe-ZSM-5上初始丙烯产率显著高于FeSiO2，这是由于ZSM-5上存在弱和/或中等酸位点以及寡核铁物种和铁氧化物，增强了N2O的活化。采用固态方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石与采用液态离子交换和水热合成方法制备的Fe-ZSM-5沸石相比，具有相当的活性和更好的抗焦稳定性。未来的研究可能会集中在优化合成方法，以进一步提高Fe-ZSM-5沸石在N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷反应中的催化性能和稳定性。
