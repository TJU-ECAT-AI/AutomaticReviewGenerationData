                The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis have been elucidated, revealing the pivotal role of structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites in determining catalytic activity and selectivity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs outshine α- and δ-Ga2O3 in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity, with surface acidity density decreasing in the order of β > γ > δ > α, mirroring the surface basicity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. The initial activity is contingent on surface acid density, peaking on β-Ga2O3 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, acting as Lewis acid sites, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 are constituted by octahedral Ga3+, with weaker acidity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. The reconstruction from octahedral to tetrahedral Ga cations might generate surface Lewis acid sites, explaining the activity trend \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. Surface basic sites are insufficient for CO2 adsorption and activation, leading to lower CO2 conversion than C3H8 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. However, surface base site density correlates with initial activity, demonstrating its promotional effect \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. The reduction of Ga cations benefits DHP only at the initial stage of the reaction \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. At the steady state, the reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without the redox cycle \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights into the structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis open avenues for the design of novel catalysts with enhanced activity and selectivity. Future research might focus on the controlled synthesis of Ga2O3 polymorphs with tailored surface properties and Lewis acid sites, aiming to maximize C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity. The development of advanced characterization techniques could provide deeper insights into the dynamic changes in surface properties under reaction conditions. Moreover, the exploration of synergistic effects between Ga2O3 and other metal oxides might lead to the discovery of new catalysts with improved stability and activity. The understanding of the role of surface basic sites in the initial period of the reaction suggests the potential of modifying the surface chemistry to enhance the promotional effect on activity. The investigation of the redox properties of Ga cations and their influence on the reaction mechanism at different stages could provide guidelines for the design of catalysts with optimized performance over the entire reaction period.

                Ga2O3多晶型在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系揭示了结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位点在决定催化活性和选择性中的关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。β-和γ-Ga2O3多晶型在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面超越α-和δ-Ga2O3，表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，与表面碱性相吻合\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。初始活性依赖于表面酸密度，在β-Ga2O3上达到峰值\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。β-和γ-Ga2O3含有四面体Ga阳离子，充当Lewis酸位点，而α-和δ-Ga2O3由八面体Ga3+构成，酸性较弱\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。从八面体到四面体Ga阳离子的重构可能生成表面Lewis酸位点，解释了活性趋势\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。表面碱性位点不足以吸附和活化CO2，导致CO2转化率低于C3H8\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。然而，表面碱位密度与初始活性相关，展示了其在反应初期的促进作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。Ga阳离子的还原仅在反应初期对DHP有益\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。在稳态下，反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，无需红ox循环\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: Ga2O3多晶型在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系的洞察为设计具有增强活性和选择性的新型催化剂开辟了道路。未来的研究可能集中在控制合成具有定制表面性质和Lewis酸位点的Ga2O3多晶型，旨在最大化C3H8转化和C3H6选择性。先进表征技术的发展可以提供对反应条件下表面性质动态变化的更深入理解。此外，探索Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物之间的协同效应可能导致发现具有改进稳定性和活性的新催化剂。对表面碱性位点在反应初期对活性的促进作用的理解表明，通过修改表面化学增强促进作用的潜力。对Ga阳离子的红ox性质及其对不同反应阶段反应机制的影响的调查可以为设计在整个反应期间具有优化性能的催化剂提供指导。
