                The catalytic performance of different Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP reactions is influenced by their structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 exhibit higher C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity compared to α- and δ-Ga2O3. The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, correlating with the surface basicity. The initial activity is dependent on the surface acid density and is maximized on β-Ga2O3. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, which are coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites and act as Lewis acid sites. α- and δ-Ga2O3 are constituted by octahedral Ga3+, which are more coordinated and have weaker acidity. The reconstruction from octahedral to tetrahedral Ga cations might occur, generating surface Lewis acid sites. The differences in Lewis acidity explain the activity trend. Surface basic sites are too few for CO2 adsorption and activation, leading to lower CO2 conversion than C3H8. However, the surface base site density shows a correlation with initial activity, demonstrating its promotional effect in the initial period of the reaction. The reduction of Ga cations takes place and benefits DHP only at the initial stage of the reaction. At the steady state, the reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without the redox cycle.
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore the role of surface defects and structural modifications in enhancing the catalytic efficiency of Ga2O3 polymorphs. Future research endeavors might focus on developing novel synthesis methods to control the polymorphic forms and surface properties of Ga2O3, aiming to maximize the population of coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites. Additionally, the investigation of the redox properties of Ga cations and their impact on the reaction mechanism could provide insights into the development of more stable and active catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions. The exploration of alternative reaction pathways, such as the utilization of CO2 as a co-reactant, could also be a promising direction to enhance the selectivity and efficiency of propane dehydrogenation.

                不同Ga2O3多晶型在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能受其结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位点的影响\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4\]。β-和γ-Ga2O3相比α-和δ-Ga2O3表现出更高的C3H8转化率和C3H6选择性。表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，与表面碱性相一致。初始活性依赖于表面酸度密度，并在β-Ga2O3上达到最大。β-和γ-Ga2O3含有四面体Ga阳离子，这些是配位不饱和的Ga3+位点，充当Lewis酸位点。α-和δ-Ga2O3由八面体Ga3+构成，这些位点的配位程度更高，酸性较弱。从八面体到四面体Ga阳离子的重构可能会发生，生成表面Lewis酸位点。Lewis酸性的差异解释了活性趋势。表面碱性位点对于CO2吸附和活化来说过少，导致CO2转化率低于C3H8。然而，表面碱位密度与初始活性相关，表明其在反应初期的促进作用。Ga阳离子的还原仅在反应初期对DHP有利。在稳态下，反应通过在Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，无需红ox循环。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现，我们有机会探索表面缺陷和结构修改在提高Ga2O3多晶型催化效率方面的作用。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的合成方法来控制Ga2O3的多晶型形式和表面性质，旨在最大化配位不饱和Ga3+位点的数量。此外，Ga阳离子的红ox性质及其对反应机制的影响的调查可能为开发更稳定、更活性的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂提供见解。利用CO2作为共反应物的替代反应途径的探索也可能是一个有希望的方向，以提高丙烷脱氢的选择性和效率。
