<English>
The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions is a pivotal process that involves the interaction of reactants with surface oxygen, leading to the creation of oxygen vacancies and subsequent reoxidation by gas-phase oxygen \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Central to this mechanism is ΔEv, the energy required for oxygen vacancy formation, which significantly influences both the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Lower ΔEv values indicate easier oxide reduction, enhancing catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability \[10.1140/epjb_e2010-00295-x\]. This is particularly evident in ceria surfaces, where lower ΔEv on {110} and {100} surfaces facilitates CO reaction, enhancing catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability \[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.06.008\]. The role of ΔEv as a descriptor of catalytic activity is further highlighted, as it affects both CO reaction energy and O2 adsorption energy at vacancies \[10.1021/jp802296g\].

Dopants play a crucial role in modifying ΔEv, thereby impacting catalytic activity. Substituting Zn atoms in ZnO(1010) with dopants like Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, and Cd decreases ΔEv, enhancing catalytic activity and oxidizing ability at the surface \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Conversely, dopants such as Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, and Pb increase ΔEv, potentially reducing catalytic efficiency \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. In ceria doped with Pt, the presence of the dopant weakens the oxygen-surface bond, facilitating oxygen vacancy formation with less energy, resulting in enhanced catalytic activity, particularly for methane activation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005\]. Similarly, Zr dopants in ceria lower ΔEv, enhancing lattice oxygen mobility and catalytic activity \[10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5\].

The energy of oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) is a critical factor in determining the catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability of oxide catalysts in propane dehydrogenation reactions. Lower ΔEv values facilitate easier oxygen vacancy formation, enhancing the oxide's oxidative power and catalytic activity for oxidation reactions \[10.1039/c1jm11238d\]. This indicates a higher density of oxygen vacancies, increasing surface reactivity and oxidizing capability, which is crucial for the efficiency of propane dehydrogenation catalysts.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism and the role of ΔEv in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions provide a robust foundation for the design of efficient propane dehydrogenation catalysts. The modulation of ΔEv through the strategic use of dopants offers a promising avenue for enhancing catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel dopants that can further reduce ΔEv while maintaining the stability and reoxidizability of the oxide surface. Additionally, advanced computational methods, such as density functional theory (DFT), can be employed to predict the effects of various dopants on ΔEv and catalytic performance, guiding the rational design of catalysts. The exploration of new oxide materials with inherently lower ΔEv values could also be a fruitful direction, potentially leading to the discovery of more efficient and stable catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and other oxidation reactions.

Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中起着关键作用，涉及反应物与表面氧的相互作用，导致氧空位的形成和随后通过气相氧的再氧化\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。在这个机制中，ΔEv，即氧空位形成所需的能量，对催化活性和氧化物表面的氧化能力有显著影响\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。较低的ΔEv值表明氧化物更容易还原，从而增强催化活性和表面氧化能力\[10.1140/epjb_e2010-00295-x\]。这在铈表面尤为明显，其中{110}和{100}表面上较低的ΔEv促进了CO反应，增强了催化活性和表面氧化能力\[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.06.008\]。ΔEv作为催化活性描述符的作用进一步突出，因为它影响CO反应能量和O2在空位上的吸附能量\[10.1021/jp802296g\]。

掺杂剂在通过修改ΔEv影响催化活性方面起着关键作用。在ZnO(1010)中用Li、Na、K、Rb、Cs、Os、Rh、Ir、Ni、Pd、Pt、Cu、Ag、Au和Cd等掺杂剂替代Zn原子可以降低ΔEv，增强表面的催化活性和氧化能力\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。相反，Mg、Ca、Sr、Ba、Sc、Y、La、Ti、Zr、Hf、Ce、V、Nb、Ta、Cr、Mo、W、Mn、Re、Fe、Ru、Co、Al、Ga、Sn和Pb等掺杂剂会增加ΔEv，可能降低催化效率\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。在Pt掺杂的铈中，掺杂剂的存在削弱了氧-表面键，以较低的能量促进了氧空位的形成，导致催化活性增强，特别是在甲烷活化方面\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005\]。同样，铈中的Zr掺杂剂降低了ΔEv，增强了晶格氧的流动性，提高了催化活性\[10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5\]。

氧空位形成能量(ΔEv)是决定氧化物催化剂在丙烷脱氢反应中的催化活性和表面氧化能力的关键因素。较低的ΔEv值促进了更容易的氧空位形成，增强了氧化物的氧化能力，提高了氧化反应的催化活性\[10.1039/c1jm11238d\]。这表明氧空位的密度更高，表面反应性和氧化能力增加，这对丙烷脱氢催化剂的效率至关重要。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：Mars-van Krevelen机制和ΔEv在氧化物催化的氧化反应中的作用为设计高效的丙烷脱氢催化剂提供了坚实的基础。通过战略使用掺杂剂调节ΔEv为增强催化活性和表面氧化能力提供了一条有希望的途径。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发新型掺杂剂上，这些掺杂剂可以进一步降低ΔEv，同时保持氧化物表面的稳定性和可再氧化性。此外，可以利用先进的计算方法，如密度泛函理论(DFT)，来预测各种掺杂剂对ΔEv和催化性能的影响，指导催化剂的理性设计。探索具有固有较低ΔEv值的新氧化物材料也可能是一个富有成效的方向，可能发现更有效、更稳定的丙烷脱氢和其他氧化反应催化剂。
