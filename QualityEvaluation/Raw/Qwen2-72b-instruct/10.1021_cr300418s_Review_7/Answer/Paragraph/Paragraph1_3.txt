<English>
The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions is a pivotal process that involves the interaction of reactants with surface oxygen, leading to the creation of oxygen vacancies [10.1021/jp071671n]. The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) is a critical parameter that indicates the ease of lattice oxygen participation in the reaction, significantly impacting both catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface [10.1021/jp071671n]. Lower ΔEv values signify easier oxygen vacancy formation, enhancing the oxide's oxidative power and catalytic activity for oxidation reactions [10.1039/c1jm11238d]. Doping with specific elements can lower ΔEv, enhancing catalytic activity by preferentially locating at the surface [10.1021/jp071671n]. Conversely, other dopants increase ΔEv, potentially reducing catalytic efficiency [10.1021/jp071671n]. The role of ΔEv in determining catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability is underscored by its influence on the dynamics of oxygen vacancy formation and reinsertion [10.1021/jp2049603].

Dopants like Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, and Cd decrease ΔEv, enhancing catalytic activity and oxidizing ability by preferentially locating at the surface [10.1021/jp071671n]. Conversely, Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, and Pb increase ΔEv, potentially reducing catalytic efficiency [10.1021/jp071671n]. Pt dopants in ceria weaken the oxygen-surface bond, facilitating oxygen vacancy formation with less energy, enhancing catalytic activity for methane activation and oxidation reactions [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005]. Zr dopants lower ΔEv, enhancing lattice oxygen mobility and catalytic activity by promoting the reduction of Ce4+ to Ce3+, facilitating oxygen release for CO oxidation [10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5].

ΔEv is a key descriptor of catalytic activity, as it affects the energy of CO reaction with surface oxygen atoms and the adsorption energy of O2 at a vacancy [10.1021/jp802296g]. Dopants lower ΔEv, enhancing the oxide's oxidizing ability and catalytic activity [10.1021/jp103604b]. The catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability are inversely proportional to ΔEv, as a lower ΔEv facilitates faster oxygen vacancy creation, enhancing the catalyst's oxidation activity [10.1016/j.cattod.2010.10.061].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions, with its critical dependence on ΔEv, presents a fertile ground for future research. The modulation of ΔEv through doping with specific elements offers a pathway to enhance catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability. Future studies could explore the use of novel dopants that further lower ΔEv while maintaining the balance necessary for efficient reoxidation. The development of advanced computational methods, such as density functional theory (DFT), could aid in the prediction of ΔEv for various dopants, guiding the design of more efficient catalysts. Moreover, the investigation of the role of ΔEv in the catalytic cycle of complex reactions, such as the partial oxidation of hydrocarbons, could provide insights into the optimization of catalytic systems for industrial applications. The exploration of the interplay between ΔEv and other surface properties, such as electronic structure and morphology, could also lead to a more comprehensive understanding of the catalytic mechanism and the design of multifunctional catalysts.

Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中是一个关键过程，涉及反应物与表面氧的相互作用，导致氧空位的形成\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。氧空位形成所需能量（ΔEv）是一个关键参数，表明晶格氧参与反应的容易程度，对催化活性和氧化物表面的氧化能力有重大影响\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。较低的ΔEv值表明氧空位形成更容易，增强了氧化物的氧化能力和催化活性\[10.1039/c1jm11238d\]。通过特定元素的掺杂可以降低ΔEv，通过优先定位在表面增强催化活性\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。相反，其他掺杂剂增加ΔEv，可能降低催化效率\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。ΔEv在确定催化活性和表面氧化能力中的作用通过其对氧空位形成和再插入动力学的影响得到强调\[10.1021/jp2049603\]。

Li、Na、K、Rb、Cs、Os、Rh、Ir、Ni、Pd、Pt、Cu、Ag、Au和Cd等掺杂剂降低ΔEv，通过优先定位在表面增强催化活性和氧化能力\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。相反，Mg、Ca、Sr、Ba、Sc、Y、La、Ti、Zr、Hf、Ce、V、Nb、Ta、Cr、Mo、W、Mn、Re、Fe、Ru、Co、Al、Ga、Sn和Pb等掺杂剂增加ΔEv，可能降低催化效率\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。在CeO2中，Pt掺杂剂削弱了氧-表面键，以较低的能量促进氧空位的形成，增强了甲烷活化和氧化反应的催化活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005\]。Zr掺杂剂降低ΔEv，通过促进Ce4+到Ce3+的还原，增强晶格氧的流动性，促进CO氧化的催化活性\[10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5\]。

ΔEv是催化活性的关键描述符，因为它影响表面氧原子与CO反应的能量和O2在空位处的吸附能量\[10.1021/jp802296g\]。掺杂剂降低ΔEv，增强氧化物的氧化能力和催化活性\[10.1021/jp103604b\]。催化活性和表面氧化能力与ΔEv成反比，较低的ΔEv促进更快的氧空位形成，增强催化剂的氧化活性\[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.10.061\]。

综合理解与未来展望：Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中，其对ΔEv的依赖性，为未来研究提供了肥沃的土壤。通过特定元素的掺杂调节ΔEv为增强催化活性和表面氧化能力提供了一条途径。未来的研究可以探索使用新型掺杂剂进一步降低ΔEv，同时保持高效再氧化所需的平衡。开发先进的计算方法，如密度泛函理论（DFT），可以帮助预测不同掺杂剂的ΔEv，指导更高效催化剂的设计。此外，研究ΔEv在复杂反应（如烃的部分氧化）催化循环中的作用，可以为工业应用中催化系统的优化提供见解。探索ΔEv与其他表面性质（如电子结构和形态）之间的相互作用，也可能导致对催化机制的更全面理解，以及多功能催化剂的设计。
