<English>
The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions is a dynamic process involving the interaction of reactants with surface oxygen, leading to the creation of oxygen vacancies and subsequent reoxidation by gas-phase oxygen \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Central to this mechanism is ΔEv, the energy required for oxygen vacancy formation, which significantly influences both the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Lower ΔEv values indicate easier reduction of the oxide, enhancing catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability \[10.1140/epjb_e2010-00295-x\]. This is particularly evident in ceria, where the (110) surface, with its favorable vacancy formation energy, is optimal for reactions such as CO oxidation to CO2 and NO2/NO reduction to NO/N2 \[10.1039/b514782d\].

Dopants play a crucial role in modifying ΔEv, thereby affecting the catalytic properties of the oxide. For instance, substituting Zn atoms in ZnO(1010) with specific metal dopants like Li, Na, K, Rb, Cs, Os, Rh, Ir, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag, Au, and Cd decreases ΔEv, enhancing surface catalytic properties \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. Conversely, dopants such as Mg, Ca, Sr, Ba, Sc, Y, La, Ti, Zr, Hf, Ce, V, Nb, Ta, Cr, Mo, W, Mn, Re, Fe, Ru, Co, Al, Ga, Sn, and Pb increase ΔEv, potentially reducing catalytic efficiency \[10.1021/jp071671n\]. The reduction in ΔEv by dopants like Pt in ceria weakens the oxygen-surface bond, facilitating oxygen vacancy formation and enhancing catalytic activity for methane activation and oxidation reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005\].

The role of ΔEv in catalytic activity is further highlighted in CO oxidation reactions, where the catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability are inversely proportional to the energy required for oxygen vacancy formation \[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.10.061\]. Lower ΔEv values facilitate faster oxygen vacancy creation, boosting the oxidation activity. This is also observed in ceria-doped with Zr, where the reduction in ΔEv enhances lattice oxygen mobility and catalytic activity \[10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5\].

In AuxTi1xO2, CO oxidation using surface oxygen atoms is exothermic, favoring thermodynamics. Doping with Au weakens the surface oxygen bond, enhancing the oxygen vacancy's reactivity and improving the oxide's oxidizing ability \[10.1007/s10562-005-0014-6\]. The balance between oxide reduction and oxidation rates is essential for a good catalyst, ensuring rapid oxygen vacancy replenishment post-oxidation reaction.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions, with its focus on ΔEv as a critical factor, opens up avenues for the design of more efficient catalysts. The modulation of ΔEv through the strategic use of dopants presents a promising approach to enhance catalytic activity and surface oxidizing ability. Future research might explore the development of novel dopants that can further reduce ΔEv while maintaining the balance necessary for effective reoxidation of the surface. Additionally, the investigation of the interplay between ΔEv and other surface properties, such as electronic structure and surface morphology, could provide deeper insights into the catalytic mechanisms and guide the design of advanced catalysts for various oxidation reactions. The optimization of ΔEv could potentially lead to the discovery of new catalysts for emerging applications in sustainable energy conversion and environmental remediation.

<Chinese>
Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中是一种动态过程，涉及反应物与表面氧的相互作用，导致氧空位的形成和随后通过气相氧的再氧化\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。这一机制的核心是ΔEv，即氧空位形成所需的能量，它对氧化物表面的催化活性和氧化能力有重大影响\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。较低的ΔEv值表明氧化物更容易还原，从而增强催化活性和表面氧化能力\[10.1140/epjb_e2010-00295-x\]。这一点在铈中尤为明显，其中(110)表面，由于其有利的空位形成能量，是CO氧化为CO2和NO2/NO还原为NO/N2等反应的理想场所\[10.1039/b514782d\]。

掺杂剂在通过修改ΔEv影响氧化物的催化性质方面起着关键作用。例如，在ZnO(1010)中用特定金属掺杂剂如Li、Na、K、Rb、Cs、Os、Rh、Ir、Ni、Pd、Pt、Cu、Ag、Au和Cd取代Zn原子可以降低ΔEv，增强表面催化性能\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。相反，Mg、Ca、Sr、Ba、Sc、Y、La、Ti、Zr、Hf、Ce、V、Nb、Ta、Cr、Mo、W、Mn、Re、Fe、Ru、Co、Al、Ga、Sn和Pb等掺杂剂会增加ΔEv，可能降低催化效率\[10.1021/jp071671n\]。铈中Pt掺杂剂通过降低ΔEv削弱了氧-表面键，促进了氧空位的形成，增强了甲烷活化和氧化反应的催化活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.05.005\]。

ΔEv在催化活性中的作用在CO氧化反应中进一步凸显，其中催化活性和表面氧化能力与氧空位形成所需能量成反比\[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.10.061\]。较低的ΔEv值促进了更快的氧空位形成，增强了氧化活性。这一点在Zr掺杂的铈中也可以观察到，其中ΔEv的降低增强了晶格氧的流动性，提高了催化活性\[10.1007/s10562-010-0446-5\]。

在AuxTi1xO2中，使用表面氧原子的CO氧化是放热的，有利于热力学。Au掺杂削弱了表面氧键，增强了氧空位的反应性，提高了氧化物的氧化能力\[10.1007/s10562-005-0014-6\]。氧化物还原和氧化速率之间的平衡对于一个好的催化剂至关重要，确保了氧化反应后氧空位的快速补充。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中，以ΔEv作为关键因素，为设计更有效的催化剂开辟了途径。通过战略使用掺杂剂调节ΔEv提供了一种增强催化活性和表面氧化能力的有希望的方法。未来的研究可能会探索开发新的掺杂剂，这些掺杂剂可以进一步降低ΔEv，同时保持表面有效再氧化所需的平衡。此外，ΔEv与其他表面性质（如电子结构和表面形态）之间的相互作用的调查可能会为催化机制提供更深入的见解，并指导各种氧化反应的先进催化剂的设计。ΔEv的优化可能最终导致发现用于新兴应用（如可持续能源转换和环境修复）的新催化剂。
