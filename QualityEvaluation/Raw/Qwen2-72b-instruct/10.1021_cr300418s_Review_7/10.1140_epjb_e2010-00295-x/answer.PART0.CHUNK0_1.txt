<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism involves surface oxygens from ceria participating in the PROX reaction, which proceeds via the mechanism involving the removal and re-oxidation of oxygen from the ceria support. The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) is an important probe of the reducibility of the oxide, and lower ΔEv values indicate easier reduction and higher catalytic activity and oxidizing ability of the oxide surface.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>
