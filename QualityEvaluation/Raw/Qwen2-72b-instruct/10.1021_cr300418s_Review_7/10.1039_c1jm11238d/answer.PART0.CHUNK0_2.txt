<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism operates in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions by involving the lattice oxygen of the oxide surface in the reaction, where the oxygen is transferred to the reactant, forming an oxygen vacancy on the surface. The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) is significant in determining both the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface. A lower ΔEv indicates a higher rate of oxygen vacancy formation, which enhances the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1.在氧化物催化的氧化反应中，Mars-van Krevelen机制通过将氧化物表面的晶格氧参与反应，将氧转移到反应物，从而在表面形成氧空位。氧空位形成所需的能量(ΔEv)对于确定氧化物表面的催化活性和氧化能力具有重要意义。较低的ΔEv表明氧空位形成速率较高，从而增强了氧化物表面的催化活性和氧化能力。
