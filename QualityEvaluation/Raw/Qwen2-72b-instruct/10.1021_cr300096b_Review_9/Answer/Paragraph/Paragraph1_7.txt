                    The pivotal role of bimetallic catalysts, particularly Pt-Ni systems, in the selective hydrogenation of C═O bonds is underscored in industrial and research applications, as detailed in reference \[10.1016/j.cej.2011.09.023\]. These catalysts significantly enhance hydrogenation activities, offering distinct properties compared to monometallic catalysts. The synergistic effects of Pt-Ni compounds shift reduction peaks and boost Ni/Al2O3's activity and selectivity. Supported PtNi catalysts exhibit the highest activity in maleic anhydride hydrogenation, crucial for chemical, pharmaceutical, and food industries. Optimization methods include XRD, H2 TPR for catalyst characterization, and flow reactor studies for activity evaluation. The process is controlled by H2 dissociation on nickel, and adding a small amount of Pt increases the rate of H2 dissociation without sacrificing selectivity \[10.1016/j.cej.2011.09.023\].

                    双金属催化剂，特别是Pt-Ni系统，在C═O键选择性氢化中的关键作用在工业和研究应用中得到了强调，如参考文献\[10.1016/j.cej.2011.09.023\]所述。这些催化剂显著增强了氢化活性，与单金属催化剂相比，提供了独特的性质。Pt-Ni化合物的协同效应改变了还原峰，并提高了Ni/Al2O3的活性和选择性。支持的PtNi催化剂在马来酸酐氢化中表现出最高的活性，这对化学、制药和食品工业至关重要。优化方法包括XRD、H2 TPR进行催化剂表征，以及流动反应器研究进行活性评估。该过程受镍上H2解离的控制，添加少量Pt可以提高H2解离速率，而不牺牲选择性\[10.1016/j.cej.2011.09.023\]。
