        <English> 
                Comparative analysis of sulfur as an alternative oxidant in methane oxidative coupling, as explored by Zhu et al. \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_13\], reveals a lower thermodynamic driving force for overoxidation compared to traditional oxygen-based systems. The thermodynamic well for sulfur oxidation to CS2 is considerably higher in energy at 3.45 eV, contrasting with 13.41 eV for O2 oxidation of methane to CO2. This indicates a potential for improved selectivity towards alkene products. An ethylene selectivity of up to 18% can be achieved on a PdS catalyst at 1050 C, albeit high temperatures are required due to the thermodynamics of the reaction and the relatively high CH activation barriers on sulfide catalysts, which are poor hydrogen abstractors compared to metal oxides. These barriers range from 1.91 eV on MoS2 to 2.68 eV for TiS2. The performance of this catalyst is relatively modest and far from practical consideration, but future studies can identify catalysts with higher conversion rates by descriptor-based theoretical screening. The MS bond energy is observed to be a good descriptor for both CH activation and CC coupling reaction barriers.
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed in the reference \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_13\], there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies in propane dehydrogenation catalysts. Future research endeavors might focus on developing novel sulfide catalysts with enhanced hydrogen abstraction capabilities, aiming to lower the CH activation barriers. Theoretical screening based on descriptors such as MS bond energy could guide the design of catalysts with improved selectivity and conversion rates. Additionally, the exploration of sulfur-based oxidative coupling processes at lower temperatures, potentially through the use of promoters or co-catalysts, could mitigate the high-temperature requirement and enhance the practicality of this approach.

                通过Zhu等人在\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_13\]中的研究，对硫作为甲烷氧化偶联中替代氧化剂的比较分析显示，与传统的基于氧气的系统相比，硫氧化具有较低的过氧化热力学驱动力。硫氧化至CS2的热力学井的能量显著较高，为3.45 eV，而氧气氧化甲烷至CO2的能量为13.41 eV。这表明了提高烯烃产品选择性的潜力。在1050 C下，PdS催化剂上可实现高达18%的乙烯选择性，尽管由于反应的热力学和硫化物催化剂上相对较高的CH活化能垒，需要高温，这些硫化物催化剂作为氢抽象剂的性能较差，与金属氧化物相比。这些能垒在MoS2上为1.91 eV，TiS2上为2.68 eV。该催化剂的性能相对温和，远未达到实用考虑，但未来的研究可以通过基于描述符的理论筛选识别出具有更高转化率的催化剂。观察到MS键能是CH活化和CC偶联反应能垒的良好描述符。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到在\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_13\]中讨论的进展和发现，我们有机会探索丙烷脱氢催化剂的新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发具有增强氢抽象能力的新型硫化物催化剂，旨在降低CH活化能垒。基于MS键能等描述符的理论筛选可以指导设计具有改进选择性和转化率的催化剂。此外，通过使用促进剂或共催化剂探索较低温度下的硫基氧化偶联过程，可以减轻高温要求，增强这种方法的实用性。
