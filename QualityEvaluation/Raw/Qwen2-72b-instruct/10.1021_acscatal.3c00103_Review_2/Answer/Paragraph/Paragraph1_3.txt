                    Recent advancements in strategies for enhancing catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes have significantly addressed fabrication challenges and optimized performance for industrial applications \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. The 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ catalyst, with its optimized composition of 1000 ppm Pt and 10% Zn on HZSM-5, has achieved high propylene yields and stability via Lewis acidic sites and small PtZn particles, challenging conventional wisdom in catalyst design \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. Moreover, the encapsulated Ga-Pt nanocatalysts in S-1 zeolites have enhanced propane dehydrogenation stability, selectivity, and activity retention, addressing fabrication challenges and optimizing performance \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]. Recent strategies for enhancing catalytic stability in direct dehydrogenation use PtREE alloy nanoparticles on mesoporous zeolites with silanol nests, enabling stable, active, and selective intermetallic compounds for propane dehydrogenation, addressing fabrication and performance challenges \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in catalyst design, such as the 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ and Ga-Pt@S-1 nanocatalysts, have opened new avenues for exploring the role of Lewis acidic sites and encapsulation in enhancing catalytic stability and selectivity \[10.1039/C9CY00237E, 10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that combine the benefits of Lewis acidity and encapsulation, potentially leading to the discovery of more efficient and stable catalysts for industrial dehydrogenation processes. The use of rare-earth elements in PtREE alloy nanoparticles on mesoporous zeolites with silanol nests has shown promise in enhancing catalytic stability, activity, and selectivity \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. This approach could be further explored to develop catalysts with improved performance and durability, addressing the challenges in catalyst design and fabrication for industrial applications.

                    最近在直接脱氢过程中增强支撑金属催化剂催化稳定性的策略方面取得了显著进展，显著解决了制造挑战并优化了工业应用的性能\[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]。10Zn0.1Pt/HZ催化剂，其优化的组成包括1000 ppm Pt和10% Zn在HZSM-5上，通过Lewis酸性位点和小PtZn颗粒实现了高丙烯产量和稳定性，挑战了催化剂设计的传统观念\[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]。此外，封装在S-1沸石中的Ga-Pt纳米催化剂增强了丙烷脱氢的稳定性、选择性和活性保留，解决了制造挑战并优化了性能\[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]。最近在直接脱氢中增强催化稳定性的策略使用了在具有硅醇巢的介孔沸石上的PtREE合金纳米颗粒，使稳定、活性和选择性的金属间化合物成为可能，解决了制造和性能挑战\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。

                    综合理解与未来展望: 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ和Ga-Pt@S-1纳米催化剂在催化剂设计方面的进展，如Lewis酸性位点和封装的作用，为探索增强催化稳定性和选择性的新途径开辟了道路\[10.1039/C9CY00237E, 10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发结合Lewis酸性和封装优势的新型催化剂上，这可能引导发现更高效、更稳定的工业脱氢过程催化剂。在具有硅醇巢的介孔沸石上使用稀土元素的PtREE合金纳米颗粒在增强催化稳定性、活性和选择性方面显示出潜力\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。这种方法可以进一步探索，以开发性能和耐久性改进的催化剂，解决工业应用中催化剂设计和制造的挑战。
