                    Recent advancements in strategies for enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes have significantly addressed fabrication challenges and optimized performance for industrial applications \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. Catalysts such as 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ, featuring PtZn and Zn's Lewis acidic sites, have demonstrated boosted stability and propylene yield, tackling the intricacies of catalyst design and performance optimization \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. The utilization of Pt-Sn/Al3+penta has been pivotal in enhancing dehydrogenation stability, effectively preventing agglomeration \[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.06.002\]. GaPt@S-1 nanocatalysts have also shown promise, boosting product desorption and propene selectivity, with zeolite support ensuring stability \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]. These advancements, alongside the use of Pt alloy nanoparticles on mesoporous zeolites with silanol nests for enhanced catalytic stability, have significantly contributed to the field \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The recent advancements in enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts for direct dehydrogenation processes have opened new avenues for exploration. The utilization of bimetallic catalysts, such as Pt-Sn and Pt-Ga, in conjunction with tailored supports, has shown remarkable potential in boosting dehydrogenation stability and selectivity. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of hierarchical structures and the optimization of metal-support interactions to further enhance catalyst performance. The exploration of novel promoters and the integration of advanced synthesis techniques could also play a pivotal role in addressing the challenges of catalyst design and performance optimization. Moreover, the investigation of the underlying mechanisms of enhanced stability and selectivity could provide insights into the rational design of next-generation catalysts for industrial dehydrogenation processes.

                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 最近在增强直接脱氢过程中支撑金属催化剂催化稳定性的策略方面取得的进展为探索开辟了新的途径。使用双金属催化剂，如Pt-Sn和Pt-Ga，结合定制的载体，在提高脱氢稳定性和选择性方面显示出巨大潜力。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发层次结构和优化金属-载体相互作用上，以进一步提高催化剂性能。新型促进剂的探索和先进合成技术的整合也可能在解决催化剂设计和性能优化的挑战中发挥关键作用。此外，增强稳定性和选择性的潜在机制的研究可以为下一代工业脱氢过程催化剂的合理设计提供见解。
