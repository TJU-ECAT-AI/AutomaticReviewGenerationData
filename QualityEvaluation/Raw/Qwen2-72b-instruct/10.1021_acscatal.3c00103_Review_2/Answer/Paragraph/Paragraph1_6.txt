                    Recent advancements in strategies for enhancing the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes have significantly addressed fabrication challenges and optimized performance for industrial applications \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. The development of 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ catalysts, featuring Lewis acidic sites and small PtZn particles, has demonstrated a propylene yield of 46% with stability over 65 hours, showcasing an unconventional design that challenges traditional norms \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. The stabilization of Pt-Sn clusters on Al2O3 through the presence of Al3+penta sites has proven to be key for dehydrogenation stability and regenerability, preventing agglomeration and ensuring sinter-resistance \[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.06.002\]. Additionally, the utilization of Ga-Pt/S-1 zeolite nanocatalysts has shown promise in boosting propene selectivity, formation rate, and regeneration stability, addressing fabrication and performance optimization \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\].

The unconventional design of 10Zn0.1Pt/HZ catalysts, which defies norms with a higher Zn content and lower Pt, suggests a paradigm shift in catalyst design, where the focus is not solely on precious metals but also on the synergistic effects of bimetallic compositions \[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]. The stabilization of Pt-Sn clusters through Al3+penta sites on Al2O3 highlights the importance of support modification in enhancing stability and regenerability \[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.06.002\]. Moreover, the use of Ga-Pt/S-1 zeolite nanocatalysts for propene selectivity and regeneration stability points towards the potential of zeolite-encapsulated catalysts in addressing fabrication challenges and optimizing performance \[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the exploration of novel bimetallic compositions, the development of advanced support materials, and the optimization of zeolite-encapsulated catalysts for enhanced stability and performance in direct dehydrogenation processes.

                    综合理解与未来展望: 最近在直接脱氢过程中增强支撑金属催化剂催化稳定性的进展为研究和开发开辟了新的途径。10Zn0.1Pt/HZ催化剂的非传统设计，其锌含量更高，铂含量更低，违背了规范，表明催化剂设计的范式转变，重点不仅在于贵金属，还在于双金属组成的协同效应\[10.1039/C9CY00237E\]。通过Al2O3上的Al3+penta位点稳定Pt-Sn簇突出了支持修改在增强稳定性和可再生性方面的重要性\[10.1016/j.jechem.2021.06.002\]。此外，Ga-Pt/S-1沸石纳米催化剂用于丙烯选择性和再生稳定性，指出了沸石封装催化剂在解决制造挑战和优化性能方面的潜力\[10.1016/j.jcis.2021.02.129\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在探索新的双金属组成、开发先进的支撑材料以及优化沸石封装催化剂，以增强直接脱氢过程中的稳定性和性能。
