<English>
The deactivation mechanisms of metal oxide catalysts, specifically Cr-based and VOx, in oxidative dehydrogenation processes are multifaceted and complex. CrOx/silica catalysts, for instance, experience rapid deactivation due to coke deposition and the reduction of CrOx, converting the coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ state into the inactive Cr2+ state \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. This deactivation is exacerbated by the formation of coke precursors from the abstraction of hydrogen from allylic positions in higher alkanes, leading to decreased alkene yields \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. For VOx catalysts, deactivation is observed with increasing VOx content above the theoretical monolayer coverage, resulting in decreased ODH activity \[10.1002/ejic.201701049\].

Regeneration strategies for these catalysts include reoxidation with air, CO2, or O2. Reoxidation with air or CO2 at high temperatures (550-650 degrees Celsius) is effective in recovering most of the activity and selectivity of Cr-based catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. However, achieving a complete recovery of active sites is challenging and requires well-dispersed Cr species \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. For VOx catalysts, CO2 addition in the feed gas of C3H8/O2 can mitigate overoxidation by decreasing surface molecular oxygen species and partially reoxidizing VOx catalysts, converting V2O3 to V2O4 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. However, CO2 cannot fully reoxidize VOx catalysts to V2O5 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

The addition of CO2 in oxidative dehydrogenation processes has been shown to enhance the selectivity towards propylene production and reduce carbon formation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. CO2 can re-oxidize partially reduced metal oxides, suppress the formation of surface carbon, and allow the reaction to proceed via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. However, the effectiveness of CO2 addition depends on the catalyst support, with strong basic sites leading to increased acidity and coke formation \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature highlights the intricate nature of deactivation mechanisms and regeneration strategies for Cr-based and VOx catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation processes. The potential of CO2 addition for mitigating overoxidation and enhancing selectivity towards propylene production presents a promising avenue for future research. However, the specific details of how CO2 addition achieves these effects, particularly in relation to the catalyst support, remain to be fully elucidated. Future research endeavors might focus on developing a deeper understanding of the redox mechanisms involved in the entire reaction network, including CO2ODHP, DHP, and RWGS \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. Additionally, the exploration of novel catalyst supports that can optimize the benefits of CO2 addition while minimizing negative effects such as increased acidity and coke formation is a critical area for investigation. The development of advanced preparation methods that ensure well-dispersed Cr species and the use of additives like MgO to reduce coke formation could also significantly enhance the performance of these catalysts \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. Overall, the field is poised for innovative advancements that could lead to more efficient and sustainable oxidative dehydrogenation processes.

金属氧化物催化剂，特别是Cr基和VOx催化剂，在氧化脱氢过程中的失活机制是多方面的且复杂的。例如，CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂由于焦炭沉积和CrOx的还原而迅速失活，将配位不饱和的Cr3+状态转化为不活跃的Cr2+状态\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。这种失活因从较高烷烃的烯丙位中抽氢形成焦炭前体而加剧，导致烯烃产量下降\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。对于VOx催化剂，当VOx含量超过理论单层覆盖度时，观察到失活，导致ODH活性下降\[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]。

这些催化剂的再生策略包括使用空气、CO2或O2进行再氧化。在高温(550-650摄氏度)下使用空气或CO2进行再氧化在恢复Cr基催化剂的大部分活性和选择性方面是有效的\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。然而，实现活性位点的完全恢复具有挑战性，需要确保Cr物种的均匀分散\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。对于VOx催化剂，C3H8/O2进料气体中CO2的添加可以通过减少表面分子氧物种并部分再氧化VOx催化剂，将V2O3转化为V2O4，从而缓解过度氧化\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。然而，CO2无法将VOx催化剂完全再氧化为V2O5\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。

氧化脱氢过程中CO2的添加已被证明可以提高对丙烯生产的选择性并减少碳形成\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。CO2可以再氧化部分还原的金属氧化物，抑制表面碳的形成，并允许反应通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后跟逆向水煤气变换反应进行\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。然而，CO2添加的有效性取决于催化剂载体，强碱性位点会导致酸性增加和焦炭形成\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：文献强调了Cr基和VOx催化剂在氧化脱氢过程中失活机制和再生策略的复杂性。CO2添加在缓解过度氧化和提高对丙烯生产的选择性方面的潜力为未来的研究提供了一条有希望的途径。然而，CO2添加如何实现这些效果的具体细节，特别是与催化剂载体的关系，仍有待充分阐明。未来的研究工作可能集中在对整个反应网络中涉及的氧化还原机制的更深入理解上，包括CO2ODHP、DHP和RWGS\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。此外，探索可以优化CO2添加的好处同时最小化负面影响（如酸性增加和焦炭形成）的新型催化剂载体是一个关键的研究领域。开发确保Cr物种均匀分散的先进制备方法以及使用MgO等添加剂来减少焦炭形成，也可能显著提高这些催化剂的性能\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。总体而言，该领域正准备进行创新性进展，这可能导致更高效和可持续的氧化脱氢过程。
