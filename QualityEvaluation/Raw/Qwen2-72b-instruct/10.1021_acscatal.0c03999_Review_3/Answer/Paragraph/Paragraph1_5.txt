<English>
The deactivation mechanisms of metal oxide catalysts, particularly Cr-based and VOx, in oxidative dehydrogenation processes are multifaceted and complex. CrOx/silica catalysts, for instance, suffer from rapid deactivation due to coke deposition and the reduction of CrOx, which converts the coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ state into the inactive Cr2+ state \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. The deactivation of VOx/SiO2 catalysts is accelerated with increasing VOx content above the theoretical monolayer coverage, leading to a decrease in catalytic activity and selectivity to propylene \[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]. The primary causes of deactivation for Cr-based catalysts include coke deposition and the reduction of active sites, while for VOx catalysts, it involves the sintering of vanadium oxide species and the formation of larger V2O5 crystallites or mixed oxides such as AlVO4 \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

Regeneration strategies for these catalysts involve reoxidation with air, CO2, or O2. Reoxidation with air or CO2 at high temperatures (550-650 degrees Celsius) is effective in recovering most of the activity and selectivity of Cr-based catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. For VOx catalysts, the addition of CO2 in the feed gas of C3H8/O2 can mitigate overoxidation by decreasing surface molecular oxygen species and partially reoxidizing VOx catalysts, converting V2O3 to V2O4 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. However, achieving a complete recovery of active sites is challenging and requires proper preparation methods that ensure well-dispersed Cr species \[10.1021/cr5002436\].

The effectiveness of CO2 addition for mitigating overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions is suggested by the literature, as CO2 can re-oxidize partially reduced metal oxides, suppress the formation of surface carbon, and enhance the selectivity towards propylene production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. Reoxidation methods using CO2 are effective in regenerating the catalyst but cannot completely reoxidize it to its V2O5 state, only to V2O4 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. The presence of WOx hinders the clustering of VOx species during the reaction, preventing the formation of non-selective oxygen, which reduces the probability of CO2 formation and thus total oxidation of propane \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The literature highlights the intricate nature of deactivation mechanisms and regeneration strategies for Cr-based and VOx catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation processes. The potential of CO2 addition for mitigating overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions opens up new avenues for performance optimization. Future research might focus on developing novel preparation methods for Cr-based catalysts that ensure well-dispersed Cr species, enhancing the effectiveness of regeneration strategies. Additionally, the exploration of alternative regeneration methods that can fully reoxidize VOx catalysts to their V2O5 state could lead to significant improvements in catalytic performance. The role of CO2 in suppressing the formation of surface carbon and allowing the reaction to proceed via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction suggests a promising direction for mitigating overoxidation and enhancing selectivity. The investigation of the effect of different catalyst supports on CO2 adsorption and its impact on catalytic performance could provide insights into the design of more efficient catalysts. Moreover, the development of catalysts with reduced environmental concerns, such as the use of Cr, and the exploration of alternative metals with similar catalytic properties could pave the way for more sustainable oxidative dehydrogenation processes.

<Chinese>
金属氧化物催化剂，特别是Cr基和VOx催化剂，在氧化脱氢过程中的失活机制是多方面的和复杂的。例如，CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂由于焦炭沉积和CrOx的还原而迅速失活，这将配位不饱和的Cr3+状态转化为不活跃的Cr2+状态\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。VOx/SiO2催化剂的失活随着VOx含量超过理论单层覆盖度而加速，导致催化活性和丙烯选择性下降\[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]。Cr基催化剂的主要失活原因包括焦炭沉积和活性位点的还原，而VOx催化剂则涉及五氧化二钒物种的烧结和形成更大的V2O5结晶体或如AlVO4的混合氧化物\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

这些催化剂的再生策略涉及使用空气、CO2或O2进行再氧化。在高温(550-650摄氏度)下使用空气或CO2对Cr基催化剂的活性和选择性恢复大多数是有效的\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。对于VOx催化剂，通过在C3H8/O2进料气体中添加CO2可以减少过度氧化，通过减少表面分子氧物种并部分再氧化VOx催化剂，将V2O3转化为V2O4\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。然而，完全恢复活性位点具有挑战性，需要确保Cr物种良好分散的适当制备方法\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。

文献表明，CO2添加在缓解VOx催化反应中的过度氧化方面具有潜力，因为CO2可以再氧化部分还原的金属氧化物，抑制表面碳的形成，并增强对丙烯生产的选择性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。使用CO2的再氧化方法在再生催化剂方面是有效的，但不能完全将其再氧化到V2O5状态，只能到V2O4\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。WOx的存在阻碍了反应过程中VOx物种的聚集，防止了非选择性氧的形成，这减少了CO2的形成概率，从而减少了丙烷的完全氧化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：文献强调了Cr基和VOx催化剂在氧化脱氢过程中失活机制和再生策略的复杂性。CO2添加在缓解VOx催化反应中的过度氧化方面的潜力为性能优化开辟了新的途径。未来的研究可能集中在开发确保Cr物种良好分散的Cr基催化剂新型制备方法，以增强再生策略的有效性。此外，探索能够将VOx催化剂完全再氧化到V2O5状态的替代再生方法可能会显著提高催化性能。CO2在抑制表面碳的形成和允许反应通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后跟逆向水煤气变换反应进行方面的作用，表明了缓解过度氧化和提高选择性的有希望的方向。研究不同催化剂载体对CO2吸附的影响及其对催化性能的影响，可能为设计更有效的催化剂提供见解。此外，开发减少环境问题的催化剂，如使用Cr，以及探索具有类似催化性质的替代金属，可能为更可持续的氧化脱氢过程铺平道路。
