<English>
The deactivation mechanisms of metal oxide catalysts, particularly Cr-based and VOx, in oxidative dehydrogenation processes are multifaceted, involving coke deposition and the reduction of active sites \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. CrOx/silica catalysts, for instance, suffer from rapid deactivation due to coke deposition and the conversion of coordinatively unsaturated Cr3+ to inactive Cr2+ \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. For VOx catalysts, deactivation is exacerbated by increased VOx content above monolayer coverage, leading to reduced surface reoxidation and decreased ODH activity \[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]. The addition of a small amount of O2 can decrease the deactivation rate of CrOx/silica catalysts but significantly reduces the selectivity for C3H6 \[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]. Reoxidation methods using air or CO2 at high temperatures (550-650 degrees Celsius) are effective in recovering most of the activity and selectivity of Cr-based catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. However, achieving a complete recovery of active sites is challenging without proper preparation methods that ensure well-dispersed Cr species \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. For VOx catalysts, CO2 addition in the feed gas of C3H8/O2 can mitigate overoxidation by decreasing surface molecular oxygen species and partially reoxidizing VOx catalysts from V2O3 to V2O4 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. The effectiveness of various regeneration methods, such as reoxidation with air, CO2, or O2, is not directly compared in terms of their effectiveness, temperature requirements, and impact on catalytic performance \[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]. However, CO2 addition can enhance the selectivity towards propylene production and reduce carbon formation in propane dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The deactivation of metal oxide catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation processes, particularly Cr-based and VOx, is a complex issue that requires a multifaceted approach for mitigation. The potential of CO2 addition for mitigating overoxidation in VOx-catalyzed reactions and enhancing the selectivity towards propylene production presents a promising avenue for future research \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. The use of CO2 can also suppress the formation of surface carbon and allow the reaction to proceed via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. However, the effect of CO2 depends strongly on the catalyst support, and if the basic sites are too strong, CO2 can irreversibly adsorb and poison these sites, increasing the acidity of the support and increasing coke formation \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. Future research might focus on developing catalysts with optimized basic sites that can effectively utilize CO2 for regeneration while minimizing coke formation. Additionally, the development of preparation methods that ensure well-dispersed Cr species could lead to a complete recovery of active sites in Cr-based catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]. The use of MgO can also improve the performance of supported vanadium catalysts by reducing coke formation \[10.1021/cr5002436\]. These findings suggest that there is a need for further research on the development of novel catalysts and regeneration methods that can effectively mitigate deactivation mechanisms and optimize catalytic performance in oxidative dehydrogenation processes.

<Chinese>
金属氧化物催化剂，特别是Cr基和VOx催化剂，在氧化脱氢过程中的失活机制是多方面的，涉及焦炭沉积和活性位点的还原\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。例如，CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂由于焦炭沉积和从配位不饱和的Cr3+转化为不活跃的Cr2+而迅速失活\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。对于VOx催化剂，当VOx含量超过单层覆盖时，失活加剧，导致表面再氧化减少和ODH活性下降\[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]。添加少量O2可以降低CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂的失活速率，但显著降低了C3H6的选择性\[10.1002/cctc.201200397\]。使用空气或CO2在高温(550-650摄氏度)下的再氧化方法在恢复Cr基催化剂的大部分活性和选择性方面是有效的\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。然而，如果没有确保Cr物种良好分散的适当制备方法，完全恢复活性位点是具有挑战性的\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。对于VOx催化剂，C3H8/O2进料气体中CO2的添加可以通过减少表面分子氧物种和将VOx催化剂从V2O3部分再氧化为V2O4来缓解过度氧化\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。各种再生方法，如使用空气、CO2或O2的再氧化，在其有效性、温度要求和对催化性能的影响方面没有直接比较\[10.1002/ejic.201701049\]。然而，CO2的添加可以提高丙烷脱氢中丙烯的产率并减少碳的形成\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：金属氧化物催化剂在氧化脱氢过程中的失活，特别是Cr基和VOx催化剂，是一个复杂的问题，需要多方面的方法来缓解。CO2添加在缓解VOx催化反应中的过度氧化和提高丙烯产率方面的潜力为未来的研究提供了一个有希望的方向\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。CO2的使用也可以抑制表面碳的形成，并允许反应通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后跟逆水煤气变换反应进行\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。然而，CO2的效果强烈依赖于催化剂载体，如果碱性位点太强，CO2可以不可逆地吸附并毒害这些位点，增加载体的酸性并增加焦炭的形成\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。未来的研究可能集中在开发具有优化碱性位点的催化剂，这些催化剂可以有效地利用CO2进行再生，同时最小化焦炭的形成。此外，确保Cr物种良好分散的制备方法的发展可以导致Cr基催化剂中活性位点的完全恢复\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_3\]。MgO的使用也可以通过减少焦炭的形成来改善支撑的钒催化剂的性能\[10.1021/cr5002436\]。这些发现表明，需要进一步研究开发新型催化剂和再生方法，以有效缓解失活机制并优化氧化脱氢过程中的催化性能。
