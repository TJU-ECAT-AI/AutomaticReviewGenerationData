However, it does mention that the design of high performance cathode electrocatalysts is essential for polymer electrolyte fuel cells, and that the enhancement of electrocatalytic activity of Pt by alloying with Fe results in a maximum activity at about 50% Fe content, which is 25 times higher than pure Pt activity.
