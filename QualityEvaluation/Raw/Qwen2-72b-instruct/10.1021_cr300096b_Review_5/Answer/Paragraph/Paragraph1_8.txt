                    The thermodynamic stability and structural dynamics of bimetallic catalysts, particularly in Pt-based systems, are significantly influenced by reaction conditions, as evidenced by studies on Pt3dPt subsurface structures \[10.1007/s11244-010-9447-2\]. These structures are stable in vacuum and with hydrogen but segregate under oxygen, with PtNiPt exhibiting the greatest resistance and PtTiPt being the most susceptible. This stability trend, confirmed on single crystal and polycrystalline Pt, correlates with d-band center differences, enabling bimetallic alloy screening. Activation barriers for oxygen-induced segregation vary by crystal facet, with PtNiPt showing the highest resistance and PtTiPt being the most susceptible.

                    Surface segregation in Pt-based bimetallic catalysts, notably Pt-3d alloys, critically influences catalytic activities, enhancing oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) performance compared to pure Pt \[10.1016/j.susc.2008.11.036\]. This phenomenon significantly alters catalytic properties, with surface segregation under adsorbates playing a key role in activity improvement. Remarkably, the ORR is uniquely active on the Pt-skin surface of Pt3Co alloys, with reaction rates four times higher than pure Pt. Studying Pt alloys under surface adsorption is essential for optimizing catalytic applications, as it affects surface composition and electronic structure, determining catalytic properties.

                    Pt基双金属催化剂的热力学稳定性和结构动力学在很大程度上受到反应条件的影响，这一点在Pt3dPt亚表面结构的研究中得到了证明\[10.1007/s11244-010-9447-2\]。这些结构在真空和氢气中是稳定的，但在氧气下会发生分层，其中PtNiPt表现出最强的抵抗力，而PtTiPt是最容易受到影响的。这一稳定性趋势在单晶和多晶Pt上得到证实，与d带中心的差异相关，这使得双金属合金的筛选成为可能。氧气诱导的分层的激活势垒因晶面而异，PtNiPt显示出最高的抵抗力，而PtTiPt是最容易受到影响的。

                    在Pt基双金属催化剂中，特别是在Pt-3d合金中，表面分层对催化活性有重要影响，与纯Pt相比，它提高了氧还原反应（ORR）的性能\[10.1016/j.susc.2008.11.036\]。这一现象显著改变了催化性质，表面分层在活性改善中起着关键作用。值得注意的是，Pt3Co合金的Pt-皮肤表面上的ORR是独一无二的活跃，反应速率是纯Pt的四倍。在表面吸附下研究Pt合金对于优化催化应用至关重要，因为它影响表面组成和电子结构，决定了催化性质。
