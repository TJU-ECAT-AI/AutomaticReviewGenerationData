This layer shields the alloy from corrosion, enhancing its stability and catalytic properties in fuel cell applications.

In Pt-3d bimetallic catalysts, 3d metals enhance surface adsorption, stabilizing Pt-skin surfaces, particularly under oxygen-covered conditions \[10.1021/jp8046888\].
