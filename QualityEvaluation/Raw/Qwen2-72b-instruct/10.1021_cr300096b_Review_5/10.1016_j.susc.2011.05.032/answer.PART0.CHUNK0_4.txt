<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The thermodynamic preferences and reaction conditions can influence the stability, surface segregation, and structural configuration of bimetallic catalysts, particularly in Pt-based systems such as Pt-3d alloys. The extent of Pt surface segregation to the outermost layer of these Pt3Ni/M (111) surfaces can be affected by the addition of a third transition metal element M. For instance, replacing Ni with transition metals V, Fe, Co, Mo, Tc, Ru, W, Re, Os, and Ir would lead to even stronger Pt segregation extent to the surface, while replacing Ni with transition metals Ag, Au, and Pd could completely prohibit Pt segregating to the surface. The consequent implications for their catalytic properties and performance in various applications include enhanced catalytic activity for oxygen reduction reaction (ORR) in fuel cells, which is critical for the performance of polymer electrolyte membrane fuel cells (PEMFC).

1.热力学偏好和反应条件可以影响双金属催化剂的稳定性、表面分层和结构配置，特别是在基于铂的系统中，如Pt-3d合金。添加第三过渡金属元素M可以影响这些Pt3Ni/M (111)表面上铂向最外层的表面分层程度。例如，用过渡金属V、Fe、Co、Mo、Tc、Ru、W、Re、Os和Ir取代Ni会导致铂向表面的分层程度更强，而用过渡金属Ag、Au和Pd取代Ni则可能完全阻止铂向表面分层。这对它们在各种应用中的催化性质和性能的影响包括在燃料电池中增强对氧还原反应（ORR）的催化活性，这对聚合物电解质膜燃料电池（PEMFC）的性能至关重要。
