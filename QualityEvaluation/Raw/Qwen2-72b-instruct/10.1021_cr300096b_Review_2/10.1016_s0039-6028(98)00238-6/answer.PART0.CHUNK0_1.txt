1.The literature does not provide information on the characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces synthesized by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. However, it does discuss the study of ultrathin metal overlayers on W(111) by high-resolution photoelectron spectroscopy using synchrotron radiation. The bimetallic systems are prepared by adsorbing approximately one monolayer of K, Co, Ni, Pd, Pt, Cu, Ag and Au on W(111) at 100 K. The W 4f7/2 core levels from the W(111) substrate are measured. The characterization of these bimetallic surfaces is crucial for understanding the electronic and chemical properties of the interfaces, which is important for surface science and catalysis research.
