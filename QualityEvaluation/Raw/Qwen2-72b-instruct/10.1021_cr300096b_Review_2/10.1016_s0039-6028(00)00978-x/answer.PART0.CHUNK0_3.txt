1.The literature does not provide information on the characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces synthesized by depositing a Ni monolayer on Pt(111) single crystal or polycrystalline Pt substrates. However, it does discuss the preparation of a model bimetallic surface by depositing Pt atoms onto a Ni(100) surface as a substrate, which is an alternative approach to understand the fundamental properties and reactivity of PtNi bimetallic surfaces. This process is crucial for structural modeling, DFT calculations, and our comprehensive understanding of the electronic and chemical properties of these bimetallic surfaces in the context of advanced surface science and catalysis research because it allows for the correlation of changes in surface structure and related electronic states with chemical reactivity upon the addition of a second constituent.
