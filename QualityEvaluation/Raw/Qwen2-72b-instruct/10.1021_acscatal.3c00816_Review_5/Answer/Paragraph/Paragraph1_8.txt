    Hierarchical zeolites, characterized by the integration of micropores with mesopores/macropores, significantly enhance the accessibility for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. This structural feature improves metal dispersion through defect sites, thereby outperforming conventional zeolites in reactions involving bulky molecules due to better reactant accessibility and reduced metal aggregation \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. Synthesis methods for these materials involve the use of structure directing agents and high-temperature crystallization, creating pore structures that boost catalytic efficiency \[10.3390/catal9020174\].

    The integration of micropores with mesopores/macropores in hierarchical zeolites enhances accessibility for PDH reactions without compromising confinement effects \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Synthesis methods include desilication, self-assembly, bifunctional surfactants, and cotemplates such as TPAOH/TBPOH \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Types of hierarchical zeolites, including mesoporous, 2D/layered, and pillared structures, improve PDH performance with higher surface areas, weaker acidity, and better stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. This leads to increased propane conversion and propylene selectivity. Intermetallic compounds form more readily on hierarchical structures, boosting PDH performance and stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Higher silanol concentration in hydrothermal-synthesized hierarchical zeolites extends catalytic lifetime \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Enhanced metal-support interactions due to the hierarchical structure result in higher dispersion and electron density over metal sites, improving PDH performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\].

    Hierarchical zeolites, combining micropores with meso- and/or macropores, enhance accessibility for PDH reactions by providing unimpeded transport paths and improved micropore accessibility without sacrificing intrinsic catalytic activity \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. These materials, with micro-mesoporous, micro-macroporous, and micro-meso-macroporous structures, show potential in catalytic reactions, including PDH \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. Synthesis methods include in situ creation of microporous and mesoporous products with or without secondary hard or soft templates, and postsynthetic modification of as-synthesized zeolites to introduce a hierarchical structure \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. Hierarchical zeolites improve PDH reaction performance by enhancing mass transport properties, activity, selectivity, and cycle time, making them superior to traditional zeolites \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\].

    TS-1@SBA-16, a composite material combining TS-1 zeolite with mesoporous SBA-16 silica, was synthesized to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. This hierarchical structure boosts PDH activity, achieving higher propane conversion rates than TS-1 or SBA-16 alone \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The superior catalytic performance is attributed to the synergistic effect of the hierarchical porous structure, suitable acid acidity, and the electronic effect of Ti species \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\].

    层状沸石，通过将微孔与介孔/大孔结合，显著提高了丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这种结构特征通过缺陷位点改善了金属分散，因此在涉及大分子的反应中，与传统沸石相比，具有更好的反应物可及性和减少的金属聚集，从而表现出更优的性能\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这些材料的合成方法涉及结构导向剂的使用和高温结晶，形成提高催化效率的孔结构\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。

    层状沸石通过将微孔与介孔/大孔结合，提高了PDH反应的可及性，同时不损害固有的限制效应\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。合成方法包括脱硅、自组装、双功能表面活性剂，以及TPAOH/TBPOH等共模板\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。层状沸石的类型，包括介孔、二维/层状和柱状结构，通过提高表面积、减弱酸性、和更好的稳定性，改善了PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。这导致了丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性的增加。在层状结构上，金属间化合物更容易形成，从而提高了PDH性能和稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。在水热合成的层状沸石中，较高的硅醇浓度延长了催化寿命\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。由于层状结构，金属-载体相互作用的增强导致金属位点上更高的分散度和电子密度，从而改善了PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

    结合微孔与介孔和/或大孔的层状沸石，通过提供无障碍的传输路径和改善微孔的可及性，同时不牺牲固有的催化活性，提高了PDH反应的可及性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。这些材料，具有微-介孔、微-大孔和微-介-大孔结构，在催化反应中显示出潜力，包括PDH\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。合成方法包括在有或没有二次硬或软模板的情况下，原位创建微孔和介孔产品，以及对合成沸石进行后合成改性，以引入层状结构\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。层状沸石通过增强质量传输特性、活性、选择性和循环时间，改善了PDH反应性能，使其优于传统沸石\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。

    TS-1@SBA-16，一种结合TS-1沸石与介孔SBA-16二氧化硅的复合材料，被合成以增强PDH反应的可及性\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。这种层状结构提高了PDH活性，实现了比TS-1或SBA-16更高的丙烷转化率\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。优越的催化性能归因于层状多孔结构、合适的酸酸度和Ti物种的电子效应的协同效应\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。
