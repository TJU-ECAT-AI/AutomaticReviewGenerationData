<English>
Hierarchical zeolites, characterized by the integration of mesopores/macropores with micropores, significantly enhance the accessibility and efficiency of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. This unique structure prevents metal aggregation, thereby boosting catalytic selectivity towards propylene. Synthesis methods typically involve the use of structure directing agents and high-temperature crystallization processes \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. Compared to conventional zeolites, hierarchical zeolites offer superior performance in reactions involving bulky molecules, as evidenced by improved catalytic efficiency in various processes \[10.3390/catal9020174\].

The synthesis of hierarchical zeolites encompasses a range of techniques, including desilication, self-assembly, the use of bifunctional surfactants, and cotemplating with tetrapropylammonium hydroxide (TPAOH) and tetrabutylphosphonium hydroxide (TBPOH) \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. These methods result in materials with higher surface areas, weaker acidity, and improved stability, which are crucial for enhancing PDH reactions. The hierarchical structure facilitates intermetallic compound formation, contributing to superior PDH performance and stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Hydrothermal synthesis, in particular, leads to higher silanol concentration, which boosts the catalytic lifetime \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\].

Main types of hierarchical zeolites include micro-mesoporous, micro-macroporous, and micro-meso-macroporous structures \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. Synthesis methods are categorized into in situ and postsynthetic approaches. The in situ method involves the fabrication of microporous and mesoporous products with or without secondary hard or soft templates \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. The postsynthetic approach introduces a hierarchical structure post-treatment of as-synthesized zeolites \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. These hierarchical zeolites improve PDH reaction performance by enhancing mass transport properties, increasing activity and selectivity, and optimizing cycle time \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\].

A notable example is the TS-1@SBA-16 composite material, which combines TS-1 zeolite with mesoporous SBA-16 silica \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. This hierarchical structure boosts PDH activity, achieving higher propane conversion rates than TS-1 or SBA-16 alone \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The superior performance is attributed to the synergistic effect of the hierarchical porous structure, suitable acid acidity, and the electronic effect of titanium (Ti) species \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in hierarchical zeolite synthesis and their applications in PDH reactions open up new avenues for catalytic research. Future directions might focus on the development of novel synthesis methods that can further enhance the control over pore size distribution and metal dispersion. Additionally, the exploration of new hierarchical structures that can optimize the balance between micropore confinement effects and meso/macropore accessibility could lead to more efficient catalysts. The integration of computational modeling with experimental studies could also provide deeper insights into the structure-performance relationships of hierarchical zeolites, guiding the design of next-generation catalysts for PDH and other reactions involving bulky molecules.

<Chinese>
层次状沸石，通过将介孔/大孔与微孔结合，显著提高了丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性和效率\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这种独特的结构防止了金属聚集，从而提高了对丙烯的催化选择性。合成方法通常涉及结构导向剂的使用和高温结晶过程\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。与传统沸石相比，层次状沸石在涉及大分子的反应中表现出更优的性能，如在各种过程中改进的催化效率所证明\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。

层次状沸石的合成涵盖了多种技术，包括脱硅、自组装、使用双功能表面活性剂，以及与四丙基铵氢氧化物（TPAOH）和四丁基膦氢氧化物（TBPOH）的共模板化\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。这些方法产生了具有更高表面积、较弱酸性和改进稳定性的材料，这对于增强PDH反应至关重要。层次结构促进了金属间化合物的形成，对PDH性能和稳定性做出了贡献\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。特别是，水热合成导致更高的硅醇浓度，从而提高了催化寿命\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

主要类型的层次状沸石包括微介孔、微大孔和微介大孔结构\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。合成方法分为原位和后合成方法。原位方法涉及在有或没有二级硬模板或软模板的情况下制备微孔和介孔产品\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。后合成方法在合成沸石后引入层次结构\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。这些层次状沸石通过增强质量传输特性、提高活性和选择性以及优化循环时间来改善PDH反应性能\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。

一个值得注意的例子是TS-1@SBA-16复合材料，它将TS-1沸石与介孔SBA-16二氧化硅结合\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。这种层次结构提高了PDH活性，实现了比TS-1或SBA-16更高的丙烷转化率\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。其优越性能归因于层次多孔结构、适宜的酸酸度和钛（Ti）物种的电子效应的协同作用\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望: 层次状沸石合成及其在PDH反应中的应用的进展为催化研究开辟了新的途径。未来方向可能集中在开发新的合成方法，以进一步增强对孔径分布和金属分散的控制。此外，探索新的层次结构，以优化微孔限制效应与介孔/大孔可及性之间的平衡，可能会导致更有效的催化剂。将计算建模与实验研究相结合，也可能为层次状沸石的结构-性能关系提供更深入的见解，指导下一代催化剂的设计，用于PDH和其他涉及大分子的反应。
