<English>
    Hierarchical zeolites, by integrating micropores with mesopores/macropores, significantly enhance the accessibility and efficiency of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. These materials boost catalytic efficiency by improving metal dispersion and preventing metal aggregation, crucial for PDH reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. The synthesis of hierarchical zeolites involves structure directing agents and high-temperature crystallization processes \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. Main types include micro-mesoporous, micro-macroporous, and micro-meso-macroporous structured zeolites, which offer better reactant accessibility and increased active metal dispersion \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\].

    The integration of intrinsic micropores with mesoporous/macroporous networks in hierarchical zeolites enhances micropore accessibility for PDH reactions without compromising confinement effects \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Synthesis methods include desilication, self-assembly, bifunctional surfactants, and cotemplating with TPAOH and TBPOH \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. These zeolites offer higher surface areas, weaker acidity, and improved stability, boosting propane conversion and propylene selectivity \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. The hierarchical structure facilitates intermetallic compound formation, enhancing PDH performance and stability \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Higher silanol concentration in hydrothermal-synthesized hierarchical zeolites results in superior catalytic lifetime \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Enhanced metal-support interactions lead to higher dispersion and electron density over metal sites, improving PDH performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\].

    A novel composite material, TS-1@SBA-16, synthesized by enwrapping TS-1 zeolite with mesoporous SBA-16 silica, combines micropores and mesopores to enhance PDH reaction accessibility \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The 10% TS-1 content catalyst showed peak PDH activity, achieving higher propane conversion than TS-1 or SBA-16-supported catalysts \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The superior performance of Pt-SnOx/TS-1@SBA-16 is attributed to its hierarchical porous structure, suitable acid acidity, and Ti species' electronic effect, facilitating mass transfer and catalytic efficiency \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\].

    ※※※
    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Hierarchical zeolites, with their unique pore structures, have revolutionized the field of catalysis, particularly in PDH reactions. The synthesis methods, including desilication, self-assembly, and cotemplating, offer versatile routes to tailor the hierarchical structure for optimized performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. The enhanced metal-support interactions and the synergistic effect of hierarchical porous structures, suitable acid acidity, and Ti species' electronic effect, as seen in the case of TS-1@SBA-16, highlight the potential for further advancements \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. Future research endeavors might focus on developing novel synthesis strategies to create hierarchical zeolites with tailored pore architectures for specific catalytic applications, including PDH reactions. Additionally, exploring the role of different metal species and their interactions with hierarchical zeolites could lead to the discovery of new catalysts with enhanced selectivity and stability.

<Chinese>
    层状沸石通过结合微孔和介孔/大孔，显著提高了丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性和效率\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这些材料通过改善金属分散和防止金属聚集，提高了催化效率，对PDH反应至关重要\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。层状沸石的合成涉及结构导向剂和高温结晶过程\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。主要类型包括微-介孔、微-大孔和微-介-大孔结构的沸石，提供了更好的反应物可及性和增加的活性金属分散\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。

    层状沸石通过将内在微孔与介孔/大孔网络集成，增强了PDH反应的微孔可及性，而不损害限制效应\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。合成方法包括脱硅、自组装、双功能表面活性剂和与TPAOH和TBPOH的共模板\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。这些沸石提供了更高的表面积、较弱的酸性，和改进的稳定性，提高了丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。层状结构促进了金属间化合物的形成，增强了PDH性能和稳定性\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。水热合成的层状沸石中较高的硅醇浓度导致了优越的催化寿命\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。增强的金属-载体相互作用导致金属位点上更高的分散和电子密度，改善了PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

    一种新型复合材料TS-1@SBA-16，通过将TS-1沸石与介孔SBA-16二氧化硅包裹，结合微孔和介孔，增强了PDH反应的可及性\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。10% TS-1含量的催化剂显示了峰值PDH活性，实现了比TS-1或SBA-16支持的催化剂更高的丙烷转化率\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。Pt-SnOx/TS-1@SBA-16的卓越性能归因于其层状多孔结构、合适的酸酸度和Ti物种的电子效应，促进了质量传递和催化效率\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。

    ※※※
    综合理解与未来展望: 层状沸石，以其独特的孔结构，已经彻底改变了催化领域，特别是在PDH反应中。合成方法，包括脱硅、自组装和共模板，提供了多样的途径来定制层状结构以优化性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。增强的金属-载体相互作用和TS-1@SBA-16中层状多孔结构、合适的酸酸度和Ti物种电子效应的协同效应，突显了进一步发展的潜力\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新型合成策略，以创建具有定制孔架构的层状沸石，用于特定的催化应用，包括PDH反应。此外，探索不同金属物种及其与层状沸石的相互作用，可能会导致发现具有增强选择性和稳定性的新型催化剂。
