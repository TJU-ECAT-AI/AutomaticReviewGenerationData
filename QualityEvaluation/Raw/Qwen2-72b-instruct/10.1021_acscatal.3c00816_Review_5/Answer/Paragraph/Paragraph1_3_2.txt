Hierarchical zeolites, by integrating mesopores/macropores with micropores, significantly enhance the accessibility and catalytic efficiency for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. These materials, characterized by their hierarchical structures, outperform conventional zeolites in reactions involving bulky molecules due to improved metal dispersion and catalytic efficiency \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. Synthesis methods typically involve the use of structure directing agents and high-temperature crystallization processes \[10.3390/catal9020174\].

The synthesis of hierarchical zeolites can be achieved through various techniques, including desilication, self-assembly, the use of bifunctional surfactants, and cotemplating with TPAOH and TBPOH \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. These methods result in materials with higher surface areas, weaker acidity, and improved stability, which are crucial for PDH reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. The hierarchical structure facilitates the formation of intermetallic compounds, boosts silanol concentration, and strengthens metal-support interactions, leading to higher propane conversion, propylene selectivity, and enhanced PDH performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\].

In situ and postsynthetic approaches are common synthesis methods for hierarchical zeolites \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. The in situ method involves the creation of microporous and mesoporous products with or without secondary hard or soft templates during zeolite synthesis, while the postsynthetic approach modifies as-synthesized zeolites to introduce a hierarchical structure \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. These zeolites improve PDH reaction performance by enhancing mass transport properties, increasing activity and selectivity, and reducing cycle time \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\].

A notable example is TS-1@SBA-16, a composite material combining TS-1 zeolite with mesoporous SBA-16 silica \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. This hierarchical structure, featuring micropores and mesopores, led to a catalyst with a 10% TS-1 content showing the highest PDH activity \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The initial propane conversion was notably higher than over TS-1 or SBA-16-supported catalysts, and the superior catalytic performance is attributed to the synergistic effect of the hierarchical porous structure, suitable acid acidity, and electronic effect of Ti species \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in hierarchical zeolite synthesis and their application in PDH reactions have opened up new avenues for catalytic efficiency enhancement. The future of PDH catalyst development may focus on the optimization of synthesis methods to achieve more precise control over pore size and distribution, aiming to further improve metal dispersion and accessibility. Additionally, the exploration of novel structure directing agents and templating strategies could lead to the creation of hierarchical zeolites with tailored properties for specific catalytic reactions. The integration of computational modeling and machine learning techniques could also accelerate the discovery of new hierarchical zeolite structures and their potential applications in PDH and other catalytic processes.

层次状沸石通过将介孔/大孔与微孔结合，显著提高了丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性和催化效率\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这些材料，以其层次结构为特征，由于金属分散和催化效率的提高，在涉及大分子的反应中优于传统沸石\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。合成方法通常涉及结构导向剂的使用和高温结晶过程\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。

层次状沸石的合成可以通过多种技术实现，包括脱硅、自组装、使用双功能表面活性剂，以及与TPAOH和TBPOH共模板\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。这些方法产生了具有更高表面积、较弱酸性和改进稳定性的材料，这对PDH反应至关重要\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。层次结构促进了金属间化合物的形成，提高了硅醇浓度，加强了金属-支撑物的相互作用，导致更高的丙烷转化率、丙烯选择性和增强的PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

原位和后合成方法是层次状沸石常见的合成方法\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。原位方法涉及在沸石合成过程中使用或不使用二次硬模板或软模板创建微孔和介孔产品，而后合成方法修改合成沸石以引入层次结构\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。这些沸石通过增强质量传输特性、提高活性和选择性、减少循环时间来改善PDH反应性能\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。

一个显著的例子是TS-1@SBA-16，一种结合TS-1沸石与介孔SBA-16二氧化硅的复合材料\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。这种具有微孔和介孔的层次结构导致了10% TS-1含量的催化剂显示出最高的PDH活性\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。初始丙烷转化率明显高于TS-1或SBA-16支撑的催化剂，而优越的催化性能归因于层次多孔结构、合适的酸酸度和Ti物种电子效应的协同效应\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。

综合理解与未来展望: 层次状沸石合成的进展及其在PDH反应中的应用为催化效率的提高开辟了新的途径。PDH催化剂开发的未来可能集中在优化合成方法，以实现对孔径和分布的更精确控制，旨在进一步改善金属分散和可及性。此外，探索新型结构导向剂和模板策略可能导致具有针对特定催化反应的定制特性的层次状沸石的创建。计算建模和机器学习技术的整合也可能加速新层次状沸石结构的发现及其在PDH和其他催化过程中的潜在应用。
