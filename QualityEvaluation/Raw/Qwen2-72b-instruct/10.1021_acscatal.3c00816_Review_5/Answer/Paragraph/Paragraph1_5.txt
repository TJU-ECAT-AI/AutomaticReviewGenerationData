<English>
    Hierarchical zeolites, by virtue of their integrated micropore and meso/macropore networks, significantly enhance the accessibility and efficiency of propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. These materials, characterized by their unique pore structures, prevent metal aggregation and improve metal dispersion, crucial factors for enhancing catalytic selectivity to propylene \[10.3390/catal9020174\]. The hierarchical architecture facilitates the formation of intermetallic compounds and strengthens metal-support interactions, leading to higher dispersion and electron density over metal sites, thus boosting PDH performance \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. 

    Synthesis methods for hierarchical zeolites are diverse, encompassing desilication, self-assembly, and the use of bifunctional surfactants and cotemplates like TPAOH and TBPOH \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. These approaches enable the creation of zeolites with higher surface areas, weaker acidity, and improved stability, which are pivotal for achieving higher propane conversion and propylene selectivity in PDH reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]. Hydrothermal synthesis, in particular, yields hierarchical zeolites with higher silanol concentration, ensuring superior catalytic lifetime \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\].

    The in situ synthesis method creates hierarchical zeolites by fabricating microporous and mesoporous products with or without secondary hard or soft templates \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. The postsynthetic approach modifies as-synthesized zeolites to introduce a hierarchical structure, often involving the use of surfactants, including cationic polymers and organosilanes, in soft templating to create mesopores \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]. These synthesis strategies significantly improve the utilization effectiveness of zeolite materials, minimizing energy, time, and raw materials consumption \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\].

    A novel composite material, TS-1@SBA-16, combines TS-1 zeolite with mesoporous SBA-16 silica, integrating micropores and mesopores to enhance PDH reaction accessibility \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. This catalyst, optimized at 10% TS-1 content, achieves higher propane conversion rates than TS-1 or SBA-16 alone, thanks to its hierarchical porous structure, suitable acid acidity, and Ti species' electronic effect \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]. The Pt-SnOx/TS-1@SBA-16 catalyst's superior catalytic performance is attributed to these synergistic effects \[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\].

    ※※※
    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in hierarchical zeolite synthesis and their applications in PDH reactions open up new avenues for catalytic research. Future directions might focus on the development of novel synthesis methods that can tailor the hierarchical structure to specific catalytic needs, such as optimizing the size and distribution of meso/macropores for enhanced mass transport and accessibility. Additionally, the exploration of new metal-support interactions and intermetallic compounds could lead to the discovery of more efficient and stable catalysts for PDH and other reactions. The integration of computational modeling with experimental approaches could also provide deeper insights into the structure-performance relationships of hierarchical zeolites, guiding the design of next-generation catalysts. Moreover, the investigation of sustainable synthesis routes, such as using renewable resources and minimizing waste, could contribute to the development of greener and more efficient catalytic processes.

<Chinese>
    层状沸石通过其独特的微孔和介/大孔网络的结合，显著提高了丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性和效率\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。这些材料的特殊孔结构防止了金属聚集，改善了金属分散，这是提高催化选择性至丙烯的关键因素\[10.3390/catal9020174\]。层状结构促进了金属间化合物的形成，增强了金属-载体相互作用，导致金属位点上更高的分散度和电子密度，从而增强了PDH性能\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

    层状沸石的合成方法多样，包括脱硅、自组装以及使用双功能表面活性剂和TPAOH、TBPOH等共模板剂\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。这些方法使沸石具有更高的表面积、较弱的酸性和更好的稳定性，这对于PDH反应中实现更高的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性至关重要\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。特别是水热合成，产生具有更高硅醇浓度的层状沸石，确保了更优异的催化寿命\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5\]。

    原位合成方法通过使用或不使用二次硬或软模板来制造微孔和介孔产品，创建层状沸石\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。后合成方法通过引入层状结构来修改合成沸石，通常涉及使用包括阳离子聚合物和有机硅烷在内的表面活性剂在软模板中创建介孔\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。这些合成策略显著提高了沸石材料的利用效率，减少了能源、时间和原材料的消耗\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016\]。

    一种新型复合材料TS-1@SBA-16，将TS-1沸石与介孔SBA-16二氧化硅结合，整合微孔和介孔以增强PDH反应的可及性\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。这种催化剂在10% TS-1含量时，比单独的TS-1或SBA-16实现了更高的丙烷转化率，归功于其层状多孔结构、适宜的酸性酸度和Ti物种的电子效应\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。Pt-SnOx/TS-1@SBA-16催化剂的卓越催化性能归因于这些协同效应\[10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7\]。

    ※※※
    综合理解与未来展望: 层状沸石合成的进展及其在PDH反应中的应用为催化研究开辟了新的途径。未来的研究方向可能集中在开发新型合成方法，以根据特定的催化需求定制层状结构，例如优化介/大孔的大小和分布，以增强质量传递和可及性。此外，探索新的金属-载体相互作用和金属间化合物可能发现更高效、更稳定的PDH和其他反应催化剂。将计算建模与实验方法的结合也可以为层状沸石的结构-性能关系提供更深入的见解，指导下一代催化剂的设计。此外，对可持续合成路线的调查，如使用可再生资源和减少废物，可以为开发更绿色、更高效的催化过程做出贡献。
