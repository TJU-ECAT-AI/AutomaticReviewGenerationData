<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The review discusses the catalytic conversion of n-butane by oxidative dehydrogenation as an alternative to direct dehydrogenation, focusing on catalytic systems, kinetic studies, and proposed reaction mechanisms.
2.Special attention is given to the hydrocarbon activation process and factors governing catalytic efficiency.
3.Various catalysts are discussed, including unsupported catalysts, supported catalysts, and promoters.
4.Factors affecting both activity and selectivity are explored, including catalyst composition, operating conditions, catalyst reducibility, oxygen reactivity and availability, nature of oxygen species, electrical conductivity, surface basicity, and other factors.
5.The nature of active and selective sites is also considered.
6.Kinetic studies and reaction mechanisms are analyzed.
7.Conclusions and future trends are summarized.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1.该综述讨论了n-丁烷通过氧化脱氢的催化转化作为直接脱氢的替代方案，重点关注催化系统、动力学研究和提出的反应机制。
2.特别关注烃类的活化过程和影响催化效率的主要因素。

5.还考虑了活性和选择性位点的性质。
6.分析了动力学研究和反应机制。
7.总结了结论和未来趋势。
