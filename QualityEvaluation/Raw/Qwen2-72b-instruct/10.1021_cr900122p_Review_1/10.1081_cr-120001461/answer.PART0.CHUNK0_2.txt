<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The review discusses the catalytic conversion of n-butane by oxidative dehydrogenation as an alternative to direct dehydrogenation, focusing on catalytic systems, kinetic studies, and proposed reaction mechanisms.
2.Special attention is given to the hydrocarbon activation process and factors influencing catalytic efficiency.
3.Various catalysts are considered, including unsupported catalysts, supported catalysts, and promoters.
4.Catalyst composition, operating conditions, reducibility, oxygen reactivity and availability, nature of oxygen species, electrical conductivity, surface basicity, and other factors affect activity and selectivity.
5.The nature of active and selective sites is also explored.
6.Kinetic studies and reaction mechanisms are analyzed to understand the process better.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1.该综述讨论了n-丁烷通过氧化脱氢的催化转化作为直接脱氢的替代方案，重点关注催化系统、动力学研究和提出的反应机制。
2.特别关注的是烃类的活化过程以及影响催化效率的主要因素。
3.考虑了各种催化剂，包括无载体催化剂、有载体催化剂和促进剂。
4.催化剂组成、操作条件、还原性、氧气反应性和可利用性、氧物种的性质、电导率、表面碱性以及其他因素影响活性和选择性。
5.还探讨了活性和选择性位点的性质。
6.通过动力学研究和反应机制分析，以更好地理解该过程。
