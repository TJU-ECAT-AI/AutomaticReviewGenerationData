<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The review discusses the catalytic conversion of n-butane by oxidative dehydrogenation as an alternative to direct dehydrogenation, focusing on catalytic systems, kinetic studies, and proposed reaction mechanisms.
2.Special attention is given to the hydrocarbon activation process and factors influencing catalytic efficiency.
3.Both unsupported and supported catalysts are considered, along with the use of promoters.
4.Catalyst composition, operating conditions, reducibility, oxygen reactivity and availability, nature of oxygen species, electrical conductivity, surface basicity, and other factors are discussed.
5.The nature of active and selective sites is also explored.
6.Kinetic studies and reaction mechanisms, including C-H bond activation, M-alkyl intermediates, and 2H-abstraction, are analyzed.
7.All these factors collectively influence the process efficiency, with site isolation playing a role in addressing selectivity issues.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

1.该综述讨论了n-丁烷通过氧化脱氢的催化转化作为直接脱氢的替代方案，重点关注催化系统、动力学研究和提出的反应机制。
2.特别关注烃类的活化过程和影响催化效率的主要因素。
3.考虑了无载体和有载体的催化剂，以及促进剂的使用。
4.讨论了催化剂组成、操作条件、还原性、氧的反应性和可用性、氧物种的性质、催化剂的电导率、表面碱性以及其他因素。
5.还探讨了活性和选择性位点的性质。
6.分析了动力学研究和反应机制，包括C-H键活化、M-烷基中间体和2H-抽提。
7.所有这些因素共同影响过程效率，位点隔离在解决选择性问题中发挥作用。
