However, it does mention that ZSM-5 zeolite, which has an MFI topology, is used as a support for platinum catalysts in PDH due to its unique physical properties, such as a special three-dimensional channel and adjustable Si-Al ratio. These properties make ZSM-5 an interesting material for applications as a catalyst carrier in PDH reactions. The literature also notes that the capacity of catalysts supported on ZSM-5 zeolite to accommodate coke is much better than that of γ-Al2O3 supported catalysts, possibly due to the relatively large surface area and particular channel character of ZSM-5.
