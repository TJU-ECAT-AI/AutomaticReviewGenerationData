                    The synthesis methods for metal-zeolite catalysts used in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions are diverse, with postsynthesis methods like ion exchange and impregnation, and direct synthesis introducing metal sites during zeolite crystallization \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]. MFI and BEA topological zeolites dominate in PDH applications due to their facile synthesis procedure, tunable acidity, and moderate pore size \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]. ZSM-5 zeolite, with an MFI topology, is favored in PDH catalysts due to its three-dimensional channel structure and adjustable Si-Al ratio, enhancing its suitability as a catalyst carrier \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Its superior coke accommodation capacity compared to γ-Al2O3 supports is attributed to its large surface area and channel characteristics \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Incorporating metal promoters, such as zinc, improves reaction selectivity by neutralizing support acidity \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. The method of promoter addition influences the final properties of the support and metallic phases \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Sn-Beta zeolites, featuring single-site Sn atoms in the BEA framework, are key in catalytic biomass conversion and PDH reactions \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. The Lewis acid sites from single-site Sn facilitate dehydrogenation, while Brnsted acid sites cause cracking \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. A high L/B ratio, indicative of more Lewis acid sites, enhances PDH efficiency \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Sn-Beta zeolites are synthesized through a two-step post-synthesis process: dealumination of H-Beta and Sn incorporation via impregnation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Dealumination efficiency is crucial for subsequent Sn incorporation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. High HNO3 concentration aids Al removal, creating lattice defects for Sn incorporation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. The crystalline structure and pore architecture are preserved post-Sn incorporation, with Sn species tetrahedrally coordinated into the framework \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Sn incorporation alters the acidity, creating Lewis acid sites crucial for PDH \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Na-Sn-Beta zeolites with high Lewis acidity and low B/L ratio show optimal PDH performance \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. Theoretical calculations suggest direct dehydrogenation pathways are more feasible \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. A mesoporous MFI zeolite synthesized using a multi-ammonium surfactant as a structure-directing agent has gained attention for its hierarchical micro-/mesoporous structure, which boosts catalytic performance by facilitating reactant and product diffusion and enhancing metal nanoparticle dispersion \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. This structure, featuring ultrathin zeolite frameworks and interconnected mesopores, supports the discovery of not only REE-based but also other transition metal-based alloy catalysts, expanding opportunities in catalytic applications for PDH reactions \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\].

Direct synthesis methods that introduce metal sites during zeolite crystallization may also be refined to achieve better integration of metal species with the zeolite structure. The exploration of new metal promoters, beyond zinc and tin, could expand the range of active sites available for PDH reactions. The synthesis of Sn-Beta zeolites could be further optimized to improve dealumination efficiency and Sn incorporation, potentially leading to enhanced catalytic performance.                     金属-沸石催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的合成方法多种多样，包括后合成方法如离子交换和浸渍，以及直接合成在沸石结晶过程中引入金属位点\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]。MFI和BEA拓扑沸石在PDH应用中占据主导地位，因为它们的合成程序相对简单，酸度可调，孔径适中\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]。ZSM-5沸石，具有MFI拓扑结构，在PDH催化剂中受到青睐，因为其三维通道结构和可调的Si-Al比例，增强了其作为催化剂载体的适用性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。与γ-Al2O3载体相比，其优越的焦炭容纳能力归因于其较大的表面积和通道特性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。加入金属促进剂，如锌，通过中和载体酸性提高反应选择性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。促进剂添加方法影响最终载体和金属相的性质\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。Sn-Beta沸石，其BEA框架中具有单个Sn原子，在催化生物质转化和PDH反应中起关键作用\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。来自单个Sn原子的Lewis酸位促进脱氢，而Brnsted酸位导致裂解\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。较高的L/B比，表明更多的Lewis酸位，提高了PDH效率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。Sn-Beta沸石通过两步后合成过程合成：H-Beta的脱铝和Sn通过浸渍引入\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。脱铝效率对于后续Sn引入至关重要\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。高浓度HNO3有助于Al的去除，为Sn引入创造晶格缺陷\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。Sn引入后，结晶结构和孔结构得以保留，Sn物种以四面体方式配位到框架中\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。Sn引入改变了酸性，创建了对PDH至关重要的Lewis酸位\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。具有高Lewis酸性和低B/L比的Na-Sn-Beta沸石显示出最佳PDH性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。理论计算表明直接脱氢途径更可行\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。使用多铵表面活性剂作为结构导向剂合成的介孔MFI沸石因其分层微/介孔结构而受到关注，该结构通过促进反应物和产物扩散以及增强金属纳米粒子分散来提高催化性能\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。这种结构，具有超薄沸石框架和相互连接的介孔，支持REE基和其他过渡金属合金催化剂的发现，为PDH反应的催化应用开辟了机会\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。
