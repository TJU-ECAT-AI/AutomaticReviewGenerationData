                    The synthesis of metal-zeolite catalysts for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions, with a focus on MFI and BEA topological zeolites, reveals their dominance due to unique structural characteristics and synthesis methods \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. ZSM-5 zeolite, with an MFI topology, is favored for its three-dimensional channel and adjustable Si-Al ratio, making it an effective support for platinum catalysts. Its superior coke accommodation capacity over γ-Al2O3 is attributed to its large surface area and channel structure. Incorporating metal promoters, such as zinc, enhances reaction selectivity by neutralizing support acidity \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Sn-Beta zeolites, featuring single-site Sn atoms in the BEA framework, are significant in PDH reactions due to their Lewis acid sites, crucial for dehydrogenation, and Brnsted acid sites responsible for cracking \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. A mesoporous MFI zeolite, synthesized using a multi-ammonium surfactant, has gained attention for its unique structure featuring ultrathin frameworks and interconnected mesopores, enhancing catalytic performance in PDH reactions by facilitating reactant and product diffusion and increasing metal nanoparticle dispersion \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. Metal-zeolite catalysts for PDH are synthesized using post-transition metals (Sn, Zn, Ga, In, Fe) and pre-transition metals (Ce, La, Y) as co-catalysts, with the latter offering specific catalytic properties and easy regeneration, though synthesis is challenging \[10.1016/j.ces.2023.118450\]. Cerium substitution into ZSM-5 zeolite modifies its morphologies and textural properties, affecting metallic particle dispersion and enhancing the interaction with Sn species, leading to higher amounts of Sn in oxidative states, stabilizing active sites and improving reaction activity and stability in PDH reactions \[10.1039/C6RA04173F\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in metal-zeolite catalyst synthesis for PDH reactions, particularly focusing on MFI and BEA topological zeolites, suggest a promising direction for future research. The unique structural characteristics of these zeolites, such as their pore geometry and acidity, significantly influence activity, selectivity, and coke formation in PDH reactions. The synthesis methods, including postsynthesis and direct synthesis, offer opportunities for optimizing metal loading, developing hierarchical pore structures for improved mass transfer, and adjusting Si/Al ratios to prevent coke deposits. Future PDH catalyst design may benefit from exploring the use of Ce-modified ZSM-5 zeolites as supports for bimetallic PtSn catalysts to improve reaction activity and stability. Additionally, the development of alloy catalysts, including REE-based and transition metal-based alloys, using MFI and BEA zeolites as supports, opens new avenues for catalytic applications. Theoretical calculations and experimental findings suggest that direct dehydrogenation pathways are more feasible on Sn-Beta zeolites, indicating potential for developing advanced PDH catalysts for propylene production. Tailoring zeolite properties, such as increased thermal stability and reduced acidity, could lead to more stable and selective PDH catalysts in the future. The use of multi-ammonium surfactants for synthesizing mesoporous MFI zeolites with ultrathin frameworks and interconnected mesopores offers a novel approach for enhancing catalytic performance in PDH reactions. These insights offer potential for developing advanced PDH catalysts with improved selectivity and stability.

                    MFI和BEA拓扑结构的金属-沸石催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的合成,揭示了它们由于独特的结构特性和合成方法而占据主导地位\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。ZSM-5沸石,具有MFI拓扑结构,因其三维通道和可调的Si-Al比例而受到青睐,这使其成为铂催化剂的有效载体。它在γ-Al2O3上的优越的焦炭容纳能力可归因于其大表面积和通道结构。通过加入金属促进剂,如锌,通过中和载体酸性来提高反应选择性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。Sn-Beta沸石,在BEA框架中具有单个Sn原子,在PDH反应中具有重要意义,因为它们的Lewis酸位点对于脱氢至关重要,而Brnsted酸位点负责裂解\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。使用多铵表面活性剂合成的介孔MFI沸石因其独特的结构而受到关注,该结构具有超薄框架和相互连接的介孔,通过促进反应物和产物的扩散以及增加金属纳米粒子的分散性,在PDH反应中增强了催化性能\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。PDH的金属-沸石催化剂使用后过渡金属(Sn, Zn, Ga, In, Fe)和前过渡金属(Ce, La, Y)作为共催化剂,后者提供特定的催化性能和易于再生,尽管合成具有挑战性\[10.1016/j.ces.2023.118450\]。铈取代ZSM-5沸石修改了其形态和纹理性质,影响了金属粒子的分散,并增强了与Sn物种的相互作用,导致氧化态Sn物种的量增加,稳定了活性位点,并提高了PDH反应的反应活性和稳定性\[10.1039/C6RA04173F\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望:在PDH反应中,特别是在MFI和BEA拓扑结构的金属-沸石催化剂合成方面的进展,为未来的研究指明了有希望的方向。这些沸石的独特结构特性,如它们的孔几何形状和酸性,对PDH反应中的活性、选择性和焦炭形成有重大影响。合成方法,包括后合成和直接合成,为优化金属负载、开发改进质量转移的分级孔结构以及调整Si/Al比例以防止焦炭沉积提供了机会。未来的PDH催化剂设计可以从探索使用Ce改性的ZSM-5沸石作为双金属PtSn催化剂的载体以提高反应活性和稳定性中受益。此外,使用MFI和BEA沸石作为载体开发合金催化剂,包括REE基和过渡金属基合金,为催化应用开辟了新的途径。理论计算和实验发现表明,直接脱氢途径在Sn-Beta沸石上更可行,表明有可能为丙烯生产开发先进的PDH催化剂。通过调整沸石性能,如提高热稳定性并降低酸性,可以导致未来更稳定和选择性的PDH催化剂。使用多铵表面活性剂合成具有超薄框架和相互连接介孔的介孔MFI沸石为在PDH反应中增强催化性能提供了一种新颖的方法。这些见解为开发具有改进选择性和稳定性的先进PDH催化剂提供了可能性。
