                    The synthesis methods for metal-zeolite catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions are diverse, encompassing postsynthesis techniques such as ion exchange and impregnation, as well as direct synthesis where metal sites are introduced during zeolite crystallization \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]. Among these, MFI and BEA topological zeolites dominate due to their facile synthesis procedure, tunable acidity, and moderate pore size, which are crucial for catalyst effectiveness \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]. ZSM-5 zeolite, with its MFI topology, is favored in PDH due to its unique three-dimensional channel and adjustable Si-Al ratio, enhancing its suitability as a catalyst carrier \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Its superior coke accommodation capacity over γ-Al2O3-supported catalysts is attributed to its large surface area and channel character \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Incorporating metal promoters, such as zinc, improves reaction selectivity by neutralizing acidity, affecting the final properties of the support and metallic phases \[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]. Sn-Beta zeolites, featuring single-site Sn atoms in the BEA framework, are effective in PDH reactions due to their Lewis acid sites, which facilitate dehydrogenation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. A high L/B ratio is beneficial for PDH, as it increases the dehydrogenation rate relative to cracking \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. The Na-Sn-Beta-30 catalyst, with the highest Lewis acidity and lowest B/L ratio, shows the best PDH performance \[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]. A mesoporous MFI zeolite, synthesized using a multi-ammonium surfactant, has gained attention for its unique structure featuring ultrathin frameworks and interconnected mesopores, enhancing catalytic performance by facilitating reactant and product diffusion and increasing metal nanoparticle dispersion \[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]. Its use as a support enables the discovery of alloy catalysts, including REE-based and transition metal-based alloys, expanding opportunities in catalytic applications \[10.1038/s41586-2020-2671-4\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in metal-zeolite catalysts for PDH reactions, particularly the dominance of MFI and BEA topological zeolites, suggest a promising direction for future catalyst design. The synthesis methods, structural characteristics, and performance factors of these catalysts offer insights into optimizing metal loading, developing hierarchical pore structures for improved mass transfer, and adjusting Si/Al ratios to prevent coke deposits and improve stability \[10.1039/D0CS00814A\]. The effectiveness of MFI and BEA zeolites in PDH reactions, attributed to their structural characteristics, facilitates propane adsorption and activation while inhibiting coke and side reactions. This implies potential for further optimization of zeolite structures and incorporation of other active metals to improve selectivity and stability. Future research endeavors might focus on exploring emerging fields and innovative methodologies, such as the development of stable and regenerable pre-transition element alloy catalysts, expanding the potential scope of PDH industrial catalysts \[10.1016/j.ces.2023.118450\].

                    金属-沸石催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的合成方法多样，包括后合成技术如离子交换和浸渍，以及直接合成，其中金属位点在沸石结晶过程中引入\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]。其中，MFI和BEA拓扑结构的沸石由于其简便的合成程序、可调的酸性和适中的孔径，在催化剂有效性方面占据主导地位\[10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_2\]。ZSM-5沸石，具有MFI拓扑结构，在PDH中受到青睐，因其独特的三维通道和可调的Si-Al比，增强了其作为催化剂载体的适用性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。它对焦炭的容纳能力优于γ-Al2O3支持的催化剂，归因于其较大的表面积和通道特性\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。加入金属促进剂，如锌，通过中和酸性提高反应选择性，影响支撑体和金属相的最终性质\[10.1016/j.cej.2015.01.008\]。Sn-Beta沸石，其BEA框架中具有单个Sn原子，由于其Lewis酸位点，对PDH反应有效，促进脱氢\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。较高的L/B比对PDH有利，因为它增加了脱氢率与裂解的比率\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。Na-Sn-Beta-30催化剂，具有最高的Lewis酸性和最低的B/L比，显示出最佳的PDH性能\[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019\]。使用多铵表面活性剂合成的介孔MFI沸石因其独特的结构，即超薄框架和相互连接的介孔，而受到关注，这种结构通过促进反应物和产物的扩散以及增加金属纳米粒子的分散，增强了催化性能\[10.1038/s41586-020-2671-4\]。其作为支撑体的使用使合金催化剂的发现成为可能，包括基于REE和过渡金属的合金，为催化应用开辟了新的机会\[10.1038/s41586-2020-2671-4\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: MFI和BEA拓扑结构的沸石在PDH反应中的金属-沸石催化剂中的主导地位，为未来催化剂设计指明了有希望的方向。这些催化剂的合成方法、结构特征和性能因素为优化金属负载、开发改进质量传递的分级孔结构、调整Si/Al比以防止焦炭沉积和提高稳定性提供了见解\[10.1039/D0CS00814A\]。MFI和BEA沸石在PDH反应中的有效性，归因于其结构特征，促进了丙烷的吸附和活化，同时抑制了焦炭和副反应。这表明了进一步优化沸石结构和掺入其他活性金属以提高选择性和稳定性的潜力。未来的研究努力可能集中在探索新兴领域和创新方法，如开发稳定和可再生的前过渡元素合金催化剂，扩大PDH工业催化剂的潜在范围\[10.1016/j.ces.2023.118450\]。
