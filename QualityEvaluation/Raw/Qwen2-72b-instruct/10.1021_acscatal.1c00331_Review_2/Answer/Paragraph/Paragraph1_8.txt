                    The comprehensive effects and mechanisms of hydrogen cofeeding on coke formation, catalyst stability, and reaction dynamics in the propane dehydrogenation (PDH) process are elucidated through a synthesis of findings from multiple studies \[10.1002/anie.201701115\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\], \[10.1039/c3cp50646k\], \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\], \[10.1006/jcat.1996.0361\], \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_2\], and \[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\]. Hydrogen cofeeding suppresses coke formation by maintaining a reducing atmosphere, preventing coke precursor formation, and enhancing hydrogen-assisted dehydrogenation \[10.1002/anie.201701115\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. This results in improved catalyst stability and prolonged catalyst life, enhancing process efficiency and performance by reducing deactivation and regeneration needs \[10.1002/anie.201701115\]. Hydrogen cofeeding also boosts alkene formation by enhancing hydrogen-assisted dehydrogenation, with minimal impact on alkene selectivity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]. It significantly reduces coke formation by hydrogenating coke precursor species and alters coke deposits, promoting smaller, less defective graphite crystallites \[10.1039/c3cp50646k\]. Hydrogen cofeeding raises the energy barrier for the third, undesired cleavage step more than the first two, enhancing selectivity towards alkene formation \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]. It suppresses coke precursor formation, preventing coke buildup and maintaining catalyst activity \[10.1006/jcat.1996.0361\]. Hydrogen cofeeding decreases coke on the support while keeping metal coke levels stable, weakens support acidity, and converts Lewis to Brnsted acid sites, reducing coking \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_2\]. It decreases the total coke amount, especially on the support, promoting softer coke formation \[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights gained from the comprehensive analysis of hydrogen cofeeding in PDH processes suggest a promising avenue for enhancing catalyst performance and process efficiency. The multifaceted role of hydrogen in suppressing coke formation, altering coke deposits, and enhancing alkene selectivity opens up opportunities for developing more efficient and stable catalysts. Future research could focus on optimizing the hydrogen-to-propane ratio to maximize the benefits while minimizing any potential drawbacks. Additionally, exploring the effects of hydrogen cofeeding on different catalyst compositions and reaction conditions could lead to the discovery of novel catalysts with enhanced stability and selectivity. The impact of hydrogen cofeeding on the energy barriers and reaction mechanisms also warrants further investigation to fully understand its role in the PDH process. These advancements could potentially lead to significant improvements in the overall efficiency and performance of PDH processes, contributing to the broader field of catalysis and chemical engineering.

                    通过综合多个研究的发现\[10.1002/anie.201701115\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\], \[10.1039/c3cp50646k\], \[10.1039/c3cp53796j\], \[10.1006/jcat.1996.0361\], \[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_2\], 和\[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\], 深入阐述了氢气共馈在丙烷脱氢(PDH)过程中对焦炭形成、催化剂稳定性和反应动力学的全面影响和机制。氢气共馈通过维持还原气氛，防止焦炭前体形成，增强氢辅助脱氢，抑制焦炭形成\[10.1002/anie.201701115\], \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。这导致催化剂稳定性的提高和催化剂寿命的延长，通过减少失活和再生需求，提高了过程效率和性能\[10.1002/anie.201701115\]。氢气共馈还通过增强氢辅助脱氢，促进烯烃形成，对烯烃选择性影响最小\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.016\]。它通过氢化焦炭前体物种显著减少焦炭形成，改变焦炭沉积，促进形成更小、缺陷更少的石墨结晶体\[10.1039/c3cp50646k\]。氢气共馈提高了第三步，不希望的裂解步骤的能量障碍，超过前两步，增强对烯烃形成的选择性\[10.1039/c3cp53796j\]。它抑制焦炭前体形成，防止焦炭积累，维持催化剂活性\[10.1006/jcat.1996.0361\]。氢气共馈减少了支撑上的焦炭，同时保持金属焦炭水平稳定，削弱支撑酸性，将Lewis转化为Brnsted酸位点，减少焦化\[10.1021/acscatal.1c00331_2\]。它减少了总焦炭量，特别是在支撑上，促进形成更软的焦炭\[10.1007/s11244-011-9708-8\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 从氢气共馈在PDH过程中的全面分析中获得的见解表明，提高催化剂性能和过程效率的前景广阔。氢气在抑制焦炭形成、改变焦炭沉积和增强烯烃选择性方面的多方面作用为开发更高效、更稳定的催化剂开辟了机会。未来的研究可以集中在优化氢气与丙烷的比例，以最大化益处，同时将任何潜在的缺点降至最低。此外，探索氢气共馈对不同催化剂组成和反应条件的影响，可能会发现具有增强稳定性和选择性的新型催化剂。氢气共馈对能量障碍和反应机制的影响也值得进一步研究，以充分理解其在PDH过程中的作用。这些进步可能会显著提高PDH过程的整体效率和性能，为催化和化学工程领域做出贡献。
