                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide showing superior activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.%. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, particularly potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The improved reducibility of Fe2O3 on active carbon, proceeding through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe, contributes to the high catalytic performance among the tested catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. The redox cycle of Fe oxide is likely crucial in CO2-ODHP reactions, as it facilitates the oxidative dehydrogenation process \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. Ceria (CeO2) is a promising support material due to its ability to store and release oxygen, which may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2 and provide lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The combination of Fe and Ni on a ceria support enhances the catalytic activity for CO2-ODHP by promoting the oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts, as well as the availability of oxygen for CO2 activation, are influenced by the properties of CeO2 as a support material \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The Fe3Ni1 catalyst, consisting of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe under reaction conditions, favors oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1002/aic.16670\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the studies suggest that the reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts play a significant role in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Active carbon and ceria (CeO2) have been identified as promising support materials due to their ability to enhance the catalytic activity and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts. The redox cycle of Fe oxide is crucial for the oxidative dehydrogenation process, and the addition of alkaline metal oxides can further enhance the activity of the catalysts. The combination of Fe and Ni on a ceria support has shown promising results in promoting the oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane. Future research efforts should focus on optimizing the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts, as well as exploring the potential of other support materials and bimetallic compositions to enhance the catalytic activity and selectivity for CO2-ODHP reactions. The development of new catalysts with improved performance and stability could lead to more efficient and sustainable processes for the conversion of CO2 to value-added chemicals and fuels.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确定为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出比铬和铜氧化物更优的活性和更慢的失活\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%。添加碱金属氧化物，特别是钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。Fe2O3在活性碳上的还原性提高，从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe，对测试催化剂的高催化性能有贡献\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2-ODHP反应中可能是关键的，因为它促进了氧化脱氢过程\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。CeO2（铈氧化物）由于其储存和释放氧气的能力，可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，并提供晶格氧来抑制焦炭的生成，因此是一种有前景的支撑材料\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe和Ni在CeO2支撑上的组合通过促进丙烷的氧化CH键断裂，增强了CO2-ODHP的催化活性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。CeO2作为支撑材料的性质影响Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性、稳定性和CO2活化所需的氧气的可用性\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Ni1催化剂，由反应条件下的金属Ni和氧化Fe组成，有利于通过CO2-ODHP途径进行氧化C-H键断裂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：研究结果表明，Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料在它们的CO2-ODHP反应性能中起着重要作用。活性碳和CeO2（铈氧化物）由于能够增强Fe氧化物基催化剂的催化活性和稳定性，已被确定为有前景的支撑材料。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环对于氧化脱氢过程至关重要，添加碱金属氧化物可以进一步增强催化剂的活性。Fe和Ni在CeO2支撑上的组合在促进丙烷的氧化CH键断裂方面显示出有希望的结果。未来的研究工作应集中在优化Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和稳定性，以及探索其他支撑材料和双金属组合的潜力，以增强CO2-ODHP反应的催化活性和选择性。开发具有改进性能和稳定性的新型催化剂，可以导致更有效和可持续的CO2转化为高价值化学品和燃料的过程。
