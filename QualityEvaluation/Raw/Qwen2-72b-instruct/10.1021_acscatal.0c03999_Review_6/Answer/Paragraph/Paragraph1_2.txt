                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide showing superior activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.% \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, particularly potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The formation of carbon deposits or gasification of the support after catalytic tests suggests a correlation with the reaction mechanism and the optimization of catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\].

                The reducibility of Fe2O3/AC is notably improved, with its reduction proceeding through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. However, the reducibility of Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts is hindered due to the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. For instance, when Fe2O3 is loaded on γ-Al2O3, a spinel phase FeAl2O3 is formed, which is difficult to reduce; Fe3+ cations are reduced to Fe2+ ions that are strongly stabilized in the MgO matrix \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC contributes to the high catalytic performance among the tested catalysts, and the redox cycle of Fe oxide is likely crucial in CO2ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\].

                Ceria (CeO2) is a favorable oxide support for Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions due to its ability to store and release oxygen, which may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2 and provide lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, exhibits promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway, suggesting that the combination of Fe and Ni on a ceria support can enhance catalytic activity by promoting oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].

                The effects of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane have been explored, with Pt-based bimetallic catalysts outperforming Pd-based catalysts in terms of conversion and stability \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The selectivity of FexNiy catalysts toward CO2-ODHP can be modified by changing the Ni content in the bimetallic composition, with increasing the Ni atomic ratio above 1 altering the selectivity to favor the dry reforming pathway rather than CO2-ODHP \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. CeO2, as a support material, may influence the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions by affecting the reducibility and stability of the catalysts, as well as the availability of oxygen for CO2 activation \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The Fe3Ni1 catalyst, consisting of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe under reaction conditions, favors oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway, indicating the role of reducibility and support materials in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions \[10.1002/aic.16670\].

                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The insights gained from the studies on Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions underscore the pivotal role of reducibility and support materials in catalytic performance. Active carbon emerges as a superior support material, enhancing the activity and stability of iron oxide catalysts. The redox cycle of Fe oxide, particularly on active carbon, is crucial for high catalytic performance. Ceria, with its oxygen storage and release properties, offers a promising alternative as a support material, potentially enabling direct CO bond scission of CO2 and providing lattice oxygen for coke suppression. The combination of Fe and Ni on ceria support enhances the catalytic activity for CO2-ODHP by promoting oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane. Bimetallic interactions, particularly with Pt-based catalysts, show promise in improving conversion and stability. The tunability of selectivity in FexNiy catalysts by altering the Ni content suggests a path for optimizing catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP. Future research should focus on elucidating the specific mechanisms involved in the interaction between reducibility, support materials, and catalytic performance. This includes the development of advanced characterization techniques to monitor the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and composition under reaction conditions. Additionally, the exploration of novel support materials and bimetallic compositions that can further enhance the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts is warranted. The optimization of catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and sustainable processes for the conversion of CO2 to value-added chemicals and fuels.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确认为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢至苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出优于铬和铜氧化物的活性和更慢的失活速率\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所使用的活性碳类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。添加碱金属氧化物，特别是钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。催化测试后形成的碳沉积物或支撑材料的气化表明与CO2-ODHP反应机制和催化系统优化的相关性\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。

                Fe2O3/AC的还原性显著提高，其还原过程从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。然而，Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂的还原性受到Fe氧化物与支撑材料之间强相互作用的阻碍\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。例如，当Fe2O3加载在γ-Al2O3上时，形成难以还原的尖晶石相FeAl2O3；Fe3+离子被还原为在MgO基质中强烈稳定的Fe2+离子\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性对测试催化剂的高催化性能有贡献，Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2ODHP中可能起着关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。

                由于CeO2（铈氧化物）能够储存和释放氧气，这可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，并提供用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧，因此它作为Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的支撑材料是可取的\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，在CO2-ODHP途径中对丙烯表现出有希望的选择性，表明Fe和Ni在铈支撑上的组合可以通过促进丙烷的氧化性CH键断裂来增强催化活性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。

                已经探索了双金属相互作用对丙烷催化还原CO2的影响，Pt基双金属催化剂在转化率和稳定性方面优于Pd基催化剂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。通过改变双金属组成中的Ni含量，FexNiy催化剂对CO2-ODHP的选择性可以被修改，Ni原子比超过1时选择性倾向于支持干重整途径而非CO2-ODHP\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。作为支撑材料，CeO2可能通过影响Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和稳定性以及CO2活化所需的氧气的可用性，影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Ni1催化剂，由反应条件下的金属Ni和氧化Fe组成，有利于通过CO2-ODHP途径进行氧化性C-H键断裂，表明还原性和支撑材料在其CO2-ODHP反应中的性能中起作用\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。

                综合理解与未来展望：从Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的研究中获得的见解强调了还原性和支撑材料在催化性能中的关键作用。活性碳作为铁氧化物催化剂的优越支撑材料出现，提高了其活性和稳定性。Fe氧化物，特别是在活性碳上的氧化还原循环，对于高催化性能至关重要。铈，由于其氧气储存和释放特性，提供了作为支撑材料的有希望的替代方案，可能使CO2的直接CO键断裂成为可能，并提供晶格氧用于焦炭抑制。Fe和Ni在铈支撑上的组合通过促进丙烷的氧化性CH键断裂增强了CO2-ODHP的催化活性。特别是Pt基催化剂的双金属相互作用在提高转化率和稳定性方面显示出前景。通过改变Ni含量调整FexNiy催化剂的选择性表明了优化CO2-ODHP催化系统的路径。未来的研究应集中在阐明还原性、支撑材料和催化性能之间的具体机制上。这包括开发先进的表征技术来监测催化剂结构和组成在反应条件下的动态变化。此外，探索可以进一步增强Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和稳定性的新型支撑材料和双金属组合是值得的。CO2-ODHP催化系统的优化可能最终导致开发更有效和可持续的过程，用于将CO2转化为增值化学品和燃料。
