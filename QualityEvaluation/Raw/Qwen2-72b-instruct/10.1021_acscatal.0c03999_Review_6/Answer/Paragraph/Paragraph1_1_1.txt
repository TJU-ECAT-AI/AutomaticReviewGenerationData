                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide exhibiting the best activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.% \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, particularly potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The formation of carbon deposits or gasification of the support after catalytic tests suggests a correlation with the reaction mechanism and the optimization of catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\].

                The reducibility of Fe2O3/AC is notably improved, with its reduction proceeding through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. However, the reducibility of Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts is hindered due to the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. For instance, when Fe2O3 is loaded on γ-Al2O3, a spinel phase FeAl2O3 is formed, which is difficult to reduce; Fe3+ cations are reduced to Fe2+ ions that are strongly stabilized in the MgO matrix \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. The enhanced reducibility of Fe2O3 on AC contributes to the high catalytic performance among the tested catalysts, and the redox cycle of Fe oxide is likely crucial in CO2ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\].

                Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support for Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions due to its ability to store and release oxygen, which may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2, while also providing available lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway, suggesting that the combination of Fe and Ni on a ceria support can enhance the catalytic activity for CO2-ODHP by promoting the oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].

                The effects of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane have been investigated, revealing that Pt-based bimetallic catalysts outperform Pd-based catalysts in terms of conversion and stability \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The selectivity of FexNiy catalysts toward CO2-ODHP can be modified by changing the Ni content in the bimetallic composition, with increasing the Ni atomic ratio above 1 altering the selectivity to favor the dry reforming pathway rather than CO2-ODHP \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. CeO2, as a support material, influences the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions by affecting the reducibility and stability of the catalysts, as well as the availability of oxygen for CO2 activation \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The Fe3Ni1 catalyst, consisting of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe under reaction conditions, favors oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1002/aic.16670\].

                The proposed reaction mechanism involving the formation of potassium ferrite as the active phase is supported by the observed correlation between the surface concentration of potassium and the catalyst activity \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. These findings suggest that the reducibility of the Fe oxide and the nature of the support material play crucial roles in the catalytic performance for CO2-ODHP reactions.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。在二氧化碳存在下，活性碳被确认为苯乙烯脱氢制乙苯最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出最佳活性和较慢的失活速度，相比铬和铜氧化物\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。添加碱金属氧化物，特别是钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。催化测试后形成碳沉积物或支撑材料的气化，表明与反应机制和CO2-ODHP催化系统的优化有关\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。

                Fe2O3/AC的还原性显著提高，其还原过程从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。然而，Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂的还原性受到Fe氧化物与支撑材料之间强相互作用的阻碍\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。例如，当Fe2O3加载在γ-Al2O3上时，形成难以还原的尖晶石相FeAl2O3；Fe3+离子还原为Fe2+离子，这些离子在MgO基质中被强烈稳定\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。Fe2O3在AC上的增强还原性对测试催化剂的高催化性能有贡献，Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2ODHP中可能是关键\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。

                由于CeO2能够存储和释放氧气，这可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，同时提供可用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧，因此它是Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中良好的氧化物支撑材料\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性，表明Fe和Ni在CeO2支撑上的组合可以通过促进丙烷的氧化CH键断裂来增强CO2-ODHP的催化活性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。

                已经研究了双金属相互作用对丙烷催化还原CO2的影响，发现基于Pt的双金属催化剂在转化率和稳定性方面优于基于Pd的催化剂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。通过改变双金属组成中的Ni含量，可以修改FexNiy催化剂对CO2-ODHP的选择性，Ni原子比超过1时，选择性转向有利于干重整途径，而不是CO2-ODHP\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。作为支撑材料，CeO2通过影响催化剂的还原性和稳定性，以及CO2活化所需的氧气的可用性，影响Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。在反应条件下，由金属Ni和氧化Fe组成的Fe3Ni1催化剂有利于通过CO2-ODHP途径进行氧化C-H键断裂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。

                涉及钾铁矿形成作为活性相的建议反应机制，由钾表面浓度与催化剂活性之间的观察到的相关性支持\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。这些发现表明，Fe氧化物的还原性和支撑材料的性质在CO2-ODHP反应的催化性能中起着至关重要的作用。
