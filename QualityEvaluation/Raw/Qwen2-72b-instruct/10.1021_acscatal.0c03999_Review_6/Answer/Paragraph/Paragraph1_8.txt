                    The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide exhibiting the best activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.% \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, such as potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The formation of carbon deposits or gasification of the support after catalytic tests can be attributed to the reaction mechanism and the optimization of catalytic systems for the CO2-ODHP process \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The reducibility of Fe2O3/AC is improved, and its reduction proceeds through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. However, the reducibility of Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts is hindered due to the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support because it has the ability to store/release oxygen and may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2, while also providing available lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts play a significant role in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions, and optimizing these factors can lead to improved catalytic systems for this process \[10.1002/aic.16670\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the studies suggest that the reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts significantly impact their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material, with iron oxide showing the best activity and slower deactivation. The addition of alkaline metal oxides enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon. Ceria (CeO2) is a promising support material due to its ability to store/release oxygen and provide lattice oxygen for coke suppression. The Fe3Ni catalyst shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway. Future research efforts might focus on developing new support materials that can further enhance the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts, as well as exploring the effects of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane. Additionally, optimizing the composition and structure of Fe oxide-based catalysts could lead to improved catalytic activity and stability in CO2-ODHP reactions.

                    Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确定为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出最佳的活性和较慢的失活，与铬和铜氧化物相比\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。添加碱金属氧化物，如钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。催化测试后形成碳沉积物或支撑材料的气化可归因于反应机制和CO2-ODHP过程催化系统的优化\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。Fe2O3/AC的还原性得到改善，其还原过程从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。然而，由于Fe氧化物与支撑材料之间的强烈相互作用，Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂的还原性受到阻碍\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。CeO2是一种良好的氧化物支撑材料，因为它具有储存/释放氧气的能力，可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，同时提供可用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料在它们的CO2-ODHP反应性能中起着重要作用，优化这些因素可以导致这种过程的改进的催化系统\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：研究结果表明，Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料对其在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能有重大影响。活性碳被确定为最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物显示出最佳的活性和较慢的失活。添加碱金属氧化物提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性。CeO2是一种有前景的支撑材料，因为它具有储存/释放氧气的能力，并提供晶格氧用于焦炭抑制。Fe3Ni催化剂通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的支撑材料，以进一步提高Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和稳定性，以及探索双金属相互作用对丙烷催化还原CO2的影响。此外，优化Fe氧化物基催化剂的组成和结构可能导致CO2-ODHP反应中改进的催化活性和稳定性。
