                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide exhibiting the best activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.%. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, particularly potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The improved reducibility of Fe2O3 on active carbon (AC) contributes to the high catalytic performance among the tested catalysts, and the redox cycle of Fe oxide is likely crucial in CO2-ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support due to its ability to store and release oxygen, which may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2 and provide lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway, suggesting that the combination of Fe and Ni on a ceria support can enhance the catalytic activity for CO2-ODHP by promoting the oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts play a significant role in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions, and optimizing these factors can lead to improved catalytic systems for this process \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6, 10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the studies on Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions suggest that the reducibility and support materials play a crucial role in their performance. Active carbon and ceria have been identified as promising support materials due to their ability to enhance the catalytic activity and stability of Fe oxide catalysts. The redox cycle of Fe oxide and the availability of lattice oxygen from ceria are critical for CO2 activation and the oxidative CH bond cleavage of propane. Future research efforts should focus on developing a deeper understanding of the specific mechanisms involved in the CO2-ODHP process and optimizing the catalytic systems for this reaction. This could involve exploring the use of other reducible oxide supports, investigating the effects of different transition metal oxides, and studying the role of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane. Additionally, the development of new catalysts with enhanced reducibility and stability, as well as the optimization of reaction conditions, could lead to improved performance and selectivity in CO2-ODHP reactions.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确认为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最适宜支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出最佳活性和较慢的失活速度，与铬和铜氧化物相比\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%。添加碱金属氧化物，特别是钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。Fe2O3在活性碳(AC)上的还原性提高，对测试催化剂的高催化性能有贡献，Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2-ODHP中可能至关重要\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。CeO2(铈氧化物)是氧化物支撑材料的良好选择，因为它能够储存和释放氧气，这可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，并提供晶格氧以抑制焦炭生成\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性，表明Fe和Ni在铈氧化物支撑上的组合可以通过促进丙烷的氧化性CH键断裂来增强CO2-ODHP的催化活性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料在它们在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能中起着重要作用，优化这些因素可以导致这种过程的催化系统的改进\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7, 10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6, 10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：关于Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的研究结果表明，还原性和支撑材料在其性能中起着至关重要的作用。活性碳和铈氧化物由于能够增强Fe氧化物催化剂的催化活性和稳定性，被确认为有前景的支撑材料。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环和铈氧化物提供的晶格氧对CO2活化和丙烷的氧化性CH键断裂至关重要。未来的研究工作应集中在对CO2-ODHP过程中的具体机制的深入理解以及这种反应的催化系统的优化。这可能涉及探索其他可还原氧化物支撑材料的使用，研究不同过渡金属氧化物的影响，以及研究双金属相互作用在丙烷催化还原CO2中的作用。此外，开发具有增强还原性和稳定性的新催化剂，以及反应条件的优化，可以导致CO2-ODHP反应中性能和选择性的提高。
