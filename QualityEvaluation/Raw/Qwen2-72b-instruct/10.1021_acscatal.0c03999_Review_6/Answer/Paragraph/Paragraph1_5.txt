                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide showing superior activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.% \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, such as potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The formation of carbon deposits or gasification of the support after catalytic tests can be attributed to the reaction mechanism and the optimization of catalytic systems for the CO2-ODHP process \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The reducibility of Fe2O3/AC is improved, and its reduction proceeds through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. However, the reducibility of Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts is hindered due to the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support because it has the ability to store/release oxygen and may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2, while also providing available lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The effects of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane have been discussed, with Pt-based bimetallic catalysts outperforming Pd-based catalysts in terms of conversion and stability \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The selectivity of FexNiy catalysts toward CO2-ODHP can be modified by changing the Ni content in the bimetallic composition \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. CeO2 is described as a readily reducible oxide with the ability to reversibly convert between Ce4+ and Ce3+, which is critical for oxygen exchange properties important for CO2 activation \[10.1002/aic.16670\]. The Fe3Ni1 catalyst, consisting of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe under reaction conditions, favors oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1002/aic.16670\].

                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the studies suggest that the reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts play a significant role in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide showing superior activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.%. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, such as potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1. The formation of carbon deposits or gasification of the support after catalytic tests can be attributed to the reaction mechanism and the optimization of catalytic systems for the CO2-ODHP process. The reducibility of Fe2O3/AC is improved, and its reduction proceeds through Fe2O3 to Fe3O4, FeO, and finally Fe. However, the reducibility of Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3 and hydrotalcite-derived FeMg catalysts is hindered due to the strong interaction between Fe oxide and the support. Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support because it has the ability to store/release oxygen and may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2, while also providing available lattice oxygen for coke suppression. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway. The effects of bimetallic interactions on the catalytic reduction of CO2 by propane have been discussed, with Pt-based bimetallic catalysts outperforming Pd-based catalysts in terms of conversion and stability. The selectivity of FexNiy catalysts toward CO2-ODHP can be modified by changing the Ni content in the bimetallic composition. CeO2 is described as a readily reducible oxide with the ability to reversibly convert between Ce4+ and Ce3+, which is critical for oxygen exchange properties important for CO2 activation. The Fe3Ni1 catalyst, consisting of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe under reaction conditions, favors oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway. Future research efforts could focus on the development of new support materials that enhance the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts, as well as the optimization of the bimetallic composition to improve the selectivity toward CO2-ODHP. Additionally, the investigation of the reaction mechanism and the role of the redox cycle of Fe oxide in CO2-ODHP could provide further insights into the optimization of catalytic systems for this process.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确认为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出比铬和铜氧化物更优的活性和更慢的失活\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。添加碱金属氧化物，如钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。催化测试后形成碳沉积物或支撑材料的气化可归因于反应机制和CO2-ODHP过程催化系统的优化\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。Fe2O3/AC的还原性得到改善，其还原过程从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。然而，由于Fe氧化物与支撑材料之间的强烈相互作用，Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂的还原性受到阻碍\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。CeO2是一种良好的氧化物支撑材料，因为它具有储存/释放氧气的能力，可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，同时提供可用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。已经讨论了双金属相互作用对丙烷催化还原CO2的影响，Pt基双金属催化剂在转化率和稳定性方面优于Pd基催化剂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。FexNiy催化剂对CO2-ODHP的选择性可以通过改变双金属组成中的Ni含量来修改\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。CeO2被描述为一种可逆地在Ce4+和Ce3+之间转换的可还原氧化物，这对CO2激活重要的氧交换性质至关重要\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。Fe3Ni1催化剂，由反应条件下的金属Ni和氧化Fe组成，有利于通过CO2-ODHP途径进行氧化C-H键断裂\[10.1002/aic.16670\]。

                综合理解与未来展望：研究结果表明，Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料在它们的CO2-ODHP反应性能中起着重要作用。活性碳被确认为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出比铬和铜氧化物更优的活性和更慢的失活。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%。添加碱金属氧化物，如钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1。催化测试后形成碳沉积物或支撑材料的气化可归因于反应机制和CO2-ODHP过程催化系统的优化。Fe2O3/AC的还原性得到改善，其还原过程从Fe2O3到Fe3O4，FeO，最后到Fe。然而，由于Fe氧化物与支撑材料之间的强烈相互作用，Fe2O3/γ-Al2O3和水滑石衍生的FeMg催化剂的还原性受到阻碍。CeO2是一种良好的氧化物支撑材料，因为它具有储存/释放氧气的能力，可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，同时提供可用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性。已经讨论了双金属相互作用对丙烷催化还原CO2的影响，Pt基双金属催化剂在转化率和稳定性方面优于Pd基催化剂。FexNiy催化剂对CO2-ODHP的选择性可以通过改变双金属组成中的Ni含量来修改。CeO2被描述为一种可逆地在Ce4+和Ce3+之间转换的可还原氧化物，这对CO2激活重要的氧交换性质至关重要。Fe3Ni1催化剂，由反应条件下的金属Ni和氧化Fe组成，有利于通过CO2-ODHP途径进行氧化C-H键断裂。未来的研究工作可以集中在开发增强Fe氧化物基催化剂还原性和稳定性的新支撑材料，以及优化双金属组成以提高CO2-ODHP的选择性。此外，对反应机制和Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2-ODHP中的作用的调查可以为优化该过程的催化系统提供进一步的见解。
