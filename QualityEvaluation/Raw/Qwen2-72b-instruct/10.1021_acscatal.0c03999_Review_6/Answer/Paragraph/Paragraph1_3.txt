                The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions is significantly influenced by their reducibility and the nature of the support materials \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. Active carbon has been identified as the most suitable support material for the dehydrogenation of ethylbenzene to styrene in the presence of carbon dioxide, with iron oxide showing superior activity and slower deactivation compared to chromium and copper oxides \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The type of active carbon used and the iron content significantly impact the catalytic behavior, with the optimum amount of iron being 5 wt.% \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The addition of alkaline metal oxides, such as potassium, enhances the activity of iron oxide supported on active carbon, with the best alkali metal/Fe atomic ratio being 0.1 \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]. The improved reducibility of Fe2O3 on active carbon (AC) contributes to the high catalytic performance among the tested catalysts, and the redox cycle of Fe oxide is likely crucial in CO2-ODHP reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]. Ceria (CeO2) is a good choice of oxide support because it has the ability to store/release oxygen, which may induce direct CO bond scission of CO2, while also providing available lattice oxygen for coke suppression \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The Fe3Ni catalyst, consisting of oxidized Fe and metallic Ni, shows promising selectivity toward propylene via the CO2-ODHP pathway \[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]. The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts play a significant role in their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions, and optimizing these factors can lead to improved catalytic systems for this process \[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The findings from the studies suggest that the reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts significantly impact their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Active carbon and ceria have been identified as suitable support materials, with ceria's ability to store and release oxygen playing a crucial role in the reaction mechanism. The redox cycle of Fe oxide is also critical, as it influences the catalytic activity and stability. The addition of alkaline metal oxides as promoters can further enhance the activity of the catalysts. Future research efforts might focus on developing new support materials that can improve the reducibility and stability of Fe oxide-based catalysts, as well as optimizing the redox cycle of Fe oxide for enhanced performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. The exploration of bimetallic interactions, such as the combination of Fe and Ni, could also lead to the development of more selective and stable catalysts for CO2-ODHP. Additionally, the investigation of the effects of different iron contents and the role of alkaline metal oxides in the catalytic behavior of Fe oxide-based catalysts could provide further insights into the optimization of catalytic systems for this process.

                Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能受到其还原性和支撑材料性质的显著影响\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。活性碳被确定为在二氧化碳存在下将乙苯脱氢为苯乙烯的最合适的支撑材料，铁氧化物表现出比铬和铜氧化物更优的活性和更慢的失活\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。所用活性碳的类型和铁含量对催化行为有重大影响，铁的最佳含量为5 wt.%\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。添加碱金属氧化物，如钾，提高了铁氧化物在活性碳上的活性，最佳的碱金属/Fe原子比为0.1\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。Fe2O3在活性碳(AC)上的可还原性提高，对测试催化剂的高催化性能有贡献，Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环在CO2-ODHP反应中可能起着关键作用\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6\]。CeO2是一种良好的氧化物支撑材料，因为它具有储存/释放氧气的能力，这可能诱导CO2的直接CO键断裂，同时提供可用于焦炭抑制的晶格氧\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe3Ni催化剂，由氧化Fe和金属Ni组成，通过CO2-ODHP途径对丙烯显示出有希望的选择性\[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w\]。Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料在它们在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能中起着重要作用，优化这些因素可以导致这种过程的催化系统的改进\[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：研究结果表明，Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和支撑材料对它们在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能有重大影响。活性碳和CeO2被确定为合适的支撑材料，CeO2储存和释放氧气的能力在反应机制中起着关键作用。Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环也是关键的，因为它影响催化活性和稳定性。添加碱金属氧化物作为促进剂可以进一步提高催化剂的活性。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发新的支撑材料，以改善Fe氧化物基催化剂的还原性和稳定性，以及优化Fe氧化物的氧化还原循环，以提高CO2-ODHP反应的性能。探索双金属相互作用，如Fe和Ni的组合，也可能导致CO2-ODHP的更选择性和更稳定的催化剂的发展。此外，研究不同铁含量的影响和碱金属氧化物在Fe氧化物基催化剂催化行为中的作用，可以为这种过程的催化系统的优化提供进一步的见解。
