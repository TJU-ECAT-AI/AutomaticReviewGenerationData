<English>
The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, a pivotal process in the petrochemical industry, is significantly influenced by the choice of catalyst and the nature of the oxidant, with CO2 emerging as a promising alternative to traditional oxidants like O2 \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. Redox-active metal oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, employ the Mars-van Krevelen (MvK) mechanism for C-H bond activation, donating oxygen to the alkane to form an alkene and water, with CO2 regenerating the catalyst by refilling lattice oxygen vacancies \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. In contrast, redox-inactive catalysts, such as Ga2O3/MxOy, activate CO2 through heterolytic dissociation into CO and a reactive oxygen species (*O), which dehydrogenates the alkane, but can lead to cracking and coke formation, deactivating the catalyst \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Traditional oxidants like O2 can over-oxidize, whereas CO2 is milder and prevents over-oxidation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\].

V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters are effective for CO2 activation due to their redox properties and oxygen mobility, facilitating CO2 dissociation with varying energy barriers \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. V2O5 has a high barrier for CO2 oxidation, VOx/SiO2 has a lower but still significant barrier, while CeO2 facilitates easier CO2 dissociation due to oxygen vacancies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Transition metal clusters are also active for CO2 dissociation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\].

The activation of the secondary CAH bond of propane is the rate-limiting step, while the re-oxidation of the catalyst with CO2 occurs on a faster timescale \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. CO2 suppresses surface carbon formation, enhancing catalyst stability and activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. DFT calculations confirm that CO2-assisted propane dehydrogenation can proceed via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The utilization of CO2 as an oxidant in oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes presents a sustainable and environmentally friendly approach, particularly when considering the global efforts to mitigate CO2 emissions. The effectiveness of redox-active metal oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, in utilizing CO2 for oxidative dehydrogenation, with CO2 re-oxidizing partially reduced metal oxides, highlights the potential for developing catalysts with enhanced CO2 activation capabilities \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the design of bifunctional catalysts that can efficiently dissociate CO2 into CO and *O, which dehydrogenates alkanes, while minimizing cracking and coke formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. The exploration of novel catalysts with lower energy barriers for CO2 dissociation, such as CeO2 and transition metal clusters, could lead to more efficient and selective oxidative dehydrogenation processes \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Additionally, the integration of CO2 utilization in oxidative dehydrogenation with other CO2 conversion technologies, such as the reverse water gas shift reaction, could enhance the overall efficiency and sustainability of the process \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

<Chinese>
在烷烃的氧化脱氢过程中，催化剂的选择和氧化剂的性质对过程有显著影响，CO2作为一种替代传统氧化剂如O2的有前途的氧化剂，正在烷烃氧化脱氢领域崭露头角\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。红氧化金属氧化物催化剂，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，采用Mars-van Krevelen（MvK）机制激活C-H键，向烷烃供氧形成烯烃和水，CO2通过填充晶格氧空位来再生催化剂\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。相比之下，非红氧化催化剂，如Ga2O3/MxOy，通过异裂CO2成CO和活性氧物种(*O)，脱氢烷烃，但可能导致裂解和焦炭形成，使催化剂失活\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。传统氧化剂如O2可能过度氧化，而CO2更温和，防止过度氧化\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。

V2O5，VOx/SiO2，CeO2和过渡金属簇由于其红氧化性质和氧流动性，对CO2激活有效，促进CO2的解离，能量障碍各不相同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。V2O5对CO2氧化有高障碍，VOx/SiO2有较低但仍然显著的障碍，而CeO2由于氧空位而更容易解离CO2\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。过渡金属簇也对CO2解离活跃\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。

丙烷的次级CAH键的激活是限速步骤，而催化剂与CO2的再氧化在更快的时间尺度上发生\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。CO2抑制表面碳的形成，增强催化剂的稳定性和活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。DFT计算证实，CO2辅助的丙烷脱氢可以通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后跟逆水煤气转换反应进行\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。

※※※
综合理解和未来展望：在烷烃氧化脱氢中使用CO2作为氧化剂提供了一种可持续和环境友好的方法，特别是在全球努力减少CO2排放的背景下。红氧化金属氧化物催化剂，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，在利用CO2进行氧化脱氢方面的有效性，CO2再氧化部分还原的金属氧化物，突显了开发具有增强CO2激活能力的催化剂的潜力\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在设计双功能催化剂上，这些催化剂可以有效地将CO2解离成CO和*O，脱氢烷烃，同时最小化裂解和焦炭形成\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。探索具有较低CO2解离能量障碍的新型催化剂，如CeO2和过渡金属簇，可能会导致更高效和选择性的氧化脱氢过程\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。此外，将CO2利用在氧化脱氢中与其它CO2转换技术，如逆水煤气转换反应，的整合，可以提高过程的整体效率和可持续性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。
