In the oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, CO2 plays a pivotal role as an oxidant, maintaining higher oxidation states of chromium and vanadium species over Cr2O3 and V2O5 catalysts, facilitating catalyst regeneration \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. Redox-active metal oxides, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, employ the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, donating oxygen to alkanes to form alkenes and water, with catalyst regeneration via CO2 reduction \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Redox-inactive catalysts, e.g., Ga2O3/MxOy, activate CO2 through heterolytic dissociation, yielding CO and *O for dehydrogenation, but risk cracking and coke formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Traditional oxidants like O2 can over-oxidize, while CO2 is milder and prevents over-oxidation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Catalysts like V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters are effective for CO2 activation due to redox properties and oxygen mobility, with varying energy barriers for CO2 dissociation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. The oxidized diamond-supported V2O5 catalyst demonstrates high activity for dehydrogenation of C3 and C4 alkanes, highlighting its effectiveness in CO2 activation for oxidative dehydrogenation processes \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\].

The mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration for redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxides are not well-documented in the literature \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. In oxidative dehydrogenation, redox-active oxides activate C-H bonds via the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, forming water and leaving an oxygen vacancy that requires an oxidant for regeneration \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Redox-inactive oxides directly reduce the oxidant without forming a vacancy \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. CO2 as an oxidant can either dissociate directly or be hydrogenated before forming CO \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. V2O5 has a high barrier for CO2 oxidation (3.16 eV), making the process difficult \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. VOx/SiO2 has a lower barrier (1.76 eV) but still significant \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. CeO2 (110) surfaces with oxygen vacancies have a lower barrier (1.36 eV) for CO2 dissociation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Transition metal clusters are highly active for CO2 dissociation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\].

In oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, redox-active metal oxides like V2O5 and VOx/SiO2 activate C-H bonds through redox cycles, with CO2 partially oxidizing the catalyst from V2O3 to V2O4, participating in the conversion of propane to propylene \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. CO2 suppresses surface carbon formation, enhancing catalyst stability and activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. Redox-inactive catalysts, such as CeO2 and transition metal clusters, have less effective CO2 activation due to higher energy barriers for CO2 dissociation \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. DFT calculations confirm CO2-assisted propane dehydrogenation proceeds via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. The activation of the secondary CAH bond of propane is the rate-limiting step, while catalyst re-oxidation with CO2 is faster \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. V2O5 and VOx/SiO2 are more effective in utilizing CO2 for oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, with CO2 re-oxidizing partially reduced metal oxides \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

在烷烃的氧化脱氢反应中，CO2扮演着关键的氧化剂角色，维持Cr2O3和V2O5催化剂上铬和钒物种的高氧化态，促进催化剂再生\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。红氧化金属氧化物，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，采用Mars-van Krevelen机制，向烷烃供氧形成烯烃和水，通过CO2还原实现催化剂再生\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。红氧化性不活跃的催化剂，如Ga2O3/MxOy，通过异裂解离活化CO2，产生CO和*O进行脱氢，但存在裂解和焦炭形成的风险\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。传统氧化剂如O2可能过度氧化，而CO2更温和，防止过度氧化\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。V2O5，VOx/SiO2，CeO2和过渡金属簇等催化剂由于其红氧化性质和氧流动性，对CO2活化有效，CO2解离的能量壁垒各不相同\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。氧化金刚石支撑的V2O5催化剂在C3和C4烷烃脱氢中表现出高活性，突显了其在CO2活化氧化脱氢过程中的有效性\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。

红氧化活性和红氧化性不活跃金属氧化物的C-H键活化和催化剂再生机制在文献中记录不详\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。在氧化脱氢反应中，红氧化活性氧化物通过Mars-van Krevelen机制活化C-H键，形成水并留下需要氧化剂再生的氧空位\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。红氧化性不活跃的氧化物直接还原氧化剂而不形成空位\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。CO2作为氧化剂，可以直接解离或在形成CO前被氢化\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。V2O5对CO2氧化有高壁垒（3.16 eV），使过程变得困难\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。VOx/SiO2有较低的壁垒（1.76 eV），但仍然显著\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。具有氧空位的CeO2（110）表面有较低的壁垒（1.36 eV）用于CO2解离\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。过渡金属簇对CO2解离高度活跃\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。

在烷烃的氧化脱氢反应中，红氧化活性金属氧化物如V2O5和VOx/SiO2通过红氧化循环活化C-H键，CO2部分氧化催化剂从V2O3到V2O4，参与丙烷转化为丙烯的过程\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。CO2抑制表面碳形成，增强催化剂稳定性和活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。红氧化性不活跃的催化剂，如CeO2和过渡金属簇，由于CO2解离的能量壁垒较高，CO2活化效果较差\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。DFT计算证实，CO2辅助的丙烷脱氢通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后逆向水煤气变换反应进行\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。丙烷的次级CAH键活化是速率限制步骤，而催化剂通过CO2的再氧化更快\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。V2O5和VOx/SiO2在利用CO2进行丙烷氧化脱氢方面更有效，CO2再氧化部分还原的金属氧化物\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。
