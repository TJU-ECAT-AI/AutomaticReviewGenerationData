                The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, particularly propane, is a critical process for the production of propylene, a vital petrochemical feedstock. This process is significantly influenced by the choice of catalyst and the oxidant used. Redox-active metal oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, activate C-H bonds via the Mars-van Krevelen (MvK) mechanism, donating oxygen to form alkenes and water, and regenerate through CO2 reduction \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. In contrast, redox-inactive catalysts, like Ga2O3/MxOy, activate CO2 heterolytically into CO and *O, which dehydrogenates alkanes, but can induce cracking and coke formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Traditional oxidants like O2 risk over-oxidation, whereas CO2, a milder oxidant, avoids this issue \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Among catalysts, V2O5, VOx/SiO2, and CeO2 are highly active due to redox properties and oxygen mobility, while transition metal clusters like Mo2C are also effective \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. CO2 dissociation mechanisms involve CO2 activation by the catalyst, forming CO and *O, with energy barriers influenced by the catalyst's redox properties \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\].

                In oxidative dehydrogenation, CO2 maintains higher oxidation states of chromium and vanadium species, acting as an oxidant to regenerate redox-active metal oxide catalysts like V2O5 \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. The oxidized diamond-supported V2O5 catalyst shows high activity for C3 and C4 alkane dehydrogenation, highlighting its effectiveness \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. Detailed mechanisms for C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration, especially for redox-inactive catalysts, are not well-documented \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\].

                Redox-active metal oxides activate C-H bonds via the MvK mechanism, forming water and leaving an oxygen vacancy that requires an oxidant for regeneration. Redox-inactive oxides directly reduce the oxidant without needing a vacancy \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. CO2 as an oxidant can either dissociate directly or be hydrogenated before CO formation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Catalysts vary in effectiveness: V2O5 has a high barrier for CO2 oxidation, VOx/SiO2 has a lower but still significant barrier, while CeO2 and transition metal clusters show easier CO2 dissociation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\].

                CO2 acts as a mild oxidant, partially oxidizing redox-active metal oxide catalysts like V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, facilitating C-H bond activation and propylene production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. This contrasts with redox-inactive catalysts such as CeO2 and transition metal clusters, which have higher energy barriers for CO2 dissociation, making CO2 activation less effective \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. CO2's role in suppressing surface carbon formation enhances catalyst stability and activity \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. Among catalysts, V2O5 and VOx/SiO2 are more effective in utilizing CO2 for oxidative dehydrogenation of propane, with CO2 re-oxidizing partially reduced metal oxides \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

                DFT calculations confirm that CO2-assisted propane dehydrogenation can proceed via direct oxidative dehydrogenation and non-oxidative dehydrogenation followed by the reverse water gas shift reaction, with the activation of the secondary CAH bond of propane as the rate-limiting step \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. The reverse water gas shift reaction, coupled with dehydrogenation, improves equilibrium conversion toward propylene production \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]. However, the role of CO2 in propane dehydrogenation over vanadium oxide-based catalysts remains unclear \[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\].

Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can efficiently activate CO2 for oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, particularly propane, while minimizing the formation of by-products such as CO and CO2. The design of bifunctional catalysts that can simultaneously activate CO2 and dehydrogenate alkanes is a promising direction. Additionally, the investigation of the role of CO2 in the regeneration of redox-inactive catalysts could lead to the discovery of new mechanisms and pathways for alkane dehydrogenation. The optimization of reaction conditions, such as temperature and pressure, to enhance the selectivity and yield of propylene is also a critical area of research. Furthermore, the integration of in situ spectroscopic techniques with computational methods could provide deeper insights into the catalytic mechanisms and the role of CO2 in oxidative dehydrogenation processes.

                丙烷等烷烃的氧化脱氢是生产丙烯这一重要石化原料的关键过程。这一过程受催化剂选择和所用氧化剂的显著影响。红氧化金属氧化物催化剂，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，通过Mars-van Krevelen（MvK）机制激活C-H键，捐赠氧气形成烯烃和水，并通过CO2还原再生\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。相比之下，如Ga2O3/MxOy等红氧化不活跃的催化剂，异裂CO2成CO和*O，脱氢烷烃，但可能诱导裂解和焦炭形成\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。传统氧化剂如O2存在过度氧化的风险，而CO2作为一种温和的氧化剂，避免了这一问题\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。在催化剂中，V2O5，VOx/SiO2和CeO2由于红氧化性质和氧流动性而高度活跃，而Mo2C等过渡金属簇也有效\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。CO2解离机制涉及催化剂激活CO2，形成CO和*O，能量障碍受催化剂红氧化性质的影响\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。

                在氧化脱氢中，CO2维持铬和钒物种的较高氧化态，作为氧化剂再生V2O5等红氧化金属氧化物催化剂\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。氧化金刚石支撑的V2O5催化剂对C3和C4烷烃脱氢显示出高活性，突显其有效性\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。C-H键激活和催化剂再生的详细机制，特别是对于红氧化不活跃的催化剂，记录不详\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。

                红氧化金属氧化物通过MvK机制激活C-H键，形成水并留下需要氧化剂再生的氧空位。红氧化不活跃的氧化物直接还原氧化剂而无需空位\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。CO2作为氧化剂可以直接解离或在CO形成前被氢化\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。催化剂的有效性各不相同：V2O5对CO2氧化有高障碍，VOx/SiO2有较低但仍然显著的障碍，而CeO2和过渡金属簇显示更容易的CO2解离\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。

                CO2作为一种温和的氧化剂，部分氧化V2O5和VOx/SiO2等红氧化金属氧化物催化剂，促进C-H键激活和丙烯生产\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。这与CeO2和过渡金属簇等红氧化不活跃的催化剂形成对比，这些催化剂具有更高的CO2解离能量障碍，使CO2激活效果较差\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。CO2在抑制表面碳形成方面的作用增强了催化剂的稳定性和活性\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。在催化剂中，V2O5和VOx/SiO2在利用CO2进行丙烷氧化脱氢方面更有效，CO2再氧化部分还原的金属氧化物\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。

                DFT计算证实，CO2辅助的丙烷脱氢可以通过直接氧化脱氢和非氧化脱氢后跟逆水煤气变换反应进行，丙烷的二级CAH键激活是速率限制步骤\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。逆水煤气变换反应与脱氢结合，提高了向丙烯生产的平衡转化\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。然而，CO2在钒氧化物基催化剂上的丙烷脱氢作用仍然不清楚\[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.015\]。
