                    The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes, particularly propane, is a critical process for the production of light olefins, with CO2 emerging as a promising oxidant for catalyst regeneration and C-H bond activation \[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]. Redox-active metal oxides, such as V2O5 and VOx/SiO2, employ the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where lattice oxygen is donated to the alkane, forming alkenes and water, with CO2 refilling the oxygen vacancies for catalyst regeneration \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. In contrast, redox-inactive catalysts, like Ga2O3/MxOy, activate CO2 through heterolytic dissociation into CO and *O, which dehydrogenates the alkane but can lead to cracking and coke formation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Traditional oxidants like O2 can over-oxidize, whereas CO2 is milder and prevents over-oxidation \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. Catalysts like V2O5, VOx/SiO2, CeO2, and transition metal clusters are effective for CO2 activation due to their redox properties and oxygen mobility \[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]. CO2 dissociation mechanisms involve activation by the catalyst, forming CO and *O, with energy barriers varying by catalyst type \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]. Redox-active metal oxides typically have lower barriers due to their redox properties \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The utilization of CO2 as an oxidant in oxidative dehydrogenation processes presents a sustainable and environmentally friendly approach to alkane conversion. The effectiveness of CO2 in catalyst regeneration and C-H bond activation, particularly over redox-active metal oxides, highlights its potential as a mild oxidant that prevents over-oxidation. Future research should focus on developing catalysts with enhanced CO2 activation capabilities, aiming to lower the energy barriers for CO2 dissociation. This could involve the design of bifunctional catalysts that simultaneously activate CO2 and dehydrogenate alkanes, potentially improving the overall efficiency of the process. Additionally, the investigation of novel redox-inactive catalysts that can effectively activate CO2 without leading to coke formation and catalyst deactivation is a promising avenue. The exploration of CO2 activation mechanisms on various catalyst surfaces, including the role of oxygen vacancies, could provide insights into optimizing catalyst design for enhanced CO2 utilization. Moreover, the integration of CO2 utilization in oxidative dehydrogenation with other processes, such as the reverse water gas shift reaction, could improve equilibrium conversion toward desired products, such as propylene. The development of catalysts that can synergistically activate CO2 and dehydrogenate alkanes, while maintaining high stability and selectivity, is a critical step toward sustainable chemical production.

                    丙烷等烷烃的氧化脱氢是生产轻质烯烃的关键过程，其中CO2作为催化剂再生和C-H键活化的有前途的氧化剂正在崭露头角\[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00268-2\]。红氧化金属氧化物，如V2O5和VOx/SiO2，采用Mars-van Krevelen机制，其中晶格氧被捐赠给烷烃，形成烯烃和水，CO2填补氧空位以再生催化剂\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。相比之下，红氧化惰性催化剂，如Ga2O3/MxOy，通过异裂解离CO2成CO和*O，这脱氢烷烃但可能导致裂解和焦炭形成\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。传统氧化剂如O2可能过度氧化，而CO2更温和，防止过度氧化\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。催化剂如V2O5，VOx/SiO2，CeO2和过渡金属簇由于其红氧化性质和氧流动性而对CO2激活有效\[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9\]。CO2解离机制涉及催化剂的激活，形成CO和*O，能量障碍因催化剂类型而异\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。红氧化金属氧化物通常由于其红氧化性质而具有较低的障碍\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_12\]。
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望: 在氧化脱氢过程中利用CO2作为氧化剂为烷烃转化提供了一种可持续和环境友好的方法。CO2在催化剂再生和C-H键活化中的有效性，特别是在红氧化金属氧化物上，突显了其作为温和氧化剂的潜力，可以防止过度氧化。未来的研究应集中在开发具有增强CO2激活能力的催化剂上，旨在降低CO2解离的能量障碍。这可能涉及设计双功能催化剂，同时激活CO2和脱氢烷烃，可能提高过程的整体效率。此外，调查新型红氧化惰性催化剂，可以有效地激活CO2，而不会导致焦炭形成和催化剂失活，是一个有希望的方向。探索各种催化剂表面上的CO2激活机制，包括氧空位的作用，可以为优化催化剂设计以增强CO2利用提供见解。此外，将CO2利用在氧化脱氢中与其他过程，如逆水煤气变换反应，的整合，可以改善向所需产品，如丙烯，的平衡转化。开发能够协同激活CO2和脱氢烷烃，同时保持高稳定性和选择性的催化剂，是向可持续化学生产迈出的关键一步。
