                    Pt-based bimetallic catalysts significantly impact CO oxidation, water gas shift reaction, and methane conversions, as evidenced by studies on PtFe and PtNi catalysts \[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]. In CO oxidation, PtFe and PtNi catalysts exhibit enhanced activity due to interface confinement effects and specific surface structures like NiO1x/PtNiPt(111) \[10.1021/cr300096b_12\]. The water gas shift reaction benefits from the addition of Re, Co, and Au to Pt-based catalysts, with dispersion and alloyed metal nature being key factors \[10.1016/j.apcata.2005.08.009\]. Methane conversions are improved by PtCo and PtNi, with increased reducibility and facilitated chain growth \[10.1021/cr300096b_12\]. Pt-Fe nanoparticles supported on silica spheres exhibit high activity and stability in CO oxidation under realistic conditions, maintaining 100% CO conversion and selectivity at room temperature \[10.1126/science.1188267\]. PtSnOx catalyzes CO oxidation at moderate temperatures through a synergistic bifunctional mechanism, where Pt offers CO adsorption sites and SnOx facilitates oxygen adsorption \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]. AuPt/CeO2 catalysts exhibit high activity in CO oxidation and the water gas shift reaction, achieving 78% CO conversion at 250°C \[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]. PtCu and PdCu supported on ceria show higher activity in oxygen-enhanced water gas shift (OWGS) than monometallic counterparts, with less pronounced effects in WGS \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]. Bimetallic catalysts, especially those with Pt-skin surfaces and 3d transition metals, outperform pure Pt in CO oxidation, water gas shift, and methane conversions \[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]. Fe-Pt bimetallic catalysts exhibit high selectivity for CO oxidation at low temperatures, attributed to subsurface Fe's influence on surface Pt's electronic structure \[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\].

                    基于Pt基双金属催化剂在CO氧化、水煤气变换反应和甲烷转化中发挥着显著作用的研究，如PtFe和PtNi催化剂的研究\[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]。在CO氧化中，PtFe和PtNi催化剂由于界面限制效应和特定的表面结构（如NiO1x/PtNiPt(111)）而表现出增强的活性\[10.1021/cr300096b_12\]。水煤气变换反应得益于向Pt基催化剂中添加Re、Co和Au，其中分散性和合金金属性质是关键因素\[10.1016/j.apcata.2005.08.009\]。甲烷转化通过PtCo和PtNi得到改善，这归因于还原性的增加和链增长的促进\[10.1021/cr300096b_12\]。支持在硅胶球上的Pt-Fe纳米颗粒在实际条件下表现出CO氧化的高活性和稳定性，保持100%的CO转化率和室温下的选择性\[10.1126/science.1188267\]。PtSnOx通过协同双功能机制催化中温下的CO氧化，其中Pt提供CO吸附位点，SnOx促进氧气吸附\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]。AuPt/CeO2催化剂在CO氧化和水煤气变换反应中表现出高活性，250°C时达到78%的CO转化率\[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]。支持在铈上的PtCu和PdCu在氧气增强的水煤气变换（OWGS）中比单金属催化剂表现出更高的活性，而在WGS中效果不那么明显\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]。双金属催化剂，特别是具有Pt皮肤表面和3d过渡金属的催化剂，在CO氧化、水煤气变换和甲烷转化中优于纯Pt\[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]。Fe-Pt双金属催化剂在低温下对CO氧化表现出高选择性，归因于亚表面Fe对表面Pt电子结构的影响\[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\]。
