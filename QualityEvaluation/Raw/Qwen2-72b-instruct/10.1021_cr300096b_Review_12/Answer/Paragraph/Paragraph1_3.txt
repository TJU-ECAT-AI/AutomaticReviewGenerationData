                    The study reveals that adjusting the Pt/Ni ratio in bimetallic PtNi catalysts for methane reforming with CO2 impacts surface properties and catalytic behavior \[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]. Adding a small amount of Pt (0.3-0.5 wt.%) to Ni catalysts decreases NiO size, facilitates its reduction, and ensures a more uniform distribution of nickel particles, as evidenced by TPR, FTIR, and XPS data \[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]. Pt-Fe nanoparticles on nanosized silica spheres exhibit high activity and stability in CO oxidation under realistic conditions, including water and CO2, typical of PEMFC environments \[10.1126/science.1188267\]. These catalysts maintain 100% CO conversion and selectivity at room temperature, with sustained high activity at lower temperatures. The high reactivity is attributed to CUF sites at the peripheries of FeO nanoislands on Pt(111) surfaces \[10.1126/science.1188267\]. Bimetallic catalysts, notably Pt-based ones, significantly impact CO oxidation, water gas shift reaction, and methane conversions \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]. The PtSnOx system catalyzes CO oxidation at moderate temperatures through a synergistic bifunctional mechanism, where Pt adsorbs CO and SnOx facilitates oxygen adsorption \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]. PtSn/AC-N catalysts demonstrate high CO conversion and selectivity, especially at lower temperatures, under hydrogen-rich conditions \[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]. Bimetallic catalysts, notably AuPt/CeO2, exhibit high activity in the water gas shift reaction, achieving 78% CO conversion at 250°C \[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]. Platinum's introduction facilitates the formation of partly oxidized gold species, enhancing ceria's reduction to Ce3+ species, creating oxygen vacancies, and reducing ceria's average size \[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]. Bimetallic catalysts, notably PtCu and PdCu on ceria, show higher activity in oxygen-enhanced water gas shift (OWGS) than monometallic counterparts, with less pronounced effects in water gas shift (WGS) \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]. Strong metal-metal interactions, as evidenced by TPR and OSC measurements, make these catalysts less pyrophoric \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]. EXAFS spectra reveal Cu alloys with Pt and Pd, and the presence of Cu in PdCu/CeO2 facilitates CO2 desorption, reducing surface carbonate on CeO2 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]. Bimetallic catalysts, especially those with Pt-skin surfaces and 3d transition metals, outperform pure Pt in CO oxidation, water gas shift, and methane conversions \[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]. Their reversible structural modulation, controlled by oxidation and reduction cycles, tunes surface CO adsorption and oxidation efficiency \[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]. Bimetallic catalysts, notably Pt-based ones, play a crucial role in industrial processes like fuel cells and catalytic reforming \[10.1016/j.apsusc.2009.03.086\]. Reversible structural changes under redox conditions may impact their performance in CO oxidation, water gas shift reaction, and methane conversions \[10.1016/j.apsusc.2009.03.086\]. Fe-Pt bimetallic catalysts exhibit high selectivity for CO oxidation at low temperatures, attributed to the electronic structure modification by subsurface Fe, influencing surface species adsorption and reaction \[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\]. Pt-skin structures with subsurface Fe show enhanced activity for hydrogenation and ORR \[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\].

                    基于\[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]参考文献的深度分析，调整Pt/Ni比率在用于CO2甲烷重整的PtNi双金属催化剂中影响表面性质和催化行为。向Ni催化剂中添加少量Pt（0.3-0.5 wt.%）可减少NiO尺寸，促进其还原，并确保镍颗粒更均匀的分布，这由TPR，FTIR和XPS数据证实\[10.1007/s11144-007-5139-8\]。在纳米尺寸的二氧化硅球体上的Pt-Fe纳米粒子在包括水和CO2在内的实际条件下，在PEMFC环境中表现出高活性和稳定性\[10.1126/science.1188267\]。这些催化剂在室温下保持100％的CO转化率和选择性，并在较低温度下保持高活性。高反应性归因于Pt（111）表面上FeO纳米岛边缘的CUF位点\[10.1126/science.1188267\]。值得注意的是，基于Pt的双金属催化剂对CO氧化，水煤气变换反应和甲烷转化有重大影响\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]。PtSnOx系统通过协同双功能机制在中温下催化CO氧化，其中Pt吸附CO，SnOx促进氧气吸附\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]。PtSn/AC-N催化剂在氢气丰富的条件下表现出高CO转化率和选择性，特别是在较低温度下\[10.1016/j.apcatb.2011.06.014\]。值得注意的是，AuPt/CeO2双金属催化剂在水煤气变换反应中表现出高活性，250°C时CO转化率达到78％\[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]。铂的引入促进了部分氧化金物种的形成，增强了CeO2还原为Ce3+物种，创造了氧空位，并减少了CeO2的平均尺寸\[10.1016/j.cattod.2010.04.005\]。值得注意的是，PtCu和PdCu在CeO2上的双金属催化剂在氧增强水煤气变换（OWGS）中的活性高于单金属对应物，而在水煤气变换（WGS）中的效果不那么明显\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]。TPR和OSC测量证实的强金属-金属相互作用使这些催化剂的热爆性降低\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]。EXAFS光谱显示Cu与Pt和Pd形成合金，PdCu/CeO2中Cu的存在促进了CO2的解吸，减少了CeO2上的表面碳酸盐\[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.10.014\]。特别是，具有Pt皮肤表面和3d过渡金属的双金属催化剂在CO氧化，水煤气变换和甲烷转化方面优于纯Pt\[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]。它们的可逆结构调制，由氧化和还原循环控制，调节表面CO吸附和氧化效率\[10.1002/cphc.200900053\]。值得注意的是，基于Pt的双金属催化剂在诸如燃料电池和催化重整的工业过程中起着至关重要的作用\[10.1016/j.apsusc.2009.03.086\]。在氧化还原条件下可逆的结构变化可能会影响它们在CO氧化，水煤气变换反应和甲烷转化中的性能\[10.1016/j.apsusc.2009.03.086\]。Fe-Pt双金属催化剂在低温下对CO氧化表现出高选择性，归因于Fe对表面Pt电子结构的修改，影响表面物种吸附和反应\[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\]。具有Fe亚表面的Pt皮肤结构在氢化和ORR中表现出增强的活性\[10.1016/s1872-2067(09)60037-1\]。
