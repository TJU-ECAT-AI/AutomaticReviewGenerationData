However, it does mention that PtPd/CeO2 catalysts exhibit high activity for selective CO oxidation in the presence of hydrogen. The literature does not discuss the potential insights that future UHV studies could provide in correlating these findings with model surfaces and supported catalysts, nor does it address the current limitations in UHV studies on Pt-based bimetallic surfaces.
