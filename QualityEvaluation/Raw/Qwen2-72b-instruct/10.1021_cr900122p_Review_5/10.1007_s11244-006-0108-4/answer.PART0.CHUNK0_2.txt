<EnglishVersionAnswer>

1.The literature does not provide a comprehensive mechanism for alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides. It does, however, mention that the reaction begins with the activation of the CH bond of the alkane by the tantalum hydride, leading to a surface tantalum alkyl complex. From this complex, a carbene hydride and an olefin hydride species can result from alpha and beta hydrogen abstractions. The interaction of the carbene and the olefin can lead to a tantalum cyclobutane, which decomposes to afford a new carbene and a new olefin. The hydrogenation of these species produces the new alkanes. The selectivity of products in alkane metathesis is governed by the [1,2] and [1,3] interactions of methyl or ethyl substituents in the metallacyclobutane intermediates, similar to olefin metathesis.

1.文献没有提供由硅胶支持的钽氢化物催化的烷基化反应的全面机制。然而，它确实提到反应开始于钽氢化物对烷基的CH键的活化，形成表面钽烷基复合物。从这个复合物中，可以通过α和β氢的抽象产生一个羰基氢和一个烯烃氢物种。羰基和烯烃的相互作用可以产生一个钽环丁烷，它分解产生一个新的羰基和一个新的烯烃。这些物种的氢化产生新的烷基。在烷基化反应中，产物的选择性由金属环丁烷中间体中甲基或乙基取代基的[1,2]和[1,3]相互作用控制，类似于烯烃的环化反应。
