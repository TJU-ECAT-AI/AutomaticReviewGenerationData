The catalytic cycle commences with CH bond activation, forming a surface tantalum alkyl complex, which subsequently yields carbene and olefin hydride species through alpha-H and beta-H abstractions \[10.1007/s11244-006-0108-4\]. Interaction of these species leads to a tantalum cyclobutane, decomposing to new carbene and olefin intermediates, which hydrogenate to produce new alkanes \[10.1007/s11244-006-0108-4\].
