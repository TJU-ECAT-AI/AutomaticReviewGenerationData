                    Comprehensive mechanistic investigation of alkane metathesis processes catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides: From linear to cyclic alkanes and the role of reaction intermediates \[10.1007/s11244-006-0108-4\], \[10.1351/pac-con-08-07-18\], \[10.1021/cr900122\], \[10.1039/b823226a\], \[10.1126/science.276.5309.99\], \[10.1002/anie.200602171\], \[10.1021/cr900122p_5\], \[10.1002/chem.200390162\].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive mechanistic investigation of alkane metathesis catalyzed by silica-supported tantalum hydrides has revealed intricate details about the role of intermediates such as alkenes and metallacyclobutanes in reaction selectivity and catalyst performance. From linear to cyclic alkanes, the two-site and one-site mechanisms have been elucidated, with tantalum hydrides acting as intermediates in the generation of alkenes and alkylidene hydrides. The formation of metallacyclobutane intermediates through γ-H activation is crucial for cyclic alkane transformations. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of ancillary ligands that enhance catalyst performance without hindering selectivity, as well as the exploration of novel metal hydrides for alkane metathesis. The optimization of reaction conditions and the design of catalysts with improved stability and selectivity for specific alkane conversions are promising avenues for further investigation. Additionally, the selective transfer of two carbons, as observed in the transformation of 2-methylpropane to 2,3-dimethylbutane, presents an opportunity to expand the scope of alkane metathesis to include more complex alkane transformations.

                    关于硅胶支持的钽氢化物催化下的烷烃复分解过程的全面机制研究：从线性到环状烷烃，以及反应中间体的作用 \[10.1007/s11244-006-0108-4\], \[10.1351/pac-con-08-07-18\], \[10.1021/cr900122\], \[10.1039/b823226a\], \[10.1126/science.276.5309.99\], \[10.1002/anie.200602171\], \[10.1021/cr900122p_5\], \[10.1002/chem.200390162\].

                    综合理解与未来展望: 关于硅胶支持的钽氢化物催化下的烷烃复分解的全面机制研究揭示了反应中间体如烯烃和金属环丁烷在反应选择性和催化剂性能中的复杂作用。从线性到环状烷烃，双位点和单位点机制已被阐明，钽氢化物作为生成烯烃和烷基化氢的中间体。通过γ-H活化形成金属环丁烷中间体对于环状烷烃的转化至关重要。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发增强催化剂性能而不影响选择性的辅助配体，以及探索新型金属氢化物用于烷烃复分解。优化反应条件和设计具有改进稳定性和选择性的催化剂用于特定的烷烃转化是进一步研究的有希望的方向。此外，观察到的2-甲基丙烷转化为2,3-二甲基丁烷的两个碳的有选择性转移，为扩大烷烃复分解的范围以包括更复杂的烷烃转化提供了机会。
