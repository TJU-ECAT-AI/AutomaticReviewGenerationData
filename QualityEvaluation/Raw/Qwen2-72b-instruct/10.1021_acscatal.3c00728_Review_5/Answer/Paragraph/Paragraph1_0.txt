This comprehensive analysis has elucidated the catalytic mechanisms on potential active sites of tricoordination B species with varying dimensional geometric configurations, thereby providing effective guidance for the development of more efficient ODHP catalysts in the future \[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_5\].

Moreover, the exploration of synergistic effects between B and other elements could lead to the design of catalysts with enhanced selectivity and stability, potentially revolutionizing the field of propane dehydrogenation.
