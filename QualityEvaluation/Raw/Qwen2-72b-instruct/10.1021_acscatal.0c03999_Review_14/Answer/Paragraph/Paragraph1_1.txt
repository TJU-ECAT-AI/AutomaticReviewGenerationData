                Recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants have been extensively studied with a variety of catalysts, including transition metal/metal oxides and metal oxides from the main group elements \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. These catalysts have been explored for reactions with soft oxidants such as CO2, N2O, sulfur-containing compounds, and halogens/halides. Despite the similarities in catalyst systems for CO2- and N2OODHP, inherent differences in reaction properties and the challenge of dealing with corrosiveness have complicated research advancement, resulting in unique challenges and future research directions for each route \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Future efforts should focus on designing catalysts that can effectively activate the CH bond in propane while maintaining good regenerability, and on renovating reaction systems to better accommodate industrial processes while addressing the challenges posed by various acid gases \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. CO2 plays a significant role in ODHP by completing redox cycles, shifting the reaction equilibrium towards product formation, and serving as an agent for coke removal \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. However, rapid deactivation due to reduction of active sites and coke formation is a major issue in CO2ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Similar challenges exist in N2OODHP, where deactivation and regeneration of catalysts remain problematic \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. The introduction of exogenous mild oxidizers, such as in catalytic oxyhalogenation, offers a promising route for single-step C3H6 production, achieving yields up to 55% \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Gaseous sulfur compounds are also effective mediators for DHP due to their high affinity for hydrogen abstraction \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. However, the nature of active sites in sulfur-mediated DHP is unclear due to a lack of catalyst characterization and mechanistic studies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. To overcome these hurdles, significant efforts should be directed towards developing active and stable catalysts with a deep understanding of structure-activity relationships and reaction kinetics \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Theoretical studies have provided insights into mechanistic understanding and catalyst design principles, but more progress is needed in developing better ODHP catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Alternative technologies, such as chemical looping and membrane reactors, are being developed to enhance process efficiency and minimize energy loss \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\].

                最近在使用软氧化剂进行丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)的进展中,已经广泛研究了各种催化剂,包括过渡金属/金属氧化物和主族元素的金属氧化物\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。这些催化剂已被用于与软氧化剂如CO2、N2O、含硫化合物和卤素/卤化物的反应。尽管CO2-和N2OODHP的催化剂系统相似,但反应性质的固有差异和处理腐蚀性的困难使得研究进展复杂化,为每条路线带来了独特的挑战和未来的研究方向\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。未来的努力应集中在设计能够有效激活丙烷中的CH键同时保持良好再生性的催化剂,以及改造反应系统以更好地适应工业过程,同时解决各种酸性气体带来的挑战\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。CO2在ODHP中起着重要作用,通过完成氧化还原循环、将反应平衡向产物侧转移以及作为焦炭去除的媒介\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。然而,由于活性位点的还原和焦炭形成,CO2ODHP中存在快速失活的问题\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。N2OODHP中也存在类似的挑战,催化剂的失活和再生仍然是问题\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。通过催化氧卤化引入的外源性温和氧化剂为单步C3H6生产提供了一条有希望的路线,实现了高达55%的产率\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。由于其对氢原子从烃分子中抽离的高亲和力,气态硫化合物也是DHP的有效媒介\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。然而,由于缺乏催化剂表征和机理研究,硫介导的DHP中活性位点的确切性质仍然不清楚\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。为了克服这些障碍,应致力于开发具有结构-活性关系和反应动力学深入理解的活性和稳定催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。理论研究为机制理解和催化剂设计原则提供了有用见解,但在开发更好的ODHP催化剂方面仍需取得更多进展\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。正在开发替代技术,如化学循环和膜反应器,以提高过程效率和最小化能量损失\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。
