                Recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants have been extensively studied with a variety of catalysts, including transition metal/metal oxides and metal oxides from the main group elements \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. These catalysts have been explored for reactions with soft oxidants such as CO2, N2O, sulfur-containing compounds, and halogens/halides. Despite the similarities in catalyst systems for CO2- and N2OODHP, inherent differences in reaction properties and the challenge of dealing with corrosiveness have complicated research advancement \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Unique challenges and future research directions are identified for each reaction route. Future efforts should focus on designing catalysts that can effectively activate the CH bond in propane while maintaining good regenerability, and on renovating reaction systems to better accommodate industrial processes while addressing the challenges posed by various acid gases \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. CO2 plays a significant role in ODHP by completing redox cycles, shifting the reaction equilibrium towards product formation, and serving as an agent for coke removal \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. However, rapid deactivation due to reduction of active sites and coke formation is a major issue in CO2ODHP \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Similar challenges exist in N2OODHP, where deactivation and regeneration of catalysts remain problematic \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. The introduction of exogenous mild oxidizers, such as in catalytic oxyhalogenation, offers a promising route for single-step C3H6 production, achieving yields up to 55% \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Gaseous sulfur compounds are also effective mediators for DHP due to their high affinity for hydrogen abstraction \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. However, the nature of active sites in sulfur-mediated DHP is unclear due to a lack of catalyst characterization and mechanistic studies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. To overcome these hurdles, significant efforts should be directed towards developing active and stable catalysts with a deep understanding of structure-activity relationships and reaction kinetics \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Theoretical studies have provided insights into mechanistic understanding and catalyst design principles, but more progress is needed in developing better ODHP catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]. Alternative technologies, such as chemical looping and membrane reactors, are being developed to enhance process efficiency and minimize energy loss \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\].

                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed in the file, there lies an opportunity to explore emerging fields and innovative methodologies. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of novel catalysts that can withstand corrosive environments while maintaining high selectivity and conversion rates. The design of these catalysts should incorporate a deep understanding of the structure-activity relationship and reaction kinetics to optimize performance. Additionally, the exploration of alternative oxidants, such as gaseous disulfur (S2), which can suppress overoxidation to COx and potentially lead to more selective propylene formation, is a promising direction \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]. Studies on metal sulfide catalysts have shown that the apparent activation energies for propylene formation are closely related to the XPS sulfur binding energies, indicating that the strength of the M-S bond is crucial for the activity of SODHP \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]. Kinetic data over a sulfided ZrO2 catalyst suggest a rate law that is first-order in propane and zero-order in sulfur, pointing to a mechanism similar to the Mars van Krevelen cycle of traditional ODHP \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]. These findings highlight the potential of SODHP as a selective and efficient route for oxidative propylene production, motivating further research in this area \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]. Moreover, the performance of steam-activated Fe-zeolites in the N2O-mediated oxidative dehydrogenation of propane has been investigated, revealing that the presence of extraframework iron species, generated during steam treatment, is essential for producing active catalysts \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]. The nature and distribution of these species, the zeolite host, and the catalyst acidity had no significant effect on the initial propene yield but strongly influenced the deactivation behavior \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]. FeZSM-5 showed remarkable resistance against deactivation, while FeBEA deactivated rapidly and completely \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]. The initial propene yield over FeZSM-5 and FeBEA was higher than over commercial H-ZSM-5 and H-BEA, although the residual activities after 400 min on stream were similar \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]. These insights can guide the development of more robust and efficient catalysts for ODHP processes.

                最近在使用软氧化剂进行丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)的进展中，已经广泛研究了包括过渡金属/金属氧化物和主族元素金属氧化物在内的各种催化剂 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。这些催化剂已被用于与软氧化剂如CO2、N2O、含硫化合物和卤素/卤化物的反应。尽管CO2-和N2OODHP的催化剂系统相似，但反应性质的固有差异和处理腐蚀性的困难已经使研究进展复杂化 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。为每条路线确定了独特的挑战和未来的研究方向。未来的工作应集中在设计能够有效激活丙烷中的CH键同时保持良好再生性的催化剂，以及改造反应系统以更好地适应工业过程，同时解决由各种酸性气体带来的挑战 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。CO2在ODHP中起着重要作用，通过完成氧化还原循环、将反应平衡向产物侧移动以及作为焦炭去除的媒介 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。然而，由于活性位点的还原和焦炭形成，CO2ODHP中快速失活是一个主要问题 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。在N2OODHP中也存在类似的挑战，催化剂的失活和再生仍然是一个问题 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。引入外源性温和氧化剂，如催化氧卤化，为单步C3H6生产提供了一条有希望的途径，产量可达55% \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。气态硫化合物由于其对氢原子从烃分子中抽离的高亲和力，也是DHP的有效媒介 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。然而，由于缺乏催化剂表征和机理研究，硫介导的DHP中活性位点的性质仍然不清楚 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。为了克服这些障碍，应将重大努力投入到开发具有结构-活性关系和反应动力学深入理解的活性和稳定催化剂 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。理论研究为机制理解和催化剂设计原则提供了有用的见解，但在开发更好的ODHP催化剂方面仍需取得更多进展 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。正在开发替代技术，如化学循环和膜反应器，以提高过程效率并最小化能源损失 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14\]。

                综合理解与未来展望: 考虑到文件中讨论的先进成果和发现，我们有机会探索新兴领域和创新方法。未来的研究努力可能集中在开发能够在腐蚀性环境中保持高选择性和转化率的同时，开发新型催化剂。这些催化剂的设计应结合对结构-活性关系和反应动力学的深刻理解，以优化性能。此外，探索替代氧化剂，如气态二硫化物(S2)，它可以抑制氧化为COx，并可能导致更选择性的丙烯形成，是一个有希望的方向 \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]。对金属硫化物催化剂的研究表明，丙烯形成的表观活化能与XPS硫结合能密切相关，表明M-S键的强度对SODHP的活性至关重要 \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]。在硫化ZrO2催化剂上的动力学数据表明，丙烷为一级反应，硫为零级反应，这表明SODHP可能通过类似于传统ODHP的Mars van Krevelen循环的机制进行 \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]。这些发现突出了SODHP作为选择性和高效氧化丙烯生产途径的潜力，激励了该领域的进一步研究 \[10.1039/D0CY01039A\]。此外，已经研究了蒸汽活化Fe-沸石在N2O介导的丙烷氧化脱氢中的性能，揭示了在蒸汽处理过程中生成的框架外铁物种对于产生活性催化剂至关重要 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]。这些物种的性质和分布、沸石宿主和催化剂酸度对初始丙烯产量没有显著影响，但强烈影响了失活行为 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]。FeZSM-5表现出对失活的显著抵抗力，而FeBEA则迅速且完全失活 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]。FeZSM-5和FeBEA上的初始丙烯产量高于商业H-ZSM-5和H-BEA，尽管在流经400分钟后，残余活性相似 \[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005\]。这些见解可以指导开发更坚固和更有效的ODHP过程催化剂。
