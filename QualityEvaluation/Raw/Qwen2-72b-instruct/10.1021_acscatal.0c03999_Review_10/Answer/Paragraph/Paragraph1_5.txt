        <English> 
                The potential and challenges of sulfur-based alternative oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) for propylene production have been elucidated in recent studies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. Sulfur-based oxidants, including H2S, S2, and SO2, have demonstrated the ability to enhance propylene yield compared to conventional oxidants. The effectiveness of SO2 in this process is evidenced by the formation of oxidative condensation products (OCP) on the catalyst surface, which selectively accelerate the interaction of propane with SO2 \[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]. However, the use of SO2 results in low propylene yield due to the formation of COx products over bulk metal oxide catalysts. The use of a soft oxidant like S2 can mitigate overoxidation of propane over ZrO2. A significant challenge in designing efficient catalysts for S2ODHP is the formation of byproducts such as CS2. Future research should concentrate on developing a structure-activity relationship to rationally design catalysts in this field. The role of sulfur as a promoter or inhibitor is well-documented in hydrogenation reactions, and sulfur compounds like S2 and H2S have been used as cocatalysts to reduce overoxidation during oxidative dehydrogenation reactions. Shorter residence times are required to suppress the cracking reaction, and sulfur in the diatomic state may abstract hydrogen from an alkane. The presence of S2 improves C3H6 yield and selectivity, but the conversion is barely affected by the catalysts, indicating the dominance of gas-phase reactions. Sulfur-based byproducts like CS2 are observed in the reactor effluent. The apparent activation barrier for propylene formation decreases with lower metal-sulfur bond strength, indicating a more active sulfur species. CH activation proceeds by a surface sulfur species, and propylene and H2S formation leads to sulfur vacancy, which is replenished by gas phase S2 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The exploration of sulfur-based oxidants in the ODHP process has revealed promising avenues for enhancing propylene yield. The use of SO2, despite its challenges, and the potential of S2 as a soft oxidant offer insights into the design of more efficient catalysts. Future research should focus on optimizing catalyst design to improve selectivity and activity towards propylene formation, while minimizing byproduct formation. Developing a structure-activity relationship will be crucial for rational catalyst design. The role of sulfur compounds as cocatalysts to reduce overoxidation during oxidative dehydrogenation reactions should be further investigated. Shorter residence times and the use of sulfur in the diatomic state as an active species are key parameters to consider for suppressing the cracking reaction. The formation of sulfur-based byproducts and their impact on catalyst performance should be thoroughly examined. Understanding the activation barrier for propylene formation and the role of metal-sulfur bond strength in catalyst activity will guide the development of more active sulfur species. The replenishment of sulfur vacancies by gas phase S2 suggests a dynamic interplay between gas-phase and surface reactions that should be explored in depth. Overall, the optimization of sulfur-based oxidants and catalyst design in the ODHP process holds significant potential for improving propylene production efficiency.

                硫基替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）制丙烯生产中的潜力和挑战已在最近的研究中得到阐明\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。包括H2S、S2和SO2在内的硫基氧化剂已被证明能够提高丙烯产量，与传统氧化剂相比。SO2在这一过程中的有效性通过在催化剂表面形成氧化缩合产物（OCP）得以体现，这些产物选择性地加速了丙烷与SO2的相互作用\[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]。然而，使用SO2会导致丙烯产量较低，因为会在大块金属氧化物催化剂上形成COx产物。使用像S2这样的软氧化剂可以减少丙烷在ZrO2上的过度氧化。设计高效S2ODHP催化剂的一个重大挑战是副产物如CS2的形成。未来的研究应集中在开发结构-活性关系，以理性设计这一领域的催化剂。硫作为促进剂或抑制剂的作用在氢化反应中已有充分研究，像S2和H2S这样的硫化合物已被用作共催化剂，以减少氧化脱氢反应中的过度氧化。需要更短的停留时间来抑制裂化反应，而二原子态的硫可能从烷烃中抽离氢。S2的存在提高了C3H6的产量和选择性，但转化率几乎不受催化剂的影响，这表明了气相反应的主导地位。在反应器流出物中观察到像CS2这样的硫基副产物。丙烯形成的表观活化能随着金属-硫键强度的降低而降低，表明了更活跃的硫物种。CH活化通过表面硫物种进行，丙烯和H2S的形成导致硫空位，这些空位由气相S2补充\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 硫基氧化剂在ODHP过程中的探索揭示了提高丙烯产量的有希望的途径。尽管存在挑战，SO2的使用和S2作为软氧化剂的潜力为设计更高效催化剂提供了见解。未来的研究应集中在优化催化剂设计，以提高丙烯形成的选择性和活性，同时最小化副产物的形成。开发结构-活性关系对于理性催化剂设计至关重要。应进一步研究硫化合物作为共催化剂以减少氧化脱氢反应中过度氧化的作用。需要更短的停留时间和使用二原子态的硫作为活性物种来抑制裂化反应。应彻底检查硫基副产物的形成及其对催化剂性能的影响。理解丙烯形成的活化能障碍和金属-硫键强度在催化剂活性中的作用将指导更活跃硫物种的开发。气相S2补充硫空位表明了气相和表面反应之间的动态相互作用，这应该深入探索。总体而言，硫基氧化剂的优化和ODHP过程中的催化剂设计在提高丙烯生产效率方面具有重大潜力。
