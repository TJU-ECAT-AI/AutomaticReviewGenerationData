        <English> 
                The potential and challenges of sulfur-based alternative oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) for propylene production have been illuminated by recent studies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. Sulfur-based oxidants, including H2S, S2, and SO2, have demonstrated the ability to enhance propylene yield compared to conventional oxidants. SO2, in particular, has been shown to be effective in the selective oxidation of propane to propylene, with the formation of oxidative condensation products (OCP) on the catalyst surface accelerating the interaction of propane with SO2 \[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]. However, the use of SO2 results in low propylene yield due to the formation of COx products over bulk metal oxide catalysts. The use of a soft oxidant like S2 can reduce the overoxidation of propane over ZrO2, and the presence of S2 improves C3H6 yield and selectivity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. A major challenge in designing efficient catalysts for S2ODHP is the formation of byproducts such as CS2. Future research should focus on developing a structure-activity relationship to rationally design catalysts in this field. The role of sulfur as a promoter or inhibitor is well studied in hydrogenation reactions, and sulfur compounds like S2 and H2S have been used as cocatalysts to reduce overoxidation during oxidative dehydrogenation reactions. Shorter residence times are required to suppress the cracking reaction, and sulfur in the diatomic state may abstract hydrogen from an alkane. The apparent activation barrier for propylene formation decreases with lower metal-sulfur bond strength, indicating a more active sulfur species. CH activation proceeds by a surface sulfur species, and propylene and H2S formation leads to sulfur vacancy, which is replenished by gas phase S2 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The exploration of sulfur-based oxidants in the ODHP process has revealed promising avenues for enhancing propylene yield. The effectiveness of SO2 in accelerating the interaction of propane with the catalyst surface, despite its limitations in COx formation, highlights the need for catalyst design optimization. The use of S2 as a soft oxidant to reduce overoxidation and improve C3H6 yield and selectivity underscores its potential as an alternative to conventional oxidants. Future research should focus on addressing the challenge of byproduct formation, particularly CS2, and developing a structure-activity relationship to guide rational catalyst design. The role of sulfur compounds as cocatalysts in reducing overoxidation during oxidative dehydrogenation reactions suggests a broader application in the field of catalysis. Shorter residence times and the activation of sulfur species for propylene formation point towards the need for advanced catalysts that can efficiently manage the reaction kinetics. The replenishment of sulfur vacancies by gas phase S2 indicates a dynamic interplay between gas-phase and surface reactions, which should be further explored. In summary, the potential of sulfur-based oxidants in the ODHP process is significant, but challenges remain in terms of byproduct formation and catalyst design. Future research should aim to optimize catalyst design, understand the role of sulfur compounds in reaction kinetics, and explore the broader implications of sulfur-based oxidants in catalysis.

                硫基替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）制丙烯生产中的潜力和挑战已由近期研究揭示\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。包括H2S，S2和SO2在内的硫基氧化剂已证明能够与传统氧化剂相比提高丙烯产量。特别是，SO2已被证明在丙烷选择性氧化为丙烯中有效，通过在催化剂表面形成氧化缩合产物（OCP）加速丙烷与SO2的相互作用\[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]。然而，SO2的使用导致丙烯产量低，因为在其上形成COx产物。使用像S2这样的软氧化剂可以减少ZrO2上的丙烷过度氧化，而S2的存在提高了C3H6的产量和选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。设计高效S2ODHP催化剂的主要挑战是CS2等副产品的形成。未来的研究应集中在开发结构-活性关系以合理设计该领域的催化剂。硫作为促进剂或抑制剂的作用在氢化反应中得到了广泛研究，像S2和H2S这样的硫化合物已被用作减少氧化脱氢反应中过度氧化的助催化剂。需要较短的停留时间来抑制裂解反应，而二原子状态的硫可能从烷烃中提取氢。丙烯形成的表观活化能随着金属-硫键强度的降低而降低，表明更活跃的硫物种。CH活化通过表面硫物种进行，丙烯和H2S的形成导致硫空位，这由气相S2补充\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：在ODHP过程中探索硫基氧化剂揭示了提高丙烯产量的有希望的途径。SO2在加速丙烷与催化剂表面相互作用的有效性，尽管其在COx形成方面的局限性，突出了催化剂设计优化的必要性。使用S2作为软氧化剂来减少过度氧化并提高C3H6的产量和选择性，突出了其作为传统氧化剂的替代品的潜力。未来的研究应集中在解决CS2等副产品形成的挑战，以及开发结构-活性关系以指导合理催化剂设计。硫化合物作为助催化剂在减少氧化脱氢反应中过度氧化的作用表明了其在催化领域更广泛的应用。需要较短的停留时间和硫物种的丙烯形成活化表明需要能够有效管理反应动力学的先进催化剂。气相S2补充硫空位表明气相和表面反应之间的动态相互作用，这应进一步探索。总之，硫基氧化剂在ODHP过程中的潜力是显著的，但在副产品形成和催化剂设计方面仍存在挑战。未来的研究应旨在优化催化剂设计，理解硫化合物在反应动力学中的作用，并探索硫基氧化剂在催化中的更广泛含义。
