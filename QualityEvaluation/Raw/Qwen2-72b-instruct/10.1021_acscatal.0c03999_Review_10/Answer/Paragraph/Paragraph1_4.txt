        <English> 
                The potential and challenges of sulfur-based alternative oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) for propylene production have been elucidated in recent studies \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. Sulfur-based oxidants, including H2S, S2, and SO2, have demonstrated the ability to enhance propylene yield compared to conventional oxidants. SO2, in particular, has been shown to be effective in the selective oxidation of propane to propylene, with the formation of oxidative condensation products (OCP) on the catalyst surface accelerating the interaction of propane with SO2 \[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]. However, the use of SO2 as an oxidant results in low propylene yield due to the formation of COx products over bulk metal oxide catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. The use of a soft oxidant like S2 can reduce the overoxidation of propane over ZrO2, and the presence of S2 improves C3H6 yield and selectivity, although the conversion is barely affected by the catalysts, indicating the dominance of gas-phase reactions \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. A major challenge in designing efficient catalysts for S2ODHP is the formation of byproducts such as CS2 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. Future research should focus on developing a structureactivity relationship to rationally design catalysts in this field \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The use of sulfur-based oxidants in the ODHP process offers a promising avenue for improving propylene yield. However, the challenges associated with each oxidant, such as byproduct formation and overoxidation, must be addressed. Future research should focus on optimizing catalyst design to enhance the activity and selectivity of OCP towards propylene formation, as well as investigating the role of different sulfur-based oxidants in the ODHP process. Developing a structureactivity relationship will be crucial for rational catalyst design in this field. Additionally, exploring the effectiveness of other sulfur-based oxidants and their impact on propylene yield, as well as the development of catalysts that can optimize the overall process, will be important areas for future research. The role of sulfur as a promoter or inhibitor in hydrogenation reactions, and its use as a cocatalyst to reduce overoxidation during oxidative dehydrogenation reactions, should also be further investigated. Shorter residence times and the use of sulfur in the diatomic state may help to suppress the cracking reaction and abstract hydrogen from an alkane, respectively. Understanding the apparent activation barrier for propylene formation and the role of metal-sulfur bond strength in this process will also be important for improving catalyst design. Finally, the replenishment of sulfur vacant sites by gas phase S2 should be considered in the design of efficient catalysts for the ODHP process.

                硫基替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）制丙烯生产中的潜力和挑战已在最近的研究中得到阐明\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。包括H2S、S2和SO2在内的硫基氧化剂已证明能够与传统氧化剂相比提高丙烯产量。特别是，SO2已被证明在丙烷选择性氧化为丙烯中有效，通过在催化剂表面形成氧化缩合产物（OCP）加速丙烷与SO2的相互作用\[10.1016/S0167-2991(08)60185-8\]。然而，使用SO2作为氧化剂会导致丙烯产量低，因为会在大块金属氧化物催化剂上形成COx产物\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。使用像S2这样的软氧化剂可以减少ZrO2上的丙烷过度氧化，而S2的存在可以提高C3H6的产量和选择性，尽管催化剂几乎不影响转化率，这表明气相反应占主导地位\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。设计高效S2ODHP催化剂的主要挑战是形成CS2等副产品\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。未来的研究应集中在开发结构活性关系，以合理设计该领域的催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望：在ODHP过程中使用硫基氧化剂为提高丙烯产量提供了一条有希望的途径。然而，必须解决与每个氧化剂相关的挑战，如副产品形成和过度氧化。未来的研究应集中在优化催化剂设计，以增强OCP对丙烯形成的活性和选择性，以及调查不同硫基氧化剂在ODHP过程中的作用。在该领域开发结构活性关系对于合理催化剂设计至关重要。此外，探索其他硫基氧化剂的有效性和它们对丙烯产量的影响，以及开发可以优化整个过程的催化剂，将是未来研究的重要领域。硫在氢化反应中作为促进剂或抑制剂的作用，以及其作为减少氧化脱氢反应中过度氧化的助催化剂的作用，也应进一步研究。较短的停留时间和硫的二原子状态的使用可能有助于抑制裂解反应和从烷烃中提取氢。了解丙烯形成的表观活化能障碍以及金属硫键强度在这一过程中的作用，对于改进催化剂设计也非常重要。最后，应考虑气相S2对硫空位位点的补充，以设计ODHP过程的高效催化剂。
