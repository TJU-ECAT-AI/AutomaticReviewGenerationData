                The potential and challenges of sulfur-based alternative oxidants in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) for propylene production have been elucidated through a critical examination of their effectiveness, associated problems, and future directions for catalyst design optimization. Sulfur-based oxidants, such as H2S, S2, and SO2, have demonstrated promising outcomes in enhancing propylene yield compared to conventional oxidants \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. However, the use of SO2 as an oxidant in ODHP results in low propylene yield due to the formation of COx products over bulk metal oxide catalysts \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. The introduction of H2S as a promoter in the presence of a V2O5/A12O3 catalyst has been shown to significantly enhance propene yield \[10.1080/17415990412331327909\]. The use of a soft oxidant like S2 can reduce the overoxidation of propane over ZrO2, and the presence of S2 improves C3H6 yield and selectivity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. A major challenge in designing efficient catalysts for S2ODHP is the formation of byproducts such as CS2 \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]. Future research should focus on developing a structure-activity relationship to rationally design catalysts in this field \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The exploration of sulfur-based oxidants in ODHP presents a promising avenue for improving propylene yield. The effectiveness of H2S and S2 in enhancing yield and selectivity, while mitigating overoxidation, highlights their potential as alternative oxidants. However, the formation of COx products with SO2 and byproducts like CS2 with S2 underscores the need for catalyst design optimization. Future research should concentrate on developing a deeper understanding of the structure-activity relationship, aiming to design catalysts that can efficiently harness the benefits of sulfur-based oxidants while minimizing byproduct formation. This could involve the investigation of novel catalyst compositions and reaction conditions that promote the selective formation of propylene. Additionally, the role of sulfur in the diatomic state as an active species for hydrogen abstraction from alkanes opens up new possibilities for catalyst design. The development of catalysts that can maintain a high concentration of active sulfur species while minimizing sulfur vacancy could lead to significant improvements in propylene yield. Furthermore, the exploration of other sulfur-based oxidants and their impact on propylene yield could provide valuable insights into the design of more efficient and selective catalysts for ODHP. The optimization of residence times to suppress cracking reactions and the investigation of the effects of metal-sulfur bond strength on propylene formation could also contribute to the rational design of catalysts for this process. In summary, the potential of sulfur-based oxidants in ODHP is significant, but their effective utilization requires a comprehensive understanding of the underlying chemistry and the development of optimized catalysts.

                通过批判性检查硫基替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）中生产丙烯的效率、相关问题以及催化剂设计优化的未来方向，揭示了硫基替代氧化剂在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）中生产丙烯的潜力和挑战。硫基氧化剂，如H2S、S2和SO2，在提高丙烯产量方面与传统氧化剂相比展现出有希望的结果\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。然而，SO2作为ODHP中的氧化剂，由于在块体金属氧化物催化剂上形成COx产物，导致丙烯产量低\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。在V2O5/A12O3催化剂存在下引入H2S作为促进剂，已被证明能显著提高丙烯产量\[10.1080/17415990412331327909\]。使用像S2这样的软氧化剂可以减少ZrO2上丙烷的过度氧化，而S2的存在提高了C3H6的产量和选择性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。设计高效S2ODHP催化剂的主要挑战是形成CS2等副产品\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。未来的研究应集中在开发结构-活性关系，以合理设计这一领域的催化剂\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_10\]。
                ※※※
                综合理解与未来展望: 硫基氧化剂在ODHP中的探索为提高丙烯产量提供了一条有希望的途径。H2S和S2在提高产量和选择性的同时减轻过度氧化的有效性，突显了它们作为替代氧化剂的潜力。然而，SO2形成COx产物和S2形成CS2等副产品，凸显了催化剂设计优化的必要性。未来的研究应集中于开发对结构-活性关系的更深入理解，旨在设计能够有效利用硫基氧化剂的好处同时最小化副产品形成的催化剂。这可能涉及调查能够促进丙烯选择性形成的新型催化剂组成和反应条件。此外，二原子状态的硫作为从烷烃中提取氢的活性物种的作用，为催化剂设计开辟了新的可能性。开发能够保持高浓度活性硫物种同时最小化硫空位的催化剂，可能会显著提高丙烯产量。此外，探索其他硫基氧化剂及其对丙烯产量的影响，可能为设计更高效、更选择性的ODHP催化剂提供有价值的见解。优化停留时间以抑制裂解反应，以及研究金属-硫键强度对丙烯形成的影响，也可能有助于这一过程的催化剂的合理设计。总之，硫基氧化剂在ODHP中的潜力是显著的，但其有效利用需要对基础化学的全面理解以及优化催化剂的开发。
