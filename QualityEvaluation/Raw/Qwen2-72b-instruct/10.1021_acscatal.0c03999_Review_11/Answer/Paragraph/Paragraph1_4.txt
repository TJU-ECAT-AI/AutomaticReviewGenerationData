                Halogen-assisted methods for selective propane dehydrogenation have been extensively studied, offering a range of approaches to convert propane (C3H8) to propene (C3H6) with high selectivity \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]. These methods include dehydrogenation with molecular halogens, oxidative dehydrogenation in the presence of halogen and oxygen, propane oxyhalogenation using halides and oxygen, and molten metal halide salts assisted oxidative dehydrogenation. The catalytic transformation of propane in these processes is distinct from other alkanes like methane and ethane due to the specific reactivity of each alkane \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]. Iodine-mediated dehydrogenation, alkali metal chloride-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, metal iodide-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, and bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation are among the methods for selectively dehydrogenating propane to propene \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Iodine-mediated dehydrogenation involves the substitution of molecular iodine with hydrocarbons, but iodine is poorly reactive toward saturated hydrocarbons \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. A free radical mechanism was proposed for the stoichiometric iodination, consisting of successive hydrogen abstraction reactions involving both atomic and molecular iodine \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Molten salts (metal chlorides and iodides) catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation uses supported alkali metal chloride catalysts, which have been demonstrated to be one of the most promising candidates for the oxidative dehydrogenation of light aliphatic alkanes (C2C4) to olefins \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes can also be catalyzed by iodide-based molten salts \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Recently, Stucky et al. developed a low-temperature route for propane dehydrogenation by using bihalogens (Br2 and I2) as the mediators \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. A high yield of C3H6+C3H7X (X = Br and I) up to 80% was attained at moderate reaction temperatures below 800 K \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The interplay between Br2 and I2 was believed to be crucial, combining the high reactivity of bromine and high selectivity of iodine \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The exploration of halogen-assisted methods for selective propane dehydrogenation has revealed a plethora of approaches, each with its own advantages and challenges. The development of bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation by Stucky et al. represents a significant advancement, offering a low-temperature route with high yields and selectivity \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Future research endeavors might focus on the optimization of reaction conditions, the development of more efficient catalysts, and the exploration of novel halogen combinations. The understanding of the interplay between different halogens, such as bromine and iodine, could lead to the design of more selective and reactive processes. Additionally, the functions of metal iodides in the dehydrogenation process remain to be elucidated, presenting an opportunity for further investigation. The development of sustainable and cost-effective methods for propane dehydrogenation could significantly impact the chemical industry, offering alternative routes to propene production that supplement current methods based on steam cracking, fluidic catalytic cracking of naphtha and other hydrocarbons, and SAPO-34 catalyzed methanol to mixed olefins \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\].

                卤素辅助的丙烷选择性脱氢方法已经广泛研究，提供了一系列将丙烷（C3H8）转化为丙烯（C3H6）的高选择性途径\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。这些方法包括使用分子卤素的脱氢、在卤素和氧气存在下的氧化脱氢、使用卤化物和氧气的丙烷氧卤化，以及熔融金属卤化盐辅助的氧化脱氢。在这些过程中，丙烷的催化转化与甲烷和乙烷等其他烷烃明显不同，这是由于每种烷烃的特定反应性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。选择性将丙烷脱氢至丙烯的方法包括碘介导的脱氢、碱金属氯化物催化的氧化脱氢、金属碘化物催化的氧化脱氢和双卤素辅助的脱氢\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。碘介导的脱氢涉及分子碘与烃类的置换，但碘对饱和烃类的反应性较差\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。提出了一个自由基机制来描述计量碘化，包括涉及原子和分子碘的连续氢原子抽提反应\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。熔融盐（金属氯化物和碘化物）催化的氧化脱氢使用了支持的碱金属氯化物催化剂，这些催化剂已被证明是将轻质脂族烷烃（C2C4）氧化脱氢为烯烃的最有希望的候选者之一\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。烷烃的氧化脱氢也可以通过碘化物基熔融盐催化\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。最近，Stucky等人开发了一种使用双卤素（Br2和I2）作为介质的低温丙烷脱氢途径\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。在800 K以下的温和反应温度下，C3H6+C3H7X（X = Br和I）的高产率高达80%\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。Br2和I2之间的相互作用被认为至关重要，结合了溴的高反应性和碘的高选择性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。

                综合理解与未来展望：卤素辅助的丙烷选择性脱氢方法的探索揭示了大量途径，每种途径都有其自身的优势和挑战。Stucky等人开发的双卤素辅助脱氢代表了重大进展，提供了一种低温路线，具有高产率和选择性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。未来的研究工作可能集中在反应条件的优化、更高效催化剂的开发以及新型卤素组合的探索上。对不同卤素，如溴和碘之间的相互作用的理解，可能会导致设计出更具选择性和反应性的过程。此外，金属碘化物在脱氢过程中的功能仍有待阐明，为更深入的研究提供了机会。开发可持续和成本效益高的丙烷脱氢方法可能对化学工业产生重大影响，提供补充当前基于蒸汽裂解、流体催化裂化石脑油和其他烃类以及SAPO-34催化甲醇转化为混合烯烃的丙烯生产方法的替代途径\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。
