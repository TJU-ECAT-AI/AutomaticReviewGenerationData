                Halogen-assisted methods for selective propane dehydrogenation have been extensively explored, offering a unique role in the dehydrogenation of various alkanes, affording an exceptionally high selectivity to the respective alkenes \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The methods include iodine-mediated dehydrogenation, alkali metal chloride-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, metal iodide-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, and bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation. Iodine-mediated dehydrogenation involves the substitution of molecular iodine with hydrocarbons, but iodine is poorly reactive toward saturated hydrocarbons \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Molten salts (metal chlorides and iodides) catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation uses supported alkali metal chloride catalysts, which have been demonstrated to be one of the most promising candidates for the oxidative dehydrogenation of light aliphatic alkanes (C2C4) to olefins \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes can also be catalyzed by iodide-based molten salts \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Recently, Stucky et al. developed a low-temperature route for propane dehydrogenation by using bihalogens (Br2 and I2) as the mediators, achieving a high yield of C3H6+C3H7X (X = Br and I) up to 80% at moderate reaction temperatures below 800 K \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The interplay between Br2 and I2 was believed to be crucial, combining the high reactivity of bromine and high selectivity of iodine \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Propane (C3H8) can be selectively dehydrogenated to propene (C3H6) through halogen-mediated pathways at relatively mild temperatures between 300 and 700 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]. The catalytic transformation of propane in these processes is distinct from other alkanes like methane and ethane due to the specific reactivity of each alkane \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\].
                ※※※
                Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The advancements in halogen-assisted methods for selective propane dehydrogenation have opened up new avenues for the production of propene. The unique role of halogens in the dehydrogenation of various alkanes, affording an exceptionally high selectivity to the respective alkenes, has been well-documented. The methods for selectively dehydrogenating propane to propene include iodine-mediated dehydrogenation, alkali metal chloride-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, metal iodide-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, and bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation. The interplay between Br2 and I2 in the bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation was believed to be crucial, combining the high reactivity of bromine and high selectivity of iodine. The catalytic transformation of propane in these processes is distinct from other alkanes like methane and ethane due to the specific reactivity of each alkane. Future research endeavors might focus on the development of new catalysts and the optimization of reaction conditions to further improve the selectivity and yield of propene. The exploration of new halogen-assisted methods for the dehydrogenation of other alkanes, such as butane and pentane, might also be a promising direction.

                卤素辅助的丙烷选择性脱氢方法已被广泛研究，为丙烯的生产开辟了新的途径\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。这些方法包括碘介导的脱氢、碱金属氯化物催化的氧化脱氢、金属碘化物催化的氧化脱氢和双卤素辅助的脱氢。碘介导的脱氢涉及分子碘与烃类的置换，但碘对饱和烃类的反应性较差\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。最近，Stucky等人开发了一种低温路线，使用双卤素(Br2和I2)作为介质进行丙烷脱氢，实现了在800 K以下的温和反应温度下高达80%的C3H6+C3H7X(X=Br和I)的高产率\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。Br2和I2之间的相互作用被认为是关键，结合了溴的高反应性和碘的高选择性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。丙烷(C3H8)可以通过卤素介导的途径在300到700摄氏度的相对温和温度下选择性地脱氢为丙烯(C3H6)\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。这些过程中的丙烷催化转化与甲烷和乙烷等其他烷烃明显不同，这是由于每种烷烃的特定反应性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。

                综合理解与未来展望: 卤素辅助的丙烷选择性脱氢方法的进展为丙烯的生产开辟了新的途径。卤素在各种烷烃脱氢中的独特作用，为相应的烯烃提供了异常高的选择性，已被充分记录。选择性将丙烷脱氢为丙烯的方法包括碘介导的脱氢、碱金属氯化物催化的氧化脱氢、金属碘化物催化的氧化脱氢和双卤素辅助的脱氢。双卤素辅助脱氢中Br2和I2之间的相互作用被认为是关键，结合了溴的高反应性和碘的高选择性。这些过程中的丙烷催化转化与甲烷和乙烷等其他烷烃明显不同，这是由于每种烷烃的特定反应性。未来的研究工作可能集中在开发新的催化剂和优化反应条件，以进一步提高丙烯的选择性和产率。探索丁烷和戊烷等其他烷烃的新型卤素辅助脱氢方法也可能是一个有前景的方向。
