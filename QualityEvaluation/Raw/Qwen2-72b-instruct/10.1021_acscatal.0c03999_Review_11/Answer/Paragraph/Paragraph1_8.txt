                Halogen-assisted methods for selective propane dehydrogenation have been extensively explored, offering a unique role in the dehydrogenation of various alkanes, affording an exceptionally high selectivity to the respective alkenes \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The methods include iodine-mediated dehydrogenation, alkali metal chloride-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, metal iodide-catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation, and bihalogen-aided dehydrogenation. Iodine-mediated dehydrogenation involves the substitution of molecular iodine with hydrocarbons, but iodine is poorly reactive toward saturated hydrocarbons \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. A free radical mechanism was proposed for the stoichiometric iodination, consisting of successive hydrogen abstraction reactions involving both atomic and molecular iodine \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Molten salts (metal chlorides and iodides) catalyzed oxidative dehydrogenation uses supported alkali metal chloride catalysts, which have been demonstrated to be one of the most promising candidates for the oxidative dehydrogenation of light aliphatic alkanes (C2C4) to olefins \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes can also be catalyzed by iodide-based molten salts \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Recently, Stucky et al. developed a low-temperature route for propane dehydrogenation by using bihalogens (Br2 and I2) as the mediators \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. A high yield of C3H6+C3H7X (X = Br and I) up to 80% was attained at moderate reaction temperatures below 800 K \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. The interplay between Br2 and I2 was believed to be crucial, combining the high reactivity of bromine and high selectivity of iodine \[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]. Propane (C3H8) can be selectively dehydrogenated to propene (C3H6) through halogen-mediated pathways at relatively mild temperatures between 300 and 700 degrees Celsius \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]. The methods include dehydrogenation with molecular halogens, oxidative dehydrogenation in the presence of halogen and oxygen, propane oxyhalogenation using halides and oxygen, and molten metal halide salts assisted oxidative dehydrogenation \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]. The catalytic transformation of propane in these processes is distinct from other alkanes like methane and ethane due to the specific reactivity of each alkane \[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\].

                卤素辅助的丙烷脱氢方法已被广泛研究，为各种烷烃的脱氢提供了独特的作用，对相应的烯烃具有异常高的选择性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。这些方法包括碘介导的脱氢、碱金属氯化物催化的氧化脱氢、金属碘化物催化的氧化脱氢和双卤素辅助的脱氢。碘介导的脱氢涉及分子碘与烃类的置换，但碘对饱和烃类的反应性较差\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。提出了一个自由基机制，用于描述计量碘化反应，该反应涉及原子和分子碘参与的连续氢原子抽提反应\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。熔盐（金属氯化物和碘化物）催化的氧化脱氢使用支持的碱金属氯化物催化剂，已被证明是轻质脂肪族烷烃（C2C4）氧化脱氢制烯烃的最有希望的候选催化剂之一\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。烷烃的氧化脱氢也可以通过碘化物基熔盐催化\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。最近，Stucky等人开发了一种低温路线，使用双卤素（Br2和I2）作为介质进行丙烷脱氢\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。在800 K以下的温和反应温度下，C3H6+C3H7X（X = Br和I）的高产率高达80%\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。Br2和I2之间的相互作用被认为是关键的，结合了溴的高反应性和碘的高选择性\[10.1021/acs.chemrev.6b00551\]。丙烷（C3H8）可以通过卤素介导的途径在300到700摄氏度的相对温和温度下选择性地脱氢为丙烯（C3H6）\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。这些方法包括分子卤素的脱氢、在卤素和氧气存在下的氧化脱氢、使用卤化物和氧气的丙烷氧卤化，以及熔融金属卤化盐辅助的氧化脱氢\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。这些过程中的丙烷催化转化与甲烷和乙烷等其他烷烃明显不同，因为每种烷烃具有特定的反应性\[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_11\]。
