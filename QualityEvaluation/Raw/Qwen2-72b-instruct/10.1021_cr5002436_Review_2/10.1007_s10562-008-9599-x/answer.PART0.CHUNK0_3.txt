1.The literature does not provide a historical overview of chromium oxide-based catalysts since their discovery in 1933. However, it highlights the significance of chromia-based catalysts in various industrial processes, including catalytic oxidation of organic compounds, large-scale polymerization of ethylene into polyethylene, chlorinated carbon removal, and non-oxidative dehydrogenation of propane to produce propylene. The focus areas of research include the structure of supported chromium oxides, the nature of active sites, and the mechanisms of catalytic reactions. The oxidation state of active chromium species remains a topic of debate, with some studies favoring Cr2+ species and others advocating the importance of Cr3+ sites or both. The preparation methods for chromia-based catalysts, such as impregnation, coprecipitation, and atomic layer epitaxy, are also discussed, with an emphasis on achieving high dispersion of chromia species on the support.

1.文献没有提供自1933年发现以来铬氧化物基催化剂的历史概览。然而，它强调了铬基催化剂在各种工业过程中的重要性，包括有机化合物的催化氧化、大规模将乙烯聚合为聚乙烯、氯化碳的去除以及非氧化脱氢反应，将丙烷转化为丙烯。研究的重点领域包括支撑的铬氧化物的结构、活性位点的本质以及催化反应的机制。活性铬物种的氧化态仍然是一个有争议的话题，一些研究倾向于Cr2+物种，而其他研究则强调Cr3+位点的重要性，或两者都有。还讨论了铬基催化剂的制备方法，如浸渍、共沉淀和原子层外延，重点是实现在支撑材料上高分散的铬氧化物物种。
