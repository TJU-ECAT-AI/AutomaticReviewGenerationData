1.The literature does not provide a historical overview of chromium oxide-based catalysts since their discovery in 1933. However, it highlights the significance of chromia-based catalysts in various industrial processes such as catalytic oxidation of organic compounds, polymerization of ethylene into polyethylene, chlorinated carbon removal, and non-oxidative dehydrogenation of propane to produce propylene. The focus areas include the structure of supported chromium oxides, the nature of active sites, and the mechanisms of catalytic reactions. The oxidation state of active chromium species is a topic of ongoing debate, with some studies favoring Cr2+ species and others advocating the importance of Cr3+ sites or both. The preparation methods for chromia-based catalysts include impregnation, coprecipitation, and atomic layer epitaxy, with the need to achieve high dispersion by anchoring chromia species onto the surface of the support. Sol-gel preparation is a versatile synthetic route that can lead to large surface area materials with metal ions in a highly dispersed state. The use of mesoporous molecular sieves combined with the formation of chromia-doped gel inside their pores may give rise to catalysts with unusual properties, as the porous structure can be independently manipulated, whereas the sol-gel step would favor high chromia dispersion.

1.文献没有提供自1933年发现以来铬氧化物基催化剂的历史概览。然而，它强调了基于铬的催化剂在各种工业过程中的重要性，如有机化合物的催化氧化、乙烯聚合成聚乙烯、氯化碳的去除，以及非氧化脱氢丙烷以生产丙烯。关注领域包括支撑的铬氧化物的结构、活性位点的性质以及催化反应的机制。活性铬物种的氧化态是一个持续争论的话题，一些研究倾向于Cr2+物种，而其他研究则强调Cr3+位点的重要性，或两者都有。铬基催化剂的制备方法包括浸渍、共沉淀和原子层外延，需要通过将铬氧化物物种锚定在支撑物表面来实现高分散。溶胶-凝胶制备是一种通用的合成途径，可以产生具有高度分散金属离子的大表面积材料。将铬氧化物掺杂的凝胶形成在其孔隙内的介孔分子筛的使用可能会产生具有不寻常性质的催化剂，因为多孔结构可以独立地进行操作，而溶胶-凝胶步骤将有利于高铬分散。
