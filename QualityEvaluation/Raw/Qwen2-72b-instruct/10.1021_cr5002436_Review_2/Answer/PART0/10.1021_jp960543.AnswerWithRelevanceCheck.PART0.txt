      <English>
          Chromium oxide-based catalysts have been crucial in the chemical industry for the production of several important commodity chemicals since their discovery in 1933. Although the historical overview of their evolution is not provided, the focus is on the study of supported chromium oxide catalysts using in situ Raman spectroscopy under catalytic conditions. The research highlights the evolution of surface chromium oxide species on various oxide supports and their reactivity in methanol oxidation and butane dehydrogenation/oxidation. The study contributes to the understanding of the structure and redox behavior of chromium oxide catalysts, which are crucial for their industrial applications in the production of high-density polyethylene (HDPE) and linear low-density polyethylene (LLDPE) using Cr/SiO2 catalysts, known as the Phillips catalyst, and the dehydrogenation of butane to butene using Cr/Al2O3 catalysts. The research focus areas include the elucidation of the molecular structure of supported chromium oxides, which depends on the pretreatment, Cr loading, and support type and composition. Raman spectroscopy is noted as an excellent technique for studying supported metal oxide catalysts, as it can discriminate between different molecular states of supported metal oxide species. The redox behavior of supported chromium oxides is strongly dependent on the specific support ligand, and the catalytic action of supported chromium oxide catalysts in redox reactions is determined by its reduction and reoxidation properties.

          自1933年发现以来，铬氧化物基催化剂在化工行业中对于生产几种重要的商品化学品至关重要。虽然没有提供其演变的历史概览，但研究重点是使用原位拉曼光谱在催化条件下研究支撑的铬氧化物催化剂。研究强调了在各种氧化物支撑上表面铬氧化物物种的演变及其在甲醇氧化和丁烷脱氢/氧化中的反应性。该研究有助于理解铬氧化物催化剂的结构和氧化还原行为，这些催化剂对于工业应用至关重要，例如使用Cr/SiO2催化剂（即菲利普斯催化剂）生产高密度聚乙烯（HDPE）和线性低密度聚乙烯（LLDPE），以及使用Cr/Al2O3催化剂将丁烷脱氢为丁烯。研究重点领域包括阐明支撑铬氧化物的分子结构，这取决于预处理、Cr负载量和支持物类型和组成。指出拉曼光谱是研究支撑金属氧化物催化剂的一种极佳技术，因为它可以区分支撑金属氧化物物种的不同分子状态。支撑铬氧化物的氧化还原行为强烈依赖于特定支撑配体，支撑铬氧化物催化剂在氧化还原反应中的催化作用由其还原和再氧化性质决定。
