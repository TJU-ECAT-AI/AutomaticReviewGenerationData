                    The evolution of chromium oxide-based catalysts since their discovery in 1933 has been pivotal in industrial dehydrogenation processes, particularly for propane dehydrogenation to propene [\[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]]. High dispersion of chromium oxide species on mesoporous supports enhances catalytic activity, with CO2 acting as an oxidant in oxidative dehydrogenation [\[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]]. Research has focused on the structure of supported chromium oxides, active sites, and reaction mechanisms, with the oxidation state of chromium species (Cr2+ or Cr3+) being a debated topic [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. Preparation methods, including impregnation, coprecipitation, and atomic layer epitaxy, aim to achieve high dispersion of chromia on supports [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. Sol-gel preparation is noted for creating materials with high surface area and dispersed metal ions [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. Combining mesoporous molecular sieves with chromia-doped gel inside their pores results in catalysts with unique properties [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. Chromium oxide-based catalysts have been pivotal in the chemical industry for producing commodity chemicals [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. In situ Raman spectroscopy has revealed the evolution of surface chromium oxide species on various supports and their reactivity in methanol oxidation and butane dehydrogenation/oxidation [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. The molecular structure of supported chromium oxides, influenced by pretreatment, Cr loading, and support type, is a key research area [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. Raman spectroscopy is an excellent technique for studying supported metal oxide catalysts, as it can distinguish between different molecular states [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. The redox behavior of supported chromium oxides is support-dependent, affecting their catalytic action in redox reactions [\[10.1021/jp960543\]].
                    ※※※
                    Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The evolution of chromium oxide-based catalysts since 1933 has been marked by significant advancements in understanding their structure, active sites, and reaction mechanisms. The pivotal role of these catalysts in industrial dehydrogenation processes, particularly propane dehydrogenation to propene, has been underscored by their ability to achieve high catalytic activity through high dispersion of chromium oxide species on mesoporous supports. The use of CO2 as an oxidant in oxidative dehydrogenation has also been a key development. Future research directions might focus on the optimization of preparation methods, such as sol-gel and atomic layer epitaxy, to further enhance the dispersion and stability of chromium species. The exploration of new support materials and the development of in situ spectroscopic techniques for real-time monitoring of catalytic reactions could provide deeper insights into the dynamic behavior of chromium oxide species. Additionally, the investigation of the role of chromium species in different oxidation states and the impact of promoters on catalytic performance could lead to the design of more efficient and selective catalysts for dehydrogenation processes.

                    自1933年发现以来，基于铬氧化物的催化剂在工业脱氢过程中，特别是在丙烷脱氢制丙烯中起着关键作用 [\[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]]. 高分散的铬氧化物物种在介孔支撑上的催化活性增强，CO2在氧化脱氢中作为氧化剂发挥作用 [\[10.1016/j.apcata.2009.11.040\]]. 研究集中在支撑的铬氧化物的结构，活性位点和反应机制上，铬物种的氧化态（Cr2+或Cr3+）是一个有争议的话题 [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. 制备方法，包括浸渍，共沉淀和原子层外延，旨在实现支撑上的高铬酸盐分散 [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. 溶胶-凝胶制备被注意用于创建具有高表面积和分散金属离子的材料 [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. 将铬酸盐掺杂的凝胶与介孔分子筛内部的孔隙结合，产生具有独特性质的催化剂 [\[10.1007/s10562-008-9599-x\]]. 基于铬氧化物的催化剂在化学工业中生产商品化学品方面起着关键作用 [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. 原位拉曼光谱揭示了各种支撑上表面铬氧化物物种的演变及其在甲醇氧化和丁烷脱氢/氧化中的反应性 [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. 受支撑影响的支撑的铬氧化物的分子结构是一个关键的研究领域 [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. 拉曼光谱是研究支撑金属氧化物催化剂的优秀技术，因为它可以区分不同的分子状态 [\[10.1021/jp960543\]]. 支撑依赖的支撑的铬氧化物的氧化还原行为影响它们在氧化还原反应中的催化作用 [\[10.1021/jp960543\]].
                    ※※※
                    综合理解与未来展望：自1933年以来，基于铬氧化物的催化剂的演变被显著的进展所标记，理解它们的结构，活性位点和反应机制。这些催化剂在工业脱氢过程中的关键作用，特别是在丙烷脱氢制丙烯中，通过在介孔支撑上高分散的铬氧化物物种实现高催化活性的能力得到了强调。CO2作为氧化脱氢中的氧化剂的使用也是一个关键的发展。未来的研究方向可能集中在优化制备方法，如溶胶-凝胶和原子层外延，以进一步增强铬物种的分散性和稳定性。探索新的支撑材料和开发原位光谱技术进行实时监测催化反应可能为理解铬氧化物物种的动态行为提供更深入的见解。此外，研究不同氧化态的铬物种的作用和促进剂对催化性能的影响可能导向设计更有效和选择性的脱氢过程催化剂。
