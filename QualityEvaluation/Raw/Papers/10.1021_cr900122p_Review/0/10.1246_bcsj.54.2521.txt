Highly Active Aluminas far Isomerization of 1-Butene and Hydrogen Exchanges between Ethylene or Cyclopropane and Deuterium Get access
Masahiro Utiyama,
Hideshi Hattori,
Kozo Tanabe
Bulletin of the Chemical Society of Japan, Volume 54, Issue 8, August 1981, Pages 2521–2522, https://doi.org/10.1246/bcsj.54.2521
Published: 02 June 2006 Article history
Cite
Permissions
Share
Abstract
Among eleven kinds of aluminas, alumina V prepared from aluminium sulfate with urea exhibited a pronounced activity for the isomerization of 1-butene, while alumina III or II prepared from aluminium nitrate with ammonia or urea showed the highest activities for the exchange reactions between ethylene or cyclopropane and deuterium, respectively.
