      <English>
          The primary challenges in elucidating Ga speciation in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts under propane dehydrogenation (PDH) conditions include:

1. The active gallium species are highly dispersed and only present under reaction conditions, making them difficult to detect and characterize.
2. The nature of the active species remains unclear due to their transient nature during catalysis.

In situ and operando characterization techniques, particularly X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS), contribute to overcoming these challenges and advancing our understanding of structure-activity relationships in these catalytic systems by:

1. Allowing detection of dispersed and isolated species under reaction conditions.
2. Providing information on the local structure and oxidation state of gallium species during catalysis.
3. Enabling the correlation between structural changes and the degree of reduction of gallium species.
4. Facilitating the study of catalyst behavior under realistic reaction conditions, including temperature and gas composition.
5. Revealing the reduction process from Ga3+ binuclear species to isolated Ga+ ions.
6. Providing insights into the nature of the active gallium species, such as their oxidation state, coordination number, and location within the zeolite framework.

These advanced characterization techniques have significantly advanced our understanding of the evolution of gallium species during propane dehydrogenation, revealing the formation of isolated Ga+ species as the likely active sites for the reaction. This information is critical for establishing structure-activity relationships and guiding the design of improved catalysts for propane dehydrogenation and related reactions.

      <Chinese>
          在丙烷脱氢（PDH）条件下阐明Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中Ga物种的主要挑战包括：

1. 活性镓物种高度分散，且仅在反应条件下存在，使其难以检测和表征。
2. 由于活性物种在催化过程中的瞬态性质，其本质仍不清楚。

原位和操作表征技术，特别是X射线吸收光谱（XAS），通过以下方式有助于克服这些挑战并推进我们对这些催化系统中结构-活性关系的理解：

1. 允许在反应条件下检测分散和孤立的物种。
2. 提供催化过程中镓物种的局部结构和氧化态信息。
3. 能够关联镓物种的结构变化和还原程度。
4. 促进在实际反应条件下（包括温度和气体组成）研究催化剂行为。
5. 揭示了从Ga3+双核物种到孤立Ga+离子的还原过程。
6. 提供有关活性镓物种性质的见解，如它们的氧化态、配位数和在沸石骨架中的位置。

这些先进的表征技术显著提高了我们对丙烷脱氢过程中镓物种演变的理解，揭示了孤立Ga+物种的形成可能是反应的活性位点。这一信息对建立结构-活性关系和指导改进丙烷脱氢及相关反应的催化剂设计至关重要。
