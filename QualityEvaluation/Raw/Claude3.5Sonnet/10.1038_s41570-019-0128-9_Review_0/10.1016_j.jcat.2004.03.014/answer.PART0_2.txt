The literature provides insights into the performance of chromium oxide catalysts, particularly Cr-MCM-41, in CO2 oxidative dehydrogenation reactions:

1. Support material: MCM-41 was found to be an effective support for chromium oxide catalysts. Cr-MCM-41 showed the highest activity among the tested M-MCM-41 catalysts, with 30% propane conversion and over 90% propene selectivity.

2. CO2 partial pressure: Increasing CO2 partial pressure led to increased propene and CO formation rates, while H2 formation remained constant. This suggests that CO2 plays a role in shifting the reaction equilibrium and potentially in catalyst regeneration.

3. Mechanistic pathway: The reaction involves a redox cycle between Cr(VI)O4 tetrahedra (active species) and Cr(III)O6 octahedra (less active species). Cr(VI) in tetrahedral coordination acts as the active site for propane dehydrogenation, while being reduced to Cr(III) in octahedral coordination.

4. Catalyst regeneration: Both O2 and CO2 can regenerate the active Cr(VI)O4 species from the reduced Cr(III)O6 form, with O2 being more efficient. This regeneration ability is crucial for maintaining catalyst activity.

5. Reaction conditions: The study used a gas mixture of N2:CO2:C3H8 at a 10:34:6 molar ratio, achieving 17% propane conversion and 15.9% propene yield.

6. Catalyst treatment: Oxidation treatment with O2/N2 at 550°C for 1 hour was effective in maintaining catalyst activity.

Key challenges and trade-offs include:

1. Balancing the CO2 partial pressure to optimize propene formation while managing CO production.
2. Maintaining the active Cr(VI) species during the reaction, as it tends to reduce to less active Cr(III).
3. Optimizing catalyst regeneration methods to efficiently convert Cr(III) back to Cr(VI) without disrupting the reaction.
4. Managing coke formation, which was enhanced in CO2 atmosphere compared to He.

The study primarily focused on Cr-MCM-41, so direct comparisons with other supports like SiO2, H-ZSM-5, Al2O3, ZrO2, and CeO2 are not available in this specific literature. Further research would be needed to fully compare the performance across these different support materials.

6. 催化剂处理：在550°C下用O2/N2处理1小时能有效维持催化剂活性。
