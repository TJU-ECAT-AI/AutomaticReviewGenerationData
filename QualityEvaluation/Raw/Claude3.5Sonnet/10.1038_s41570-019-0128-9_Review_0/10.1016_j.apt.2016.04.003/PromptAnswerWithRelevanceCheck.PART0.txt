[3] "The reactant stream, consisting of 10 vol.% ethane, 50 vol.% carbon dioxide and 40 vol.% nitrogen, was introduced into the reactor at a total flow rate of 75 ml/min. The reaction temperature ranged between 550 and 700 C."

[4] "It has been indicated that CO2 increases the yield of ethylene via a redox mechanism in which the catalyst undergoes reduction by ethane and re-oxidation (by CO2) cycles or reaction coupling between a simple dehydrogenation of ethane and the reverse watergas shift reaction (RWGS)"
