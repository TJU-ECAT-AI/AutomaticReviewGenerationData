      <English>
          While the literature primarily focuses on gallium oxide catalysts rather than chromium oxide, it provides insights that may be relevant to understanding the influence of support materials and CO2 on oxidative dehydrogenation reactions:

1. Support materials significantly impact catalyst performance. TiO2, Al2O3, and ZrO2 supports resulted in better dehydrogenation catalysts compared to SiO2 and MgO, due to a higher number of medium-strong and strong acid sites on the surface.

2. CO2 partial pressure affects catalyst activity differently depending on the support. It promotes activity for Ga2O3/TiO2 but inhibits it for Ga2O3/ZrO2 and Ga2O3/Al2O3. An optimal CO2/C3H8 ratio exists (e.g., 2 for Ga2O3/TiO2) beyond which activity decreases.

3. CO2 plays dual roles: positively by removing adsorbed H2 through the reverse water-gas shift reaction, and negatively by displacing adsorbed propane from basic sites on the catalyst surface.

4. The interaction between the active metal oxide and the support material is crucial in determining catalyst behavior, influencing H2 adsorption capacity and acid-base properties.

5. Similar effects have been observed for silica-supported Cr2O3, suggesting that these findings may be relevant to chromium oxide catalysts as well.

Key challenges in optimizing overall performance likely include:
- Balancing the positive and negative effects of CO2
- Optimizing support-metal oxide interactions
- Finding the ideal CO2/hydrocarbon ratio
- Maximizing the number of appropriate acid sites on the catalyst surface

Trade-offs may exist between activity, selectivity, and stability as reaction conditions are varied.

      <Chinese>
          虽然这篇文献主要集中在氧化镓催化剂而非氧化铬催化剂，但它提供了一些可能与理解载体材料和CO2对氧化脱氢反应影响相关的见解：

1. 载体材料显著影响催化剂性能。TiO2、Al2O3和ZrO2载体比SiO2和MgO载体产生更好的脱氢催化剂，这是由于表面上具有更多的中强度和强度酸性位点。

2. CO2分压对不同载体的催化剂活性影响不同。它促进了Ga2O3/TiO2的活性，但抑制了Ga2O3/ZrO2和Ga2O3/Al2O3的活性。存在最佳CO2/C3H8比率（如Ga2O3/TiO2为2），超过此比率活性降低。

3. CO2起双重作用：正面作用是通过逆水煤气反应除去吸附的H2，负面作用是置换催化剂表面碱性位点上吸附的丙烷。

4. 活性金属氧化物与载体材料之间的相互作用在决定催化剂行为方面起着关键作用，影响H2吸附能力和酸碱性质。

5. 类似的效应也在二氧化硅负载的Cr2O3上观察到，表明这些发现可能也适用于氧化铬催化剂。

优化整体性能的主要挑战可能包括：
- 平衡CO2的正面和负面效应
- 优化载体-金属氧化物相互作用
- 找到理想的CO2/烃比
- 最大化催化剂表面适当酸性位点的数量

在改变反应条件时，活性、选择性和稳定性之间可能存在权衡。
