2. Optimizing support-chromium interactions: The choice of support material significantly affects catalyst performance, necessitating careful selection and optimization.

3. Regeneration efficiency: Although oxygen treatment can partially regenerate the catalysts, complete restoration of activity remains a challenge.

4. Temperature optimization: Higher temperatures increase conversion but may affect selectivity and accelerate catalyst deactivation.

To fully optimize overall performance, further research is needed to address these challenges, particularly in developing more stable high-valent Cr species and improving regeneration techniques.
