<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Chromium oxide-based catalysts have become crucial in chemical industries since their discovery, finding significant applications in polymerization and dehydrogenation processes. Key industrial applications include:

1. The Philips polymerization catalyst (chromium-on-silica), responsible for about one-third of global high-density polyethylene (HDPE) production.

2. Light paraffin dehydrogenation catalysts (chromium-on-alumina) used in the Houdry CATOFIN and CATADIENE processes.

Research focus areas have centered on understanding the molecular structure of chromium oxides and their relationship to catalytic performance. This includes investigating:

1. The role of different chromium oxidation states (Cr6+, Cr3+, and Cr2+) in alkane dehydrogenation reactions.
2. Support materials (e.g., silica, alumina, and silica-alumina mixtures) and their effects on catalyst performance.
3. The nature of active sites and reaction mechanisms.

The evolution of these catalysts has been driven by their industrial importance, leading to extensive research aimed at improving their efficiency and understanding their fundamental properties. This ongoing work continues to contribute to their significance in dehydrogenation processes and other applications.

自发现以来，铬氧化物基催化剂在化学工业中变得至关重要，在聚合和脱氢过程中找到了重要应用。主要工业应用包括：

1. 菲利普斯聚合催化剂（铬-硅）负责全球约三分之一的高密度聚乙烯（HDPE）生产。

2. 轻质石蜡脱氢催化剂（铬-氧化铝）用于Houdry CATOFIN和CATADIENE工艺。

研究重点领域集中在理解铬氧化物的分子结构及其与催化性能的关系。这包括研究：

1. 不同铬氧化态（Cr6+、Cr3+和Cr2+）在烷烃脱氢反应中的作用。
2. 载体材料（如二氧化硅、氧化铝和硅铝混合物）及其对催化剂性能的影响。
3. 活性位点的性质和反应机理。

这些催化剂的发展一直由其工业重要性驱动，导致了广泛的研究，旨在提高其效率并理解其基本特性。这项持续的工作继续为其在脱氢过程和其他应用中的重要性做出贡献。
