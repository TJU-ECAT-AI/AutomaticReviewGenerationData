<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration differ between redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts in the oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) of alkanes. Traditional oxidants like O2 typically involve a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism with redox-active catalysts, where lattice oxygen participates in the reaction. In contrast, CO2 as an oxidant offers a milder alternative that can suppress over-oxidation.

For CO2-ODHP, the effectiveness of catalysts varies. CeO2-supported bimetallic catalysts like FeNi and NiPt have shown promise. The FeNi catalyst favors C-H bond activation for the ODH pathway, while NiPt promotes C-C bond cleavage for dry reforming. The choice of catalyst influences the reaction pathway and product selectivity.

CO2 activation mechanisms differ among catalysts. On FeNi surfaces, CO2 likely dissociates to provide oxygen for the ODH reaction. The FeO/Ni interface facilitates selective C-H bond scission to produce propylene. In contrast, on Pt-terminated Ni3Pt surfaces, CO2 activation leads to C-C bond cleavage, promoting the dry reforming pathway.

The effectiveness of CO2 as an oxidant is also related to its ability to shift the reaction equilibrium by consuming H2 through the reverse water-gas shift reaction. This increases propane conversion compared to direct dehydrogenation.

While specific energy barriers for CO2 dissociation are not provided, the study suggests that the nature of the catalyst surface (e.g., bulk Fe3Ni, FeO/Ni interface, or Pt-terminated Ni3Pt) significantly influences the activation pathway and energy barriers for CO2-mediated reactions.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

在烷烃的氧化脱氢(ODH)反应中，氧化还原活性和非活性金属氧化物催化剂的C-H键活化和催化剂再生机理存在差异。传统的氧化剂如O2通常在氧化还原活性催化剂上遵循Mars-van Krevelen机理，其中晶格氧参与反应。相比之下，CO2作为氧化剂提供了一种更温和的替代方案，可以抑制过度氧化。

对于CO2-ODHP，不同催化剂的有效性各不相同。CeO2负载的双金属催化剂如FeNi和NiPt表现出良好的前景。FeNi催化剂有利于ODH途径的C-H键活化，而NiPt促进干重整的C-C键断裂。催化剂的选择影响反应途径和产物选择性。

CO2的活化机理因催化剂而异。在FeNi表面，CO2可能解离以提供ODH反应所需的氧。FeO/Ni界面促进选择性C-H键断裂生成丙烯。相比之下，在Pt终止的Ni3Pt表面，CO2的活化导致C-C键断裂，促进干重整途径。

CO2作为氧化剂的有效性还与其通过逆水气变换反应消耗H2从而改变反应平衡的能力有关。这增加了与直接脱氢相比的丙烷转化率。

虽然没有提供CO2解离的具体能垒，但研究表明催化剂表面的性质（如体相Fe3Ni、FeO/Ni界面或Pt终止的Ni3Pt）显著影响CO2介导反应的活化途径和能垒。
