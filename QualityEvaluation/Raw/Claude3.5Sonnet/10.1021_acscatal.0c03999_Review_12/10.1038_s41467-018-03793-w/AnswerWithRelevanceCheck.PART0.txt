      <English>
          The mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration differ between redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) of alkanes. CO2 acts as a milder oxidant compared to traditional oxidants like O2, suppressing over-oxidation and minimizing carbon oxide production.

For CO2-ODHP, bimetallic catalysts supported on CeO2 show promise:
1. Fe3Ni/CeO2 favors C-H bond activation for the ODH pathway, promoting propylene production.
2. Ni3Pt/CeO2 favors C-C bond cleavage, leading to dry reforming of propane.

The effectiveness of catalysts in activating CO2 varies:
- CeO2 support can store/release oxygen, potentially facilitating CO2 activation and providing lattice oxygen for coke suppression.
- Fe3Ni surfaces likely dissociate CO2 to provide oxygen for the ODH reaction.
- The FeO/Ni interface facilitates selective C-H bond scission.
- Pt-terminated Ni3Pt surfaces promote C-C bond cleavage for dry reforming.

CO2 also enhances propane conversion by consuming H2 through the reverse water-gas shift reaction. While specific energy barriers for CO2 dissociation are not provided, the catalyst surface composition significantly influences the activation pathway and energy barriers for CO2-mediated reactions.

      <Chinese>
          在烷烃的氧化脱氢(ODH)反应中，氧化还原活性和非活性金属氧化物催化剂的C-H键活化和催化剂再生机理存在差异。与传统的O2等氧化剂相比，CO2作为更温和的氧化剂，可抑制过度氧化并最小化碳氧化物的生成。

对于CO2-ODHP，以CeO2为载体的双金属催化剂表现出良好前景：
1. Fe3Ni/CeO2有利于ODH途径的C-H键活化，促进丙烯生成。
2. Ni3Pt/CeO2有利于C-C键断裂，导致丙烷的干重整。

不同催化剂在活化CO2方面的有效性各不相同：
- CeO2载体可以储存/释放氧，潜在地促进CO2的活化并提供晶格氧以抑制积碳。
- Fe3Ni表面可能解离CO2以提供ODH反应所需的氧。
- FeO/Ni界面促进选择性C-H键断裂。
- 以Pt为终端的Ni3Pt表面促进干重整的C-C键断裂。

CO2还通过逆水气变换反应消耗H2来增强丙烷的转化。虽然未提供CO2解离的具体能垒，但催化剂表面组成显著影响CO2介导反应的活化途径和能垒。
