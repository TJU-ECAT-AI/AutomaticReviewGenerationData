<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration differ between redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) of alkanes. 

For redox-active catalysts like V2O5 or VOx/SiO2, the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism typically operates. The lattice oxygen participates in C-H bond activation, forming hydroxyl groups and reducing the metal center. The catalyst is then regenerated by gas-phase oxygen, which re-oxidizes the metal and removes the hydroxyl groups.

In contrast, redox-inactive catalysts like CeO2 cannot easily change oxidation states. Instead, they may activate C-H bonds through surface oxygen vacancies or Lewis acid sites. Regeneration occurs through adsorption and activation of oxidants on the surface.

Traditional oxidants like O2 are strong and can lead to overoxidation. CO2 as a soft oxidant offers several advantages: it reduces deep oxidation and coking, while promoting propane conversion through the reverse water-gas shift reaction.

Different catalysts show varying effectiveness in activating CO2 for propane ODH:

- CeO2 is promising due to its ability to directly cleave the C=O bond in CO2.
- Fe-Ni clusters on CeO2 favor C-H bond activation.
- Pt-containing bimetallic clusters tend to promote C-C cleavage instead.

The effectiveness depends on the catalyst's ability to lower the energy barrier for CO2 dissociation and subsequent oxygen insertion into the alkane. CeO2-based catalysts, especially when doped with transition metals like Fe, show good potential for CO2 activation and selective ODH of propane.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

在烷烃氧化脱氢(ODH)反应中，氧化还原活性和非氧化还原活性金属氧化物催化剂的C-H键活化和催化剂再生机理存在差异。

对于V2O5或VOx/SiO2等氧化还原活性催化剂，通常遵循Mars-van Krevelen机理。晶格氧参与C-H键活化，形成羟基并还原金属中心。然后气相氧重新氧化金属并去除羟基，从而再生催化剂。

相比之下，CeO2等氧化还原非活性催化剂难以改变氧化态。它们可能通过表面氧空位或Lewis酸位活化C-H键。再生是通过氧化剂在表面吸附和活化实现的。

传统氧化剂如O2强度较大，可能导致过度氧化。CO2作为软氧化剂具有几个优势：它减少深度氧化和积碳，同时通过逆水煤气反应促进丙烷转化。

不同催化剂在活化CO2用于丙烷ODH方面表现不同：

- CeO2由于能够直接裂解CO2中的C=O键而具有前景。
- CeO2上的Fe-Ni簇有利于C-H键活化。
- 含Pt的双金属簇倾向于促进C-C键裂解。

效果取决于催化剂降低CO2解离能垒和随后氧插入烷烃的能力。基于CeO2的催化剂，尤其是掺杂Fe等过渡金属时，在CO2活化和选择性丙烷ODH方面表现出良好的潜力。
