<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The mechanisms of C-H bond activation and catalyst regeneration differ between redox-active and redox-inactive metal oxide catalysts in oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) of alkanes. 

For redox-active catalysts like V2O5 or VOx/SiO2, the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism typically operates. This involves the lattice oxygen of the catalyst abstracting hydrogen from the alkane, followed by catalyst re-oxidation by gas-phase oxygen. The catalyst undergoes redox cycles.

In contrast, redox-inactive catalysts like CeO2 cannot easily change oxidation states. They likely activate C-H bonds through surface oxygen vacancies or adsorbed oxygen species. Regeneration occurs through gas-phase oxygen replenishing these active sites.

Traditional oxidants like O2 can lead to overoxidation. CO2 as a softer oxidant has several advantages: it avoids deep oxidation, reduces coking, and enhances alkane conversion via the reverse water-gas shift reaction.

For CO2 activation, CeO2 shows promise due to its ability to directly cleave the C=O bond. Transition metal clusters like Fe-Ni on CeO2 are effective, with Fe-Ni favoring C-H activation while Pt-based catalysts favor C-C cleavage. The effectiveness depends on the catalyst's ability to lower energy barriers for CO2 dissociation and subsequent ODH steps.

Overall, CO2-assisted ODH over metal-doped CeO2 catalysts offers a promising approach by combining the benefits of CO2 as a soft oxidant with tunable C-H activation properties of transition metals and the CO2 activation ability of CeO2.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

在烷烃的氧化脱氢(ODH)过程中，氧化还原活性和非氧化还原活性金属氧化物催化剂的C-H键活化和催化剂再生机制有所不同。

对于V2O5或VOx/SiO2等氧化还原活性催化剂，通常遵循Mars-van Krevelen机制。这涉及催化剂的晶格氧从烷烃中提取氢，随后催化剂被气相氧重新氧化。催化剂经历氧化还原循环。

相比之下，CeO2等非氧化还原活性催化剂不能轻易改变氧化态。它们可能通过表面氧空位或吸附氧物种来活化C-H键。再生是通过气相氧补充这些活性位点来实现的。

传统的氧化剂如O2可能导致过度氧化。CO2作为一种较温和的氧化剂具有几个优势：它避免了深度氧化，减少了结焦，并通过逆水气变换反应增强了烷烃的转化。

对于CO2的活化，CeO2因其直接裂解C=O键的能力而显示出前景。CeO2上的Fe-Ni等过渡金属簇是有效的，Fe-Ni有利于C-H活化，而基于Pt的催化剂有利于C-C裂解。效果取决于催化剂降低CO2解离和随后ODH步骤能量障碍的能力。

总的来说，在金属掺杂CeO2催化剂上进行CO2辅助ODH提供了一种有前景的方法，它结合了CO2作为温和氧化剂的优势，过渡金属可调的C-H活化特性，以及CeO2的CO2活化能力。
