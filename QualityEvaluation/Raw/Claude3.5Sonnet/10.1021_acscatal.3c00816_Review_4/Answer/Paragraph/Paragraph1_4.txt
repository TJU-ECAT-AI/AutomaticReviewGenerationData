<English>
The preparation methods, composition, and zeolite topology significantly influence the performance of zeolite-based catalysts in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions, particularly for BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate zeolites. Key factors contributing to improved propylene selectivity, conversion rates, and catalyst stability include:

1. Preparation methods:
   - Dealumination of Beta zeolites creates T-vacant sites and reduces acidity, enhancing structural and acidic properties [10.1002/cctc.201801708].
   - Two-step post-synthesis procedures involving dealumination and metal incorporation allow better control of metal distribution [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019].
   - Sequential impregnation methods, such as Zn ion-exchange followed by Pt impregnation, result in higher metal dispersion and improved stability [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4].

2. Composition:
   - Bimetallic systems, particularly PtSn, show improved performance and stability [10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008].
   - Optimal metal loadings are crucial: for example, 0.5 wt% Pt and 1.6-2.6 wt% Zn in Beta zeolites [10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028].
   - The Si/Al ratio significantly affects catalyst performance, with intermediate ratios (8.7 ≤ nSi/nAl ≤ 26) often yielding the best results [10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028].

3. Zeolite topology:
   - BEA-type zeolites feature a three-dimensional structure with 12-ring channel systems, providing larger pores than ZSM-5 and facilitating better dispersion of metal species [10.1002/cctc.201801708].
   - SAPO-34, a silicoaluminophosphate zeolite with CHA topology, shows superior performance due to its unique structure, weak surface acidity, and excellent shape selectivity for propylene [10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008].

4. Metal-support interactions:
   - Strong metal-support interactions (SMSI) can be regulated by altering defect amounts in supports [10.1039/D0CS00814A].
   - Framework-Sn in Beta zeolites can anchor Pt precursors, forming confined Pt subnanometric clusters with improved stability [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022].

5. Acidity control:
   - Reducing Brønsted acidity or controlling its distribution within the zeolite structure improves catalyst performance [10.1021/ie901465s].
   - The ratio of Lewis to Brønsted acid sites (L/B ratio) is crucial, with a high L/B ratio beneficial for PDH [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019].

6. Confinement effect:
   - Zeolite pores and electronic effects of additives (e.g., Sn) inhibit coke formation and sintering of metal clusters [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022].
   - The confinement of metal species in zeolite frameworks leads to improved dispersion and stability [10.1039/C9CY01907C].

These factors collectively contribute to improved propylene selectivity (up to 99%), high conversion rates (40-72%), and excellent catalyst stability (maintaining activity for over 250 hours) in PDH reactions using BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate zeolite-based catalysts [10.1039/C9CY01907C] [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The development of zeolite-based catalysts for propane dehydrogenation has shown significant progress, particularly in the areas of BEA topologic and silicoaluminophosphate zeolites. The synergistic effects of carefully tailored preparation methods, optimized compositions, and strategic metal-support interactions have led to catalysts with remarkable performance and stability.

Looking forward, several promising research directions emerge:

1. Advanced characterization techniques: Developing and applying more sophisticated in-situ and operando spectroscopic methods could provide deeper insights into the dynamic behavior of active sites during PDH reactions, enabling further optimization of catalyst designs.

2. Hierarchical zeolites: Exploring the potential of hierarchical zeolite structures that combine micropores and mesopores could enhance mass transfer properties while maintaining the shape-selective advantages of zeolites.

3. Single-atom catalysts: Further investigation into the preparation and stabilization of single-atom catalysts within zeolite frameworks could lead to highly efficient and stable PDH catalysts with maximized atom utilization.

4. Machine learning and high-throughput experimentation: Implementing these techniques could accelerate the discovery and optimization of novel zeolite-based catalysts for PDH, potentially uncovering unexpected synergies between composition, structure, and performance.

5. Green synthesis methods: Developing more environmentally friendly and cost-effective methods for zeolite synthesis and modification could make these advanced catalysts more accessible for industrial applications.

6. Multifunctional catalysts: Designing zeolite-based catalysts that can perform PDH in tandem with other valuable transformations could lead to more efficient and integrated chemical processes.

By pursuing these research directions, the field of zeolite-based catalysts for propane dehydrogenation has the potential to achieve even higher levels of performance, stability, and sustainability, paving the way for more efficient and environmentally friendly production of propylene.

<Chinese>
制备方法、成分和沸石拓扑结构显著影响了沸石基催化剂在丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中的性能，特别是对于BEA拓扑结构和硅铝磷酸盐沸石。以下是影响丙烯选择性、转化率和催化剂稳定性的关键因素：

1. 制备方法：
   - Beta沸石的脱铝形成T空位和降低酸性，增强了结构和酸性特性 [10.1002/cctc.201801708]。
   - 涉及脱铝和金属引入的两步后合成程序可以更好地控制金属分布 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]。
   - 顺序浸渍方法，如锌离子交换后铂浸渍，可获得更高的金属分散度和改善稳定性 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_4]。

2. 成分：
   - 双金属体系，特别是PtSn，表现出更好的性能和稳定性 [10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008]。
   - 最佳金属负载量至关重要：例如，Beta沸石中0.5 wt% Pt和1.6-2.6 wt% Zn [10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028]。
   - Si/Al比显著影响催化剂性能，中等比例（8.7 ≤ nSi/nAl ≤ 26）通常产生最佳结果 [10.1016/j.apcata.2006.03.028]。

3. 沸石拓扑结构：
   - BEA型沸石具有三维结构和12元环通道系统，提供比ZSM-5更大的孔道，有利于金属物种更好地分散 [10.1002/cctc.201801708]。
   - SAPO-34，一种具有CHA拓扑结构的硅铝磷酸盐沸石，由于其独特的结构、弱表面酸性和对丙烯的优异形状选择性，表现出卓越的性能 [10.1016/j.catcom.2009.07.008]。

4. 金属-载体相互作用：
   - 通过改变载体中的缺陷数量可以调节强金属-载体相互作用（SMSI）[10.039/D0CS00814A]。
   - Beta沸石中的骨架锡可以锚定铂前体，形成受限的亚纳米铂簇，提高稳定性 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022]。

5. 酸度控制：
   - 降低Brønsted酸性或控制其在沸石结构中的分布可改善催化剂性能 [10.1021/ie901465s]。
   - Lewis酸位与Brønsted酸位的比例（L/B比）至关重要，高L/B比有利于PDH反应 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.12.019]。

6. 限域效应：
   - 沸石孔道和添加剂（如Sn）的电子效应抑制了焦炭形成和金属簇的烧结 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.03.022]。
   - 金属物种在沸石骨架中的限域导致分散度和稳定性的提高 [10.1039/C9CY01907C]。

这些因素共同促进了BEA拓扑结构和硅铝磷酸盐沸石基催化剂在PDH反应中丙烯选择性（高达99%）、高转化率（40-72%）和优异的催化剂稳定性（活性保持超过250小时）[10.1039/C9CY01907C] [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.12.037]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：丙烷脱氢沸石基催化剂的发展取得了显著进展，特别是在BEA拓扑结构和硅铝磷酸盐沸石领域。精心设计的制备方法、优化的成分和策略性的金属-载体相互作用的协同效应，导致了具有卓越性能和稳定性的催化剂。

展望未来，几个有前景的研究方向浮现：

1. 先进表征技术：开发和应用更复杂的原位和操作中光谱方法，可以深入了解PDH反应过程中活性位点的动态行为，从而进一步优化催化剂设计。

2. 分级沸石：探索结合微孔和介孔的分级沸石结构的潜力，可以增强传质性能，同时保持沸石的形状选择性优势。

3. 单原子催化剂：进一步研究沸石骨架中单原子催化剂的制备和稳定化，可能导致高效稳定的PDH催化剂，实现原子利用率的最大化。

4. 机器学习和高通量实验：实施这些技术可以加速PDH新型沸石基催化剂的发现和优化，可能揭示成分、结构和性能之间意想不到的协同作用。

5. 绿色合成方法：开发更环保、更具成本效益的沸石合成和改性方法，可以使这些先进催化剂在工业应用中更易获得。

6. 多功能催化剂：设计能够同时进行PDH和其他有价值转化的沸石基催化剂，可能导致更高效和集成的化学过程。

通过追求这些研究方向，丙烷脱氢沸石基催化剂领域有潜力实现更高水平的性能、稳定性和可持续性，为更高效、更环保的丙烯生产铺平道路。
