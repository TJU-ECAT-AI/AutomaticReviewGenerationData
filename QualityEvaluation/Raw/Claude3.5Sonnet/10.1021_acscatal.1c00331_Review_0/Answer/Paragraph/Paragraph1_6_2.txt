The process involves the conversion of propane to propene and hydrogen using specific catalysts under high-temperature conditions.

Key considerations for PDH include:

   a) Noble metal-based catalysts: Primarily platinum (Pt) based, often modified with promoters like tin (Sn), indium (In), or gallium (Ga).

3. Industrial applications: PDH has become increasingly important for on-purpose propylene production, with several commercial processes in operation, such as the Oleflex (UOP) and Catofin (Lummus) processes [Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The field of PDH catalysis is rapidly evolving, driven by the need for more efficient and sustainable propylene production. Future research directions may focus on:

1. Advanced characterization techniques: Developing in-situ and operando methods to better understand coke formation mechanisms and catalyst deactivation at the molecular level.

PDH的关键考虑因素包括：

1. 反应条件：PDH是一个高度吸热反应（ΔH298 = 124.3 kJ/mol），需要高温（通常为550-750°C）和低压条件才能实现经济可行的转化率[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。这些条件是必要的，因为存在热力学限制，需要推动平衡向丙烯形成方向移动。

   a) 贵金属基催化剂：主要是铂（Pt）基催化剂，通常用锡（Sn）、铟（In）或镓（Ga）等促进剂进行改性。
   b) 金属氧化物基催化剂：铬氧化物（CrOx）是最突出的例子，尽管正在探索镓氧化物（Ga2O3）和钒氧化物（VOx）等替代品[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。

1. 催化剂设计：
