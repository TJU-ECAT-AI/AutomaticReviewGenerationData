The process involves an endothermic reaction typically performed at high temperatures (550-750°C) using supported catalysts. Key considerations for PDH include:

Low pressures and low propane partial pressures are preferred to achieve higher conversions. However, these conditions also promote side reactions and coke formation.

2. Catalyst types: Commercially used catalysts are primarily Pt-based and CrOx-based. Pt catalysts offer high activity but suffer from rapid deactivation due to coking. CrOx catalysts are cheaper but raise environmental concerns due to Cr(VI) toxicity [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. Recent research has focused on developing alternative catalysts, such as vanadium-, gallium-, and zirconium-based materials.

3. Industrial applications: PDH has become a significant contributor to global propylene supply, with several commercial processes like Oleflex and Catofin in operation [Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

Coke formation on Pt-based catalysts significantly impacts process efficiency by causing rapid catalyst deactivation, reducing propylene selectivity, and necessitating frequent regeneration cycles. The extent of coking depends on factors such as catalyst particle size, reaction temperature, and gas composition [Placeholder_Of_DOI4].

Strategies to mitigate coke formation and improve catalyst regeneration include:

1. Catalyst modification: Alloying Pt with elements like Sn, Zn, or Ga to reduce coke formation and improve selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].
2. Support optimization: Using supports with low Brønsted acidity or modifying them to suppress coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI6].
3. Reaction condition control: Co-feeding H2 or CO2 to inhibit coke precursor formation and promote coke removal [Placeholder_Of_DOI7].
4. Advanced catalyst designs: Developing single-atom catalysts or intermetallic compounds to isolate active sites and minimize side reactions [Placeholder_Of_DOI8].
5. Regeneration processes: Implementing efficient oxidative regeneration cycles, often using air or oxychlorination treatments [Placeholder_Of_DOI9].

These strategies aim to extend catalyst lifetime, maintain high propylene selectivity, and improve overall process efficiency in industrial PDH operations.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive analysis of PDH catalysts reveals a complex interplay between catalyst design, reaction conditions, and coking behavior. While significant progress has been made in developing more efficient and stable catalysts, there are still opportunities for further improvement. Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. Advanced in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic nature of catalyst active sites and coke formation mechanisms during PDH reactions.
2. Developing novel multifunctional catalysts that can simultaneously promote dehydrogenation and inhibit coke formation, potentially through the incorporation of coke-resistant materials or in-situ coke removal capabilities.
3. Exploring the potential of machine learning and artificial intelligence in catalyst design and process optimization, potentially leading to the discovery of novel catalyst formulations or operating conditions that maximize propylene yield while minimizing coking.

These research directions could lead to significant advancements in PDH technology, making it an even more attractive option for on-purpose propylene production in the evolving landscape of the petrochemical industry.

1. 反应条件：由于其吸热性质（ΔH298 = 124.3 kJ/mol），该过程需要高温[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。低压和低丙烷分压有利于获得更高的转化率。然而，这些条件也促进了副反应和积碳的形成。

2. 催化剂类型：商业使用的催化剂主要是基于Pt和CrOx的。Pt催化剂活性高，但由于结焦而迅速失活。CrOx催化剂较便宜，但由于Cr(VI)的毒性而引起环境问题[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。近期研究重点是开发替代催化剂，如基于钒、镓和锆的材料。

缓解积碳形成和改善催化剂再生的策略包括：

1. 催化剂改性：将Pt与Sn、Zn或Ga等元素合金化，以减少积碳形成并提高选择性[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。
2. 载体优化：使用低Brønsted酸性载体或对其进行改性以抑制积碳形成[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]。
3. 反应条件控制：共进料H2或CO2以抑制积碳前体的形成并促进积碳去除[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]。
4. 先进催化剂设计：开发单原子催化剂或金属间化合物，以隔离活性位点并最小化副反应[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]。
5. 再生过程：实施高效的氧化再生循环，通常使用空气或氧氯化处理[Placeholder_Of_DOI9]。

这些研究方向可能会导致PDH技术的重大进展，使其在不断变化的石油化工行业格局中成为一个更具吸引力的定向丙烯生产选择。
