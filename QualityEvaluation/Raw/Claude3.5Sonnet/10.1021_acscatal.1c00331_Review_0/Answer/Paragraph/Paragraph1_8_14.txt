Catalytic direct dehydrogenation of propane (PDH) is a promising method for propene production, addressing the growing demand for propylene that traditional methods like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking struggle to meet. The process involves converting propane to propene and hydrogen using specific catalysts under high-temperature conditions.

Key considerations for PDH include:

1. Reaction conditions: PDH is highly endothermic, requiring temperatures of 550-750°C and low pressures to achieve economically viable conversions. The process is thermodynamically limited, with commercial processes typically achieving per-pass conversions of 30-60% [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

2. Catalyst types: Two main categories of catalysts are used in PDH:
   a) Noble metal-based catalysts, primarily platinum (Pt), often modified with elements like Sn, Zn, Ga, or In.
   b) Metal oxide-based catalysts, with chromium oxide (CrOx) being the most prominent.

   Pt-based catalysts are widely used due to their high activity, but they suffer from rapid deactivation due to coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

3. Industrial applications: PDH has become increasingly important for on-purpose propylene production, especially with the availability of low-cost propane from shale gas. Commercial processes like Oleflex (UOP) and Catofin use Pt-based and CrOx-based catalysts, respectively [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

Coke formation on Pt-based catalysts significantly impacts process efficiency by causing catalyst deactivation, reducing propylene selectivity, and necessitating frequent regeneration. The deactivation of Pt-based catalysts for PDH mainly originates from coke formation, which is influenced by catalyst particle size and structure [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

Strategies to mitigate coke formation and improve catalyst regeneration include:

1. Catalyst modification: Alloying Pt with other metals (e.g., Sn, Zn, Ga) to reduce coke formation and improve selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

2. Optimizing catalyst structure: Achieving atomic-level dispersion of Pt particles to reduce multiple Pt-Pt sites, which are known to promote deep dehydrogenation and coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

3. Support modification: Using supports with low Brønsted acidity or poisoning acid sites to reduce coke formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

4. Reaction condition optimization: Controlling temperature, pressure, and feed composition, including co-feeding H2 or CO2 to suppress coke precursor formation [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

5. Continuous Catalyst Regeneration (CCR) technology: Implemented by UOP, allowing noble metal catalysts to maintain stable activity for extended periods without shutting down the reactor [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

6. Developing alternative catalysts: Research into novel materials like single-atom catalysts (SACs) or intermetallic compounds (e.g., PtGa) shows promise in addressing coking issues while maintaining high activity and selectivity [Placeholder_Of_DOI].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The development of PDH technology has seen significant progress in recent years, driven by the need for more efficient and sustainable propylene production. Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. Advanced catalyst design: Exploring novel materials and structures, such as single-atom catalysts or bimetallic nanoparticles, to further improve activity, selectivity, and coke resistance.

2. In-situ regeneration techniques: Developing methods for continuous or semi-continuous catalyst regeneration without process interruption, potentially using innovative reactor designs or microwave-assisted regeneration.

3. Process intensification: Integrating PDH with other processes, such as membrane separation or oxidative dehydrogenation, to overcome thermodynamic limitations and improve overall efficiency.

PDH的关键考虑因素包括：

1. 反应条件：PDH是高度吸热的，需要550-750°C的高温和低压才能实现经济可行的转化率。该过程受热力学限制，商业过程通常实现30-60%的单程转化率 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

2. 催化剂类型：PDH主要使用两类催化剂：
   a) 贵金属基催化剂，主要是铂（Pt），通常用Sn、Zn、Ga或In等元素进行改性。
   b) 金属氧化物基催化剂，以氧化铬（CrOx）为最突出代表。

   铂基催化剂因其高活性而被广泛使用，但由于积碳形成而容易快速失活 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

3. 工业应用：PDH在定向丙烯生产中变得越来越重要，特别是在页岩气提供低成本丙烷的情况下。商业工艺如Oleflex（UOP）和Catofin分别使用铂基和氧化铬基催化剂 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

铂基催化剂上的积碳形成显著影响过程效率，导致催化剂失活，降低丙烯选择性，并需要频繁再生。PDH中铂基催化剂的失活主要源于积碳形成，这受到催化剂颗粒尺寸和结构的影响 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

减少积碳形成和改善催化剂再生的策略包括：

1. 催化剂改性：将Pt与其他金属（如Sn、Zn、Ga）合金化，以减少积碳形成并提高选择性 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

2. 优化催化剂结构：实现Pt颗粒的原子级分散，以减少已知会促进深度脱氢和积碳形成的多重Pt-Pt位点 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

3. 载体改性：使用低Brønsted酸性的载体或毒化酸性位点以减少积碳形成 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。

4. 反应条件优化：控制温度、压力和进料组成，包括共进H2或CO2以抑制积碳前体的形成 [Placeholder_Of_DOI]。
