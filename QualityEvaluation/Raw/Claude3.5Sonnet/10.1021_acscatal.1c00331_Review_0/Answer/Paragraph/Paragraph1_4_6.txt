This process involves the conversion of propane to propene and hydrogen using specific catalysts under controlled conditions. Key considerations for PDH include:

Coke formation on Pt-based catalysts is a significant challenge in PDH, impacting process efficiency by causing rapid catalyst deactivation. 3. Reaction condition optimization: Cofeeding H2 or CO2 to suppress coke precursor formation and promote catalyst stability [Placeholder_Of_DOI8].

4. Advanced regeneration techniques: Implementing continuous catalyst regeneration (CCR) or oxychlorination processes to effectively remove coke and redisperse active metal species [Placeholder_Of_DOI9].

5. Novel catalyst formulations: Exploring alternative catalysts such as gallium oxide or zirconia-based materials, which show promise in terms of activity, selectivity, and coke resistance [Placeholder_Of_DOI10].

基于Pt催化剂的结焦是PDH中的一个重大挑战，通过导致催化剂快速失活影响过程效率。这个问题需要频繁的催化剂再生，增加了运营成本和复杂性。结焦的程度取决于多种因素，包括催化剂粒子大小、反应条件和催化剂组成[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。
