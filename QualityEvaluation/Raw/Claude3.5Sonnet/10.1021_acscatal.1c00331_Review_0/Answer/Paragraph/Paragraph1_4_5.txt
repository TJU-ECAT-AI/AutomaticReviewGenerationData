The process involves the conversion of propane to propene and hydrogen using specific catalysts. Key considerations for PDH include:

1. Reaction conditions: PDH is highly endothermic (ΔH298 = 124.3 kJ/mol) and requires high temperatures (550-750°C) and low pressures to achieve economically viable conversions [Placeholder_Of_DOI1]. These conditions are necessary due to thermodynamic limitations and to overcome equilibrium constraints.

2. Catalyst types: Two main classes of catalysts are used in PDH:
   a) Noble metal-based catalysts, primarily platinum (Pt)
   b) Metal oxide-based catalysts, particularly chromium oxide (CrOx)
Pt-based catalysts are widely used due to their high activity and selectivity, but they face challenges with coking and high costs [Placeholder_Of_DOI2]. CrOx catalysts are cheaper but raise environmental concerns due to the toxicity of Cr(VI) species.

3. Industrial applications: PDH has been commercialized in processes such as Oleflex (UOP) and Catofin, contributing significantly to global propylene production. As of 2000, there were several commercial PDH units in operation, with an aggregate capacity of 900,000 MTA polymer-grade propylene [Placeholder_Of_DOI3].

Coke formation on Pt-based catalysts significantly impacts process efficiency by causing rapid catalyst deactivation, reducing propylene yield, and necessitating frequent regeneration. 1. Catalyst modification: Alloying Pt with metals like Sn, Zn, or In to improve stability and reduce coking [Placeholder_Of_DOI5].

4. Process optimization: Co-feeding H2 or CO2 to inhibit coke formation and enhance catalyst stability [Placeholder_Of_DOI8].
5. Regeneration techniques: Implementing efficient regeneration cycles, such as oxidative treatment followed by reduction, to remove coke and restore catalyst activity [Placeholder_Of_DOI9].

These strategies aim to extend catalyst lifetime, improve selectivity, and enhance overall process efficiency in industrial PDH operations.

催化丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）是一种重要的丙烯生产方法，由于全球丙烯需求的增加和页岩气中低成本丙烷的可用性而得到推动。该过程涉及使用特定催化剂将丙烷转化为丙烯和氢气。PDH的关键考虑因素包括：

1. 反应条件：PDH是强吸热反应（ΔH298 = 124.3 kJ/mol），需要高温（550-750°C）和低压才能实现经济可行的转化率[Placeholder_Of_DOI1]。这些条件是由于热力学限制和克服平衡约束所必需的。

2. 催化剂类型：PDH主要使用两类催化剂：
   a) 贵金属基催化剂，主要是铂（Pt）
   b) 金属氧化物基催化剂，特别是氧化铬（CrOx）
Pt基催化剂因其高活性和选择性而被广泛使用，但面临结焦和高成本的挑战[Placeholder_Of_DOI2]。CrOx催化剂较便宜，但由于Cr(VI)物种的毒性而引起环境关注。

3. 工业应用：PDH已在Oleflex（UOP）和Catofin等工艺中商业化，为全球丙烯生产做出重大贡献。截至2000年，已有多个商业PDH装置投入运营，聚合物级丙烯的总产能为900,000 MTA[Placeholder_Of_DOI3]。

Pt基催化剂上的焦炭形成显著影响过程效率，导致催化剂快速失活，降低丙烯产率，并需要频繁再生。结焦程度取决于催化剂粒径、反应温度和气体组成等因素[Placeholder_Of_DOI4]。

减少焦炭形成和改善催化剂再生的策略包括：

1. 催化剂改性：将Pt与Sn、Zn或In等金属合金化，以提高稳定性并减少结焦[Placeholder_Of_DOI5]。
2. 优化催化剂结构：开发单原子催化剂或金属间化合物，以隔离活性位点并最小化副反应[Placeholder_Of_DOI6]。
3. 载体改性：使用低布朗斯特酸性的载体或添加助剂以抑制焦炭形成[Placeholder_Of_DOI7]。
4. 工艺优化：共进料H2或CO2以抑制焦炭形成并增强催化剂稳定性[Placeholder_Of_DOI8]。
5. 再生技术：实施高效再生循环，如氧化处理后还原，以去除焦炭并恢复催化剂活性[Placeholder_Of_DOI9]。

5. 可持续催化：研究地球丰富和环境友好的材料作为贵金属催化剂的替代品，符合绿色化学和循环经济原则。

这些进展可能彻底革新PDH技术，使其在经济上更具可行性，更加环保，从而巩固其作为未来丙烯生产关键工艺的地位。
