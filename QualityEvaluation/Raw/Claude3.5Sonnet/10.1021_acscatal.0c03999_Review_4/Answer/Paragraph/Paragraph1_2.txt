<English>
The structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. This review section focuses on the structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis.

1. Structural Characteristics:
β-Ga2O3, with its monoclinic structure, has gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436]. This unique structure results in a high concentration of coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites, which are crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]. Among the different polymorphs, β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 are constituted by octahedral Ga3+ [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

2. Surface Properties:
The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This correlates with the catalytic activity trend, where β- and γ-Ga2O3 outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The weak acid site density is crucial for achieving high activity and stability, while medium-strong acid sites show a negative correlation with activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021].

3. Lewis Acid Sites:
Coordinatively unsaturated tetrahedral Ga3+ cations act as weak Lewis acid sites and are crucial for dehydrogenation activity [10.1021/cr5002436]. The population of catalytically relevant (cus) Ga species is directly related to the surface area of Ga2O3, which varies among different polymorphs [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, forming coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites that act as strong Lewis acid sites [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

4. Reaction Mechanisms:
The reaction mechanism involves two stages:
a) Initial stage: Reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting the dehydrogenation of propane (DHP) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
b) Steady-state: The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

On a Ga2O3 (100) surface, C-H bond activation occurs when a surface oxygen abstracts a hydrogen from the propane molecule, forming a propyl species that coordinates with a Ga surface site and a hydroxyl group [10.1021/cr5002436].

5. CO2 Influence:
CO2 plays multiple roles in the reaction:
a) It improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation.
b) It assists in decreasing coke deposition through the Boudouart reaction.
c) It helps with product desorption, particularly ethylene.
d) However, CO2 can also competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

6. Catalyst Preparation and Composition:
The preparation method and treatment process affect the activity and stability of Ga2O3-containing catalysts [10.1039/C5CY02161H]. Coprecipitation methods tend to produce gallia-alumina solid solutions with a larger amount of surface Ga3+ sites with weak Lewis acidity, leading to better performance in propane dehydrogenation with CO2 compared to catalysts prepared using physical mixture methods [10.1039/C5CY02161H].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:
The comprehensive analysis of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP reactions reveals the critical role of structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites in determining catalytic performance. Future research directions may include:

1. Tailoring catalyst synthesis methods to optimize the distribution of weak Lewis acid sites and control the ratio of tetrahedral to octahedral Ga3+ sites.

2. Investigating the synergistic effects between Ga2O3 and other metal oxides or supports to enhance catalytic activity and stability.

3. Developing in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and surface properties during the reaction.

4. Exploring the potential of novel Ga2O3-based materials, such as mixed metal oxides or supported nanostructures, to further improve catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions.

5. Conducting more detailed studies on the role of CO2 in the reaction mechanism, particularly its interaction with different types of active sites on the catalyst surface.

6. Investigating the long-term stability and deactivation mechanisms of Ga2O3-based catalysts under industrially relevant conditions to address potential challenges in large-scale applications.

By focusing on these areas, researchers can potentially develop more efficient and stable catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions, contributing to the broader goal of sustainable chemical production and CO2 utilization.

<Chinese>
不同Ga2O3多形体的结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位显著影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。本综述部分重点关注Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系。

1. 结构特征：
β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布[10.1021/cr5002436]。这种独特的结构导致了高浓度的配位不饱和表面(cus)Ga3+位点，这对于CO2气氛中的碳氢化合物活化至关重要[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。在不同的多形体中，β-和γ-Ga2O3含有四面体配位的Ga阳离子，而α-和δ-Ga2O3由八面体配位的Ga3+构成[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

2. 表面性质：
表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱度也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这与催化活性趋势相关，其中β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面优于α-和δ-Ga2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。弱酸位密度对于获得高活性和稳定性至关重要，而中强酸位与活性呈负相关[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。

3. Lewis酸位：
配位不饱和的四面体Ga3+阳离子作为弱Lewis酸位，对脱氢活性至关重要[10.1021/cr5002436]。具有催化相关性的(cus)Ga物种的数量与Ga2O3的表面积直接相关，而表面积在不同多形体之间有所不同[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。β-和γ-Ga2O3含有四面体Ga阳离子，形成配位不饱和的Ga3+位点，作为强Lewis酸位[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

4. 反应机理：
反应机理包括两个阶段：
a) 初始阶段：Ga阳离子还原（Ga3+到Ga+）发生，有利于丙烷脱氢（DHP）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
b) 稳态阶段：反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，不涉及氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

在Ga2O3 (100)表面上，C-H键活化发生时，表面氧从丙烷分子中提取氢原子，形成与Ga表面位点和羟基配位的丙基物种[10.1021/cr5002436]。

5. CO2的影响：
CO2在反应中起多重作用：
a) 作为温和的氧化剂改善烯烃产率，并将平衡向产物形成方向移动。
b) 通过Boudouart反应协助减少积碳沉积。
c) 帮助产物（特别是乙烯）脱附。
d) 然而，CO2也可以在碱性位点上竞争性吸附，可能对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

6. 催化剂制备和组成：
制备方法和处理过程影响Ga2O3含有催化剂的活性和稳定性[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。共沉淀法倾向于产生具有更多弱Lewis酸性表面Ga3+位点的镓-铝固溶体，与使用物理混合方法制备的催化剂相比，在CO2存在下的丙烷脱氢反应中表现更好[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。

综合理解与未来展望：
对Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP反应中的全面分析揭示了结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位在决定催化性能方面的关键作用。未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 调整催化剂合成方法，以优化弱Lewis酸位的分布并控制四面体和八面体Ga3+位点的比例。

2. 研究Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物或载体之间的协同效应，以提高催化活性和稳定性。

3. 开发原位表征技术，以更好地理解反应过程中催化剂结构和表面性质的动态变化。

4. 探索新型Ga2O3基材料的潜力，如混合金属氧化物或负载型纳米结构，以进一步提高CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。

5. 对CO2在反应机理中的作用进行更详细的研究，特别是其与催化剂表面不同类型活性位点的相互作用。

6. 在工业相关条件下研究Ga2O3基催化剂的长期稳定性和失活机理，以解决大规模应用中可能面临的挑战。

通过关注这些领域，研究人员可能开发出更高效、更稳定的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂，为可持续化学品生产和CO2利用的更广泛目标做出贡献。
