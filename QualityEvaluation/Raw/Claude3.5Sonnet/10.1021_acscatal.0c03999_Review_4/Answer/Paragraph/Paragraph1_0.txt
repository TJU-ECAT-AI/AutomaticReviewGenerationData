<English>
The structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Among the various polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-Ga2O3), β- and γ-Ga2O3 exhibit superior performance in terms of C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Structural Characteristics:
1. β-Ga2O3 has a monoclinic structure with gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436].
2. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations in their structures, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 are primarily composed of octahedral Ga3+ [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Surface Properties:
1. Surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
2. The weak acid site density for gallia-alumina mixed oxides is approximately linearly related to the tetrahedral Ga3+ population [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021].
3. Mesoporosity facilitates the dehydrogenation reaction by allowing rapid diffusion of products, preventing side reactions and improving selectivity to propene [10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019].

Lewis Acid Sites:
1. Coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites act as Lewis acid centers and are crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004].
2. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites that act as strong Lewis acid sites, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 have weaker acidity due to their octahedral Ga3+ configuration [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
3. The proportion of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites (Ga(IV)) correlates with weak acid sites, implying their essential role in creating surface acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Catalytic Performance and Mechanisms:
1. Initial activity is dependent on surface acid density and maximizes on β-Ga2O3, with surface base site density also correlating with initial activity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
2. The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle at steady-state [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
3. Hydrocarbon activation occurs on low-coordinated Ga3+ cations, forming propyl-Ga species, which then decompose to generate dehydrogenation products [10.1039/C5CY02161H].
4. The presence of acid sites facilitates certain reaction steps, providing an alternative pathway to promote C3H6 formation by bypassing the rate-determining step in C3H8 heterolytic dissociation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

CO2 Influence:
1. CO2 improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation [10.1021/cr5002436].
2. It assists in decreasing coke deposition through the Boudouart reaction and helps with product desorption [10.1021/cr5002436].
3. However, CO2 can competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis reveal the critical role of surface acidity, particularly weak Lewis acid sites associated with coordinatively unsaturated tetrahedral Ga3+ cations. Future research directions may focus on:

1. Developing synthesis methods to optimize the distribution and strength of Lewis acid sites on Ga2O3 surfaces, potentially through controlled doping or surface modification techniques.
2. Investigating the synergistic effects between Ga2O3 and other metal oxides or supports to enhance catalytic performance and stability.
3. Exploring in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and surface properties during the reaction, especially during the transition from initial to steady-state stages.
4. Utilizing advanced computational modeling to predict and design Ga2O3-based catalysts with optimized structural characteristics and surface properties for improved CO2-ODHP performance.
5. Investigating the potential of other Ga2O3 polymorphs or mixed-phase catalysts to combine the beneficial properties of different structural configurations for enhanced catalytic activity and selectivity.

These research directions could lead to the development of more efficient and stable Ga2O3-based catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions, contributing to the broader field of sustainable chemical production and CO2 utilization.

<Chinese>
不同Ga2O3多形体的结构特征、表面性质和Lewis酸位点显著影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。在各种多形体（α-、β-、γ-和δ-Ga2O3）中，β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面表现出优异的性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

结构特征：
1. β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子均匀分布在四面体和八面体构型之间[10.1021/cr5002436]。
2. β-和γ-Ga2O3的结构中含有四面体Ga阳离子，而α-和δ-Ga2O3主要由八面体Ga3+组成[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

表面性质：
1. 表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱性也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
2. 镓-氧化铝混合氧化物的弱酸位点密度与四面体Ga3+的数量呈近似线性关系[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。
3. 介孔结构通过允许产物快速扩散来促进脱氢反应，防止副反应并提高丙烯的选择性[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019]。

Lewis酸位点：
1. 配位不饱和表面(cus) Ga3+位点充当Lewis酸中心，对CO2气氛中的碳氢化合物活化至关重要[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。
2. β-和γ-Ga2O3含有作为强Lewis酸位点的配位不饱和Ga3+位点，而α-和δ-Ga2O3由于其八面体Ga3+构型而具有较弱的酸性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
3. 四面体Ga3+位点（Ga(IV)）的比例与弱酸位点相关，表明它们在创造表面酸性方面的重要作用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

催化性能和机理：
1. 初始活性取决于表面酸密度，在β-Ga2O3上达到最大值，表面碱位点密度也与初始活性相关[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
2. 在稳态下，反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，不涉及氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
3. 碳氢化合物的活化发生在低配位Ga3+阳离子上，形成丙基-Ga物种，然后分解生成脱氢产物[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。
4. 酸位点的存在促进了某些反应步骤，提供了一种替代途径来促进C3H6的形成，绕过C3H8异裂解离的速率确定步骤[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

CO2的影响：
1. CO2通过作为温和的氧化剂并将平衡转向产物形成来提高烯烃产率[10.1021/cr5002436]。
2. 它通过Boudouart反应帮助减少积碳沉积，并促进产物脱附[10.1021/cr5002436]。
3. 然而，CO2可以在碱性位点上竞争性吸附，可能对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系揭示了表面酸性，特别是与配位不饱和四面体Ga3+阳离子相关的弱Lewis酸位点的关键作用。未来的研究方向可能集中在：

1. 开发优化Ga2O3表面Lewis酸位点分布和强度的合成方法，可能通过控制掺杂或表面修饰技术实现。
2. 研究Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物或载体之间的协同效应，以提高催化性能和稳定性。
3. 探索原位表征技术，以更好地理解反应过程中催化剂结构和表面性质的动态变化，特别是从初始阶段到稳态阶段的转变过程。
4. 利用先进的计算建模来预测和设计具有优化结构特征和表面性质的Ga2O3基催化剂，以改善CO2-ODHP性能。
5. 研究其他Ga2O3多形体或混相催化剂的潜力，结合不同结构配置的有益性质，以提高催化活性和选择性。

这些研究方向可能导致开发出更高效、更稳定的Ga2O3基催化剂用于CO2-ODHP反应，为可持续化学品生产和CO2利用的广泛领域做出贡献。
