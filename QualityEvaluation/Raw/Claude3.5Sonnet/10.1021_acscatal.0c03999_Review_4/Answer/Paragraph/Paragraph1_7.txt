<English>
The structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. This review section analyzes the structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis, focusing on the influence of structural characteristics, surface properties, and reaction mechanisms on catalytic activity and selectivity.

1. Structural Characteristics and Polymorphs:
β-Ga2O3, with its monoclinic structure, has gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436]. This unique structure results in a high concentration of weak Lewis acid sites on the surface, which are crucial for dehydrogenation activity. Among the different polymorphs, β- and γ-Ga2O3 outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The superior performance of β-Ga2O3 is attributed to an abundance of surface medium-strong Lewis acid sites related to unsaturated Ga3+ cations [10.1039/C5CY02161H].

2. Surface Properties:
a) Acidity and Basicity: The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This correlation between acidity/basicity and catalytic activity suggests that surface acid-base properties play a crucial role in CO2-ODHP reactions.

b) Lewis Acid Sites: Coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites act as Lewis acid centers and are crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, forming coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites that act as strong Lewis acid sites, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 have octahedral Ga3+ with weaker acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

c) Weak vs. Strong Acid Sites: Weak surface acidity, specifically weak Lewis acid site density, is crucial for achieving high activity and stability in propane dehydrogenation [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]. This suggests that the strength of acid sites plays a significant role in determining catalytic performance.

3. Reaction Mechanisms and Activity Trends:
a) Initial Stage: The initial activity is dependent on surface acid density and maximizes on β-Ga2O3. Surface base site density also correlates with initial activity, demonstrating its promotional effect in the initial period of reaction [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. Reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting the dehydrogenation of propane (DHP) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

b) Steady-State: At the steady state, the reaction is proposed to proceed via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The presence of acid sites can facilitate certain reaction steps, providing an alternative pathway to promote C3H6 formation by bypassing the rate-determining step in C3H8 heterolytic dissociation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

c) CO2 Influence: CO2 improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation. It also assists in decreasing coke deposition and helps with product desorption [10.1021/cr5002436]. However, CO2 can competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

4. Structure-Property-Performance Relationships:
The superior performance of β- and γ-Ga2O3 can be attributed to their higher surface acidity density and the presence of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites, which act as Lewis acid sites [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The population of catalytically relevant (cus) Ga species is directly related to the surface area of Ga2O3, which varies among different polymorphs [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]. Higher surface area generally leads to a greater abundance of these active sites, resulting in improved catalytic activity.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive analysis of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP reactions reveals the intricate interplay between structural characteristics, surface properties, and catalytic performance. Future research endeavors might focus on the following areas:

1. Polymorph Engineering: Developing methods to synthesize and stabilize specific Ga2O3 polymorphs with optimized surface properties could lead to more efficient catalysts. This could involve exploring novel synthesis techniques or using dopants to stabilize desired structures.

2. In-situ Characterization: Employing advanced in-situ characterization techniques to study the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and surface properties during the reaction could provide deeper insights into the reaction mechanisms and the role of different active sites.

3. Computational Modeling: Utilizing advanced computational methods to model the behavior of different Ga2O3 polymorphs and their interactions with reactants could help predict and design more effective catalysts.

4. Hybrid Materials: Exploring the potential of hybrid materials combining Ga2O3 with other metal oxides or supports could lead to synergistic effects and enhanced catalytic performance.

5. Structure-Reactivity Relationships: Conducting more comprehensive studies comparing all Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-) under identical reaction conditions could provide a more complete understanding of structure-reactivity relationships in CO2-ODHP reactions.

6. Catalyst Stability: Investigating methods to enhance the long-term stability of Ga2O3 catalysts, particularly in the presence of CO2, could lead to more practical and industrially viable catalytic systems.

These research directions could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and stable Ga2O3-based catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions, contributing to the broader field of sustainable chemical production and CO2 utilization.

<Chinese>
不同Ga2O3多形体的结构特征、表面性质和路易斯酸位显著影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能。本综述部分分析了Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系，重点关注结构特征、表面性质和反应机理对催化活性和选择性的影响。

1. 结构特征和多形体：
β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布[10.1021/cr5002436]。这种独特的结构导致表面上存在高浓度的弱路易斯酸位，这对脱氢活性至关重要。在不同的多形体中，β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面优于α-和δ-Ga2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。β-Ga2O3的卓越性能归因于与未饱和Ga3+阳离子相关的表面中强路易斯酸位的丰富性[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。

2. 表面性质：
a) 酸度和碱度：表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱度也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。酸度/碱度与催化活性之间的这种相关性表明，表面酸碱性质在CO2-ODHP反应中起着至关重要的作用。

b) 路易斯酸位：配位不饱和表面（cus）Ga3+位作为路易斯酸中心，对CO2气氛中的烃类活化至关重要[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。β-和γ-Ga2O3含有四面体Ga阳离子，形成配位不饱和Ga3+位，作为强路易斯酸位，而α-和δ-Ga2O3具有八面体Ga3+，酸性较弱[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

c) 弱酸位vs强酸位：弱表面酸度，特别是弱路易斯酸位密度，对于在丙烷脱氢中实现高活性和稳定性至关重要[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。这表明酸位强度在决定催化性能方面起着重要作用。

3. 反应机理和活性趋势：
a) 初始阶段：初始活性取决于表面酸度密度，在β-Ga2O3上达到最大值。表面碱位密度也与初始活性相关，表明其在反应初期的促进作用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。Ga阳离子的还原（Ga3+到Ga+）发生，有利于丙烷脱氢（DHP）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

b) 稳态：在稳态下，反应被提议通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，不涉及氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。酸位的存在可以促进某些反应步骤，通过绕过C3H8异裂解离中的速率决定步骤，提供一种替代途径来促进C3H6的形成[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

c) CO2的影响：CO2通过作为温和的氧化剂并将平衡移向产物形成来改善烯烃产率。它还有助于减少积碳沉积并帮助产物脱附[10.1021/cr5002436]。然而，CO2可以竞争性地吸附在碱性位点上，可能会对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

4. 结构-性质-性能关系：
β-和γ-Ga2O3的卓越性能可归因于它们较高的表面酸度密度和四面体Ga3+位的存在，这些位点作为路易斯酸位[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。催化相关的（cus）Ga物种的数量与Ga2O3的表面积直接相关，这在不同的多形体之间有所不同[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。较高的表面积通常导致这些活性位点的丰度更大，从而提高催化活性。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：对Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP反应中的全面分析揭示了结构特征、表面性质和催化性能之间的复杂相互作用。未来的研究努力可能会集中在以下领域：

1. 多形体工程：开发合成和稳定具有优化表面性质的特定Ga2O3多形体的方法可能会导致更高效的催化剂。这可能涉及探索新的合成技术或使用掺杂剂来稳定所需的结构。

2. 原位表征：采用先进的原位表征技术来研究反应过程中催化剂结构和表面性质的动态变化，可以对反应机理和不同活性位点的作用提供更深入的见解。

3. 计算机模拟：利用先进的计算方法来模拟不同Ga2O3多形体的行为及其与反应物的相互作用，可以帮助预测和设计更有效的催化剂。

4. 混合材料：探索将Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物或载体结合的混合材料的潜力，可能会产生协同效应并增强催化性能。

5. 结构-反应性关系：在相同反应条件下对所有Ga2O3多形体（α-、β-、γ-和δ-）进行更全面的比较研究，可以对CO2-ODHP反应中的结构-反应性关系提供更完整的理解。

6. 催化剂稳定性：研究提高Ga2O3催化剂长期稳定性的方法，特别是在CO2存在的情况下，可能会导致更实用和工业可行的催化系统。

这些研究方向可能会导致开发出更高效和稳定的基于Ga2O3的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂，为可持续化学品生产和CO2利用的更广泛领域做出贡献。
