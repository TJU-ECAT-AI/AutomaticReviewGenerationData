<English>
The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis are significantly influenced by their structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites. The literature provides insights into these relationships, particularly for β- and γ-Ga2O3 polymorphs.

Structural Characteristics:
β-Ga2O3 has a monoclinic structure with gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436]. This unique structure results in a high concentration of weak Lewis acid sites on the surface, which are crucial for dehydrogenation activity. γ-Ga2O3 also contains tetrahedral Ga cations, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 are primarily composed of octahedral Ga3+ [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Surface Properties:
The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This acidity trend correlates strongly with catalytic activity. The presence of coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites, particularly in β-Ga2O3, is crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004].

Lewis Acid Sites:
Lewis acid sites, specifically coordinatively unsaturated tetrahedral Ga3+ cations, are prerequisites for high dehydrogenation activity [10.1021/cr5002436]. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain these tetrahedral Ga cations, forming strong Lewis acid sites, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 have weaker acidity due to their octahedral Ga3+ configuration [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Catalytic Performance:
β- and γ-Ga2O3 outperform α- and δ-Ga2O3 in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This superior performance is attributed to their higher surface acidity density and the presence of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites. Interestingly, weak surface acidity, specifically weak Lewis acid site density, is crucial for achieving high activity and stability, while medium-strong acid sites show a negative correlation with activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021].

Reaction Mechanisms:
The CO2-ODHP reaction over Ga2O3 catalysts proceeds through different mechanisms during initial and steady-state stages:

1. Initial stage: Reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting the dehydrogenation of propane (DHP) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. CO2 plays a promoting role during this stage [10.1016/S0926-860X(03)00368-5].

2. Steady-state: The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The C-H bond is activated by a surface oxygen that abstracts a hydrogen from the propane molecule, forming a propyl species that coordinates with a Ga surface site and a hydroxyl group [10.1021/cr5002436].

The presence of acid sites facilitates certain reaction steps, providing an alternative pathway to promote C3H6 formation by bypassing the rate-determining step in C3H8 heterolytic dissociation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

CO2 Influence:
CO2 improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation. It also assists in decreasing coke deposition and helps with product desorption. However, CO2 can competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially affecting performance negatively [10.1021/cr5002436].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:

The comprehensive analysis of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis reveals the critical role of structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites in determining catalytic performance. While β- and γ-Ga2O3 have shown superior performance, there is still room for further optimization and exploration.

Future research directions may include:

1. Detailed investigation of α- and δ-Ga2O3 polymorphs: Although these polymorphs show lower activity, understanding their behavior could lead to novel catalyst designs or hybrid structures that combine the strengths of different polymorphs.

2. Tailoring surface acidity: Given the importance of weak Lewis acid sites, research could focus on methods to precisely control the density and strength of these sites on the catalyst surface.

3. Exploring Ga2O3-based composite materials: The promising performance of Ga2O3-Al2O3 solid solutions suggests that other composite materials could potentially enhance catalytic activity and stability.

4. In-depth mechanistic studies: Further investigation into the reaction mechanisms, particularly during the transition from initial to steady-state stages, could provide insights for catalyst optimization.

5. Development of structure-activity relationships: Establishing quantitative relationships between structural features, surface properties, and catalytic performance could guide rational catalyst design.

6. Investigating the role of CO2: A deeper understanding of CO2's multifaceted role in the reaction could lead to strategies for optimizing its use and mitigating its competitive adsorption on basic sites.

These research directions could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and stable catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions, contributing to the broader field of sustainable chemical production and CO2 utilization.

<Chinese>
Ga2O3多晶型在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性能-性能关系受到其结构特征、表面性质和路易斯酸位点的显著影响。文献对这些关系提供了见解，特别是对于β-和γ-Ga2O3多晶型。

结构特征：
β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布 [10.1021/cr5002436]。这种独特的结构导致表面上存在高浓度的弱路易斯酸位点，这对脱氢活性至关重要。γ-Ga2O3也含有四面体Ga阳离子，而α-和δ-Ga2O3主要由八面体Ga3+组成 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

表面性质：
表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱度也遵循相同的趋势 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这种酸度趋势与催化活性强烈相关。配位不饱和表面（cus）Ga3+位点的存在，特别是在β-Ga2O3中，对于在CO2气氛中活化烃类至关重要 [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。

路易斯酸位点：
路易斯酸位点，特别是配位不饱和的四面体Ga3+阳离子，是高脱氢活性的先决条件 [10.1021/cr5002436]。β-和γ-Ga2O3含有这些四面体Ga阳离子，形成强路易斯酸位点，而α-和δ-Ga2O3由于其八面体Ga3+构型而具有较弱的酸度 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

催化性能：
β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化率和C3H6选择性方面优于α-和δ-Ga2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这种优异的性能归因于它们较高的表面酸度密度和四面体Ga3+位点的存在。有趣的是，弱表面酸度，特别是弱路易斯酸位点密度，对于实现高活性和稳定性至关重要，而中强酸位点与活性呈负相关 [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。

反应机理：
Ga2O3催化剂上的CO2-ODHP反应在初始和稳态阶段通过不同的机理进行：

1. 初始阶段：发生Ga阳离子的还原（Ga3+到Ga+），有利于丙烷脱氢（DHP）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。CO2在这个阶段起促进作用 [10.1016/S0926-860X(03)00368-5]。

2. 稳态阶段：反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，没有氧化还原循环 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。C-H键被表面氧活化，从丙烷分子中提取氢原子，形成与Ga表面位点和羟基配位的丙基物种 [10.1021/cr5002436]。

酸位点的存在促进了某些反应步骤，提供了一种替代途径来促进C3H6的形成，绕过C3H8异裂解离中的速率决定步骤 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

CO2的影响：
CO2通过作为温和的氧化剂并将平衡转移向产物形成来改善烯烃产率。它还有助于减少积碳沉积并帮助产物脱附。然而，CO2可以竞争性地吸附在碱性位点上，可能会对性能产生负面影响 [10.1021/cr5002436]。

综合理解与未来展望：

对Ga2O3多晶型在CO2-ODHP催化中的全面分析揭示了结构特征、表面性质和路易斯酸位点在决定催化性能方面的关键作用。虽然β-和γ-Ga2O3表现出优异的性能，但仍有进一步优化和探索的空间。

未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 对α-和δ-Ga2O3多晶型的详细研究：尽管这些多晶型显示出较低的活性，但了解它们的行为可能会导致新型催化剂设计或结合不同多晶型优势的混合结构。

2. 调整表面酸度：鉴于弱路易斯酸位点的重要性，研究可以集中在精确控制催化剂表面这些位点的密度和强度的方法上。

3. 探索基于Ga2O3的复合材料：Ga2O3-Al2O3固溶体的良好性能表明，其他复合材料可能会增强催化活性和稳定性。

4. 深入的机理研究：对反应机理的进一步研究，特别是从初始阶段到稳态阶段的过渡，可能为催化剂优化提供见解。

5. 建立结构-活性关系：建立结构特征、表面性质和催化性能之间的定量关系可以指导理性催化剂设计。

6. 研究CO2的作用：更深入地了解CO2在反应中的多方面作用可能会导致优化其使用和减轻其在碱性位点上竞争性吸附的策略。

这些研究方向可能会导致更高效和稳定的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂的开发，为可持续化学品生产和CO2利用的更广泛领域做出贡献。
