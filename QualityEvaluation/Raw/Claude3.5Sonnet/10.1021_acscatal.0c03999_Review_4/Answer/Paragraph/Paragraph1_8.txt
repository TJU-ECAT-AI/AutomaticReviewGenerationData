<English>
The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis reveal a complex interplay between structural characteristics, surface properties, and reaction mechanisms that significantly influence catalytic activity and selectivity. 

Among the different Ga2O3 polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-), β- and γ-Ga2O3 exhibit superior performance in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This superior activity is attributed to their unique structural characteristics and surface properties:

1. Structural Characteristics:
β-Ga2O3 has a monoclinic structure with gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436]. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations in their structure, which form coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites acting as strong Lewis acid sites. In contrast, α- and δ-Ga2O3 are constituted by octahedral Ga3+, which are more coordinated and exhibit weaker acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

2. Surface Properties:
The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This correlates strongly with the catalytic activity trend observed. The presence of coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites is crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004].

3. Lewis Acid Sites:
The concentration and strength of Lewis acid sites, particularly those associated with tetrahedral Ga3+ ions, play a crucial role in determining catalytic performance and stability [10.1039/C5CY02161H]. Weak Lewis acid sites, specifically weak Lewis acid site density on the surface, are crucial for achieving high activity and stability [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021].

4. Reaction Mechanisms:
The proposed mechanism for CO2-ODHP reactions involves two stages:

a) Initial stage: Reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting the dehydrogenation of propane (DHP) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

b) Steady-state: The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

The reaction mechanism on a Ga2O3 (100) surface involves C-H bond activation when a surface oxygen abstracts a hydrogen from the propane molecule, forming a propyl species that coordinates with a Ga surface site and a hydroxyl group [10.1021/cr5002436].

5. Influence of CO2:
CO2 plays multiple roles in the reaction:
- It improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation.
- It assists in decreasing coke deposition through the Boudouart reaction.
- CO2 helps with product desorption, particularly ethylene.
- However, CO2 can also competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

6. Pore Structure:
Mesoporosity facilitates the dehydrogenation reaction by allowing rapid diffusion of products, which may prevent side reactions and improve selectivity to propene [10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis reveal the critical role of surface acidity, particularly weak Lewis acid sites associated with tetrahedral Ga3+ ions. Future research directions could focus on:

1. Developing novel synthesis methods to optimize the distribution and strength of Lewis acid sites on Ga2O3 surfaces.
2. Investigating the synergistic effects between Ga2O3 and other metal oxides to enhance catalytic performance and stability.
3. Exploring the potential of mixed Ga2O3 polymorphs or defect-engineered Ga2O3 to combine the advantages of different structural characteristics.
4. Utilizing advanced in-situ characterization techniques to gain deeper insights into the dynamic changes of catalyst structure and surface properties during the reaction.
5. Developing computational models to predict and optimize the performance of Ga2O3-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions.

These research directions could lead to the development of more efficient and stable catalysts for propane dehydrogenation, contributing to the broader goal of sustainable chemical production.

<Chinese>
Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系揭示了结构特征、表面性质和反应机理之间的复杂相互作用，这些因素显著影响催化活性和选择性。

在不同的Ga2O3多形体（α-、β-、γ-和δ-）中，β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面表现出优异性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这种优异的活性归因于它们独特的结构特征和表面性质：

1. 结构特征：
β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布[10.1021/cr5002436]。β-和γ-Ga2O3的结构中都含有四面体配位的Ga阳离子，形成配位不饱和的Ga3+位点，作为强Lewis酸位点。相比之下，α-和δ-Ga2O3由八面体配位的Ga3+构成，配位更加饱和，表现出较弱的酸性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

2. 表面性质：
表面酸性密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱性也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这与观察到的催化活性趋势强烈相关。配位不饱和表面（cus）Ga3+位点对于CO2气氛中的烃类活化至关重要[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。

3. Lewis酸位点：
Lewis酸位点的浓度和强度，特别是与四面体配位Ga3+离子相关的位点，在决定催化性能和稳定性方面起着关键作用[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。弱Lewis酸位点，特别是表面弱Lewis酸位点密度，对于实现高活性和稳定性至关重要[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。

4. 反应机理：
CO2-ODHP反应的提议机理包括两个阶段：

a) 初始阶段：发生Ga阳离子的还原（Ga3+到Ga+），有利于丙烷脱氢（DHP）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

b) 稳态阶段：反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，不涉及氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

在Ga2O3（100）表面上的反应机理涉及C-H键活化，当表面氧原子从丙烷分子中提取氢原子时，形成与Ga表面位点和羟基配位的丙基物种[10.1021/cr5002436]。

5. CO2的影响：
CO2在反应中发挥多重作用：
- 作为温和的氧化剂提高烯烃产率，并将平衡向产物形成方向移动。
- 通过Boudouart反应协助降低积碳沉积。
- CO2有助于产物脱附，特别是乙烯。
- 然而，CO2也可以在碱性位点上竞争性吸附，可能对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

6. 孔结构：
介孔结构通过允许产物快速扩散来促进脱氢反应，这可能防止副反应并提高丙烯的选择性[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系揭示了表面酸性，特别是与四面体配位Ga3+离子相关的弱Lewis酸位点的关键作用。未来的研究方向可以集中在：

1. 开发新型合成方法，优化Ga2O3表面Lewis酸位点的分布和强度。
2. 研究Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物之间的协同效应，以提高催化性能和稳定性。
3. 探索混合Ga2O3多形体或缺陷工程Ga2O3的潜力，结合不同结构特征的优势。
4. 利用先进的原位表征技术，深入了解反应过程中催化剂结构和表面性质的动态变化。
5. 开发计算模型，预测和优化Ga2O3基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能。

这些研究方向可能导致开发出更高效和稳定的丙烷脱氢催化剂，为可持续化学品生产的更广泛目标做出贡献。
