<English>
The structural characteristics, surface properties, and Lewis acid sites of different Ga2O3 polymorphs significantly influence their catalytic performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Among the various polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-Ga2O3), β- and γ-Ga2O3 demonstrate superior catalytic activity and selectivity in propane dehydrogenation reactions [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Structural Characteristics:
1. β-Ga2O3 has a monoclinic structure with gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436].
2. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations, while α- and δ-Ga2O3 are primarily composed of octahedral Ga3+ [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Surface Properties:
1. Surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
2. The presence of weak Lewis acid sites, particularly coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga3+ sites, is crucial for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004].
3. Mesoporosity and uniform pore size distribution contribute to higher selectivity towards propene by facilitating rapid product diffusion [10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019].

Lewis Acid Sites:
1. β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites that act as strong Lewis acid sites [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
2. The proportion of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites correlates with weak acid site density, which is crucial for catalytic activity [10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021].
3. Low-coordinated Ga3+ sites are highly effective in the heterolytic dissociative adsorption of propane [10.1039/C5CY02161H].

Mechanisms and Activity Trends:
1. Initial stage: Reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting propane dehydrogenation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
2. Steady-state: The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].
3. C-H bond activation occurs when a surface oxygen abstracts a hydrogen from the propane molecule, forming a propyl species that coordinates with a Ga surface site and a hydroxyl group [10.1021/cr5002436].
4. The presence of acid sites facilitates certain reaction steps, providing an alternative pathway to promote C3H6 formation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Role of CO2:
1. CO2 improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation [10.1021/cr5002436].
2. It assists in decreasing coke deposition through the Boudouart reaction and helps with product desorption [10.1021/cr5002436].
3. However, CO2 can also competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The superior performance of β- and γ-Ga2O3 in CO2-ODHP reactions can be attributed to their unique structural characteristics, particularly the presence of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites and high surface acidity density. Future research should focus on optimizing the balance between weak and medium-strong acid sites, as weak surface acidity has been shown to be crucial for high activity and stability. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects of Ga2O3 with other metal oxides, such as Al2O3, may lead to the development of more efficient catalysts. Further investigation into the role of CO2 in the reaction mechanism and its impact on catalyst stability over extended periods could provide insights for improving long-term catalyst performance. Finally, in-depth studies comparing the catalytic behavior of all Ga2O3 polymorphs under identical reaction conditions would be valuable for a comprehensive understanding of structure-property-performance relationships in CO2-ODHP catalysis.

<Chinese>
不同Ga2O3多形体的结构特征、表面性质和路易斯酸位对其在CO2-ODHP反应中的催化性能有显著影响。在各种多形体（α-、β-、γ-和δ-Ga2O3）中，β-和γ-Ga2O3在丙烷脱氢反应中表现出优越的催化活性和选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

结构特征：
1. β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布[10.1021/cr5002436]。
2. β-和γ-Ga2O3都含有四面体配位的Ga阳离子，而α-和δ-Ga2O3主要由八面体配位的Ga3+组成[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

表面性质：
1. 表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱性也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
2. 弱路易斯酸位的存在，特别是配位不饱和表面（cus）Ga3+位，对CO2气氛中的烃类活化至关重要[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。
3. 介孔结构和均匀的孔径分布通过促进产物快速扩散，有助于提高丙烯的选择性[10.1016/j.catcom.2013.01.019]。

路易斯酸位：
1. β-和γ-Ga2O3含有作为强路易斯酸位的配位不饱和Ga3+位[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
2. 四面体配位Ga3+位的比例与弱酸位密度相关，这对催化活性至关重要[10.1016/j.jcat.2008.03.021]。
3. 低配位Ga3+位在丙烷的异裂解离吸附中高度有效[10.1039/C5CY02161H]。

机理和活性趋势：
1. 初始阶段：Ga阳离子还原（Ga3+到Ga+）发生，有利于丙烷脱氢[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
2. 稳态：反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，无氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。
3. C-H键活化发生时，表面氧从丙烷分子中提取氢原子，形成与Ga表面位点和羟基配位的丙基物种[10.1021/cr5002436]。
4. 酸位的存在促进某些反应步骤，提供了促进C3H6生成的替代途径[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

CO2的作用：
1. CO2通过作为温和的氧化剂并将平衡转向产物形成来改善烯烃产率[10.1021/cr5002436]。
2. 它通过Boudouart反应帮助减少积碳沉积，并促进产物脱附[10.1021/cr5002436]。
3. 然而，CO2也可以在碱性位点上竞争性吸附，可能对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：β-和γ-Ga2O3在CO2-ODHP反应中的优越性能可归因于其独特的结构特征，特别是四面体Ga3+位的存在和高表面酸度密度。未来的研究应集中在优化弱酸位和中强酸位之间的平衡，因为弱表面酸性已被证明对高活性和稳定性至关重要。此外，探索Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物（如Al2O3）的协同效应可能会导致更高效催化剂的开发。进一步研究CO2在反应机理中的作用及其对催化剂长期稳定性的影响，可为改善催化剂的长期性能提供见解。最后，在相同反应条件下对所有Ga2O3多形体的催化行为进行深入比较研究，将有助于全面理解CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系。
