<English>
The structure-property-performance relationships of Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis reveal significant influences on catalytic activity and selectivity. Among the different polymorphs (α-, β-, γ-, and δ-Ga2O3), β- and γ-Ga2O3 exhibit superior performance in C3H8 conversion and C3H6 selectivity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This superior activity can be attributed to their structural characteristics and surface properties.

Structural Characteristics:
β-Ga2O3 has a monoclinic structure with gallium atoms equally distributed between tetrahedral and octahedral configurations [10.1021/cr5002436]. Both β- and γ-Ga2O3 contain tetrahedral Ga cations in their structure, which form coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites acting as strong Lewis acid sites. In contrast, α- and δ-Ga2O3 are constituted by octahedral Ga3+, which are more coordinated and exhibit weaker acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Surface Properties:
The surface acidity density decreases in the order of β > γ > δ > α, with surface basicity following the same trend [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. This correlation between surface acidity and catalytic activity highlights the importance of acidic sites in CO2-ODHP reactions. The presence of weak Lewis acid sites, particularly coordinatively unsaturated tetrahedral Ga3+ cations, is crucial for dehydrogenation activity [10.1021/cr5002436].

Lewis Acid Sites:
The population of catalytically relevant coordinatively unsaturated surface (cus) Ga species is a strong function of the surface area of Ga2O3 among different polymorphs [10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]. These sites are essential for hydrocarbon activation in a CO2 atmosphere. The proportion of tetrahedral Ga3+ sites correlates with weak acid sites, implying their essential role in creating surface acidity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

Reaction Mechanisms:
The CO2-ODHP reaction over Ga2O3 catalysts proceeds through different mechanisms during the initial and steady-state stages:

1. Initial stage: The reduction of Ga cations (Ga3+ to Ga+) occurs, benefiting the dehydrogenation of propane (DHP) [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The initial activity is dependent on the surface acid density and maximizes on β-Ga2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

2. Steady-state: The reaction proceeds via heterolytic dissociation on Ga oxide without a redox cycle [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]. The presence of acid sites facilitates certain reaction steps, providing an alternative pathway to promote C3H6 formation by bypassing the rate-determining step in C3H8 heterolytic dissociation [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4].

The role of CO2 in the reaction is multifaceted. It improves olefin yield by acting as a mild oxidant and shifting equilibrium towards product formation. CO2 also assists in decreasing coke deposition and helps with product desorption, particularly ethylene [10.1021/cr5002436]. However, CO2 can competitively adsorb on basic sites, potentially negatively affecting performance [10.1021/cr5002436].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The extensive research on Ga2O3 polymorphs in CO2-ODHP catalysis has provided valuable insights into the structure-property-performance relationships. However, there are still areas that warrant further investigation. Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. Detailed comparative studies of α-, β-, γ-, and δ-Ga2O3 polymorphs under identical reaction conditions to elucidate the specific contributions of each polymorph to catalytic performance.

2. In-depth investigation of the reaction mechanisms during the transition from initial to steady-state stages, particularly focusing on the role of Ga+ species and their evolution over time.

3. Development of novel synthesis methods to enhance the population of coordinatively unsaturated Ga3+ sites and optimize the balance between weak and medium-strong acid sites.

4. Exploration of advanced characterization techniques to probe the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and surface properties under reaction conditions.

5. Investigation of the synergistic effects between Ga2O3 and other metal oxides or supports to develop more efficient and stable catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions.

6. Computational studies to model the interaction between CO2, propane, and various Ga2O3 surfaces to gain atomic-level insights into the reaction mechanisms and guide catalyst design.

These future directions could lead to the development of more efficient and selective Ga2O3-based catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions, contributing to the broader field of sustainable chemical production and CO2 utilization.

<Chinese>
Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的结构-性质-性能关系显示出对催化活性和选择性的显著影响。在不同的多形体（α-、β-、γ-和δ-Ga2O3）中，β-和γ-Ga2O3在C3H8转化和C3H6选择性方面表现出优异的性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。这种优越的活性可归因于它们的结构特征和表面性质。

结构特征：
β-Ga2O3具有单斜结构，其中镓原子在四面体和八面体构型之间均匀分布[10.1021/cr5002436]。β-和γ-Ga2O3都含有四面体Ga阳离子，形成配位不饱和的Ga3+位点，作为强Lewis酸位点。相比之下，α-和δ-Ga2O3由八面体Ga3+构成，配位更加饱和，表现出较弱的酸性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

表面性质：
表面酸度密度按β > γ > δ > α的顺序递减，表面碱度也遵循相同的趋势[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。表面酸度与催化活性之间的这种相关性突出了酸性位点在CO2-ODHP反应中的重要性。弱Lewis酸位点的存在，特别是配位不饱和的四面体Ga3+阳离子，对脱氢活性至关重要[10.1021/cr5002436]。

Lewis酸位点：
具有催化相关性的配位不饱和表面（cus）Ga物种的数量与不同多形体中Ga2O3的表面积呈强相关性[10.1016/j.catcom.2012.11.004]。这些位点对于CO2氛围中的烃类活化至关重要。四面体Ga3+位点的比例与弱酸位点相关，表明它们在创造表面酸度方面起着重要作用[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

反应机理：
Ga2O3催化剂上的CO2-ODHP反应在初始和稳态阶段通过不同的机理进行：

1. 初始阶段：发生Ga阳离子的还原（Ga3+到Ga+），有利于丙烷的脱氢（DHP）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。初始活性取决于表面酸度密度，在β-Ga2O3上达到最大值[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

2. 稳态阶段：反应通过Ga氧化物上的异裂解离进行，没有氧化还原循环[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。酸性位点的存在促进了某些反应步骤，提供了一种替代途径来促进C3H6的形成，绕过了C3H8异裂解离中的速率决定步骤[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_4]。

CO2在反应中的作用是多方面的。它通过作为温和的氧化剂和将平衡转向产物形成来改善烯烃产率。CO2还有助于减少积碳沉积并帮助产物脱附，特别是乙烯[10.1021/cr5002436]。然而，CO2可以在碱性位点上竞争性吸附，可能会对性能产生负面影响[10.1021/cr5002436]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：对Ga2O3多形体在CO2-ODHP催化中的广泛研究为结构-性质-性能关系提供了宝贵的见解。然而，仍有一些领域值得进一步研究。未来的研究努力可能会集中在：

1. 在相同反应条件下对α-、β-、γ-和δ-Ga2O3多形体进行详细的比较研究，以阐明每种多形体对催化性能的具体贡献。

2. 深入研究从初始到稳态阶段的反应机理，特别关注Ga+物种的作用及其随时间的演变。

3. 开发新型合成方法，以增强配位不饱和Ga3+位点的数量，并优化弱酸位点和中强酸位点之间的平衡。

4. 探索先进的表征技术，以探测反应条件下催化剂结构和表面性质的动态变化。

5. 研究Ga2O3与其他金属氧化物或载体之间的协同效应，以开发更高效和稳定的CO2-ODHP反应催化剂。

6. 进行计算研究，模拟CO2、丙烷和各种Ga2O3表面之间的相互作用，以获得原子级的反应机理洞察，指导催化剂设计。

这些未来方向可能会导致开发出更高效和选择性的Ga2O3基催化剂用于CO2-ODHP反应，为可持续化学品生产和CO2利用的广泛领域做出贡献。
