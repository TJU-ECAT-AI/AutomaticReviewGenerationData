This approach allows for a more standardized comparison across different catalyst types, particularly for propane and isobutane dehydrogenation reactions.

By excluding factors such as pressure, temperature, preparation method, promoter, and catalyst support, the methodology simplifies the comparative analysis. This simplification enables a more direct comparison of catalyst performance, but it also means that potentially important variables are not considered in the evaluation.

1. Standardization: It provides a common basis for comparing catalysts across different studies and formulations.
