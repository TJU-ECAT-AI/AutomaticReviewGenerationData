Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address the challenges of increasing industrial demand for propylene and limitations of traditional production methods in several ways:

1. PDH offers a more direct and economical route for propylene production, especially with the growing availability of low-cost propane from shale gas.

2. PDH provides an on-purpose propylene production method, allowing manufacturers to better control output based on demand.

3. PDH reduces dependence on traditional feedstocks like naphtha and oil byproducts, which are subject to price fluctuations and supply constraints.

Innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed to overcome deactivation issues, enhance stability, and improve recyclability in PDH processes:

1. Supported vanadium catalysts are being explored as alternatives to expensive platinum-based and toxic chromium-based catalysts, offering good activity, selectivity, and regeneration performance.

2. Research is focusing on understanding the surface structure and behavior of catalysts under reaction conditions, using techniques like in situ DRIFTS experiments and DFT calculations.

3. The role of surface-bound hydroxyl groups on catalysts like VOx/γ-Al2O3 is being investigated, with findings suggesting that these groups can improve catalyst stability by suppressing coke deposition.

4. Tailoring the coverage of surface hydroxyl groups on supported catalysts is being explored as a method to balance activity and stability in PDH reactions.

5. Understanding the relationship between catalyst surface structure, oxidation state, and performance is helping researchers design catalysts with improved longevity and resistance to deactivation.

These strategies aim to develop more efficient, stable, and recyclable catalysts for both oxidative and non-oxidative PDH processes, addressing the key challenges in industrial propylene production.

丙烷脱氢（PDH）技术通过以下几种方式解决了工业丙烯需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的挑战：

1. PDH为丙烯生产提供了一条更直接、更经济的路径，特别是随着页岩气开采带来的低成本丙烷供应增加。

2. PDH提供了一种定向丙烯生产方法，使制造商能够根据需求更好地控制产量。

3. PDH减少了对传统原料如石脑油和石油副产品的依赖，这些原料容易受价格波动和供应限制的影响。

正在开发创新的催化剂设计策略，以克服失活问题、提高稳定性并改善PDH过程中的可回收性：

1. 正在探索支持的钒催化剂作为昂贵的铂基和有毒的铬基催化剂的替代品，提供良好的活性、选择性和再生性能。

2. 研究重点是利用原位DRIFTS实验和DFT计算等技术，了解催化剂在反应条件下的表面结构和行为。

3. 正在研究表面羟基在VOx/γ-Al2O3等催化剂上的作用，研究结果表明这些基团可以通过抑制焦炭沉积来提高催化剂稳定性。

4. 正在探索调整支持催化剂表面羟基覆盖率的方法，以平衡PDH反应中的活性和稳定性。

5. 了解催化剂表面结构、氧化态和性能之间的关系，有助于研究人员设计寿命更长、抗失活能力更强的催化剂。

这些策略旨在为氧化和非氧化PDH过程开发更高效、更稳定和可回收的催化剂，解决工业丙烯生产中的关键挑战。
