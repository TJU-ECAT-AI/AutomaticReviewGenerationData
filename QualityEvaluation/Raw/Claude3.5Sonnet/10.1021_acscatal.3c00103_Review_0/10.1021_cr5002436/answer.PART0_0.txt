<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) techniques address the challenges of increasing industrial demand for propylene and limitations of traditional production methods in several ways:

1. PDH offers a more economical and efficient conversion technology compared to traditional high-energy, low-selectivity processes.

2. It takes advantage of the recent boom in shale gas production, which has made the United States a low-cost chemical producer.

3. PDH processes are optimized to produce polymer-quality olefins, with about 5 million tons of propylene produced annually through this method.

To overcome deactivation issues and enhance stability and recyclability, several innovative catalyst design strategies are being developed:

1. Incorporation of a regeneration step under an oxidative atmosphere to address coke-induced deactivation.

2. Research efforts to lower noble metal loading, increase catalyst stability, and improve energy efficiency of the dehydrogenation process.

3. Exploration of oxidative dehydrogenation, which eliminates thermodynamic limitations and allows for full conversion of paraffins.

These strategies aim to improve the overall performance and sustainability of PDH processes, making them more viable for industrial-scale propylene production.

丙烷脱氢（PDH）技术通过以下方式解决了工业对丙烯需求增加和传统生产方法局限性的挑战：

1. 与传统的高能耗、低选择性工艺相比，PDH提供了更经济、更高效的转化技术。

2. 它利用了近期页岩气生产的繁荣，使美国成为低成本化学品生产国。

3. PDH工艺经过优化，可生产聚合物级纯度的烯烃，每年通过这种方法生产约500万吨丙烯。

为了克服失活问题并提高稳定性和可回收性，正在开发几种创新的催化剂设计策略：

1. 在氧化性气氛下引入再生步骤，以解决焦炭导致的失活问题。

2. 研究努力降低贵金属负载量，提高催化剂稳定性，并提高脱氢过程的能源效率。

3. 探索氧化脱氢，消除热力学限制，实现烷烃的完全转化。

这些策略旨在提高PDH工艺的整体性能和可持续性，使其更适合工业规模的丙烯生产。
