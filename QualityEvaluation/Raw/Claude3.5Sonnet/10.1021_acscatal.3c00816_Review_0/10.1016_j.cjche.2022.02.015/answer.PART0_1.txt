<EnglishVersionAnswer>

PDH technology offers several key advantages that make it a promising method for propylene production:

1. Feedstock availability: PDH can utilize the increasing supply of propane from shale gas, providing a stable and abundant feedstock source.

2. Direct production: Unlike conventional methods, PDH directly converts propane to propylene, potentially improving process efficiency.

3. Environmental benefits: As a cleaner utilization of fossil energy, PDH contributes to carbon neutrality goals.

4. Addressing limitations of current catalysts: PDH research aims to develop alternatives to platinum and chromium catalysts, which face scarcity and toxicity issues respectively.

5. Meeting growing demand: PDH helps address the worldwide increasing demand for propylene.

6. Selectivity: PDH can potentially offer high propylene selectivity compared to some conventional methods.

These advantages position PDH as a promising technology to address challenges in feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity faced by conventional propylene production methods.

5. 满足不断增长的需求：PDH有助于满足全球不断增长的丙烯需求。

6. 选择性：与一些传统方法相比，PDH可能提供更高的丙烯选择性。

这些优势使PDH成为一种有前途的技术，可以解决传统丙烯生产方法在原料可用性、过程效率和产品选择性方面面临的挑战。
