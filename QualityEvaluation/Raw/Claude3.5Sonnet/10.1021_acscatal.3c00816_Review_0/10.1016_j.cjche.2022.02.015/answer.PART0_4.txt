<EnglishVersionAnswer>

PDH technology offers several key advantages that make it the most promising method for propylene production:

1. Feedstock availability: PDH directly utilizes propane, which is increasingly available from shale gas resources. This addresses the challenge of feedstock supply faced by conventional methods.

2. Process efficiency: As a direct conversion process, PDH is more efficient compared to traditional cracking methods that produce propylene as a by-product.

3. Product selectivity: PDH can achieve high propylene selectivity, allowing for more targeted production compared to less selective conventional processes.

4. Environmental considerations: PDH represents a cleaner utilization of fossil energy resources, aligning with goals for carbon neutrality and reduced environmental impact.

5. Economic benefits: PDH reduces reliance on expensive and scarce platinum catalysts used in some conventional methods, potentially lowering production costs.

These advantages allow PDH to address growing propylene demand while overcoming limitations of feedstock availability, efficiency, selectivity, and sustainability associated with conventional production routes.

PDH技术提供了几个关键优势，使其成为最有前景的丙烯生产方法：

1. 原料可用性：PDH直接利用丙烷，而丙烷越来越多地来自页岩气资源。这解决了传统方法面临的原料供应挑战。

2. 工艺效率：作为一种直接转化工艺，PDH比传统的将丙烯作为副产品的裂解方法更高效。

3. 产品选择性：PDH可以实现高丙烯选择性，与选择性较低的传统工艺相比，允许更有针对性的生产。

4. 环境考虑：PDH代表了化石能源资源的更清洁利用，符合碳中和和减少环境影响的目标。

5. 经济效益：PDH减少了对某些传统方法中使用的昂贵和稀缺铂催化剂的依赖，可能降低生产成本。

这些优势使PDH能够满足不断增长的丙烯需求，同时克服与传统生产路线相关的原料可用性、效率、选择性和可持续性的限制。
