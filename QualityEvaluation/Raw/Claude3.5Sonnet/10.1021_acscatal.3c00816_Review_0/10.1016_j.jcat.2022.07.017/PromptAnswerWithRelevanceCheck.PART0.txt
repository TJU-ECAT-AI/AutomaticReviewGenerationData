3. Higher selectivity to propylene, especially when using vanadium-based catalysts which are highly active and selective.

4. Good regeneration ability of catalysts, improving process efficiency.

5. On-purpose production of propylene to meet increasing demand, rather than as a byproduct.

PDH technology specifically addresses challenges of conventional methods by providing a more efficient, selective, and cost-effective route to produce propylene using readily available propane feedstock.
