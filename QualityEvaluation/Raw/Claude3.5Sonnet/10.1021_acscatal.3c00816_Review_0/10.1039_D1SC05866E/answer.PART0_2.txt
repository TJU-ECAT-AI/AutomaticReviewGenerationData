<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The key advantages of propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology for propylene production include:

1. Utilization of low-cost propane feedstock from shale gas.
2. Direct production of propylene without byproducts.
3. Addressing the increasing global demand for propylene.

PDH technology addresses challenges faced by conventional methods in several ways:

1. Feedstock availability: It uses abundant and low-cost propane from shale gas.
2. Process efficiency: It directly converts propane to propylene in a single step.
3. Product selectivity: PDH can achieve high propylene selectivity compared to other processes.

However, current PDH technologies still face some challenges, such as catalyst deactivation and environmental concerns with certain catalysts. The development of new catalysts, like the In-CHA catalyst described in this study, aims to overcome these limitations by offering better stability, selectivity, and environmental compatibility.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

丙烷直接脱氢（PDH）技术用于丙烯生产的主要优势包括：

1. 利用来自页岩气的低成本丙烷原料。
2. 直接生产丙烯，无副产品。
3. 满足不断增长的全球丙烯需求。

PDH技术通过以下几种方式解决了传统方法面临的挑战：

1. 原料可用性：使用丰富且低成本的页岩气丙烷。
2. 工艺效率：在单一步骤中直接将丙烷转化为丙烯。
3. 产品选择性：与其他工艺相比，PDH可以实现更高的丙烯选择性。

然而，目前的PDH技术仍面临一些挑战，如催化剂失活和某些催化剂的环境问题。本研究中描述的In-CHA催化剂等新型催化剂的开发旨在通过提供更好的稳定性、选择性和环境兼容性来克服这些限制。
