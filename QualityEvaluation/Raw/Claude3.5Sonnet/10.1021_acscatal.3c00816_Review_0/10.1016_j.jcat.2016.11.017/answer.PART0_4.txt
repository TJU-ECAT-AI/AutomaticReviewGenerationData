1. Utilization of abundant propane feedstock, especially in North America and the Middle East.

2. Direct conversion of propane to propylene, which can potentially improve process efficiency compared to conventional methods.

3. Ongoing research to develop new and improved catalysts, focusing on enhancing stability, activity, and selectivity.

4. Commercial viability, as demonstrated by existing processes like Catofin and Oleflex.

PDH addresses challenges faced by conventional production methods by:

1. Providing an alternative source of propylene, reducing dependence on traditional petroleum-based feedstocks.
