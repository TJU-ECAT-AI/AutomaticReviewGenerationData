Propane direct dehydrogenation (PDH) technology has emerged as the most promising method for propylene production due to several key advantages:

1. Feedstock availability: PDH addresses the challenge of fossil fuel scarcity by utilizing propane, which is abundant due to the discovery of shale gas. This ensures a stable and readily available feedstock for propylene production.

2. Process efficiency: PDH offers a direct route to convert propane into propylene, which is more efficient compared to conventional methods that may involve multiple steps or complex cracking processes.

3. Product selectivity: While pure platinum catalysts suffer from low selectivity, the development of bimetallic catalysts (e.g., PtIn, PtSn) has significantly improved propylene selectivity in PDH processes.

4. Environmental friendliness: Pt-based catalysts used in PDH are more environmentally friendly compared to Cr-based catalysts, addressing growing environmental concerns in chemical production.

5. High yields and stability: Commercial PDH processes using Pt- and Cr-based catalysts deliver high initial yields and stability, making it an attractive option for large-scale propylene production.

6. Meeting growing demand: PDH technology is well-positioned to meet the increasing global demand for propylene, which is crucial for the production of various important chemicals and materials like polypropylene, propylene oxide, and acrylonitrile.

7. Adaptability to changing feedstock landscape: As the availability of traditional fossil fuel feedstocks becomes limited, PDH offers a viable alternative that can utilize the growing supplies of propane from shale gas production.

These advantages make PDH technology a superior choice for propylene production, addressing the challenges of feedstock availability, process efficiency, and product selectivity faced by conventional production methods.

丙烷直接脱氢(PDH)技术因以下关键优势成为最有前景的丙烯生产方法：

1. 原料可用性：PDH通过利用页岩气开采带来的丰富丙烷资源，解决了化石燃料稀缺的挑战。这确保了丙烯生产的原料稳定且易得。

2. 工艺效率：与可能涉及多个步骤或复杂裂解过程的传统方法相比，PDH提供了一种将丙烷直接转化为丙烯的更高效路径。

3. 产品选择性：虽然纯铂催化剂的选择性较低，但双金属催化剂（如PtIn、PtSn）的开发显著提高了PDH过程中的丙烯选择性。

4. 环境友好性：PDH使用的铂基催化剂比铬基催化剂更环保，解决了化学品生产中日益增长的环境问题。

5. 高产量和稳定性：使用铂和铬基催化剂的商业PDH工艺提供了高初始产量和稳定性，使其成为大规模丙烯生产的理想选择。

6. 满足日益增长的需求：PDH技术能够满足全球日益增长的丙烯需求，这对于生产聚丙烯、环氧丙烷和丙烯腈等各种重要化学品和材料至关重要。

7. 适应不断变化的原料格局：随着传统化石燃料原料的可用性受限，PDH提供了一种可行的替代方案，能够利用页岩气生产带来的不断增长的丙烷供应。

这些优势使PDH技术成为丙烯生产的优选方法，解决了传统生产方法在原料可用性、工艺效率和产品选择性方面面临的挑战。
