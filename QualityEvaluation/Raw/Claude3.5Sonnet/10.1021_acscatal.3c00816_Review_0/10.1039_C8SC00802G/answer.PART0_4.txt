<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Propane dehydrogenation (PDH) technology has emerged as the most promising method for propylene production due to several key advantages:

1. Feedstock availability: PDH addresses the challenge of fossil fuel scarcity by utilizing propane, which is increasingly available due to the discovery of shale gas. This ensures a stable and potentially more economical feedstock source.

2. Meeting growing demand: As propylene is a crucial compound in the chemical industry, used in the production of various important products, PDH offers a direct route to meet the increasing global demand for propylene.

3. Process efficiency: Commercial PDH processes using Pt- and Cr-based catalysts deliver high initial yields and stability, indicating good process efficiency.

4. Environmental considerations: Pt-based catalysts used in PDH are more environmentally friendly compared to Cr-based catalysts, aligning with growing environmental concerns in industrial processes.

5. Selectivity improvements: While pure Pt catalysts suffer from low selectivity towards propylene, research is ongoing to improve selectivity through alloying with other metals, addressing the challenge of product selectivity.

6. Reduced coke formation: Efforts to alloy Pt with other metals also aim to enhance anti-coking ability, which is a significant challenge in conventional production methods.

By addressing these key aspects, PDH technology offers a more tailored and efficient approach to propylene production compared to conventional methods, making it particularly promising in the face of changing feedstock availability and increasing demand for propylene.

丙烷脱氢（PDH）技术因几个关键优势而成为最有前景的丙烯生产方法：

1. 原料可用性：PDH通过利用丙烷来解决化石燃料稀缺的挑战，而丙烷因页岩气的发现而越来越多。这确保了稳定且可能更经济的原料来源。

2. 满足不断增长的需求：由于丙烯是化学工业中的关键化合物，用于生产各种重要产品，PDH提供了一种直接途径来满足全球不断增长的丙烯需求。

3. 工艺效率：使用铂和铬基催化剂的商业PDH工艺具有高初始产率和稳定性，表明工艺效率良好。

4. 环境考虑：PDH中使用的铂基催化剂比铬基催化剂更环保，符合工业过程中日益增长的环境关注。

5. 选择性改进：虽然纯铂催化剂对丙烯的选择性较低，但通过与其他金属合金化来提高选择性的研究正在进行中，解决了产品选择性的挑战。

6. 降低积碳形成：将铂与其他金属合金化的努力还旨在提高抗积碳能力，这是传统生产方法中的一个重大挑战。

通过解决这些关键方面，PDH技术相比传统方法提供了一种更有针对性和高效的丙烯生产方法，使其在原料可用性变化和丙烯需求增加的情况下特别有前景。
