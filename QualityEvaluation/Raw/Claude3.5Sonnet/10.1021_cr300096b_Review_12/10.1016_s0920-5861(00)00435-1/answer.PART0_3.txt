1. Formation of PtSn alloys with hydroxy groups on Pt/SnOx interfaces, which are involved in CO oxidation.

3. Pt3Sn alloy can adsorb oxygen more readily at lower temperatures compared to PtSn alloy.

4. Intimate contact between metallic phase and SnOx interface enhances adsorption ability and CO oxidation activity.
