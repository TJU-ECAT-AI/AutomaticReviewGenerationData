Bimetallic Pt-Sn catalysts influence CO oxidation mechanisms and efficiencies in several ways:

1. PtSn alloys with hydroxy groups on Pt/SnOx interfaces are involved in CO oxidation.

2. The presence of Pt3Sn alloy phase enables catalytic CO oxidation at lower temperatures.

3. Pt3Sn can adsorb oxygen more readily at lower temperatures compared to PtSn alloy.

4. The intimate contact between the metallic phase and SnOx interface enhances adsorption ability and CO oxidation activity.
