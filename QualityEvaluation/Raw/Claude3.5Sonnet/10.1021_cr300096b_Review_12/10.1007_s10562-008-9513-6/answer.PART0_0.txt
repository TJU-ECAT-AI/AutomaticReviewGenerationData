For Pt-Fe catalysts, a bifunctional mechanism has been proposed that differs from mechanisms seen in other supported catalysts. This involves a noncompetitive dual site mechanism where platinum sites adsorb CO while iron sites dissociatively adsorb O2, enhancing surface reactions between neighboring sites.
