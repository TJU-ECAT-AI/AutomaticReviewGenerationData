The literature provides some insights into how bimetallic Pt-Pd catalysts influence CO oxidation mechanisms and efficiencies:

1. Bimetallic Pt-Pd systems enhance catalyst properties through synergistic electronic and geometric effects.

2. Increasing Pd content in Pt-Pd catalysts generally improves CO oxidation activity, with an optimal Pt:Pd ratio of 1:7 showing the best performance (76% CO conversion, 55% selectivity at 90-110°C).

3. The superior activity of Pt-Pd catalysts is attributed to higher dispersion of supported particles and suppression of particle growth.
