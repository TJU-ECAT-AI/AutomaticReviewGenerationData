The study provides insights into how same-valence dopants (SVDs) and high-valence dopants (HVDs) influence the catalytic properties of different metal oxides:

1. For TiO2, both SVDs and HVDs (V, Cr, Mo, Mn, W) lowered the energy of vacancy formation, making the doped oxides better oxidants than undoped TiO2. This contradicts the expectation that HVDs would increase vacancy formation energy.

2. The dopants' primary role is to change the binding energy of oxygen atoms in the surface layer, rather than directly participating in the oxidation reaction.

3. For ZnO, HVDs increased the energy of vacancy formation, consistent with the initial hypothesis.

4. The study reveals that dopant behavior can vary significantly between different host oxides (e.g., TiO2 vs. ZnO), challenging the formulation of general predictive rules.

5. The research highlights the complexity of dopant effects, which depend on factors such as the nature of the host oxide (reducible vs. irreducible) and the specific dopant-oxide interaction.

Challenges in formulating general predictive rules for dopant behavior persist due to:

1. Varying effects of dopants in different host oxides.
2. Complex interactions between dopants and host oxides.
3. The influence of factors such as reducibility of the host oxide.
4. The need to consider both thermodynamic and kinetic aspects of catalytic reactions.

Further research is needed to develop a comprehensive understanding of dopant behavior across various oxide systems and catalytic applications.

这项研究提供了有关相同价态掺杂剂(SVDs)和高价态掺杂剂(HVDs)如何影响不同金属氧化物的催化性能的见解：

1. 对于TiO2，SVDs和HVDs（V、Cr、Mo、Mn、W）都降低了空位形成能，使掺杂氧化物成为比未掺杂TiO2更好的氧化剂。这与HVDs会增加空位形成能的预期相矛盾。

2. 掺杂剂的主要作用是改变表面层氧原子的结合能，而不是直接参与氧化反应。

3. 对于ZnO，HVDs增加了空位形成能，这与最初的假设一致。

4. 研究表明，掺杂剂的行为在不同的宿主氧化物（如TiO2与ZnO）之间可能存在显著差异，这给制定通用预测规则带来了挑战。

5. 研究强调了掺杂剂效应的复杂性，这取决于诸如宿主氧化物的性质（可还原vs.不可还原）和特定的掺杂剂-氧化物相互作用等因素。

制定掺杂剂行为通用预测规则的挑战仍然存在，原因如下：

1. 掺杂剂在不同宿主氧化物中的效果各不相同。
2. 掺杂剂与宿主氧化物之间的复杂相互作用。
3. 宿主氧化物的可还原性等因素的影响。
4. 需要考虑催化反应的热力学和动力学方面。

需要进一步研究以全面了解掺杂剂在各种氧化物系统和催化应用中的行为。
