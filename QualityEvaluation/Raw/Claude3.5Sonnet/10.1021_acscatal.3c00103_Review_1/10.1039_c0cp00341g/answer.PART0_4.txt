The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) consists of three steps:

1. Dissociative adsorption: Propane adsorbs onto the catalyst surface and dissociates into adsorbed propyl and hydrogen.

2. β-H extraction: A hydrogen atom is removed from the β-carbon of the adsorbed propyl species, forming adsorbed propylene.

3. Desorption: The propylene product desorbs from the catalyst surface.

There is ongoing debate about whether the dissociative adsorption or β-H extraction is the rate-controlling step. Some studies suggest dissociative adsorption is rate-limiting due to the strong C-H bond in propane. Others argue β-H extraction is the slow step based on kinetic isotope effects.

This uncertainty impacts our understanding of PDH in several ways:

1. Catalyst design: If dissociative adsorption is rate-limiting, catalysts should be optimized for C-H activation. If β-H extraction is slow, catalysts promoting H transfer may be more effective.

2. Reaction conditions: The rate-determining step influences how temperature and pressure affect reaction rates.

3. Kinetic modeling: Accurate models require correct identification of the rate-limiting step.

4. Mechanistic understanding: The nature of the transition state and reaction intermediates depends on which step is rate-controlling.

5. Process optimization: Reactor design and operating conditions should target the slowest step for maximum efficiency.

Resolving this debate is crucial for developing more efficient PDH catalysts and processes. Further experimental and theoretical studies are needed to definitively determine the rate-controlling step under various conditions.

丙烷脱氢(PDH)的反向Horiuti-Polanyi机制包括三个步骤：

1. 解离吸附：丙烷吸附在催化剂表面并解离成吸附的丙基和氢。

2. β-H提取：从吸附的丙基物种的β碳上移除一个氢原子，形成吸附的丙烯。

3. 脱附：丙烯产物从催化剂表面脱附。

关于解离吸附还是β-H提取是速率控制步骤，目前仍存在争议。一些研究表明，由于丙烷中C-H键的强度，解离吸附是速率限制步骤。其他研究则基于动力学同位素效应，认为β-H提取是缓慢步骤。

这种不确定性对我们理解PDH过程有以下几个方面的影响：

1. 催化剂设计：如果解离吸附是速率限制步骤，催化剂应该被优化以促进C-H活化。如果β-H提取是缓慢步骤，促进H转移的催化剂可能更有效。

2. 反应条件：速率决定步骤影响温度和压力如何影响反应速率。

3. 动力学建模：准确的模型需要正确识别速率限制步骤。

4. 机理理解：过渡态和反应中间体的性质取决于哪个步骤是速率控制步骤。

5. 过程优化：反应器设计和操作条件应针对最慢的步骤以实现最大效率。

解决这个争议对开发更高效的PDH催化剂和工艺至关重要。需要进一步的实验和理论研究来明确确定在各种条件下的速率控制步骤。
