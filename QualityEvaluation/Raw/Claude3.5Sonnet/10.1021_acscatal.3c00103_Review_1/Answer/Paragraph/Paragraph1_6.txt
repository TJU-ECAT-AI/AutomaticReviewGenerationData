The reverse Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) consists of three main steps:

1. Dissociative adsorption: Propane molecules adsorb onto the catalyst surface and dissociate into adsorbed propyl and hydrogen species.

2. β-H extraction: A hydrogen atom is extracted from the β-carbon of the adsorbed propyl species, forming an adsorbed propylene intermediate.

3. Desorption: The adsorbed propylene and hydrogen desorb from the catalyst surface as products.

There is an ongoing debate in the scientific community regarding which step is the rate-controlling step in this reaction. Some studies suggest that the dissociative adsorption process is rate-limiting, while others indicate that the β-H extraction step controls the overall reaction rate [10.1039/D0CS01262A]. This uncertainty depends on various factors such as catalyst surface, cluster size, and reaction conditions.

For example:
- On Pt(211) and Pt(100) surfaces with smaller Pt cluster sizes, the first C-H bond activation (dissociative adsorption) is proposed as the rate-determining step (RDS).
- On Pt(111) surfaces, some studies suggest the second C-H bond activation (β-H extraction) is the RDS, while others identify the first C-H activation as rate-limiting [10.1039/D0CS01262A].

The debate impacts our understanding of the PDH process in several ways:

1. Catalyst design: Without a clear consensus on the RDS, it becomes challenging to optimize catalyst properties specifically for enhancing the rate-limiting step [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016].

2. Reaction conditions: The optimal temperature, pressure, and reactant concentrations may vary depending on which step is rate-limiting, making it difficult to establish universal operating parameters [10.1039/D0CS01262A].

3. Kinetic modeling: Accurate kinetic models require correct identification of the RDS, and the current uncertainty may lead to discrepancies in predicting reaction rates and yields [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016].

4. Mechanistic understanding: The ambiguity in the RDS complicates efforts to fully elucidate the reaction mechanism and identify intermediate species [10.1039/D0CS01260B].

5. Catalyst comparison: Evaluating the performance of different catalysts becomes more challenging when the RDS may vary across catalyst types and surface structures [10.1039/D0CS01262A].

6. Catalyst deactivation: Understanding the rate-limiting step could provide insights into the primary causes of catalyst deactivation, affecting strategies for catalyst regeneration and longevity [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016].

This ongoing debate emphasizes the complexity of the PDH process and highlights the need for further research combining experimental and computational approaches to gain a more comprehensive understanding of the reaction mechanism and resolve the uncertainty surrounding the rate-determining step [10.1039/D0CS01262A].

反向Horiuti-Polanyi机理的丙烷脱氢（PDH）过程包括三个主要步骤：

1. 解离性吸附：丙烷分子吸附在催化剂表面并解离，形成吸附的丙基和氢物种。

2. β-H提取：从吸附的丙基物种的β-碳上提取一个氢原子，形成吸附的丙烯中间体。

3. 脱附：吸附的丙烯和氢从催化剂表面脱附，形成产物。

科学界对于这一反应中哪一步是速率控制步骤存在持续争论。一些研究表明解离性吸附过程是速率限制步骤，而其他研究则指出β-H提取步骤控制整体反应速率 [10.1039/D0CS01262A]。这种不确定性取决于诸如催化剂表面、簇大小和反应条件等各种因素。

例如：
- 在较小Pt簇尺寸的Pt(211)和Pt(100)表面上，第一个C-H键活化（解离性吸附）被认为是速率决定步骤（RDS）。
- 在Pt(111)表面上，一些研究表明第二个C-H键活化（β-H提取）是RDS，而其他研究则认为第一个C-H活化是速率限制步骤 [10.1039/D0CS01262A]。

这一争论影响了我们对PDH过程的理解，主要体现在以下几个方面：

1. 催化剂设计：由于对RDS缺乏明确共识，难以针对性地优化催化剂属性以增强速率限制步骤 [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016]。

2. 反应条件：最佳温度、压力和反应物浓度可能因速率限制步骤的不同而变化，这使得建立通用的操作参数变得困难 [10.1039/D0CS01262A]。

3. 动力学建模：准确的动力学模型需要正确识别RDS，当前的不确定性可能导致预测反应速率和产率时出现差异 [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016]。

4. 机理理解：RDS的模糊性使得完全阐明反应机理和识别中间物种的努力变得复杂 [10.1039/D0CS01260B]。

5. 催化剂比较：当RDS可能因催化剂类型和表面结构而异时，评估不同催化剂的性能变得更具挑战性 [10.1039/D0CS01262A]。

6. 催化剂失活：了解速率限制步骤可能为催化剂失活的主要原因提供洞见，影响催化剂再生和寿命策略 [10.1016/j.fuproc.2016.04.016]。

这一持续的争论强调了PDH过程的复杂性，并突出了需要进一步结合实验和计算方法的研究，以获得对反应机理更全面的理解，并解决围绕速率决定步骤的不确定性 [10.1039/D0CS01262A]。

综合理解与未来展望：围绕丙烷脱氢反向Horiuti-Polanyi机理中速率控制步骤的持续争论揭示了多相催化的复杂性。这种复杂性呼吁在催化剂设计和表征方面采用创新方法。

未来的研究努力可能集中在：

4. 多尺度建模：将原子尺度模拟与反应器尺度模型相结合，可以提供对PDH过程更全面的理解，弥合基础机理洞察与实际工业应用之间的差距。

这些未来方向不仅可以解决当前的争论，还可以为更高效和选择性的PDH过程铺平道路，为可持续化学生产的更广泛领域做出贡献。
