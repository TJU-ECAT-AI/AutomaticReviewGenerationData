<English>
The reducibility and support materials of Fe oxide-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in CO2-ODHP reactions. Key findings from the literature review include:

1. Support Material: 
Active carbon, particularly low-ash content varieties like D-90, has been found to be the most effective support for Fe oxide catalysts in CO2-ODHP reactions [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]. CeO2 has also been identified as a promising support due to its oxygen storage/release capability, which facilitates CO2 activation and suppresses coke formation [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]. The choice of support material significantly affects catalyst performance, with SiO2-supported catalysts showing unique benefits from CO2 promotion in some cases [10.1016/S0920-5861(98)00245-4].

2. Reducibility:
The reducibility of Fe oxide catalysts plays a crucial role in their performance. Fe2O3 supported on activated carbon (AC) exhibits improved reducibility, following the pathway Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe, which contributes to its high catalytic performance [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]. However, the reducibility can be retarded when Fe2O3 is supported on materials like γ-Al2O3 due to strong interactions forming hardly reducible phases [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6].

3. Promoters and Additives:
The addition of alkali metal oxides (Li, Na, K) significantly enhances catalyst activity, with an optimal alkali metal to Fe atomic ratio of 0.1 [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]. Noble metals (Pd, Ag, Pt) and other elements (Nb) also enhance the reducibility of Fe-based catalysts, improving catalytic activity and selectivity [10.1007/s11144-008-5339-x].

4. Reaction Mechanism:
The proposed mechanism for CO2-ODHP over Fe oxide-based catalysts involves two main steps: (1) C3H8 oxidation to C3H6 using lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4, and (2) reoxidation of the reduced Fe oxide by CO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]. The presence of oxidized Fe species plays a crucial role in promoting the selective formation of propylene [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w].

5. Catalyst Stability:
Fe-doped CeO2 catalysts demonstrate enhanced stability and promising catalytic performance for propane oxidative dehydrogenation using CO2 as a soft oxidant [10.1039/D0CY00586J]. The oxygen mobility and storage capacity (OSC) of ceria-based catalysts contribute to the formation of stable surface active sites [10.1039/D0CY00586J].

6. Optimization Strategies:
To optimize catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP, research should focus on:
- Using high-quality, low-ash content active carbon supports or reducible oxides like CeO2 [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7, 10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w].
- Maintaining an optimal iron content (around 5 wt%) [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7].
- Incorporating appropriate promoters and additives [10.1007/s11144-008-5339-x].
- Tailoring the surface structure to optimize CO2 adsorption and subsequent reaction steps [10.1002/aic.16670].
- Balancing the reducibility of the catalyst with its ability to be reoxidized by CO2 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comprehensive review of Fe oxide-based catalysts for CO2-ODHP reactions reveals the complex interplay between catalyst composition, structure, and performance. Future research directions could include:

1. Development of novel support materials: Exploring hybrid supports combining the benefits of active carbon and reducible oxides like CeO2 to optimize both reducibility and oxygen storage capacity.

2. In-situ characterization techniques: Advancing operando spectroscopy methods to better understand the dynamic changes in catalyst structure and oxidation state during the reaction.

3. Rational design of multi-component catalysts: Investigating synergistic effects between Fe oxides, noble metals, and promoters to create highly efficient and stable catalytic systems.

4. Computational modeling: Utilizing advanced DFT calculations and machine learning approaches to predict optimal catalyst compositions and structures for CO2-ODHP reactions.

5. Process intensification: Exploring novel reactor designs and operating conditions to maximize the benefits of CO2 as a soft oxidant while minimizing side reactions and catalyst deactivation.

6. Sustainable catalyst synthesis: Developing eco-friendly methods for catalyst preparation, potentially utilizing biomass-derived carbons or waste materials as precursors for supports or active components.

7. Mechanistic studies: Conducting more detailed isotope labeling experiments and kinetic analyses to elucidate the precise reaction pathways and rate-determining steps in CO2-ODHP over Fe oxide-based catalysts.

These research directions aim to address the current limitations in catalyst performance and stability, potentially leading to more efficient and sustainable processes for propylene production using CO2 as a soft oxidant.

<Chinese>
Fe氧化物基催化剂的可还原性和载体材料显著影响其在CO2-ODHP反应中的性能。文献综述的主要发现包括：

1. 载体材料：
活性炭，特别是低灰分含量的D-90型，被发现是CO2-ODHP反应中Fe氧化物催化剂最有效的载体[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]。由于CeO2具有氧储存/释放能力，可促进CO2活化并抑制积碳，因此也被认为是一种有前景的载体材料[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。载体材料的选择显著影响催化剂性能，在某些情况下，SiO2负载的催化剂显示出独特的CO2促进效果[10.1016/S0920-5861(98)00245-4]。

2. 可还原性：
Fe氧化物催化剂的可还原性在其性能中起着至关重要的作用。负载在活性炭（AC）上的Fe2O3表现出改善的可还原性，遵循Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe的还原路径，这有助于其高催化性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]。然而，当Fe2O3负载在γ-Al2O3等材料上时，由于形成难以还原的相，可还原性可能会受到抑制[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]。

3. 助剂和添加剂：
碱金属氧化物（Li、Na、K）的添加显著提高了催化剂活性，最佳碱金属与Fe的原子比为0.1 [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]。贵金属（Pd、Ag、Pt）和其他元素（Nb）也能增强Fe基催化剂的可还原性，提高催化活性和选择性[10.1007/s11144-008-5339-x]。

4. 反应机理：
Fe氧化物基催化剂上CO2-ODHP的提议机理包括两个主要步骤：（1）使用来自Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧将C3H8氧化为C3H6，（2）CO2重新氧化还原后的Fe氧化物[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]。氧化态Fe物种的存在在促进丙烯的选择性生成中起着关键作用[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。

5. 催化剂稳定性：
Fe掺杂的CeO2催化剂在使用CO2作为软氧化剂的丙烷氧化脱氢反应中表现出增强的稳定性和有前景的催化性能[10.1039/D0CY00586J]。基于氧化铈的催化剂的氧移动性和氧储存容量（OSC）有助于形成稳定的表面活性位点[10.1039/D0CY00586J]。

6. 优化策略：
为优化CO2-ODHP的催化系统，研究应关注：
- 使用高质量、低灰分含量的活性炭载体或可还原氧化物如CeO2 [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7, 10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。
- 维持最佳铁含量（约5 wt%）[10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]。
- 引入适当的助剂和添加剂[10.1007/s11144-008-5339-x]。
- 调整表面结构以优化CO2吸附和后续反应步骤[10.1002/aic.16670]。
- 平衡催化剂的可还原性与其被CO2重新氧化的能力[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_6]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：对Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP反应中的全面综述揭示了催化剂成分、结构和性能之间复杂的相互作用。未来的研究方向可能包括：

1. 开发新型载体材料：探索结合活性炭和可还原氧化物（如CeO2）优点的混合载体，以优化可还原性和氧储存容量。

2. 原位表征技术：推进操作中光谱法，以更好地理解反应过程中催化剂结构和氧化态的动态变化。

3. 多组分催化剂的理性设计：研究Fe氧化物、贵金属和助剂之间的协同效应，创造高效稳定的催化系统。

4. 计算机模拟：利用先进的DFT计算和机器学习方法预测CO2-ODHP反应的最佳催化剂组成和结构。

5. 过程强化：探索新型反应器设计和操作条件，以最大化CO2作为软氧化剂的优势，同时最小化副反应和催化剂失活。

6. 可持续催化剂合成：开发环境友好的催化剂制备方法，可能利用生物质衍生碳或废弃物作为载体或活性组分的前体。

7. 机理研究：进行更详细的同位素标记实验和动力学分析，阐明Fe氧化物基催化剂上CO2-ODHP的精确反应路径和速率决定步骤。

这些研究方向旨在解决当前催化剂性能和稳定性的限制，有可能导致更高效、更可持续的丙烯生产过程，使用CO2作为软氧化剂。
