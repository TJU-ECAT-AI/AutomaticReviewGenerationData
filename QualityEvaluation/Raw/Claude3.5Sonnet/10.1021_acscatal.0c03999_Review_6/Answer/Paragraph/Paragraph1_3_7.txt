To optimize catalytic systems for CO2-ODHP reactions, several strategies can be employed:
1. Use of high-quality, low-ash content active carbon supports or reducible oxides like CeO2 to enhance Fe oxide reducibility and CO2 activation.
2. Incorporation of alkali metal promoters or noble metals to enhance catalyst activity and stability.
3. Tailoring the bimetallic composition and metal loading to control the balance between different reaction pathways and minimize deactivation.
4. Designing catalysts that facilitate efficient redox cycling of Fe oxide while maintaining the ability to be reoxidized by CO2.
5. Optimizing the interaction between the active metal oxide and the support material to achieve an optimal balance of reducibility, acidity, and stability.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The complex interplay between Fe oxide, support materials, and promoters in CO2-ODHP catalysts opens up several avenues for future research and development. 2. 引入碱金属助剂或贵金属以提高催化剂活性和稳定性。
3. 调整双金属组成和金属负载，以控制不同反应途径之间的平衡并最小化失活。
4. 设计可以促进Fe氧化物高效氧化还原循环的催化剂，同时保持被CO2重新氧化的能力。
