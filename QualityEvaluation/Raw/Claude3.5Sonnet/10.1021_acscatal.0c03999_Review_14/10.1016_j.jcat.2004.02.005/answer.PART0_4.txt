The Fe-zeolites demonstrate remarkable performance at significantly higher space velocities than typically reported.

However, persistent challenges remain, particularly catalyst deactivation due to coke formation. This issue can be addressed through regeneration in oxygen at 773 K, which completely recovers the initial high activity.
