<English>
Recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants have shown promising results with various catalyst systems and oxidants. This review focuses on the performance of different catalysts, the role of soft oxidants, and the challenges and future directions in this field.

Catalyst Systems and Performance:
1. Transition Metal/Metal Oxides:
   - CrOx/silica catalysts exhibit high activity but suffer from rapid deactivation due to coke deposition and reduction of active Cr3+ to inactive Cr2+ [10.1002/cctc.201200397].
   - VOx catalysts show high C3H6 selectivity (95-98%) but at low conversion rates [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14].
   - Ni-promoted CrOx/SBA-15 catalysts demonstrate improved stability and performance, with 10% higher CO2 conversion compared to non-promoted catalysts [10.1002/cctc.201200397].

2. Main Group Element Metal Oxides:
   - Ga2O3 shows comparable selectivity to CrOx below 40% conversion [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14].
   - Steam-activated Fe-zeolites (MFI and BEA) achieve propylene yields up to 25% at 723 K with N2O as the oxidant [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005].

3. Mixed Oxides and Other Catalysts:
   - Mg/Dy/Li/Cl/O mixed oxides achieve a yield of 40% [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].
   - Pt-H3PMo12O40 catalyst shows high selectivity (94-96%) at 200°C but with low conversion (approximately 20%) [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].
   - Ru/TiO2 catalyst demonstrates 95% selectivity toward propylene at 50% propane conversion with molecular chlorine as the oxidant [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].

Soft Oxidants:
1. CO2: Enhances propane dehydrogenation by increasing equilibrium conversion, maintaining catalyst activity, and inhibiting coke formation [10.1007/s11144-005-0268-4].
2. N2O: Improves propylene selectivity by slowing down catalyst reoxidation and suppressing consecutive oxidation of propene [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010].
3. SO2: Acts as an effective oxidizing agent for hydrocarbon dehydrogenation, with iron oxides supported on activated carbon achieving high propylene yields [10.1007/BF02071147].
4. Molecular Chlorine: Identified as the most advantageous oxidizing agent based on thermodynamic calculations [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].

Persistent Challenges:
1. Balancing selectivity and conversion: Achieving high propylene yields while minimizing the formation of carbon oxides (COx) remains a significant challenge [10.1021/jp070747c].
2. Catalyst stability: Deactivation due to coke formation and reduction of active species is a common issue [10.1002/cctc.201200397].
3. Reaction temperature management: Optimizing reaction temperatures to balance activity and selectivity is crucial [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].
4. Corrosion issues: Potential corrosion problems with halogen-based systems need to be addressed [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].

Future Research Directions:
1. Improving C-H bond activation strategies to enhance catalyst performance.
2. Enhancing catalyst regenerability to address deactivation issues.
3. Optimizing reaction systems to balance selectivity and conversion.
4. Exploring novel promoters and catalyst compositions to improve stability and activity.
5. Investigating combinations of soft oxidants to fine-tune catalyst performance.
6. Developing strategies to maintain optimal catalyst reduction levels during reactions.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The field of ODHP using soft oxidants has shown significant progress in recent years, with various catalyst systems and oxidants demonstrating promising results. The use of soft oxidants, particularly N2O and CO2, has led to improved selectivity and reduced coke formation compared to traditional O2-based systems. However, the persistent challenge of balancing high selectivity with high conversion rates remains a key focus for future research.

Moving forward, the development of advanced catalyst systems that can effectively utilize soft oxidants while maintaining stability and high performance is crucial. This may involve the design of multi-functional catalysts that combine the benefits of different active species or the exploration of novel support materials to enhance catalyst dispersion and stability.

Additionally, the integration of in-situ characterization techniques and advanced computational modeling could provide deeper insights into reaction mechanisms and catalyst behavior under operating conditions. This knowledge could guide the rational design of next-generation catalysts and reaction systems.

Furthermore, the exploration of hybrid processes that combine ODHP with other technologies, such as membrane reactors or plasma-assisted catalysis, could open new avenues for process intensification and improved energy efficiency. As the demand for propylene continues to grow, the development of economically viable and sustainable ODHP processes using soft oxidants will play a crucial role in shaping the future of the petrochemical industry.

<Chinese>
使用软氧化剂的丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）的最新进展显示了各种催化剂系统和氧化剂的有希望结果。本综述重点关注不同催化剂的性能、软氧化剂的作用以及该领域的挑战和未来方向。

催化剂系统和性能：
1. 过渡金属/金属氧化物：
   - CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂表现出高活性，但由于积碳沉积和活性Cr3+还原为非活性Cr2+而迅速失活 [10.1002/cctc.201200397]。
   - VOx催化剂显示高C3H6选择性（95-98%），但转化率低 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]。
   - Ni促进的CrOx/SBA-15催化剂显示出改进的稳定性和性能，CO2转化率比非促进催化剂高10% [10.1002/cctc.201200397]。

2. 主族元素金属氧化物：
   - Ga2O3在40%以下转化率时显示出与CrOx相当的选择性 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]。
   - 蒸汽活化的Fe-沸石（MFI和BEA）在723 K使用N2O作为氧化剂时，丙烯收率可达25% [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005]。

3. 混合氧化物和其他催化剂：
   - Mg/Dy/Li/Cl/O混合氧化物达到40%的收率 [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。
   - Pt-H3PMo12O40催化剂在200°C时显示高选择性（94-96%），但转化率低（约20%）[10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。
   - Ru/TiO2催化剂使用分子氯作为氧化剂时，在50%丙烷转化率下对丙烯的选择性达到95% [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。

软氧化剂：
1. CO2：通过增加平衡转化率、维持催化剂活性和抑制积碳形成来增强丙烷脱氢 [10.1007/s11144-005-0268-4]。
2. N2O：通过减缓催化剂再氧化和抑制丙烯的连续氧化来改善丙烯选择性 [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010]。
3. SO2：作为碳氢化合物脱氢的有效氧化剂，活性炭负载的氧化铁可达到高丙烯收率 [10.1007/BF02071147]。
4. 分子氯：根据热力学计算被确定为最有利的氧化剂 [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。

持续存在的挑战：
1. 平衡选择性和转化率：在实现高丙烯收率的同时最小化碳氧化物（COx）的形成仍是一个重大挑战 [10.1021/jp070747c]。
2. 催化剂稳定性：由于积碳形成和活性物种还原导致的失活是一个常见问题 [10.1002/cctc.201200397]。
3. 反应温度管理：优化反应温度以平衡活性和选择性至关重要 [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。
4. 腐蚀问题：需要解决卤素基系统的潜在腐蚀问题 [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。

未来研究方向：
1. 改进C-H键活化策略以提高催化剂性能。
2. 增强催化剂再生能力以解决失活问题。
3. 优化反应系统以平衡选择性和转化率。
4. 探索新型促进剂和催化剂组成以提高稳定性和活性。
5. 研究软氧化剂的组合以微调催化剂性能。
6. 开发策略以在反应过程中维持最佳催化剂还原水平。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：使用软氧化剂的ODHP领域在近年来取得了显著进展，各种催化剂系统和氧化剂展示了有希望的结果。软氧化剂（特别是N2O和CO2）的使用相比传统的O2基系统导致了选择性的提高和积碳形成的减少。然而，平衡高选择性和高转化率的持续挑战仍然是未来研究的重点。

展望未来，开发能有效利用软氧化剂同时保持稳定性和高性能的先进催化剂系统至关重要。这可能涉及设计结合不同活性物种优点的多功能催化剂，或探索新型载体材料以增强催化剂分散性和稳定性。

此外，原位表征技术和先进计算模型的整合可能为反应机理和催化剂在操作条件下的行为提供更深入的见解。这些知识可以指导下一代催化剂和反应系统的理性设计。

更进一步，探索将ODHP与其他技术（如膜反应器或等离子体辅助催化）相结合的混合工艺，可能为过程强化和提高能源效率开辟新途径。随着丙烯需求的持续增长，开发经济可行和可持续的使用软氧化剂的ODHP工艺将在塑造石油化工行业的未来中发挥关键作用。
