<English>
Recent advances in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) using soft oxidants have shown promising results across various catalyst systems. This review focuses on the performance of different catalysts, persistent challenges, and future research directions in ODHP processes.

Catalyst Types and Performance:

1. Transition Metal/Metal Oxides:
   - CrOx-based catalysts: High C3H6 selectivity at elevated C3H8 conversion [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]. CrOx/silica catalysts show high activity but suffer from rapid deactivation due to coke deposition and CrOx reduction [10.1002/cctc.201200397].
   - VOx-based catalysts: High C3H6 selectivity (95-98%) at low conversion rates [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]. VOx/γ-Al2O3 catalysts show improved performance with well-dispersed vanadia species [10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5].
   - Molybdenum-based catalysts: NiMoO4 catalysts show improved performance with CO2 and N2O as promoters [10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00120-2].

2. Main Group Element Metal Oxides:
   - Fe-zeolites: Steam-activated Fe-zeolites (MFI and BEA) show high efficiency with N2O, achieving propylene yields up to 25% at 723 K [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005].
   - Ga2O3: Shows comparable selectivity to CrOx below 40% conversion [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14].

3. Supported Catalysts:
   - Mesoporous silica-supported catalysts: High surface area materials (up to 1000 m2/g) with dispersed VOx species show propene yields up to 25% [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010].
   - Ni-promoted CrOx/SBA-15: Shows improved stability and performance in CO2-ODHP [10.1002/cctc.201200397].

4. Novel Catalyst Systems:
   - Supported alkali metal chloride melts: Show excellent catalytic performance in ODH of ethane and propane [10.1007/s11244-008-9102-3].
   - Metal sulfide catalysts: S-ZrO2 demonstrates high propylene selectivity (86%) in sulfur-ODHP (SODHP) [10.1039/D0CY01039A].

Soft Oxidants:
Various soft oxidants have been explored, including CO2, N2O, SO2, and halogens. N2O generally improves selectivity but decreases overall activity compared to O2 [10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]. CO2 enhances propane conversion and maintains catalyst activity by inhibiting coke formation [10.1007/s11144-005-0268-4].

Persistent Challenges:
1. Balancing selectivity and conversion: High propylene selectivity often comes at the cost of low conversion rates [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].
2. Catalyst deactivation: Coke formation and active site reduction remain significant issues [10.1002/cctc.201200397].
3. Carbon oxide formation: Minimizing COx production at high propane conversion rates is still a challenge [10.1021/jp070747c].
4. Corrosion: Potential issues with halogen-based systems [10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018].

Future Research Directions:
1. Optimizing catalyst composition and structure: Developing catalysts with well-dispersed active species and improved stability [10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5].
2. Enhancing C-H bond activation: Exploring novel catalyst formulations and reaction conditions to improve propane activation.
3. Improving catalyst regenerability: Developing strategies to address coke formation and active site reduction [10.1002/cctc.201200397].
4. Investigating synergistic effects of multiple soft oxidants: Combining different oxidants to optimize selectivity and conversion [10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00120-2].
5. Exploring novel reaction systems: Investigating alternative reactor designs and process configurations to enhance ODHP performance.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The field of ODHP using soft oxidants has seen significant advancements in recent years, with various catalyst systems and oxidants showing promising results. The integration of novel materials, such as mesoporous supports and metal sulfide catalysts, with carefully selected soft oxidants has opened new avenues for improving selectivity and conversion rates. Moving forward, research efforts should focus on developing multifunctional catalysts that can effectively balance C-H bond activation, selective oxidation, and resistance to deactivation. Additionally, the exploration of hybrid systems combining different soft oxidants and in-situ catalyst regeneration techniques could lead to breakthroughs in ODHP performance. As the demand for propylene continues to grow, the development of more efficient and sustainable ODHP processes using soft oxidants will play a crucial role in meeting future industrial needs while minimizing environmental impact.

<Chinese>
最近使用软氧化剂进行丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）的研究在各种催化剂体系中取得了令人鼓舞的结果。本综述重点关注不同催化剂的性能、持续存在的挑战以及ODHP工艺的未来研究方向。

催化剂类型和性能：

1. 过渡金属/金属氧化物：
   - 基于CrOx的催化剂：在较高的C3H8转化率下具有高C3H6选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]。CrOx/二氧化硅催化剂显示出高活性，但由于焦炭沉积和CrOx还原而导致快速失活[10.1002/cctc.201200397]。
   - 基于VOx的催化剂：在低转化率下具有高C3H6选择性（95-98%）[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]。VOx/γ-Al2O3催化剂在钒物种分散良好时表现更好[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]。
   - 基于钼的催化剂：NiMoO4催化剂在使用CO2和N2O作为助剂时性能提高[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00120-2]。

2. 主族元素金属氧化物：
   - Fe-沸石：蒸汽活化的Fe-沸石（MFI和BEA）在使用N2O时表现出高效率，在723 K下丙烯收率可达25%[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005]。
   - Ga2O3：在40%以下的转化率下与CrOx表现出相当的选择性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_14]。

3. 负载型催化剂：
   - 介孔二氧化硅负载催化剂：具有高比表面积（高达1000 m2/g）和分散的VOx物种的材料显示出高达25%的丙烯收率[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010]。
   - Ni促进的CrOx/SBA-15：在CO2-ODHP中表现出改善的稳定性和性能[10.1002/cctc.201200397]。

4. 新型催化剂系统：
   - 负载的碱金属氯化物熔体：在乙烷和丙烷的ODH中表现出优异的催化性能[10.1007/s11244-008-9102-3]。
   - 金属硫化物催化剂：S-ZrO2在硫-ODHP（SODHP）中表现出高丙烯选择性（86%）[10.1039/D0CY01039A]。

软氧化剂：
研究了各种软氧化剂，包括CO2、N2O、SO2和卤素。与O2相比，N2O通常改善选择性但降低整体活性[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]。CO2通过抑制焦炭形成来增强丙烷转化并维持催化剂活性[10.1007/s11144-005-0268-4]。

持续存在的挑战：
1. 平衡选择性和转化率：高丙烯选择性往往以低转化率为代价[10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。
2. 催化剂失活：焦炭形成和活性位还原仍然是重要问题[10.1002/cctc.201200397]。
3. 碳氧化物形成：在高丙烷转化率下最小化COx生成仍然是一个挑战[10.1021/jp070747c]。
4. 腐蚀：基于卤素系统可能存在潜在问题[10.1016/j.apcata.2015.05.018]。

未来研究方向：
1. 优化催化剂组成和结构：开发具有良好分散活性物种和改善稳定性的催化剂[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]。
2. 增强C-H键活化：探索新型催化剂配方和反应条件以改善丙烷活化。
3. 改善催化剂再生能力：开发策略来解决焦炭形成和活性位还原问题[10.1002/cctc.201200397]。
4. 研究多种软氧化剂的协同效应：结合不同氧化剂以优化选择性和转化率[10.1016/S0920-5861(03)00120-2]。
5. 探索新型反应系统：研究替代反应器设计和工艺配置以增强ODHP性能。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：近年来，使用软氧化剂进行ODHP的领域取得了显著进展，各种催化剂系统和氧化剂都显示出令人鼓舞的结果。将新型材料（如介孔载体和金属硫化物催化剂）与精心选择的软氧化剂相结合，为提高选择性和转化率开辟了新的途径。展望未来，研究工作应集中在开发能够有效平衡C-H键活化、选择性氧化和抗失活能力的多功能催化剂。此外，探索结合不同软氧化剂的混合系统和原位催化剂再生技术可能会带来ODHP性能的突破。随着丙烯需求的持续增长，开发更高效、更可持续的使用软氧化剂的ODHP工艺将在满足未来工业需求的同时最大限度地减少环境影响方面发挥关键作用。
