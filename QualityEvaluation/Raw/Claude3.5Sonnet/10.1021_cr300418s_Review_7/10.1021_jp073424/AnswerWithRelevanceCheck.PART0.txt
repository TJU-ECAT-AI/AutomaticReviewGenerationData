      <English>
          The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions operates as follows:

          1. Oxygen for the oxidation reaction is supplied from the surface of the oxide catalyst.
          2. This creates an oxygen vacancy on the oxide surface.
          3. The reduced oxide is then reoxidized by reacting with oxygen molecules from the gas phase.

          The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv) is significant in determining both the catalytic activity and oxidizing ability of the oxide surface:

          1. ΔEv serves as a good indicator of the oxide's oxidizing power. A lower ΔEv generally indicates a higher oxidizing ability.
          2. The ease of forming oxygen vacancies (lower ΔEv) is a measure of the oxidative power of the oxide in the Mars-van Krevelen mechanism.
          3. Doping can affect ΔEv and thus the oxidative power of the oxide. For example, doping ZnO with Au, Ag, or Cu decreases ΔEv, increasing its oxidative power, while doping with Al or Ga increases ΔEv, decreasing its oxidative power.

          However, it's important to note that a low ΔEv alone does not guarantee good catalytic performance. The oxide must also be able to efficiently reoxidize (i.e., react with gaseous oxygen to refill the vacancies) to complete the catalytic cycle. If this step is too slow, the oxide may be a poor catalyst despite having a low ΔEv. Therefore, while a lower ΔEv generally indicates higher oxidizing ability, the overall catalytic activity depends on the balance between the ease of oxygen vacancy formation and the ability to replenish these vacancies from the gas phase.

          Mars-van Krevelen机制在氧化物催化的氧化反应中的运作如下：

          1. 氧化反应所需的氧直接来自氧化物催化剂的表面。
          2. 这在氧化物表面创造了一个氧空位。
          3. 然后，还原的氧化物通过与气相中的氧分子反应而重新氧化。

          氧空位形成所需的能量（ΔEv）在决定氧化物表面的催化活性和氧化能力方面具有重要意义，原因如下：

          1. ΔEv作为氧化物氧化能力的良好指标。较低的ΔEv通常表示更强的氧化能力。
          2. 形成氧空位的容易程度（较低的ΔEv）是Mars-van Krevelen机制中氧化物氧化能力的衡量标准。
          3. 可以通过掺杂来操纵ΔEv，从而影响氧化物的氧化能力。例如，用Au、Ag或Cu掺杂ZnO会降低ΔEv，增加氧化能力，而用Al或Ga掺杂会增加ΔEv，降低氧化能力。

          然而，需要注意的是，仅仅低ΔEv并不能保证良好的催化性能。氧化物还必须能够有效地重新氧化（即与气态氧反应以填补空位）以完成催化循环。如果这一步骤太慢，尽管ΔEv低，氧化物可能是一种不好的催化剂。因此，虽然较低的ΔEv通常表示较高的氧化能力，但整体催化活性取决于氧空位形成的容易程度与从气相补充这些空位的能力之间的平衡。
