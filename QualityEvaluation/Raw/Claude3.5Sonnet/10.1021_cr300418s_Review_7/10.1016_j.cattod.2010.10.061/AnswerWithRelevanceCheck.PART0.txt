      <English>
          The Mars-van Krevelen mechanism in oxide-catalyzed oxidation reactions operates through the following steps:

1. CO removes an oxygen atom from the oxide surface, forming CO2 and creating an oxygen vacancy defect.
2. The oxygen vacancy is then replenished by gas-phase oxygen.

The energy required for oxygen vacancy formation (ΔEv or Ef) is crucial in determining both the catalytic activity and the oxidizing ability of the oxide surface. The significance of ΔEv lies in its inverse relationship with the oxidation activity of the catalyst. A lower ΔEv indicates:

1. Easier formation of oxygen vacancies
2. Higher concentration of active sites
3. Enhanced oxidation activity

Doping oxide surfaces with certain metals can significantly affect ΔEv. For example:

1. Doping TiO2 with Fe decreases ΔEv from 2.7 eV to 1.24 eV, reducing it by 1.5 eV.
2. Au doping makes the vacancy formation process exothermic.

These modifications of the surface properties can lead to enhanced catalytic activity for oxidation reactions. The lower energy changes involved in vacancy creation, subsequent O2 adsorption, and vacancy healing in doped systems compared to undoped oxides contribute to their improved oxidation activity.

However, it's important to note that while a lower ΔEv generally indicates better catalytic performance, other factors such as the stability of the doped surface and the interaction with reactant molecules also play crucial roles in determining the overall catalytic efficiency.

          在氧化物催化的氧化反应中，Mars-van Krevelen机理通过以下步骤运作：

1. CO从氧化物表面移除一个氧原子，形成CO2并创建氧空位缺陷。
2. 然后由气相氧补充这个氧空位。

氧空位形成所需的能量（ΔEv或Ef）对于决定氧化物表面的催化活性和氧化能力至关重要。ΔEv的重要性在于其与催化剂氧化活性的反比关系。较低的ΔEv表明：

1. 更容易形成氧空位
2. 更高的活性位点浓度
3. 增强的氧化活性

用某些金属掺杂氧化物表面可以显著影响ΔEv。例如：

1. 用Fe掺杂TiO2将ΔEv从2.7 eV降低到1.24 eV，减少了1.5 eV。
2. Au掺杂使空位形成过程变成放热的。

这些表面性质的修改可以导致氧化反应的催化活性增强。与未掺杂氧化物相比，掺杂系统中空位创建、随后的O2吸附和空位愈合所涉及的较低能量变化有助于它们改善的氧化活性。

然而，需要注意的是，虽然较低的ΔEv通常表示更好的催化性能，但其他因素如掺杂表面的稳定性和与反应物分子的相互作用也在决定整体催化效率中起着关键作用。
