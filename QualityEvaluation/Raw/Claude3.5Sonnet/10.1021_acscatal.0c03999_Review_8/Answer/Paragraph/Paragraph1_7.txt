<English>
The use of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) offers significant advantages in terms of propylene selectivity compared to other oxidants, particularly oxygen (O2). The key advantages of using N2O include:

1. Higher propylene selectivity: N2O consistently provides higher propene selectivity compared to O2 at similar propane conversion levels across various catalysts [10.1021/jp070747c]. This improved selectivity is attributed to N2O's lower ability to reoxidize reduced catalyst species, particularly vanadium oxide (VOx) and molybdenum species [10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010].

2. Reduced over-oxidation: N2O inhibits direct propane oxidation and consecutive oxidation of propene to COx, leading to improved selectivity [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025]. This is due to the lower steady-state concentration of active lattice oxygen species when using N2O [10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5].

3. Lower operation temperature: N2O-ODHP can be carried out at 643-773 K, which is significantly lower than the 823-923 K required for mixed metal oxide catalysts with other oxidants [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.10.020].

4. Dual benefits: The reaction allows for the functionalization of propane, a cheap and abundant feedstock, while also utilizing N2O, an environmentally harmful gas [10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035].

Various catalysts have been employed in N2O-ODHP, including:

1. Iron zeolites, particularly steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5, which show remarkable performance with initial propene yields up to 25% [10.1021/ie0493863].
2. Vanadium-based catalysts supported on silica-based materials, such as VOx/SiO2, VOx/MCM-41, and VOx/MCM-48 [10.1021/jp070747c].
3. Molybdenum-based catalysts, including NiMoO4 [10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005].

However, there are critical challenges associated with catalyst deactivation and stability:

1. Coke formation: The main cause of catalyst deactivation, leading to a decrease in propylene yield and propane conversion over time [10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032].
2. Reduction of active species: The limited ability of N2O to reoxidize reduced catalyst species can lead to a gradual loss of active sites over time [10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028].
3. Lower overall activity: The use of N2O results in lower overall catalytic activity compared to O2, with decreased initial rates of product formation [10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5].

To address these limitations and make N2O-ODHP more feasible for practical implementation, several strategies and research directions can be proposed:

1. Catalyst design optimization: Develop catalysts with improved resistance to coke formation and better stability in reduced states [10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005].
2. Regeneration strategies: Optimize regeneration processes to efficiently remove coke and restore catalyst activity [10.1021/ie0493863].
3. Hybrid oxidant systems: Investigate the use of N2O in combination with other oxidants to achieve an optimal balance between activity and selectivity [10.1016/j.cattod.2004.09.043].
4. Process engineering: Explore reactor designs and operating conditions that can maximize the benefits of N2O while minimizing its drawbacks [10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025].
5. Mechanistic studies: Conduct further research to better understand the reaction mechanisms and the role of different oxygen species in the ODHP process [10.1016/j.apcata.2006.11.021].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The use of N2O as an oxidant in ODHP represents a promising approach to enhance propylene selectivity while potentially addressing environmental concerns related to N2O emissions. The unique properties of N2O as a milder oxidant compared to O2 offer opportunities to design more selective catalytic processes. Future research efforts should focus on developing novel catalyst formulations that can maintain high selectivity while improving stability and resistance to deactivation. This may involve exploring bimetallic systems, advanced support materials, or hierarchical pore structures in zeolites to optimize mass transport and coke resistance. Additionally, the integration of N2O-ODHP with other industrial processes that produce N2O as a by-product could lead to more sustainable and economically viable propylene production methods. The development of in-situ characterization techniques and advanced modeling approaches will be crucial in unraveling the complex interplay between N2O, catalyst structure, and reaction mechanisms, potentially leading to breakthroughs in catalyst design and process optimization.

<Chinese>
使用一氧化二氮（N2O）作为丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）的氧化剂与其他氧化剂（特别是氧气O2）相比，在丙烯选择性方面具有显著优势。使用N2O的主要优势包括：

1. 更高的丙烯选择性：在相似的丙烷转化水平下，N2O在各种催化剂上都能提供比O2更高的丙烯选择性[10.1021/jp070747c]。这种改进的选择性归因于N2O较低的重氧化还原催化剂物种的能力，特别是钒氧化物（VOx）和钼物种[10.1016/j.jcat.2010.06.010]。

2. 减少过度氧化：N2O抑制直接丙烷氧化和丙烯连续氧化为COx，从而提高选择性[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025]。这是由于使用N2O时活性晶格氧物种的稳态浓度较低[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]。

3. 较低的操作温度：N2O-ODHP可在643-773 K进行，明显低于使用其他氧化剂的混合金属氧化物催化剂所需的823-923 K[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.10.020]。

4. 双重益处：该反应既可以实现丙烷（一种廉价且丰富的原料）的功能化，又可以利用N2O（一种环境有害气体）[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035]。

N2O-ODHP中使用了各种催化剂，包括：

1. 铁沸石，特别是蒸汽活化的Fe-ZSM-5，表现出显著性能，初始丙烯产率高达25%[10.1021/ie0493863]。
2. 基于硅基材料的钒基催化剂，如VOx/SiO2、VOx/MCM-41和VOx/MCM-48 [10.1021/jp070747c]。
3. 钼基催化剂，包括NiMoO4 [10.1016/j.cattod.2004.03.005]。

然而，催化剂失活和稳定性方面存在关键挑战：

1. 积碳形成：这是催化剂失活的主要原因，导致丙烯产率和丙烷转化率随时间降低[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032]。
2. 活性物种还原：N2O重氧化还原催化剂物种的能力有限，可能导致活性位随时间逐渐损失[10.1016/j.jcat.2015.12.028]。
3. 总体活性较低：使用N2O导致总体催化活性低于O2，产物形成的初始速率降低[10.1016/S0926-860X(01)00836-5]。

为了解决这些限制并使N2O-ODHP更适合实际应用，可以提出几种策略和研究方向：

1. 催化剂设计优化：开发具有改进的抗积碳能力和在还原状态下更好稳定性的催化剂[10.1016/j.jcat.2004.02.005]。
2. 再生策略：优化再生过程以有效去除积碳并恢复催化剂活性[10.1021/ie0493863]。
3. 混合氧化剂系统：研究将N2O与其他氧化剂结合使用，以在活性和选择性之间达到最佳平衡[10.1016/j.cattod.2004.09.043]。
4. 工艺工程：探索能够最大化N2O优势同时最小化其缺点的反应器设计和操作条件[10.1016/j.jcat.2005.05.025]。
5. 机理研究：进行进一步研究以更好地理解反应机理和不同氧物种在ODHP过程中的作用[10.1016/j.apcata.2006.11.021]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：使用N2O作为ODHP的氧化剂代表了一种提高丙烯选择性的有前景方法，同时可能解决与N2O排放相关的环境问题。与O2相比，N2O作为更温和的氧化剂的独特性质为设计更具选择性的催化过程提供了机会。未来的研究工作应集中在开发新型催化剂配方，以在保持高选择性的同时提高稳定性和抗失活能力。这可能涉及探索双金属系统、先进的载体材料或沸石的层次孔结构，以优化质量传输和抗积碳能力。此外，将N2O-ODHP与其他产生N2O作为副产品的工业过程整合，可能导致更可持续和经济可行的丙烯生产方法。原位表征技术和先进建模方法的发展将对揭示N2O、催化剂结构和反应机理之间的复杂相互作用至关重要，可能导致催化剂设计和过程优化的突破。
