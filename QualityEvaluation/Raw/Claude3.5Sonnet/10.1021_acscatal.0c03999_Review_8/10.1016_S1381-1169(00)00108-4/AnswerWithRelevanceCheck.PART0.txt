      <English>
          The use of nitrous oxide (N2O) as an oxidant in the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) shows promising results in terms of selectivity. The interaction of N2O with ZSM-5 type zeolites produces a highly active surface oxygen species called α-oxygen. This α-oxygen demonstrates high selectivity for various hydrocarbon conversions, including propane to propylene.

Advantages in terms of propylene selectivity:
1. High selectivity: 85-95% selectivity for propylene formation from propane at conversions up to 10%.
2. Consistent selectivity: The selectivity remains constant over time.

Catalysts employed:
The main catalyst discussed is ZSM-5 type zeolites, particularly HZSM-5.

Critical challenges:
1. Catalyst deactivation: The activity of the catalyst decreases rapidly over time, even though selectivity remains constant.
2. Low conversion: The high selectivity is reported at relatively low conversions (up to 10%).

Proposed strategies for addressing limitations:
1. Develop more stable catalysts that resist deactivation.
2. Investigate the mechanisms of catalyst deactivation to inform better catalyst design.
3. Explore modifications to ZSM-5 or entirely new catalyst materials that maintain high selectivity while improving stability.
4. Study reaction conditions (temperature, pressure, feed composition) to optimize both selectivity and catalyst longevity.
5. Investigate regeneration methods for deactivated catalysts to extend their useful life.
6. Research ways to increase conversion while maintaining high selectivity.

These strategies could potentially overcome the stability issues and low conversion rates while maintaining the high selectivity advantage of N2O in ODHP, making it a more feasible option for practical implementation.

丙烯选择性方面的优势：
1. 高选择性：在转化率达10%时，丙烷到丙烯的选择性为85-95%。
2. 一致的选择性：选择性随时间保持恒定。

使用的催化剂：
主要讨论的催化剂是ZSM-5型沸石，特别是HZSM-5。

关键挑战：
1. 催化剂失活：催化剂的活性随时间迅速下降，尽管选择性保持不变。
2. 低转化率：高选择性是在相对较低的转化率（最高10%）下报告的。

解决局限性的建议策略：
1. 开发更稳定的、能够抵抗失活的催化剂。
2. 研究催化剂失活机制，为更好的催化剂设计提供信息。
3. 探索ZSM-5的改性或全新的催化剂材料，在保持高选择性的同时提高稳定性。
4. 研究反应条件（温度、压力、进料组成）以优化选择性和催化剂寿命。
5. 研究失活催化剂的再生方法，以延长其使用寿命。
6. 研究在保持高选择性的同时提高转化率的方法。

这些策略可能有助于克服稳定性问题和低转化率，同时保持N2O在ODHP中的高选择性优势，使其成为更可行的实际应用选择。
