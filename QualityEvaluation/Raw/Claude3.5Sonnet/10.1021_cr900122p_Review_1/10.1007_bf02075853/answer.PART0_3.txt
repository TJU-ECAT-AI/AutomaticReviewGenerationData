<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The oxidative dehydrogenation of alkanes (ODH) is a complex process influenced by various factors. V-Mg-O catalysts show activity and selectivity for propane and butane ODH. However, achieving high selectivity and conversion simultaneously is challenging, as selectivity to the desired alkene product (e.g., propylene) typically decreases with increasing alkane conversion.

The reaction mechanism is not fully understood but is thought to involve a redox cycle between V5+ and V4+ species. Catalyst reducibility appears to play a crucial role in determining selectivity, with more easily reduced catalysts showing higher selectivity to the desired alkene product. This suggests that the ability of the catalyst to undergo redox cycles under reaction conditions is favorable for ODH.

The study does not directly discuss site isolation, but it implies that the nature of the active sites and their redox properties are important for controlling selectivity. The addition of various metal oxide promoters (K2O, Cr2O3, Sm2O3, Nb2O5) to V-Mg-O catalysts generally decreased both activity and selectivity, highlighting the sensitivity of the catalyst performance to composition and surface properties.

While the exact mechanism of how catalyst reducibility specifically facilitates the dehydrogenation reaction is not known, it is clear that the interplay between catalyst composition, surface properties, and redox behavior collectively influences the efficiency of the ODH process. Further research is needed to fully elucidate the reaction mechanism and optimize catalyst design for improved performance in alkane ODH.

烷烃的氧化脱氢(ODH)是一个受多种因素影响的复杂过程。V-Mg-O催化剂对丙烷和丁烷的ODH显示出活性和选择性。然而，同时实现高选择性和高转化率是具有挑战性的，因为随着烷烃转化率的增加，目标烯烃产物(如丙烯)的选择性通常会降低。

反应机理尚未完全理解，但被认为涉及V5+和V4+物种之间的氧化还原循环。催化剂的可还原性似乎在决定选择性方面起着关键作用，更容易被还原的催化剂对目标烯烃产物表现出更高的选择性。这表明催化剂在反应条件下进行氧化还原循环的能力有利于ODH。

该研究没有直接讨论位点隔离，但暗示了活性位点的性质及其氧化还原性质对控制选择性很重要。向V-Mg-O催化剂中添加各种金属氧化物助剂(K2O、Cr2O3、Sm2O3、Nb2O5)通常会降低活性和选择性，突显了催化剂性能对组成和表面性质的敏感性。

虽然目前还不清楚催化剂可还原性如何特异性地促进脱氢反应的确切机理，但很明显，催化剂组成、表面性质和氧化还原行为之间的相互作用共同影响了ODH过程的效率。需要进一步的研究来充分阐明反应机理，并优化催化剂设计以提高烷烃ODH的性能。
