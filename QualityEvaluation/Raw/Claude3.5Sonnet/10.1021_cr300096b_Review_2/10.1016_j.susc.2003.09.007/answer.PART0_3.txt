<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The characterization of Ni/Pt bimetallic surfaces is conducted through a comprehensive set of surface science techniques and theoretical calculations. These include:

1. Physical vapor deposition of Ni onto Pt(111) single crystals under different conditions (300K, 600K, with/without annealing).

2. Temperature programmed desorption (TPD) of H2 to study chemical properties.

3. Scanning tunneling microscopy (STM) to investigate surface structure.

4. Low energy ion scattering spectroscopy (LEIS) to analyze surface composition.

5. X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS), including grazing emission XPS, to examine surface and near-surface composition.

6. Auger electron spectroscopy (AES) for compositional analysis.

7. Density functional theory (DFT) calculations to explore the effects of Ni overlayers, subsurface Ni, and surface/bulk alloying on metal-hydrogen binding energy.

This multi-technique approach is crucial because it provides a comprehensive understanding of the bimetallic surfaces' structure, composition, and chemical properties. The combination of experimental techniques and theoretical calculations allows researchers to correlate surface structure and composition with electronic and chemical properties. This comprehensive characterization is essential for accurate structural modeling, informed DFT calculations, and a deeper understanding of how these bimetallic surfaces behave in catalytic processes. By using multiple complementary techniques, researchers can validate their findings and gain insights into the complex relationships between surface structure, composition, and reactivity in these advanced catalytic materials.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

Ni/Pt双金属表面的表征是通过一系列综合的表面科学技术和理论计算来进行的。这些包括：

1. 在不同条件下（300K，600K，有/无退火）通过物理气相沉积将Ni沉积到Pt(111)单晶上。

2. 使用H2的程序升温脱附（TPD）来研究化学性质。

3. 使用扫描隧道显微镜（STM）来研究表面结构。

4. 使用低能离子散射谱（LEIS）来分析表面组成。

5. 使用X射线光电子能谱（XPS），包括掠射发射XPS，来检查表面和近表面组成。

6. 使用俄歇电子能谱（AES）进行成分分析。

7. 使用密度泛函理论（DFT）计算来探索Ni覆盖层、亚表面Ni以及表面/体相合金化对金属-氢结合能的影响。

这种多技术方法至关重要，因为它提供了对双金属表面结构、组成和化学性质的全面理解。实验技术和理论计算的结合使研究人员能够将表面结构和组成与电子和化学性质相关联。这种全面的表征对于准确的结构建模、有依据的DFT计算以及深入理解这些双金属表面在催化过程中的行为至关重要。通过使用多种互补技术，研究人员可以验证他们的发现，并深入了解这些先进催化材料中表面结构、组成和反应活性之间的复杂关系。
