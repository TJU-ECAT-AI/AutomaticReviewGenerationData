<English>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. The active sites can be classified into three types: one-dimensional (1-D), two-dimensional (2-D), and three-dimensional (3-D) dispersed active sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

1-D Edge Active Sites:
1-D active sites, which are linearly dispersed on the edge of 2-D h-BN sheets, have shown superior performance in ODHP reactions. For example, h-BN with 1-D edge active sites exhibited a propene selectivity of 79% at a propane conversion of 14%, outperforming conventional catalysts like V/SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. The edge-hydroxylated boron nitride (BNOH) efficiently catalyzed ODHP, reaching a propylene selectivity of 80.2% with only 0.5% CO2 at a propane conversion of 20.6% [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040].

Reaction Mechanism:
DFT calculations revealed that the reaction starts with hydrogen abstraction from the second carbon of propane, followed by O-O bond cleavage to create B-O and N-O species [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. The edge BOH groups may be dehydrogenated by O2, generating BO and hydroperoxyl (HO2) radicals. The BO radical catalyzes hydrogen abstraction of alkanes to produce alkyl radicals, while the HO2 radical may also be involved in the dehydrogenation in the gas phase [10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028].

Site Activity:
The zig-B edge was found to be more active than the armchair or zig-N edge sites, with propane dehydrogenation occurring more easily on oxygen-modified edge sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. N-terminated edges are particularly effective for oxygen activation, crucial for oxidation reactions [10.1002/smll.201701857].

2-D and 3-D Active Sites:
While 1-D edge sites show promising results, other spatial distributions also play important roles. B2O3/SBA-15 catalysts demonstrated higher activity due to abundant tricoordinated boroxol and hydroxylated linear boron species on the surface, indicating the importance of well-distributed 2-D active sites [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]. The use of honeycomb cordierite structures with straight channels allows for high gas hourly space velocity (GHSV) and improved mass transfer, suggesting the importance of 3-D structural design in catalyst performance [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040].

Performance Improvement Strategies:
Understanding the importance of edge sites has led to strategies for improving catalyst performance, such as:
a) Preoxygen modification of edge sites to reduce the induction period and improve ODHP performance.
b) Increasing the density of edge sites by reducing the size of BN nanosheets and increasing the edge/surface ratio.
c) Creating 1-D shaped materials to increase the number of active sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

Challenges and Future Directions:
Despite the excellent selectivity of olefin products, some drawbacks exist for the h-BN materials with 1-D active sites in the ODHP reactions. These include the existence of an induction period, low density of edge active sites, and stability issues [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. Researchers are working to overcome these challenges and develop more efficient catalysts for commercial applications.

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The study of B-based catalysts for ODHP reactions has revealed the critical role of spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in determining catalytic performance. While 1-D edge active sites have shown promising results, the interplay between 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D structures offers opportunities for further optimization.

Future research directions may include:
1. Developing hybrid catalysts that combine the advantages of different spatial distributions, such as 1-D edge sites with 3-D porous structures for improved mass transfer.
2. Exploring novel synthesis methods to create tailored active site distributions, potentially using advanced techniques like atomic layer deposition or 3D printing.
3. Investigating the dynamic behavior of active sites under reaction conditions to better understand and control catalyst stability and regeneration.
4. Applying machine learning algorithms to predict optimal spatial distributions and compositions for specific reaction conditions.
5. Exploring the potential of doping or creating heterostructures to further enhance the performance of B-based catalysts.

By integrating these approaches, researchers may develop next-generation catalysts that offer unprecedented selectivity, activity, and stability for ODHP reactions, potentially revolutionizing propylene production in the chemical industry.

<Chinese>
B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度对丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的性能有显著影响。活性位点可以分为三种类型：一维(1-D)、二维(2-D)和三维(3-D)分散活性位点[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

一维边缘活性位点：
线性分布在2-D h-BN片边缘的1-D活性位点在ODHP反应中表现出优异的性能。例如，具有1-D边缘活性位点的h-BN在14%的丙烷转化率下展现出79%的丙烯选择性，优于传统的V/SiO2催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。边缘羟基化的氮化硼(BNOH)高效催化ODHP，在20.6%的丙烷转化率下达到80.2%的丙烯选择性，同时CO2生成量仅为0.5%[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]。

反应机理：
DFT计算揭示反应始于丙烷第二个碳原子上的氢抽取，随后O-O键断裂生成B-O和N-O物种[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。边缘BOH基团可能被O2脱氢，生成BO和过氧化氢(HO2)自由基。BO自由基催化烷烃的氢抽取产生烷基自由基，而HO2自由基也可能参与气相中的脱氢[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028]。

位点活性：
锯齿状B边缘比扶手椅型或锯齿状N边缘位点更活跃，丙烷脱氢在氧修饰的边缘位点上更容易发生[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。N端接边缘对氧活化特别有效，这对氧化反应至关重要[10.1002/smll.201701857]。

二维和三维活性位点：
虽然1-D边缘位点显示出有希望的结果，但其他空间分布也扮演着重要角色。B2O3/SBA-15催化剂由于表面上丰富的三配位硼氧环和羟基化线性硼物种而表现出更高的活性，说明了良好分布的2-D活性位点的重要性[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]。使用具有直通道的蜂窝状堇青石结构允许高气体小时空速(GHSV)和改善的质量传递，表明3-D结构设计在催化剂性能中的重要性[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]。

性能改进策略：
理解边缘位点的重要性导致了改进催化剂性能的策略，如：
a) 边缘位点的预氧化修饰，以减少诱导期并改善ODHP性能。
b) 通过减小BN纳米片的尺寸和增加边缘/表面比来增加边缘活性位点的密度。
c) 创建1-D形状的材料以增加活性位点的数量[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

挑战和未来方向：
尽管h-BN材料中1-D活性位点在ODHP反应中表现出优异的烯烃选择性，但仍存在一些缺点。这些包括诱导期的存在、边缘活性位点密度低和稳定性问题[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。研究人员正在努力克服这些挑战，开发更高效的商业应用催化剂。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：对B基催化剂在ODHP反应中的研究揭示了活性位点的空间分布和维度在决定催化性能中的关键作用。虽然1-D边缘活性位点显示出有希望的结果，但1-D、2-D和3-D结构之间的相互作用为进一步优化提供了机会。

未来研究方向可能包括：
1. 开发结合不同空间分布优势的混合催化剂，如具有3-D多孔结构的1-D边缘位点，以改善质量传递。
2. 探索新型合成方法以创建定制的活性位点分布，可能使用原子层沉积或3D打印等先进技术。
3. 研究反应条件下活性位点的动态行为，以更好地理解和控制催化剂的稳定性和再生。
4. 应用机器学习算法预测特定反应条件下的最佳空间分布和组成。
5. 探索掺杂或创建异质结构的潜力，以进一步提高B基催化剂的性能。

通过整合这些方法，研究人员可能开发出下一代催化剂，为ODHP反应提供前所未有的选择性、活性和稳定性，有可能彻底改变化学工业中的丙烯生产。
