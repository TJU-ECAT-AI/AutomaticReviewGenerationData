<English>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. The active sites can be classified into three types: one-dimensional (1-D), two-dimensional (2-D), and three-dimensional (3-D) dispersed active sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

1-D Active Sites:
1-D active sites are primarily located at the edges of 2-D h-BN sheets and boron nitride nanotubes (BNNTs) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. These edge sites, particularly BOH groups, play a crucial role in the catalyst's performance [10.1002/cctc.201700004]. The 1-D edge structure offers unique advantages:

1. High selectivity: h-BN with 1-D edge active sites exhibited a propene selectivity of 79% at a propane conversion of 14%, outperforming conventional catalysts like V/SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

2. Stabilization of intermediates: Unlike 0-D sites (e.g., V/SiO2) that create highly reactive propyl radicals, 1-D edge sites stabilize the propyl radical, leading to improved selectivity [10.126/science.aaf7885].

3. Suppression of overoxidation: The 1-D nature of BN edges prevents overoxidation of adsorbed species, which is typical of 2-D surface catalysts [10.126/science.aaf7885].

4. Oxygen activation: Edge B atoms are involved in O2 activation, forming BOH and BO active sites [10.1002/cctc.201700725].

Reaction mechanism for 1-D edge sites:
1. Hydrogen abstraction from the second carbon of propane initiates the reaction.
2. O-O bond cleavage creates B-O and N-O species to accommodate H and secondary propyl radicals [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].
3. Zig-B edges are more active than armchair or zig-N edge sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

2-D Active Sites:
2-D active sites are found on the surfaces of materials like B2O3/SBA-15 catalysts. These sites offer:

1. Higher activity due to abundant tricoordinated boroxol and hydroxylated linear boron species [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040].
2. Improved mass transfer and accessibility in layered structures [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021].

3-D Active Sites:
3-D active sites are observed in structures like B2O3 clusters on carbon nanofiber surfaces at high calcination temperatures [10.1002/cssc.201402363]. These sites contribute to:

1. Enhanced thermal stability and resistance to oxidation [10.1002/anie.200901826].
2. Improved mass transfer in hierarchical porous structures [10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743].

Understanding these unique properties and reaction mechanisms contributes to the design of more efficient catalysts for commercial applications by:

1. Optimizing edge exposure: Maximizing the number of accessible 1-D edge sites can enhance catalytic performance [10.1002/smll.201701857].
2. Controlling active site formation: Tailoring synthesis conditions to promote the formation of specific active sites (e.g., BOH vs. BO) [10.1002/cctc.201700725].
3. Balancing dimensionality: Combining the benefits of 1-D edge sites with 2-D and 3-D structures to optimize mass transfer and thermal properties [10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028].
4. Enhancing stability: Developing strategies to improve the durability of 1-D edge sites and prevent sintering [10.1002/anie.202106713].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The research on B-based catalysts for ODHP reactions has revealed the critical role of spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in determining catalyst performance. While 1-D edge sites have shown promising results in terms of selectivity and activity, the integration of 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D structures could lead to synergistic effects that enhance overall catalyst performance.

Future research directions may include:

1. Developing novel synthesis methods to create hierarchical structures that combine the benefits of different dimensionalities, such as 1-D edge sites on 3-D porous supports.
2. Investigating the dynamic behavior of active sites under reaction conditions to better understand how the spatial distribution and dimensionality evolve during the catalytic process.
3. Exploring the potential of hybrid materials that incorporate both B-based active sites and other catalytic elements to further improve selectivity and stability.
4. Utilizing advanced characterization techniques and in-situ studies to gain deeper insights into the structure-performance relationships of B-based catalysts with varying spatial distributions and dimensionalities.
5. Developing computational models that can predict the optimal spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites for specific reaction conditions, enabling more rational catalyst design.

By pursuing these research directions, it may be possible to develop next-generation B-based catalysts that offer superior performance in ODHP reactions, potentially revolutionizing propylene production in commercial applications.

<Chinese>
B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度对丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）反应的性能有显著影响。活性位点可分为三类：一维（1-D）、二维（2-D）和三维（3-D）分散活性位点[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

1-D活性位点：
1-D活性位点主要位于2-D h-BN片和氮化硼纳米管（BNNTs）的边缘[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。这些边缘位点，特别是BOH基团，在催化剂性能中起着至关重要的作用[10.1002/cctc.201700004]。1-D边缘结构具有独特的优势：

1. 高选择性：具有1-D边缘活性位点的h-BN在14%的丙烷转化率下表现出79%的丙烯选择性，优于传统的V/SiO2催化剂[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

2. 中间体稳定：与创建高活性丙基自由基的0-D位点（如V/SiO2）不同，1-D边缘位点能稳定丙基自由基，从而提高选择性[10.126/science.aaf7885]。

3. 抑制过度氧化：BN边缘的1-D特性可防止吸附物种的过度氧化，这是2-D表面催化剂的典型特征[10.126/science.aaf7885]。

4. 氧气活化：边缘B原子参与O2活化，形成BOH和BO活性位点[10.1002/cctc.201700725]。

1-D边缘位点的反应机理：
1. 反应始于丙烷第二个碳原子的氢抽提。
2. O-O键断裂产生B-O和N-O物种，以容纳H和仲丙基自由基[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。
3. 锯齿形B边缘比扶手椅型或锯齿形N边缘位点更活跃[10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

2-D活性位点：
2-D活性位点存在于B2O3/SBA-15等材料的表面。这些位点提供：

1. 由于丰富的三配位硼氧环和羟基化线性硼物种，活性更高[10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]。
2. 在层状结构中改善了质量传递和可及性[10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021]。

3-D活性位点：
3-D活性位点在高温煅烧时观察到，如碳纳米纤维表面的B2O3簇[10.1002/cssc.201402363]。这些位点贡献于：

1. 提高热稳定性和抗氧化性[10.1002/anie.200901826]。
2. 在分层多孔结构中改善质量传递[10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743]。

理解这些独特的性质和反应机理有助于设计更高效的商业应用催化剂：

1. 优化边缘暴露：最大化可及的1-D边缘位点数量可以提高催化性能[10.1002/smll.201701857]。
2. 控制活性位点形成：调整合成条件以促进特定活性位点（如BOH vs. BO）的形成[10.1002/cctc.201700725]。
3. 平衡维度：结合1-D边缘位点与2-D和3-D结构的优势，以优化质量传递和热性能[10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028]。
4. 提高稳定性：开发策略以改善1-D边缘位点的耐久性并防止烧结[10.1002/anie.202106713]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：针对ODHP反应的B基催化剂研究揭示了活性位点的空间分布和维度在决定催化剂性能方面的关键作用。虽然1-D边缘位点在选择性和活性方面表现出令人鼓舞的结果，但整合1-D、2-D和3-D结构可能会产生协同效应，从而提高整体催化剂性能。

未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 开发新型合成方法，创建结合不同维度优势的层次结构，如3-D多孔支撑体上的1-D边缘位点。
2. 研究反应条件下活性位点的动态行为，以更好地理解空间分布和维度在催化过程中的演变。
3. 探索同时包含B基活性位点和其他催化元素的混合材料的潜力，以进一步提高选择性和稳定性。
4. 利用先进的表征技术和原位研究，深入了解具有不同空间分布和维度的B基催化剂的结构-性能关系。
5. 开发计算模型，预测特定反应条件下活性位点的最佳空间分布和维度，实现更合理的催化剂设计。

通过追求这些研究方向，可能开发出在ODHP反应中性能卓越的新一代B基催化剂，有潜力革新商业应用中的丙烯生产。
