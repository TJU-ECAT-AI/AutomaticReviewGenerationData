<English>
The spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites in B-based catalysts significantly influence their performance in oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions. The active sites can be classified into three types: one-dimensional (1-D), two-dimensional (2-D), and three-dimensional (3-D) dispersed active sites [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

1-D Active Sites:
1-D active sites, typically found on the edges of 2-D h-BN sheets and in boron nitride nanotubes (BNNTs), have shown superior performance in ODHP reactions [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]. These edge sites, particularly when hydroxylated (BNOH), demonstrate high propylene selectivity and catalytic stability [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]. The 1-D nature of these sites offers unique advantages:

1. Stabilization of intermediates: Unlike 0-D sites (e.g., V/SiO2) that create highly reactive propyl radicals, 1-D edge sites stabilize these intermediates, reducing overoxidation [10.1126/science.aaf7885].
2. Controlled reactivity: The linear structure limits the diffusion of adsorbates, preventing overoxidation typical of 2-D surface catalysts [10.1126/science.aaf7885].
3. Specific active structures: Oxygen-terminated armchair boron nitride edges (>BOON<) act as highly effective active sites for ODHP reactions [10.1126/science.aaf7885].

2-D Active Sites:
2-D active sites are typically found on the surfaces of layered boron-containing materials. These sites offer:

1. High surface area: 2-D materials like h-BN nanosheets provide large surface areas for catalytic reactions [10.1002/cctc.201700725].
2. Tunable surface chemistry: The surface of 2-D materials can be functionalized to optimize catalytic performance [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017].
3. Efficient mass transfer: The planar structure of 2-D materials facilitates the movement of reactants and products [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021].

3-D Active Sites:
3-D active sites are found in bulk boron-containing materials and offer:

1. High thermal stability: 3-D structures often exhibit better thermal stability, crucial for high-temperature ODHP reactions [10.1002/anie.200901826].
2. Multifunctional catalytic sites: 3-D structures can incorporate various types of active sites within a single catalyst particle [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031].
3. Enhanced porosity: 3-D structures often feature hierarchical porosity, improving mass transfer and accessibility of active sites [10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743].

The performance of these catalysts is significantly influenced by the nature and distribution of their active sites. For instance, h-BN with 1-D edge active sites exhibited a propene selectivity of 79% at a propane conversion of 14%, outperforming conventional catalysts like V/SiO2 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1].

Understanding the unique properties and reaction mechanisms of these sites, particularly 1-D edge active sites, contributes to the design of more efficient catalysts for commercial applications in several ways:

1. Optimizing edge exposure: Maximizing the number of accessible edge sites can enhance catalytic performance [10.1002/smll.201701857].
2. Tailoring surface chemistry: Modifying edge termination and functionalization can optimize catalytic properties [10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62786-4].
3. Controlling active site density: Balancing the concentration of active sites with stability and accessibility is crucial for long-term performance [10.1002/anie.201914696].
4. Enhancing mass transfer: Designing catalyst structures that facilitate efficient movement of reactants and products can improve overall performance [10.1016/S1872-2067(21)63809-3].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The field of B-based catalysts for ODHP reactions has seen significant advancements in recent years, with a growing understanding of the importance of spatial distribution and dimensionality of active sites. Moving forward, research efforts could focus on:

1. Developing novel synthesis methods to create catalysts with precisely controlled distributions of 1-D, 2-D, and 3-D active sites.
2. Exploring hybrid catalysts that combine the advantages of different dimensionalities, such as 1-D edge sites on 3-D porous structures.
3. Investigating the dynamic behavior of active sites under reaction conditions to design more stable and efficient catalysts.
4. Utilizing advanced characterization techniques and in-situ studies to gain deeper insights into the structure-activity relationships of B-based catalysts.
5. Exploring the potential of machine learning and high-throughput screening to accelerate the discovery and optimization of new B-based catalyst compositions and structures.

These research directions could lead to the development of next-generation B-based catalysts with improved activity, selectivity, and stability for commercial ODHP applications, potentially revolutionizing propylene production technologies.

<Chinese>
B基催化剂中活性位点的空间分布和维度（1-D、2-D和3-D）对其在丙烷氧化脱氢（ODHP）反应中的性能有显著影响。活性位点可以分为三类：一维（1-D）、二维（2-D）和三维（3-D）分散活性位点 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

1-D活性位点：
1-D活性位点通常位于2-D六方氮化硼（h-BN）片的边缘和氮化硼纳米管（BNNTs）中，在ODHP反应中表现出优异的性能 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。这些边缘位点，特别是羟基化后（BNOH），表现出高丙烯选择性和催化稳定性 [10.1016/j.cjche.2020.07.040]。1-D位点的特性提供了独特的优势：

1. 中间体稳定：与0-D位点（如V/SiO2）不同，1-D边缘位点可以稳定丙基自由基中间体，减少过度氧化 [10.1126/science.aaf7885]。
2. 受控反应性：线性结构限制了吸附物的扩散，防止了典型的2-D表面催化剂的过度氧化 [10.1126/science.aaf7885]。
3. 特定活性结构：氧化端基的扶手椅型氮化硼边缘（>BOON<）作为ODHP反应的高效活性位点 [10.1126/science.aaf7885]。

2-D活性位点：
2-D活性位点通常位于层状含硼材料的表面。这些位点提供：

1. 高比表面积：如h-BN纳米片等2-D材料为催化反应提供了大的表面积 [10.1002/cctc.201700725]。
2. 可调表面化学：2-D材料的表面可以进行功能化以优化催化性能 [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017]。
3. 高效质量传递：2-D材料的平面结构有利于反应物和产物的移动 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021]。

3-D活性位点：
3-D活性位点存在于体相含硼材料中，提供：

1. 高热稳定性：3-D结构通常表现出更好的热稳定性，这对高温ODHP反应至关重要 [10.1002/anie.200901826]。
2. 多功能催化位点：3-D结构可以在单个催化剂颗粒中包含各种类型的活性位点 [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.11.031]。
3. 增强的孔隙度：3-D结构通常具有层次孔隙度，改善了质量传递和活性位点的可及性 [10.1016/j.apsusc.2023.156743]。

这些催化剂的性能受其活性位点的性质和分布的显著影响。例如，具有1-D边缘活性位点的h-BN在14%的丙烷转化率下表现出79%的丙烯选择性，优于传统的V/SiO2催化剂 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_1]。

理解这些位点的独特性质和反应机理，特别是1-D边缘活性位点，有助于设计更高效的商业应用催化剂：

1. 优化边缘暴露：最大化可及边缘位点的数量可以增强催化性能 [10.1002/smll.201701857]。
2. 定制表面化学：修饰边缘端基和功能化可以优化催化性能 [10.1016/S1872-2067(17)62786-4]。
3. 控制活性位点密度：平衡活性位点浓度与稳定性和可及性对长期性能至关重要 [10.1002/anie.201914696]。
4. 增强质量传递：设计有利于反应物和产物高效移动的催化剂结构可以提高整体性能 [10.1016/S1872-2067(21)63809-3]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：近年来，ODHP反应的B基催化剂领域取得了重大进展，对活性位点的空间分布和维度重要性的理解不断深入。展望未来，研究努力可以集中在：

1. 开发新型合成方法，创造具有精确控制的1-D、2-D和3-D活性位点分布的催化剂。
2. 探索结合不同维度优势的混合催化剂，如在3-D多孔结构上的1-D边缘位点。
3. 研究反应条件下活性位点的动态行为，以设计更稳定和高效的催化剂。
4. 利用先进的表征技术和原位研究，深入了解B基催化剂的结构-活性关系。
5. 探索机器学习和高通量筛选的潜力，加速新型B基催化剂组成和结构的发现和优化。

这些研究方向可能导致下一代B基催化剂的开发，具有更高的活性、选择性和稳定性，适用于商业ODHP应用，可能彻底改变丙烯生产技术。
