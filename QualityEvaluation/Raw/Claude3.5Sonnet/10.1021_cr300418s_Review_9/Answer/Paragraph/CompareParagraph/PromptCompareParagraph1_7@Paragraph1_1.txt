2. For Zr-doped NiO, the best performance was achieved with 10% NiZrO catalyst, yielding 60% ethane conversion and 66% ethylene selectivity [10.1016/j.apsusc.2012.01.120].

2. Dopants modify the quantity and mobility of exchanged oxygen species in NiO [10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004].
3. Higher valence dopants create more positive charge in the NiO lattice, resulting in fewer oxygen species and higher ethylene selectivity [10.1016/j.jcat.2009.12.004].

3. The formation of NiNb solid solutions alters the electronic structure and oxygen binding properties [10.1016/j.apcata.2007.02.030]

Catalytic Performance:
