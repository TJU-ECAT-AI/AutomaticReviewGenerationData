Comparative analysis of bimetallic catalysts' performance, mechanisms, and characteristics in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions with propane: An experimental and theoretical study focusing on Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2

The bimetallic catalysts Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 have emerged as promising systems for CO2-ODH (oxidative dehydrogenation) and DRP (dry reforming) of propane, respectively. These catalysts exhibit distinct compositions, selectivities, stabilities, and reaction mechanisms, which have been elucidated through a combination of experimental and theoretical studies.

Composition and Structure:
Fe3Ni1/CeO2 consists of metallic Ni and oxidized Fe supported on CeO2, while Ni3Pt1/CeO2 contains metallic Ni and Pt on the same support [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]. In situ XANES measurements confirmed that under reaction conditions, Fe in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exists in oxidized forms, whereas both catalysts contain metallic Ni (Ni0) [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3]. The presence of oxidized Fe in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 plays a crucial role in its catalytic performance.

Selectivity and Activity:
Fe3Ni1/CeO2 demonstrates high selectivity (58.2%) towards propylene production via the CO2-ODHP pathway [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]. In contrast, Ni3Pt1/CeO2 primarily performs DRP with high selectivity (88-96%) towards CO production [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w] [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3]. Ni3Pt1/CeO2 also exhibits higher CO2 conversion (39%) compared to Fe3Ni1/CeO2 [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w].

Stability:
Ni3Pt1/CeO2 demonstrates superior stability compared to monometallic Ni3 and other precious metal bimetallic catalysts with Fe3 and Co3 [10.1002/aic.16670]. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 also shows good stability, maintaining steady-state performance in CO2-ODH reactions [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3].

Reaction Mechanisms:
DFT studies reveal that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CH bond cleavage, promoting the CO2-ODHP pathway. On bulk-terminated Fe3Ni(111), the pathway for oxidative CH bond cleavage has a lower energy than CC bond scission [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]. Conversely, Ni3Pt1/CeO2 prefers CC bond cleavage, facilitating the DRP pathway. On Pt-terminated Ni3Pt(111), the CC bond cleavage pathway has a lower energy than CH bond breaking [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w].

Synergistic Effects:
The bimetallic Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalyst exhibits a synergistic effect, with higher propylene yields (1.6%) compared to its monometallic counterparts (Ni: 0.4%, Fe: 0.2%) [10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]. This synergy is attributed to the unique interaction between metallic Ni and oxidized Fe species.

Theoretical Insights:
DFT calculations reveal that in the presence of oxygen (from CO2 dissociation), Fe in Fe3Ni1/CeO2 segregates to the surface due to stronger Fe-O bonding compared to Ni-O [10.1002/aic.16670]. This surface segregation of oxidized Fe is key to promoting the CO2-ODHP pathway. For Ni3Pt1/CeO2, the higher Ni content likely promotes the formation of metallic Ni particles, which favors the DRP reaction.

Environmental Implications:
Both catalysts offer promising routes for CO2 utilization, addressing global concerns of alkene production and CO2 emissions reduction. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 demonstrates potential for CO2-ODH reactions, contributing to the production of valuable alkenes while consuming CO2. Ni3Pt1/CeO2, on the other hand, provides an efficient pathway for syngas production through DRP.

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:
The comparative study of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 and Ni3Pt1/CeO2 catalysts offers valuable insights into the design of efficient bimetallic systems for CO2 utilization in propane conversion. The distinct performances of these catalysts in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions highlight the importance of metal combination, oxidation states, and surface structure in determining catalytic behavior.

Future research directions may include:

1. Exploring novel bimetallic combinations and support materials to further enhance selectivity and stability in CO2-ODH and DRP reactions.
2. Investigating the potential of these catalysts for other alkane substrates, expanding their applicability in the petrochemical industry.
3. Developing in-operando characterization techniques to gain deeper insights into the dynamic nature of catalyst surfaces under reaction conditions.
4. Integrating machine learning approaches with DFT calculations to accelerate the discovery of optimal catalyst compositions and structures.
5. Scaling up these catalytic systems for industrial applications, addressing challenges in catalyst synthesis, reactor design, and process optimization.

By building upon the fundamental understanding gained from these studies, researchers can work towards developing more efficient and sustainable catalytic processes for CO2 utilization and valuable chemical production, contributing to the broader goals of carbon neutrality and circular economy in the chemical industry.

比较分析双金属催化剂在丙烷的CO2-ODH和DRP反应中的性能、机理和特性：以Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2为重点的实验和理论研究

双金属催化剂Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2分别在丙烷的CO2-ODH（氧化脱氢）和DRP（干重整）反应中表现出良好的前景。这些催化剂具有不同的组成、选择性、稳定性和反应机理，通过实验和理论研究的结合已经得到阐明。

组成和结构：
Fe3Ni1/CeO2由金属Ni和氧化Fe负载在CeO2上组成，而Ni3Pt1/CeO2则包含金属Ni和Pt负载在相同的载体上[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。原位XANES测量证实，在反应条件下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2中的Fe以氧化态存在，而两种催化剂都含有金属Ni（Ni0）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2中氧化Fe的存在对其催化性能起着至关重要的作用。

选择性和活性：
Fe3Ni1/CeO2通过CO2-ODHP途径展现出对丙烯生产的高选择性（58.2%）[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。相比之下，Ni3Pt1/CeO2主要进行DRP反应，对CO的选择性高达88-96%[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w] [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3]。Ni3Pt1/CeO2还表现出较高的CO2转化率（39%），相比Fe3Ni1/CeO2更高[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。

稳定性：
Ni3Pt1/CeO2相比单金属Ni3和其他含Fe3和Co3的贵金属双金属催化剂表现出更优越的稳定性[10.1002/aic.16670]。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH反应中也表现出良好的稳定性，能够保持稳态性能[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_3]。

反应机理：
DFT研究表明，Fe3Ni1/CeO2倾向于CH键断裂，促进CO2-ODHP途径。在体相终止的Fe3Ni(111)表面上，氧化性CH键断裂的能量路径低于CC键断裂[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。相反，Ni3Pt1/CeO2更倾向于CC键断裂，有利于DRP途径。在Pt终止的Ni3Pt(111)表面上，CC键断裂的能量路径低于CH键断裂[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。

协同效应：
双金属Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂表现出协同效应，其丙烯产率（1.6%）高于相应的单金属催化剂（Ni：0.4%，Fe：0.2%）[10.1038/s41467-018-03793-w]。这种协同作用归因于金属Ni和氧化Fe物种之间的独特相互作用。

理论洞见：
DFT计算表明，在存在氧（来自CO2解离）的情况下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2中的Fe会因Fe-O键比Ni-O键更强而偏析到表面[10.1002/aic.16670]。这种氧化Fe的表面偏析对促进CO2-ODHP途径至关重要。对于Ni3Pt1/CeO2，较高的Ni含量可能促进金属Ni颗粒的形成，有利于DRP反应。

环境影响：
这两种催化剂为CO2利用提供了有前景的途径，解决了全球烯烃生产和CO2减排的问题。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在CO2-ODH反应中展现出潜力，有助于在消耗CO2的同时生产有价值的烯烃。而Ni3Pt1/CeO2则通过DRP提供了合成气生产的高效途径。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：
对Fe3Ni1/CeO2和Ni3Pt1/CeO2催化剂的比较研究为设计高效的双金属系统以用于丙烷转化中的CO2利用提供了宝贵的见解。这些催化剂在CO2-ODH和DRP反应中的不同表现突出了金属组合、氧化态和表面结构在决定催化行为中的重要性。

未来的研究方向可能包括：

1. 探索新型双金属组合和载体材料，以进一步提高CO2-ODH和DRP反应的选择性和稳定性。
2. 研究这些催化剂对其他烷烃底物的潜力，扩大它们在石油化工行业的应用范围。
3. 开发原位表征技术，以深入了解催化剂表面在反应条件下的动态性质。
4. 将机器学习方法与DFT计算相结合，加速发现最佳催化剂组成和结构。
5. 将这些催化系统扩大到工业应用，解决催化剂合成、反应器设计和工艺优化方面的挑战。

通过建立在这些研究所获得的基础理解之上，研究人员可以致力于开发更高效、更可持续的催化过程，用于CO2利用和有价值化学品的生产，为化工行业更广泛的碳中和和循环经济目标做出贡献。
