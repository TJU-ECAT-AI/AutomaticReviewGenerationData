Halogen-assisted methods for selectively dehydrogenating propane to propene include:

1. Dehydrogenation with molecular halogens (X2): This method uses halogens directly to remove hydrogen from propane. For example, using Cl2 with a 0.28% Ru/TiO2 catalyst at 400°C achieves 50% propane conversion with 95% propene selectivity.

2. Oxidative dehydrogenation in the presence of halogens (X2 + O2): This approach combines halogens with oxygen to facilitate the dehydrogenation process.

3. Propane oxyhalogenation via halides (HX + O2): This method uses hydrogen halides and oxygen to convert propane to propene. The reaction proceeds through the oxidation of hydrogen halide with O2, producing alkyl halide, alkene, and water.

4. Molten metal halide salt-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation (LiX + O2): This approach uses metal iodides like LiI, CdI2, ZnI2, and PbI2 in a molten salt form. The reaction occurs between 450-600°C and can achieve propene yields up to 70%.

The reaction conditions vary for each method:
- Temperatures generally range from 300-700°C
- Catalysts used include Ru/TiO2 for halo-dehydrogenation and various metal iodides for molten salt approaches
- Reactants differ based on the specific method (e.g., Cl2, HX + O2, or metal iodides + O2)

Propane's reactivity in these processes differs significantly from methane and ethane due to differences in the alkanes' structures and bond strengths. Propane has both primary and secondary carbon atoms, making it more reactive than methane or ethane, which only have primary carbons. This structural difference affects the ease of C-H bond activation and the stability of intermediates formed during the dehydrogenation process, leading to different reaction pathways and product distributions compared to shorter chain alkanes.

卤素辅助选择性脱氢丙烷制丙烯的方法包括：

1. 分子卤素脱氢（X2）：这种方法直接使用卤素从丙烷中移除氢。例如，在400°C下使用0.28% Ru/TiO2催化剂和Cl2可以实现50%的丙烷转化率和95%的丙烯选择性。

2. 卤素存在下的氧化脱氢（X2 + O2）：这种方法将卤素与氧气结合，以促进脱氢过程。

3. 通过卤化物的丙烷氧卤化（HX + O2）：这种方法使用氢卤化物和氧气将丙烷转化为丙烯。反应通过氢卤化物与O2的氧化进行，生成烷基卤化物、烯烃和水。

4. 熔融金属卤化物盐辅助氧化脱氢（LiX + O2）：这种方法使用LiI、CdI2、ZnI2和PbI2等金属碘化物的熔融盐形式。反应在450-600°C之间进行，可以达到高达70%的丙烯产率。

每种方法的反应条件各不相同：
- 温度通常在300-700°C范围内
- 使用的催化剂包括用于卤素脱氢的Ru/TiO2和用于熔融盐方法的各种金属碘化物
- 反应物根据具体方法而不同（例如，Cl2、HX + O2或金属碘化物 + O2）

丙烷在这些过程中的反应性与甲烷和乙烷显著不同，这是由于烷烃结构和键强度的差异。丙烷同时具有伯碳和仲碳原子，使其比只有伯碳的甲烷或乙烷更具反应性。这种结构差异影响了C-H键活化的难易程度和脱氢过程中形成的中间体的稳定性，导致与短链烷烃相比，反应途径和产物分布不同。
