Noble metal-based catalysts (particularly platinum) and metal oxide-based catalysts have distinct characteristics in the nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins:

Composition:
- Noble metal catalysts: Primarily platinum-based, often modified or supported on alumina.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Include chromia-alumina, gallium oxide, chromium oxide, vanadium oxide, zinc oxide, and molybdenum oxide.

Effectiveness:
- Platinum catalysts: High activity and selectivity for dehydrogenation of paraffins to olefins, especially for long-chain linear paraffins.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Varying levels of activity, with gallium oxide showing the highest activity among metal oxides for ethane dehydrogenation.

Industrial applications:
- Platinum catalysts: Widely used in industrial processes like UOP's Pacol and Oleflex processes for producing linear olefins and light olefins (propylene, isobutylene).
- Metal oxide catalysts: Chromia-alumina catalysts have been used in older processes like Catofin. Other metal oxides are still in research stages for potential industrial applications.

Recent developments:
- Platinum catalysts: Focus on catalyst modifications to improve selectivity and stability.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Research on enhancing activity and selectivity through various treatments, such as sulfating silica supports.

Potential advantages:
- Platinum catalysts: High activity, selectivity, and stability, especially for long-chain paraffins. Well-established in industrial processes.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Potentially lower cost compared to noble metals. Some show promise for specific applications, like ethane dehydrogenation with CO2 as a mild oxidant.

Overall, while platinum-based catalysts dominate industrial applications due to their superior performance, research continues on metal oxide catalysts to find more cost-effective and equally efficient alternatives for specific dehydrogenation processes.

贵金属基催化剂（特别是铂）和金属氧化物基催化剂在轻烯烃的非氧化脱氢中具有不同的特征：

成分：
- 贵金属催化剂：主要是铂基，通常经过改性或负载在氧化铝上。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：包括氧化铬-氧化铝、氧化镓、氧化铬、氧化钒、氧化锌和氧化钼。

有效性：
- 铂催化剂：对烷烃脱氢生成烯烃具有高活性和选择性，特别是对长链直链烷烃。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：活性水平不同，其中氧化镓在乙烷脱氢中显示出最高的活性。

工业应用：
- 铂催化剂：广泛应用于工业过程，如UOP的Pacol和Oleflex工艺，用于生产直链烯烃和轻质烯烃（丙烯、异丁烯）。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：氧化铬-氧化铝催化剂曾用于较早的Catofin等工艺。其他金属氧化物仍处于研究阶段，有望用于工业应用。

最新发展：
- 铂催化剂：关注催化剂改性以提高选择性和稳定性。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：研究通过各种处理方法提高活性和选择性，如硫酸化硅胶载体。

潜在优势：
- 铂催化剂：高活性、选择性和稳定性，特别适用于长链烷烃。在工业过程中已经得到广泛应用。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：与贵金属相比，潜在成本较低。一些催化剂在特定应用中显示出潜力，如使用CO2作为温和氧化剂的乙烷脱氢。

总的来说，由于铂基催化剂性能优越，在工业应用中占主导地位，但研究人员仍在继续研究金属氧化物催化剂，以寻找更具成本效益且同样高效的替代品，用于特定的脱氢过程。
