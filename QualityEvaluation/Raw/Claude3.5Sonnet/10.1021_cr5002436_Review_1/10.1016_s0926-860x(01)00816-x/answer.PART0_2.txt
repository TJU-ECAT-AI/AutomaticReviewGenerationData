Noble metal-based catalysts, particularly platinum, and metal oxide-based catalysts have different compositions and applications in the nonoxidative dehydrogenation of light olefins:

Composition:
- Noble metal catalysts: Primarily platinum-based, often modified or supported on alumina.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Include chromia-alumina, gallium oxide, chromium oxide, vanadium oxide, zinc oxide, and molybdenum oxide.

Effectiveness:
- Platinum catalysts: High activity and stability, selective dehydrogenation with minimum cracking.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Varying effectiveness, with some showing high selectivity (e.g., vanadia-silica-zirconia for propylene production).

Industrial applications:
- Platinum catalysts: Used for long-chain linear olefins production, especially in biodegradable detergent manufacturing.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Used in various light olefin dehydrogenation processes, including ethane and propane dehydrogenation.

Recent developments:
- Platinum catalysts: Modifications to improve selectivity and stability.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Development of complex mixed oxide systems (e.g., vanadia-silica-zirconia) for improved performance.

Potential advantages:
- Platinum catalysts: High activity, stability, and selectivity for long-chain paraffins.
- Metal oxide catalysts: Lower cost, potential for high selectivity in specific applications, and ability to be tailored for different feedstocks.

Overall, both types of catalysts have their place in the industry, with platinum catalysts excelling in long-chain olefin production and metal oxide catalysts showing promise in light olefin dehydrogenation, particularly when optimized for specific reactions.

贵金属基催化剂（尤其是铂）和金属氧化物基催化剂在轻质烯烃的非氧化脱氢中有不同的组成和应用：

组成：
- 贵金属催化剂：主要是铂基，通常经过改性或负载在氧化铝上。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：包括铬-氧化铝、氧化镓、氧化铬、氧化钒、氧化锌和氧化钼。

有效性：
- 铂催化剂：高活性和稳定性，选择性脱氢且裂解最小化。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：有效性不同，有些显示高选择性（例如，用于丙烯生产的钒-硅-锆催化剂）。

工业应用：
- 铂催化剂：用于长链线性烯烃生产，特别是在生物可降解洗涤剂制造中。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：用于各种轻质烯烃脱氢过程，包括乙烷和丙烷脱氢。

最新发展：
- 铂催化剂：改进以提高选择性和稳定性。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：开发复杂的混合氧化物系统（如钒-硅-锆）以提高性能。

潜在优势：
- 铂催化剂：对长链烷烃具有高活性、稳定性和选择性。
- 金属氧化物催化剂：成本较低，在特定应用中具有高选择性的潜力，能够针对不同原料进行定制。

总的来说，这两类催化剂在工业中都有其用途，铂催化剂在长链烯烃生产中表现出色，而金属氧化物催化剂在轻质烯烃脱氢中显示出前景，特别是在针对特定反应进行优化时。
