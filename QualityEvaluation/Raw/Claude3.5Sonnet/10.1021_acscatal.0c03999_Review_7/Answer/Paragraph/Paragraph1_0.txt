The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) is influenced by several key factors, including catalyst reducibility, support materials, and bimetallic composition. Among the various Fe oxide-based catalysts studied, Fe2O3/AC (iron oxide supported on activated carbon) has shown superior performance in both C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7].

The reducibility of the catalyst plays a crucial role in its catalytic activity. Fe2O3/AC exhibits improved reducibility compared to other Fe oxide-based catalysts, with its reduction proceeding through Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. This enhanced reducibility is key to the high catalytic performance of Fe2O3/AC compared to other tested catalysts.

The support material significantly influences the catalyst's performance. Activated carbon (AC) as a support enhances the reducibility of Fe2O3, which contributes to its superior activity [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. Other supports like γ-Al2O3 and MgO can hinder the reducibility of Fe oxide due to strong interactions between the Fe oxide and the support material. Additionally, CeO2 has been identified as a promising support for Fe-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP due to its ability to activate CO2 through direct C=O bond scission [10.1039/D0CY00586J].

The proposed reaction mechanism for CO2-ODHP over Fe oxide-based catalysts involves a redox cycle. The process consists of two main steps: (1) C3H8 oxidation to C3H6 using lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4, and (2) reoxidation of reduced Fe oxide by CO2, which acts as a soft oxidant [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. This redox cycle allows for the continuous regeneration of the active sites on the catalyst surface, maintaining its activity throughout the reaction.

The presence of oxidized Fe under reaction conditions is crucial for favoring oxidative C-H bond cleavage in the CO2-ODHP pathway [10.1002/aic.16670]. The extent of Fe oxidation plays a significant role in catalyst performance, with higher oxidation of Fe correlating with better activity and stability in CO2-ODHP.

Bimetallic compositions, such as Fe-Ni and Fe-Pt, have shown promising results in CO2-ODHP. The selectivity towards olefins can be tuned by adjusting the bimetallic composition of the active sites [10.1039/D0CY00586J]. For example, Fe-Ni surfaces favor C-H bond scissoring, while Pt-rich surfaces favor C-C cleavage.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The superior performance of Fe2O3/AC in CO2-ODHP opens up new avenues for catalyst design in this field. Future research could focus on optimizing the AC support properties, such as porosity and surface functionality, to further enhance the reducibility and dispersion of Fe oxide species. Additionally, exploring novel bimetallic compositions with Fe oxide could lead to improved catalyst stability and selectivity.

The role of CeO2 as a support material in activating CO2 suggests that investigating other support materials with similar oxygen storage capacity and mobility could be beneficial. This could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and stable catalysts for CO2-ODHP.

The proposed redox cycle mechanism provides a foundation for further mechanistic studies. Advanced in-situ characterization techniques could be employed to gain deeper insights into the dynamic changes of Fe oxide species during the reaction, which could guide the design of more efficient catalysts.

Lastly, the influence of reaction conditions, such as temperature and CO2/C3H8 ratio, on the performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts could be systematically studied to optimize the process conditions for industrial applications of CO2-ODHP using these catalysts.

Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2辅助丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中的性能受到多个关键因素的影响，包括催化剂的可还原性、载体材料和双金属组成。在研究的各种Fe氧化物基催化剂中，Fe2O3/AC（活性炭负载的氧化铁）在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面都表现出优异的性能[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。

催化剂的可还原性在其催化活性中起着至关重要的作用。与其他Fe氧化物基催化剂相比，Fe2O3/AC表现出更好的可还原性，其还原过程经历Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。这种增强的可还原性是Fe2O3/AC与其他测试的催化剂相比具有高催化性能的关键。

载体材料对催化剂的性能有显著影响。作为载体的活性炭（AC）增强了Fe2O3的可还原性，这有助于其优越的活性[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。其他载体如γ-Al2O3和MgO由于Fe氧化物与载体材料之间的强相互作用，可能会阻碍Fe氧化物的可还原性。此外，由于CeO2能够通过直接C=O键断裂活化CO2，因此被认为是Fe基催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的一种有前景的载体[10.1039/D0CY00586J]。

Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的proposed反应机理涉及一个氧化还原循环。该过程包括两个主要步骤：（1）使用来自Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧将C3H8氧化为C3H6，以及（2）通过CO2作为软氧化剂重新氧化还原后的Fe氧化物[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。这个氧化还原循环允许催化剂表面活性位点的持续再生，在整个反应过程中维持其活性。

反应条件下氧化Fe的存在对于通过CO2-ODHP途径促进氧化性C-H键断裂至关重要[10.1002/aic.16670]。Fe氧化程度在催化剂性能中起着重要作用，Fe的更高氧化程度与CO2-ODHP中更好的活性和稳定性相关。

双金属组成，如Fe-Ni和Fe-Pt，在CO2-ODHP中显示出有前景的结果。通过调整活性位点的双金属组成，可以调节烯烃的选择性[10.1039/D0CY00586J]。例如，Fe-Ni表面有利于C-H键断裂，而富Pt表面有利于C-C键断裂。

※※※

综合理解和未来展望：Fe2O3/AC在CO2-ODHP中的优异性能为该领域的催化剂设计开辟了新的途径。未来的研究可以集中在优化AC载体的属性，如孔隙度和表面功能性，以进一步提高Fe氧化物物种的可还原性和分散性。此外，探索Fe氧化物的新型双金属组成可能会导致催化剂稳定性和选择性的提高。

CeO2作为载体材料在活化CO2中的作用表明，研究具有类似氧储存能力和迁移能力的其他载体材料可能会有益。这可能会导致开发出更高效和稳定的CO2-ODHP催化剂。

proposed的氧化还原循环机理为进一步的机理研究提供了基础。可以采用先进的原位表征技术来深入了解反应过程中Fe氧化物物种的动态变化，这可以指导更高效催化剂的设计。

最后，可以系统地研究反应条件（如温度和CO2/C3H8比率）对Fe氧化物基催化剂性能的影响，以优化工业应用中使用这些催化剂进行CO2-ODHP的工艺条件。
