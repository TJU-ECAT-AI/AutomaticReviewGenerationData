<English>
The performance of Fe oxide-based catalysts in CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (CO2-ODHP) is significantly influenced by catalyst reducibility and support materials, particularly in the case of Fe2O3/AC (iron oxide supported on activated carbon). 

Fe2O3/AC has demonstrated superior performance in both C3H8 conversion and C3H6 yield compared to other Fe oxide-based catalysts [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. The improved reducibility of Fe2O3 on activated carbon is a key factor in its high catalytic performance. H2-TPR results show that Fe2O3/AC undergoes a complete reduction process: Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. This enhanced reducibility is crucial for the catalyst's effectiveness in the CO2-ODHP process.

The choice of support material significantly influences catalyst performance. Activated carbon (AC) has been found to be particularly effective, enhancing the reducibility of Fe2O3 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]. In contrast, other supports like γ-Al2O3 and MgO can hinder Fe oxide reducibility due to strong metal-support interactions. The type of support has been shown to have a stronger influence on catalytic activity than the concentration of iron [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7].

CeO2 has also been identified as a promising support for Fe-based catalysts in CO2-ODHP. Its ability to activate CO2 through direct C=O bond scission, along with its oxygen mobility and oxygen storage capacity (OSC), contributes to the formation of stable surface active sites [10.1039/D0CY00586J].

The proposed reaction mechanism for CO2-ODHP over Fe oxide-based catalysts involves a redox cycle, which is crucial for maintaining catalytic activity. The process consists of two main steps:

1. C3H8 oxidation to C3H6 using lattice oxygen from Fe2O3 or Fe3O4
2. Reoxidation of the reduced Fe oxide by CO2, which acts as a soft oxidant [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]

This redox cycle can be represented as:
MeFemOn + CO2 → MeFemOn+1 + CO [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]

The ability of Fe oxide to undergo this redox cycle efficiently is crucial for its effectiveness in the CO2-ODHP process. The presence of oxidized Fe under reaction conditions is key to favoring oxidative C-H bond cleavage via the CO2-ODHP pathway [10.1002/aic.16670].

In bimetallic systems, such as Fe-Ni and Fe-Pt, the composition of the catalyst affects its performance and selectivity. Fe-Ni surfaces favor C-H bond scissoring, while Pt-rich surfaces favor C-C cleavage [10.1039/D0CY00586J]. The extent of Fe oxidation in these bimetallic systems also plays a significant role in catalyst performance and stability [10.1002/aic.16670].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements in Fe oxide-based catalysts for CO2-ODHP, future research directions could focus on further optimizing the interplay between Fe oxide species and support materials. The development of novel support materials or composite supports that can enhance both the reducibility of Fe oxide and the activation of CO2 could lead to more efficient catalysts. Additionally, exploring the synergistic effects in multi-component catalysts, combining Fe with other transition metals and promoters, may offer new opportunities for improving catalyst performance and selectivity. 

Furthermore, in-depth studies on the reaction mechanism, particularly using advanced in-situ characterization techniques, could provide valuable insights into the dynamic changes of the catalyst during the reaction. This knowledge could guide the rational design of more stable and active catalysts for CO2-ODHP. Lastly, considering the growing importance of sustainable chemical processes, integrating CO2-ODHP with CO2 capture technologies or renewable energy sources could pave the way for more environmentally friendly propylene production processes.

<Chinese>
Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2辅助丙烷氧化脱氢（CO2-ODHP）中的性能受到催化剂可还原性和载体材料的显著影响，特别是在Fe2O3/AC（活性炭负载的氧化铁）的情况下。

与其他Fe氧化物基催化剂相比，Fe2O3/AC在C3H8转化率和C3H6产率方面表现出优异的性能 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。Fe2O3在活性炭上的改进可还原性是其高催化性能的关键因素。H2-TPR结果表明，Fe2O3/AC经历了完整的还原过程：Fe2O3 → Fe3O4 → FeO → Fe [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。这种增强的可还原性对催化剂在CO2-ODHP过程中的有效性至关重要。

载体材料的选择对催化剂性能有显著影响。活性炭（AC）被发现特别有效，能够增强Fe2O3的可还原性 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]。相比之下，其他载体如γ-Al2O3和MgO由于强金属-载体相互作用可能会阻碍Fe氧化物的可还原性。研究表明，载体类型对催化活性的影响比铁的浓度更强 [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]。

CeO2也被确定为Fe基催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的一种有前景的载体。其通过直接C=O键裂解激活CO2的能力，以及其氧移动性和氧存储容量（OSC），有助于形成稳定的表面活性位点 [10.1039/D0CY00586J]。

Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP中的反应机理涉及一个氧化还原循环，这对维持催化活性至关重要。该过程主要包括两个步骤：

1. 使用来自Fe2O3或Fe3O4的晶格氧将C3H8氧化为C3H6
2. 通过CO2（作为软氧化剂）重新氧化还原后的Fe氧化物 [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_7]

这个氧化还原循环可以表示为：
MeFemOn + CO2 → MeFemOn+1 + CO [10.1016/S0926-860X(00)00514-7]

Fe氧化物高效进行这种氧化还原循环的能力对其在CO2-ODHP过程中的有效性至关重要。在反应条件下氧化Fe的存在对于通过CO2-ODHP途径促进氧化C-H键断裂至关重要 [10.1002/aic.16670]。

在双金属体系中，如Fe-Ni和Fe-Pt，催化剂的组成影响其性能和选择性。Fe-Ni表面有利于C-H键断裂，而富Pt表面有利于C-C键断裂 [10.1039/D0CY00586J]。这些双金属体系中Fe的氧化程度也在催化剂性能和稳定性方面起着重要作用 [10.1002/aic.16670]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：考虑到Fe氧化物基催化剂在CO2-ODHP方面的进展，未来的研究方向可以集中在进一步优化Fe氧化物物种和载体材料之间的相互作用。开发能够同时增强Fe氧化物可还原性和CO2活化的新型载体材料或复合载体可能会导致更高效的催化剂。此外，探索多组分催化剂中的协同效应，将Fe与其他过渡金属和促进剂结合，可能为提高催化剂性能和选择性提供新的机会。

此外，对反应机理的深入研究，特别是使用先进的原位表征技术，可以提供关于催化剂在反应过程中动态变化的宝贵见解。这些知识可以指导更稳定和活性的CO2-ODHP催化剂的理性设计。最后，考虑到可持续化学过程的日益重要性，将CO2-ODHP与CO2捕获技术或可再生能源源相结合，可能为更环保的丙烯生产工艺铺平道路。
