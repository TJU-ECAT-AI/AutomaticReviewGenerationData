<English>
Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions by creating a multi-scale porous structure. This structure improves mass transfer, diffusion of reactants and products, and maintains the intrinsic catalytic properties of the zeolite [10.1039/D0CS01260B]. The combination of micropores and mesopores/macropores achieves enhanced micropore accessibility without sacrificing the intrinsic confinement effect of micropores [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5].

The main types of hierarchical zeolites for PDH reactions include:
1. Mesoporous zeolites (e.g., hierarchical ZSM-5, silicalite-1 nanosheets)
2. Macroporous zeolites
3. Micro-mesoporous zeolites
4. Micro-macroporous zeolites
5. Micro-meso-macroporous zeolites
6. Hierarchical zeolite composites (e.g., TS-1@SBA-16)
7. Dealuminated zeolites (e.g., beta zeolite)

Synthesis methods for hierarchical zeolites include:
1. Post-treatment synthetic routes:
   - Desilication: Treatment of ZSM-5 with NaOH solution under mild conditions [10.1016/j.cjche.2021.11.020]
   - Dealumination
   - Selective etching with alkaline or fluoride agents [10.1002/adma.201803966]
2. In situ synthetic routes:
   - Using organic or inorganic templates during zeolite crystallization [10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016]
   - Repetitive branching synthesis strategy
   - Utilization of long chain alkyl structure-directing agents [10.1039/D0CS01260B]
3. Self-assembly methods:
   - Enwrapping zeolite crystals with ordered mesoporous silica (OMS) [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
4. Bottom-up and top-down methods [10.1002/adma.201803966]
5. Use of specific structure-directing agents:
   - Tetra-(n-butyl)phosphonium hydroxide (TBPOH) for hierarchical silicalite-1 nanosheets
   - Tetrapropylammonium hydroxide (TPAOH) for conventional silicalite-1 [10.3390/catal9020174]

Hierarchical zeolites significantly improve PDH reaction performance compared to traditional zeolites:
1. Enhanced accessibility to active sites and improved mass transfer [10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7]
2. Higher propane conversion and propylene selectivity:
   - PtSn supported on hierarchical ZSM-5 shows 50% propane conversion and 95% propylene selectivity, compared to 30% conversion and 65% selectivity for conventional ZSM-5 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
   - Hierarchical Ga-MFI zeolite catalysts demonstrate 26 times higher conversion and 10-100% increased propylene selectivity compared to bulk Ga-MFI catalysts [10.1039/D0CS01260B]
3. Improved catalyst stability and lifetime due to better coke resistance [10.1016/j.cjche.2021.11.020]
4. Higher turnover frequencies (TOF):
   - Pt catalysts on hierarchical S-1 zeolites show a TOF of 1112.2 h⁻¹ compared to 157.6 h⁻¹ for bulk S-1 supports [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
5. Better dispersion and stabilization of metal nanoparticles [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011]
6. Reduced side reactions, such as cracking and oligomerization [10.3390/catal9020174]

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The development of hierarchical zeolites has significantly advanced the field of propane dehydrogenation catalysis by addressing key limitations of traditional zeolites. The synergistic combination of micropores and mesopores/macropores offers a promising platform for designing highly efficient and stable PDH catalysts. Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. Optimizing the balance between micropores and mesopores/macropores to maximize catalytic performance while maintaining stability.
2. Developing novel synthesis methods that allow for precise control over pore size distribution and connectivity.
3. Exploring the potential of hierarchical zeolites in other industrially relevant reactions beyond PDH.
4. Investigating the long-term stability and regeneration capabilities of hierarchical zeolite catalysts under industrial conditions.
5. Combining hierarchical zeolites with advanced metal nanoparticle designs to further enhance catalytic performance and selectivity.
6. Utilizing advanced characterization techniques to gain deeper insights into the structure-property relationships of hierarchical zeolites and their impact on PDH reactions.
7. Exploring the potential of hierarchical zeolites in continuous flow reactors and process intensification strategies for PDH.

These research directions could lead to the development of next-generation PDH catalysts with superior performance, longer lifetimes, and improved economic viability for industrial applications.

<Chinese>
分级沸石通过创建多尺度多孔结构，将微孔与介孔和/或大孔相结合，以增强丙烷脱氢（PDH）反应的可及性。这种结构改善了传质、反应物和产物的扩散，同时保持了沸石的固有催化性能 [10.1039/D0CS01260B]。微孔和介孔/大孔的结合在不牺牲微孔固有限域效应的情况下实现了微孔可及性的提高 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]。

PDH反应中使用的主要分级沸石类型包括：
1. 介孔沸石（例如，分级ZSM-5，硅铝酸盐-1纳米片）
2. 大孔沸石
3. 微-介孔沸石
4. 微-大孔沸石
5. 微-介-大孔沸石
6. 分级沸石复合材料（例如，TS-1@SBA-16）
7. 脱铝沸石（例如，β沸石）

分级沸石的合成方法包括：
1. 后处理合成路线：
   - 脱硅：在温和条件下用NaOH溶液处理ZSM-5 [10.1016/j.cjche.2021.11.020]
   - 脱铝
   - 用碱性或氟化物试剂选择性刻蚀 [10.1002/adma.201803966]
2. 原位合成路线：
   - 在沸石结晶过程中使用有机或无机模板 [10.1021/acs.chemrev.0c00016]
   - 重复分支合成策略
   - 利用长链烷基结构导向剂 [10.1039/D0CS01260B]
3. 自组装方法：
   - 用有序介孔二氧化硅（OMS）包裹沸石晶体 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
4. 自下而上和自上而下的方法 [10.1002/adma.201803966]
5. 使用特定的结构导向剂：
   - 四-(正丁基)磷鎓氢氧化物（TBPOH）用于分级硅铝酸盐-1纳米片
   - 四丙基铵氢氧化物（TPAOH）用于常规硅铝酸盐-1 [10.3390/catal9020174]

与传统沸石相比，分级沸石显著改善了PDH反应性能：
1. 提高了活性位点的可及性和改善了传质 [10.1007/s40242-019-9120-7]
2. 更高的丙烷转化率和丙烯选择性：
   - PtSn负载在分级ZSM-5上显示50%的丙烷转化率和95%的丙烯选择性，而常规ZSM-5为30%的转化率和65%的选择性 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
   - 分级Ga-MFI沸石催化剂与块状Ga-MFI催化剂相比，转化率提高26倍，丙烯选择性提高10-100% [10.1039/D0CS01260B]
3. 由于更好的抗焦性，催化剂稳定性和寿命得到改善 [10.1016/j.cjche.2021.11.020]
4. 更高的转换频率（TOF）：
   - 负载在分级S-1沸石上的Pt催化剂显示TOF为1112.2 h⁻¹，而块状S-1载体为157.6 h⁻¹ [10.1021/acscatal.3c00816_5]
5. 金属纳米粒子的更好分散和稳定 [10.1016/j.jcat.2021.09.011]
6. 减少副反应，如裂解和低聚反应 [10.3390/catal9020174]

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：分级沸石的开发通过解决传统沸石的关键限制，显著推进了丙烷脱氢催化领域。微孔和介孔/大孔的协同组合为设计高效稳定的PDH催化剂提供了一个有前景的平台。未来的研究努力可能会集中在：

1. 优化微孔和介孔/大孔之间的平衡，以最大化催化性能同时保持稳定性。
2. 开发新的合成方法，允许精确控制孔径分布和连通性。
3. 探索分级沸石在PDH以外其他工业相关反应中的潜力。
4. 研究分级沸石催化剂在工业条件下的长期稳定性和再生能力。
5. 将分级沸石与先进的金属纳米粒子设计相结合，进一步提高催化性能和选择性。
6. 利用先进的表征技术，深入了解分级沸石的结构-性能关系及其对PDH反应的影响。
7. 探索分级沸石在连续流反应器和PDH过程强化策略中的潜力。

这些研究方向可能导致下一代PDH催化剂的开发，具有更优越的性能、更长的使用寿命和更高的工业应用经济可行性。
