<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions by integrating the intrinsic micropores of zeolites with an auxiliary mesoporous/macroporous network. This structure achieves enhanced micropore accessibility while maintaining the intrinsic confinement effect of micropores.

The main types and synthesis methods of these materials include:
1. Mesoporous zeolites prepared via desilication or self-assembly
2. 2D/layered zeolites synthesized using bifunctional surfactants
3. Pillared zeolites synthesized using TPAOH and TBPOH as cotemplates

Hierarchical zeolites significantly improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites. For example, PtSn supported on hierarchical ZSM-5 shows much higher initial propane conversion (50%) and propylene selectivity (95%) compared to microporous ZSM-5 (30% conversion and 65% selectivity). Additionally, Pt catalysts supported on hierarchical S-1 zeolites exhibit a much higher turnover frequency (TOF) of 1112.2 h−1 compared to bulk S-1 supports (TOF of 157.6 h−1), demonstrating the enhanced catalytic performance of hierarchical zeolites in PDH reactions.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

分级沸石通过将沸石的固有微孔与辅助介孔/大孔网络相结合，来增强PDH反应的可及性。这种结构实现了增强的微孔可及性，同时保持了微孔的固有限制效应。

这些材料的主要类型和合成方法包括：
1. 通过脱硅或自组装制备的介孔沸石
2. 使用双功能表面活性剂合成的2D/层状沸石
3. 使用TPAOH和TBPOH作为共模板合成的柱状沸石

与传统沸石相比，分级沸石显著提高了PDH反应的性能。例如，负载在分级ZSM-5上的PtSn显示出更高的初始丙烷转化率（50%）和丙烯选择性（95%），而微孔ZSM-5的性能较低（转化率30%，选择性65%）。此外，负载在分级S-1沸石上的Pt催化剂表现出更高的转化频率（TOF）1112.2 h−1，而体相S-1支持的TOF仅为157.6 h−1，这证明了分级沸石在PDH反应中的增强催化性能。
