<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Hierarchical zeolites combine micropores and mesopores/macropores to enhance accessibility for PDH reactions by providing an auxiliary mesoporous/macroporous network in addition to the intrinsic micropores of zeolites. This combination achieves improved micropore accessibility without sacrificing the confinement effect of micropores.

The main types of hierarchical zeolites for PDH reactions include:
1. Mesoporous zeolites prepared via desilication or self-assembly
2. 2D/layered zeolites synthesized using bifunctional surfactants
3. Pillared zeolites synthesized using TPAOH and TBPOH as cotemplates

These materials are synthesized through various methods such as desilication, self-assembly, use of bifunctional surfactants, and templating with specific agents like TPAOH and TBPOH.

Hierarchical zeolites significantly improve the performance of PDH reactions compared to traditional zeolites. For example, PtSn supported on hierarchical ZSM-5 showed much higher initial propane conversion (50% vs 30%) and propylene selectivity (95% vs 65%) compared to the parent microporous ZSM-5. Additionally, a Pt catalyst supported on hierarchical S-1 exhibited a much higher Pt-related turnover frequency (1112.2 h-1) compared to the catalyst with bulk S-1 supports (157.6 h-1). These improvements can be attributed to the enhanced accessibility and better diffusion properties provided by the hierarchical structure.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

分级沸石通过在固有的微孔结构中增加辅助介孔/大孔网络，来提高PDH反应的可及性。这种组合实现了微孔可及性的提高，同时不牺牲微孔的限制效应。

用于PDH反应的主要分级沸石类型包括：
1. 通过脱硅或自组装制备的介孔沸石
2. 使用双功能表面活性剂合成的二维/层状沸石
3. 使用TPAOH和TBPOH作为共模板合成的柱状沸石

这些材料通过各种方法合成，如脱硅、自组装、使用双功能表面活性剂，以及使用特定的模板剂如TPAOH和TBPOH。

与传统沸石相比，分级沸石显著提高了PDH反应的性能。例如，负载在分级ZSM-5上的PtSn催化剂表现出更高的初始丙烷转化率（50%对30%）和丙烯选择性（95%对65%），相比于母体微孔ZSM-5。此外，负载在分级S-1上的Pt催化剂展现出更高的Pt相关转化频率（1112.2 h-1），相比于负载在体相S-1上的催化剂（157.6 h-1）。这些改进可归因于分级结构提供的增强可及性和更好的扩散性能。
