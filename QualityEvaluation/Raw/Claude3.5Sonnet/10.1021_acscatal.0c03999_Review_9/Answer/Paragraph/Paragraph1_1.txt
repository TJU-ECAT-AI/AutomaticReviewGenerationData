<English>
The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are significantly influenced by their synthesis methods. This review analyzes the impact of various preparation techniques on catalytic performance, evaluated through key performance metrics.

Steam Activation:
Steam activation has emerged as a crucial method for enhancing catalytic performance in ODHP reactions. Steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts consistently demonstrate superior activity and selectivity compared to other preparation methods [10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035]. The process typically involves calcination followed by treatment with 30 vol% H2O in N2 flow at 873 K for 5 hours [10.1039/B305568J]. This treatment leads to the formation of extra-framework iron species, which are more active than framework iron species for N2O-ODHP [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9].

Key performance metrics for steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 include:
1. C3H8 conversion: 45-52%
2. N2O conversion: 86-96%
3. C3H6 selectivity: 45-50%
4. C3H6 yield: 19-25% at 723 K
[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035] [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]

The superior performance of steam-activated catalysts is attributed to the presence of oligonuclear FexOy clusters, which are more catalytically active than isolated iron ions [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003].

Solid-State Ion Exchange:
Solid-state ion exchange methods, using precursors like ferrocene or FeCl3, have also shown promise in preparing effective Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts for ODHP reactions. These methods produce various active iron species, including large oxide clusters, small oxygen-containing nanoclusters (Fe4O4, Fe3O4), and isolated iron species [10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038].

A Fe-ZSM-5 catalyst prepared via solid-state exchange using ferrocene as the iron precursor exhibited:
1. Maximum initial propylene yield: 30%
2. Stable propane conversion rate: ~13 mmol gcat^-1 h^-1
3. Propylene production rate: ~6 mmol gcat^-1 h^-1 (maintained for >40 hours)
[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032]

Liquid Ion Exchange:
Liquid ion exchange methods have also been employed to synthesize Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts. These methods introduce extra-framework iron species to the zeolite host, resulting in ferric ions with different nuclearity (isolated ions, oligonuclear clusters, and Fe2O3 nanoparticles) [10.1039/c3cy20782j].

Fe-ZSM-5 prepared by liquid ion exchange has shown:
1. C3H6 yield: Up to 20%
2. Improved performance with acid post-treatments, increasing C3H6 yield to ~25%
[10.1039/c3cy20782j]

Comparison of Methods:
While all methods produce active catalysts, steam activation consistently yields superior performance. Steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 shows higher resistance to deactivation by coking compared to other methods [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]. However, catalysts prepared by methods leading to extra-framework iron (including steam activation and some liquid ion exchange techniques) may have lower resistance to deactivation over extended periods [10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038].

The effectiveness of these catalysts is influenced by reaction conditions, particularly temperature. Optimal performance is typically observed in the range of 673-773 K, with a volcano-shaped curve for C3H6 yield peaking around 673-723 K [10.1039/c3cy20782j].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The synthesis method of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites plays a crucial role in determining their catalytic performance in N2O-assisted ODHP reactions. Steam activation has emerged as a particularly effective method, consistently producing catalysts with superior activity and selectivity. This is largely due to the formation of extra-framework iron species and oligonuclear FexOy clusters, which are more catalytically active than framework or isolated iron species.

Future research directions may focus on:
1. Optimizing steam activation parameters (temperature, duration, steam concentration) to further enhance catalyst performance and stability.
2. Developing hybrid synthesis methods that combine the advantages of different techniques, such as solid-state ion exchange followed by steam activation.
3. Investigating the potential of novel iron precursors or zeolite modifications to improve catalyst effectiveness and longevity.
4. Exploring the use of in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the evolution of iron species during the reaction and deactivation processes.
5. Developing regeneration strategies to extend catalyst lifetime and improve the overall efficiency of the ODHP process.

These advancements could lead to more efficient and sustainable production of propylene, addressing the growing demand for this important chemical feedstock while minimizing environmental impact.

<Chinese>
铁含量ZSM-5分子筛作为N2O辅助丙烷氧化脱氢(ODHP)反应的催化剂，其有效性和性能显著受到其合成方法的影响。本综述分析了各种制备技术对催化性能的影响，并通过关键性能指标进行评估。

蒸汽活化：
蒸汽活化已成为提高ODHP反应催化性能的关键方法。蒸汽活化的Fe-ZSM-5催化剂在活性和选择性方面始终表现出优于其他制备方法的性能[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035]。该过程通常包括焙烧，然后在873 K下用30体积%的H2O在N2流中处理5小时[10.1039/B305568J]。这种处理导致形成框架外铁物种，它们比框架内铁物种对N2O-ODHP更活跃[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9]。

蒸汽活化Fe-ZSM-5的关键性能指标包括：
1. C3H8转化率：45-52%
2. N2O转化率：86-96%
3. C3H6选择性：45-50%
4. C3H6产率：在723 K时为19-25%
[10.1016/j.ces.2004.07.035] [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]

蒸汽活化催化剂的优异性能归因于存在寡核FexOy簇，它们比孤立的铁离子具有更高的催化活性[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]。

固态离子交换：
使用二茂铁或FeCl3等前驱体的固态离子交换方法也在制备ODHP反应的有效Fe-ZSM-5催化剂方面显示出潜力。这些方法产生各种活性铁物种，包括大氧化物簇、小的含氧纳米簇（Fe4O4、Fe3O4）和孤立的铁物种[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038]。

使用二茂铁作为铁前驱体通过固态交换制备的Fe-ZSM-5催化剂表现出：
1. 最大初始丙烯产率：30%
2. 稳定的丙烷转化速率：~13 mmol gcat^-1 h^-1
3. 丙烯生产速率：~6 mmol gcat^-1 h^-1（维持>40小时）
[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032]

液相离子交换：
液相离子交换方法也被用于合成Fe-ZSM-5催化剂。这些方法将框架外铁物种引入分子筛主体，产生具有不同核数的铁离子（孤立离子、寡核簇和Fe2O3纳米颗粒）[10.1039/c3cy20782j]。

通过液相离子交换制备的Fe-ZSM-5表现出：
1. C3H6产率：高达20%
2. 经过酸后处理后性能得到改善，C3H6产率提高到~25%
[10.1039/c3cy20782j]

方法比较：
虽然所有方法都能产生活性催化剂，但蒸汽活化一直产生最佳性能。蒸汽活化的Fe-ZSM-5与其他方法相比，对焦炭沉积导致的失活具有更高的抗性[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]。然而，通过导致框架外铁的方法（包括蒸汽活化和一些液相离子交换技术）制备的催化剂在长期使用中可能具有较低的抗失活能力[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038]。

这些催化剂的有效性受反应条件影响，特别是温度。最佳性能通常在673-773 K范围内观察到，C3H6产率呈火山形曲线，在673-723 K左右达到峰值[10.1039/c3cy20782j]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：铁含量ZSM-5分子筛的合成方法在决定其N2O辅助ODHP反应的催化性能方面起着关键作用。蒸汽活化已成为一种特别有效的方法，始终产生具有优越活性和选择性的催化剂。这主要是由于形成了框架外铁物种和寡核FexOy簇，它们比框架内或孤立的铁物种具有更高的催化活性。

未来的研究方向可能集中在：
1. 优化蒸汽活化参数（温度、持续时间、蒸汽浓度）以进一步提高催化剂性能和稳定性。
2. 开发结合不同技术优势的混合合成方法，如固态离子交换后进行蒸汽活化。
3. 研究新型铁前驱体或分子筛改性的潜力，以提高催化剂的有效性和寿命。
4. 探索使用原位表征技术，以更好地理解反应和失活过程中铁物种的演变。
5. 开发再生策略以延长催化剂寿命并提高ODHP过程的整体效率。

这些进展可能导致更高效和可持续的丙烯生产，满足这一重要化学原料不断增长的需求，同时最大限度地减少环境影响。
