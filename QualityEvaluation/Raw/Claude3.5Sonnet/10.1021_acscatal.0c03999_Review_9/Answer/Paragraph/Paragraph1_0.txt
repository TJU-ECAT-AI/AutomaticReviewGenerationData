<English>
The effectiveness and performance of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites as catalysts in N2O-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation of propane (ODHP) reactions are significantly influenced by their synthesis methods. This review analyzes the impact of various synthesis techniques on catalytic performance, evaluated through key performance metrics.

Steam Activation:
Steam activation emerges as a crucial method for enhancing catalytic performance. Steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 catalysts consistently demonstrate superior activity and selectivity compared to other preparation methods [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]. Key performance metrics for steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 include C3H8 conversion of 48-52%, N2O conversion of 86-96%, C3H6 selectivity of 45-50%, and C3H6 yield of 22-25% at temperatures around 450-723 K [10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9] [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003].

The effectiveness of steam activation is attributed to the formation of extraframework iron species, particularly oligonuclear FexOy clusters, which are more active than framework iron or isolated iron ions [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]. Steam treatment at 600°C significantly alters the iron species distribution, reducing Brønsted acid sites and increasing Td-coordinated Fe(III) ions in FeAAlASi extra-framework species (43-47%) and Fe-oxide-like particles (30-33%) [10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010].

Solid-State Ion Exchange:
Solid-state ion exchange methods, using precursors like ferrocene or FeCl3, also produce effective catalysts. Fe-ZSM-5 prepared via solid-state exchange with ferrocene followed by severe calcination exhibited a maximal initial propylene yield of 30% and maintained stable propane conversion and propylene production rates for over 40 hours [10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032]. This method generates highly isolated FeOAl species, which are identified as active sites for the ODHP reaction.

Liquid Ion Exchange:
Liquid ion exchange methods, including reductive solution ion-exchange, have shown promising results. FeMFI prepared by this method achieved a maximal propylene yield of about 14% at 673 K, which could be increased to 25% with subsequent acid post-treatments [10.1039/c3cy20782j]. This synthesis technique introduces extra-framework iron species with different nuclearity, including isolated ions, oligonuclear clusters, and Fe2O3 nanoparticles.

Comparative Performance:
While all methods can produce active catalysts, steam-activated Fe-ZSM-5 generally demonstrates the highest initial activity and selectivity. However, catalysts prepared by methods leading to extra-framework iron often show lower resistance to deactivation [10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038]. The optimal catalyst performance is achieved through a balance of extra-framework iron species (enhancing initial activity) and controlled acidity (improving selectivity and stability).

Reaction Conditions and Catalyst Stability:
The catalytic performance is significantly influenced by reaction conditions, particularly temperature and time-on-stream. Optimal performance is typically observed in the temperature range of 673-773 K. However, all catalysts experience some degree of deactivation due to coke formation over time. Steam-activated catalysts generally show better resistance to deactivation, maintaining higher propylene yields for longer periods [10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:
The synthesis method of iron-containing ZSM-5 zeolites plays a crucial role in determining their catalytic performance in N2O-assisted ODHP reactions. Steam activation emerges as a particularly effective technique, producing catalysts with superior activity and selectivity. However, each synthesis method offers unique advantages and potential for optimization.

Future research directions may include:

1. Hybrid synthesis methods: Combining different techniques, such as liquid ion exchange followed by steam activation, to optimize both iron distribution and catalyst stability.

2. In-situ characterization: Developing advanced techniques to study the evolution of iron species and acid sites during the reaction, providing deeper insights into the structure-performance relationships.

3. Tailored pore structures: Exploring hierarchical zeolites or controlled mesoporosity to enhance mass transfer and resistance to coking.

4. Alternative metals or bimetallics: Investigating the incorporation of other transition metals or bimetallic systems to further improve catalytic performance and stability.

5. Continuous regeneration systems: Developing innovative reactor designs that allow for continuous catalyst regeneration, addressing the deactivation issues observed in long-term operation.

6. Computational modeling: Utilizing advanced modeling techniques to predict optimal synthesis conditions and catalyst compositions for enhanced ODHP performance.

By addressing these areas, researchers can work towards developing more efficient and stable catalysts for N2O-assisted ODHP reactions, potentially leading to more sustainable propylene production processes.

<Chinese>
铁含量ZSM-5沸石作为N2O辅助氧化脱氢丙烷（ODHP）反应的催化剂的有效性和性能受其合成方法的显著影响。本综述分析了各种合成技术对催化性能的影响，通过关键性能指标进行评估。

蒸汽活化：
蒸汽活化成为提高催化性能的关键方法。蒸汽活化的Fe-ZSM-5催化剂在活性和选择性方面始终表现出优于其他制备方法的性能[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]。蒸汽活化Fe-ZSM-5的关键性能指标包括在450-723 K温度下，C3H8转化率为48-52%，N2O转化率为86-96%，C3H6选择性为45-50%，C3H6收率为22-25%[10.1021/acscatal.0c03999_9] [10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]。

蒸汽活化的有效性归因于形成了骨架外铁物种，特别是寡核FexOy簇，这些物种比骨架铁或孤立铁离子更活跃[10.1016/j.apcata.2004.03.003]。在600°C进行的蒸汽处理显著改变了铁物种的分布，减少了布朗斯特酸位，增加了FeAAlASi骨架外物种中的四配位Fe(III)离子（43-47%）和Fe-氧化物样颗粒（30-33%）[10.1016/j.jcat.2012.12.010]。

固态离子交换：
使用二茂铁或FeCl3等前体的固态离子交换方法也能生产有效的催化剂。通过二茂铁固态交换后进行严格煅烧制备的Fe-ZSM-5展现出30%的最大初始丙烯收率，并在40小时以上保持稳定的丙烷转化率和丙烯生产率[10.1016/j.micromeso.2014.07.032]。这种方法生成高度分散的FeOAl物种，被认定为ODHP反应的活性位点。

液相离子交换：
包括还原性溶液离子交换在内的液相离子交换方法显示出良好的结果。通过这种方法制备的FeMFI在673 K下达到约14%的最大丙烯收率，经过后续的酸处理可以增加到25%[10.1039/c3cy20782j]。这种合成技术引入了不同核数的骨架外铁物种，包括孤立离子、寡核簇和Fe2O3纳米粒子。

性能比较：
虽然所有方法都能生产活性催化剂，但蒸汽活化的Fe-ZSM-5通常表现出最高的初始活性和选择性。然而，通过导致骨架外铁形成的方法制备的催化剂往往表现出较低的抗失活能力[10.1016/j.apcata.2012.06.038]。最佳催化剂性能是通过平衡骨架外铁物种（提高初始活性）和控制酸度（提高选择性和稳定性）来实现的。

反应条件和催化剂稳定性：
催化性能受反应条件，特别是温度和反应时间的显著影响。最佳性能通常在673-773 K的温度范围内观察到。然而，所有催化剂随时间推移都会因焦炭形成而出现一定程度的失活。蒸汽活化的催化剂通常表现出更好的抗失活能力，能在更长时间内保持较高的丙烯收率[10.1016/j.jcat.2007.04.010]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：
铁含量ZSM-5沸石的合成方法在决定其N2O辅助ODHP反应的催化性能中起着至关重要的作用。蒸汽活化作为一种特别有效的技术脱颖而出，产生了具有卓越活性和选择性的催化剂。然而，每种合成方法都提供了独特的优势和优化潜力。

未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 混合合成方法：结合不同技术，如液相离子交换后进行蒸汽活化，以优化铁分布和催化剂稳定性。

2. 原位表征：开发先进技术以研究反应过程中铁物种和酸位的演变，提供更深入的结构-性能关系洞察。

3. 定制孔结构：探索分级沸石或控制介孔度，以增强质量传递和抗焦炭能力。

4. 替代金属或双金属：研究其他过渡金属的掺入或双金属系统，以进一步提高催化性能和稳定性。

5. 连续再生系统：开发创新的反应器设计，允许催化剂连续再生，解决长期运行中观察到的失活问题。

6. 计算机建模：利用先进的建模技术预测最佳合成条件和催化剂组成，以增强ODHP性能。

通过解决这些领域的问题，研究人员可以致力于开发更高效、更稳定的N2O辅助ODHP反应催化剂，潜在地导向更可持续的丙烯生产工艺。
