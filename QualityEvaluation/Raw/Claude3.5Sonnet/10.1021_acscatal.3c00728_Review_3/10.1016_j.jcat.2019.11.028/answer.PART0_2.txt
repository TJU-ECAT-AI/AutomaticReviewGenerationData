3. B2O3 nanowires covering both inside and outside of BPO4 hollow spheres: This sandwich-like structure ensures efficient utilization of both internal and external surfaces of the support, further increasing the number of active sites.

1. Simplicity: No need for templates or post-processing steps.
2. Direct formation of the desired structure: The sandwich-like hollow spheres are formed in a single step.
3. Versatility: The method can potentially be adapted for other similar catalyst systems.

This synthesis method is particularly effective for high-temperature catalytic applications, such as the oxidative dehydrogenation of propane to propene. The resulting catalyst shows excellent productivity and stability at temperatures up to 550°C, outperforming many metal-based catalysts.

For reactions requiring lower temperatures or different active species, alternative synthesis methods might be more suitable. In conclusion, the one-step template-free synthesis of B2O3@BPO4 sandwich-like hollow spheres effectively creates a 3-D dispersed active site structure that enhances catalytic performance, particularly for high-temperature applications. 3. B2O3纳米线覆盖BPO4中空球的内外表面：这种三明治状结构确保了支撑物内外表面的高效利用，进一步增加了活性位点的数量。

将B活性组分引入这些结构的方法是一步法无模板合成。这种方法对于创建具有独特结构特征的B2O3@BPO4复合材料非常有效。该方法的优势包括：

1. 简单性：不需要模板或后处理步骤。
2. 直接形成所需结构：三明治状中空球在单一步骤中形成。
3. 多功能性：该方法可能适用于其他类似的催化剂系统。

总之，B2O3@BPO4三明治状中空球的一步法无模板合成有效地创建了一种三维分散活性位点结构，增强了催化性能，特别适用于高温应用。未来的研究可以探索这种方法对其他催化剂系统和反应条件的适应性。
