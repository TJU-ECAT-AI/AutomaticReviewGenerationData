B-based materials with 3-D dispersed active sites in porous frameworks exhibit several structural characteristics that enhance catalytic performance in propane dehydrogenation:

1. High surface area and porous structure: These materials often feature micro-, meso-, and macroporous structures, providing large internal surface areas and improving mass transfer [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3]. For example, ordered macroporous BPO4 materials have interconnected pores with an average diameter of 200 nm, enhancing accessibility to active sites [10.1016/S1872-2067(20)63654-3].

2. Diverse boron species: Multiple types of boron species can be incorporated, including framework trigonal and tetrahedral boron, as well as terminal BOH groups. This diversity contributes to improved catalytic activity and selectivity [10.1039/D0CS01174F].

3. Open coordination of boron sites: Boron species are often anchored to the support matrix in an open coordination form, allowing for better accessibility of reactants to the active sites [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017].

4. Defect-rich structures: Incompletely crystallized zeolites with low crystallinity exhibit superior catalytic activity and higher olefin productivity at low temperatures [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017].

5. Uniform dispersion: Active boron species are often uniformly dispersed throughout the support material, ensuring a homogeneous distribution of active sites [10.1126/science.abe7935].

6. Thermal stability: Many B-based materials, such as silicon boride (SiB3-6), exhibit good oxidation resistance at high temperatures, ensuring catalytic stability [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014].

Methods used to incorporate B active components into these structures include:

1. Hydrothermal synthesis: This method is effective for creating borosilicate zeolites with well-dispersed boron species [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021].

2. Incipient wetness impregnation: This technique is used to incorporate boron oxide into carbon nanotubes and other support materials [10.1002/anie.200901826].

3. Chemical vapor deposition (CVD): This method is effective for creating uniform, thin layers of h-BN on cordierite supports [10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028].

4. Electrospinning: This technique is highly effective for creating porous boron-doped silica nanofibers in a single step [10.1016/S1872-2067(21)63809-3].

5. Template-assisted synthesis: This method is used to create ordered macroporous BPO4 materials with controlled pore sizes and structures [10.1016/S1872-2067(20)63654-3].

6. One-step template-free synthesis: This approach is effective for creating B2O3@BPO4 sandwich-like hollow spheres, combining the advantages of homogeneous and heterogeneous catalysis [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028].

The effectiveness of these methods varies depending on the specific catalytic application. For propane dehydrogenation, methods that create defect-rich structures, open coordination sites, and uniform dispersion of boron species tend to be more effective. However, the choice of method should be based on the specific requirements of the catalytic application, considering factors such as desired boron distribution, stability of active sites, and the nature of the support framework.

具有三维分散活性位点的B基多孔框架材料在烷烃脱氢中表现出几种增强催化性能的结构特征：

1. 高比表面积和多孔结构：这些材料通常具有微孔、介孔和大孔结构，提供大的内部表面积并改善传质 [10.1021/acscatal.3c00728_3]。例如，有序大孔BPO4材料具有平均直径为200 nm的互连孔，增强了活性位点的可及性 [10.1016/S1872-2067(20)63654-3]。

2. 多样的硼物种：可以包含多种类型的硼物种，包括骨架三配位和四配位硼，以及端基BOH基团。这种多样性有助于提高催化活性和选择性 [10.1039/D0CS01174F]。

3. 硼位点的开放配位：硼物种通常以开放配位形式锚定在支撑基质上，使反应物更容易接触到活性位点 [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017]。

4. 缺陷丰富的结构：结晶度低的不完全结晶沸石表现出优越的催化活性和较高的低温烯烃生产率 [10.1016/j.jcat.2022.02.017]。

5. 均匀分散：活性硼物种通常均匀分散在整个支撑材料中，确保活性位点的均匀分布 [10.1126/science.abe7935]。

6. 热稳定性：许多B基材料，如硼化硅（SiB3-6），在高温下表现出良好的抗氧化性，确保催化稳定性 [10.1016/j.jcat.2018.11.014]。

将B活性组分引入这些结构的方法包括：

1. 水热合成：这种方法有效地创建具有良好分散硼物种的硼硅沸石 [10.1016/j.jcat.2020.03.021]。

2. 浸渍法：这种技术用于将氧化硼引入碳纳米管和其他支撑材料 [10.1002/anie.200901826]。

3. 化学气相沉积（CVD）：这种方法有效地在堇青石支撑体上创建均匀、薄层的h-BN [10.1016/j.cattod.2018.12.028]。

4. 电纺丝：这种技术在单步中高效创建多孔硼掺杂二氧化硅纳米纤维 [10.1016/S1872-2067(21)63809-3]。

5. 模板辅助合成：这种方法用于创建具有可控孔径和结构的有序大孔BPO4材料 [10.1016/S1872-2067(20)63654-3]。

6. 一步无模板合成：这种方法有效地创建B2O3@BPO4三明治状空心球，结合了均相和多相催化的优势 [10.1016/j.jcat.2019.11.028]。

这些方法的有效性因特定的催化应用而异。对于烷烃脱氢，创建缺陷丰富的结构、开放配位位点和均匀分散的硼物种的方法往往更有效。然而，方法的选择应基于催化应用的具体要求，考虑诸如所需的硼分布、活性位点的稳定性以及支撑框架的性质等因素。
