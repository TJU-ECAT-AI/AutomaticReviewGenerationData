      <English>
          Recent advancements in enhancing catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts for direct dehydrogenation processes include:

1. Using zeolites as supports: Zeolites with well-defined microporous structures and excellent thermal stability are used to stabilize metal species.

2. Encapsulation of metal catalysts: Zeolite-encaged metal catalysts show remarkable catalytic stability, which is beneficial for heterogeneous catalysis.

3. Bimetallic clusters: Highly dispersed bimetallic clusters (e.g., Ga-Pt, Pt-Zn, Pt-Sn) are encapsulated within zeolites to improve catalyst performance and stability.

4. In-situ preparation methods: Techniques such as hydrothermal processes followed by ligand-protected direct H2 reduction are used to synthesize these catalysts.

5. Specific zeolite types: Purely siliceous zeolites like silicalite-1 (S-1, MFI zeotype) are used as supports for metal clusters.

These advancements address challenges in catalyst design, fabrication, and performance optimization by:

1. Improving thermal stability
2. Enhancing catalytic performance
3. Increasing durability in high-temperature reactions
4. Providing better dispersion of metal species
5. Offering confinement effects that improve stability and regeneration
6. Preventing metal sintering and deactivation

The resulting catalysts, such as GaPt@S-1, demonstrate excellent catalytic performance and high stability during propane dehydrogenation (PDH), making them valuable for industrial applications. These strategies have significantly improved the lifespan and efficiency of catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes.

          最近在提高直接脱氢过程中负载金属催化剂的催化稳定性方面的进展包括：

1. 使用沸石作为载体：利用具有明确微孔结构和优异热稳定性的沸石来稳定金属物种。

2. 金属催化剂的包封：沸石包封的金属催化剂表现出显著的催化稳定性，这对于多相催化反应非常有利。

3. 双金属簇：高度分散的双金属簇（如Ga-Pt、Pt-Zn、Pt-Sn）被包封在沸石中，以提高催化剂的性能和稳定性。

4. 原位制备方法：使用水热法等技术，随后进行配体保护的直接H2还原来合成这些催化剂。

5. 特定沸石类型：使用纯硅沸石如硅铝酸盐-1（S-1，MFI类型）作为金属簇的载体。

这些进展通过以下方式解决了催化剂设计、制造和性能优化的挑战：

1. 提高热稳定性
2. 增强催化性能
3. 提高高温反应的耐久性
4. 提供更好的金属物种分散性
5. 提供改善稳定性和再生性的限制效应
6. 防止金属烧结和失活

由此产生的催化剂，如GaPt@S-1，在丙烷脱氢（PDH）过程中表现出优异的催化性能和高稳定性，使其在工业应用中具有重要价值。这些策略显著提高了直接脱氢过程中催化剂的使用寿命和效率。
