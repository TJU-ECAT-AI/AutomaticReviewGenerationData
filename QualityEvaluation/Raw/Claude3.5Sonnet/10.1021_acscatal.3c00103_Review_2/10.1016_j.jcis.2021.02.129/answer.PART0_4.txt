<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Recent advancements in enhancing catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts for direct dehydrogenation processes include:

1. Using zeolites as supports: Zeolites with well-defined microporous structures and excellent thermal stability are used to stabilize metal species.

2. Encapsulation of metal catalysts: Zeolite-encaged metal catalysts show remarkable catalytic stability, which is beneficial for heterogeneous catalysis.

3. Bimetallic clusters: Highly dispersed bimetallic clusters (e.g., Ga-Pt) are encapsulated within zeolites to improve catalyst performance and stability.

4. In-situ preparation methods: Techniques such as hydrothermal processes followed by ligand-protected direct H2 reduction are used to synthesize these catalysts.

5. Siliceous zeolites: Purely siliceous zeolites like silicalite-1 (S-1, MFI zeotype) are used as supports for metal clusters.

These advancements address challenges in catalyst design and performance optimization by improving stability, enhancing catalytic performance, and potentially increasing the lifespan of catalysts in industrial applications. The encapsulation strategy, in particular, helps protect the metal species from sintering and deactivation during high-temperature reactions, which is crucial for maintaining long-term catalytic activity in industrial processes.

最近在提高直接脱氢过程中负载金属催化剂的催化稳定性方面的进展包括：

1. 使用沸石作为载体：利用具有明确微孔结构和优异热稳定性的沸石来稳定金属物种。

2. 金属催化剂的包封：沸石包封的金属催化剂表现出显著的催化稳定性，这对于多相催化反应非常有利。

3. 双金属簇：高度分散的双金属簇（例如Ga-Pt）被包封在沸石中，以提高催化剂的性能和稳定性。

4. 原位制备方法：使用水热法等技术，随后进行配体保护的直接H2还原来合成这些催化剂。

5. 纯硅沸石：使用纯硅沸石如硅铝酸盐-1（S-1，MFI类型）作为金属簇的载体。

这些进展通过提高稳定性、增强催化性能以及潜在地延长催化剂在工业应用中的使用寿命，解决了催化剂设计和性能优化方面的挑战。特别是包封策略有助于保护金属物种在高温反应中免受烧结和失活，这对于在工业过程中保持长期催化活性至关重要。
