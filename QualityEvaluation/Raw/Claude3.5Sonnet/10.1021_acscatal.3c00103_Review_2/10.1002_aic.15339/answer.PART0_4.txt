Recent advancements in enhancing catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts for direct dehydrogenation processes include:

1. Doping catalysts with promoter elements: The study investigates doping Pt/CeO2-Al2O3 catalysts with Ga to enhance catalyst performance.

2. Improving redox properties: Ga doping enhances the redox properties of CeO2, increasing oxygen storage capacity and mobility, which helps eliminate coke deposition.

3. Modifying active metal nanoparticles: Ga addition decorates Pt nanoparticles by forming PtGa alloys, which improves catalytic performance.

4. Geometric effects: The promoter element decreases Pt ensemble size or blocks low-coordinated defect sites, suppressing side reactions like hydrogenolysis and deep dehydrogenation.

5. Electronic effects: The promoter lowers desorption barriers for alkenes and coke precursors by donating electrons to Pt atoms, reducing hydrogenolysis and coking.

These strategies address challenges in catalyst design by improving selectivity, stability, and resistance to coking. They optimize performance by modifying both the support material (CeO2-Al2O3) and the active metal (Pt) simultaneously, potentially leading to more efficient and longer-lasting catalysts for industrial applications.

最近在提高直接脱氢过程中负载金属催化剂的催化稳定性方面的进展包括：

1. 掺杂促进元素：研究探讨了用Ga掺杂Pt/CeO2-Al2O3催化剂以提高催化剂性能。

2. 改善氧化还原性能：Ga掺杂增强了CeO2的氧化还原性能，提高了氧储存能力和移动性，有助于消除积碳。

3. 修饰活性金属纳米粒子：添加Ga通过形成PtGa合金来修饰Pt纳米粒子，从而改善催化性能。

4. 几何效应：促进元素减小Pt集合体尺寸或阻塞低配位缺陷位点，抑制副反应如氢解和深度脱氢。

5. 电子效应：促进元素通过向Pt原子捐赠电子来降低烯烃和积碳前体的脱附势垒，减少氢解和积碳。

这些策略通过提高选择性、稳定性和抗积碳能力来解决催化剂设计中的挑战。它们通过同时修饰载体材料（CeO2-Al2O3）和活性金属（Pt）来优化性能，可能会带来更高效、更持久的工业应用催化剂。
