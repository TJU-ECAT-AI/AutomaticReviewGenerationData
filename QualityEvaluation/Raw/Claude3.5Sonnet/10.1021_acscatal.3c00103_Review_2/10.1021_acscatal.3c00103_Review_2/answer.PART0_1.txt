<EnglishVersionAnswer>

Recent strategies and approaches developed to enhance the catalytic stability of supported metal catalysts in direct dehydrogenation processes include:

1. Introduction of promoters: This involves adding metal promoters to form alloys or intermetallic compounds in Pt-based catalysts, and introducing other metal oxides to form oxide compounds in metal oxide-based catalysts. These strategies help improve the dispersion of active species, modify the coordination and electronic state of active sites, and inhibit catalytic deactivation.

2. Regulation with noble metals: For metal oxide-based catalysts, introducing trace amounts of noble metals like Pt can improve dehydrogenation performance by stabilizing the active metal oxide species and enhancing their catalytic properties.

3. Subsurface regulation: This method involves tuning the composition of the subsurface layers in metal alloy catalysts to optimize adsorptive properties and enhance catalytic performance.

4. Multipromoter systems: Some research has focused on using multiple promoters simultaneously to achieve synergistic effects and further improve catalyst stability and performance.

5. Coreshell structures: Developing catalysts with specific coreshell structures has been explored to optimize the surface and subsurface compositions for enhanced stability and selectivity.

These advancements address challenges in catalyst design by:
- Improving dispersion and stability of active species
- Enhancing resistance to coking and sintering
- Optimizing electronic and geometric properties of active sites
- Increasing selectivity towards desired products
- Prolonging catalyst lifetime and improving regeneration capabilities

These strategies contribute to the development of more efficient, stable, and selective catalysts for industrial applications in direct dehydrogenation processes, potentially leading to improved process economics and sustainability.

最近开发的用于提高直接脱氢过程中负载金属催化剂催化稳定性的策略和方法包括：

1. 引入助剂：这包括在铂基催化剂中添加金属助剂以形成合金或金属间化合物，以及在金属氧化物基催化剂中引入其他金属氧化物以形成氧化物化合物。这些策略有助于改善活性物种的分散性，修饰活性位点的配位和电子状态，并抑制催化失活。

2. 贵金属调节：对于金属氧化物基催化剂，引入少量贵金属（如铂）可以通过稳定活性金属氧化物物种并增强其催化性能来改善脱氢性能。

3. 亚表面调节：这种方法涉及调整金属合金催化剂亚表面层的组成，以优化吸附性能并提高催化性能。

4. 多助剂系统：一些研究集中在同时使用多种助剂，以实现协同效应并进一步提高催化剂的稳定性和性能。

5. 核壳结构：开发具有特定核壳结构的催化剂，以优化表面和亚表面组成，提高稳定性和选择性。

这些进展通过以下方式解决了催化剂设计中的挑战：
- 改善活性物种的分散性和稳定性
- 增强抗结焦和烧结能力
- 优化活性位点的电子和几何性质
- 提高对目标产物的选择性
- 延长催化剂寿命并改善再生能力

这些策略有助于开发更高效、更稳定、更具选择性的催化剂，用于直接脱氢过程的工业应用，可能会提高工艺经济性和可持续性。
