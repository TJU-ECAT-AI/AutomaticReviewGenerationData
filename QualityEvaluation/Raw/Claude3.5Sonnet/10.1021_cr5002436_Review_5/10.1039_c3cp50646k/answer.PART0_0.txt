The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for catalytic dehydrogenation reactions consists of four main steps:

1. Adsorption: The reactant molecule adsorbs onto the catalyst surface.

2. Dissociation: The adsorbed molecule dissociates, typically losing a hydrogen atom that remains bound to the catalyst.

3. Rearrangement: The remaining molecular fragment rearranges on the catalyst surface.

4. Desorption: The product molecule desorbs from the catalyst surface.

There is ongoing debate about which step is rate-limiting. Some researchers argue the dissociation step is rate-limiting, while others propose desorption as the slowest step. The adsorption and rearrangement steps are generally considered to be fast.

This mechanism remains controversial despite decades of study for several reasons:

1. Complexity: The actual process likely involves many elementary steps and intermediates that are challenging to observe directly.

2. Catalyst dependence: Different catalysts may exhibit different rate-limiting steps.

3. Reaction conditions: Temperature, pressure, and reactant concentrations can affect which step is slowest.

4. Advances in analytical techniques: New methods continue to provide fresh insights into surface reactions.

5. Theoretical modeling: Improvements in computational chemistry allow for more detailed mechanistic predictions to be tested.

6. Industrial relevance: Understanding the rate-limiting step is crucial for catalyst design and process optimization.

The ongoing interest and debate highlight the importance of the Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism in heterogeneous catalysis and the challenges in fully elucidating complex surface reactions.

Horiuti-Polanyi催化脱氢反应机理包括四个主要步骤：

1. 吸附：反应物分子吸附在催化剂表面。

2. 解离：吸附的分子解离，通常失去一个氢原子，该氢原子仍与催化剂结合。

3. 重排：剩余的分子片段在催化剂表面重排。

4. 脱附：产物分子从催化剂表面脱附。

关于哪一步是速率限制步骤，存在持续的争论。一些研究人员认为解离步骤是速率限制步骤，而其他人则提出脱附是最慢的步骤。吸附和重排步骤通常被认为是快速的。

尽管经过数十年的研究，这种机理仍然存在争议，原因如下：

1. 复杂性：实际过程可能涉及许多难以直接观察的基元步骤和中间体。

2. 催化剂依赖性：不同的催化剂可能表现出不同的速率限制步骤。

3. 反应条件：温度、压力和反应物浓度可能影响哪一步最慢。

4. 分析技术的进步：新方法不断为表面反应提供新的见解。

5. 理论建模：计算化学的改进允许对更详细的机理预测进行测试。

6. 工业相关性：了解速率限制步骤对催化剂设计和工艺优化至关重要。

持续的兴趣和争论突显了Horiuti-Polanyi机理在多相催化中的重要性，以及完全阐明复杂表面反应的挑战。
