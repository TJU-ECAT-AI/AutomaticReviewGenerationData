<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for catalytic dehydrogenation reactions consists of four main steps:

1. Dissociative adsorption of the alkane (e.g. isobutane) on the catalyst surface
2. Dehydrogenation of the adsorbed alkyl species to form an adsorbed alkene
3. Desorption of the alkene product
4. Recombination and desorption of hydrogen

There has been ongoing debate about which step is rate-limiting. Traditionally, the second step (dehydrogenation) was often considered rate-limiting. However, the paper presents evidence that for isobutane dehydrogenation over Pt-based catalysts, the first step (dissociative adsorption) appears to be rate-limiting based on deuterium tracing studies.

The mechanism remains a topic of interest and controversy because:

1. It applies to many industrially important catalytic reactions
2. The rate-limiting step can vary depending on the specific reaction conditions and catalyst
3. Understanding the mechanism is crucial for rational catalyst design
4. Advanced experimental and computational techniques continue to provide new insights
5. The interplay between different steps is complex and can be affected by catalyst modifications

Despite nearly a century of study, there are still open questions about the details of surface species and transition states involved. The mechanism provides a framework for ongoing research to elucidate these molecular-level details and connect them to macroscopic reaction kinetics.

Horiuti-Polanyi催化脱氢反应机理包括四个主要步骤：

1. 烷烃(如异丁烷)在催化剂表面的解离吸附
2. 吸附的烷基物种脱氢形成吸附的烯烃
3. 烯烃产物的脱附
4. 氢的重组和脱附

关于哪一步是速率限制步骤一直存在争议。传统上，第二步(脱氢)通常被认为是速率限制步骤。然而，该论文提供的证据表明，对于基于Pt催化剂的异丁烷脱氢反应，根据氘示踪研究，第一步(解离吸附)似乎是速率限制步骤。

该机理仍然是一个备受关注和争议的话题，原因如下：

1. 它适用于许多工业上重要的催化反应
2. 速率限制步骤可能因具体反应条件和催化剂而异
3. 理解该机理对于合理设计催化剂至关重要
4. 先进的实验和计算技术不断提供新的见解
5. 不同步骤之间的相互作用复杂，可能受到催化剂修饰的影响

尽管已经研究了近一个世纪，但关于表面物种和过渡态的细节仍有待解答。该机理为正在进行的研究提供了一个框架，以阐明这些分子水平的细节，并将其与宏观反应动力学联系起来。
