The Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism for catalytic dehydrogenation reactions consists of four main steps:

1. Adsorption: The alkane molecule adsorbs onto the catalyst surface.

2. First C-H bond cleavage: One C-H bond is broken, forming an adsorbed alkyl species and a hydrogen atom on the catalyst surface.

3. Second C-H bond cleavage: Another C-H bond is broken, forming the adsorbed alkene and a second hydrogen atom.

4. Desorption: The alkene and hydrogen molecules desorb from the catalyst surface.

There is ongoing debate about which step is rate-limiting. Some researchers argue it is the initial C-H bond breaking (step 2), while others suggest it could be the second C-H cleavage (step 3) or even the desorption step (step 4). The rate-limiting step may depend on specific reaction conditions and catalyst properties.

This mechanism remains a topic of scientific interest and controversy despite nearly a century of study for several reasons:

1. Complexity: The actual process involves multiple elementary steps and intermediates, making it challenging to isolate and study individual steps.

2. Catalyst diversity: Different catalysts can alter the energetics of each step, potentially changing the rate-limiting step.

3. Reaction conditions: Temperature, pressure, and reactant concentrations can affect which step becomes rate-limiting.

4. Advanced techniques: New experimental and computational methods continue to provide fresh insights into the mechanism.

5. Industrial relevance: Understanding and optimizing this mechanism is crucial for improving industrial dehydrogenation processes.

6. Theoretical interest: The mechanism serves as a model system for studying heterogeneous catalysis and surface chemistry.

The ongoing research and debate surrounding the Horiuti-Polanyi mechanism highlight the complexity of catalytic processes and the importance of continued investigation in this field.

1. 吸附：烷烃分子吸附在催化剂表面。

2. 第一个C-H键断裂：一个C-H键断裂，在催化剂表面形成吸附的烷基物种和一个氢原子。

3. 第二个C-H键断裂：另一个C-H键断裂，形成吸附的烯烃和第二个氢原子。

4. 脱附：烯烃和氢分子从催化剂表面脱附。

关于哪一步是速率限制步骤，仍存在争议。一些研究人员认为是初始C-H键断裂（第2步），而其他人则认为可能是第二个C-H键断裂（第3步）甚至是脱附步骤（第4步）。速率限制步骤可能取决于特定的反应条件和催化剂性质。

尽管已经研究了近一个世纪，这个机理仍然是科学兴趣和争议的话题，原因如下：

2. 催化剂多样性：不同的催化剂可以改变每个步骤的能量学，潜在地改变速率限制步骤。

3. 反应条件：温度、压力和反应物浓度可以影响哪个步骤成为速率限制步骤。

4. 先进技术：新的实验和计算方法继续为机理提供新的见解。

5. 工业相关性：理解和优化这个机理对改进工业脱氢过程至关重要。

6. 理论兴趣：该机理作为研究多相催化和表面化学的模型系统。

围绕Horiuti-Polanyi机理的持续研究和争论突出了催化过程的复杂性以及在该领域继续研究的重要性。
