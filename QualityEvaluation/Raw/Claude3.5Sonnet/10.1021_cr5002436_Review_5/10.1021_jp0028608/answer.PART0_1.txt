1. Adsorption: The reactants (hydrogen and unsaturated molecule) adsorb onto the catalyst surface.

2. First hydrogen transfer: One hydrogen atom transfers to the adsorbed unsaturated molecule, forming a half-hydrogenated intermediate.

3. Second hydrogen transfer: The second hydrogen atom transfers, completing the hydrogenation.

4. Desorption: The fully hydrogenated product desorbs from the catalyst surface.
