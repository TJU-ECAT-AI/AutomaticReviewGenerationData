<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The primary synthesis method for metal-zeolite catalysts used in propane dehydrogenation (PDH) reactions mentioned in this study is incipient wetness impregnation. The literature does not explicitly state why MFI (ZSM-5) and BEA (Beta) topological zeolites dominate in PDH applications. However, it does provide insights into their effectiveness.

The structural characteristics contributing to the effectiveness of MFI and BEA zeolites include their three-dimensional microporous nature and smaller pore sizes compared to mesoporous materials like SBA-15. The study shows that activity in propane dehydrogenation decreases with increasing pore size, with ZSM-5 showing the highest propane conversion, followed by Beta and then SBA-15.

The three-dimensional microporous structure of these zeolites appears to induce optimal catalytic properties of Pt sites. This suggests that the confinement effect and the specific pore geometry of MFI and BEA zeolites play a crucial role in enhancing the catalytic performance in PDH reactions.

For future developments in PDH catalyst design, these findings imply that focusing on three-dimensional microporous materials with optimized pore sizes could lead to more effective catalysts. The challenge would be to balance the high activity provided by smaller pores with the need to minimize coke formation and maintain catalyst stability over time.

本研究中提到的丙烷脱氢(PDH)反应中使用的金属-沸石催化剂的主要合成方法是浸渍法。文献并未明确说明为什么MFI(ZSM-5)和BEA(Beta)拓扑结构的沸石在PDH应用中占主导地位。然而，它确实提供了关于它们有效性的见解。

MFI和BEA沸石的结构特征对其有效性的贡献包括它们的三维微孔性质以及与介孔材料（如SBA-15）相比较小的孔径。研究表明，丙烷脱氢活性随孔径增大而降低，ZSM-5显示出最高的丙烷转化率，其次是Beta，然后是SBA-15。

这些沸石的三维微孔结构似乎能诱导Pt位点的最佳催化性能。这表明MFI和BEA沸石的限域效应和特定的孔道几何结构在增强PDH反应的催化性能方面起着至关重要的作用。

对于PDH催化剂设计的未来发展，这些发现意味着关注具有优化孔径的三维微孔材料可能会导致更有效的催化剂。挑战将是平衡较小孔径提供的高活性与最小化积碳形成和维持催化剂长期稳定性的需求。
