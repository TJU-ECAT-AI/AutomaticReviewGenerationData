<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The recent boom in shale gas exploitation has renewed interest in propane dehydrogenation for propylene production. This is driven by increasing demand for propylene relative to ethylene in plastics manufacturing. 

Key technological advancements include the development of operando spectroscopy techniques to study catalyst behavior under realistic reaction conditions. This allows monitoring of coke formation and combustion in real-time.

Current limitations of dehydrogenation processes include rapid catalyst deactivation due to coke formation. The high temperatures and low pressures required for the reaction promote coke buildup. Additionally, increasing reaction temperature improves propane conversion but reduces propylene selectivity due to increased cracking reactions.

Areas for improvement include developing more coke-resistant catalysts, optimizing reaction conditions to balance conversion and selectivity, and implementing better process monitoring and control systems. Enhancing catalyst regeneration methods could also improve overall process efficiency and sustainability.

</EnglishVersionAnswer>

关键的技术进步包括开发了原位光谱技术，可以在实际反应条件下研究催化剂的行为。这允许实时监测焦炭的形成和燃烧。

需要改进的领域包括开发更耐焦炭的催化剂，优化反应条件以平衡转化率和选择性，以及实施更好的过程监控和控制系统。改进催化剂再生方法也可以提高整体过程效率和可持续性。
