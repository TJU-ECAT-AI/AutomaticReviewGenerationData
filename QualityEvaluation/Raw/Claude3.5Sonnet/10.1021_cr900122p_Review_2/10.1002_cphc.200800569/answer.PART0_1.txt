<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts significantly influence the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations. For zeolite-catalyzed reactions:

1. Modification with metals like Zn or Ga enhances the conversion of light alkanes to aromatics with high selectivity.

2. These catalysts are bifunctional: metal species facilitate dehydrogenation of alkanes, while Brønsted acid sites catalyze oligomerization and cyclization of olefins.

3. The addition of Zn to zeolites affects the H/D exchange reaction by:
   - Providing regioselectivity
   - Increasing the reaction rate
   - Decreasing the activation energy

4. Zn influences the activation of C-H bonds in small alkanes through the formation of Zn-alkyl species, which then interact with Brønsted acid sites.

5. The synergistic effect between metal species and acid sites is crucial for enhancing the catalytic activity and selectivity in alkane transformations.

These factors contribute to the industrial significance of metal-modified zeolites by improving their performance in important processes such as the conversion of light alkanes to higher-value aromatic products.

金属基催化剂的组成、结构和反应条件显著影响烷烃转化的机理和效率。对于沸石催化反应：

1. 用锌或镓等金属改性可以提高轻烷烃向芳烃转化的选择性和效率。

2. 这些催化剂具有双功能性：金属物种促进烷烃脱氢，而Brønsted酸位催化烯烃的寡聚和环化。

3. 向沸石中添加锌会影响H/D交换反应：
   - 提供区域选择性
   - 提高反应速率
   - 降低活化能

4. 锌通过形成锌-烷基物种影响小分子烷烃C-H键的活化，这些物种随后与Brønsted酸位相互作用。

5. 金属物种和酸位之间的协同效应对提高烷烃转化的催化活性和选择性至关重要。

这些因素通过改善金属改性沸石在重要过程（如将轻烷烃转化为高附加值芳烃产品）中的性能，提高了它们的工业重要性。
