      <English>
          The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts significantly influence the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations:

1. Supported vanadium oxides:
   - Composition: Active sites involve isolated VO species.
   - Structure: Surface V density is crucial; both too low and too high densities reduce selectivity.
   - Support: Silica is considered the optimal support for selectivity.
   - Reaction conditions: Narrow optimal temperature window around 500°C. Lower temperatures favor combustion, while higher temperatures (>550°C) lead to unselective gas-phase reactions.

2. Metal-exchanged zeolites:
   - Composition: Various metals can be used, including Ga, Zn, and others.
   - Structure: Different zeolite structures (e.g., MFI, MOR, ZSM-5) influence catalytic properties.
   - Reaction mechanisms: These catalysts can transform alkanes into alkenes and aromatics through processes involving CH bond activation and formation of metal-alkyl intermediates.
   - Specific examples:
     a. Ga-zeolites act as multifunctional catalysts, with CH bond activation occurring at Ga sites.
     b. Zn-zeolites show similar reactivity, including H2 and alkane activation, H/D exchange, and formation of aromatics from alkanes.

Industrial significance:
1. Processes like Cyclar use Ga-zeolites to convert LPG into hydrogen and liquid aromatics in a single step.
2. These catalysts offer efficient routes for alkane transformation, allowing for the production of valuable chemical products from readily available alkane feedstocks.
3. The ability to control reaction conditions and catalyst composition enables optimization of selectivity and efficiency in various alkane transformation processes.

          金属基催化剂的组成、结构和反应条件显著影响烷烃转化的机理和效率：

1. 负载型钒氧化物：
   - 组成：活性位点涉及孤立的VO物种。
   - 结构：表面V密度至关重要；密度过高或过低都会降低选择性。
   - 载体：二氧化硅被认为是最佳的选择性载体。
   - 反应条件：最佳温度窗口较窄，约为500°C。较低温度有利于燃烧，而较高温度（>550°C）会导致非选择性气相反应。

2. 金属交换沸石：
   - 组成：可以使用各种金属，包括Ga、Zn等。
   - 结构：不同的沸石结构（如MFI、MOR、ZSM-5）影响催化性能。
   - 反应机理：这些催化剂可以通过涉及CH键活化和形成金属-烷基中间体的过程将烷烃转化为烯烃和芳烃。
   - 具体例子：
     a. Ga沸石作为多功能催化剂，CH键活化在Ga位点上发生。
     b. Zn沸石显示类似的反应活性，包括H2和烷烃活化、H/D交换以及从烷烃形成芳烃。

工业重要性：
1. 像Cyclar这样的工艺使用Ga沸石将LPG一步转化为氢气和液体芳烃。
2. 这些催化剂为烷烃转化提供了高效途径，允许从易得的烷烃原料生产有价值的化学品。
3. 控制反应条件和催化剂组成的能力使得在各种烷烃转化过程中优化选择性和效率成为可能。
