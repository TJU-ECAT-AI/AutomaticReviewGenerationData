The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts, particularly Zn-exchanged zeolites, significantly influence the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations. 

Zinc can be introduced into zeolites through various methods, including aqueous or solid ion exchange techniques and vapor deposition of ZnCl2 on H-ZSM5. The resulting Zn-doped zeolites are effective catalysts for alkane transformations, particularly in aromatization processes.

The mechanism of alkane activation in Zn-exchanged zeolites involves a series of steps. The first step is the dehydrogenation of alkanes (such as ethane or propane) to corresponding alkenes. This is followed by dehydrocyclooligomerisation of the alkenes.

The specific mechanism for ethane activation involves alkyl rupture of the C-H bond, with the zinc cation acting as an acceptor for the alkyl group. The catalytic cycle consists of three main steps: 
1. Rupture of the ethane C-H bond on the Zn-O pair
2. Formation of ethene from the alkyl group bound to Zn
3. Formation of dihydrogen from the Brønsted proton and hydrogen bound to Zn

This mechanism demonstrates how the structure of the Zn-exchanged zeolite, particularly the Zn-O pairs, facilitates the activation and transformation of alkanes.

The industrial significance of these catalysts is evident from their use in commercial processes. For example, Zn-doped catalysts are employed in the Cyclar process, which is a commercial application for the aromatization of light hydrocarbons.

The efficiency of these catalysts in alkane transformations, particularly in processes like oxidative dehydrogenation and aromatization, makes them valuable for industrial applications. Their ability to activate C-H bonds and facilitate the formation of value-added products from simple alkanes contributes to their importance in the petrochemical industry.

Zn交换沸石中烷烃活化的机理涉及一系列步骤。第一步是烷烃（如乙烷或丙烷）脱氢生成相应的烯烃。随后是烯烃的脱氢环化寡聚。

乙烷活化的具体机理涉及C-H键的烷基断裂，锌阳离子作为烷基的接受体。催化循环包括三个主要步骤：
1. 在Zn-O对上断裂乙烷C-H键
2. 从与Zn结合的烷基形成乙烯
3. 从Brønsted质子和与Zn结合的氢形成氢气

这一机理展示了Zn交换沸石的结构，特别是Zn-O对，如何促进烷烃的活化和转化。
