<EnglishVersionAnswer>

The composition, structure, and reaction conditions of metal-based catalysts significantly influence the mechanisms and efficiency of alkane transformations. For supported vanadium oxides and metal-exchanged zeolites:

1. The nature and dispersion of the metal oxide phase greatly affect catalyst behavior.

2. In W/HZSM-5 catalysts, three types of supported tungsten species are present: isolated tetrahedral species grafted to Brønsted acid sites, octahedral polymeric species, and WO3 crystallites. 

3. The relative amounts of these species change with tungsten loading, affecting dispersion and catalyst performance.

4. Formaldehyde selectivity is related to the dispersion of the tungsten oxide phase, which is influenced by metal loading.

5. Both active oxygen ions and Brønsted acidic sites on HZSM-5 are active centers for methane activation.

6. The structure of the catalyst affects its ability to activate gas-phase oxygen, which is crucial for the partial oxidation of methane.

7. The dispersion of metal species impacts the number of exposed active sites and, consequently, the catalyst's efficiency.

These factors contribute to the industrial significance of these catalysts by influencing their activity, selectivity, and stability in alkane transformation processes like oxidative dehydrogenation and zeolite-catalyzed reactions. Understanding and optimizing these parameters can lead to more efficient and economically viable industrial processes.

金属基催化剂的组成、结构和反应条件显著影响烷烃转化的机理和效率。对于负载的钒氧化物和金属交换的沸石：

1. 金属氧化物相的性质和分散度极大地影响催化剂的行为。

2. 在W/HZSM-5催化剂中，存在三种支持的钨物种：接枝到Brønsted酸位点的孤立四面体物种、八面体聚合物种和WO3晶体。

3. 这些物种的相对含量随钨负载量的变化而变化，影响分散度和催化剂性能。

4. 甲醛选择性与钨氧化物相的分散度有关，而分散度又受金属负载量的影响。

5. HZSM-5上的活性氧离子和Brønsted酸性位点都是甲烷活化的活性中心。

6. 催化剂的结构影响其活化气相氧的能力，这对甲烷部分氧化至关重要。

7. 金属物种的分散度影响暴露的活性位点数量，从而影响催化剂的效率。

这些因素通过影响烷烃转化过程（如氧化脱氢和沸石催化反应）中催化剂的活性、选择性和稳定性，从而影响这些催化剂的工业重要性。理解和优化这些参数可以导致更高效和经济可行的工业过程。
