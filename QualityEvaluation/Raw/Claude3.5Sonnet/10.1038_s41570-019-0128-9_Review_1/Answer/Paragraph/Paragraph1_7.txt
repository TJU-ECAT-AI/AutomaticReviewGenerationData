Comparative Analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and Vanadium Oxide Catalysts in CO2-ODH Reactions: Performance, Selectivity, and the Influence of Supports and Composition on Reaction Mechanisms and Product Formation

The development of efficient catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) reactions has garnered significant attention due to its potential for sustainable alkene production. This review focuses on comparing the performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, with vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions.

1. Activity and Selectivity:
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, especially Fe3Ni1/CeO2, have demonstrated remarkable performance in CO2-ODH reactions. Flow reactor results indicate that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 achieves high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in the conversion of C2H6 and CO2 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In comparison, V2O5 catalysts dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres can exhibit up to 90% propylene selectivity during 8-h cycles. However, V2O5 catalysts suffer from declining conversion over time, decreasing from 60% to 20% within one cycle [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

2. Reaction Mechanisms:
The reaction mechanisms of these catalyst systems differ significantly. V-based catalysts typically operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts operate through selective C-H cleavage. DFT calculations on mixed FeNi(111) surfaces indicate that the CH bond cleavage pathway is energetically more favorable than CC bond cleavage, leading to preferential dehydrogenation of ethane to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

3. Support Materials:
For both catalyst types, support materials play a significant role in determining catalytic performance. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 as a support is crucial for CO2 activation and helps prevent coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The reducible nature of CeO2 promotes active metal dispersion and facilitates CO2 dissociation through its redox properties [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

4. Composition and Structural Factors:
The composition and structure of these catalysts significantly influence their performance. In FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, the Fe:Ni ratio dramatically affects product selectivity. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CO2-ODH, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 shows the highest yield of CO (with CO selectivity >98%) through dry reforming [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The interaction between Fe and Ni creates distinct active sites that contribute to high selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For V2O5 catalysts, better dispersion on the support leads to improved performance compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

5. Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
V2O5 catalysts face challenges from acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns related to V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show promise in overcoming some of these limitations, offering better stability and resistance to coking. The abundance of reactive lattice O atoms in CeO2 protects the material by readily removing any coke deposits through oxidation [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

Integrative Understanding and Prospective Outlook:
The comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions reveals promising avenues for future research and development. The superior performance of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 in terms of selectivity and stability suggests that further exploration of bimetallic catalysts supported on reducible oxides could lead to significant advancements in CO2-ODH processes.

Future research directions may include:

1. Optimization of FexNiy ratios and dispersion on CeO2 supports to further enhance selectivity and stability.
2. Investigation of other bimetallic combinations supported on CeO2 or similar reducible oxides to identify potentially superior catalysts.
3. In-depth mechanistic studies to elucidate the synergistic effects between Fe and Ni, and their interaction with the CeO2 support.
4. Development of novel synthesis methods to control the surface structure and composition of bimetallic catalysts more precisely.
5. Long-term stability studies and scale-up experiments to assess the industrial viability of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts.
6. Exploration of strategies to mitigate the challenges faced by V2O5 catalysts, such as developing new support materials or modifying surface properties to reduce acidity and improve stability.

These research directions could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and sustainable catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions, contributing to the broader goal of utilizing CO2 as a feedstock in chemical processes.

FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响

CO2辅助氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）反应高效催化剂的开发因其在可持续烯烃生产方面的潜力而受到重视。本综述重点比较了FexNiy/CeO2催化剂（特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2）与钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能。

1. 活性和选择性：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，尤其是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在CO2-ODH反应中表现出卓越的性能。流动反应器结果表明，Fe3Ni1/CeO2在C2H6和CO2转化过程中实现了高C2H4选择性（>75%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5催化剂在8小时循环期间可以展现高达90%的丙烯选择性。然而，V2O5催化剂的转化率随时间下降，在一个循环内从60%降至20%[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

2. 反应机理：
这些催化剂系统的反应机理有显著差异。基于V的催化剂通常通过Mars-van Krevelen机制运作，其中来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起关键作用[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂通过选择性C-H键断裂进行反应。对混合FeNi(111)表面的DFT计算表明，CH键断裂途径在能量上比CC键断裂更有利，导致乙烷优先脱氢形成乙烯[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

3. 支撑材料：
对于这两种类型的催化剂，支撑材料在决定催化性能方面都起着重要作用。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散度[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为支撑材料对CO2活化至关重要，并且由于其释放和恢复氧原子的能力而有助于防止结焦[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。CeO2的可还原性质促进了活性金属的分散，并通过其氧化还原性质促进CO2解离[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

4. 组成和结构因素：
这些催化剂的组成和结构显著影响其性能。在FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中，Fe:Ni比例极大地影响产物选择性。Fe3Ni1/CeO2有利于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2通过干重整显示出最高的CO产率（CO选择性>98%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，有助于提高选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上的更好分散导致比块状V2O5更好的性能[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

5. 挑战和改进潜力：
V2O5催化剂面临酸性位点促进烯烃裂解和V2O5粉尘相关健康问题的挑战[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在克服这些限制方面显示出潜力，提供更好的稳定性和抗结焦能力。CeO2中丰富的反应性晶格O原子通过氧化作用保护材料，可以轻易去除任何焦炭沉积物[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：
FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析揭示了未来研究和开发的有前途途径。Fe3Ni1/CeO2在选择性和稳定性方面的优异性能表明，进一步探索支撑在可还原氧化物上的双金属催化剂可能会导致CO2-ODH过程的重大进展。

未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 优化FexNiy比例和在CeO2支撑上的分散，以进一步提高选择性和稳定性。
2. 研究其他支撑在CeO2或类似可还原氧化物上的双金属组合，以识别潜在的更优催化剂。
3. 深入的机理研究，阐明Fe和Ni之间的协同效应，以及它们与CeO2支撑之间的相互作用。
4. 开发新型合成方法，以更精确地控制双金属催化剂的表面结构和组成。
5. 长期稳定性研究和放大实验，以评估FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的工业可行性。
6. 探索缓解V2O5催化剂面临挑战的策略，如开发新的支撑材料或修改表面性质以降低酸性并提高稳定性。

这些研究方向可能会导致更高效和可持续的CO2-ODH反应催化剂的开发，为更广泛的将CO2作为化学过程原料的目标做出贡献。
