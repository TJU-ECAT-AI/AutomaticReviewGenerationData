<English>
Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions reveals significant differences in performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation.

Activity and Selectivity:
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrate high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in CO2-ODH of ethane [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, V2O5 catalysts can achieve up to 90% propylene selectivity but suffer from declining conversion over time, decreasing from 60% to 20% within one 8-hour cycle [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. However, it's important to note that FeNi/CeO2 catalysts show promising selectivity for oxidative dehydrogenation of ethane (ODEC) with ethylene selectivity up to 30% [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

Reaction Mechanisms:
V-based catalysts typically operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts operate through selective C-H cleavage, with the interaction between Fe and Ni creating distinct active sites that contribute to high selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. DFT calculations indicate that on the mixed FeNi(111) surface, CH bond cleavage is energetically more favorable than CC bond cleavage, leading to preferential dehydrogenation of ethane to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

Support Materials:
For both catalyst types, support materials play a significant role. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 as a support is crucial for CO2 activation and helps prevent coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. CeO2 is specifically chosen for its ability to promote active metal dispersion and facilitate CO2 dissociation through its redox properties [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

Composition and Structure:
The Fe:Ni ratio in FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts dramatically affects product selectivity, with Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favoring CO2-ODH and Fe1Ni3/CeO2 favoring dry reforming [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For V2O5 catalysts, better dispersion on the support leads to improved performance compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The mixed FeNi surface is found to be more favorable for the ODEC pathway compared to Ni-terminated surfaces, suggesting that the composition and structure of the catalyst play a crucial role in determining its performance [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
V2O5 catalysts face challenges from acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns related to V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show promise in overcoming some of these limitations, offering better stability and resistance to coking [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. However, further research is needed to fully understand their potential for improvement and long-term stability.

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: Considering the advancements and findings discussed, there are several promising avenues for future research in CO2-ODH catalysis. The synergistic effects observed in bimetallic FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts suggest that exploring other combinations of transition metals supported on CeO2 or similar reducible oxides could lead to improved catalytic performance. Future studies might focus on fine-tuning the metal ratios and investigating the role of metal-support interactions in determining catalyst stability and selectivity.

Additionally, the differences in reaction mechanisms between V-based and FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts provide an opportunity to develop hybrid systems that combine the best features of both. For instance, incorporating small amounts of vanadium into the FexNiy/CeO2 system might enhance oxygen mobility and further improve selectivity. Computational studies, such as advanced DFT calculations and machine learning approaches, could be employed to predict optimal catalyst compositions and structures before experimental validation.

Furthermore, addressing the stability issues observed in both catalyst types remains a critical area for improvement. Developing strategies to mitigate catalyst deactivation, such as exploring novel support materials or introducing promoters, could significantly enhance the industrial viability of these catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions. Long-term stability studies under realistic operating conditions will be crucial in assessing the practical applicability of these catalytic systems.

Lastly, considering the growing importance of sustainable chemistry, future research should also focus on the life cycle analysis and environmental impact of these catalysts. This holistic approach will ensure that the development of CO2-ODH catalysts aligns with broader goals of green chemistry and sustainable industrial processes.

<Chinese>
FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析揭示了在性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响方面存在显著差异。

活性和选择性：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在乙烷的CO2-ODH反应中表现出高C2H4选择性（>75%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，V2O5催化剂可以达到90%的丙烯选择性，但转化率随时间下降，在8小时的循环内从60%下降到20%[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。然而，值得注意的是，FeNi/CeO2催化剂在乙烷氧化脱氢（ODEC）反应中表现出高达30%的乙烯选择性[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

反应机理：
基于V的催化剂通常通过Mars-van Krevelen机制运作，其中来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起着关键作用[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂通过选择性C-H键断裂运作，Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，有助于高选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。DFT计算表明，在混合FeNi(111)表面上，CH键断裂在能量上比CC键断裂更有利，导致乙烷优先脱氢形成乙烯[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

支撑材料：
对于两种类型的催化剂，支撑材料都起着重要作用。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为支撑材料对CO2活化至关重要，并且由于其释放和恢复氧原子的能力而有助于防止结焦[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。选择CeO2是因为其促进活性金属分散和通过其氧化还原特性促进CO2解离的能力[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

组成和结构：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中Fe:Ni比例显著影响产物选择性，Fe3Ni1/CeO2倾向于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2倾向于干重整[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上的更好分散导致比体相V2O5更好的性能[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。混合FeNi表面比Ni终止表面更有利于ODEC途径，这表明催化剂的组成和结构在决定其性能方面起着关键作用[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

挑战和改进潜力：
V2O5催化剂面临酸性位点促进烯烃裂解和V2O5粉尘相关健康问题的挑战[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在克服这些限制方面显示出希望，提供更好的稳定性和抗结焦能力[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。然而，需要进一步研究以全面了解它们的改进潜力和长期稳定性。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：考虑到讨论的进展和发现，CO2-ODH催化领域未来研究有几个有前景的方向。在双金属FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中观察到的协同效应表明，探索其他过渡金属组合并支持在CeO2或类似可还原氧化物上可能会带来更好的催化性能。未来的研究可能会集中在精细调节金属比例，并研究金属-支撑材料相互作用在决定催化剂稳定性和选择性方面的作用。

此外，基于V的催化剂和FexNiy/CeO2催化剂之间的反应机理差异为开发结合两者最佳特性的混合系统提供了机会。例如，将少量钒掺入FexNiy/CeO2系统可能会增强氧的流动性并进一步提高选择性。可以采用计算研究，如高级DFT计算和机器学习方法，在实验验证之前预测最佳催化剂组成和结构。

此外，解决两种催化剂类型中观察到的稳定性问题仍然是一个关键的改进领域。开发减轻催化剂失活的策略，如探索新型支撑材料或引入助催化剂，可能会显著提高这些催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的工业可行性。在实际运行条件下进行长期稳定性研究对评估这些催化系统的实际适用性至关重要。

最后，考虑到可持续化学的日益重要性，未来的研究还应关注这些催化剂的生命周期分析和环境影响。这种整体方法将确保CO2-ODH催化剂的开发与绿色化学和可持续工业过程的更广泛目标保持一致。
