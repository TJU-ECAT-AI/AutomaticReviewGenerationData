Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation

The development of efficient catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) of light alkanes has gained significant attention due to its potential for producing valuable olefins while utilizing CO2. This review focuses on comparing the performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, with vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions.

1. Activity and Selectivity:
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, especially Fe3Ni1/CeO2, have shown promising results in CO2-ODH reactions. Flow reactor studies indicate that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in the conversion of C2H6 and CO2 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In comparison, vanadium oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres, can achieve up to 90% propylene selectivity during 8-hour cycles. However, V2O5 catalysts suffer from declining conversion over time, decreasing from 60% to 20% within one cycle [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

2. Reaction Mechanisms:
The reaction mechanisms of these catalyst systems differ significantly. V-based catalysts typically operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts operate through selective C-H cleavage, with the interaction between Fe and Ni creating distinct active sites that contribute to high selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. DFT calculations on FeNi/CeO2 catalysts further support this, indicating that on the mixed FeNi(111) surface, CH bond cleavage is energetically more favorable than CC bond cleavage, leading to preferential dehydrogenation of ethane to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

3. Support Materials:
For both catalyst types, support materials play a significant role in determining catalytic performance. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. Supported VOx catalysts are more selective than bulk V2O5 due to VOx-support interactions stabilizing particularly active VOx surface structures [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 as a support is crucial for CO2 activation and helps prevent coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The reducible nature of CeO2 promotes active metal dispersion and facilitates CO2 dissociation through its redox properties [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

4. Composition and Structure:
The composition and structure of these catalysts significantly influence their performance. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, the Fe:Ni ratio dramatically affects product selectivity. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CO2-ODH, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 favors dry reforming with high CO selectivity (>98%) [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The interaction between Fe and Ni creates distinct active sites, leading to higher selectivity compared to monometallic Fe/CeO2 or Ni/CeO2 catalysts [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For V2O5 catalysts, better dispersion on the support leads to improved performance compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

5. Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
Both catalyst systems face unique challenges. V2O5 catalysts struggle with acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns related to V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show promise in overcoming some of these limitations, offering better stability and resistance to coking. The abundance of reactive lattice oxygen atoms in CeO2 helps protect the material by facilitating the removal of coke deposits through oxidation [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:
The comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions reveals distinct advantages and challenges for each system. While V2O5 catalysts have shown high selectivity, they face issues with stability and potential health hazards. On the other hand, FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrate promising performance in terms of selectivity and stability.

Future research directions may focus on:
1. Optimizing the composition and structure of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts to further enhance their selectivity and stability.
2. Investigating the long-term performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts in continuous flow reactors to assess their industrial viability.
3. Exploring novel support materials or modifications to CeO2 to improve CO2 activation and prevent coking in FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts.
4. Developing strategies to mitigate the challenges faced by V2O5 catalysts, such as addressing the acidic sites that promote alkene cracking.
5. Conducting comprehensive life cycle assessments and techno-economic analyses to evaluate the environmental and economic impacts of both catalyst systems in large-scale applications.

By addressing these research areas, we can work towards developing more efficient and sustainable catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions, contributing to the broader goal of utilizing CO2 in value-added chemical production processes.

<Chinese>
FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响

CO2辅助轻烷烃氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）高效催化剂的开发因其在利用CO2的同时生产有价值烯烃的潜力而受到广泛关注。本综述重点比较了FexNiy/CeO2催化剂（特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2）与钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能。

1. 活性和选择性：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，尤其是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在CO2-ODH反应中表现出良好的结果。流动反应器研究表明，Fe3Ni1/CeO2在C2H6和CO2转化中表现出高C2H4选择性（>75%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，钒氧化物催化剂，如分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5，在8小时循环中可达到90%的丙烯选择性。然而，V2O5催化剂存在转化率随时间下降的问题，在一个循环内从60%降至20%[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

2. 反应机理：
这些催化剂系统的反应机理存在显著差异。基于V的催化剂通常通过Mars-van Krevelen机理运作，其中来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起着关键作用[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂通过选择性C-H键断裂进行反应，Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，有助于提高选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对FeNi/CeO2催化剂的DFT计算进一步支持了这一点，表明在混合FeNi(111)表面上，CH键断裂在能量上比CC键断裂更有利，导致乙烷优先脱氢形成乙烯[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

3. 支撑材料：
对于这两种类型的催化剂，支撑材料在决定催化性能方面都起着重要作用。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。负载的VOx催化剂比块状V2O5更具选择性，这是由于VOx-支撑材料相互作用稳定了特别活性的VOx表面结构[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为支撑材料对CO2活化至关重要，并且由于其释放和恢复氧原子的能力而有助于防止结焦[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。CeO2的可还原性质促进了活性金属的分散，并通过其氧化还原性质促进CO2解离[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

4. 组成和结构：
这些催化剂的组成和结构显著影响其性能。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，Fe:Ni比率对产物选择性有显著影响。Fe3Ni1/CeO2有利于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2有利于干重整，具有高CO选择性（>98%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，导致比单金属Fe/CeO2或Ni/CeO2催化剂具有更高的选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上更好的分散性导致比块状V2O5性能更好[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

5. 挑战和改进潜力：
两种催化剂系统都面临着独特的挑战。V2O5催化剂存在酸性位点促进烯烃裂解和V2O5粉尘相关的健康问题[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在克服这些限制方面显示出前景，提供更好的稳定性和抗结焦性。CeO2中丰富的反应性晶格氧原子通过促进焦炭沉积物的氧化去除来帮助保护材料[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：
FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析揭示了每个系统的不同优势和挑战。虽然V2O5催化剂表现出高选择性，但它们面临稳定性和潜在健康危害的问题。另一方面，FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在选择性和稳定性方面表现出有希望的性能。

未来研究方向可能集中在：
1. 优化FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的组成和结构，以进一步提高其选择性和稳定性。
2. 在连续流动反应器中研究FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的长期性能，以评估其工业可行性。
3. 探索新型支撑材料或CeO2的改性，以改善FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中的CO2活化并防止结焦。
4. 开发策略来缓解V2O5催化剂面临的挑战，如解决促进烯烃裂解的酸性位点问题。
5. 进行全面的生命周期评估和技术经济分析，以评估两种催化剂系统在大规模应用中的环境和经济影响。

通过解决这些研究领域，我们可以致力于开发更高效、更可持续的CO2-ODH反应催化剂，为更广泛的CO2增值化学品生产过程目标做出贡献。
