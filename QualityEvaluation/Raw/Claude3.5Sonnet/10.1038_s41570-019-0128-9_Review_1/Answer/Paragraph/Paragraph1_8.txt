Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation

The development of efficient catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) of alkanes is crucial for sustainable chemical production. This review compares the performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, with vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions, focusing on their activity, selectivity, and the role of support materials and composition in determining their performance.

1. Activity and Selectivity:
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, especially Fe3Ni1/CeO2, demonstrate high selectivity in CO2-ODH reactions. For ethane dehydrogenation, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits C2H4 selectivity exceeding 75% [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In comparison, vanadium oxide catalysts, such as V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres, can achieve up to 90% propylene selectivity. However, V2O5 catalysts suffer from declining conversion over time, decreasing from 60% to 20% within one 8-hour cycle [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

2. Reaction Mechanisms:
The reaction mechanisms of these catalysts differ significantly. V-based catalysts typically operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts operate through selective C-H cleavage. DFT calculations indicate that on the mixed FeNi(111) surface, CH bond cleavage is energetically more favorable than CC bond cleavage, leading to preferential dehydrogenation of ethane to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

3. Support Materials:
For both catalyst types, support materials play a significant role in determining their performance. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 as a support is crucial for CO2 activation and helps prevent coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms. The reducible nature of CeO2 promotes active metal dispersion and facilitates CO2 dissociation through its redox properties [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

4. Composition and Structural Factors:
The composition and structure of these catalysts significantly influence their performance. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, the Fe:Ni ratio dramatically affects product selectivity. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CO2-ODH, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 has the highest yield of CO (with CO selectivity >98%) by means of dry reforming [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The interaction between Fe and Ni creates distinct active sites that contribute to high selectivity. For V2O5 catalysts, better dispersion on the support leads to improved performance compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

5. Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
Both catalyst types face unique challenges. V2O5 catalysts struggle with acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns related to V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show promise in overcoming some of these limitations, offering better stability and resistance to coking. The abundance of reactive lattice O atoms in CeO2 protects the material by readily removing any coke deposits through oxidation [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook:
The comparison between FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions reveals distinct advantages and challenges for each system. While V2O5 catalysts have shown high selectivity, their declining activity over time and potential health concerns present significant hurdles. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, offer promising performance with high selectivity and improved stability.

Future research directions may include:

1. Optimizing the composition of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts to further enhance selectivity and stability.
2. Investigating the long-term performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts under industrial conditions.
3. Exploring novel support materials or modifications to CeO2 to improve CO2 activation and prevent coking.
4. Developing in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic behavior of active sites during CO2-ODH reactions.
5. Investigating the potential of combining the best features of both catalyst systems, such as incorporating vanadium into FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts to create hybrid materials with enhanced performance.

These research avenues could lead to the development of more efficient and sustainable catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions, contributing to the broader goal of utilizing CO2 as a feedstock in chemical production.

FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响

开发高效的CO2辅助烷烃氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）催化剂对于可持续化学品生产至关重要。本综述比较了FexNiy/CeO2催化剂（特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2）与钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的性能，重点关注它们的活性、选择性以及支撑材料和组成在决定其性能方面的作用。

1. 活性和选择性：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，尤其是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，在CO2-ODH反应中表现出高选择性。对于乙烷脱氢，Fe3Ni1/CeO2的C2H4选择性超过75% [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，钒氧化物催化剂，如分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5，可以达到90%的丙烯选择性。然而，V2O5催化剂的转化率随时间下降，在一个8小时周期内从60%降至20% [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

2. 反应机理：
这些催化剂的反应机理有显著差异。基于V的催化剂通常通过Mars-van Krevelen机理运作，其中来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起关键作用 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂通过选择性C-H键断裂进行反应。DFT计算表明，在混合FeNi(111)表面上，CH键断裂在能量上比CC键断裂更有利，导致乙烷优先脱氢形成乙烯 [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

3. 支撑材料：
对于这两种类型的催化剂，支撑材料在决定其性能方面都起着重要作用。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散性 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为支撑材料对CO2活化至关重要，并且由于其释放和恢复氧原子的能力，有助于防止结焦。CeO2的可还原性质促进了活性金属的分散，并通过其氧化还原特性促进CO2解离 [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

4. 组成和结构因素：
这些催化剂的组成和结构对其性能有显著影响。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，Fe:Ni比例对产物选择性有显著影响。Fe3Ni1/CeO2有利于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2通过干重整产生最高的CO产率（CO选择性>98%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，有助于高选择性。对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上的更好分散导致比块状V2O5更好的性能 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

5. 挑战和改进潜力：
这两种类型的催化剂都面临独特的挑战。V2O5催化剂面临酸性位点促进烯烃裂解和与V2O5粉尘相关的健康问题 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂显示出克服其中一些限制的前景，提供更好的稳定性和抗结焦能力。CeO2中活性晶格O原子的丰富性通过容易氧化去除任何焦炭沉积来保护材料 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：
比较FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的表现，揭示了每个系统的独特优势和挑战。虽然V2O5催化剂表现出高选择性，但其活性随时间下降和潜在的健康问题构成了重大障碍。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，提供了有前景的性能，具有高选择性和改进的稳定性。

未来研究方向可能包括：

1. 优化FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的组成，以进一步提高选择性和稳定性。
2. 在工业条件下研究FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的长期性能。
3. 探索新型支撑材料或对CeO2的改性，以改善CO2活化和防止结焦。
4. 开发原位表征技术，以更好地理解CO2-ODH反应过程中活性位点的动态行为。
5. 研究结合两种催化剂系统最佳特征的潜力，例如将钒掺入FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中，创造具有增强性能的混合材料。

这些研究途径可能导致开发出更高效、更可持续的CO2-ODH反应催化剂，为更广泛的利用CO2作为化学品生产原料的目标做出贡献。
