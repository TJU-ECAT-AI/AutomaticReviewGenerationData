Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation

The development of efficient catalysts for CO2-assisted oxidative dehydrogenation (CO2-ODH) reactions has gained significant attention due to its potential for sustainable production of valuable olefins. This review focuses on the comparison between FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, and vanadium oxide catalysts in terms of their performance, selectivity, and underlying reaction mechanisms.

1. Activity and Selectivity:
FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts have shown promising results in CO2-ODH reactions. Flow reactor studies indicate that Fe3Ni1/CeO2 exhibits high C2H4 selectivity (>75%) in the conversion of C2H6 and CO2 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. In contrast, vanadium oxide catalysts, specifically V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres, can achieve up to 90% propylene selectivity during 8-hour cycles. However, the conversion for V2O5 catalysts decreases from 60% to 20% within one cycle, indicating potential stability issues [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

2. Reaction Mechanisms:
The reaction mechanisms of these catalysts differ significantly. V-based catalysts primarily operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism, where oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. On the other hand, Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts operate through selective C-H cleavage. DFT calculations on mixed FeNi(111) surfaces indicate that the pathway for selective CH bond cleavage is energetically more favorable than CC bond cleavage, leading to preferential dehydrogenation of ethane to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

3. Support Materials and Their Influence:
For both catalyst types, support materials play a significant role in determining catalytic performance. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. Supported VOx catalysts are more selective than bulk V2O5 due to VOx-support interactions that can stabilize particularly active VOx surface structures [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 as a support is crucial for CO2 activation and helps prevent coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The reducible CeO2 oxide promotes active metal dispersion and facilitates CO2 dissociation through its ability to shift easily between reduced and oxidized states [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

4. Composition and Structural Factors:
The composition and structure of these catalysts significantly influence their performance. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, the Fe:Ni ratio dramatically affects product selectivity. Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CO2-ODH, while Fe1Ni3/CeO2 has the highest yield of CO (with CO selectivity >98%) by means of dry reforming [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. The interaction between Fe and Ni creates distinct active sites that contribute to high selectivity in CO2-ODH reactions [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

For V2O5 catalysts, better dispersion on the support leads to improved performance compared to bulk V2O5 [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

5. Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
V2O5 catalysts face challenges from acidic sites promoting alkene cracking and health concerns related to V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show promise in overcoming some of these limitations, offering better stability and resistance to coking. The abundance of reactive lattice O atoms in CeO2 protects the material by readily removing any coke deposits through oxidation [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: 
Considering the advancements and findings discussed, there are several promising directions for future research in CO2-ODH catalysis. The superior performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, in terms of selectivity and stability opens up possibilities for further optimization of bimetallic catalysts. Future studies could focus on:

1. Fine-tuning the Fe:Ni ratio and exploring other bimetallic combinations to enhance selectivity and stability further.
2. Investigating the long-term stability of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts under industrial conditions.
3. Developing advanced characterization techniques to better understand the nature of active sites and the role of metal-support interactions in these catalysts.
4. Exploring the potential of combining the best features of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts, possibly through the development of hybrid or multi-component catalysts.
5. Investigating the scalability and economic viability of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts for industrial applications.

These research directions could potentially lead to the development of more efficient and sustainable catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions, contributing to the broader goal of utilizing CO2 as a feedstock in the chemical industry.

FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响

CO2辅助氧化脱氢（CO2-ODH）反应高效催化剂的开发因其在可持续生产有价值烯烃方面的潜力而受到广泛关注。本综述重点比较了FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2，与钒氧化物催化剂在性能、选择性和基本反应机理方面的差异。

1. 活性和选择性：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中表现出良好的结果。流动反应器研究表明，Fe3Ni1/CeO2在C2H6和CO2转化中表现出高C2H4选择性（>75%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。相比之下，钒氧化物催化剂，特别是分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5，在8小时循环中可以达到90%的丙烯选择性。然而，V2O5催化剂的转化率在一个循环内从60%下降到20%，表明可能存在稳定性问题[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

2. 反应机理：
这些催化剂的反应机理存在显著差异。V基催化剂主要通过Mars-van Krevelen机理运作，其中来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起着关键作用[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。另一方面，Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂通过选择性C-H键断裂进行反应。对混合FeNi(111)表面的DFT计算表明，选择性CH键断裂的路径在能量上比CC键断裂更有利，导致乙烷优先脱氢形成乙烯[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

3. 支撑材料及其影响：
对于这两种类型的催化剂，支撑材料在决定催化性能方面都起着重要作用。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。由于VOx-支撑材料相互作用可以稳定特别活性的VOx表面结构，支撑的VOx催化剂比块状V2O5更具选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为支撑材料对CO2活化至关重要，并且由于其释放和回收氧原子的能力而有助于防止结焦[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。可还原的CeO2氧化物促进活性金属分散，并通过其在还原和氧化状态之间容易转换的能力促进CO2解离[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

4. 组成和结构因素：
这些催化剂的组成和结构显著影响其性能。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，Fe:Ni比例对产物选择性有显著影响。Fe3Ni1/CeO2有利于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2通过干重整具有最高的CO产率（CO选择性>98%）[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。Fe和Ni之间的相互作用创造了独特的活性位点，有助于在CO2-ODH反应中实现高选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上的更好分散导致比块状V2O5更好的性能[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

5. 挑战和改进潜力：
V2O5催化剂面临酸性位点促进烯烃裂解和V2O5粉尘相关健康问题的挑战[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在克服这些限制方面显示出前景，提供更好的稳定性和抗结焦能力。CeO2中丰富的反应性晶格O原子通过容易氧化去除任何焦炭沉积物来保护材料[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：
考虑到讨论的进展和发现，CO2-ODH催化领域的未来研究有几个有希望的方向。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，特别是Fe3Ni1/CeO2在选择性和稳定性方面的优异性能，为进一步优化双金属催化剂开辟了可能性。未来的研究可以集中在：

1. 精细调整Fe:Ni比例并探索其他双金属组合，以进一步提高选择性和稳定性。
2. 在工业条件下研究FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的长期稳定性。
3. 开发先进的表征技术，以更好地理解这些催化剂中活性位点的性质和金属-支撑材料相互作用的作用。
4. 探索结合FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂最佳特性的潜力，可能通过开发混合或多组分催化剂。
5. 研究FexNiy/CeO2催化剂在工业应用中的可扩展性和经济可行性。

这些研究方向可能导致开发出更高效、更可持续的CO2-ODH反应催化剂，为化学工业中利用CO2作为原料的更广泛目标做出贡献。
