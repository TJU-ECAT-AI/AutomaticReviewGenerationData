Comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions: Performance, selectivity, and the influence of supports and composition on reaction mechanisms and product formation

Recent advancements in catalytic materials for CO2-mediated oxidative dehydrogenation (ODH) reactions have highlighted the potential of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, particularly Fe3Ni1/CeO2, as promising alternatives to traditional vanadium oxide catalysts. This comparative analysis explores the performance, selectivity, and underlying mechanisms of these two catalyst types, emphasizing the roles of support materials, composition, and structural factors in determining their efficacy.

1. Activity and Selectivity:
Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts have demonstrated remarkable performance in CO2-ODH reactions, achieving C2H4 selectivity exceeding 75% in ethane dehydrogenation [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. This high selectivity is attributed to the synergistic interaction between Fe and Ni, creating distinct active sites that promote selective C-H bond cleavage. In contrast, vanadium oxide catalysts, particularly V2O5 dispersed in mesoporous SiO2 nanospheres, can achieve up to 90% propylene selectivity. However, V2O5 catalysts suffer from declining conversion rates, decreasing from 60% to 20% within a single 8-hour cycle [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

2. Reaction Mechanisms:
The superior performance of FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts is rooted in their unique reaction mechanism. DFT calculations reveal that on mixed FeNi(111) surfaces, the pathway for selective C-H bond cleavage is energetically more favorable than C-C bond cleavage, leading to preferential ethane dehydrogenation to form ethylene [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]. This mechanism differs significantly from vanadium-based catalysts, which typically operate through a Mars-van Krevelen mechanism. In V-based systems, oxygen atoms from the support play a crucial role in improving alkene selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

3. Support Materials and Their Influence:
The choice of support material significantly impacts catalyst performance for both FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide systems. In V2O5 catalysts, the support affects reducibility, acid-base properties, and dispersion, ultimately influencing catalytic activity and selectivity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. For FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts, CeO2 serves as a crucial component, facilitating CO2 activation and preventing coking due to its ability to release and recover oxygen atoms. The reducible nature of CeO2 promotes active metal dispersion and accommodates CO2 dissociation, enhancing overall catalyst performance [10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004].

4. Composition and Structural Factors:
The Fe:Ni ratio in FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts plays a pivotal role in determining product selectivity. While Fe3Ni1/CeO2 favors CO2-ODH, Fe1Ni3/CeO2 demonstrates a higher propensity for dry reforming, achieving CO selectivity exceeding 98% [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. This compositional sensitivity highlights the importance of precise catalyst design in tailoring reaction outcomes. For V2O5 catalysts, improved dispersion on the support leads to enhanced performance compared to bulk V2O5, emphasizing the significance of structural considerations in catalyst development [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

5. Challenges and Potential for Improvement:
Despite their promising performance, both catalyst systems face unique challenges. V2O5 catalysts struggle with acidic sites that promote undesired alkene cracking, and there are health concerns associated with V2O5 dust [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]. FexNiy/CeO2 catalysts show potential in overcoming some of these limitations, offering improved stability and resistance to coking. The abundance of reactive lattice oxygen atoms in CeO2-supported catalysts facilitates the removal of coke deposits through oxidation, enhancing catalyst longevity [10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1].

※※※
Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The comparative analysis of FexNiy/CeO2 and vanadium oxide catalysts in CO2-ODH reactions reveals promising avenues for future research and development. The superior selectivity and stability of Fe3Ni1/CeO2 catalysts, coupled with their unique reaction mechanism, suggest potential for further optimization. Future studies could focus on fine-tuning the Fe:Ni ratio and exploring novel support materials to enhance catalytic performance and longevity. Additionally, the development of in-situ characterization techniques could provide deeper insights into the dynamic behavior of these catalysts under reaction conditions, potentially leading to the design of more efficient and selective catalytic systems. The integration of advanced computational modeling with experimental studies may also accelerate the discovery of new, high-performance bimetallic catalysts for CO2-ODH reactions, potentially expanding the scope beyond ethane and propane to longer-chain hydrocarbons.

FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析：性能、选择性以及支撑材料和组成对反应机理和产物形成的影响

1. 活性和选择性：
Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中表现出显著的性能，在乙烷脱氢反应中实现了超过75%的C2H4选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。这种高选择性归因于Fe和Ni之间的协同作用，创造了促进选择性C-H键断裂的独特活性位点。相比之下，钒氧化物催化剂，特别是分散在介孔SiO2纳米球中的V2O5，可以达到高达90%的丙烯选择性。然而，V2O5催化剂存在转化率下降的问题，在单个8小时循环内从60%降至20%[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

2. 反应机理：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂的卓越性能源于其独特的反应机理。DFT计算表明，在混合FeNi(111)表面上，选择性C-H键断裂的路径在能量上比C-C键断裂更有利，导致优先将乙烷脱氢形成乙烯[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。这种机理与钒基催化剂有显著不同，后者通常通过Mars-van Krevelen机理运作。在V基系统中，来自支撑材料的氧原子在提高烯烃选择性方面起着关键作用[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

3. 支撑材料及其影响：
对于FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物系统，支撑材料的选择对催化剂性能有显著影响。在V2O5催化剂中，支撑材料影响还原性、酸碱性质和分散性，最终影响催化活性和选择性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。对于FexNiy/CeO2催化剂，CeO2作为关键组件，促进CO2活化并防止结焦，这得益于其释放和恢复氧原子的能力。CeO2的可还原性质促进了活性金属的分散和CO2的解离，提高了整体催化剂性能[10.1016/j.jcat.2016.02.004]。

4. 组成和结构因素：
FexNiy/CeO2催化剂中的Fe:Ni比例在决定产物选择性方面起着关键作用。Fe3Ni1/CeO2倾向于CO2-ODH，而Fe1Ni3/CeO2表现出更高的干重整倾向，CO选择性超过98%[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。这种组成敏感性突显了精确催化剂设计在调整反应结果方面的重要性。对于V2O5催化剂，在支撑材料上的改进分散性导致了比体相V2O5更优的性能，强调了结构考虑在催化剂开发中的重要性[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

5. 挑战和改进潜力：
尽管表现出令人鼓舞的性能，但两种催化剂系统都面临独特的挑战。V2O5催化剂存在促进不期望的烯烃裂解的酸性位点问题，并且还有与V2O5粉尘相关的健康问题[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。FexNiy/CeO2催化剂显示出克服这些限制的潜力，提供了改进的稳定性和抗结焦能力。CeO2支撑的催化剂中丰富的活性晶格氧原子通过氧化促进了焦炭沉积物的去除，提高了催化剂的寿命[10.1038/s41570-019-0128-9_1]。

※※※
综合理解与未来展望：FexNiy/CeO2和钒氧化物催化剂在CO2-ODH反应中的比较分析揭示了未来研究和发展的有希望方向。Fe3Ni1/CeO2催化剂的卓越选择性和稳定性，加上其独特的反应机理，表明了进一步优化的潜力。未来的研究可以集中在微调Fe:Ni比例和探索新型支撑材料上，以提高催化性能和寿命。此外，原位表征技术的开发可以提供对这些催化剂在反应条件下动态行为的更深入了解，可能导致设计更高效和选择性的催化系统。将先进的计算模型与实验研究相结合，也可能加速发现新的、高性能的双金属催化剂用于CO2-ODH反应，可能将范围扩大到乙烷和丙烷以外的长链烃类。
