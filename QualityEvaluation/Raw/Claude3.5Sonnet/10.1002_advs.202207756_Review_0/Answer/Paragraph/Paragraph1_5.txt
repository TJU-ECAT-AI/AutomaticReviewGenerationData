The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This shift is driven by several factors:

1. Increasing global demand: Propylene consumption reached 90 million tons in 2015 and is expected to grow to 191 megatons by 2030 [10.1002/cctc.201500916] [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].

2. Changes in feedstock availability: The shale gas boom has led to a shift from naphtha to propane as a feedstock, creating a propylene supply gap [10.1039/d0cs00983k].

3. Limitations of traditional methods: Conventional processes like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking (FCC) cannot meet the growing demand [10.1039/d0cs00814a].

PDH has emerged as a promising alternative, currently accounting for about 10% of global propylene production [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]. The development of efficient catalysts for PDH has been a key focus area, with Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts receiving significant attention.

The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved considerably over time:

1. Initially, Ga/H-ZSM-5 was used as a bifunctional catalyst for propane aromatization in the commercial Cyclar Process [10.1039/d1gc03700e].

2. Research has shown that Ga can catalyze propane dehydrogenation through various species, including Ga+, [GaO]+, [Ga(OH)2]+, [GaOH]2+, [GaH]2+, [GaH2]+, and Ga+H+ pairs [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].

There is an ongoing debate about the nature of active Ga species in PDH catalysts:

1. Some researchers propose that single [GaH]2+ sites are the active species [10.1039/d1sc05866e].

2. Others suggest that Ga+H+ pair sites are the stable active species [10.1039/d1sc05866e].

3. Recent studies have identified Lewis(Ga+)-Brønsted acid pairs as the active sites for propane dehydrogenation over Ga/H-ZSM-5 [10.1039/d0cs00983k].

Recent research findings have contributed significantly to our current knowledge of these catalysts:

1. The Ga speciation and PDH performance are dependent on both Ga/Al ratios and Al distributions within the zeolites [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].

2. Supports like ZSM-5, Al2O3, and SBA-15 can strengthen the interaction between Ga species and the support, influencing catalytic performance [10.1039/c6ra27204e].

3. Lowering surface acidity can enhance catalytic performance. For example, the addition of 3-mercaptopropyl-trimethoxysilane (MPS) to Ga/MFI catalysts reduces Brønsted acid sites and increases Lewis acid sites, resulting in improved performance [10.1039/d1gc03700e].

4. Ga-exchanged species associated with Al-pairs in Ga/H-MFI catalyze both propane and butane dehydrogenation, while isolated Ga species did not show this activity [10.1039/d1sc05866e].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The evolution of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH reflects the complex interplay between various factors, including Ga speciation, support materials, and acid-base properties. Future research may focus on:

1. Advanced characterization techniques: Developing in-situ and operando methods to better understand the dynamic nature of active sites under reaction conditions.

2. Tailored catalyst design: Utilizing the knowledge of Ga speciation and support interactions to create more efficient and stable catalysts.

3. Multifunctional catalysts: Exploring the synergistic effects of combining Ga with other elements to enhance PDH performance.

4. Sustainability: Investigating more environmentally friendly preparation methods and exploring the potential of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts in other reactions related to the valorization of light alkanes.

These research directions could lead to more efficient PDH processes, helping to bridge the growing gap between propylene supply and demand while addressing sustainability concerns in the chemical industry.

<Chinese>
日益增长的丙烯需求显著影响了新生产方法的发展，特别是丙烷脱氢（PDH）。这一转变由几个因素驱动：

1. 全球需求增加：丙烯消耗量在2015年达到9000万吨，预计到2030年将增至1.91亿吨 [10.1002/cctc.201500916] [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。

2. 原料供应变化：页岩气热潮导致从石脑油向丙烷作为原料的转变，造成丙烯供应缺口 [10.1039/d0cs00983k]。

3. 传统方法的局限性：蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化（FCC）等常规工艺无法满足日益增长的需求 [10.1039/d0cs00814a]。

PDH已成为一种有前景的替代方法，目前占全球丙烯产量的约10% [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。开发高效的PDH催化剂一直是研究的重点领域，其中Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂受到了广泛关注。

对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的理解随时间不断发展：

1. 最初，Ga/H-ZSM-5作为双功能催化剂用于商业Cyclar工艺中的丙烷芳构化 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。

2. 研究表明，Ga可以通过多种物种催化丙烷脱氢，包括Ga+、[GaO]+、[Ga(OH)2]+、[GaOH]2+、[GaH]2+、[GaH2]+和Ga+H+对 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。

关于PDH催化剂中活性Ga物种的本质，仍存在争议：

1. 一些研究者提出单一的[GaH]2+位是活性物种 [10.1039/d1sc05866e]。

2. 其他人则认为Ga+H+配对位是稳定的活性物种 [10.1039/d1sc05866e]。

3. 最近的研究将Lewis(Ga+)-Brønsted酸对确定为Ga/H-ZSM-5上丙烷脱氢的活性位点 [10.1039/d0cs00983k]。

近期研究发现对我们当前对这些催化剂的了解做出了重要贡献：

1. Ga物种分布和PDH性能取决于Ga/Al比例和沸石中Al的分布 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。

2. ZSM-5、Al2O3和SBA-15等载体可以增强Ga物种与载体之间的相互作用，影响催化性能 [10.1039/c6ra27204e]。

3. 降低表面酸度可以提高催化性能。例如，向Ga/MFI催化剂中添加3-巯丙基三甲氧基硅烷（MPS）可以减少Brønsted酸位并增加Lewis酸位，从而提高性能 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。

4. 与Ga/H-MFI中Al对相关的Ga交换物种可以同时催化丙烷和丁烷脱氢，而孤立的Ga物种则没有表现出这种活性 [10.1039/d1sc05866e]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的演变反映了Ga物种、载体材料和酸碱性质等多种因素之间的复杂相互作用。未来的研究可能集中在：

1. 先进表征技术：开发原位和操作条件下的方法，以更好地理解反应条件下活性位点的动态特性。

2. 定制催化剂设计：利用对Ga物种和载体相互作用的了解，创造更高效、更稳定的催化剂。

3. 多功能催化剂：探索将Ga与其他元素结合的协同效应，以提高PDH性能。

4. 可持续性：研究更环保的制备方法，并探索Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在轻烷烃增值相关反应中的潜力。

这些研究方向可能导致更高效的PDH工艺，有助于弥合不断扩大的丙烯供需缺口，同时解决化工行业的可持续性问题。
