<English>
The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This shift is driven by several factors:

1. Global propylene demand is expected to reach 191 megatons by 2030, up from 130 megatons in 2019 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].
2. Traditional production methods like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking (FCC) can no longer meet the increasing demand [10.1039/d0cs00814a].
3. The availability of low-cost propane from shale gas has made PDH more economically attractive [10.1002/cctc.201500916].

PDH has emerged as a promising on-purpose propylene production technology, currently accounting for about 10% of global propylene production [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].

The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved significantly over time. Initially, Ga/H-ZSM-5 was primarily used as a bifunctional catalyst for propane aromatization in the commercial Cyclar Process [10.1039/d1gc03700e]. However, research has shown that Ga can catalyze propane dehydrogenation through various species.

There is an ongoing debate about the nature of active Ga species in PDH catalysts. Different studies have proposed various active sites:

1. Tetrahedrally coordinated Ga3+ species are considered active sites for C-H activation [10.1039/d1gc03700e].
2. [GaH]2+ ions, together with a basic framework oxygen of the Brønsted-acid site, have been identified as active sites [10.1039/d0cs00814a].
3. Lewis(Ga+)-Brønsted acid pairs have been proposed as active sites for propane dehydrogenation over Ga/H-ZSM-5 [10.1039/d0cs00983k].
4. Bell and coworkers believe that single [GaH]2+ sites are the active species, while Schreiber et al. reported that Ga+H+ pair sites are the stable active sites [10.1039/d1sc05866e].

Recent research findings have contributed to our current knowledge of these catalysts:

1. The Ga speciation and PDH performance are dependent on both Ga/Al ratios and Al distributions of the zeolites [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].
2. Supports like ZSM-5, Al2O3, and SBA-15 can strengthen the interaction between gallium species and the support, unlike SiO2 [10.1039/c6ra27204e].
3. Lowering surface acidity can enhance catalytic performance. For example, the addition of 3-mercaptopropyl-trimethoxysilane (MPS) to Ga/MFI catalysts reduces Brønsted acid sites and increases Lewis acid sites, resulting in improved performance [10.1039/d1gc03700e].
4. Ga-exchanged species associated with Al-pairs in Ga/H-MFI catalyze both propane and butane dehydrogenation, while isolated Ga species did not show this activity [10.1039/d1sc05866e].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The evolving landscape of propylene production, particularly through PDH, represents a significant shift in the petrochemical industry. The ongoing research into Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts highlights the complexity of heterogeneous catalysis and the importance of understanding the interplay between different species in catalyst performance.

Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. Developing in-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic nature of active sites under reaction conditions.
2. Exploring the synergistic effects between different Ga species and support materials to optimize catalyst performance.
3. Investigating the potential of novel preparation methods to control the distribution and nature of active Ga species.
4. Studying the long-term stability and regeneration of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts under industrial conditions.
5. Exploring the potential of combining Ga with other elements to create more efficient and stable PDH catalysts.

These research directions could lead to the development of more efficient, stable, and selective catalysts for PDH, ultimately helping to meet the growing global demand for propylene while addressing environmental and economic concerns.

<Chinese>
日益增长的丙烯需求极大地推动了新的生产方法的发展，尤其是丙烷脱氢（PDH）。这一转变由几个因素驱动：

1. 全球丙烯需求预计将从2019年的1.3亿吨增至2030年的1.91亿吨 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。
2. 传统的生产方法如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化（FCC）已无法满足不断增长的需求 [10.1039/d0cs00814a]。
3. 来自页岩气的低成本丙烷使PDH在经济上更具吸引力 [10.1002/cctc.201500916]。

PDH已成为一种有前景的定向丙烯生产技术，目前占全球丙烯产量的约10% [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。

对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的理解随时间显著演变。最初，Ga/H-ZSM-5主要用作商业Cyclar工艺中丙烷芳构化的双功能催化剂 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。然而，研究表明Ga可以通过各种物种催化丙烷脱氢。

关于PDH催化剂中活性Ga物种的性质仍存在争议。不同的研究提出了各种活性位点：

1. 四面体配位的Ga3+物种被认为是C-H活化的活性位点 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。
2. [GaH]2+离子与Brønsted酸位点的基本骨架氧一起被确定为活性位点 [10.1039/d0cs00814a]。
3. Lewis(Ga+)-Brønsted酸对被提出作为Ga/H-ZSM-5上丙烷脱氢的活性位点 [10.1039/d0cs00983k]。
4. Bell及其同事认为单一[GaH]2+位点是活性物种，而Schreiber等人报告Ga+H+配对位点是稳定的活性位点 [10.1039/d1sc05866e]。

最近的研究发现为我们对这些催化剂的认知做出了贡献：

1. Ga物种和PDH性能依赖于Ga/Al比例和沸石中Al的分布 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。
2. ZSM-5、Al2O3和SBA-15等载体可以增强镓物种与载体之间的相互作用，而SiO2则不行 [10.1039/c6ra27204e]。
3. 降低表面酸度可以提高催化性能。例如，向Ga/MFI催化剂中添加3-巯丙基三甲氧基硅烷（MPS）可减少Brønsted酸位点并增加Lewis酸位点，从而提高性能 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。
4. 与Ga/H-MFI中Al对相关的Ga交换物种可催化丙烷和丁烷脱氢，而孤立的Ga物种则不显示此活性 [10.1039/d1sc05866e]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：丙烯生产格局的演变，特别是通过PDH，代表了石化行业的重大转变。对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的持续研究突显了多相催化的复杂性，以及理解催化剂性能中不同物种相互作用的重要性。

未来的研究努力可能集中在：

1. 开发原位表征技术，以更好地理解反应条件下活性位点的动态性质。
2. 探索不同Ga物种与载体材料之间的协同效应，以优化催化剂性能。
3. 研究新型制备方法，以控制活性Ga物种的分布和性质。
4. 研究Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在工业条件下的长期稳定性和再生。
5. 探索将Ga与其他元素结合以创造更高效、更稳定的PDH催化剂的潜力。

这些研究方向可能导致开发出更高效、更稳定和更选择性的PDH催化剂，最终有助于满足不断增长的全球丙烯需求，同时解决环境和经济问题。
