<English>
The growing demand for propylene has significantly influenced the development of new production methods, particularly propane dehydrogenation (PDH). This shift is driven by several factors:

1. Global propylene demand is expected to reach 191 megatons by 2030, up from 130 megatons in 2019 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].
2. Traditional production methods like steam cracking and fluid catalytic cracking (FCC) cannot meet this increasing demand [10.1126/science.abe7935].
3. The availability of low-cost propane from shale gas has made PDH more commercially attractive [10.1002/anie.201800123].

PDH has emerged as a promising alternative, currently accounting for about 10% of global propylene production [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]. However, the development of efficient catalysts for PDH remains a significant challenge.

The understanding of Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts for PDH has evolved considerably over time. Initially, Ga/H-ZSM-5 was used as a bifunctional catalyst for propane aromatization in the commercial Cyclar Process [10.1039/d1gc03700e]. Recent research has focused on elucidating the nature of active Ga species and their role in the dehydrogenation mechanism.

There is an ongoing debate about the active Ga species in PDH catalysts. Various Ga species have been proposed as active centers, including:

1. Ga+, [GaO]+, [Ga(OH)2]+, [GaOH]2+, [GaH]2+, [GaH2]+, and Ga+H+ pairs [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].
2. Tetrahedrally coordinated Ga3+ species [10.1039/d1gc03700e].
3. [GaH]2+ ions together with a basic framework oxygen of the Brønsted-acid site [10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012].

Recent research findings have contributed significantly to our current knowledge of these catalysts:

1. Lercher's group found that in Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts, there is a very low enthalpy barrier of 56 kJ·mol^-1 for heterolytically activating the propane C-H bond on [GaH]2+ species [10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012].
2. Studies have shown that both Ga speciation and PDH performance are dependent on the Ga/Al ratios and Al distributions within the zeolites [10.1002/advs.202207756_0].
3. Research has demonstrated that supports like ZSM-5, Al2O3, and SBA-15 can strengthen the interaction between gallium species and the support, influencing catalytic performance [10.1039/c6ra27204e].
4. The addition of 3-mercaptopropyl-trimethoxysilane (MPS) to Ga/MFI catalysts has been found to reduce Brønsted acid sites and increase Lewis acid sites, resulting in improved performance [10.1039/d1gc03700e].

Despite these advancements, the precise nature of the active sites under reaction conditions and the roles of Brønsted and Lewis acid sites in Ga-containing MFI catalysts remain subjects of ongoing research and debate [10.1039/d0cs00983k].

※※※

Integrative understanding and prospective outlook: The evolving landscape of propylene production, particularly through PDH, represents a significant shift in the petrochemical industry. The ongoing research into Ga/H-ZSM-5 catalysts highlights the complexity of heterogeneous catalysis and the challenges in designing efficient, stable, and selective catalysts.

Future research endeavors might focus on:

1. In-situ characterization techniques to better understand the dynamic behavior of Ga species under reaction conditions.
2. Developing novel synthesis methods to control the distribution and nature of active Ga species on different supports.
3. Exploring the synergistic effects between Ga species and other elements to enhance catalyst performance.
4. Investigating the potential of machine learning and high-throughput experimentation to accelerate catalyst discovery and optimization.
5. Addressing the challenge of catalyst deactivation, potentially through innovative regeneration methods or by designing more coke-resistant catalysts.

These research directions could lead to more efficient PDH processes, helping to bridge the growing gap between propylene supply and demand while also contributing to more sustainable chemical production methods.

<Chinese>
由于对丙烯的需求不断增长，促进了新的生产方法的发展，特别是丙烷脱氢（PDH）。这一转变是由几个因素驱动的：

1. 预计到2030年，全球丙烯需求将从2019年的1.3亿吨增加到1.91亿吨 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。
2. 传统的生产方法如蒸汽裂解和流化催化裂化（FCC）无法满足这种日益增长的需求 [10.1126/science.abe7935]。
3. 从页岩气中获得的低成本丙烷使PDH在商业上更具吸引力 [10.1002/anie.201800123]。

PDH已成为一种有前景的替代方法，目前占全球丙烯产量的约10% [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。然而，开发高效的PDH催化剂仍然是一个重大挑战。

对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂在PDH中的理解已经随时间的推移而显著发展。最初，Ga/H-ZSM-5被用作商业Cyclar工艺中丙烷芳构化的双功能催化剂 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。近期研究集中在阐明活性Ga物种的性质及其在脱氢机理中的作用。

关于PDH催化剂中活性Ga物种的争论仍在继续。已经提出了各种Ga物种作为活性中心，包括：

1. Ga+, [GaO]+, [Ga(OH)2]+, [GaOH]2+, [GaH]2+, [GaH2]+, 和Ga+H+对 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。
2. 四面体配位的Ga3+物种 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。
3. [GaH]2+离子与Brønsted酸位点的碱性骨架氧结合 [10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012]。

近期研究结果对我们对这些催化剂的当前认知做出了重大贡献：

1. Lercher小组发现，在Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂中，在[GaH]2+物种上异裂活化丙烷C-H键的焓垒很低，仅为56 kJ·mol^-1 [10.1021/accountsmr.0c00012]。
2. 研究表明，Ga物种分布和PDH性能都依赖于沸石中的Ga/Al比例和Al分布 [10.1002/advs.202207756_0]。
3. 研究证明，ZSM-5、Al2O3和SBA-15等载体可以增强镓物种与载体之间的相互作用，从而影响催化性能 [10.1039/c6ra27204e]。
4. 向Ga/MFI催化剂中添加3-巯丙基三甲氧基硅烷（MPS）可以减少Brønsted酸位点并增加Lewis酸位点，从而提高性能 [10.1039/d1gc03700e]。

尽管取得了这些进展，但在反应条件下活性位点的精确性质以及Ga含MFI催化剂中Brønsted和Lewis酸位点的作用仍然是正在进行的研究和争论的主题 [10.1039/d0cs00983k]。

※※※

综合理解与未来展望：丙烯生产格局的演变，特别是通过PDH，代表了石油化工行业的重大转变。对Ga/H-ZSM-5催化剂的持续研究突显了多相催化的复杂性，以及在设计高效、稳定和选择性催化剂方面的挑战。

未来的研究努力可能集中在：

1. 开发原位表征技术，以更好地理解反应条件下Ga物种的动态行为。
2. 开发新型合成方法，以控制不同载体上活性Ga物种的分布和性质。
3. 探索Ga物种与其他元素之间的协同效应，以增强催化剂性能。
4. 研究机器学习和高通量实验在加速催化剂发现和优化方面的潜力。
5. 解决催化剂失活的挑战，可能通过创新的再生方法或设计更耐焦的催化剂。

这些研究方向可能导致更高效的PDH过程，有助于缩小丙烯供需之间不断扩大的差距，同时也为更可持续的化学品生产方法做出贡献。
