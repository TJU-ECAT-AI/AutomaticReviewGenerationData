The study employs several key characterization methods to verify dopant incorporation and examine structural changes in metal oxide catalysts:

1. Temperature-Programmed Reduction X-ray Absorption Near Edge Spectroscopy (TPR-XANES): This technique provides direct insights into the reduction behavior of the catalysts, revealing how dopants affect ceria reducibility and the extent of surface and bulk reduction.

2. X-ray Absorption Fine Structure (XAFS) analysis: Used in conjunction with TPR-XANES, this method allows for quantitative analysis of Ce oxidation states through linear combination fitting of XANES spectra.

3. Diffuse Reflectance Fourier Transform Infrared Spectroscopy (DRIFTS): This technique is employed to study the decomposition kinetics of surface formate species, providing information on how dopants influence reaction intermediates.

4. Catalytic activity testing: Performance evaluation in water-gas shift reaction conditions helps correlate structural changes with catalytic behavior.

These techniques collectively address the challenges in establishing structure-performance relationships by:

1. Providing direct evidence of dopant-induced changes in catalyst reducibility and oxygen mobility.
2. Offering quantitative data on changes in cerium oxidation states.
3. Revealing the impact of dopants on reaction intermediate stability and reactivity.
4. Correlating structural modifications with catalytic performance.

However, to fully rule out alternative explanations and confirm homogeneous dopant distribution, additional characterization methods such as high-resolution transmission electron microscopy (HRTEM) with elemental mapping, X-ray photoelectron spectroscopy (XPS) for surface composition analysis, and extended X-ray absorption fine structure (EXAFS) for local structure determination would be beneficial.

<ChineseVersionAnswer>

该研究采用了几种关键的表征方法来验证掺杂剂的引入并检查金属氧化物催化剂的结构变化：

1. 程序升温还原X射线吸收近边结构光谱（TPR-XANES）：这种技术直接揭示了催化剂的还原行为，显示了掺杂剂如何影响氧化铈的可还原性以及表面和体相还原的程度。

2. X射线吸收精细结构（XAFS）分析：与TPR-XANES结合使用，这种方法通过XANES光谱的线性组合拟合，可以对Ce的氧化态进行定量分析。

3. 漫反射傅里叶变换红外光谱（DRIFTS）：这种技术用于研究表面甲酸盐物种的分解动力学，提供了掺杂剂如何影响反应中间体的信息。

4. 催化活性测试：在水煤气变换反应条件下进行性能评估，有助于将结构变化与催化行为相关联。

这些技术共同解决了建立结构-性能关系的挑战，方法如下：

1. 提供掺杂剂诱导的催化剂可还原性和氧移动性变化的直接证据。
2. 提供铈氧化态变化的定量数据。
3. 揭示掺杂剂对反应中间体稳定性和反应性的影响。
4. 将结构修饰与催化性能相关联。

然而，为了完全排除其他解释并确认掺杂剂的均匀分布，还需要额外的表征方法，如带有元素映射的高分辨率透射电子显微镜（HRTEM）、用于表面成分分析的X射线光电子能谱（XPS）以及用于确定局部结构的扩展X射线吸收精细结构（EXAFS）。
